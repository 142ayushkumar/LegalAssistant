Coconut Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Coconut Development Board Act, 1979.BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirty-eight Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Coconut Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Coconut Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1987 .
Coconut Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 2--> In section 4 of the Coconut Development Board Act, 1979 , in sub-section (4), in clause (b), for the words "the Agriculture Commissioner", the words "the Horticulture Commissioner" shall be substituted.
