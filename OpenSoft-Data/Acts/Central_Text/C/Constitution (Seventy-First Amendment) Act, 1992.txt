Constitution (Seventy-First Amendment) Act, 1992_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Constitution of India. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Forty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Constitution (Seventy-First Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Constitution(Seventy-first Amendment) Act, 1992.
Constitution (Seventy-First Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 2--> In the Eighth Schedule to the Constitution,- (a) existing entry 7 shall be re-numbered as entry 8, and before entry8 as so re-numbered, the entry "7. Konkani." shall be inserted; (b) existing entry 8 shall be re-numbered as entry 10, and before entry 10 as so re-numbered, the entry "9. Manipuri." shall be inserted; (c) existing entries 9 to 15 shall be re-numbered as entries 12 to 18 respectively, and before entry 12 as so re-numbered, the entry "11.Nepali." shall be inserted.
