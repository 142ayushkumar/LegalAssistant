Central Silk Board (Amendment) Act, 1952_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Central Silk Board Act, 1948. BE it enacted by Parliament as follow :-
Central Silk Board (Amendment) Act, 1952_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Central Silk Board (Amendment) Act, 1952.
Central Silk Board (Amendment) Act, 1952_Section 2--> In section 4 of the Central Silk Board Act, 1948 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for clause (c) of sub-section (3), the following clause shall be substituted, namely :- "(c) three persons elected by the members of the House of the People from among themselves and one person elected by the members of the Council of States from among themselves;"
Central Silk Board (Amendment) Act, 1952_Section 3--> In section 6 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1) for the words "The Board shall elect from among its members" the words "The Central Government shall appoint from among the members of the Board" shall be substituted.
Central Silk Board (Amendment) Act, 1952_Section 4--> In section 13 of the principal Act, in clause (b) of sub-section (2),the words "the election of the Vice-Chairman of the Board; and" shall be omitted.
