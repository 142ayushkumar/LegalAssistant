Constitution (Seventy-Seventh Amendment) Act, 1995_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Constitution of India.BE it enacted by Parliament in the Forty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Constitution (Seventy-Seventh Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Constitution (Seventy-seventh Amendment) Act, 1995.
Constitution (Seventy-Seventh Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 2--> In article 16 of the Constitution of India , afterclause (4), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(4A) Nothing in this article shall prevent the State from making anyprovision for reservation in matters of promotion to any class orclasses of posts in the services under the State in favour of theScheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes which, in the opinion of the State, are not adequately represented in the services under theState.".
