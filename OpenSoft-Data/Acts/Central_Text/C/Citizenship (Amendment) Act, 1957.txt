Citizenship (Amendment) Act, 1957_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Citizenship Act, 1955. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Citizenship (Amendment) Act, 1957_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Citizenship (Amendment) Act, 1957.
Citizenship (Amendment) Act, 1957_Section 2--> In the First Schedule to the Citizenship Act, 1955 (57 of 1955), under the heading "A.The following Commonwealth countries: -", after entry 8, the following entries shall be inserted, namely : - "9.Ghana. 10.Federation of Malaya. 11.Singapore.".
