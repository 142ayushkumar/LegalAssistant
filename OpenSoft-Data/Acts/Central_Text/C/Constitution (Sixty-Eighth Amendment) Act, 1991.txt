Constitution (Sixty-Eighth Amendment) Act, 1991_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Constitution of India.BE it enacted by Parliament in the Forty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Constitution (Sixty-Eighth Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Constitution (Sixty-eighth Amendment) Act, 1991.
Constitution (Sixty-Eighth Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 2--> In article 356 of the Constitution, inclause (4), in the third Proviso, for the words "four years", the words "five years" shall be substituted.
