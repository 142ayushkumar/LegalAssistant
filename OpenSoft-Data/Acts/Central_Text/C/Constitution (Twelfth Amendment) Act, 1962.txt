Constitution (Twelfth Amendment) Act, 1962_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Constitution of India BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Constitution (Twelfth Amendment) Act, 1962_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Constitution (Twelfth Amendment) Act, 1962. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 20th day ofDecember 1961.
Constitution (Twelfth Amendment) Act, 1962_Section 2--> In the FirstSchedule to the Constitution , under the heading "THE UNIONTERRITORIES", after entry 7, the following entry shall be inserted,namely:- 8. Goa, Daman The territories which immediately before and Diu the twentieth day of December, 1961 were comprised in Goa, Daman and Diu.".
Constitution (Twelfth Amendment) Act, 1962_Section 3--> In article 240 of the Constitution, inclause (1), after entry (c), the following entry shall be inserted,namely:- "(d) Goa, Daman and Diu.".
