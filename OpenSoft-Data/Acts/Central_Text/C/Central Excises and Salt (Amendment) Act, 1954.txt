Central Excises and Salt (Amendment) Act, 1954_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Central Excises and Salt (Amendment) Act, 1954_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Central Excises and Salt (Amendment) Act, 1954. 2.It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 30th day of July, 1954.
Central Excises and Salt (Amendment) Act, 1954_Section 2--> In sub-item II of Item No.9 in the First schedule to the Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944 , the following shall be inserted at the end namely:- "(3) Biris in the manufacture of which any process has been (Three rupees per thousand.)" conducted with the aid of machines operated with or without the aid of power.
Central Excises and Salt (Amendment) Act, 1954_Section 3--> The amendment made by section 2 shall apply to biris manufactured as specified therein which are lying in stock on the date of commencement of this act in any premises where biris tare manufactured or in any premises appurtenant thereto as it applies to biris similarly manufactured on or after the said date.
Central Excises and Salt (Amendment) Act, 1954_Section 4--> The Central Excises and salt (Amendment) Ordinance, 1954) is hereby repealed.
