Constitution (Seventy-Sixth Amendment) Act, 1994_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Constitution of India.BE it enacted by Parliament in the Forty-fifth Year of the Republic ofIndia as follows:-
Constitution (Seventy-Sixth Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Constitution (Seventy-sixth Amendment) Act, 1994.
Constitution (Seventy-Sixth Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 2--> In the Ninth Schedule to the Constitution , after entry 257 and before the Explanation, thefollowing entry shall be inserted, namely:- "257A. The Tamil Nadu Backward Classes, Scheduled Castes andScheduled Tribes (Reservation of Seats in Educational Institution and of Appointments or Posts in the Services under the State) Act, 1993 Tamil Nadu Act 45 of 1994."
