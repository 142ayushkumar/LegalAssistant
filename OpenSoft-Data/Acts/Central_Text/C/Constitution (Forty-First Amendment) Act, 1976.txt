Constitution (Forty-First Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Constitution of India.Be it enacted by Parliament in the Twenty-Seventh year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Constitution (Forty-First Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Constitution (Forty-firstAmendment) Act, 1976.
Constitution (Forty-First Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> In article 316 of the Constitution , inclause (2), for the words "sixty years", the words "sixty-two years"shall be substituted.
