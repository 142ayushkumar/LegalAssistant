Constitution (Third Amendment) Act, 1954_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Constitution of India.BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Constitution (Third Amendment) Act, 1954_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Constitution (Third Amendment) Act 1954.
Constitution (Third Amendment) Act, 1954_Section 2--> In the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution , for entry 33 of List III, the following entry shall be substituted, namely:- "33. Trade and commerce in, and the production, supply and distribution of,:- (a) the products of any industry where the control of such industry by the Union is declared by Parliament by law to be expedient in the public interest, and imported goods of the same kind as such products; (b) foodstuffs, including edible oil seeds and oils; (c) cattle fodder, including oil cakes and other concentrates; (d) raw cotton whether ginned or unginned, and cotton seeds; and (e) raw jute.".
