Cotton Transport Repeal Act, 1995_Section Preamble--> An Act to repeal the Cotton Transport Act, 1923. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Forty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Cotton Transport Repeal Act, 1995_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Cotton Transport Repeal Act, 1995.
Cotton Transport Repeal Act, 1995_Section 2--> The Cotton Transport Act, 1923 is hereby repealed. K.L.MOHANPURA,Secy, to the Govt.of India.
