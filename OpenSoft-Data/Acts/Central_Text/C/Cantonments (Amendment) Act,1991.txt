Cantonments (Amendment) Act,1991_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Cantonments Act, 1924. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Forty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Cantonments (Amendment) Act,1991_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Cantonments (Amendment) Act, 1991.
Cantonments (Amendment) Act,1991_Section 2--> In section 27 of the Cantonments Act, 1924 , in sub-section (1), for the words "twenty-one years", the words "eighteen years" shall be substituted.
