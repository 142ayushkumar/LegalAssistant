Countess of Dufferin's Fund (Repeal) Act, 2002_Section Preamble--> An Act to repeal the Countess of Dufferin's Fund Act, 1957BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:
Countess of Dufferin's Fund (Repeal) Act, 2002_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Countess of Dufferin's Fund (Repeal) Act, 2002.
Countess of Dufferin's Fund (Repeal) Act, 2002_Section 2--> The Countess of Dufferin's Fund Act, 1957 is hereby repealed. ---- SUBHASH C. JAIN, Secy. to the Govt. of India.
