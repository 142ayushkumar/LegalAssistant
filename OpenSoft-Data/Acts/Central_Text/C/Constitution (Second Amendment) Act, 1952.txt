Constitution (Second Amendment) Act, 1952_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Constitution of India. BE it enacted by Parliament as follows:-
Constitution (Second Amendment) Act, 1952_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Constitution (SecondAmendment) Act, 1952.
Constitution (Second Amendment) Act, 1952_Section 2--> In sub-clause (b) of clause (1) of article 81 of the Constitution , the words and figures "not less than one member for every 750,000 of the population and" shall be omitted.
