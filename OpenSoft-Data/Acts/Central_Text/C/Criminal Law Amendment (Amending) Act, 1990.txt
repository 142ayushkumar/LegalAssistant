Criminal Law Amendment (Amending) Act, 1990_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Criminal Law Amendment Act, 1961.BE it enacted by Parliament in the Forty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Criminal Law Amendment (Amending) Act, 1990_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Criminal Law Amendment (Amending) Act, 1990.
Criminal Law Amendment (Amending) Act, 1990_Section 2--> In the Criminal Law Amendment Act, 1961 (22 of 1961), section 2 shall be renumbered as sub-section (1) thereof, and after sub-section (1) as so renumbered, the following sub-sections shall be inserted, namely :- "(2) Whoever publishes a map of India, which is not in conformity with the maps of India as published by the Survey of India, shall be punishable with imprisonment which may extend to six months, or with fine, or with both. (3) No court shall take cognizance of an offence punishable under sub-section (2), except on a complaint made by the Government.".
