Contingency Fund of India (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Contingency Fund of India Act, 1950. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Contingency Fund of India (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Contingency Fund of India (Amendment) Act, 1976.
Contingency Fund of India (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> In section 2 of the Contingency Fund of India Act, 1950 - (a) in the opening paragraph, for the words "thirty crores of rupees", the words "fifty crores of rupees" shall be substituted; (b) the proviso shall be omitted.
