Code of Criminal Procedure (Amendment) Bill, 2015_Section Preamble--> A Bill further to amend the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973. Be it enacted by Parliament in the Sixty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Code of Criminal Procedure (Amendment) Bill, 2015_Section 1--> (1) This Act may by called the Code of Criminal Procedure (Amendment) Act, 2015. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the Central Government may, by notification in the official Gazette, appoint.
Code of Criminal Procedure (Amendment) Bill, 2015_Section 2--> In section 196 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 sub-sections (1), (1A) and (3) shall be deleted.
