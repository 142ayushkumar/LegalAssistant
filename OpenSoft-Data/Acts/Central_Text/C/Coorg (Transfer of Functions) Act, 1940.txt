Coorg (Transfer of Functions) Act, 1940_Section Preamble--> An Act to provide for the disposal of functions heretofore vested in the Commissioner of Coorg. Whereas, the office of the Commissioner of Coorg will shortly cease to exist and it is expedient to provide for the disposal of functions heretofore vested in the said Commissioner. It is hereby enacted as follows -
Coorg (Transfer of Functions) Act, 1940_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Coorg (Transfer of Functions) Act, 1940.1 (2) It shall come into force on such date2 as the 3 [State] Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint. Received the assent of the Governor General on the 18th May, 1940 1st July, 1940 Coorg Notification No. R.F. 122/65/40, dated 19-6-1940 Substituted by the A.O. 1950
Coorg (Transfer of Functions) Act, 1940_Section 2--> (1) The enactments specified in the First Schedule in their application to the 1 [State] of Coorg are hereby amended to the extent and in the manner mentioned in the fourth column thereof. (2) The enactments specified in the Second Schedule are hereby amended to the extent and in the manner mentioned in the fourth column thereof. Substituted by the A.O. 1950
Coorg (Transfer of Functions) Act, 1940_Section 3--> The enactments and notifications specified in the Third Schedule are hereby repealed to the extent mentioned in the fourth column thereof.
Coorg (Transfer of Functions) Act, 1940_Section 4--> No enactment in force in the 1 [State] of Coorg whereby a power to make rules or bye-laws is expressed to be given subject to the condition of the rules or bye-laws being made after previous publication shall be deemed to impose that condition on the making of amendments to rules and bye-laws for the purpose only of providing for the disposal of functions heretofore vested in the Commissioner of Coorg. Substituted by the A.O. 1950
Coorg (Transfer of Functions) Act, 1940_Section 5--> Any appointment, notification, order, scheme, rule, form or bye-law made or issued, before the commencement of this Act, by an authority for the making or issuing of which a new authority is substituted by or under this Act, shall, unless inconsistent with this Act, be deemed to have been made or issued by such new authority, unless and until superseded by an appointment, notification, order, scheme, rule, form or bye-law made or issued by such new authority.
