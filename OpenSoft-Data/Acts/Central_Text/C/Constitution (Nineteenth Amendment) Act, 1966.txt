Constitution (Nineteenth Amendment) Act, 1966_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Constitution of India. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Seventeenth Year of the Republic ofIndia as follows:-
Constitution (Nineteenth Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Constitution (NineteenthAmendment) Act, 1966.
Constitution (Nineteenth Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 2--> In article 324 of the Constitution , inclause (1), the words, "including the appointment of electiontribunals for the decision of doubts and disputes arising out of or inconnection with elections to Parliament and to the Legislatures ofStates" shall be omitted.
