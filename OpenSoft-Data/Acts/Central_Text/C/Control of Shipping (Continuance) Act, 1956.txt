Control of Shipping (Continuance) Act, 1956_Section Preamble--> An Act to continue the control of Shipping Act, 1947, for a further period.  BE it enacted by Parliament in the Seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Control of Shipping (Continuance) Act, 1956_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Control of Shipping (Continuance) Act, 1956.
Control of Shipping (Continuance) Act, 1956_Section 2--> In sub-section (3), of section 1 of the control of Shipping Act, 1947 (26 of 1947), for the figures "1956", the figures "1958" shall be substituted.
