Central Excise Act, 1944_Section 1--> 1 [(1) This Act may be called the Central Excise Act, 1944.] 2 (2) It extends to the whole of India 3 [...]. (3) It shall come into force on such date4 as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint in this behalf. Substituted by Act 33 of 1996, s.71, for sub-section (1) (w.e.f. 28-9-1996). This Act has been applied to- (1) all the partially excluded areas in the State of Orissa by Orissa Government Notification No. 1226-n-C-13/44-Com., dated 21st March, 1944; (2) Darjeeling District with effect from 28th February, 1944, see Bengal Government Notification No. 342-R, dated 22nd March, 1944; (3) excluded areas in the State of Madras (with modifications), see Madras Government Notification No. 37, Fort St. George Gazette, Part I, dated 11th April, 1944; (4) partially excluded areas in the State of Madras with effect from 28th February, 1944, see Madras Government Notification No. 745-Public-Political, dated 4th March, 1944. This Act has been extended to Goa, Daman and Diu by Reg. 12 of 1962, s.3 and Schedule; to Dadra and Nagar Haveli by Reg. 6 of 1963, s.2 and Schedule I and to the whole of the Union territory of Lakshadweep by Reg. 8 of 1965, s.3 and Schedule The words "except the State of Jammu and Kashmir" omitted by Act 41 of 1954, s.2 and Schedule (w.e.f. 8-10-1954). Came into force on 28-2-1944, see Notification No. III-D, dated 26th February, 1944, Gazette of India, Extra., 1944.
