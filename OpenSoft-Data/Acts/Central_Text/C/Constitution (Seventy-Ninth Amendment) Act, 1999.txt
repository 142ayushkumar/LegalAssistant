Constitution (Seventy-Ninth Amendment) Act, 1999_Section Preamble--> An act further to amend the Constitution of India. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fiftieth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Constitution (Seventy-Ninth Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Constitution (Seventy-Ninth Amendment) Act, 1999. (2) It shall come into force on the 25th day of January, 2000.
Constitution (Seventy-Ninth Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 2--> In article 334 of the Constitution , for the words "fifty years", the words "sixty years" shall be substituted.
