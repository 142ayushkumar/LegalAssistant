Central Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Central Sales Tax Act, 1956. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Central Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Central Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1958.
Central Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 2--> In Section 1 of the Central Sales Tax Act, 1956 (74 of 1956), in sub-section Amendment (2), the words "except the State of Jammu and Kashmir" shall be omitted.
