Coal Mines Nationalisation Laws (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 20--> Every claim made, before the date on which this act receives the assent of the President, on behalf of all or any of the persons employed by the owner of- (a) a coking coal mine or group of coking coal mines or a coke oven plant; or (b) a coal mine or group of coal mines, or on behalf of a group of such persons, by a Trade Union, registered under the Trade Unions Act, 1926 (16 of 1926), or the Chief Labour Commissioner (Central) appointed by the Central Government or any officer subordinate to him, against such owner, and every action taken in relation to such claim shall be deemed to have been made or taken in the case of a coking a coal mine or a coke oven plant, in accordance with the provisions of the Coking Coal Mines (Nationalization) Act, 1972 (36 of 1972), and in the case of a coal mine, in accordance with the provisions of the Coal Mines (Nationalization) act,.1973 (26 of 1973), as amended by this act, as if the relevant Act, as amendment by this act were in force at all material times.
