Customs (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Customs Act, 1962.BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fortieth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Customs (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Customs (Amendment) Act, 1989.
Customs (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 2--> In section 123 of the Customs Act, 1962 (52 of 1962) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (2), for the words ", diamonds, manufactures of gold or diamonds", the words "and manufactures thereof" shall be substituted.
Customs (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 3--> In section 135 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), in clause (I), in the proviso, for the words "one year", the words "three years" shall be substituted.
