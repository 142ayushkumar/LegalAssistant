Constitution (Twenty-First Amendment) Act, 1967_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Constitution of India. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Eighteenth Year of the Republic ofIndia as follows:-
Constitution (Twenty-First Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Constitution (Twenty-first Amendment) Act, 1967.
Constitution (Twenty-First Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 2--> In the Eighth Schedule to the Constitution ,:- (a) entries 12 to 14 shall be re-numbered as entries 13 to 15 respec-tively, and (b) before entry "13" as so re-numbered, the entry "12. Sindhi."shall be inserted.
