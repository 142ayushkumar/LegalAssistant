Constitution (Sixtieth Amendment) Act, 1988_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Constitution of India.BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirty-ninth Year of the Republicof India as follows:-
Constitution (Sixtieth Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Constitution (SixtiethAmendment) Act, 1988.
Constitution (Sixtieth Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 2--> In article 276 of the Constitution , inclause (2),:- (a) for the words "two hundred and fifty rupees", the words "twothousand and five hundred rupees" shall be substituted; (b) the proviso shall be omitted.
