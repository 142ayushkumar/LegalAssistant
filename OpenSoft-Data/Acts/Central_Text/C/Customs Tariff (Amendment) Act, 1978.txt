Customs Tariff (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Customs Tariff Act, 1975 .BE it enacted by Parliament in the twenty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Customs Tariff (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 1--> (1) This act may be called the customs Tariff (Amendment) Act, 1978 (2) It shall come into force on such date as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official gazette, appoint.
Customs Tariff (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 2--> In section 4 of the Customs Tariff act, 1975 (51 of 1975) (hereinafter referred to as the principal act),- (a) in sub-section (1), for the words "the produce or manufacture of the United Kingdom or of such other preferential area," the words "the produce of manufacture of such preferential are" shall be substituted; (b) in sub-section (2), for the words "the produce or manufacture of the United Kingdom or of any other preferential area" the words "the produced or manufacture of any preferential area" shall be substituted; (c) in sub-section (3), for he words "united Kingdom" Means the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland and "other preferential area" the words "preferential area" shall be substituted.
Customs Tariff (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 3--> In section 7 of the principal act in sub-section 91), for the word, brackets and figure "column (6)," the words, brackets and figure "column (5)," shall be substituted.
Customs Tariff (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 4--> The first Schedule to the principal act shall be amended in the manner specified in the schedule.
