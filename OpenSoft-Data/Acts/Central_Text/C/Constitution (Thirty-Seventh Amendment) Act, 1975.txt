Constitution (Thirty-Seventh Amendment) Act, 1975_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Constitution of India.BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twenty-sixth Year of the Republicof India as follows:-
Constitution (Thirty-Seventh Amendment) Act, 1975_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Constitution(Thirty-seventh Amendment) Act, 1975.
Constitution (Thirty-Seventh Amendment) Act, 1975_Section 2--> In article 239A of the Constitution , inclause (1), for the words "Pondicherry and Mizoram", the words"Pondicherry, Mizoram and Arunachal Pradesh" shall be substituted.
Constitution (Thirty-Seventh Amendment) Act, 1975_Section 3--> In article 240 of the Constitution , inclause (1), in both the provisos, for the words "Pondicherry orMizoram", the words "Pondicherry, Mizoram or Arunachal Pradesh" shallbe substituted.
