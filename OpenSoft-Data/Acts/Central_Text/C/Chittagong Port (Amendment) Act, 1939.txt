Chittagong Port (Amendment) Act, 1939_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Chittagong Port Act, 1914, for a certain purpose. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Chittagong Port Act, 1914, for the purpose hereinafter appearing; It is hereby enacted as follows :-
Chittagong Port (Amendment) Act, 1939_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Chittagong Port (Amendment) Act, 1939.1 Received the assent of the Governor General on the 21st April, 1939.
Chittagong Port (Amendment) Act, 1939_Section 2--> In section 80 of the Chittagong Port Act, 1914, for the word " loans, " the words and figures " loans contracted before the 1st day of January, 1939, " shall be substituted.
