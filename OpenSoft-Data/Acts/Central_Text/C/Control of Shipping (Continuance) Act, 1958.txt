Control of Shipping (Continuance) Act, 1958_Section Preamble--> An Act to continue the Control of Shipping Act, 1947, for a further period. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Control of Shipping (Continuance) Act, 1958_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Control of Shipping Act, 1958.
Control of Shipping (Continuance) Act, 1958_Section 2--> In sub-section (3) of Section 1 of the Control of Shipping Act, 1947 (26 of 1947), for the figures "1958", the figures "1960" shall be substituted.
