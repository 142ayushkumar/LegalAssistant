Code of Civil Procedure (Amendment) Act, 1956_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908. BE it enacted by parliament in the Seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Code of Civil Procedure (Amendment) Act, 1956_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Code of Civil Procedure (Amendment) Act, 1956. (2) It shall come into force on such date1 as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint. 1st January 1957, vide Notification No.S R O.3192, dated 31-12-56, Gazette of India, Extraordinary, Part II, Section 3, p.2799.

Code of Civil Procedure (Amendment) Act, 1956_Section 16--> Where, before the commencement of this Act, the execution of a decree has been transferred to the collector under section 68 of the principal Act and is pending before the Collector on such commencement, then, notwithstanding the omission of sections 68 to 72 inclusive and the Third schedule to the principal Act, the decree shall be executed by the collector in accordance with the provisions of the said sections and the said Schedule, as if this Act had not been passed.
