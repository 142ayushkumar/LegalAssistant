Central Excises and Salt (Amendment) Act, 1957_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Central Excises and Salt (Amendment) Act, 1957_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Central Excises and Salt (Amendment) Act.1957.
Central Excises and Salt (Amendment) Act, 1957_Section 2--> In sub-section (2) of section 37 of the Central Excises and Salt Act, 1944 (1 of 1944)(hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in clause (xvi), the proviso shall be omitted.
Central Excises and Salt (Amendment) Act, 1957_Section 3--> In Item No.12 of the First Schedule to the principal Act, in Explanation 11, after rule (b), the following rule shall be inserted namely:- "(bb) In the case of fabrics manufactured from cotton and other yarns, the other yarns shall, for the aforesaid purpose, be deemed to be cotton yarn.".
