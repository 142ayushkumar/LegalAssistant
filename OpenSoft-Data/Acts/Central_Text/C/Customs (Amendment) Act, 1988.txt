Customs (Amendment) Act, 1988_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Customs Act, 1962.BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows: --
Customs (Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Customs (Amendment) Act, 1988. (2) It shall come into force on such date 1 as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint. 16-8-1988: Vide Notifications No.G.S.R.799(E), dated 18-7-1988, Gazette of India, Extraordinary, 1988, Part II, section.3(I).
Customs (Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 2--> In section 14 of the Customs Act, 1962 (52 of 1962.) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), (a) in sub-section (1), (I) in clause (a), the brackets and letter "(a)" shall be omitted; (ii) clause (b) shall be omitted; (b) after sub-section (1), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely:- "(1A) Subject to the provisions of sub-section (1), the price referred to in that sub-section in respect of imported goods shall be determined in accordance with the rules made in this behalf."; (c) in sub-section (2), after the words brackets and figure "in sub-section (1)", the words, brackets, figure and letter "or sub-section (1A) shall be inserted.
Customs (Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 3--> In section 156 of the principal , in sub-section (2), for clause (a), the following clause shall be substituted, namely :-- "(a) the manner of determining the price of imported goods under sub-section (1A) of section 14;".
