Constitution (Tenth Amendment) Act, 1961_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Constitution of India.BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twelfth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Constitution (Tenth Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Constitution (Tenth Amendment) Act, 1961. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 11th day of August, 1961.
Constitution (Tenth Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 2--> In the First Schedule to the Constitution , under the heading "THE UNIONTERRITORIES", after entry 6, the following entry shall be inserted,namely:- "7. Dadra and The territory which immediately before Nagar Haveli the eleventh day of August, 1961 was comprised in Free Dadra and Nagar Haveli.".
Constitution (Tenth Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 3--> In article 240 of the Constitution , in clause (1), after entry (b), the following entry shall be inserted,namely:- "(c) Dadra and Nagar Haveli.".
