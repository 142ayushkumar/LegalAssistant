Constitution (Ninety-fifth Amendment) Act, 2009_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Constitution of India. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Sixtieth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Constitution (Ninety-fifth Amendment) Act, 2009_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Constitution (Ninety-fifth amendment) Act, 2009. (2) It shall come into force on the 25th day of January, 2010.
Constitution (Ninety-fifth Amendment) Act, 2009_Section 2--> In article 334 of the Constitution, for the words "sixty years", the words "seventy years" shall be substituted. V. K. BHASIN, Secy. to the Govt. of India
