Cotton Fabrics (Additional Excise Duty) (Repeal) Act, 1967_Section Preamble--> An Act to provide for the repeal of the Cotton Fabrics (Additional Excise Duty) Act, 1957. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Eighteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Cotton Fabrics (Additional Excise Duty) (Repeal) Act, 1967_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Cotton Fabrics (Additional Excise Duty) (Repeal) Act, 1967.
Cotton Fabrics (Additional Excise Duty) (Repeal) Act, 1967_Section 2--> The Cotton Fabrics (Additional Excise Duty) Act, 1957 , is hereby repealed.
