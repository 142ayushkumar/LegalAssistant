Constitution (Ninety-sixth Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Constitution of India. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Constitution (Ninety-sixth Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Constitution (Ninety-sixth Amendment) Act, 2011.
Constitution (Ninety-sixth Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> In the Eighth Schedule to the Constitution, in entry 15, for the word "Oriya", the word "Odia" shall be substituted. V.K. BHASIN, Secy. to the Govt. of India.
