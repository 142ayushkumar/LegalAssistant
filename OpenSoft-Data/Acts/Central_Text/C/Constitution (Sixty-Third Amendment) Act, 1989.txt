Constitution (Sixty-Third Amendment) Act, 1989_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Constitution of India.BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fortieth Year of the Republic ofIndia as follows:-
Constitution (Sixty-Third Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called theConstitution (Sixty-third Amendment) Act, 1989. (2) It shall come into force with immediate effect.
Constitution (Sixty-Third Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 2--> In article 356 of the Constitution , inclause (5), the proviso shall be omitted.
Constitution (Sixty-Third Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 3--> Article 359A of the Constitution shall be omitted.
