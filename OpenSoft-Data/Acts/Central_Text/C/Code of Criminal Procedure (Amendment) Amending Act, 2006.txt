Code of Criminal Procedure (Amendment) Amending Act, 2006_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Code of Criminal Procedure (Amendment) Act, 2005. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:--
Code of Criminal Procedure (Amendment) Amending Act, 2006_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Code of Criminal Procedure (Amendment) Amending Act, 2006.
Code of Criminal Procedure (Amendment) Amending Act, 2006_Section 2--> In the Code of Criminal Procedure (Amendment) Act, 2005 , in section 1, in sub-section (2), after the words "by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint", the words "; and different dates may be appointed for different provisions of this Act" shall be inserted. K.N. CHATURVEDI, Secy. to the Government of India.
