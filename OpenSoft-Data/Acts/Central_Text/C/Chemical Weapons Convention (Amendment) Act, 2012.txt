Chemical Weapons Convention (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Chemical Weapons Convention Act, 2000. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Sixty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Chemical Weapons Convention (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Chemical Weapons Convention (Amendment) Act, 2012. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
Chemical Weapons Convention (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 2--> In the Chemical Weapons Convention Act, 2000 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in section 9, in sub-section (1),- (a) after the words "National Authority", the words "or of the Central Government" shall be inserted; (b) the following proviso shall be inserted, namely:- "Provided that such officers shall fulfil the prescribed criteria.".
