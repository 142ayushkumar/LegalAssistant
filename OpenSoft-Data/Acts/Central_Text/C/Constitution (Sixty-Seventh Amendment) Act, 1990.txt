Constitution (Sixty-Seventh Amendment) Act, 1990_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Constitution of India. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Forty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Constitution (Sixty-Seventh Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Constitution(Sixty-seventh Amendment) Act, 1990.
Constitution (Sixty-Seventh Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 2--> In article 356 of the Constitution , inclause (4), in the third proviso, for the words "three years and sixmonths", the words "four years" shall be substituted.
