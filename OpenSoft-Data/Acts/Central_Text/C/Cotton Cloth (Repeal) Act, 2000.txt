Cotton Cloth (Repeal) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> Act Objective: An Act to repeal the Cotton Cloth Act, 1918. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Cotton Cloth (Repeal) Act, 2000_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Cotton Cloth (Repeal) Act, 2000.
Cotton Cloth (Repeal) Act, 2000_Section 2--> The Cotton Cloth Act, 1918 is hereby repealed. SUBHASH C. JAIN,Secretary to the Govt. of India.
