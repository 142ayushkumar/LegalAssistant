Indian Tariff (Second Amendment) Act, 1939_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Indian Tariff Act, 1934. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Indian Tariff Act, 1934, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; It is hereby enacted as follows :-
Indian Tariff (Second Amendment) Act, 1939_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Indian Tariff (Second Amendment) Act, 1939.1 Received the assent of the Governor General on the 21st April, 1939.
