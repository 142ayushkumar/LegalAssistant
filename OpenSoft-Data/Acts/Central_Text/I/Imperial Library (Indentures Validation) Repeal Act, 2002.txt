Imperial Library (Indentures Validation) Repeal Act, 2002_Section Preamble--> An Act to repeal the Imperial Library (Indentures Validation) Act, 1902 . Be it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-third year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Imperial Library (Indentures Validation) Repeal Act, 2002_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Imperial Library (Indentures Validation) Repeal Act, 2002.
Imperial Library (Indentures Validation) Repeal Act, 2002_Section 2--> The Imperial Library (Indentures Validation) Act, 1902 is hereby repealed. SUBHASH C. JAIN, Secy. to the Govt. of India.
