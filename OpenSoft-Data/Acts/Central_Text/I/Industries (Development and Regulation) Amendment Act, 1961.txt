Industries (Development and Regulation) Amendment Act, 1961_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Industries (Development and Regulation) Act, 1951 .BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twelfth Year of the Republic of India as follows:--
Industries (Development and Regulation) Amendment Act, 1961_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Industries (Development and Regulation) Amendment Act, 1961.
Industries (Development and Regulation) Amendment Act, 1961_Section 2--> In sub-section (2) of section 1 of the Industries (Development and Regulation) Act, 1951 , (65 of 1951) the words "except the State of Jammu and Kashmir" shall be omitted.
