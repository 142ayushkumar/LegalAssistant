Indian Railway Companies (Repeal) Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> An Act to repeal the Indian Railway Companies Act, 1895.BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Indian Railway Companies (Repeal) Act, 2001_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Indian Railway Companies (Repeal) Act, 2001.
Indian Railway Companies (Repeal) Act, 2001_Section 2--> The Indian Railway Companies Act, 1895 is hereby repealed. SUBHASH C. JAIN, Secy. to the Govt. of India.
