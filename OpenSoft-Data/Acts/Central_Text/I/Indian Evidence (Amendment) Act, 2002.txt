Indian Evidence (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Indian Evidence Act, 1872. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:--
Indian Evidence (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Indian Evidence (Amendment) Act, 2002.
Indian Evidence (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 2--> In section 146 of the Indian Evidence Act, 1872 (1 of 1872) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), after clause (3), the following proviso shall be inserted, namely:- "Provided that in a prosecution for rape or attempt to commit rape, it shall not be permissible to put questions in the cross-examination of the prosecutrix as to her general immoral character.".
Indian Evidence (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 3--> In section 155 of the principal Act, clause (4) shall be omitted. SUBHASH C. JAIN, Secy. to the Govt. of India.
