Industries (Development and Regulation) Amendment Act, 1974_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Industries (Development and Regulation) Act, 1951.BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twenty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Industries (Development and Regulation) Amendment Act, 1974_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Industries (Development and Regulation) Amendment Act, 1974. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 29th June, 1974.
Industries (Development and Regulation) Amendment Act, 1974_Section 2--> In the proviso to sub-section (2) of section 18A and in the second proviso to sub-section (2) of section 18FA of the Industries (Development and Regulation) Act, 1951 (65 of 1951) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for the words "ten years", the words "twelve years" shall be substituted.
Industries (Development and Regulation) Amendment Act, 1974_Section 3--> (1) The Industries (Development and Regulation) Amendment Ordinance, 1974, (6 of 1974) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the Industries (Development and Regulation) Act, 1951 , as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the corresponding provisions of the Industries (Development and Regulation) Act, 1951 as amended by this Act.
