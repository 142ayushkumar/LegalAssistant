Indian Boilers (Amendment) Act, 1952_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Indian Boilers Act, 1923. BE it enacted by Parliament as follows:-
Indian Boilers (Amendment) Act, 1952_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Indian Boilers (Amendment) Act, 1952.
Indian Boilers (Amendment) Act, 1952_Section 2--> In section 2B of the Indian Boilers Act, 1923 , the words, brackets, letters and figures "clause (e) of section 6, clauses (c) and (d) of section 11, clause (d) of section 29" shall be omitted.
