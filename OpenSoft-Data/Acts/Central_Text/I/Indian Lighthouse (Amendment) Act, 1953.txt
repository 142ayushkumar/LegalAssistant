Indian Lighthouse (Amendment) Act, 1953_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Indian Lighthouse Act, 1927. BE it enacted by Parliament as follows: -
Indian Lighthouse (Amendment) Act, 1953_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Indian Lighthouse ( Amendment) Act, 1953.
Indian Lighthouse (Amendment) Act, 1953_Section 2--> In sub-section (2) of section 1 of the Indian Lighthouse Act, 1927 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), the words and letter 'except Part B States' shall be omitted.
Indian Lighthouse (Amendment) Act, 1953_Section 3--> In sub-section (1) of section 10 of the principal Act for the words 'two annas' the words 'four annas' shall be substituted.
Indian Lighthouse (Amendment) Act, 1953_Section 4--> If immediately before the 21st day of January, 1950, there was in force in any Part B State any law corresponding tot he principal Act, that law shall, with effect from that date be deemed to have been repealed except as respects things done or omitted to be done before that date.
