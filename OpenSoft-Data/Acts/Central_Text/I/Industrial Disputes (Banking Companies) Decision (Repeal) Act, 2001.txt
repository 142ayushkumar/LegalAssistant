Industrial Disputes (Banking Companies) Decision (Repeal) Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> An Act to repeal the Industrial Disputes (Banking Companies) Decision Act, 1955. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Industrial Disputes (Banking Companies) Decision (Repeal) Act, 2001_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Industrial Disputes (Banking Companies) Decision (Repeal) Act, 2001.
Industrial Disputes (Banking Companies) Decision (Repeal) Act, 2001_Section 2--> The Industrial Disputes (Banking Companies) Decision Act, 1955 is hereby repealed. SUBHASH C. JAIN, Secy. to the Govt. of India.
