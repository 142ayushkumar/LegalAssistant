Inland Waterways Authority of India (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Inland Waterways Authority of India Act, 1985.BE it enacted by Parliament in the forty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows.-
Inland Waterways Authority of India (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Inland Waterways Authority of India (Amendment) Act, 1993.
Inland Waterways Authority of India (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 2--> Section 16 of the Inland Waterways Authority of India Act, 1985 (82 of 1985) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) shall be omitted.
Inland Waterways Authority of India (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 3--> In section 27 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), the portion beginning with the words "but any jurisdiction functions, powers or duties" and ending with the words "be exercised, performed or discharged by the Authority" shall be omitted.
