(b)threatening a lock-out or closure, if a trade union is organised; (c)granting wage increase to workmen at crucial periods of trade union organisation, with a view to undermining the efforts of the trade union, that is to say:- 2.To dominate, interfere with or contribute support, finical or otherwise, to any trade union that is to say:- (a)an employer taking an active interest in organising a trade union of his workmen;
