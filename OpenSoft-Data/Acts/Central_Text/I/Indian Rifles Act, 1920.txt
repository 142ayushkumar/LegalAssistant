Indian Rifles Act, 1920_Section Preamble--> An Act to provide for the better discipline of police officers enrolled in Military Police or Rifle Battalions. WHEREAS it is expedient to provide for the better discipline of police officers enrolled under local Acts in Military Police or Rifle Battalions; It is hereby enacted as follows:-
Indian Rifles Act, 1920_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Indian Rifles Act, 1920.
Indian Rifles Act, 1920_Section 2--> All police officers enrolled under the provisions of any local Military Police or Rifles Act shall be subject to the discipline and , penalties prescribed by such Act, wherever serving in India.
