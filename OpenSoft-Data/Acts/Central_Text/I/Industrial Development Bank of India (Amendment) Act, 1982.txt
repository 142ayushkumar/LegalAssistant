Industrial Development Bank of India (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Industrial Development Bank of India Act, 1964BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Industrial Development Bank of India (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Industrial Development Bank of India (Amendment) Act, 1982.
Industrial Development Bank of India (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 2--> In section 4 of the Industrial Development Bank of India Act, 1964 , in the proviso, for the words "two hundred crores of rupees", the words " five hundred crores of rupees" shall be substituted.
