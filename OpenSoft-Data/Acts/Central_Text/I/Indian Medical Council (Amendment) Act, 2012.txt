Indian Medical Council (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section Preamble--> An Act farther to amend the Indian Medical Council Act, 1956. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Sixty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Indian Medical Council (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Indian Medical Council (Amendment) Act, 2012. (2) It shall come into force on the 15th day of May, 2012.
Indian Medical Council (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 2--> In section 3A of the Indian Medical Council Act, 1956, in sub-section (2), for the words "two years", the words "three years" shall be substituted. V. K. BHASIN, Secy. to the Govt. of India.
