Industries (Development and Regulation) Amendment Act, 1979_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Industries (Development and Regulation) Act, 1951BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirtieth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Industries (Development and Regulation) Amendment Act, 1979_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Industries (Development and Regulation) Amendment Act, 1979. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 30th day of December, 1978.
Industries (Development and Regulation) Amendment Act, 1979_Section 2--> In sub-section (2) of section 18FB of the Industries (Development and Regulation) Act, 1951 (65 of 1951) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in clause (b) of the proviso, for the words "five years" , the words "eight years" shall be substituted.
Industries (Development and Regulation) Amendment Act, 1979_Section 3--> In the First Schedule to the Industries (Development and Regulation) Act, 1951 ,- (a) under the heading "12.MISCELLANEOUS MECHANICAL AND ENGINEERING INDUSTRIES:" , after item (3) , the following items shall be inserted, namely:- "(4) Pressure Cookers. (5) Cutlery. (6) Steel furniture."; (b) under the heading "34.CERAMICS:" , after item (7), the following item shall be inserted , namely:- "(8) Graphite Crucibles."; (c) under the heading "38.MISCELLANEOUS INDUSTRIES:" , after item (2), thefollowing items shall be inserted, namely:- "(3) Zip fasteners (metallic and non-metallic). (4) Oil stoves. (5) Printing, including litho printing industry.".
Industries (Development and Regulation) Amendment Act, 1979_Section 4--> (1) The Industries (Development and Regulation) Amendment Ordinance, 1978(6 of 1978), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the Industries (Development and Regulation) Act, 1951 , as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the Industries (Development and Regulation) Act, 1951 , as amended by this Act.
