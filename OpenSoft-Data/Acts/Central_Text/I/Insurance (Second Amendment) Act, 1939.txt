Insurance (Second Amendment) Act, 1939_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Insurance Act, 1938 for a certain purpose. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Insurance Act, 1938 for the purpose hereinafter appearing ; It is hereby enacted as follows :-
Insurance (Second Amendment) Act, 1939_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Insurance (Second Amendment) Act, 1939.1 Received the assent of the Governor General on the 29th September, 1939.
Insurance (Second Amendment) Act, 1939_Section 2--> In clauses (a) and (b) of sub-section (2) of section 3 of the Insurance Act, 1938, after the words and figure " incorporated under the Indian Companies Act, 1913," the words and figures " or under the Indian Companies Act, 1882, or under the Indian Companies Act, 1866, or under any Act repealed thereby," shall be inserted.
