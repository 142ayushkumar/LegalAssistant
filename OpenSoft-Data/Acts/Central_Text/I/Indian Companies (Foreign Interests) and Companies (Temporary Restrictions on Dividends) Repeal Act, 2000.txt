Indian Companies (Foreign Interests) and Companies (Temporary Restrictions on Dividends) Repeal Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> Act Objective: An Act to repeal the Indian Companies (Foreign Interests) Act, 1918 and the Companies (Temporary Restrictions on Dividends) Act, 1974.BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Indian Companies (Foreign Interests) and Companies (Temporary Restrictions on Dividends) Repeal Act, 2000_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Indian Companies (ForeignInterests) and the Companies (Temporary Restrictions on Dividends)Repeal Act, 2000.
Indian Companies (Foreign Interests) and Companies (Temporary Restrictions on Dividends) Repeal Act, 2000_Section 2--> The Indian Companies(Foreign Interests) Act, 1918 and the Companies (TemporaryRestrictions on Dividends) Act, 1974 are hereby repealed. SUBHASH C. JAIN,Secretary to the Govt. of India.
