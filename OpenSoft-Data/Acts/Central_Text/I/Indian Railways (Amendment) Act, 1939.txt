Indian Railways (Amendment) Act, 1939_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Indian Railways Act, 1890, for a certain purpose. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Indian Railways Act, 1890, for the purpose hereinafter appearing ; It is hereby enacted as follows:-
Indian Railways (Amendment) Act, 1939_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Indian Railways (Amendment) Act, 1939.1 Received the assent of the Governor General on the 28th September, 1939.
Indian Railways (Amendment) Act, 1939_Section 2--> In the Indian Railways Act, 1890, after section 42A, the following section shall be inserted, namely:- " 42B. Power of the Federal Rail way Authority to fix maxi-mum and minimum rates - (1) The Federal Railway Authority may by general or special order fix maximum and minimum rates for the whole or any part of a railway, other than a minor railway, and prescribe the conditions in which such rates will apply. (2) Any complaint that a railway administration is contravening any order issued by the Federal Railway Authority in accordance with the provisions of this section shall be determined by that Authority;"
