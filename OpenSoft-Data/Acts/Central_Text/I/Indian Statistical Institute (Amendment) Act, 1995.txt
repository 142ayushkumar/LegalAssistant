Indian Statistical Institute (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Indian Statistical Institute Act.1959.BE it enacted by Parliament in the Forty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Indian Statistical Institute (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Indian Statistical Institute (Amendment) Act, 1995.(2) It shall come into force on such date as the Central Government may..by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
Indian Statistical Institute (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 2--> In section 4 of the Indian Statistical Institute Act, 1959 for the word "statistics", the words "statistics, mathematics, quantitative economics, computer science and such other subjects related to statistics" shall be substituted.
