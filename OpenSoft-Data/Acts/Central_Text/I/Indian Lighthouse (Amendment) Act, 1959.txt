Indian Lighthouse (Amendment) Act, 1959_Section Preamble--> An act further to amend the Indian Lighthouse Act, 1927. BE it enacted by parliament in the Tenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:---
Indian Lighthouse (Amendment) Act, 1959_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Indian Lighthouse (Amendment) Act, 1959.
Indian Lighthouse (Amendment) Act, 1959_Section 2--> In sub-section (1) of section 10 of the Indian Lighthouse Act, 1927 , (17 of 1927) for the words "four annas", the words "fifty naye paise" shall be substituted.
