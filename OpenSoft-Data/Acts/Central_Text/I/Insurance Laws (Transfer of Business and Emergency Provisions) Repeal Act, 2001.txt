Insurance Laws (Transfer of Business and Emergency Provisions) Repeal Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> Act Objective: An Act to repeal the Allianz Und Stuttgarter Life Insurance Bank (Transfer) Act, 1950, the Life Insurance (Emergency Provisions) Act, 1956and the General Insurance (Emergency Provisions) Act, 1971. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Insurance Laws (Transfer of Business and Emergency Provisions) Repeal Act, 2001_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Insurance Laws (Transfer of Business and Emergency Provisions) Repeal Act, 2001.
Insurance Laws (Transfer of Business and Emergency Provisions) Repeal Act, 2001_Section 2--> The Allianz Und Stuttgarter Life Insurance Bank (Transfer) Act, 1950 is hereby repealed.
Insurance Laws (Transfer of Business and Emergency Provisions) Repeal Act, 2001_Section 3--> The Life Insurance (Emergency Provisions) Act, 1956 is hereby repealed.
Insurance Laws (Transfer of Business and Emergency Provisions) Repeal Act, 2001_Section 4--> The General Insurance (Emergency Provisions) Act, 1971 is hereby repealed.
