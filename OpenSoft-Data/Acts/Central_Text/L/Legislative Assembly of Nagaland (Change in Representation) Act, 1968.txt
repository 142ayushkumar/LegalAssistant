Legislative Assembly of Nagaland (Change in Representation) Act, 1968_Section Preamble--> An Act to provide for a change in representation in the Legislature Assembly of Nagaland and for that purpose to make consequential amendments in the State of Nagaland Act 1962 and the Representation of the People Act, 1950 . BE it enacted by Parliament in the Nineteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows;-
Legislative Assembly of Nagaland (Change in Representation) Act, 1968_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Legislative Assembly of Nagaland (Change in Representation) Act, 1968.
