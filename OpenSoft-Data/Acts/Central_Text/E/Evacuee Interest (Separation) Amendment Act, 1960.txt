Evacuee Interest (Separation) Amendment Act, 1960_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Act, 1951.BE it enacted by Parliament in the Eleventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Evacuee Interest (Separation) Amendment Act, 1960_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Act, 1960. (2).It shall come into force on such date as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
Evacuee Interest (Separation) Amendment Act, 1960_Section 2--> After sub-section (2) of section 6 of the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Act, 1951 (64 of 1951) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely:- ``(3) No application under this section shall be entertained if filed after the expiry of one year from the commencement of the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Amendment Act.1960'.
Evacuee Interest (Separation) Amendment Act, 1960_Section 3--> In sub-section (1) of section 7 of the principal Act, the proviso shall be omitted.
