Essential Services (Maintenance) Ordinance Repeal Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> An ACT to repeal the Essential Services (Maintenance) Ordinance, 1941.BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:
Essential Services (Maintenance) Ordinance Repeal Act, 2001_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Essential Services (Maintenance) Ordinance Repeal Act, 2001.
Essential Services (Maintenance) Ordinance Repeal Act, 2001_Section 2--> The Essential Services (Maintenance) Ordinance, 1941 is hereby repealed.
