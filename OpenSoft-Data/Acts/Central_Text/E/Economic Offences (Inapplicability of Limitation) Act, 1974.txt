Economic Offences (Inapplicability of Limitation) Act, 1974_Section Preamble--> An Act to provide for the inapplicability of the provisions of Chapter XXXVI of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 to certain economic offences BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twenty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Economic Offences (Inapplicability of Limitation) Act, 1974_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Economic Offences (Inapplicability of Limitation) Act, 1974. (2) It extends to the territories to which the Code of Criminal Procedure applies. (3) It shall come into force on the 1st day of April, 1974.
Economic Offences (Inapplicability of Limitation) Act, 1974_Section 2--> Nothing in Chapter XXXVI of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 ,shall apply to- (i) any offence punishable under any of the enactments 1 [or provisions, if any, thereof], specified in the Schedule; or (ii) any other offence, which under the provisions of that Code may be tried along with such offence, and every offence referred to in clause (i) or in clause (ii) may be taken cognizance of by the Court having jurisdiction as if the provisions of that Chapter were not enacted. Inserted by Act 65 of 1984 w.e.f. 08-10-1984.
