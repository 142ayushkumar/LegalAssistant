Esso (Acquisition of Undertakings in India) Amendment Act, 1974_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Esso (Acquisition of Undertakings In India) Act, 1974 . BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twenty -fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Esso (Acquisition of Undertakings in India) Amendment Act, 1974_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Esso (Acquisition of Undertakings in India) Amendment Act, 1974.
Esso (Acquisition of Undertakings in India) Amendment Act, 1974_Section 2--> In section 13 of the Esso (Acquisition of Undertakings in India ) Act, 1974, (4 of 1974) in sub-section (1), for the words "one hundred and eighty days", the words "one year" shall be substituted.
