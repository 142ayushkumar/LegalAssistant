Economic Offences (Inapplicability of Limitation) Amendment Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Economic Offences (Inapplicability of Limitation) Act, 1974. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirty -Second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Economic Offences (Inapplicability of Limitation) Amendment Act, 1981_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Economic Offences (Inapplicability of Limitation) Amendment Act, 1981.
Economic Offences (Inapplicability of Limitation) Amendment Act, 1981_Section 2--> In the Economic Offences (Inapplicability of Limitation) Act, 1974 , ( 12 of 1974 ) in the Schedule, after entry 20, the following entry shall be inserted, namely:- "21.The Industries (Development and Regulation) Act, 1951 (65 of 1951).".
