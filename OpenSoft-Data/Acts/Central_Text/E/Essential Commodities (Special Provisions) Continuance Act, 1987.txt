Essential Commodities (Special Provisions) Continuance Act, 1987_Section Preamble--> An Act to continue the Essential Commodities (Special Provisions) Act, 1981 for a further period. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirty-eight Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Essential Commodities (Special Provisions) Continuance Act, 1987_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Essential Commodities (Special Provisions) Continuance Act, 1987.
Essential Commodities (Special Provisions) Continuance Act, 1987_Section 2--> In paragraph 2 of the preamble to the Essential Commodities (Special Provisions) Act, 1981 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for the words "five years", the words "ten years" shall be substituted.
Essential Commodities (Special Provisions) Continuance Act, 1987_Section 3--> In section 1 of the principal Act, in sub-section (3), for the words "five years", the words "ten years" shall be substituted.
