Estate Duty (Amendment) Act, 1960_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Estate Duty Act, 1953.BE it enacted by Parliament in the Eleventh Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Estate Duty (Amendment) Act, 1960_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Estate Duty (Amendment) Act, 1960. (2) It shall come into force on the date on which the Estate Duty (Amendment) Act, 1958 , comes into force.(33 of 1953) 2.Insertion of new section SA.) (34 of 1953.- In the Estate Duty Act, 1953 , after section 5, the following section shall be inserted, namely;- `5A.(1) The amendments made to this Act by-(Application in respect of estate duty on agricultural lands of Acts amending this Act ) ( I) the Finance Act, 1954 ,(17 of 1954) (ii) the Taxation Laws (Extension to Jammu and Kashmir) Act, 1954 , and (41 of 1954) (iii) the Repealing and Amending Act, 1957 , (36 of 1957) shall apply, and shall be deemed to have applied, to estate duty in respect of agricultural lands situate in the territories comprised in the States, expect those in the States of West Bengal and Jammu and Kashmir, but including those in the transferred territories, on and from the dates on which the amendments made by each of the amending Acts aforesaid respectively took effect; Provided that where in respect of any part of the territories comprised in any of the said States the resolution of the Legislature concerned under clause (1) of article 252 of the Constitution was passed subsequently to the date on which the said amendments would otherwise have taken effect by virtue of the foregoing provision, the said amendments shall, in respect of estate ditty on agricultural lands in that part of the said territories, be deemed to have taken effect only on the date on which this Act.became applicable to estate duty in respect of agricultural lands. (2) The amendments made to this Act by the Estate Duty (Amendment) Act, 1958 , shall also apply to the levy of estate duty in respect of agricultural lands situate in the territories comprised in the States except those in the Sates of Orissa West Bengal and Jammu and Kashmir.(33 of 1958) (3) This Act shall cease to apply to the levy to the levy of estate duty in respect of agricultural lands situate in the State of Orissa and in the transferred territories, and.notwithstanding anything contained in sub-section (1), shall be deemed to have so ceased on and from the 1st day of April, 1959. Explanation.- `Transferred territories' means the territories which as from the 1st day of November, 1956, were added to the State of West Bengal by virtue of sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Bihar and West Bengal (Transfer of Territories) Act, 1956 .(40 of 1956) 3.Omission of section 30 (33 of 1959).- Section 30 of the Estate Duty (Amendment) Act, 1958 shall be omitted.
