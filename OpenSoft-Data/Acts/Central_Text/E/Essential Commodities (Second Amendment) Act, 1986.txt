Essential Commodities (Second Amendment) Act, 1986_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Essential Commodities Act, 1955 BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirty Seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Essential Commodities (Second Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Essential Commodities (Second Amendment) Act, 1986 (2) It shall come into force on such date 1 as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint 1-5-1987 ; vide Notification No.S.O.438(E), dated 27-4-1987, Gazette of India, Extraordinary, 1987, part II section 3(ii)
Essential Commodities (Second Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 2--> In section 11 of the Essential Commodities Act, 1955 (10 of 1955) (hereinafter referred to as the principle Act),- (a) after the words " Indian Penal Code ", the words "or any person aggrieved or any recognised consumer association, whether such person is a member of that association or not" shall be inserted; (b) the following Explanation shall be added at the end, namely:- 'Explanation.- For the purposes of this section and section 12AA, "recognised consumer association" means a voluntary consumer association registered under the Companies Act,1956 (1 of 1956) or any other law for the time being in force.'.
Essential Commodities (Second Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 3--> In sub-section (1) of section 12AA of the principal Act, in clause (e), after the words "Government concerned", the words "or any person aggrieved or any recognised consumer association, whether such person is a member of that association or not," shall be inserted.
