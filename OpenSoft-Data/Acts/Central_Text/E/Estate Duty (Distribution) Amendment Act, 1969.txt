Estate Duty (Distribution) Amendment Act, 1969_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amended Estate Duty (Distribution) Act, 1962. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twentieth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Estate Duty (Distribution) Amendment Act, 1969_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Estate Duty (Distribution) Amendment Act, 1969. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st day of April, 1969.
Estate Duty (Distribution) Amendment Act, 1969_Section 2--> In the long title of the Estate Duty (Distribution) Act, 1962 (9 of 1962.) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for the figures, letters and words "12th day of August, 1965," the figures, letters and words "31st day of October, 1968" shall be substituted.
