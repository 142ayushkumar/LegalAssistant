Monopolies and Restrictive Trade Practices (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Monopolies and Restrictive Trade Practices Act, 1969 BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Monopolies and Restrictive Trade Practices (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Monopolies and Restrictive Trade Practices (Amendment) Act, 1985
Monopolies and Restrictive Trade Practices (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 2--> In the Monopolies and Restrictive Trade Practices Act, 1969 in section 20, in clause (a), for the words "twenty crores", the words "one hundred crores" shall be substituted.
