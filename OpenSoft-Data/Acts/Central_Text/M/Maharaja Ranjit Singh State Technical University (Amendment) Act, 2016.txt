Maharaja Ranjit Singh State Technical University (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Maharaja Ranjit Singh State Technical University Act, 2014. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Sixty seventh Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Maharaja Ranjit Singh State Technical University (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Maharaja Ranjit Singh State Technical University (Amendment) Act, 2016. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Maharaja Ranjit Singh State Technical University (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 2--> In the Maharaja Ranjit Singh State Technical University Act, 2014 (Punjab Act No. 5 of 2015), for the words "Maharaja Ranjit Singh State Technical University", wherever occurring, except in section 30, the words "Maharaja Ranjit Singh Punjab Technical University" shall be substituted. Vivek Puri Secretary to Government of Punjab, Department of Legal and Legislative Affairs.
