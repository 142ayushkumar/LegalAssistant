Merged States (Laws) Act, 1949_Section Preamble--> 1 An Act to extend certain laws to certain areas administered as parts of Governors' Provinces or as Chief Commissioners' Provinces;WHEREAS by orders under section 290A of the Government of India Act, 1935 (25 and 26 Geo.5 c.2), provision has been made for the administration of certain areas either as if they formed part of an adjoining Governor's province or as if they were a Chief Commissioner's Province;AND WHEREAS it is expedient to provide that certain laws should be extended to, and by virtue of such extension, be in force in, the said areas;It is hereby enacted as follows:- This Act was not adapted by the Adaptation of Laws Order, 1950 .
