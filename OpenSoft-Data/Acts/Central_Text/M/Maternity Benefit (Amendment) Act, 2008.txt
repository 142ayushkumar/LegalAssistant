Maternity Benefit (Amendment) Act, 2008_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Maternity Benefit Act, 1961. Be it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-ninth year or the Republic of India as follows,-
Maternity Benefit (Amendment) Act, 2008_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Maternity Benefit (Amendment) Act, 2008. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
Maternity Benefit (Amendment) Act, 2008_Section 2--> In the Maternity Benefit Act, 1961 (53 of 1961), for Section 8, the following shall be substituted, namely,- "8. Payment of medical bonus:-(1) Every woman entitled to maternity benefit under this Act shall also be entitled to receive from her employer a medical bonus of one thousand rupees, if no prenatal confinement and post-natal care is provided for by the employer free of charge. (2) The Central Government may before every three years, by notification in the Official Gazette increase the amount of medical bonus subject to the maximum of twenty thousand rupees".
