Mussalman Wakf Validating Act, 1930_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Mussalman Wakf Validating Act, 1930.
Mussalman Wakf Validating Act, 1930_Section 2--> The Mussalman Wakf Validating Act, 1913 , shall be deemed to apply to wakfs created before its commencement: Provided that nothing herein contained shall be deemed in any way to affect any right, title! obligation or liability already acquired, accrued or incurred before the commencement of this Act.
