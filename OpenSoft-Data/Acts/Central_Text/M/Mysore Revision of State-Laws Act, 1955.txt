Mysore Revision of State-Laws Act, 1955_Section Preamble--> An Act to repeal certain enactments and to amend certain other enactments. Whereas, for the purpose of bringing into accord with the Constitution of India and Central Acts extended to the State of Mysore, and for other purposes, it is expedient to repeal certain enactments and to amend certain other enactments. Be it enacted by the Mysore State Legislature in the Sixth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Mysore Revision of State-Laws Act, 1955_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Mysore Revision of State-Laws Act, 1955.1 Received the assent of the President on the Twenty-first day of December, 1955
Mysore Revision of State-Laws Act, 1955_Section 2--> The enactments specified in the First Schedule are hereby repealed.
Mysore Revision of State-Laws Act, 1955_Section 3--> The enactments specified in the Second Schedule are hereby amended to the extent and in the manner mentioned in the said Schedule.

THE MYSORE CINEMAS (REGULATION) ACT, 1952 (XXXI of 1952) Section 10 -For Section 10, substitute the following, namely - "10. Power to exempt -(1) The Government, if in its opinion it is necessary in public interest so to do, may, by notification in the Mysore Gazette and subject to such conditions and restrictions as may be specified in the notification, exempt from any of the provisions of this Act or of any rules made thereunder any place in which cinematograph films are exhibited - (a) the proceeds of which will be utilised for any specified charitable purpose; (b) intended solely for the exhibition of films of educational or cultural value; and (c) intended for the promotion of public health or social welfare. (2) Every notification under sub-section (1) shall be laid as soon as may be after it is issued before each House of the State Legislature while it is in session for a total period of one month which may be comprised in one session or in two or more sessions and if before the expiry of the said period, either House of the State Legislature makes any modification in the notification or directs that the notification shall not have effect and if the modification or direction is agreed to by the other House, the notification shall thereafter have effect only in such modified form or be of no effect, as the case may be".
