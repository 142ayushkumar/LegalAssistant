Oil Industry (Development) Amendment Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Oil Industry (Development) Act, 1974BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirty-Second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Oil Industry (Development) Amendment Act, 1981_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Oil Industry (Development) Amendment Act, 1981. &gt;
Oil Industry (Development) Amendment Act, 1981_Section 2--> In the Schedule to the Oil Industry (Development) Act, 1974 ,( 47 of 1974 ) in Sl.No.1 relating to crude oil, for the entry in the third column, the entry "Rupees three hundred per tonne." shall be substituted.
