Hindu Disposition of Property Act, 1916_Section Preamble--> An Act to remove certain existing disabilities in respect of the power of disposition of property by Hindus for the benefit of persons not in existence at the date of such disposition. WHEREAS it is expedient or remove certain existing disabilities in respect of the power of disposition of property by Hindus for the benefit to persons not in existence at the date of such disposition. It is hereby enacted as follows-
Hindu Disposition of Property Act, 1916_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Hindu Disposition of Property Act, 19161 . 2 (2) It extends3 to the whole of India except the State of Jammu and Kashmir. 4 [Provided that nothing contained in this Act shall apply to the Renoncants of the Union territory of Pondicherry.] For Statement of Objects and Reasons, See Gazette of India, 1916, Part V. p. 2; for Report of Select Committee see Gazette of India, 1916, Part V, p. 76; and for proceedings in Council, see Gazette of India, 1916 Part VI, pp. 9, 509, 542 and 585. Substituted by Act 48 of 1959, Section 3 and Sch. I, for sub-section (2) w.e.f. 01-02-1960. This Act has been extended to Berar by the Berar Laws Act, 1941 (4 of 1941) Inserted by Act 26 of 1968, Section 3 and Schedule Part I w.e.f. 24-05-1968.
Hindu Disposition of Property Act, 1916_Section 2--> Subject to the limitations and provisions specified in this Act, no disposition of property by a Hindu, whether by transfer inter vivos or by will, shall be invalid by reason only that any person for whose benefit it may have been made was not in existence at the date of such disposition.
Hindu Disposition of Property Act, 1916_Section 3--> The limitations and provisions referred to in section 2 shall be the following, namely :- (a) in respect of dispositions by transfer inter vivos, those contained in 1 (Chapter II) of the Transfer of Property Act, 1882 (4 of 1882) and (b) in respect of dispositions by will those contained in 2 (sections 113, 114, 115 and 116 of the Indian Succession Act, 1925 (33 of 1925). Substituted by Act 21 of 1929, Section 12, for "sections 13, 14 and 20". Substituted by Act 21 of 1929, Section 12, for "sections 100 and 101 of the Indian Succession Act, 1865 (10 of 1865).
