Hindu Marriage (Amendment) Act, 1956_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Hindu Marriage Act, 1955. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Hindu Marriage (Amendment) Act, 1956_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Hindu Marriage (Amendment) Act, 1956.
Hindu Marriage (Amendment) Act, 1956_Section 2--> In clause (d) of sub-section (1) of section 10 of the Hindu Marriage Act, 1955 (25 of 1955), for the words "has, immediately before", the words "has, for a period of not less than three years immediately preceding" shall be substituted.
