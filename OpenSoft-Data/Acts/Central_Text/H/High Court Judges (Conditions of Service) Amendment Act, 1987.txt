High Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Amendment Act, 1987_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the High Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Act, 1954. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
High Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Amendment Act, 1987_Section 1--> This Act may be called the High Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Amendment Act, 1987.
High Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Amendment Act, 1987_Section 2--> In the High Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Act, 1954 , (28 of 1954) in section 22A, in sub-section (2), for the words "an amount equal to twelve and a half percent.of his salary", the words "two thousand five hundred rupees" shall be substituted.
