High Court and Supreme Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Amendment Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> An Act Further to amend to High Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Act, 1954 and the Supreme Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Act, 1958. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Forty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:--
High Court and Supreme Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Amendment Act, 1993_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the High Court and Supreme Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Amendment Act, 1993. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st day of April 1996.
High Court and Supreme Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Amendment Act, 1993_Section 2--> In the High Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Act, 1954 , in section 22D, after clause(c), the following clause shall be inserted namely;- "(d) the value of leave travel concession provided to a Judge and members of his family,"
High Court and Supreme Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Amendment Act, 1993_Section 3--> In the Supreme Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Act, 1958 , in section 23D, after clause(c), the following clause shall be inserted namely;-- (d)" the value of leave travel provided to a Judge and members of his family",.
