High Court and Supreme Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Amendment Act, 1985_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the High Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Act, 1954 and the Supreme Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Act, 1958. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirty-Sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:--
High Court and Supreme Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Amendment Act, 1985_Section 1--> This Act may be called the High Court and Supreme Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Amendment Act, 1985
High Court and Supreme Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Amendment Act, 1985_Section 2--> In the High Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Act, 1954 , in section 22B, for the words "three hundred rupees" shall be substituted.
High Court and Supreme Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Amendment Act, 1985_Section 3--> In the Supreme Court Judges (Conditions of Service) Act, 1958 , in section 23A, for the words "three hundred rupees", the words "five hundred rupees" shall be substituted.
