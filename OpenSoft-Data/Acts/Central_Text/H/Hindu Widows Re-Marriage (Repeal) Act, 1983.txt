Hindu Widows Re-Marriage (Repeal) Act, 1983_Section Preamble--> An Act to repeal the Hindu Widows's Re-marriage Act, 1856. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirty -Fourth Year of Republic of India as follows:-
Hindu Widows Re-Marriage (Repeal) Act, 1983_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Hindu Widows' Re-marriage (Repeal) Act, 1983.
Hindu Widows Re-Marriage (Repeal) Act, 1983_Section 2--> The Hindu Widows' Re-marriage Act, 1856 is hereby repealed.
