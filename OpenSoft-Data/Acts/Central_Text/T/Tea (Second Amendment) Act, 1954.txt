Tea (Second Amendment) Act, 1954_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Tea Act, 1953. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tea (Second Amendment) Act, 1954_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Tea (Second Amendment) Act, 1954.
Tea (Second Amendment) Act, 1954_Section 2--> In section 4 of the Tea Act, 1953 .After sub-section (3), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely:- "(3A) It is hereby declared that the office of member of the Board shall not disqualify its holder for being chosen as, or for being, a member of either House of Parliament.".
