Two-Member Constituencies (Abolition) and Other Laws Repeal Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> An Act to repeal the Two-Member Constituencies (Abolition) Act, 1961 and certain other enactments. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Two-Member Constituencies (Abolition) and Other Laws Repeal Act, 2001_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Two-Member Constituencies (Abolition) and other Laws Repeal Act, 2001.
Two-Member Constituencies (Abolition) and Other Laws Repeal Act, 2001_Section 2--> The enactments specified in the Schedule are hereby repealed.
