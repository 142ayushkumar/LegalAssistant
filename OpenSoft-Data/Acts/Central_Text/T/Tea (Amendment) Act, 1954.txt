Tea (Amendment) Act, 1954_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Tea Act, 1953. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tea (Amendment) Act, 1954_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Tea (Amendment) Act, 1954.
Tea (Amendment) Act, 1954_Section 2--> In sub-section (1) of section 25 of the Tea Act, 1953 , for the words "at such rate not exceeding two rupees per one hundred pounds as the central Government may notify in the Official Gazette"' the words "at the rate of four rupees per one hundred pounds" shall be substituted.
