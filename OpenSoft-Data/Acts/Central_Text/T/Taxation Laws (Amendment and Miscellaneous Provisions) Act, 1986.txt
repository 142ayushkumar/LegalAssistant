Taxation Laws (Amendment and Miscellaneous Provisions) Act, 1986_Section 39--> After section 35N of the Wealth-tax Act , the following section shall be inserted, namely:- 36-O.Presumption as to culpable mental state.- (1) In any prosecution for any offences under this Act which requires a culpable mental state on the part of the accused, the court shall presume the existence of such mental state but it shall be a defence for the accused to prove the fact that he had no such mental state with respect to the act charged as an offences in that prosecution. Explanation.- In this sub-section, " culpable mental state" includes intention, move or knowledge of a fact, or belief in, or reason to believe, a fact. (2) For the purposes of this section, a fact is said to be proved only when the court believes it to exist beyond reasonable doubt and not merely when its existence is established by a preponderance of probability.'.
