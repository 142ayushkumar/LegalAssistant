Rubber (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section Preamble--> An act further to amends the Rubber Act, 1947. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Forty-fifth year of the republic of India as follows:-
Rubber (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 1--> This act may be called the rubber (Amendment) act, 1994.
Rubber (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 2--> In section 12 of the Rubber Act, 1947 (24 of 1947) in sub-section (1) for the words "fifty naye paise" the words "two rupees" shall be substituted.
