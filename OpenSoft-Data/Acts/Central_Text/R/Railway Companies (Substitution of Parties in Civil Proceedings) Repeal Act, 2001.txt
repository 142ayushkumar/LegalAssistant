Railway Companies (Substitution of Parties in Civil Proceedings) Repeal Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> An Act to repeal the Railway Companies (Substitution of Parties in Civil Proceedings) Act, 1946. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Railway Companies (Substitution of Parties in Civil Proceedings) Repeal Act, 2001_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Railway Companies (Substitution of Parties in Civil Proceedings) Repeal Act, 2001.
Railway Companies (Substitution of Parties in Civil Proceedings) Repeal Act, 2001_Section 2--> The Railway Companies (Substitution of Parties in Civil Proceedings) Act, 1946 is hereby repealed. SUBHASH C. JAIN, Secy. to the Govt. of India.
