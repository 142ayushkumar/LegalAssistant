Representation of People (Second Amendment) Act, 1996_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Representation of the People Act, 1950. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Forty-seventh Year of the Republic in India as follows:---
Representation of People (Second Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Representation of the People (Second Amendment) Act, 1996.
Representation of People (Second Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 2--> In the Fourth Schedule to the Representation of the People Act, 1950 .- (a) under the hearing "KARANATAKA", for the entries 1 to 5, the following entries shall be substituted, namely:--- "1.City Municipal Corporation.2.City Municipal Councils.3.Town Municipal Councils.4.Town Panchayats.5.Zilla Panchayats.6.Taluk Panchayats.7.Grama Panchayats.8 Cantonment Boards"; (b) under the heading "UTTAR PRADESH".for the entries 1 to 6, the following entries shall be substituted, namely:--- "1.Municipal Corporations.2.Municipal Councils.3.Zila Panchayats.4.Nagar Panchayats.5.Kshettra Panchayats.6.Cantonment Boards.". K.L.MOHANPURIA,Secy.to the Govt.of India.
