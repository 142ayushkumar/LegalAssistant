Repatriation of Prisoners (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> AN Act to amend the Repatriation of Prisoners Act, 2003. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Repatriation of Prisoners (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Repatriation of Prisoners (Amendment) Act, 2011.
Repatriation of Prisoners (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> In the Repatriation of Prisoners Act, 2003, in section 5, in sub-section (2), in clause (c), for the words "martial law', the words "military law shall be substituted. (V.K. Bhasin), Secy. to the Govt. of India
