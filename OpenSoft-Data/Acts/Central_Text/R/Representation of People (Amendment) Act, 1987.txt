Representation of People (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Representation of the People Act, 1950. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirty-eighth Year of the republic of India as follows:-
Representation of People (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Representation of the People (Amendment) Act , 1987.
Representation of People (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 2--> In the Third Schedule to the Representation of the People Act, 1950 (43 of 1950) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for entry No.6 relating to Karnataka, the following entry shall be substituted, namely:- "6.Karnataka-75-25-7-7-25-11".
Representation of People (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 3--> In the Fourth Schedule to the principal Act, under the heading "KARNATAKA";- (a) for the entry "2.Taluk Development Boards.", the entry "2.Mandal Panchayats." shall be substituted; (b) for the entry "5.Town Panchayats.", the entry L"5.Zilla Parishads." shall be substituted.
