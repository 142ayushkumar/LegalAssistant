Rice-Milling Industry (Regulation) Repeal Act, 1997_Section Preamble--> An Act to repeal the Rice-Milling Industry (Regulation) Act, 1958. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Forty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Rice-Milling Industry (Regulation) Repeal Act, 1997_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Rice-Milling Industry (Regulation) Repeal Act, 1997.
Rice-Milling Industry (Regulation) Repeal Act, 1997_Section 2--> The Rice-Milling Industry (Regulation) Act, 1958 (21 of 1958) is hereby repealed. RAGHBIR SINGH,Additional Secy.to the Govt.of India.
