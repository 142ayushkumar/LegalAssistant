Requisitioning and Acquisition of Immovable Property (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Requisitioning and Acquisition of Immovable Property (Amendment) Act, 1958.
Requisitioning and Acquisition of Immovable Property (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 2--> In section 1 of the Requisitioning and Acquisition of Immovable Property Act, 1952 (30 of 1952), in sub-section (3), for the word "six", the word "twelve" shall be substituted
