Representation of People (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1956_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Representation of the People Act, 1950. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Representation of People (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1956_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Representation of the People (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1956. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 8th day of November, 1956.
Representation of People (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1956_Section 2--> In sub-section (1) of section 24 of the Representation of the People Act, 1950 (43 of 1956) for the words, letters and figures "the 1st day of November, 1956", the words, letters and figures "the 16th day of December, 1956", shall be substituted and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
Representation of People (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1956_Section 3--> The Representation of the people (Amendment) Ordinance 1956 (9 of 1956), is hereby repealed.
