Rubber (Production and Marketing) Amendment Act, 1952_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Rubber (Production and Marketing) Act, 1947. BE it enacted by Parliament as follows :-
Rubber (Production and Marketing) Amendment Act, 1952_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Rubber (Production and Marketing) Amendment Act, 1952.
Rubber (Production and Marketing) Amendment Act, 1952_Section 2--> For clause (c) of sub-section (3) of section 4 of the Rubber (Production and Marketing) Act, 1947 , the following clause shall be substituted, namely :- "(c) two members to be nominated by the Government of Madras one of whom shall be an official and the other a non-official;".
