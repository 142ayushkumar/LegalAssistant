Vaccination (Repeal) Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> An Act to repeal the Vaccination Act, 1880. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Vaccination (Repeal) Act, 2001_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Vaccination (Repeal) Act, 2001.
Vaccination (Repeal) Act, 2001_Section 2--> The Vaccination Act, 1880 is hereby repealed. SUBHASH C. JAIN, Secretary to the Govt. of India.
