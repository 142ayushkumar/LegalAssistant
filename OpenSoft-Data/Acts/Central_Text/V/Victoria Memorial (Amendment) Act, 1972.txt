Victoria Memorial (Amendment) Act, 1972_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Victoria Memorial Act, 1903. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twenty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Victoria Memorial (Amendment) Act, 1972_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Victoria Memorial (Amendment) Act, 1972.
Victoria Memorial (Amendment) Act, 1972_Section 2--> In the Victoria Memorial Act, 1903 Victoria Memorial Act, 1903 , in section 2, in sub-section (1), (i) for clause (a) , the following clause shall be substituted, namely:- (a) the Minister in charge of the Ministry of the Central Government concerned with matters relating to the Victoria Memorial,". (ii) for clause (d) , the following clause shall be substitute, namely,:- "(d) two persons to be nominated by the Central Government form among persons who, in the opinion of that Government, have expert knowledge of the exhibits in the Victoria Memorial or are museologists, historians or art historians."; (iii) for clause (h) , the following clause shall be substituted, namely:- "(h) such and so may person as shall form time to time be nominated by the Trustees form among persons who, in the opinion of the Trustees, have expert knowledge of the exhibits in the Victoria Memorial or are museologists, historians or art historians, with the approval of the Central Government to represent the general body of subscribers."
