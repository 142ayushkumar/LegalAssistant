Appropriation (Railways) Vote on Account Act, 2010_Section Preamble--> An Act to provide for the withdrawal of certain sums from and out of the Consolidated Fund of India for the services of a part of the financial year 2010-11 for the purposes of Railways.Be it enacted by Parliament in the Sixty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Appropriation (Railways) Vote on Account Act, 2010_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Appropriation (Railways) Vote on Account Act, 2010.
Appropriation (Railways) Vote on Account Act, 2010_Section 2--> From and out of the Consolidated Fund of India there may be withdrawn sums not exceeding those specified in column 3 of the Schedule amounting in the aggregate to the sum of thirty thousand four hundred sixty-one crores, thirty-one lakhs and six thousand rupees towards defraying the several charges which will come in course of payment during the financial year 2010-11, in respect of the services relating to Railways specified in column 2 of the Schedule.
Appropriation (Railways) Vote on Account Act, 2010_Section 3--> The sums authorised to be withdrawn from and out of the Consolidated Fund of India by this Act shall be appropriated for the services and purposes expressed in the Schedule in relation to the said year.
