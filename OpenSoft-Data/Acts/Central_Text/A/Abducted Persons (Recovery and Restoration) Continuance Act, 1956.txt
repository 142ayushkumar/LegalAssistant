Abducted Persons (Recovery and Restoration) Continuance Act, 1956_Section Preamble--> An Act continue the Abducted Persons (Recover and Restoration) Act, 1949, for a further period. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Abducted Persons (Recovery and Restoration) Continuance Act, 1956_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Abducted Persons (Recovery and Restoration) Continuance Act, 1956.
Abducted Persons (Recovery and Restoration) Continuance Act, 1956_Section 2--> In sub-section (3) of section 1 of the Abducted Persons (Recover and Restoration) Act, 1949 (65 of 1949), for the words and figures "30th day of November, 1956", the words and figures "30th day of November, 1957) shall be substituted.
