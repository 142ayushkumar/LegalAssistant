Agricultural Produce (Grading and Marking) Amendment Act, 1960_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Agricultural Produce (Grading and Marking) Act, 1937. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Eleventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Agricultural Produce (Grading and Marking) Amendment Act, 1960_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Agricultural Produce (Grading and Marking ) Amendment Act, 1960.
Agricultural Produce (Grading and Marking) Amendment Act, 1960_Section 2--> In sub-section (2) of section 1 of the Agricultural Produce (Grading and Marking) Act, 1937 (1 of 1937), the words ``except the State of Jammu and Kashmir' shall be omitted.
