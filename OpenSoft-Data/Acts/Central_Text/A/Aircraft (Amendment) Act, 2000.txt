Aircraft (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> Act Objective: An Act further to amend the Aircraft Act, 1934 BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Aircraft (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Aircraft (Amendment) Act, 2000. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
Aircraft (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 2--> In section 11A of the Aircraft Act, 1934 , for the words "with fine which may extend to one thousand rupees", the words "with fine which may extend to ten lakh rupees" shall be substituted. SUBHASH C. JAIN, Secy. to the Govt. of India.
