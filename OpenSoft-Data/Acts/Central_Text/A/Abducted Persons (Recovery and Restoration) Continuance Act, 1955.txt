Abducted Persons (Recovery and Restoration) Continuance Act, 1955_Section Preamble--> An Act to continue the Abducted Persons (Recovery and Restoration) Act, 1949, for a further period. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Abducted Persons (Recovery and Restoration) Continuance Act, 1955_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Abducted Persons (Recovery and Restoration) Continuance Act, 1955. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 30th day of May, 1955.
Abducted Persons (Recovery and Restoration) Continuance Act, 1955_Section 2--> In sub-section (3) of section 1 of the Abducted Persons (Recovery and Restoration) Act, 1949 (45 of 1949), for the words and figures "31st day of May, 1955", the words and figures "30th day of November, 1956", shall be substituted.
Abducted Persons (Recovery and Restoration) Continuance Act, 1955_Section 3--> The Abducted Persons (Recovery and Restoration) Continuance Ordinance, 1955 (2 of 1955), is hereby repealed.
