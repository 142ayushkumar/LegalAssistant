Governors (Emoluments, Allowances and Privileges) Amendment Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Governors( Emoluments, Allowances and Privileges) Act, 1982. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Forty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows;--
Governors (Emoluments, Allowances and Privileges) Amendment Act, 1993_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Governors(Emoluments, Allowances and Privileges) Amendment Act, 1993. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st day of June , 1986.
