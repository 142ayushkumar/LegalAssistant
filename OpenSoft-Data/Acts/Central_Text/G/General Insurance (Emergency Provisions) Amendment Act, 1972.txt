General Insurance (Emergency Provisions) Amendment Act, 1972_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the General Insurance Emergency Provisions Act, 1971.) BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twenty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows: -
General Insurance (Emergency Provisions) Amendment Act, 1972_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the General Insurance (Emergency Provisions) Amendment Act, 1972. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 13th day of May, 1971.
General Insurance (Emergency Provisions) Amendment Act, 1972_Section 2--> Sections 2 and 3 repealed by Repealing and Amending Act, 1978
General Insurance (Emergency Provisions) Amendment Act, 1972_Section 3--> Sections 2 and 3 repealed by Repealing and Amending Act, 1978
General Insurance (Emergency Provisions) Amendment Act, 1972_Section 4--> The provisions of the General Insurance Emergency Provisions Act, 1971 , (17 of 1971) as amended by this Act, shall have effect notwithstanding any judgment, decree or order of any Court or Tribunal.
