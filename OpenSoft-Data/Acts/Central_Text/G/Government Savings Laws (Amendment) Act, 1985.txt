Government Savings Laws (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Government Savings Banks Act, 1873 and the Government Savings Certificates Act, 1959. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Government Savings Laws (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Government Savings Laws (Amendment) Act, 1985.
Government Savings Laws (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 2--> In the Government Savings Banks Act, 1873 ,- (a) in sub-section (4) of section 4A,- (i) in clause (a), for the words, "five thousand rupees", the words "such limit as may be prescribed" shall be substituted; (ii) in clause (b), for the words "aforesaid limit of five thousand rupees", the words, brackets and letter "limit prescribed under clause (a)" shall be substituted; (b) in sub-section (2) of section 15, after clause (h) , the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(i) the limit under clause (a) of sub-section (4) of section 4A.".
Government Savings Laws (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 3--> In the Government Savings Banks Act, 1873 ,- (a) in sub-section (4) of section 7, for the words "five thousand rupees", the words "such limit as may be prescribed" shall be substituted; (b) in sub-section (2) of section 12, after clause (I), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(ia) the limit under sub-section (4) of section 7;".
