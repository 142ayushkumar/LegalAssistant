Finance Act, 1998_Section Preamble--> An Act to continue the existing rates of income-tax for the financial year 1998-99. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Forty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Finance Act, 1998_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Finance Act, 1998. (2) It shall come into force on the 1st day of April, 1998.
