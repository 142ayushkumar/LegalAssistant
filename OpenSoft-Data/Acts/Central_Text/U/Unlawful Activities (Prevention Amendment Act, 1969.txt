Unlawful Activities (Prevention Amendment Act, 1969_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Unlawful Activities (Prevention) Act,1967. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twentieth Year of the Republic India as follows :---
Unlawful Activities (Prevention Amendment Act, 1969_Section 1--> This Act may be called the unlawful Activities (Prevention) Amendment Act, 1969.
Unlawful Activities (Prevention Amendment Act, 1969_Section 2--> In section 1 of the Unlawful Activities (Prevention) Act,1967 (37 of 1967) (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act), for sub-section (2), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely :--- "(2) It extends to the whole of India : Provided that it shall come into force in the State of Jammu and Kashmir on such date as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint".
Unlawful Activities (Prevention Amendment Act, 1969_Section 3--> In the principal Act, in Chapter I, after section 2, of the following section shall be inserted, namely :-- "2A.Construction of reference to laws not in force in Jammu and Kashmir.- Any reference in this Act to law which is not in force in the State of Jammu and Kashmir shall, in relation to that State, be constructed as a reference to the corresponding law, if any, in force in that State".
