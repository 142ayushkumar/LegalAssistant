Untouchability (Offences) Amendment and Miscellaneous Provision Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Untouchability (Offences) Act, 1955and further to amend the Representation of the People Act, 1951 .BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:- -
Untouchability (Offences) Amendment and Miscellaneous Provision Act, 1976_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Untouchability (Offences) Amendment and Miscellaneous Provision Act, 1976. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.

Untouchability (Offences) Amendment and Miscellaneous Provision Act, 1976_Section 19--> References in any Act, rule, notification or order to the Untouchability (Offences) Act, 1955 (22 of 1955), shall, on the commencement of this Act, be construed as references to the Protection of Civil Rights Act, 1955 .
Untouchability (Offences) Amendment and Miscellaneous Provision Act, 1976_Section 20--> The alteration of the short title of the Untouchability (Offences) Act, 1955 (22 of 1955), shall not- (a)affect the previous operation of the Untouchability (Offences) Act , or anything duly done or suffered thereunder previous to such alteration; or(b) after any right, privilege, obligation or liability acquired, accrued or incurred under the Untouchability (Offences) Act, 1955 , previous to the alteration of its short title; or (c) affect any penalty or punishment incurred in respect of any offence committed against the Untouchability (Offences) Act, 1955 , before the commencement of this Act; or (d) affect any investigation, legal proceeding or remedy in respect of any such right, privilege, obligation, liability, penalty or punishment as aforesaid. and any such investigation, legal proceeding or remedy may be instituted, continued or enforced and any such penalty or punishment may be imposed as if this Act had not been passed.
