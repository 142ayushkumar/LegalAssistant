Union Duties of Excise (Distribution) Amendment Act, 1984_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Union Duties of Excise (Distribution) Act, 1979. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Union Duties of Excise (Distribution) Amendment Act, 1984_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Union Duties of Excise (Distribution) Amendment Act, 1984. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st day of April, 1984.
Union Duties of Excise (Distribution) Amendment Act, 1984_Section 2--> In the Union Duties of Excise (Distribution) Act, 1979 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in the long title, for the words, figures and letter "report dated the 28th day of October, 1978", the words, figures and letters "interim report dated the 14th day of November, 1983" shall be substituted.
Union Duties of Excise (Distribution) Amendment Act, 1984_Section 3--> For section 3 of the principal Act, the following section shall be substituted, namely:- "3.
