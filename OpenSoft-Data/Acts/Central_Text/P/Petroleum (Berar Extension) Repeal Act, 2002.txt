Petroleum (Berar Extension) Repeal Act, 2002_Section Preamble--> A BILL to repeal the Petroleum (Berar Extension) Act, 1937. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Petroleum (Berar Extension) Repeal Act, 2002_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Petroleum (Berar Extension) Repeal Act, 2002.
Petroleum (Berar Extension) Repeal Act, 2002_Section 2--> Repeal of Act The Petroleum (Berar Extension) Act, 1937 , is hereby repealed.
