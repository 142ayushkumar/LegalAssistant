Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) Amendment Act, 1967_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) Act, 1959 BE it enacted by Parliament in the Eighteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) Amendment Act, 1967_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) Amendment Act, 1967.
Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) Amendment Act, 1967_Section 2--> In the public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) Act, 1959 (29 of 1959) , in section 3, for the words, figures and letters "the 15th day of August, 1967", the words, figures and letters "the 31st day of December, 1968" shall be substituted.
