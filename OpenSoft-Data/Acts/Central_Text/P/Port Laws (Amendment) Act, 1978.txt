Port Laws (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend to Indian Ports Act, 1908 and the Major Port Trusts Act, 1963. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twenty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows;-
Port Laws (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Port Laws (Amendment) Act, 1978.
Port Laws (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 2--> In the Indian Ports Act, 1908 , in sub-section (1) of section 5, the following explanation shall be inserted at the end namely:- Explanation ;- For the removal of doubts, it is hereby declared that the power conferred on the Government by this sub-section includes the power to alter the limits of any port by uniting with that port any other part of any other port".
Port Laws (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 3--> In section 133 of the Major Port Trusts Act, 1963 , in sub-section (3) after the words "that corresponding law shall, on such application, cease to have force in relation to that port" the words and figures " and section 6 of the General Clause Act, 1897 (10 of 1897), shall apply as if such corresponding law were a Central Act and such cesser of operation were a repeal " shall be inserted.
