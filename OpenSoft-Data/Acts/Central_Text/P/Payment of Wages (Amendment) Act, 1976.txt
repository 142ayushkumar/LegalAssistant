Payment of Wages (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Payment of Wages Act, 1936. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Payment of Wages (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Payment of Wages (Amendment) Act, 1976. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 12th day of November, 1975.
Payment of Wages (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> In the Payment of Wages Act, 1936 (4 of 1936) (hereinafter referred to as the principle Act), in sub-section (6) of sub-section 1, for the words "four hundred rupees", the words "one thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Payment of Wages (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> In section 6 of the principle Act, the following proviso shall be inserted at the end, namely:-- "Provided that the employer may, after obtaining the written authorisation of the employed person, pay him the wages either by cheque or by crediting the wages in his bank account.".
Payment of Wages (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 4--> In sub-section (2) of section 7 of the principle Act, after clause (o), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(p) deductions, made with the written authorisation of the employed person, for contribution to the Prime Minister's National Relief Fund or to such other Fund as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, specify.".
Payment of Wages (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 5--> (1) The Payment of Wages (Amendment) Ordinance, 1975 (21 of 1975), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principle Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principle Act as amended by this Act.
