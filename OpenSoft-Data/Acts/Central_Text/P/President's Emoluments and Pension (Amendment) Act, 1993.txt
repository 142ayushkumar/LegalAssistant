President's Emoluments and Pension (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the President's Emoluments and Pension Act, 1951.BE is enacted by Parliament in the Forty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
President's Emoluments and Pension (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 1--> This Act may be called the President's Emoluments and Pension (Amendment) Act, 1993.
President's Emoluments and Pension (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 2--> In section 2 of the President's Emoluments and Pension Act, 1951 , in sub-section (2), after clause (c), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(d) to travel anywhere in India, accompanied by one person, by the highest class by air, rail or steamer".
