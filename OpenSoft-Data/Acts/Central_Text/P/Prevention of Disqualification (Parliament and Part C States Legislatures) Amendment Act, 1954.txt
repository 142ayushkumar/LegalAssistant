Prevention of Disqualification (Parliament and Part C States Legislatures) Amendment Act, 1954_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Prevention of Disqualification (Parliament and Part C States Legislatures) Act, 1953. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifth Year of our Republic as follows:--
Prevention of Disqualification (Parliament and Part C States Legislatures) Amendment Act, 1954_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Prevention of Disqualification (Parliament and Part C States Legislatures ) Amendment Act , 1954.
Prevention of Disqualification (Parliament and Part C States Legislatures) Amendment Act, 1954_Section 2--> In Section 4 of the Prevention of Disqualification (Parliament and Part C States Legislatures) Act, 1953 (1 of 1954), for the words "30th day of April", the words "31st day of December" shall be substituted and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
