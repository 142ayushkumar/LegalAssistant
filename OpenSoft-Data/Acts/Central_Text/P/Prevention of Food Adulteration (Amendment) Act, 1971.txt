Prevention of Food Adulteration (Amendment) Act, 1971_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Prevention of Food Adulteration Act, 1954. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twenty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows: --
Prevention of Food Adulteration (Amendment) Act, 1971_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Prevention of Food Adulteration (Amendment) Act, 1971. (2) It shall come into force on such date 1 as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint 26-1-1972: vide General Statutory Rules (G.S.R.) 70, dated 18-12-1971, Gazette of India , Part II, section 3 (1), p.269.
Prevention of Food Adulteration (Amendment) Act, 1971_Section 2--> Sections 2 and 3 were repealed by the Repealing and Amending Act, 1978
Prevention of Food Adulteration (Amendment) Act, 1971_Section 3--> Sections 2 and 3 were repealed by the Repealing and Amending Act, 1978
Prevention of Food Adulteration (Amendment) Act, 1971_Section 4--> In section 25 of the principal Act, in its application to the State of Jammu and Kashmir, after sub-section (2), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely: ----- "(3) (a) References to the commencement of this Act in this section shall be construed as references to the commencement of the Prevention of Food Adulteration (Amendment) Act, 1971 (41 of 1971). (b) For the avoidance of doubt, it is hereby declared that the provisions of sub-section (2) shall be without prejudice to the provisions contained in section 6 of the General Clauses Act, 1897 (10 of 1897) which shall also apply to the repeal of the corresponding law in force in the State of Jammu and Kashmir as if such corresponding law had been an enactment.".
