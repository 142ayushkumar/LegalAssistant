Public Employment (Requirement As to Residence) Amendment Act, 1969_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Public Employment (Requirement as to Residence) Act, 1957. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twentieth Year of the Republic of India as follows:--
Public Employment (Requirement As to Residence) Amendment Act, 1969_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Public Employment (Requirement as to residence) Amendment Act, 1969.
Public Employment (Requirement As to Residence) Amendment Act, 1969_Section 2--> In section 3 of the Public Employment (Requirement as to residence) Act, 1957 (44 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), -- (i)in sub-section (1), in clause (c) , for the words "local authority", the words "local or other authority" shall be substituted; (ii)in sub-section (2), clause (a) shall be re-lettered as clause (aa) and before clause (aa) as so re-lettered, the following clause shall be inserted, namely:-- (a) "Himachal Pradesh" includes the territories specified in sub-section (1) of section 5 of the Punjab Reorganisation Act, 1966 (11 of 1966);.
Public Employment (Requirement As to Residence) Amendment Act, 1969_Section 3--> In section 5 of the principal Act, for the words "ten years, the words "fifteen years" shall be substituted.
