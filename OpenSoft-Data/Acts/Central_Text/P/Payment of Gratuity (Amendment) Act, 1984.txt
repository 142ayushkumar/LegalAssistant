Payment of Gratuity (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Payment of Gratuity (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Payment of Gratuity ( Amendment ) Act, 1984. (2) It shall come into force on such date 1 as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint. 1st July 1984, vide Notification No.S.O.2144, dated 14-6-1984, Gazette of India, 1984, Part II, Section 3(ii)
