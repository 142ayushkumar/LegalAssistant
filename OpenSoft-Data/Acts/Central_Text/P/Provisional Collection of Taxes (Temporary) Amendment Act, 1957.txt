Provisional Collection of Taxes (Temporary) Amendment Act, 1957_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Provisional Collection of Taxes Act, 1931 for a temporary period. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows : -
Provisional Collection of Taxes (Temporary) Amendment Act, 1957_Section 1--> This act may be called the Provisional Collection of Taxes (Temporary Amendment) Act, 1957.
Provisional Collection of Taxes (Temporary) Amendment Act, 1957_Section 2--> Where a Bill introduced in Parliament during the period commencing on the 15th day of May, 1957, and ending with the 31st day of December, 1957, contains a declared provision as defined in section 2 of the Provisional Collection of Taxes Act, 1931 , sections 4 and 5 of that Act shall have effect in relation to such Bill as if for the words "sixtieth day" in clause (c) of sub-section (2) of section 4 and in sub-section (1) of section 5, the words "one hundred and twentieth day" had been substituted.
