Prevention of Black-Marketing and Maintenance of Supplies of Essential Commodities (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Prevention of Black-marketing and Maintenance of Supplies of Essential Commodities, act, 1980. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Prevention of Black-Marketing and Maintenance of Supplies of Essential Commodities (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 1--> This act may be called the Prevention of Black-marketing and Maintenance of Supplies of Essential Commodities (Amendment) act, 1982.
Prevention of Black-Marketing and Maintenance of Supplies of Essential Commodities (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 2--> In section 7 of the Prevention of Black-marketing and Maintenance of Supplies of Essential Commodities Act, 1980 , in sub-section (1),- (a)for the words "appropriate Government", the words, brackets and figures "the appropriate Government or an officer mentioned in sub-section (2) of section 3, as the case may be," shall be substituted; (b)after the words "that Government", the words "or officer" shall be inserted.
