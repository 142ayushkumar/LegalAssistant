Payment of Gratuity (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Sixty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Payment of Gratuity (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Payment of Gratuity (Amendment) Act, 2010. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
Payment of Gratuity (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 2--> In section 4 of the Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972, in sub-section (3), for the words "three lakhs and fifty thousand rupees", the words "ten lakh rupees" shall be substituted. V.K. BHASIN, Secy. to the Govt. of India.
