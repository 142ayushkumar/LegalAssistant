Preventive Detention (Continuance) Act, 1957_Section Preamble--> An Act to continue the Preventive Detention Act, 1950, for a further period. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Preventive Detention (Continuance) Act, 1957_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Preventive Detention (Continuance) Act, 1957.
Preventive Detention (Continuance) Act, 1957_Section 2--> In sub-section (3) of section 1 of the Preventive Detention Act, 1950 (4 of 1950), for the figures, letters, and words "31st day of December, 1957", the figures, letters and words "31st day of December, 1960" shall be substituted.
