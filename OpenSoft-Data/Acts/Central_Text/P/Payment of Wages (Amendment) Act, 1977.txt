Payment of Wages (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Payment of Wages Act, 1936. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twenty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Payment of Wages (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Payment of Wages (Amendment) Act, 1977.
Payment of Wages (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 2--> In section 7 of the Payment of Wages Act, 1936 (4 of 1936.), in sub-section (2), after clause (p), the following clause shall be inserted, namely :-- "(q) deductions for contributions to any insurance scheme framed by the Central Government for the benefit of its employees.".
