Prevention of Insults to National Honour (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the prevention of Insults to National Honour Act, 1971. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-sixth Y ear of the Republic of India as follows:-
Prevention of Insults to National Honour (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Prevention of Insults to National Honour (Amendment) Act, 2005.
Prevention of Insults to National Honour (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 2--> In the prevention of Insults to National Honour Act, 1971 , in section 2, in Explanation 4, for clause (e), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:- "(e) using the Indian National Flag,- (i) as a portion of costume, uniform or accessory of any description which is worn below the waist of any person; or (ii) by embroidering or printing it on cushions, handkerchiefs, napkins, undergarments or any dress material; or". T. K. VISWANATHAN, Secy. to the Govt. of India.
