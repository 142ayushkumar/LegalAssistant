Payment of Gratuity (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section Preamble--> An act further to amend the payment of Gratuity Act, 1972. BE it enacted by parliament in the fifty-fifth year of the Republic India as follows:-
Payment of Gratuity (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 1--> This act may be called the payment of Gratuity (amendment) act, 1994.
Payment of Gratuity (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 2--> In section 2 of the Payment of gratuity Act 1972 (hereinafter referred to as the principle act), in clause (e), (a) the words ", not exceeding two husband and five hundred rupees per mensem, or such higher amount as the central government may, having regard to the general level of wages, by notification specify," shall be omitted; (b) Explanation shall be omitted.
Payment of Gratuity (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 3--> In section 4 of the principle act,- (a) in sub-section (3) for the words "fifty thousand" the words "one lakhs" shall be substituted; (b) Sub-section (7) shall be omitted.
Payment of Gratuity (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 4--> In section 11 of the principle act, in sub-section (2) for the words "Presidency Magistrate or a Magistrate of the first class" the words "Metropolitan Magistrate or a Judicial Magistrate of he first class" shall be substituted.
