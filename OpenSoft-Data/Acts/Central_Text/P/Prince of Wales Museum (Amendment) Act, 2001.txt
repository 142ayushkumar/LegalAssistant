Prince of Wales Museum (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> An Act farther to amend the Prince of Wales Museum Act, 1909. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Prince of Wales Museum Act, 1909, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Prince of Wales Museum (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Prince of Wales Museum (Amendment) Act, 2001.
Prince of Wales Museum (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 2--> In the long title of the Prince of Wales Museum Act, 1909 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), for the words "the Prince of Wales Museum of Western India" the words "the Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya" shall be substituted.
Prince of Wales Museum (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 3--> In section 1 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), for the words "the Prince of Wales Museum" the words "the Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya" shall be substituted.
Prince of Wales Museum (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 4--> In section 2 of the principal Act, for the words "The Prince of Wales Museum of Western India" the words "The Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya" shall be substituted.
Prince of Wales Museum (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 5--> In section 3 of the principal Act, - (a) in sub-section (1), for the words "the Prince of Wales Museum of Western India", at both the places where they occur, the words "the Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya" shall be substituted; (b) in sub-section (2), for clause (c-1), the following clause shall be substituted, namely :- "(c-1) the Director, Archaeology and Museum, Maharashtra State;".
Prince of Wales Museum (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 6--> In section 10 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), after the words "The Trustees of the Prince of Wales Museum of Western India" the words "or, as the case may be, "The Trustees of the Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya" " shall be inserted.
Prince of Wales Museum (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 7--> After section 17 of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely :- "18. Construction of reference in any enactment or instrument, etc.-On the commencement of the Prince of Wales Museum (Amendment) Act, 2001 , any reference to "the Prince of Wales Museum of Western India" in any other enactment, or rules, regulations, by-laws, notifications or orders issued under any enactment or in any instrument, document or proceedings under its original name shall, unless the context otherwise requires, be deemed and construed to be the reference to "the Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya (Museum).".
