Payment of Gratuity (Amendment) Act, 2018_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972. Be it enacted by Parliament in the Sixty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Payment of Gratuity (Amendment) Act, 2018_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Payment of Gratuity (Amendment) Act, 2018. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
Payment of Gratuity (Amendment) Act, 2018_Section 2--> In the Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in section 2, for clause (k), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:- '(k) "notification" means a notification published in the Official Gazette and the expression "notified" shall be construed accordingly;'.
Payment of Gratuity (Amendment) Act, 2018_Section 3--> In section 2A of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), in the Explanation, in clause (iv), for the words "twelve weeks", the words "such period as may be notified by the Central Government from time to time" shall be substituted.
Payment of Gratuity (Amendment) Act, 2018_Section 4--> In section 4 of the principal Act, in sub-section (3), for the words "ten lakh rupees", the words "such amount as may be notified by the Central Government from time to time" shall be substituted. (Dr. G. Narayana Raju) Secretary to the Govt. of India.
