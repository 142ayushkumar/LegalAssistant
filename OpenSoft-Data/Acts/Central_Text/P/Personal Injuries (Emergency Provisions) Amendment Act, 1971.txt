Personal Injuries (Emergency Provisions) Amendment Act, 1971_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Personal Injuries (Emergency Provisions) Act, 1962. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twenty- second Year of the Republic of Indian as follows:-
Personal Injuries (Emergency Provisions) Amendment Act, 1971_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Personal Injuries (Emergency Provisions) Amendment Act, 1971.
Personal Injuries (Emergency Provisions) Amendment Act, 1971_Section 2--> Repealed by Repealing and Amending Act, 1978 .
Personal Injuries (Emergency Provisions) Amendment Act, 1971_Section 3--> Repealed by Repealing and Amending Act, 1978 .
Personal Injuries (Emergency Provisions) Amendment Act, 1971_Section 4--> For the avoidance of doubts, it is hereby declared that every Scheme made under the Personal Injuries (Emergency Provisions) Act, 1962 (59 of 1962), providing for relief in respect of personal injuries or personal service injuries sustained during the period of emergency beginning with the 26th day of October, 1962, and ending with the 10th day of January, 1968, shall continue to be in force and every person entitled to relief under the said Scheme shall continue to receive such relief in accordance with the provisions of such Scheme.(59 of 1962)
