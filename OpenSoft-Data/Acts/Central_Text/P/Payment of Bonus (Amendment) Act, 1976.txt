Payment of Bonus (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Payment of Bonus Act, 1965.BE it enacted by Parliament in the twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:--
Payment of Bonus (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Payment of Bonus (Amendment) Act, 1976 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 25th day of September, 1975.
