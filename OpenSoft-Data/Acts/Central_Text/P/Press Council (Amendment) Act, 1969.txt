Press Council (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Press Council Act, 1965. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twentieth Year of the Republic of India as follows :--
Press Council (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Press Council (Amendment) Act, 1969. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 30th day of June, 1969.
