Personal Injuries (Compensation Insurance) Amendment Act, 1971_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Personal Injuries (Compensation Insurance) Act, 1963. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twenty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows: ---
Personal Injuries (Compensation Insurance) Amendment Act, 1971_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Personal Injuries ( Compensation Insurance ) Amendment Act, 1971.
Personal Injuries (Compensation Insurance) Amendment Act, 1971_Section 2--> Repealed by Repealing and Amending Act, 1978
Personal Injuries (Compensation Insurance) Amendment Act, 1971_Section 3--> Repealed by Repealing and Amending Act, 1978
Personal Injuries (Compensation Insurance) Amendment Act, 1971_Section 4--> Repealed by Repealing and Amending Act, 1978
Personal Injuries (Compensation Insurance) Amendment Act, 1971_Section 5--> For the avoidance of doubts, it is hereby declared that every Scheme made under the Personal Injuries (Compensation Insurance) Act, 1963 (37 of 1963)., providing for compensation in respect of personal injuries sustained during the period of emergency beginning with the 26th day October, 1962, and ending with the 10th day of January, 1968, shall continue to be in force and every person entitled to compensation under the said Scheme shall continue to receive such compensation in accordance with the provisions of such Scheme.
