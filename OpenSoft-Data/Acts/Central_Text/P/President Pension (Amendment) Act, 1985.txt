President Pension (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the President's Pension Act, 1951. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
President Pension (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 1--> (1) This Act may called the President's Pension (Amendment) Act, 1985. (2) It shall come into force on such date I1 as the Central Government, may by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint. 26-12-1985: vide Notofication No. General Statutory Rules (G.S.R.) 938 (E), dated 26-12-1985, Gazette of India Extraordinary, Part II, section 3(I).
President Pension (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 2--> In the lone title to the President's Pension Act, 1951 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for the words "for the payment of", the words "for the emoluments of the President and for" shall be substituted.
President Pension (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 3--> In section 1 of the principal Act, for the word "Pension", the words "Emoluments and Pension" shall be substituted.
President Pension (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 4--> In the principal Act, after section 1, the following section shall be inserted, namely:-
President Pension (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 1A--> There shall be paid to the President by way of emoluments fifteen thousand rupees per mensem".
President Pension (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 5--> In section 2 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1) for the words "fifteen thousand rupees", the words "thirty thousand rupees" shall be subtituted.
