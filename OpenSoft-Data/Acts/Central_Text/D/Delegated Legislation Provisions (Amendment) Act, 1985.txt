Delegated Legislation Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend certain Acts to implement the recommendations of the Committees on Subordinate Legislation regarding Publication and laying of rules and other delegated legislation and certain other matters. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Delegated Legislation Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Delegated Legislation Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1985. (2) It shall come into force on such date 1 as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint, and different dates may be appointed for amendments relating to different enactments mentioned in the Schedule to this Act. 15th May, 1986 vide Notification No.G.S.R.764 (E), dated 15-5-1986, Gazette of India, 1986, Extraordinary, Part II, Section 3(I).
Delegated Legislation Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 2--> The enactments specified in the Schedule are hereby amended to the extent and in the manner mentioned in the third column thereof.
