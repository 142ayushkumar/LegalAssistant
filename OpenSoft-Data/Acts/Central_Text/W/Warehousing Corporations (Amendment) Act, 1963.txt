Warehousing Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Warehousing Corporations Act, 1962. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fourteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:--
Warehousing Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Warehousing Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1963.
Warehousing Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 2--> In sub-section (2) of section 3 of the Warehousing Corporations Act, 1962 , (58 of 1962) after the words "at New Delhi", the words "or at such other place as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, specify" shall be inserted.
