Warehousing Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Warehousing Corporations Act, 1962. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fortieth Year of the Republic of India as follows:--
Warehousing Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Warehousing Corporation (Amendment) Act, 1989.
Warehousing Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 2--> In Section 1 of the Warehousing Corporations Act, 1962 (58 of 1962) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (2), the words "except the State of Jammu and Kashmir" shall be omitted.
Warehousing Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 3--> In Section 2 of the principal Act, in clause (dd), the following shall be added at the end, namely:-- "or a corresponding new bank specified in the First Schedule to the Banking Companies (Acquisition and Transfer of Undertakings ) Act, 1980 (40 of 1980)."
Warehousing Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 4--> In Chapter I of the principal Act, after section 2, the following section shall be inserted, namely:-- "2A. Construction of references to any law not in force or any functionary not in existence in any State.- Any reference in this Act to any law which is not in force, or any functionary not in existence, in any State, shall, in relation to that State, be construed as a reference to the corresponding law in force, or corresponding functionary in existence, in that State."
