War Risks (Factories) Insurance (Repealing) Act, 1945_Section Preamble--> An Act to repeal the War Risks (Factories) Insurance Act, 1943. Whereas, it is expedient to repeal the War Risks (Factories) Insurance Act, 1943 (VI of 1943). It is hereby enacted as follows -
War Risks (Factories) Insurance (Repealing) Act, 1945_Section 1--> This Act may be called the War Risks (Factories) Insurance (Repealing) Act, 1945.1 Received the assent of His Highness the Maharaja on the 22nd day of July, 1945
War Risks (Factories) Insurance (Repealing) Act, 1945_Section 2--> The War Risks (Factories) Insurance Act, 1943 (VI of 1943), is repealed with effect from the 1st day April, 1945.
War Risks (Factories) Insurance (Repealing) Act, 1945_Section 3--> The War Risks (Factories) Insurance (Repealing) (Emergency) Act, 1945 (XI of 1945), is hereby repealed.
