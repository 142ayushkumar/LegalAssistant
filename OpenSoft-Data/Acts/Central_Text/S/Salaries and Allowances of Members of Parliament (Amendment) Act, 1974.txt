Salaries and Allowances of Members of Parliament (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Salaries and Allowances of Members of Parliament Act, 1954.BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twenty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Salaries and Allowances of Members of Parliament (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Salaries and Allowances of Members of Parliament (Amendment) Act, 1974.
Salaries and Allowances of Members of Parliament (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 2--> In section 4 of the Salaries and Allowances of Members of Parliament Act, 1954 ,(30 of 1954) in sub-clause (ii) of clause (c) of sub-section (1), for the words "thirty-two paise per kilometer", the words "one rupee per kilometer" shall be substituted.
