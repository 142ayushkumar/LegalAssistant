Sikh Gurdwaras (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Sikh Gurdwaras Act, 1925. Be it enacted by Parliament in the Sixty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follow:-
Sikh Gurdwaras (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Sikh Gurdwaras (Amendment) Act, 2016. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force from 8th October, 2003.
Sikh Gurdwaras (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 2--> In the Sikh Gurdwaras Act, 1925 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in section 49, for the proviso, the following proviso shall be substituted, namely:- "Provided that no person shall be registered as an elector who- (a) trims or shaves his beard or keshas; (b) smokes; and (c) takes alcoholic drinks.".
Sikh Gurdwaras (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 3--> In section 92 of the principal Act, for the proviso, the following proviso shall be substituted, namely:- "Provided that no person shall be registered as an elector who- (a) trims or shaves his beard or keshas; (b) smokes; and (c) takes alcoholic drinks.". Dr. G. Narayana Raju, Secretary to the Government of India.
