Sugarcane Cess (Validation) Repeal Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> An Act to repeal the Sugarcane Cess (Validation) Act, 1961. BE it enacted by the Parliament in the Fifty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Sugarcane Cess (Validation) Repeal Act, 2001_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Sugarcane Cess (Validation) Repeal Act, 2001.
Sugarcane Cess (Validation) Repeal Act, 2001_Section 2--> The Sugarcane Cess (Validation) Act, 1961 is hereby repealed. SUBHASH C. JAIN, Secy. to the Govt. of India.
