Sick Industrial Companies (Special Provisions) Amendment Act, 1991_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Sick Industrial Companies (Special Provisions) Act, 1985. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Forty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Sick Industrial Companies (Special Provisions) Amendment Act, 1991_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Sick Industrial Companies (Special Provisions) Amendment Act, 1991.
Sick Industrial Companies (Special Provisions) Amendment Act, 1991_Section 2--> In section 3 of the Sick Industrial Companies (Special Provisions) Act, 1985 , in sub-section (1), in clause (d), the words ", but does not include a Government company as defined in section 617 of that Act" shall be omitted.
