Supreme Court of India (Establishment of a Permanent Bench at Thiruvananthapuram) Bill, 2016_Section Preamble--> A Bill to provide for the establishment of a permanent Bench of the Supreme Court of India at Thiruvananthapuram. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Sixty-Seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Supreme Court of India (Establishment of a Permanent Bench at Thiruvananthapuram) Bill, 2016_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Supreme Court of India (Establishment of a Permanent Bench at Thiruvananthapuram) Act, 2016. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
Supreme Court of India (Establishment of a Permanent Bench at Thiruvananthapuram) Bill, 2016_Section 2--> There shall be established a permanent Bench of the Supreme Court of India at Thiruvananthapuram and such Judges of the Supreme Court, being not less than five in number, as the Chief Justice of India may from time to time nominate, shall sit at Thiruvananthapuram in order to exercise the jurisdiction and power for the time being vested in the Supreme Court in respect of cases arising in the southern, south-eastern and south- western States of the country.
