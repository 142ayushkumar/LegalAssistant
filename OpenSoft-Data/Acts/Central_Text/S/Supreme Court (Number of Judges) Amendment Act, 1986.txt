Supreme Court (Number of Judges) Amendment Act, 1986_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Supreme Court (Number of Judges) Act, 1956 BE it enacted by parliament in the Thirty seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows;-
Supreme Court (Number of Judges) Amendment Act, 1986_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Supreme Court (Number of Judges) Act, 1956
Supreme Court (Number of Judges) Amendment Act, 1986_Section 2--> In section 2 of the Supreme Court (Number of Judges) Act, 1956 (55 of 1956), for the word "seventeen", the word "twenty-five" shall be substituted.
