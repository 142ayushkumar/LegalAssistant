Shipping Development Fund Committee (Abolition) Amendment Act, 1987_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Shipping Development Fund Committee (Abolition) Act, 1986. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Shipping Development Fund Committee (Abolition) Amendment Act, 1987_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Shipping Development Fund Committee (Abolition) Amendment Act, 1987.
Shipping Development Fund Committee (Abolition) Amendment Act, 1987_Section 2--> In section 16 of the Shipping Development Fund Committee (Abolition) Act, 1986 , in sub-section (1), for the words "this Chapter", the words "this Act" shall be substituted.
