Supreme Court (Number of Judges) Amendment Act, 1977_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Supreme Court (Number of Judges) Act, 1956. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twenty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows :--
Supreme Court (Number of Judges) Amendment Act, 1977_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Supreme Court (Number of Judges) Amendment Act, 1977.
Supreme Court (Number of Judges) Amendment Act, 1977_Section 2--> In section 2 of the Supreme Court (Number of Judges) Act, 1956 (55 of 1956.), for the words "thirteen", the words "seventeen" shall be substituted.
