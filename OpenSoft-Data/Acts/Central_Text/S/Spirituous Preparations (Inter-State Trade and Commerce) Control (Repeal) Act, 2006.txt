Spirituous Preparations (Inter-State Trade and Commerce) Control (Repeal) Act, 2006_Section Preamble--> An Act to repeal the Spirituous Preparations (Inter- State Trade and Commerce) Control Act, 1955. Be it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Spirituous Preparations (Inter-State Trade and Commerce) Control (Repeal) Act, 2006_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Spirituous Preparations (Inter-State Trade and Commerce) Control (Repeal) Act, 2006.
Spirituous Preparations (Inter-State Trade and Commerce) Control (Repeal) Act, 2006_Section 2--> The Spirituous Preparations (Inter- State Trade and Commerce) Control Act, 1955 is hereby repealed. K. N. CHATURVEDI, Secy. to the Govt. of India.
