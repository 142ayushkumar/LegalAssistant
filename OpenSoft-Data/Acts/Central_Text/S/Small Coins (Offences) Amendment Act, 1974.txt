Small Coins (Offences) Amendment Act, 1974_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Small Coins (Offences) Act, 1971. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twenty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Small Coins (Offences) Amendment Act, 1974_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Small Coins (Offences) Amendment Act, 1974.
Small Coins (Offences) Amendment Act, 1974_Section 2--> In section 1 of the Small Coins (Offences) Act, 1971 , (52 of 1971) sub-section (2) shall be omitted.
