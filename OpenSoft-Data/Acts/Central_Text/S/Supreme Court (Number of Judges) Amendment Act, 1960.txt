Supreme Court (Number of Judges) Amendment Act, 1960_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Supreme Court (Number of Judges) Act, 1956. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Eleventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Supreme Court (Number of Judges) Amendment Act, 1960_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Supreme Court (Number of Judge) Amendment Act, 1960.
Supreme Court (Number of Judges) Amendment Act, 1960_Section 2--> In section 2 of the Supreme Court (Number of Judges) Act, 1956 (55 of 1956), for the words ``ten', the word ``thirteen' shall be substituted.
