State Bank of India (Subsidiary Banks) Amendment Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> AN Act further to amend the State Bank of India (Subsidiary Banks) Act, 1959. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
State Bank of India (Subsidiary Banks) Amendment Act, 2011_Section 1--> (i) This Act may be called the State Bank of India (Subsidiary Banks) Amendment Act, 2011. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
State Bank of India (Subsidiary Banks) Amendment Act, 2011_Section 2--> In section 2 of the State Bank of India (Subsidiary Banks) Act, 1959 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act),- (i) in clause (b), sub-clause (ii) shall be omitted; (ii) in clause (c), sub-clause (ii) shall be omitted; (iii) in clause (d), sub-clause (ii) shall be omitted.
State Bank of India (Subsidiary Banks) Amendment Act, 2011_Section 3--> In section 3 of the principal Act, clause (b) shall be omitted. (V.K. Bhasin), Secy. to the Govt. of India
