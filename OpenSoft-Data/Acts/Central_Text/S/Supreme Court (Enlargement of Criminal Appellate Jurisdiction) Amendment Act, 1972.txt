Supreme Court (Enlargement of Criminal Appellate Jurisdiction) Amendment Act, 1972_Section Preamble--> An Act to amend the Supreme Court (Enlargement of Criminal Appellate Jurisdiction) Act, 1970. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twenty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Supreme Court (Enlargement of Criminal Appellate Jurisdiction) Amendment Act, 1972_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Supreme Court (Enlargement of Criminal Appellate Jurisdiction) Amendment Act, 1972.
Supreme Court (Enlargement of Criminal Appellate Jurisdiction) Amendment Act, 1972_Section 2--> In section 1 of the Supreme Court (Enlargement of Criminal Appellate Jurisdiction) Act, 1970 , in sub-section (2), the words "except the State of Jammu and Kashmir" shall be omitted.
