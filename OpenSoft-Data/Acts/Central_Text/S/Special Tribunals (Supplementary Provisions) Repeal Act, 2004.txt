Special Tribunals (Supplementary Provisions) Repeal Act, 2004_Section Preamble--> An Act to repeal the Special Tribunals (Supplementary Provisions) Act, 1946. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Special Tribunals (Supplementary Provisions) Repeal Act, 2004_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Special Tribunals (Supplementary Provisions)Repeal Act, 2004.
Special Tribunals (Supplementary Provisions) Repeal Act, 2004_Section 2--> The Special Tribunals (Supplementary Provisions) Act, 1946 is hereby repealed. T. K. VISWANATHAN, Secy. to the Govt, of India.
