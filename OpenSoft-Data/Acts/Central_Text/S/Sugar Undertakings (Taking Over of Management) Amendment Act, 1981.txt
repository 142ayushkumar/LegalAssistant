Sugar Undertakings (Taking Over of Management) Amendment Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Sugar Undertakings (Taking Over of Management) Act, 1978.BE it enacted by Parliament in the Thirty-Second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Sugar Undertakings (Taking Over of Management) Amendment Act, 1981_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Sugar Undertakings (Taking Over of Management) Amendment Act, 1981.
Sugar Undertakings (Taking Over of Management) Amendment Act, 1981_Section 2--> In section 3 of the Sugar Undertakings (Taking Over of Management) Act, 1978 , in sub-section (5),- (a) the words "the period so specified is less than three years from the date of vesting and" shall be omitted; (b) for the words "three years" , occurring in the proviso, the words "six years shall be substituted.
