Supreme Court (Number of Judges) Amendment Act, 2008_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Supreme Court (Number of Judges) Act, 1956. Be it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Supreme Court (Number of Judges) Amendment Act, 2008_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Supreme Court (Number of Judges) Amendment Act, 2008.
Supreme Court (Number of Judges) Amendment Act, 2008_Section 2--> In section 2 of the Supreme Court (Number of Judges) Act, 1956 , for the word "twenty-five", the word "thirty" shall be substituted. T. K. VISWANATHAN, Secy. to the Govt. of India.
