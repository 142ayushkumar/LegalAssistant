Salaries and Allowances of Officers of Parliament (Amendment) Act, 1971_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Salaries and Allowances of Officers of Parliament Act, 1953. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Twenty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:---
Salaries and Allowances of Officers of Parliament (Amendment) Act, 1971_Section 1--> This Act may be called the Salaries and Allowances of Officers of Parliament (Amendment) Act, 1971.
Salaries and Allowances of Officers of Parliament (Amendment) Act, 1971_Section 2--> For section 3 of the Salaries and Allowances of Officers of Parliament Act, 1953 (10 of 1953) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), the following section shall be substituted, namely:---- "3.Salaries of officers of Parliament.- There shall be paid to each officer of Parliament a salary of two thousand, two hundred and fifty rupees per mensem".
Salaries and Allowances of Officers of Parliament (Amendment) Act, 1971_Section 3--> In section 5 of the principal Act, the words "and to the Deputy Chairman and the Deputy Speaker a sumptuary allowance of two hundred and fifty rupees per mensem" shall be added at the end.
