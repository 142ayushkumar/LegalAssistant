Sugar Cess (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section Preamble--> An Act further to amend the Sugar Cess Act, 1982. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Sixty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Sugar Cess (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 1--> (1) This Act may be called the Sugar Cess (Amendment) Act, 2015. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the Central Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
Sugar Cess (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 2--> In the Sugar Cess Act, 1982, in section 3, in sub-section (1), for the words "twenty-five rupees", the words "two hundred rupees" shall be substituted. Dr. G. Narayana Raju, Secretary to the Government of India.
