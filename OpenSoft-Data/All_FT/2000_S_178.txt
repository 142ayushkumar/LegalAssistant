Jagat Ram v Shimla Development Authority and Anr
Supreme Court of India

11 December 2000
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.3/2000
The Order of the court was as follows:
1.  In pursuance of the orders passed by this Court the parties desired that they will file compromise. The Compromise deed has been filed in this Court. The said compromise is signed by all the parties except Town and Country Planning Department. As per the said compromise the suit has to be dismissed and the decree passed by the courts below has to be set aside.
2. In view of the compromise, without entering into the merits and the contentions raised by all the parties, we are disposing of this Special Leave Petition in terms of the said compromise. Costs on the parties.
Petition disposed of.
