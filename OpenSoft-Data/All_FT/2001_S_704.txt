Akhtari and Others v State of Haryana and Others
Supreme Court of India

10 December 2001
The Order of the court was as follows:
1.  I.A. No. 5 in W.P.(C) 269/2001 Counter affidavit be filed within two weeks.
List along with connected matters.
I.A. Nos. 3 and 4 in SLP(C) 14686/2001 Exemption is allowed.
2.  It is brought to our notice that fresh elections to the post of the Sarpanch were conducted and by Notification No. SEC/E-III/2001/11838 dated 13th August, 2001 the persons who have been elected as Sarpanch have been notified.
3.  In view of this fact the order of this Court dated 28th August, 2001 has to be understood to permit the persons who had been elected as Sarpanchs and were functioning on the date of that order, to continue as such.
The applications are, accordingly, disposed of.
I.A. No. ...... in SLP(C) 10725/2001.
4. Learned counsel for the applicant is directed to comply with the office objections, if any, and get the I.A. numbered. Let a copy of the application be served on the opposite counsel.
Applications disposed of.
