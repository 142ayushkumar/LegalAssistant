Maghar Singh and Others v Waryam Singh and Others
Supreme Court of India

25 July 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.796/2001 in S.L.P.(Civil)No.6996/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  A careful perusal of the order dated 20.4.2001 sought to be reviewed and the review petition, in our view, does not disclose any valid ground justifying review of the said order.
The Review Petition is, therefore, dismissed.
Review dismissed.
