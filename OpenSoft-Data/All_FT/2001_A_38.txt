A.P.Handicrafts Develp. Corporation Limited v R. Satyanarayana and Others
Supreme Court of India

31 January 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.44/2001 in S.L.P.(C)No.10456/2000
The order of the Court was as follows:
Delay is condoned.
1.  We have carefully gone through the review petition, annexures thereto and also the order sought to be reviewed.
In our opinion, no ground is made out to review our order dated 12.7.2000. The Review Petition is, therefore, dismissed.
Review dismissed.
