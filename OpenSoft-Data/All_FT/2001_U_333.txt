Sudhir Kumar and Anr v Union of India
Supreme Court of India

12  May  2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.7744/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  In view of the facts and circumstances of the case, a sum of Rs.2 lacs towards compensation to the appellants would be reasonable and fair.
2. We, therefore, modify the order under challenge and direct the respondent to pay a sum of Rs.2 lacs to the appellants towards compensation. The order under appeal shall stand modified to this extent. The appeal is allowed. There shall be no order as to costs.
Appeal allowed.
