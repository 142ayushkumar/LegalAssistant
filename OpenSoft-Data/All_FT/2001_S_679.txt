Vejakaran Modhvadia v State of Gujarat
Supreme Court of India

11  May  2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 998/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  Appellant has been convicted for the offence under Section 302 of the Indian Penal Code and sentenced to imprisonment for life. He filed the appeal way back in 1996.
Even today his appeal has not been boarded for hearing.
2. In the light of decision of this Court in Smt. CCC Akhtari Bi vs. State of M.P. reported as 2001 Indlaw SC 20684, we suspend the sentence and order that the appellant be released on bail on his executing a bond for such an amount as to the satisfaction of the trial judge with two solvent sureties.
Appeal is disposed of accordingly.
Appeal disposed of.
