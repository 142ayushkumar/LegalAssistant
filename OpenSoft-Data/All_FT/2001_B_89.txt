Balkishan and Anr v Satya Prakash and Others
Supreme Court of India

27 March 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.328/2001 in C.A.No.4031/1995
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We have carefully gone through the judgment sought to be reviewed, the review petition and Annexure 'A'. In our view no justifiable ground is made out to warrant review of the said judgment.
The review petition is therefore dismissed.
Review Dismissed.
