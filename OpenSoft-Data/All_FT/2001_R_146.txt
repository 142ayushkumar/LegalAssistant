Rakesh Singh v State of Uttar Pradesh and Anr
Supreme Court of India

26 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 6900/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Today at the first sitting of the Court, Mr.Yogeshwar Prasad, learned Senior Counsel informed that the order recorded on 26th November, 2001 has not recorded the liberty to apply for expeditious disposal of the bail application in the pending appeal in the High Court.
2. This Bench has specific recollection to that effect and by reason therefor the words "bail application in the" be added after the words, "expeditious disposal of the" and thereafter rest of the order follows.
3. Let it be incorporated in the order dated 26-11-2001.
Order accordingly.
