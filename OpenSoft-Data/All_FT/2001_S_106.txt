Sandeep Sudeep v State of Haryana
Supreme Court of India

24 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 1821/2001From the judgment and order Dt. 22.2.01 in Cr.Misc.3645-M of 2001of the High Court of Punjab and Haryana at Chandigarh)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Heard learned counsel for the parties. The application for restoration is allowed. The SLP is restored to file.
Special leave granted.
2.  The appellant Sandeep Sudeep shall be released on bail on executing a bond of Rs.10, 000/- with two sureties each of the like amount to the satisfaction of the trial court.
The appeal is disposed of.
Appeal disposed of.
