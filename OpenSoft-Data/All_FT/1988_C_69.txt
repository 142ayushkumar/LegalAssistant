Dhirajben R. Amin and Another v Commissioner of Income Tax and Another
Supreme Court of India

22 August 1988
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In view of the decision of this court in Sunil Siddharthbhai v. CIT, the first question referred to the High Court, as mentioned in the order of the High Court under appeal, is answered in favour of the assessee and in the affirmative.
2.   The appeal is accordingly allowed. There will be no order as to costs.
Appeal allowed.
