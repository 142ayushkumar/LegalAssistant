Bharat Overseas Bank Limited v V.Raman and Anr
Supreme Court of India

30 April 2001
I.A. No.2 In C.A..Nos.457 & 458 of 1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In both these appeals, the appellants and the contesting respondent have entered into a settlement. Copy of the settlement deed together with affidavits of the parties accepting the terms of the settlement deed have been filed. We take the settlement deed on record.
2. In modification of the impugned orders, the appeals are disposed of in terms of the settlement as arrived at.
The terms of the settlement shall form a part of the order. No costs.
Appeals disposed of.
