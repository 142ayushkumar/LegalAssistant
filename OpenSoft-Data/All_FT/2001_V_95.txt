Vikram International, Gujarat v Commissioner of Customs, Kandla, Gujarat
Supreme Court of India

21 August 2001
C.A. No. 5019 of 1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  It appears that the matter in issue stands covered by Basant Industries vs. Addl. Collector of Customs, Bombay as also by a later decision of this Court in Eicher Tractors Ltd., Haryana vs. Commissioner of Customs, Mumbai.
2. In that view of the matter, these appeals succeed and the order of the Tribunal is set aside.
Appeals allowed.
