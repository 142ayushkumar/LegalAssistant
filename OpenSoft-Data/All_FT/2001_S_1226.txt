Ambika Prasad Tiwari v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others
Supreme Court of India

22 November 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.1329/2001 in S.L.P.(C) No. 14116 of 1995
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There is an inordinate delay of six years in filing the review petition. The explanation offered in the application seeking condonation of delay is neither reasonable nor satisfactory.
The application seeking condonation of delay is dismissed.
Even otherwise, we find no merit in the review petition.
2. Accordingly, the review petition is dismissed both on the ground of delay and on merits.
Review dismissed.
