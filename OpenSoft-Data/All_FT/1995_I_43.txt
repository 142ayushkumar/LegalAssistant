State of Orissa and Others v Ion Exchange India Limited
Supreme Court of India

21 July 1995
S.L.P. (Civil) Nos. 17211 to 17214 of 1995 (Petitions for special leave to appeal under article 136 of the Constitution of India against the judgment and order Dt. 4 January 1995, of the Orissa High Court in Original Jurisdiction Cases Nos. 1441 to 1444 of 1991), Dt. 21 July 1995.
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Delay condoned.
1.  Learned counsel for the petitioner states that sub-section (7) of section 24 of the Orissa Sales Tax Act, 1947 clearly says that
"the payment of the amount, if any, of tax due in accordance with the order of the Tribunal in respect of which an application has been made under sub-section (1) shall not be stayed pending the disposal of such application or any reference made in consequence thereof, but if such amount is reduced as the result of such reference, the excess tax paid shall be refunded in accordance with the provisions of section 14"
2. We further request the High Court to dispose of the references which are now pending in the High Court as expeditiously as possible, preferably within four months from today. With the above observations the special leave petition is dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
