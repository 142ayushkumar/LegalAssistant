National Textile Corporation v Tukaram Vinayak Chavan and Others
Supreme Court of India

22 March 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.285/2001 in C.A.No.854/1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Perused the review petition, the grounds for review urged as also the grounds for condonation of a delay of 333 days in filing the review petition and the materials available on record. We are of the view that no ground, satisfactory or sufficient, has been made out for condonation of such an inordinate delay. The grounds sought to be urged for review also lack merits.
2.  The application for condonation of delay and the review petition as such, shall stand dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
