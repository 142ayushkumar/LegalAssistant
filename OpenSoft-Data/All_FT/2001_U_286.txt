Union of India v K.C. Kaushal and Others
Supreme Court of India

25 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.21480/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
Heard the learned counsel for the parties.
1.  At the time of the hearing of this appeal, learned ASG appearing for the appellant has produced on record a Notification No.G.S.R. 328 (E) dated 30.4.2001 modifying the previous notification No.G.S.R. 569 (E) dated 30.9.1997. As per this notification the pay scale of Investigator Grade II in the Labour Bureau is revised at Rs.5000-150-8000/-.
2. It is also mentioned that above said revision in the scale of pay shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st day of January, 1996.
3.  In view of the aforesaid notification, the controversy in this appeal would not survive. If respondents are aggrieved by the said notification, it would be open to them to challenge the same in the appropriate forum.
The appeal stand disposed of accordingly.
Appellant to pay Rs.5000/- as costs to the respondents.
Interim order dated 14.8.2001 passed by this Court stands vacated.
Appeal disposed of.
