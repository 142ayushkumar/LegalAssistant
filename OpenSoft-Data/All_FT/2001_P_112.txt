Pheru Singh Sharma v State of Uttar Pradesh and Anr
Supreme Court of India

30 July 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.20662-20663/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  In view of the decision of this Court in Punjab National Bank. Vs. Kunj Bihar Mishra, these appeals are allowed.
2. The impugned orders are set aside and liberty is given to the respondents, if they so choose, to give an opportunity as contemplated in Kunju Bihari Mishra's case and as per law.
The appeals are allowed in aforesaid terms.
Appeals allowed.
