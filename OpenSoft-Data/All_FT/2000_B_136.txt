Bison Field A Estate v Inspecting Assistant Commissioner of Others
Supreme Court of India

14 November 2000
C.As. Nos. 1678 and 1679 of 1998
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  We find no merit in the arguments on behalf of the assessee. The matter is squarely covered by the decision that was followed by the High Court in the order under appeal, namely, the decision in Commr. of Agrl. I. T. v. Raja Rajeswari Narikelly Estate 1992 Indlaw KER 88 (Ker). We agree with that decision and dismiss these appeals with costs.
Appeals dismissed.
