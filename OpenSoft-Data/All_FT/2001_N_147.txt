New India Assurance Company Limited v Shiv Ram and Others
Supreme Court of India

6 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.20426/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Vide office report dated 28th July, 2001, counsel has not filed Vakalatnama/appearance on behalf of respondent No.4 who has become major.
2.  We are informed by the learned counsel for the respondents, on the last occasion, on account of some misunderstanding wrong statement was made that respondent No.4 has become major which has led to the passing of the order dated 10th May, 2001. He seeks to withdraw the said statement. It is allowed.
3. Accordingly, the respondent No.4 is still minor and service is already effected on counsel appearing for the minor, as per office report dated 4.5.2001. Thus service is complete.
4.  We are also informed, out of the same accident, SLP(C)No. 11176/2001 has been filed by the respondents for enhancement of the compensation. Let this matter be listed alongwith the aforesaid SLP. List it in the month of September, 2001.
Order accordingly.
