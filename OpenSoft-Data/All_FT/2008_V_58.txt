Venugopalan v State Of Kerala & Anr
Supreme Court of India

17 December 2008
CRIMINAL APPEAL No.2058 OF 2008 [Arising out of SLP(Crl.)No.6770 of 2008]
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Leave granted.
1.  Having heard learned counsel for the respective parties, and the submissions made on behalf of the appellant, that the fine amount of Rs.25,000/- has already been deposited, we while confirming the conviction, set aside the sentence regarding imprisonment.
The judgment and order of the High Court is modified accordingly.
2.  The appeal is disposed of with the aforesaid modification of the order impugned.
Appeal Disposed of
