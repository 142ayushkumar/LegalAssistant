Agricultural Produce Marketing Committee v Vijaykumar and Anr
Supreme Court of India

31 July 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.9090-9091/2001
The Order of the court was as follows:
Heard learned counel for the petitioner.
1.  Learned counsel for the petitioner has produced on record the order dated 7th June, 2001 passed by the High Court in Writ Petition Nos.11715 and; 17169 of 2001 by which the order passed by the Labour Court is set aside.
2. Considering the conflicting orders passed by the High Court, in our view, it would be open to the petitioner to approach the High Court for modification of the impugned order. With this observation this special leave petitions are dismissed.
Petitions dismissed.
