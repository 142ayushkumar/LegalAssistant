Commissioner of Income Tax, Gjarat v Elecon Engineering Company Limited
Supreme Court of India

29 August 1986
C.A. No. 663 and 664 of 1975, 104-108 of 1977
The Judgment was delivered by: R. S. PATHAK, J.
1.  It is conceded by learned counsel for the appellant that the question raised in these appeals is concluded by the decision of this court in Scientific Engineering House (P.) Ltd. v. CIT 1985 Indlaw SC 373.
2.   Accordingly, the appeals are dismissed but there is no order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed.
