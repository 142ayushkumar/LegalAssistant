Geeta Seshamani v Union of India and Anr
Supreme Court of India

19 November 2001
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  As per the office report, States of Andhra Pradesh,  Haryana, Jharkhand, Kerala, Meghalaya, Nagaland, Tamil Nadu, Uttar Pradesh and Uttaranchal have not filed the affidavits. Counsel on behalf of the State of Kerala and the Union Territory of Daman and Diu state that they will file the affidavits during the course of the date. They may do so.
2. As far as the other defaulter-States are concerned, as per this Court's order dated 24th September, 2001, each one of them will deposit in Court costs of Rs.10,000/- and they are given four weeks time to file affidavits as directed.
3.  Failing to do so could lead to other consequences. List after four weeks.
Order accordingly.
