State of Rajasthan v Akhil Bharat Krishi Goseva Sangh and Others
Supreme Court of India

10 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.8220/2000
The order of the court was as follows:
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  On the last occasion time was sought by the respondents to file counter affidavit. Today he submits he sought time earlier to bring to the notice of the State for bringing about the amendment in the Statute.
2.  However, since State Government has now stated that it is already considering the amendment in the relevant Act and the Rules, he does not want to file any counter.
3. In view of the aforesaid statement that the State Government is already considering for the amendment, he does not want to further press his case. The Special Leave Petition is accordingly disposed of in terms of the aforesaid statement.
Petition disposed of.
