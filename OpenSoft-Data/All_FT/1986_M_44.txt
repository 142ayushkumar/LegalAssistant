Kamal Kishore Laxman v Management of Pan American World Airways
Supreme Court of India

19 December 1986
Review Petition No. 559 of 1986.
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The Review Petition is allowed and the Judgment and Order made in the appeal are set aside. The appeal shall be placed on Board for hearing and final disposed on January 13, 1987.
2.   But if the respondent pay to the appellant ex gratia, a sum of Rs. 1 lakh on or before January 12, 1987 the appeal will stand dismissed with no order as to costs.
3.  If this amount is not paid by the respondent, the appeal shall be heard on merits.
Review allowed .
