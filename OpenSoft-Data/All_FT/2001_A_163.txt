Amresh Ghai v State (N.C.T. of Delhi)
Supreme Court of India

13 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 957/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Prayer for intervention is refused. We have heard learned counsel for the parties. It is stated at the Bar that during the pendency of this case the charge-sheet has been filed and the petitioner has filed an application for regular bail which is pending before the trial court.
2. Taking note of the subsequent developments we direct that the interim bail granted by our order dated 23.3.2001, will continue till the petitioners' application for bail is disposed of by the trial court and in case the prayer for bail is refused, for a period of two weeks from the date of the order.
3.  The petition for bail which is pending before the trial court will be disposed of on merits in accordance with law. These special leave petitions are disposed of on the terms aforesaid.
Petitions disposed of.
