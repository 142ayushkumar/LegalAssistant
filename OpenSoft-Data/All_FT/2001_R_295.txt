Ram Pal Singh v Regional Transport Auth.Ghaziabad
Supreme Court of India

10 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.6243/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Heard Shri Pradeep Misra learned counsel for the applicant Sadhu Ram on the application for impleadment.
2.  The learned counsel for the petitioner has no objection to the impleadment of the applicant as a respondent in the SLP. The prayer for impleadment of the applicant is allowed.
3. Learned counsel for the applicant prays for and is granted three week's time for filing counter affidavit to the SLP. Rejoinder, if any, may be filed within one week thereafter.
The case be listed after six weeks on a non-miscellaneous day.
Prayer for interim order is refused.
Order accordingly.
