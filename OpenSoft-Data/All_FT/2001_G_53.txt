General Insurance Corporation of India v Nirmala Popatrao Kale and Others
Supreme Court of India

25 January 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.884-885/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
SLP (C) 884-885/2001.
1.  The grievance of the petitioner seems to be for the following observations made in the impugned order.
"The liabilities due to this payment inter se between Respondents 1, 2 and 3 may be ascertained and settled by Respondents 1, 2 and 3."
2.  This is merely recorded for ascertaining settlement inter se between them. We do not find any ground for filing these SLPs. The Special Leave Petitions are dismissed.
SLP(C) 1160/2001.
The Special Leave Petition is dismissed.
Petitions dismissed.
