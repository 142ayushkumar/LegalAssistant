State of W B and Others v Umananda Roy and Others
Supreme Court of India

30 April 2001
I.A.Nos.3 & 5 IN C.A.No.510/1997 - for substitution of LRs. & O/R
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We find absolutely no sufficient cause to condone the inordinate delay of 7 years and 107 days and 6 years respectively in both the matters.
Interlocutory Applications are, therefore, dismissed.
2. Consequently the Civil Appeal No.335/1997 abates against the 2nd respondent and Civil Appeal No.510/1997 abates against respondent Nos.28, 41, 42, 45, 57, 72, 79 and 87.
order accordingly.
