Ompal Sharma and Another v State of Himachal Pradesh and Others
Supreme Court of India

12 July 1994
C.A. No. 4125 of 1985
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We have today pronounced judgment in titled State of H.P. v. Amar Nath Sharma1994 Indlaw SC 1826.
2.  For the reasons recorded in the said judgment, we allow this appeal and set aside the High Court judgment and dismiss the writ petition filed by Respondent 3 before the High Court.No costs
