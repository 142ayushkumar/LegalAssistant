Devinder Singh v State of Punjab
Supreme Court of India

13 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 2287/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  We have heard learned counsel for the parties and examined the counter-affidavit as well as the rejoinder-affidavit filed to this petition.
2.  We direct that in the event of his arrest, the appellant shall be released on bail on his furnishing bail bond in the sum of Rs.10, 000/- with one surety in the like amount to the satisfaction of the officer executing the warrant. The appellant shall, however, join with the investigation as and when required and shall not directly or indirectly tamper with the evidence.
The appeal stands disposed of.
Appeal disposed of.
