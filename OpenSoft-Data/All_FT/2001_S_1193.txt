State of Uttar Pradesh and Anr v Shanti Devi
Supreme Court of India

12 April 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.14952/1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted. Heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  We do not find any merit in this appeal it is accordingly dismissed.
2. However, we do direct the Appellate Authority to regularise the concerned respondent-employee atleast from the date of the order of the High Court.
3.  With the said observations the present appeal is disposed of. We make it clear that we are passing this order on the peculiar facts and circumstances of this case and not deciding the question of law which may be said to arise in this case.
Appeal dismissed.
