Amar Nath v State of Punjab
Supreme Court of India

7 January 2002
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 6729/2001
The order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Ms. Shipra Ghose, Adv. has not turned up. We pass over the matter. Even on the second call neither she turned up nor she make any representation. We remove her as A.C. in this case. We requested Mr. A. Sharan, learned senior counsel for assisting us. He graciously agreed.
2. We appoint Mr. A. Sharan, Sr. Adv. as amicus curiae in this case in place of Ms. Shipra Ghose, Adv. Registry will supply records to the learned senior counsel at the earliest.
List after two weeks.
Order accordingly.
