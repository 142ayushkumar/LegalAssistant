Messrs Fairlon Engineering Private Limited v Commr. of Customs, Mumbai
Supreme Court of India

9 August 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.860/2001 in C.A.No.2192/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  These review petitions are against common order of this Court dated March 16, 2001 declining to condone the delay of 402 and 411 days respectively in filing the appeals. Having gone through the order under review and the review petitions, we do not find any merit in the review petitions.
They are accordingly dismissed.
Petitions dismissed.
