Sudharshan v State of Maharashtra
Supreme Court of India

14 March 2001
Review Petition(Cr.) No. 200/2001 in S.L.P.(Cr.)No. 397/2000
the Order of the Court was follows:
1.  This petition is barred by 309 days for which there is no sufficient explanation for condonation of the delay. Even on merits, having carefully gone through the review petition and the papers therewith, we do not find that a case for review has been made out.
2.  The Review Petition is dismissed accordingly on the ground of delay as well as on merits.
Review dismissed.
