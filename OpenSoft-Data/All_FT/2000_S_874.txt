Union of India v Surjan Singh and Others
Supreme Court of India

27 November 2000
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.7787/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  This batch of cases is governed by the judgment of this Court in Union of India v. Mangat (Dead) by LRs and Ors. 
2000 (7) JT 356.
2. We dispose of these appeals in terms of the aforesaid judgment fixing the compensation at Rs.59,200/- per acre.
Parties to bear their own costs.
Appeals disposed of.
