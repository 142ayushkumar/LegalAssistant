Shiv Dayal Gupta v State of Rajasthan and Anr
Supreme Court of India

19 January 2001
The Order of the court was as follows:
1.  Mr. N.C. Kochhar, learned senior counsel for the petitioner submits that the petitioner has made a representation on 4th December, 2000 before the Registrar General of the Rajasthan High Court and he would be requesting the High Court for early disposal of that representation.
2. He also submits that the petitioner if still has any grievance, he may take recourse to filing a petition under Article 226 of the Income-tax, Act, 1961 and seeks to withdraw this writ petition. We record the statement of learned senior counsel and dismiss the writ petition as withdrawn.
Petition dismissed.
