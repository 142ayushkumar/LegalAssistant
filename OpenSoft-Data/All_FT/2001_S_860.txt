Suresh Kumar v State of Kerala
Supreme Court of India

14 February 2001
Review Petition(Cr.) No. 81/2001 in S.L.P.(Cr.)No. 2782/1997
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There has been an inordinate delay of 1164 days in filing the Review Petition for which there is no sufficient reason.
The application for condonation of delay is therefore dismissed.
2.  Even otherwise, we do not find any merit in the Review Petition and the same is dismissed on the ground of delay as well as on merits.
Review dismissed.
