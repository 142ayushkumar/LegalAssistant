Satish Kumar v State of Haryana
Supreme Court of India

27 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 3595-3596/2001
The order of the Court was as follows:
We have heard the learned counsel for the parties.
1.  We are of the considered opinion that this is a case in which we should request the High Court to dispose of the application filed by the petitioner under Section 439 of CrPC for granting bail at an early date.
Taking into consideration the facts and circumstances of the case, we request the High Court to dispose of the Criminal Miscellaneous No. 35056-M/2001 within a week from the date of the receipt of this order. Dasti order is permitted.
The Special Leave Petitions are disposed of in the aforesaid terms.
Petition disposed of.
