Commnr. and Secretary, Government Of Haryana and v S.K.Bagga
Supreme Court of India

27 November 2000
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.3668/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The question is whether the respondent was entitled to the salary of Principal while he was posted and was acting as Principal. Otherwise he was holding the post of Vice-Principal. The High Court has allowed his claim. The State of Haryana has relied upon Rule 3.10 of Punjab Civil Services Rules. Counter affidavit has been filed by the respondent. He has relied upon sub-rule 4.23. In the rejoinder filed by the petitioner-State there is no reply to the plea based on the sub-rule 4.23.
2. The petitioner is therefore directed to file a supplementary affidavit dealing with sub-rule 4.23 within four weeks. List after four weeks.
Order accordingly.
