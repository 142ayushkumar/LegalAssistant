C. Thisaiveerapandian v Assistant Collector of Central Excise
Supreme Court of India

8 July 2003
Cr.A. No. 783 of 2003
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Judgment also it appears that the appeal was heard in the absence of the learned counsel for the appellants. Hence, impugned order requires to be set aside.
2.  In this view of the matter, the appeal is allowed, impugned order passed by the High Court in Crl. Appeal No.552/1993 is set aside and the matter is remitted to the High Court for deciding it afresh in accordance with law.
Appeal allowed.
