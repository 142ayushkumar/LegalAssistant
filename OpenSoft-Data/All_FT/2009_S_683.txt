Asgar v State Of Uttarakhand
Supreme Court of India

31 March 2009
CRIMINAL APPEAL No.620 OF 2009(Arising out of SLP(Crl.)Nos.2375 of 2007)
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. Leave granted.
2. Considering the facts and circumstances of the present case, particularly the age of the appellant-accused, we reduce the sentence of the appellant-accused to three months only.
If the appellant has not served out the sentence, he shall be taken into custody to serve out the sentence.
3. The appeal is disposed of accordingly.
Appeal disposed of
