Commnr., Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan and Others v Rukum Pal Singh and Anr
Supreme Court of India

4 December 2000
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.11242/2000
The Order of the court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  It would be appropriate in the circumstances of the case to dispose of this appeal by stating that the interim order granted by this Court will enure to the benefits of the parties during the pendency of the matter before the Tribunal.
2. The Tribunal shall dispose of the matter as expeditiously as possible. The appeal is disposed of accordingly.
Appeal disposed of.
