Air India Ltd. v Jet Airways (India) Ltd. & Ors.
Supreme Court of India

14 July 2009
CIVIL APPEAL NO.6111 OF 2000 WITHC.A.NO.6112 OF 2000C.A.NOs.6131-6132/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows :
C.A.Nos.6111/2000 & 6112/2000
1.  Learned counsel appearing on behalf of the appellant-Air India submits that as the contract has already expired in June, 2009, these appeals have become in fructuous. The appeals are accordingly dismissed as having become in fructuous.
Interim order, if any, stands vacated.
C.A.Nos.6131-6132/2000
2.  In view of the order passed in C.A.Nos.6111/2000 &6112/2000, these appeals have also become in fructuous and the same are accordingly dismissed as having become in fructuous.
Interim order, if any, stands vacated.
Appeal dismissed
