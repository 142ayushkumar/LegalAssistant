T.N. Upadhyaya v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others
Supreme Court of India

13 October 2003
Petition(s) For Special Leave To Appeal (Civil) No.16011/2003
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  Heard the learned counsel for both sides and also the learned counsel for the State.
2.  The interim order passed by the Division Bench of the High Court is challenged before us. In the interim order it is stated as follows:
"We further clarify that the impugned order dated 25.7.2003 shall be kept in abeyance even if it has been given effect."
3.  The matter relates to transfer of two officials from one place to another. We are told the appellant has not been assigned any work against any post at present.
4. Having regard to the facts and circumstances of the case, we request the Division Bench of the High Court to dispose of the matter within a period of one month.We are told that the appellant is not being paid salary as he is not working against any post. Respondent No.1 State is directed to pay salary to the appellant.
The appeal is disposed of.
Appeal disposed of.
