Kantaru Neelakantaru Thanthri and Others v Travancore Devaswom Board and Anr
Supreme Court of India

29 October 2001
I.A.Nos. 1-5 in & S.L.P.(C)...CC 7564-7565/2001 ( For permission to file S.L.P. & with prayer for interim relief )
The order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In SLP(C)No. 17910/2001 Learned counsel states that petitioners want to file a review petition before the High Court in O.P. No. 28670/2000. Therefore, they seek leave of this Court to withdraw this petition. The Special Leave Petition is dismissed as withdrawn.
2.  In I.A.Nos. 1-5 in SLP(C)No....CC 7564-7565/2001 Permission to file SLPs is granted. In view of the fact that the tenure of Melshanti is to commence from Ist of November, we are not inclined to interfere with this matter.
3. The Special Leave Petitions are dismissed leaving the question open to be raised before the appropriate authority in regard to framing of guide line for selecting Melshanti for next year.
Petitions dismissed.
