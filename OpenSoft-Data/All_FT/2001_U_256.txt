Research Foundation For Science (8-B) and others v Union of India and another
Supreme Court of India

14 September 2001
W.P. (C) No. 657 of 1995 with S.L.P. (C) No. 16175 of 1997 and C.A. No. 7660 of 1997
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned Solicitor General states that out of the waste which has been imported some of the consignment relates to waste oil. A large part of the waste oil is contaminated and the Union of India proposes to move an application asking for appropriate direction for disposal of the said contaminated waste oil by applying the principle of polluter pays.
Application be Filed within two weeks. List thereafter.
2.  Pursuant to the order of 27-7-2001, report of CSIR is stated to have been submitted to Professor Menon which has also been placed on record. Copy of the same be given to Mr Sanjay Parikh.
To come up for further orders after four weeks.
Order accordingly.
