State of Kerala v Kakkodam Mammu Haji
Supreme Court of India

17 April 1970
C.As. Nos. 1474-1476 and 1655-1657 of 1967.
The Judgment was delivered by: SHAH, J.
1. The question which falls to be determined in these appeals is
"whether a person who sells standing timber of spontaneous growth in a forest owned by him can be deemed to be a dealer within the meaning of the Kerala General Sales Tax Act, 1972 "
2.  The appeals are dismissed with costs. There will be one hearing fee.
Appeal dismissed.
