Nafed Processed Foods Employees Union v National Agri.Co-Op.Mkt.Fed., (I)Ltd. and Anr
Supreme Court of India

4 October 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.2544/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard the learned counsel for the parties.
Leave granted.
Question which requires consideration in the matter is:
1. Whether National Agricultural Co-operative Marketing Federation of India Limited (NAFED) is the 'State' within the meaning of Article 12 of the Constitution of India.
2.  Considering the various decisions, which were cited at bar, in our view, the matter requires to be dealt with by a larger Bench. Hence, Registrar is directed to place the matter before Hon'ble the Chief Justice of India for placing it before appropriate bench.
Hearing of the matter may be expedited.
Order accordingly.
