Sunil Sharma and Others v State of Bihar
Supreme Court of India

11  May  2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 567/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Heard Mr. P.S.Mishra, learned senior counsel appearing for the petitioners, and Mrs. Sunita R.Singh, learned counsel appearing for the respondents. The order passed by the High Court refusing the prayer of the petitioners for bail is under challenge.
2.  On consideration of the facts and circumstances of the case and on perusal of the order under challenge, we dispose of this special leave petition by passing the order that if the petitioners file a fresh application for bail in the High Court, the same will be placed before the learned Judge who had disposed of the bail petitions filed by the co-accused earlier on merits.
3.  We make it clear that we have not considered and expressed any opinion on the merits of the case.
The special leave petition is disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
