Additional Commissioner of Income Tax v M. Nagappa and Others
Supreme Court of India

23 July 1997
C.As. Nos. 3108 to 3113 of 1980 (Appeals from the judgment and order Dt. 27 June 1980, of the Karnataka High Court in Writ Appeals Nos. 424 to 429 of 1974).
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The judgment under appeal has already been referred to and overruled in the case of Ganesh Dass Sreeram v. ITO 1987 Indlaw SC 28790.
2. In that view of the matter, the judgment under appeal is set aside. The appeals are allowed. There will be no order as to costs.
Appeals allowed.
