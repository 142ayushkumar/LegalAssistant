Commissioner of Customs v Rangi International
Supreme Court of India

21 January 2003
C.A. No. 3743 of 2000 (Arising as an appeal From 2002 (139) E.L.T. 739 (Goi); 2002 (141) E.L.T. A280 (S.C.))
The order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Delay in the original affidavit as well as in the additional affidavit filed by the appellant, we are not satisfied that the appellant has satisfactorily explained the delay in preferring this appeal.
2. Therefore accepting the contention of the respondent we revoke the leave granted on 12th July, 2000.
3.  Consequently this Special Leave Petition is dismissed as barred by limitation.
Petition dismissed.
