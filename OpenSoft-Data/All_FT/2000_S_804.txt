State of West Bengal v Messrs G.C.Ghosh
Supreme Court of India

27 November 2000
I.A. Nos. 3 & 4 in C.A..No.1556/1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
This order shall dispose of I.A. Nos. 3 and 4.
1. Since the payment of fee of arbitrator has been deposited, as is apparent from the order of the High Court dated 28.9.2000 and the compliance report dated 30th October, 2000, the amount of Rs. 7525/- which stands deposited may be released in favour of the arbitrator against proper receipt. Payment of the said amount shall, however, be without prejudice to the rights of the parties in Civil Appeal No. 1556/99.
2.  Notice on the prayer for stay was issued in the appeal. No objections have been filed to the same.
3.  Further proceedings before the learned arbitrator shall stay during the pendency of the appeal in this Court and till further orders.
Order accordingly.
