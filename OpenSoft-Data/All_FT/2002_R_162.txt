Rizan and Anr v State of Madhya Pradesh
Supreme Court of India

7 January 2002
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 3214/2001
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  We have heard Shri C.L. Sahu learned counsel appearing for the petitioner. Ms.Pragati Neekhra, advocate states that she has instruction to appear for the State of Madhya Pradesh and undertakes to file Vakalatname by tomorrow and counter within six weeks.
2.  The case be listed for disposal on a non-miscellaneous day after six weeks.
3.  In the meantime, the petitioner No.1 Rizan son of Sukhnath Purav shall be released on bail on executing a bond of Rs.10,000/- with two sureties each of the like amount to the satisfaction of the trial court.
Order accordingly.
