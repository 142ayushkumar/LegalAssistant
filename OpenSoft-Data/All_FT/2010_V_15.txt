Commissioner Of Central Excise, Maharashtra v Varroc Lighting Private Limited and another
Supreme Court of India

7 January 2010
CIVIL APPEAL NOS.71-72 OF 2010 (Arising out of S.L.P. (C) Nos.20246-20247 of 2008)
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Leave granted.
By consent, the matter is taken up for final hearing.
1.  After passing of the impugned order, this Court has laid down the law in the case of Commissioner of Central Excise, Pune vs. SKF India Limited, reported in [2009] 239 E.L.T.385 2009 Indlaw SC 844. We say no more, except that applicability of the judgement in the case of SKF India Limited 2009 Indlaw SC 844 [supra] to the facts of the present case will be decided by Customs, Excise and Service Tax Appellate Tribunal ['Tribunal' for short] as learned senior counsel appearing on behalf of the assessee states that SKF India Limited 2009 Indlaw SC 844 [supra] is not applicable to the facts of this case. This question may be considered by the Tribunal. Accordingly, the impugned order is set aside and the matter stand remitted to the Tribunal.
2.  The civil appeals, accordingly, stand allowed with no order as to costs.
Appeals Allowed
