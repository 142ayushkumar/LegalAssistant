State of Punjab and Others v Om Parkash Singla and Others
Supreme Court of India

2 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.17626/1999( From the judgment and order Dt. 3.2.1989 passed by the High Courtof Punjab & Haryana at Chandigarh in CW.P. 16511/1998 )
The order of the court was as follows:
Delay condoned in filing application for substitution.
The applications for substitution are allowed.
Leave granted in all other special leave petitions.
1.  The controversy raised in this appeal is identical to the one considered by this Court in State of Punjab & Ors.Vs.
Boota Singh and Anr, and State of Punjab & Ors.Vs.J.L. Gupta & Ors. Following the said decisions and for the reasons stated therein, these appeals are allowed in the same terms as set forth therein and the writ petitions filed by the respodnents are dismissed.
2.  The learned counel for the respondents submits that the respondents suffer great hardship because of escalation of prices and it is not possible for them to make both ends meet. In addition to it, they have to attend to several ailments, which is also quite expensive.
3. If that is so, it is appropriate for the respondents to make a representation to the Government and obtain the relief at their end.
Order accodingly.
