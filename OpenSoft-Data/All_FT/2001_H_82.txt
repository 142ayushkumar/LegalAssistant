Raj Kumar and Others v Haryana State and Others
Supreme Court of India

23 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.13207-13215/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel appearing for the respondents undertakes to file Vakalatnama for all the respondents.
2.  We have been informed that counter affidavit has been filed in one matter though however in three other matters the same has not been filed. Leave is given to file the counter affidavit in all the matters. Counter affidavits be filed within one week after re-opening in the new year.
3. Rejoinder affidavit, if any, be filed within two weeks thereafter. Let the matter appear four weeks after re-opening in the year year.
Order accordingly.
