Management, The Divisional Forest Officer v Mohd. Sultan
Supreme Court of India

6 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.6859/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
Heard the learned counsel for the parties.
1.  In view of the question involved in the matter and delay in raising the industrial dispute, learned counsel for the respondent submits that the order passed by the Labour Court be restored and that the order passed by the High Court granting 75 per cent of back wages be set aside.
2. In this view of the matter, impugned order passed by the High Court is set aside and the order passed by the Labour Court is restored.
3. The appeal stands allowed accordingly. No order as to costs.
Appeal allowed.
