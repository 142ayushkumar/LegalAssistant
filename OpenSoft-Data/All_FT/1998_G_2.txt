G. D. Pharmaceuticals Limited v State of W. B. and Others
Supreme Court of India

6 August 1998
C.A. No. 704 of 1993
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The learned Solicitor General at this stage fairly states that, in view of the order of this Court dated 15-9-1997 in Union of India v. G.L. Pharmaceuticals Ltd. 1997 Indlaw SC 3198 he cannot support the judgment under challenge, this Court having accepted in that matter the detailed conclusions of the Division Bench of the Calcutta High Court.
2. In the circumstances, the appeal is allowed. The judgment and order under challenge is set aside. The decision of the second appellate authority, namely, the West Bengal Commercial Taxes Tribunal, is restored. No order as to costs.
Appeal allowed.
