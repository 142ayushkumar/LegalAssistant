Dau Lal Binani v Paul Malik and Anr
Supreme Court of India

12 October 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.16488/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
We have heard learned counsel for the parties.
We do not find any infirmity in the impugned order.
The special leave petition is, therefore, dismissed.
1.  At this stage Mr. Mohta, learned Senior counsel submits that some reasonable time be granted to the petitioner to vacate and handover the vacant possession of the premises.
2. Six months' period from today is granted to the petitioner to vacate and hand over the vacant possession of the premises to the landlord subject to filing usual undertaking within three weeks from today.
Petition dismissed.
