Jagat Singh v Delhi Transport Corporation
Supreme Court of India

10 November 2009
Review Petition (c) No. 1822-1823 of 2009, S.L.P. (c) No. 3637-3638 of 2009
The Order of the Court was as follows :
The Review Petitions are dismissed on the ground of delay as well as on merits.
Order:
1. This review petition is directed against 23rd February, 2009 whereby the special leave petitions were dismissed.
2. There is delay of 125 days in filing the Review Petitions. The explanation offered therefor is not satisfactory. Even otherwise, in our view no case for review of order dated 23rd February, 2009 is made out. The Review Petitions are dismissed on the ground of delay as well as on merits.
Petitions dismissed
