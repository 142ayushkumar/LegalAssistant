U.P. Avas Evam Vikas Parishad v Raghunath Singh(Dead) and Anr
Supreme Court of India

8 October 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.14834/2000
The order of the court was as follows:
In S.L.P.(C)No.2912/2001:
1.  It is reported that the sole respondent-Roop Chand in this petition, has died in the year 1994 during the pendency of the appeal before the High Court and he was not substituted by the legal representatives and therefore, the appeal had abated. It is not denied by the petitioner's counsel.
2. We, therefore, set aside the judgment under challenge and send the matter back to the High Court.
3.  However, it will be open to the petitioner to move an application for substitution along with an application for condonation of delay before the High Court.
4. In case such an application is filed, the High Court shall consider and decide the said application in accordance with law.
With the aforesaid direction, the petition stands disposed of.
