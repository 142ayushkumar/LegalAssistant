Supreme Court of India

17 February 2005
Appeal (Civil) 6971 of 1999
The Judgment was delivered by : S. H. Kapadia, J.
1.  For the reasons given in our decision in Commissioner of Central Excise v. M.R.F. Ltd. reported in [2005 (1) Scale 554], this Civil Appeal filed by the department relating to classification of Rubberised Tyre Cord Fabric is dismissed, with no order as to costs.
