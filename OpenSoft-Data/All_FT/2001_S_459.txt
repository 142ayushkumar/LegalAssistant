State of Andhra Pradesh and Others v State Election Commission and Others
Supreme Court of India

4 April 2001
I.A. No.25-32 in S.L.P.(C) No.7979-7986/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We are not pleased to extend the time once again for postponing the election process to step in.
2. Yet in view of the plea made on behalf of the Government that they will not make a further prayer for extension hereinafter, and in view of the fact that the High Court has extended the time till the end of May, 2001 we extend the time for holding the election till 31.7.2001 as a last chance. It is made clear that no further extension will be granted on any count whatsover.
I.A. disposed of.
Application disposed of.
