Southern Petro-Chemicals Industry Corporation Ltd v Assistant Commissioner, Chennai and Anr
Supreme Court of India

26 February 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.2970/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We are not inclined to interfere with the impugned interim order passed by the Madras High Court while the writ petition against the order of assessment is pending. The Special Leave Petition is accordingly dismissed.
2.  Mr. Mishra, the learned senior counsel, however, states that some more time be given to make the deposit in question.
3. We direct that 25% of the demand shall be deposited by 31st of March, 2001 and the balance 25%, pursuant to the order of the High Court, by 31st of May, 2001.
Petition dismissed.
