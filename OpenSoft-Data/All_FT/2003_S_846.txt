Sunil Ram and Others v State of Bihar
Supreme Court of India

17 October 2003
Petition(s) For Special Leave To Appeal (Cr.) No. 2589/2003
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
Leave granted.
1.  On the facts and circumstances of the case, we think it appropriate and direct that the appellants shall be released on bail in the event of they being arrested on their furnishing self bond in the sum of Rs.10,000/- each with one surety for the like amount to the satisfaction of the arresting authority or trial court as the case may be.
2.  The appellants shall abide by the conditions enumerated in Section 438(2) of the Cr.P.C.
The appeal is allowed.
Appeal allowed.
