Bhudeo Chouhan v The State Of Bihar
Supreme Court of India

12  May  2009
CRIMINAL APPEAL No.1009 OF 2009[Arising out of SLP(Crl.) No.977 of 2009]
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. Leave granted.
2. Having heard learned counsel for the parties and the State and since allegations against the appellant do not appear to be commensurate with the post-mortem report, let the appellant be released on bail to the satisfaction of the trial court.
3. The appeal is, accordingly, allowed.
Appeal allowed
