Kunan Singh v Sikh National College and Others
Supreme Court of India

22 January 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.664/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The learned counsel for the petitioner submits that though a contention was urged before the High Court that the approval for the dismissal of the petitioner was obtained long after his dismissal, it has not been considered.
2.  We are afraid, we cannot entertain this plea in view of the fact that the record of the High Court cannot be allowed to be contradicted. State of Maharashtra Vs. Ramdas Shivnivas Nayak & Anr.
3.  In this view of the matter, we dismiss this Special Leave Petition.
Petition dismissed.
