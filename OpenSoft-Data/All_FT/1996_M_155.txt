Ms. Bindu Bansal, D/O Shri S.S v Maharshi Dayanand
Supreme Court of India

23 January 1996
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. Special leave granted.
2.In view of our judgment in Nitasha Paul's case {Civil Appeal No. 2310 of 1996 (Arising out of S.L.P. (C) No. 9776 of 1995)], this appeal is disposed of in terms of the order passed in Nitasha Paul's case. no further order is necessary in this case.
