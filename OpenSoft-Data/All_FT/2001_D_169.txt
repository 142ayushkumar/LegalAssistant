Dinesh Bali v State (Govt.Of N.C.T. of Delhi)
Supreme Court of India

11  May  2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 1375/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  Appellant has been arrested in this case on 2.11.2000. Investigation has been completed and charge-sheet has been laid. We do not find any reason to keep him as an undertrial prisoner for a further period.
2. We, therefore, order him to be released on bail on his executing a bond for an amount to the satisfaction of the trial court with two solvent sureties.
The appeal is disposed of accordingly.
Appeal disposed of.
