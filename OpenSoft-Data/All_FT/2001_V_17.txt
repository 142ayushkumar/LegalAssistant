V. Ramachandran v Collector of Trichy District and Others
Supreme Court of India

27 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.8340/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
We have heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  The Judgment/order of the High Court in which Writ Petition filed by the petitioner herein was dismissed in default on account of non-appearance of the counsel is under challenge with entering into the merits of the case.
2. We are of the view that the petitioner should be given an opportunity to place his case before the High Court accordingly, the judgment/order under challenge is set aside.
3.  The matter is remitted to the High Court for disposal of a fresh case by a single judge in accordance with law. This order is to the petitioner payment of Rs.5000/- (Rupees five thousand) to the respondent No.3, Syndicate Bank within four weeks failing which this order shall not take affect and the Special Leave Petition shall be stand disposed of.
Petition disposed of.
