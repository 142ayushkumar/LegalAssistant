Asharfi Prasad Singh v Union of India and Others
Supreme Court of India

21 March 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.315/2001 in S.L.P.(Civil)No.1944/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We have carefully gone through the order impugned in this review petition and the review petition. In our view no valid ground is made out to review the order dated 12.2.2001.
The review petition is accordingly dismissed.
Review dismissed.
