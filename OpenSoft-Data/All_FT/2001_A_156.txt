Amforge Industries Limited v Renoir Consulting Limited
Supreme Court of India

2 January 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.9866/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  After hearing the counsel for the parties, we are of the opinion that the only modification which is to be made to the order of the High Court is that instead of the petitioner herein being permitted to deposit a sum of Rs. 70 lakhs whereupon the respondent herein was to be relegated to its remedy of a civil suit, the petitioner is now permitted to furnish a bank guarantee for a sum of Rs. 70 lakhs within a period of eight weeks from today.
2. The bank guarantee will be filed with the Prothonotary and Senior Master of the Bombay High Court and if it is not filed within eight weeks from today, then the order of the Single Judge as well as that of the Division Bench will stand automatically revived.
The special leave petition is disposed of in the aforesaid terms.
Petition disposed of.
