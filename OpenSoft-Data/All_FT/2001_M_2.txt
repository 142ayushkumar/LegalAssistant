Government of A P and Others v M Ratnakar
Supreme Court of India

9 April 2001
C.A..No.4783/1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Sole respondent has not been served. No steps have been taken despite opportunities granted to serve them.
2.  We have perused the Office Report dated 28th March, 2001. No one is present on behalf of the appellant. The appeal is dismissed for non-prosecution.
Interim order, if any, shall stand vacated.
Appeal dismissed.
