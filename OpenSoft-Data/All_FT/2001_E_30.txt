Essem Consultant and Another v Papai Travel and Tours Private Limited
Supreme Court of India

3 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.3946/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Upon hearing submissions on behalf of the parties, we do feel that expeditious disposal of the special leave petition at the admission stage would sub-serve the interest of justice.
2.  The status quo as of today however be maintained and the civil suit No. 76/99 pending before the learned Civil Judge, Senior Division, 9th Court, Alipore be expedited.
It is desired that the suit be disposed of with utmost expedition and preferably within a period of 18 months.
3. It is, however, clarified that the property in question shall not be alienated until the disposal of the suit.
Special leave petition is disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
