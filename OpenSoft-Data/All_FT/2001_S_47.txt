State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v S.L. Awasthi and Others
Supreme Court of India

14 December 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.17617/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
1.  Counsel for the parties agree that the respondents in the present cases will be governed by the result of Writ Petition No. 336 of 2000 filed by the State of U.P. against the judgment of the Tribunal dated 16.11.1998.
2.  These appeals are, accordingly, disposed of in the aforesaid terms and the impugned order of the High Court is set aside. The respondents are at liberty to approach the High Court for appropriate interim relief in the aforesaid writ petition.
Appeals disposed of.
