State of Haryana and Others v Mohinder Kumar
Supreme Court of India

19 January 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.140/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Delay in SLP(C) CC Nos.128 and 137/2001 is condoned. In the peculiar facts and circumstances, particularly, the officers of the petitioner-Department had delayed for handing over possession of the allotted land, the High Court was required to issue appropriate direction. The High Court has specifically mentioned that claims of the respondents were verified.
2.  In this view of the matter there is no substance in these petitions and are, therefore, dismissed. It is clarified that these petitions are dismissed genuinly on the ground that the claims of the respondents were verified by the concerned officers.
Petitions dismissed.
