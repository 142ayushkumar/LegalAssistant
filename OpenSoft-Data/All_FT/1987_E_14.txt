Pharma Drugs Manufacturing Company v Excise and Taxation Officer, Jammu
Supreme Court of India

13 March 1987
C.A. No. 749 of 1987 (Arising out of Petition for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No. 9220 of 1985)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
1.  Having heard learned counsel for the parties, we are not satisfied that there was justification for the respondent recovering the staff charges when granting a licence under the Medicinal and Toilet Preparations (Excise Duties) Act, 1956.
2.   The appellant is entitled to an order restraining the respondent from enforcing the notices dated March 31, 1973 and August 23, 1974 and further to the refund of a sum of Rs. 8956 already paid by the appellant for the period 1960-63. We order accordingly.
3.  The appeal is disposed of in these terms. There is no order as to costs.
Appeal disposed of.
