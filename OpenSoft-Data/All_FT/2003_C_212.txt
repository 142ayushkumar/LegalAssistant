Commissioner of Central Excise, Meerut v Star Paper Mills Limited
Supreme Court of India

1 April 2003
C.A. No. 2533 of 2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  This appeal is against the order of the Tribunal dated 30-8-2000. We find that the order of the Tribunal is supported by a judgment of this Court in the case of Sinkhai Synthetics and Chemicals (P) Ltd. v. CCE, 2002 Indlaw SC 1350.
2.  In view thereof, we see no reason to interfere with the order of the Tribunal. The appeal stands dismissed. There shall be no order as to costs.
Appeal dismissed.
