Union of India and Others v K. Gurudasan and Others
Supreme Court of India

19 February 2008
Appeal (Civil) 3857 of 2003
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1.  During the pendency of these appeals, a similar question has been considered by the Constitution Bench of this Court in M. Nagaraj & Ors. Vs. Union of India & Ors. (2006) 8 SCC 212. 2006 Indlaw SC 722
2.  In view of the conclusion arrived at by the Constitution Bench in paragraphs 121 and 122 of M. Nagaraj case 2006 Indlaw SC 722 (supra), these appeals have become infructous and are, accordingly, dismissed as infructuous.
