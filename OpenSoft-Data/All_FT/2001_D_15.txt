D.P. Verma Dinesh Prasad Verma v State of Bihar and Anr
Supreme Court of India

29 October 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 2985/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Delay condoned.
Heard the learned counsel for the parties.
1.  Considering the offence alleged against the petitioner pending further investigation if petitioner is arrested in connection with Vigilance P.S.Case No.21/95 and Spl.Case Nos.8/95, 13/95 and 12/95 (arising out of Vigilance P.S.Case Nos.18/95, 24/95 and 22/95), he shall be relealsed on bail on his furnishing a bail bond for a sum of Rs.10, 000/- on a condition that he shall fully co-operate in further investigation till the chargesheet is submitted.
The special leave petitions stand disposed of accordingly.
Petitions disposed of.
