Vimi Mathur v Vikas Mathur and others
Supreme Court of India

23 August 2004
Transfer Petition (C) No. 796 of 2003
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In view of the fact that two other cases between the parties are already pending in the courts at Patna, we consider it appropriate to allow this transfer petition.
2. The learned counsel for the respondents also has no objection to adopt such a course, having regard to the fact stated above.
3.  Under the circumstances, the transfer petition is allowed and Matrimonial Case No. 31 of 2002, titled as Vikas Mathur v. Vimi Mathur, pending in the Court of Vth Additional District Judge, Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, shall stand transferred to the District Judge, Patna, Bihar, who may either hear the case himself or transfer it to a court of competent jurisdiction.
No costs.
Petition allowed.
