Balaji House and Land Owners Wel. Association and Another v Government of Andhra Pradesh and Others
Supreme Court of India

31 July 2008
Civil Appeal No. 2431 of 2002
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1.  Learned senior counsel appearing on behalf of the appellants submits, on instruction, that he does not want to proceed with the matter and seeks permission to withdraw the same to seek other alternative remedy, if there be any. Permission is granted. The appeal is accordingly dismissed as withdrawn.
Interim order, if any, shall stand vacated.
Appeal Dismissed
