Action Comm. for Imp. of R. Srikrishna and others v Union of India and others
Supreme Court of India

2 November 2006
W.P. (Civil) No(s). 527 of 1998 with W.P(C) No. 164 of 1999, W.P(C) No. 182 of 2001, W.P(C) No. 40 of 1999, W.P(C) No. 542 of 1998
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. It is submitted by the learned counsel for the petitioner that there were 1073 missing persons and out of them 444 received compensation and the rest of the dependents of the missing persons have not got any compensation. The petitioner shall furnish details of the persons who have not received compensation so far, so that the respondent may take necessary steps in the matter.
2. As regards the pending cases against 31 police officers, the State shall file a final report in this case at the earliest. The State may file the up to date action taken by the department and also give the details, which the department has already taken.
3. List on 5.12.2006.
Order accordingly
