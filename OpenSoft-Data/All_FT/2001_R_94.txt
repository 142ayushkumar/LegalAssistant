Tabassum Qureshi v Rajasthan Public Service Commission and Anr
Supreme Court of India

6 February 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.78/2001 in C.A.No.7316/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
I.A.Nos. 1, 3 and 4 are dismissed.
I.A.No.2 is allowed.
1.  We have examined the contentions raised in the review petition and perused the order under challenge.
2. In our view, there is no valid reason to review our order dated 12.12.2000. The Review Petition is, therefore, dismissed.
Review dismissed.
