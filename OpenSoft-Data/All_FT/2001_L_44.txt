Larsen and Toubro Limited v State of Orissa and Others
Supreme Court of India

20 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.13626/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Mr. Sibal, learned senior counsel appearing for the petitioner submits that against the order of rejection, the petitioner is advised to take recourse to such other remedy as may be available to it in law and he submits that the needful shall be done by the petitioner within three weeks from the date of receipt of the order of rejection.
2. The State shall issue a copy of the order of rejection and communicate the same to the petitioner urgently. The petitioner shall have three weeks time from the date of communication of the order of rejection, to take such proceedings as may be available to it in law before an appropriate forum.
3.  Till the expiry of the period of three weeks from the date of communication of the order of rejection, the respondent shall not create any third party rights.
4.  The proceedings which the petitioner may take, shall be disposed of by the appropriate forum in accordance with law.
5.  We clarify that this order shall not be considered as any expression of opinion on the merits of the case.
The special leave petition is disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
