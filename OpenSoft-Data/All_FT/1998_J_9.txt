State of Orissa and Others v Jagannath Industries and Others
Supreme Court of India

24 September 1998
C.As. Nos. 6133 and 6134 of 1995 (Appeals from the judgment and order Dt. 2 December 1994, of the Orissa High Court in O.J.C. Nos. 5754 of 1991 and 6943 of 1994)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We have heard learned counsel. We are of the view that no interference in the facts and circumstances of the case is required. The question of law is left open for decision in an appropriate case.
The appeals are dismissed, with no order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed.
