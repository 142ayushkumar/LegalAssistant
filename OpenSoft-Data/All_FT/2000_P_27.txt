N.T. Rangaswamy v P.G. Abdul Azeez
Supreme Court of India

8 December 2000
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.7382/2000
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  SLP(C)No.7382/2000. The present petition is directed against an order for extension of time for a period of six months from the date of the decree. According to the learned counsel for the respondent, the period is over. It is not disputed by the learned counsel for the petitioner.
2.  In view of this fact, the present petition has become infructuous. It is accordingly dismissed.
However, this is without prejudice to the rights of the parties.
3.  SLP(C)....CC 8153/2000. Learned counsel for the petitioner states he desires to file review before the High Court. Accordingly, he does not want to press this petition.
The special leave petition is dismissed as not pressed.
Petition dismissed.
