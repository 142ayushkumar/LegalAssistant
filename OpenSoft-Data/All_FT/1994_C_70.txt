State of Himachal Pradesh and Another v Chet Ram and Others
Supreme Court of India

12 July 1994
C.A. No. 3049 of 1987
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We have today pronounced judgment in titled State of H.P. v. Amar Nath Sharma 1994 Indlaw SC 1826.
2.  For the reasons recorded in the said judgment, we allow this appeal and set aside the High Court judgment and dismiss the writ petition filed by Respondents 1 to 22 before the High Court. No costs.
Appeal allowed.
