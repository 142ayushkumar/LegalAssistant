Prakash Ramdas Choudhary v Shantabai Darekar
Supreme Court of India

5 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.11766/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Heard Shri S.M. Jadhav learned counsel for the petitioner and Ms.Dipika A.Saitwal, Power of Attorney holder of the respondent.
2. On consideration of the matter, we are not inclined to interfere with the order passed by the High Court. Then the learned counsel for the petitioner submitted that a direction may be issued for expeditious disposal of the writ petition within the time fixed by this Court and till then the status quo order passed by this Court on 19.7.2001, may be maintained by the parties. To the said submission Ms. Dipika A.Saitwal stated that she has no objection.
3.  Accordingly, the High Court is requested to dispose of the case, Writ Petition No.2978/2001, expeditiously within three months from today and till then the status quo order passed on 19.7.2001 shall remain in force.
The SLP is disposed of on the above terms.
Petition disposed of.
