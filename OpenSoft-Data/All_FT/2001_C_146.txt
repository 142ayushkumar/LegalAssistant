Gitaben Ratanbhai Bhil v Chief G.M., Hindustan Petrol.Coprn. and Others
Supreme Court of India

20 April 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.13851/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The High court is justified in making the order on the facts setforth before it and , therefore, we are not inclined to interfere with the order made by the High Court and hence this petition is dismissed.
2. However, we are constrained to notice that only the members of one particular family though they may not fall within the prohibited degree of relationship for the purpose of grant of dealership of retail petrol outlet and they are all related to Mr.Somjibhai Damor who is stated to be a Member of Parliament and also Member of the Advisory Committee to the Petroleum and Natural Gas Ministry are allotted retail outlet/dealership. How far that factor influenced the present allotment is not very clear.
This petition is dismissed accordingly.
Petition dismissed.
