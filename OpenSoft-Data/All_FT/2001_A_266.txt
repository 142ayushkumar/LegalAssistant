Ashok Kumar and Others v Sohan Bai and Others
Supreme Court of India

19 February 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.2470/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
We are not inclined to interfere with the matter.
1.  The Special Leave Petition is dismissed. After we dismissed the matter, learned counsel for the petitioners stated that in case the petitioners are required to vacate the premises immediately, it shall create great hardship to them.
2. Therefore, some time may be granted to vacate the premises. Learned counsel appearing for the respondents has no objection. We, therefore, grant time till 31.8.2001 to the petitioners to vacate the premises on their filing usual undertaking in this Court within four weeks from today.
Petition dismissed.
