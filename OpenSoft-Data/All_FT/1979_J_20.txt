Jethmal Poonamchand Bafna v State of Maharashtra
Supreme Court of India

27 April 1979
Cr.A. No. 18 of 1973
The Judgment was delivered by: FAZAL ALI, J.
1.  We have gone through the judgment of the High Court. The appellant has been convicted u/s. 135(a) and (b) of the Customs Act and sentenced to 6 months simple imprisonment. The High Court as also the court below have found that the appellant was found in possession of 1241 wrist watches which he recovered by opening the cupboard with the keys (which he took out from the window).
2. It was also found that the appellant had keys of the flat with which the flat was opened and searched by the Customs Authorities. We do not find any error of law in the judgment of the High Court. The appeal is without any merit and is accordingly dismissed.
Appeal Dismissed.
