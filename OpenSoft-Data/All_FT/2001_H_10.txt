H.P. State Electricity Board and Anr v Ranjeet Singh and Others
Supreme Court of India

5 October 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.6998-7007/1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Applications for substitution are ordered.
1.  Leave is granted. Printing is dispensed with; appeals shall be heard on the S.L.Ps. paper-books.
2.  The interim order passed by this Court on May 12, 1999 is modified as follows:
"The appellant shall pay the amount of bonus payable to the respondents under the award as confirmed by the High Court in four instalments of two months each.
The payment shall be subject to the result of the appeals. Any amount paid to the employees to which they are found to be not entitled, may be recovered from their wages."
Order accordingly.
