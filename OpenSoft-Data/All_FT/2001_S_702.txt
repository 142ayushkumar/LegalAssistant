State of Haryana and Others v Ved Parkash and Others
Supreme Court of India

26 September 2001
CIVIL APPEAL NO. 3408/1998
The Order of the court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel on both sides are agreed that this appeal is covered by the decision of this Court in C.A. Nos. 5740-5741/1997 - State of Haryana and Ors. Vs. Ravinder Kumar and Ors. and connected batch disposed of on 31.10.2000.
2.  Following the said decision and for the reasons stated therein this appeal shall stand disposed of in the same terms as set forth therein.
Appeal disposed of.
