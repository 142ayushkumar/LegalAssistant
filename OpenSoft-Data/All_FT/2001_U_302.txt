Union of India v Shyam Sunder Kumar
Supreme Court of India

10 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.18687/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
1.  In the instant case, the writ petition filed by the appellant herein against the decision of the tribunal was dismissed. The appellant herein was not represented before the High Court when the impugned order was passed.
2.  The High Court, therefore, did not have the benefit of arguments on behalf of the petitioner therein.
3.  Considering the facts and circumstances of the present case, the High Court should have considered the case in greater detail with the assistance of the counsel for the petitioner therein.
4. We therefore, allow this appeal and direct that Civil Writ No. 3866/1999 along with miscellaneous application be listed before the High Court for disposal in accordance with law.
Appeal allowed.
