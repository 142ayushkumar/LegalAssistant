Surat Singh v Hukam Singh Negi
Supreme Court of India

13  May  2009
CIVIL APPEAL NO.3544 OF 2009
(Arising out of S.L.P. (C) No.1444 of 2008)
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Leave granted.
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  By the impugned order, the High Court has allowed the appeal without framing any substantial question of law, which is mandatory under Section 100 of the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908.
2. Accordingly, the appeal is allowed and the impugned order rendered by the High Court is set aside.
3.  Now, the High Court shall consider whether any substantial question of law arises in the second appeal and then decide the same in accordance with law after giving opportunity of hearing to the parties.
Appeal allowed
