Commissioner of Central Excise v Polyplastic
Supreme Court of India

15 January 2010
Civil Appeal Nos. 298-299 of 2010 (Arising out of S.L.P. (C) Nos. 20821-20822 of 2009)
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Leave granted.
None appears for the respondent, though served.
1.  The issue involved in these appeals stand covered by the following two decisions of this Court reported in the cases of Commissioner of Central Excise, Pune vs. SKF India Limited, reported in [2009] 239 E.L.T.385 2009 Indlaw SC 844 and Commissioner of Central Excise vs. M/s. International Auto Limited 2010 Indlaw SC 31 [Civil Appeal No.225 of 2010 @ S.L.P. (C) No.17339 of 2009] delivered on 8th January, 2010.
2.  Accordingly, the civil appeals stand disposed of with no order as to costs.
Appeals Disposed of
