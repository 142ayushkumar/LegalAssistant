B.M.Ayurved and Sankrit S. Sansthan and Anr v Union of India and Others
Supreme Court of India

18 June 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.10557/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Taken on Board.
1.  This special leave petition is directed against an interim order of the High Court directing an inspection to be carried out by Dental Council with which the University and the State Government have been permitted to be associated. We are not inclined to interfere with the interim order passed by the High Court.
2.  However, it is clarified that while carrying out inspection the competent authority on behalf of Dental Council shall make a separate note on such facts circumstances and features as are brought to its notice by representatives of State Government and the University during the inspection. The special leave petition is dismissed subject to the above said observation.
Petition dismissed.
