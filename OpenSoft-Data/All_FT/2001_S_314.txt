State of Up and Anr v Shyam Babu and Others
Supreme Court of India

30 July 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.17641/1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
S.L.P.(c) Nos. 17641/99, 23562/97, 7266/98, 721/99 and 6796/98:
1.  Learned counsel for the parties submit that by virtue of repeal of the Urban Land (Ceiling and Regulation) Act and the subsequent developments which have taken place, these special leave petitions have been rendered infructuous.
Accordingly, these special leave petitions are dismissed as infructuous.
S.L.P.(c) Nos. 6795/98 and 6798/98:
2.  No steps have been taken for completing service and bringing on record the L.Rs. of respondent No. 1 in S.L.P.(c) No. 6798/98.
3. Learned counsel for the petitioner prays for and is granted four weeks' time to take appropriate steps.
Order accordingly.
