State of Uttar Pradesh v Ishwari Singh and Another
Supreme Court of India

4 September 1995
C.A. No. 5964 of 1983
The Order of the Court was as follows:
We have today delivered judgment in Board of Revenue, U.P. v. Electronic Industries of India.
1. For the reasons recorded by us in the said judgment, we allow this appeal, set aside the impugned judgment of the High Court dated 10-9-1980 and dismiss the Civil Miscellaneous Writ Petition No. 8375 of 1973 filed by the respondent before the High Court. No costs.
Appeal allowed.
