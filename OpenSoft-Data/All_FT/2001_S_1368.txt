Bharat Coking Coal Limited and Others v Sujit Kora and Anr
Supreme Court of India

25 July 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.765/2001 in S.L.P.(Civil)No.6679/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Delay is condoned.
1.  On a careful consideration of the review petition and the order dated 30.4.2001 sought to be reviewed, we do not find any valid ground to warrant review of the said order.
The Review Petition is, therefore, dismissed.
Review dismissed.
