Bhaguji Dadu Pondkule (D) By Lrs v State of Maharashtra and Others
Supreme Court of India

27 November 2000
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.18814/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the petitioners submits that a point was raised in the High Court under Section 16 of Maharashtra Agricultural (Ceiling on Holdings) Act, 1961 ( vide para 3 and 4 of the writ petition) and the same was not considered by the High Court.
2.  So far as the points decided by the High Court are concerned, we are not inclined to interfere with the findings.
3. But in so far as the above point raised under Section 16 is concerned, it will be open to the petitioner to move the High Court for a review.
With this remark, the SLP is dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
