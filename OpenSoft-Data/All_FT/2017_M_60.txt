Pr. Commissioner of Income Tax and another v Makino India Private Limited
Supreme Court of India

3 April 2017
Civil Appeal No. 1062 of 2017
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. We have heard learned counsel for the appellant.
2. In view of the judgment dated December 16, 2016, passed by this Court in Civil Appeal No. 8498 of 2013, titled as C.I.T. and Another versus M/s. Yokogawa India Limited and other 2016 Indlaw SC 898 connected matters, this appeal is dismissed.
Appeal dismissed
