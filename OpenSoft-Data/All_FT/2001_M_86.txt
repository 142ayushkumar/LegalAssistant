J.J. Lal Pvt.Ltd. and Others v M.R.Murali and Anr
Supreme Court of India

29 October 2001
IA No 37-40 In C.A. No.3429-3432/1998
The Order of the Court was as follows:
These IAs. are for certain interim directions.
1.  Without the original records of the appeals, it is not possible to dispose them of as none of us was a party to the original order. Mr. Das, the learned senior counsel appearing for the appellants, states that the appeals in fact have already been made ready and were heard, but could not be taken up finally and the same are still pending.
2. In this view of the matter, the appeals itself together with these IAs and other connected matters be listed on a non miscellaneous day in the first week of December, 2001.
Order accordingly.
