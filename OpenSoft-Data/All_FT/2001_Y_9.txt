Yashpal Singh v State of Punjab
Supreme Court of India

6 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 2635/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that petitioner is prepared to deposit the entire amount collected from the complainant for the purpose of sending his nephew abroad.
2.  If this offer is genuine, we permit the petitioner to surrender before the court concerned and apply for bail by making the deposit of the amount of Rs.68,000/- to be paid over to the aggrieved party.
3. If any such application is made supported by such deposit we direct the court concerned to release him on bail on executing a bond for such amount as the trial judge may fix with two solvent sureties to the satisfaction of the trial judge.
With these observations this Special Leave Petition is disposed of.
Petition disposed of.
