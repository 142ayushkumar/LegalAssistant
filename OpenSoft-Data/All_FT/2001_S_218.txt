Shakina Bi and Others v Shahida Begum
Supreme Court of India

7 December 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.20956-20957/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
We find no merit in this petition.
The special leave petitions are accordingly dismissed.
1.  Learned counsel for the petitioner then urged that in case the petitioner is not granted time to vacate the premises, he will be put to great hardship.
2.  We, therefore, direct the petitioner shall not be dispossessed from the premises in dispute till 31st October, 2002, provided he files usual undertaking within four weeks from today failing which this part of the order shall stand vacated without reference to the Court.
3.  In case the respondent has any objection to this part of the Order, it would be open to him to move this Court for vacation of the Order.
Petition dismissed.
