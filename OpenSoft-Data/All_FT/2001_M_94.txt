M.V. Umang and Others v Kamla Kant Dube and Anr
Supreme Court of India

8 October 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.16879/2001
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  Let this matter be fixed in the third week of January, 2002 for final disposal on a non-miscellaneous day. All affidavits or any other documents by either of the parties be filed in the meantime. In addition to the order as above, the petitioners are directed to pay a sum of Rs.20,000/- per month commencing from the month of September, 2001 and such payments be made on or before 15th of this month covering two months September and October, 2001 and all other payments be paid by 7th of each succeeding month.
2. These payments shall be, however, without prejudice to the rights and contentions of the parties. Security furnished, however, shall continue. Stay of further proceedings before the trial court.
Order accordingly.
