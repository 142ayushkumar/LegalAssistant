Anwar Pasha v Deputy Commissioner of Income Tax
Supreme Court of India

10 December 2007
C.A. No. 5758 of 2007
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  After hearing counsel for the parties, we accept these appeals and set aside the impugned orders of the High Court and remit the case back to the High Court for a fresh decision in accordance with law, after framing a substantial question of law, in the light of the judgment of this Court in Manish Makeshwari v Asstt. CIT 2007 Indlaw SC 153(.
The appeals are allowed accordingly.
Appeals allowed.
