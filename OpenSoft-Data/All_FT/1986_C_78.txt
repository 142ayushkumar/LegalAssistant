Commissioner of Income Tax, Kerala v High Land Produce Company Limited
Supreme Court of India

8 January 1986
C.A. No. 931, 932 and 933 of 1976
The Judgment was delivered by: PATHAK, J.
1.  Having regard to the facts of the case and for the reasons contained in the judgment delivered today in CIT v. Andhra Prabha Pvt. Ltd. (C.A. No 2008 of 1981) 1986 Indlaw SC 364, these appeals fail and are accordingly dismissed without any order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed.
