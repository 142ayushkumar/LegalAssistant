Darshan Singh Chhabra v State (Govt. of N.C.T. of Delhi) and Others
Supreme Court of India

19 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 3225/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The impugned order indicates that the learned Public Prosecutor stated before the High Court that the charges under section 304-B against accused Neena had been framed under certain erroneous facts. Counsel for the informant, who is the petitioner in this Court, states that this is an incorrect recording of the High Court.
2.  In the circumstances, we call upon the learned Public Prosecutor, who was appearing before the High Court, to file an affidavit as to the correct state of affairs, and whether in fact such assertions had been made before the High Court or not. The affidavit in question be filed within four weeks from today.
Ordered Accordingly.
