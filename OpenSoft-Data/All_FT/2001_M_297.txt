Messrs Bhagwan Das and Bros., M.P v State of Madhya Pradesh and Anr
Supreme Court of India

28 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.250-252/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Delay condoned in SLP(C) Nos. 1701-1703/2001.
1. We have heard learned counsel appearing for the parties and are of the view that the orders of the High Court do not require any interference in view of the judgment of this Court in Dwaraka Das vs. State of M.P.and Anr. 1999 Indlaw SC 35.
2.  Mr. S.K. Gambhir, learned senior counsel appearing for the petitioner, has vehemently argued that in view of the judgment of this Court reported in ONGC and Anr. vs. Association of Natural Gas Consuming Industries and Ors,the High Court was not justified in rejecting the petition filed under Sections 152 and 153-A of the Code of Civil Procedure.
3. On a perusal of the said judgment, we feel that, on the facts and circumstances of the present case, it is not applicable. Therefore, the special leave petitions are dismissed.
Petitions dismissed.
