Tip Top Plastic Industries and Others v Income Tax Officer
Supreme Court of India

20 February 1995
The Order of the Court ws as follows:
1.  The Income-tax Officer has found under his order of March 19, 1990, that the petitioners are liable to pay the tax of Rs.3,54,864 on the basis of the concealed stock of the value of Rs.4,93,177 which was the value of the stock as estimated earlier under his order of March 28, 1985.
2.   We, therefore, find no substance in this petition. The special leave petition is dismissed accordingly.
Petition dismissed.
