Apl Agencies India Private Limited v Transport Commnr., Kerala and Others
Supreme Court of India

24 July 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.778/2001 in S.L.P.(Civil)No.10304/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We have perused the order under review and the contentions raised in the review petition.
2. In our view, no case is made out to review our order dated June 4, 2001. The review petition is therefore dismissed.
Review dismissed.
