Union of India and Others v Sukha and Anr
Supreme Court of India

2 November 2000
CIVIL APPEAL NOS. 7183-7184/1997
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The learned counsel appearing for the appellants in usual fairness submitted before this Court that the matter in issue stands covered by the decision of the Constitution Bench of this Court in L.Chandra Kumar Vs.Union of India and Ors.
2.  Therefore, the question of proceeding with the matter any further would not arise. The appeals are, therefore, dismissed. No order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed.
