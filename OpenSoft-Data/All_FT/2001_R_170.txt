Ram Kumar v State of Haryana and Others
Supreme Court of India

22 March 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.260/2001 in S.L.P.(Civil)No.817 of 2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Delay is condoned.
The Review Petition is dismissed.
1.  We have carefully perused the order sought to be reviewed and the review petition.
2. We are unable to discern any valid ground to review the order of this Court dated 4.1.2001.
The Review Petition is without any merit. It is accordingly dismissed.
Review dismissed.
