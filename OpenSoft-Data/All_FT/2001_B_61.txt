Baij Nath v Ram Murty (D) Through Lrs
Supreme Court of India

18 April 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.6894/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Heard learned counsel for the parties. We do not find any merit in this petition. No infirmity has been pointed which calls for our interference while exercising our power under Article 136. Accordingly, this Special Leave Petition fails and is dismissed.
2.  However, on the facts and circumstances of this case, we feel it appropriate and accordingly direct that the petitioner shall vacate the premises, in question, by or before 31st October, 2001 subject to usual undertaking to be filed within four weeks from today.
Costs on the parties.
Petition dismissed.
