Rewaram v Rajmal Jalori
Supreme Court of India

29 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.10178/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner. We are not satisfied that the order under challenge requires our interference However, the learned counsel for the petitioner submits that as the petitioner is running business he needs two years' time to vacate the premises.
2. Having heard the learned counsel for the respondent, we are of the view that in the interests of justice it would be appropriate to grant time upto 30th June, 2002 to the petitioner to vacate the premises and hand over the peaceful possession of the same to the respondent subject to his filing a usual undertaking within three weeks from today.
With the above observation, the S.L.P. is dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
