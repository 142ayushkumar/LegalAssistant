Rajasthan State Rd. Development and another v Messrs Singh Brothers
Supreme Court of India

10 December 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.16374/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
1.  The orders of the High Court granting interim relief to the respondent during the pendency of the writ petitions are set aside. The appellant will be at liberty to take further action in accordance with law.
These appeals are disposed of.
Appeals disposed of.
