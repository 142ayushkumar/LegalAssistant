Northn. Rly. Admn., Ministry of Railway v Patel Engineering Company Limited
Supreme Court of India

12 February 2008
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No(s). 16196 / 2006 (AA No. 189 / 2005)
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. Learned counsel for the respondent has invited our attention to two Vs. Bharat decisions of this Court in the case of Ace Pipeline Contracts (P) Ltd. rat Battery Petroleum Corpn. Ltd. (2007) 5 SCC 304 2007 Indlaw SC 292 and Union of India Vs. Bha Mfg. Co. (P) Ltd. (2007) 7 SCC, 684 2007 Indlaw SC 807Both these decisions relate to the appointment of arbitrator u/s. 11(6) of the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996It appears that in the subsequent decision in the case of Bharat Battery Mfg. Co. 2007 Indlaw SC 807 (supra) the earlier decision in the case of Ace Pipeline 2007 Indlaw SC 292 (supra) was not brought to the notice of the Bench, as a result of which a confusion may be created that which judgment is to be followed as both the judgments cover almost identical area of controversy.
2. Therefore, it is in the interest of justice that this case may be referred to a larger Bench so that there may not be a confusion in view of the two conflicting decisions of this Court. urgent consideration.
3. Looking to the apparent conflict, the matter requires
4. Let the papers be placed before the Hon'ble The Chief Justice for constitution of an appropriate Bench.
Order accordingly
