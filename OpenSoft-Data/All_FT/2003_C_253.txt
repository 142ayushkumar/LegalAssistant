Shambhu Investment Private Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax
Supreme Court of India

21 January 2003
C.A. Nos. 6459-6460 of 2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
We have heard the learned counsel for the parties.
1.  We see no reason to interfere with the conclusion arrived at by the High Court on the question as framed under section 256(2) of the Income-tax Act, 1961. The civil appeals are accordingly dismissed.
There shall be no order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed.
