Kamruddin N. Shaikh v Divisional Forest Officer and Others
Supreme Court of India

10 December 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.20934/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Issue notice on Special Leave Petition.
1.  Notice has been accepted by Mr. S.S. Shinde, learned counsel appearing for the state of Maharashtra. Both the learned counsel agree that the case may be disposed of at this stage. We, therefore, proceed to decide the matter.
Leave granted.
2.  This appeal is directed against the judgment of the High Court whereby the High Court has directed the Tribunal to decide the preliminary objections relating to limitation, res judicata and maintainability of the appeal, as a preliminary issues, within two months.
3. It has been stated at the Bar by learned counsel appearing for the respondents that the matter relating to preliminary objections is fixed for hearing on 18th December, 2001. In such a situation, we are of the view
that the Tribunal after hearing the preliminary issues, may also hear the appeals on merits in the same set of hearing.
With that direction, the appeal stands disposed of.
Appeal disposed of.
