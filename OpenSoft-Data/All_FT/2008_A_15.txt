A. Mohammed Yusuf Khan & Ors v K. Muthu
Supreme Court of India

25 February 2008
CONTEMPT PETITION (C) NO.153 OF 2007
IN SLP(C) NO.7057 OF 2007
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. This Contempt Petition has become infructuous in view of the fact that the Report as directed by us vide Order dt.30.11.2007 has already been submitted and the possession of the premises in question has been handed over to the petitioners.
In that view of the matter, this Contempt Petition has become infructuous and is disposed of as such.
2. The contempt notice is discharged.
Appeal disposed of
