Srilekha Ghosh (Roy) and Anr v Partha Sarathi Ghosh
Supreme Court of India

23 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.7908/1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the petitioners has circulated a letter requesting for four weeks adjournment.
2.  Shri R.N. Trivedi learned Additional Solicitor General who has been appointed as Amicus Curiae to assist the Court is indisposed.
3.  The case is adjourned for three weeks. Shri Trivedi will intimate the Registrar the name of an advocate for assisting him in the case.
4. On receipt of such intimation in this behalf, the Registry will permit the said advocate to inspect the record of the case.
The case will be listed after three weeks on a Friday.
Order accordingly.
