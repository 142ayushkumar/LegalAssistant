Kirloskar Brothers Limited v Union of India
Supreme Court of India

11  May  2001
Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No. 8161 of 2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We agree with the High Court that the filing of a writ petition seeking relief of payment of interest on delayed refund of the amount paid by the petitioner (assessee) towards the custom duty is not an appropriate remedy.
The Special Leave Petition is dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
