Haryana Tourism Corporation Limited v Presiding Officer, Labour Court and another
Supreme Court of India

24 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.7538/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  After obtaining instructions, learned counsel appearing for respondent No.2 - workman submits that on the basis of the order passed by the Labour Court and the High Court the workman would not claim back wages.
2. Considering the facts and circumstances, in our view, the impugned order passed by the Labour Court and the High Court re-instating the workman-respondent No.2 does not call for any interference.
3.  However, the order passed by the Labour Court as well as the High Court granting him 50% of back wages is set aside. The petitioner is directed to re-instate respondent No.2 on or before Ist October, 2001 without fail.
Petition stands disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
