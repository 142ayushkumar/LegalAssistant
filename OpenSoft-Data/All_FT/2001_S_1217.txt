State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v Putti Lal
Supreme Court of India

21 November 2001
CIVIL APPEAL No.3634/1998
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  State of Uttranchal has already framed the scheme pursuant to the order dated 1.5.2001 and has filed the same.
2.  Mr. P.N. Mishra and Mr. Rakesh Dwivedi, the learned senior counsel appearing for the State of U.P. state that in the State of U.P. a similar scheme has also been framed, but that is pending approval of the Cabinet. They require two weeks time for the approval of the Cabinet and finalisation of the same.
3. In view of this statement made by the learned counsel, we adjourn the matter for three weeks from today.
4.  We hope and trust that the Cabinet will take a final decision in the matter so that on the next date of hearing approved scheme may be produced before the Court. Counsel for the State of Uttranchal will serve a copy of the scheme on the counsel for the other side. Counsel for the State of U.P. may serve a copy of the same on the counsel for other side in advance.
Put up after three weeks.
Order accordingly.
