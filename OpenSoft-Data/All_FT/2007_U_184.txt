Union of India v Pradeep Shivram Dhond and another
Supreme Court of India

20 April 2007
Special Leave to Appeal (Crl) Nos. 4976/2006, SLP (Crl.) No. 5767 of 2006
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. Our attention has been invited by the learned counsel to two decisions of this Court; namely, a decision of JudgeBench in Collector of Customs, New Delhi vs. Ahmadalieva Nodira (2004) 3 SCC 549 2004 Indlaw SC 193 and subsequent decision of 2-Judge Bench in State of Uttaranchal vs. Rajesh Kuamr Gupta (2007) 1 SCC 355 2006 Indlaw SC 1464.
2. Reference was also made of Section 80 of The Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act, 1985 which reads as under :
"80. Application of the Drugs and Cosmetics Act, 1940 not barred. The provisions of this Act or the rules made thereunder shall be in addition to, and not in derogation of, the Drugs and Cosmetics Act, 1940 (23 of 1940) or the rules made thereunder."
3. In our opinion, in view of the fact that the effect of Section 80 requires to be considered, we grant leave and direct the Registry to place the papers before the Hon'ble the Chief Justice for placing the matter before a 3-Judge Bench.
Order accordingly
