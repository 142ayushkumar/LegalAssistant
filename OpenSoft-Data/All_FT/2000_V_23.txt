Assistant Commissioner of Income Tax v VXL India Limited
Supreme Court of India

8 November 2000
C.A. No. 15424 of 1996
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The appeal has been heard for some time. Counsel are now agreed that the judgment and order under appeal should be set aside and that the matter should now be considered by the assessing authority.
2.  The appeal is allowed. The order under appeal is set aside. It is made clear that the assessee shall be entitled to raise all available contentions before the assessing authority and in proceedings thereafter.
No order as to costs.
Appeal allowed.
