Babua Tazmul Hussain v State of Orissa and Others
Supreme Court of India

8 January 2002
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 3754/2001
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  Issue notice to the State of West Bengal and to the accused persons who are involved in the other connected cases as to why this case should not be transferred to any one of the Courts in the State of Orissa or in the State of West Bengal. Let the State of West Bengal be served through its Standing Counsel and the accused persons in jail be served through the Inspector General of Prisons.
2.  This petition will not come in the way of petitioner to move an application for bail in the trial Court in the light of the amended provisions.
Order accordingly.
