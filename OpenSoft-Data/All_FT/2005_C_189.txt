Indo Rama Exports v Commissioner of Central Excise, Indore
Supreme Court of India

15 April 2005
IA No. 3 in C.A. No. 8664 of 2002
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The civil appeal is treated as on the day's list by consent of the parties.
2.  It is admitted by the learned counsel appearing on behalf of the respondent that this appeal must be allowed in view of the decision in Nahar Industrial Enterprises Ltd. v. Union of India 2004 Indlaw SC 608.
IA No. 3, is allowed.
3.  The appeal is, accordingly, disposed of in terms of that judgment. No costs.
Appeal disposed of.
