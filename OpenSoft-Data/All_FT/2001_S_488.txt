State of Andhra Pradesh and Others v S. Narayana and Others
Supreme Court of India

12 July 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.15219/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  After hearing the counsel for the parties, we are of the opinion that the original application filed by the respondents before the Administrative Tribunal should be disposed of as expeditiously as possible, preferably within a period of four months from today and in the meantime, the order of the High Court shall remain stayed.
The appeal is disposed of in the aforesaid terms.
Appeal disposed of.
