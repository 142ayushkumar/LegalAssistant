Chhagan Lal (D) By Lrs. and Others v Kamal Chand (D) By Lrs. and Others
Supreme Court of India

13 March 2008
in I.A. Nos.6-8 OF 2008 IN CIVIL APPEAL NO(s). 1638 OF 2008
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1.  Two applications for modification/ clarification of order dated 26th February, 2008 of this Court have been filed by both the parties. By order dated 26.2.2008, while disposing of the appeal, this Court appointed Smt. Gulab Bai, widow of Sh. Kamal Chand, as Receiver to comply with the order of this Court and has given the appellant four weeks time to restore the possession of the suit property which was injuncted from being occupied and vacated by the Punjab National Bank.
2.  We are now informed that Smt. Gulab Bai is an old lady of about 80 years and is not in a position to undertake any major physical activity and continue with the possession of the property.
3.  In that view of the matter, in place of Smt. Gulab Bai, we appoint Shri Rishi Gupta, an Advocate of the respondent, as Receiver to comply with the order of this Court. Time to restore the possession of the suit property is also extended by another four weeks from this date.
4. Order dated 26th February, 2008 is modified to the extent mentioned hereinabove and I.As. are disposed of.
Appeal disposed of.
