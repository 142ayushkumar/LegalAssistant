Reema Sethi v Deepak Sethi
Supreme Court of India

25 April 2005
Transfer Petition (C) No. 23 of 2005
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  This petition is filed by the wife seeking transfer of HMA Petition No.806 of 2002 titled Deepak Sethi v. Reema Sethi from the Family Court at Indore (M.P.) to the Family Court at Bhiilai, District Durg, Chhattisgarh.
2.  The only ground made out is that the petitioner wife is unemployed and has no independent source of income. It is also stated that she will be put to extreme hardship if she will be made to go to Indore.
3.  On behalf of the respondent husband it is stated that he is willing to pay all travel and stay expenses on every occasion when she will be required to come to Indore.
4.  We dismiss the transfer petition with the direction to the Family Court, Indore to ensure that on every occasion, when the petitioner wife has to attend the proceedings, the respondent husband pays the travel and stay expenses of the wife.
The transfer petition stands dismissed accordingly.
Petition dismissed.
