Sunita Sharma and others v State of Rajasthan and others
Supreme Court of India

30 October 2001
Civil Appeal Nos. 5550-5551/1998
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. As requested by the learned counsel, Writ Petition(C) No. 293/1999 is dismissed as withdrawn.
2. Mr. Sushil Kumar Jain, learned counsel for the petitioners started his arguments at 3.30 p.m. and was on his legs when the Court rose for the day. The matter remained part heard.
Petition dismissed
