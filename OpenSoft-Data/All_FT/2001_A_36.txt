A.P. Industrial Infrast.Coprn.Ltd. and Anr v United Packaging Rep.By Its Partner
Supreme Court of India

3 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.926/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  On 9th February, 2001 an affidavit was filed by the petitioner-Corporation indicating therein that the market price of the land in dispute is Rs.1200/- per sq.metre and the cost of the plot works out to Rs.20,23,368/-. On the said affidavit learned counsel appearing for the respondent took time to obtain instructions as to whether his client is agreeable to pay that much amount to the petitioner.
2. When the matter was taken up today, learned counsel appearing for the respondent, states that the respondent would pay a lump sum amount of Rs.15,00,000/- to the petitioner for the purchase of allotment of land alongwith shed.
3.  This is acceptable to the learned counsel for the petitioners. The respondent shall deposit the entire amount within four months from today. In case of any default, this order shall stand automatically vacated and the matter will be heard on merits.
The special leave petition is disposed of in the aforesaid terms.
Petition disposed of.
