Rameswarlal Murlidhar v State of Orissa
Supreme Court of India

27 April 1981
C.A. No. 2863 (NT) of 1979. Appeal by special leave from the judgment and order Dt. 26 July 1979, of the Orissa High Court in S.J.C. No. 2 of 1976. The judgment of the High Court is reported in [1980] 45 STC 115
The Judgment was delivered by: TULZAPURKAR, J.
1.  It is agreed on all hands that the question raised in this appeal is covered by the decision of this Court in Maharaja Book Depot v. State of Gujarat 1978 Indlaw SC 100 and also by the ratio of the decision in Deputy Commissioner, Sales Tax (Law), Board of Revenue (Taxes), Ernakulam v. Pio Food Packers 1980 Indlaw SC 1891980 Indlaw SC 189.
The appeal is accordingly allowed with no order as to costs.
Appeal allowed.
