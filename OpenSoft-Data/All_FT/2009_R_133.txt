Bharat Shanchar Nigam Limited and Another v Rakesh Kumar Dubey
Supreme Court of India

13 August 2009
Civil Appeal No. 5397 of 2009 [Arising out of SLP(C) No. 1000/2008]
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Leave granted.
1.  The appeal is directed against an interim order dated 29th August, 2007 passed by the High Court of Judicature at Allahabad in Civil Misc. Writ Petition No.39260/2007 whereby the award passed by the Industrial Tribunal, Kanpur, in favour of the workman was stayed.
2.  Heard the learned counsel for the parties. In the facts and circumstances of this case, we direct that the respondent Rakesh Kumar Dubey shall be paid current minimum wages subject to complying with the provisions of Section 17B of the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947.
The appeal is allowed with the aforementioned direction.
3.  We would, however, request the High Court to dispose of the writ petition as expeditiously as possible.
Appeal allowed
