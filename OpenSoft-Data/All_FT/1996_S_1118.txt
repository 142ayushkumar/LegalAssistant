Union of India and Others v Sukanti and Another
Supreme Court of India

30 July 1996
C.As. Nos. ... of 1996
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1. Leave granted.
2. These appeals by special leave are against the Tribunal's order directing payment of family pension to the widows of certain casual workers, who had not been regularised in service and had died in 1978, 1976 and 1974 respectively. In view of the decision of this Court in Ram Kumar v. Union of India 1987 Indlaw SC 281591987 Indlaw SC 281591987 Indlaw SC 281591987 Indlaw SC 28159 it is clear that no retiral benefit is available to casual labourers of this kind.
3. The impugned order of the Tribunal being contrary to this decision has to be set aside. We order accordingly. The appeals are accordingly allowed. No costs.
Appeals allowed.
