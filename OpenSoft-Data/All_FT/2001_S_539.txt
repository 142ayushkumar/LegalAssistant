State of Bihar and Others v Birendra Narayan Tiwary and Others
Supreme Court of India

11 December 2001
Review Petition (C) No(s).1568/2001 In S.L.P.(C) No(s).9100/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There is an inordinate delay of 164 days in filing the review petition. Apart from this, we do not find any merit in the review petition.
2.  The review petition is dismissed on the ground of delay as well as on merits.
Review dismissed.
