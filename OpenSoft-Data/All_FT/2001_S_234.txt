Shanmugham v State of Tamil Nadu
Supreme Court of India

14 December 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 6727/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel submits that the defence contention was that the moped emerged from a side road into the main road along which the vehicle driven by the petitioner was running. In support of the said contention he invited our attention to the answer given by PW2 in his cross examination that the front wheel of the moped was hit.
2.  In the light of the said factual position brought to our notice, issue notice.
3. In the meanwhile we order the petitioner to be released on bail on his executing a bond in a sum of Rs.3000/- with two solvent sureties to the satisfaction of the trial court.
Order accordingly.
