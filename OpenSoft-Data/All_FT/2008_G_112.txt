Gordhan Singh v Ramdulari & Ors
Supreme Court of India

24 January 2008
CIVIL APPEAL NO. 7098 OF 2001
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. Having heard learned senior counsel for the appellant at length, we see no reason to interfere with the well-merited concurrent finding of facts recorded by the three courts.
2. The appeal, being devoid of merit, is, accordingly, dismissed.
Appeal dismissed
