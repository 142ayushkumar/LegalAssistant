Mudalappa v Khamarunnisa
Supreme Court of India

10 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal(Civil)...CC 5762/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The grievance of the petitioner is that he had not been served with the notice before disposing of the matter by the High Court.
2. If that is so, the order made by the High Court will have to be set aside and the matter will have to be remanded to the High Court.
3.  Issue notice to the respondents as to why the order made by the High Court should not be set aside and the matter is remitted to the High Court for fresh disposal in accordance with law.
Order accordingly.
