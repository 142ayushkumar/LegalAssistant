State of Punjab v Sarup Singh
Supreme Court of India

20 November 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.1444/2001 in S.L.P.(Civil)No.530/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There is delay of 207 days in filing of the Review Petition for which no satisfactory explanation has been offered. Even otherwise, we do not find any merit in the Review Petition.
2. The Review Petition is dismissed on the ground of delay as well as on merits.
Review dismissed.
