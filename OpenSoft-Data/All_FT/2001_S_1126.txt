Purushottam v State of Rajasthan
Supreme Court of India

12 March 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 728/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Heard Mr. R.D.Upadhyay for the petitioner and Mr. Sushil Kumar Jain for the State of Rajasthan. The petitioner is in custody since 12.5.2000 in a pending proceedings and the charges have been framed under sections 498-A, 426 and 325, Indian Penal Code, 1860. All others accused have been released on bail either under section 438 or under section 439. The present petitioner is father in law. The charge-sheet has already been filed.
2. Having regard to the facts and circumstances of the present case, we direct that the petitioner be released on bail to the satisfaction of the learned Sessions Judge, Bikaner.
The SLP is disposed of.
Petition disposed of.
