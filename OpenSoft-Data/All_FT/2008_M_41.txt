Pareed Kunju K. M. (Dead) and Another v M. Madhusudhanan and Others
Supreme Court of India

29 February 2008
Appeal (civil) 1675 of 2008 [Arising out of SLP (C) No. 16729/2006]
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Leave granted.
1.  Having regard to the fact that the appellants have paid the deficit Court fees, the impugned judgment is set aside and the matter is remitted to the High Court for consideration as to whether the appellants herein may amend their claim petition or not.
2.  It is made clear that all the contentions of the parties shall remain open.
The appeal is disposed of with the aforementioned direction.
Appeal disposed of
