Tax Recovery Officer, Vijayawada v Karri Ramakrishna Reddy
Supreme Court of India

10 November 1983
C.A. No. 324 of 1973
The Judgement was delivered by: SABYASACHI MUKHERJEE, J.
1. Heard Counsel. In all the facts and circumstances of the case and having regard to the smallness of the amount involved, we do not think that this court should interfere with the impugned order of the High Court.
The appeal is, therefore, dismissed.
Appeal dismissed.
