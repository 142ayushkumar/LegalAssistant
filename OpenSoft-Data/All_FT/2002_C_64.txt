Collector Of Central Excise, Bolpur v Ellenbarrie Industrial Gases Ltd
Supreme Court of India

21 November 2002
C.A. No. 5535 of 1997, Dt. 21 November 2002
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard the learned Senior Counsel for the appellant.
1. The Tribunal arrived at the conclusion that Carbide (Lime) Sludge arising in the manufacture of acetylene gas is not an excisable item as the Department has failed to establish that it is a marketable commodity.
In this view of the matter, this appeal is dismissed.
Appeal dismissed.
