Union of India v Union of India
Supreme Court of India

18 February 1997
C.A. No. 4988 of 1991, (Arising as an appeal against 1991 (51) E.L.T. 212 (Bom)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We have read the judgment and order under appeal. We are of the view that the High Court was right in holding that plates and black shields are not parts of photocopying machines. No interference being called for, the appeal is dismissed. No order as to costs.
Appeal dismissed.
