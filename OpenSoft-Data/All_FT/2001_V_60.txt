Madhavi Amma and Others v Velu Pillai (D) By Lrs. and Others
Supreme Court of India

26 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.16819-16820/2001
The Order of the court was as follows:
Delay condoned.
Heard the learned counsel for the parties.
Leave granted.
1.  Considering the fact that in absence of the learned counsel for the appellants the appeal was decided on merits, the impugned judgment and decree passed by the High Court is set aside.
2. Learned counsel for the parties state that the parties would file necessary application for taking of the hearing of the Second appeal before the end of second week of January, 2002. The High Court to decide the second appeal on merits and dispose it of within a period of four months from the date of filing of such application.
3.  The order of the High Court in Second Appeal No.618 of 1983 dated 9-10-2000 is set aside. The appeal stands disposed of accordingly.
Appeal disposed of.
