Sakharam Sambhaji Gawli and Anr v Dagdu
Supreme Court of India

3 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.20944/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Upon hearing the submissions made on behalf of the parties and on perusal of the records of the matter under consideration, we do deem it fit and proper to direct that upon payment of Rs.10,000/- as cost the impugned order shall stand set aside and the matter be remitted back to the appellate Forum for rehearing of the same with utmost expedition upon notice to all the parties.
2.  The appellate Forum will do it well to dispose of the matter within a period of 6 months from the date of communication of this order.
3. The cost as assessed above be paid to Mr.Deshpande's client within a period of four weeks from today. In case of non-payment of cost, this order shall stand set aside and the petition shall stand dismissed.
Special leave petition is disposed of accordingly.
Petiton disposed of.
