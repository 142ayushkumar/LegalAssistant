Bhanwar Singh And Others v Moti Singh (Dead), Through Lrs. And Others
Supreme Court of India

17 January 2008
Appeal (civil) 1078 of 2001
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1.  The learned counsel appearing for the appellants submits on instruction that the appellants do not wish to proceed with this appeal as the land in dispute has been purchased by one Bheru Singh, Sardar Singh and Ram Singh by a registered deed of Sale dated 22nd of October, 2007. Therefore, he submits that in view of the aforesaid fact and the dispute having been settled between the parties, the appeal shall not be proceeded with. Accordingly, the appeal is dismissed for non-prosecution. Interim orders, if there be any, stand vacated.
