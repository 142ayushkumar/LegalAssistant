Collector of Central Excise, Bangalore v Wipro Limited
Supreme Court of India

16 February 2005
C.A. No. 5635 of 1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In view of the concurrent findings of fact regarding the classification of the goods in question, and having regard to the further fact that a very small quantum of revenue is involved, we do not deem it fit to interfere with the impugned order.
The appeal is accordingly dismissed.
Appeal dismissed.
