Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited v Ramkrishna Balwant More and Anr
Supreme Court of India

23 February 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.1785/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Having heard the learned counsel for the petitioners in these special leave petitions, we are not inclined to interfere with the order under challenge. The special leave petitions are dismissed.
2. However, having regard to the facts and circumstances of the case and the nature of business carried on on the land in dispute, we direct that the order of eviction shall stand suspended for a period of one year, i.e. till the end of February, 2002, subject to the petitioner's filing usual undertaking within four weeks from today.
Petitions dismissed.
