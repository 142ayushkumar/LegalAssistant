Kailashi Devi and Others v Ram Pansari and Others
Supreme Court of India

7 February 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.71/2001 in S.L.P.(Civil)No.19626/1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There has been an inordinate delay of 297 days in filing the Review Petition for which there is no sufficient reason. The application for condonation of delay is dismissed.
2.  Even otherwise, we do not see any merit in the Review Petition and the same is accordingly dismissed on the ground of delay as well as on merits.
Petition dismissed.
