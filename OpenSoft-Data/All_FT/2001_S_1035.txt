State of Punjab and Others v Ram Gkopal Choudhary and Others
Supreme Court of India

27 November 2001
CIVIL APPEAL NO. 4433/1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In this matter the High Court following its decision in Satish Kumar Duggal and Ors.Vs.The State of Punjab and Ors.- Civil Writ Petition No. 3510 of 1993 decided on May 17, 1994, granted reliefs to the respondents as had been done in Satish Kumar's case (supra).
2. This Court on appeal against the order made in Satish Kumar's case (supra) allowed the same setting aside the order made by the High Court and dismissed the Writ Petition filed by the respondents. We have to follow suit in this case.
3.  Mr. R.K. Kapoor, learned counsel brings to our notice notification No. 16/3/92-2Edu-3/7472 dated March 3, 1999 issued by Government of Punjab, Department of  Education (Education 3 Branch) and makes a claim that this notification enables the respondents to make claims as had been made in the Writ Petition. This notification is not subject matter of consideration in the Writ Petition and therefore scope and the effect of the same was not the subject matter of consideration in that Writ Petition. Hence we do not think it proper to examine this aspect of the matter.
4.  In the result, this appeal is allowed, the order made by the High Court is set aside and the writ petition filed by the respondents is dismissed.
Appeal allowed.
