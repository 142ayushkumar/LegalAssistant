Commissioner of Central Excise and Customs, Cochin I v Alsthom T and D Transformers Limited
Supreme Court of India

24 February 2005
C.As. No. 5505 of 1999 with No. 1113 of 2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  It appears from the order dated 15-2-2005 that the earlier decision in Indian Aluminium Co. Ltd. v. CCE  1995 Indlaw CEGAT 654 following which the impugned order has been passed by the Tribunal, has not been appealed from by the Department.
2. In view of the decision of this Court in CIT v. Narendra Doshi2001 Indlaw SC 20755, we do not entertain these appeals.
3.  The appeals are accordingly dismissed but without any order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed.
