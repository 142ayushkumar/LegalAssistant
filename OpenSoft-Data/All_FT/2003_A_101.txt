S. D. Agency and Another v Andhra Bank and Others
Supreme Court of India

4 August 2003
C.A. No......Of 2000; S.L.P. (C)No. 18932-18935 of 2002
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  On application of the appellant, name of Respondent No.4 is deleted from array of parties.
Leave granted.
2.  The appeals filed by the appellants were dismissed by National Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission being time barred. The Commission dismissed the application seeking condonation of 129 days and resultedly the appeals were also dismissed as time barred. Having regard to the facts and circumstances of the case, we are of the view that the delay of 129 days deserved to be condoned.
3. Condoning the delay, we set aside the impugned order and remand the 1st Appeal for decision by the Commission on merits.
4.  The appeals are allowed. Parties are left to bear their own costs.
Appeals allowed.
