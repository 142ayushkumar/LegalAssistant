K.V. Sreenivasa Rao v P. Nagakumari and Others
Supreme Court of India

6 February 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.126/2001 in C.A.No.5038/1997
The order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We have carefully gone through the review petition and the order sought to be reviewed.
2. We are of the opinion that no case is made out to review our order dated 30.11.2000. The Review Petition is, therefore, dismissed.
Petition Dismissed.
