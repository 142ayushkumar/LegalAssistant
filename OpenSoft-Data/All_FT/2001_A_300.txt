S.K. Dinde v Assistant Engineer (Cxl), Belgaum
Supreme Court of India

14 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.10492/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Heard Shri S.Ravindra Bhat, learned counsel for the petitioner and Shri Mukul Rohtagi, learned Addl Solicitor General for the respondent.
2. On perusal of the judgment of the High Court under challenge and on consideration of the contentions raised by the Learned counsel for the parties, we are satisfied that on the facts and circumstances of the case, the High Court was right in quashing the award of the Labour Court on the ground of want of jurisdiction.
3.  The special leave petition being devoid of merits is dismissed. However, this order will not prevent the respondent from considering the case of the petitioner for appointment in any future vacancy in accordance with law.
Petition dismissed.
