Hi-Beam Electronics Private Limited and Another v State of Andhra Pradesh and Another
Supreme Court of India

16 September 1988
W.P. (Civil) No. 691 of 1988
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Issued rule nisi and heard counsel for the parties.
1.  In view of the principles laid down by this Court in these cases : H. Anraj v. Government of Tamil Nadu, Indian Cement Ltd. v. State of Andhra Pradesh, Weston Electroniks v. State of Gujarat and West Bengal Hosiery Association v. State of Bihar, and having regard to the facts and the circumstances of the case, we quash the Notification G.O. Ms. No. 721, Revenue (S), dated July 1, 1985.
2.  All concerned parties will pay tax at the uniform higher rate. In view of the facts and the circumstances of the case and in view of this order, the past transactions will not be affected. The writ petition is disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
