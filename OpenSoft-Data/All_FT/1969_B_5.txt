B. P. (India) Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax (Central), Calcutta
Supreme Court of India

16 December 1969
The Judgment was delivered by: SHAH, J.
1.  We find that the High Court has not passed any speaking order in disposing of the application. We have held in several cases that the High Court normally would not be justified in rejecting an application for an order calling for a statement of the case, after rule has been issued, without recording reasons in support of the order The appeal is allowed, the order of the High Court is set aside and the case is remanded to the High Court for disposal in accordance with law. There will be no order as to costs.
Appeal allowed. Case remanded.
Appeal allowed.
