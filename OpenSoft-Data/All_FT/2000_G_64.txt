1.  This appeal is directed against the judgment dt. 28th November, 1990, of the Division Bench of the Andhra Pradesh High Court under s. 256(2) of the IT Act, 1961, refusing to direct reference of the following question of law for its consideration:
"Whether, on the facts and in the circumstances of the case, the Tribunal was justified in directing the ITO to deduct interest payments made to the coparceners on the amounts lent by them to the HUF ?"
2.  Since answer to the question is covered by the decision of this Court, we set aside the impugned judgment of the High Court and treat this question as deemed to have been referred for answering in accordance with law.
3.  Following the decision of this Court in CIT vs. Venugopal Inani  and ITO vs. Smt. N. K. Sarada Thampatty 1990 Indlaw SC 4411990 Indlaw SC 441, we answer the question in the negative, in favour of the Revenue and against the assessee.
4.  The appeal is disposed of accordingly. There shall be no order as to costs
Appeal disposed of.
