State of Kerala and Others v Nature Lovers Movement and Others
Supreme Court of India

21 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.4487/2000
The order of the court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  Interim directions dated 31.3.2000 shall continue. Printing dispensed with. The appeals shall be heard on S.L.P. paper-books. Learned counsel for the parties submit that pleadings are complete Additional documents, if any, may be filed by the parties within six weeks.
2. While written submissions have been filed by the Petitioner-State as well as the respondents, Union of India has not so far filed the written submissions. Let written submissions by the Union of India be filed within three weeks.
3. Considering the nature of the controversy involved, the appeals deserve to be disposed of at an early date. We direct the appeals to be listed for hearing in January, 2002.
Order accordingly.
