State of West Bengal and Anr v Nabendu Biswas and Others
Supreme Court of India

7 December 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.7547/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard the learned counsel for the parties.
1.  For modification of the interim order, we are informed that out of 185 employees 157 employees have already received the benefit of the order of the High Court and remaining 28 employees could not avail the benefit. Having regard to the facts, we direct that the remaining 28 employees should also be given the benefit in view of the order of the High Court.
2. The benefit would be subject to the final order that will be passed by this Court. List the matter in February, 2002.
Order accordingly.
