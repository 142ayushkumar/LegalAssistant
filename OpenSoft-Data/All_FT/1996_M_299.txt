Deputy Commissioner of Sales Tax, Ernakulam v Mohan Jewellery
Supreme Court of India

1 August 1996
C.A. No. 1296 of 1988 with C.As. Nos. 4453 of 4455 of 1989. C.A. No. 1296 of 1988 is by special leave from the judgment and order Dt. 26 June 1987, of the Kerala High Court in T.R.C. No. 136 of 1984. The judgment of the High Court is reported at [1988] 71 STC 256 (Ker). C.As. Nos. 4453 to 4455 of 1989 are by special leave from the judgment and order Dt. 10 January 1989, of the Kerala High Court in T.R.C. Nos. 149 to 151 of 1988
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Having heard counsel for the appellants, we are satisfied that the High Court was right in not interfering with the finding of the Appellate Tribunal since the Appellate Tribunal had found as a fact that no manufacturing process had taken place.
2.  The civil appeals are dismissed. There shall be no order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed.
