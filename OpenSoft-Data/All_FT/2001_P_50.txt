Padmavati Devadatta Kamat v Vijaykumar Narayan Mehandale and Another
Supreme Court of India

11 September 2001
C.A. No. 7684/95
The Order fo the Court was as follows:
1.  Mr. Bhat, learned senior counsel appearing for the appellant states that if the respondents are willing to give one year's time to vacate the suit premises, he will not press this appeal. Mr. Ganpule, learned senior counsel appearing for respondents, says that he is willing to give one year's time provided all the legal representatives of Devadatta Kamat file an undertaking in this Court on the usual terms.
On this basis the appeal is disposed of as not pressed.
2. We grant one year's time to the appellant to vacate the suit premises on the condition that all the legal representatives of Devadatta Kamat, the original tenant, file an undertaking in the usual terms in the Registry within four weeks from today.
Appeal disposed of.
