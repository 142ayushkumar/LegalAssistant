Gobind Sugar Mills Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax
Supreme Court of India

16 July 1997
C.A. No. 20 of 1985 and C.A. No. 123 of 1989
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Civil Appeal No. 123 of 1989: In this case the expenditure relates to the acquisition of a leasehold right for setting up of a sugar factory. The expenditure is clearly of a capital nature.
2. The appeal is dismissed. There shall be no order as to costs Civil Appeal No. 20 of 1985: In view of the order passed in C. A. No. 123 of 1989, this appeal is dismissed.
3. There shall be no order as to costs.
Appeal dismissed.
