U.P.Rajya Vidyut Utpadan Nigam Limited and Anr v U.P. Bijli Karmchari Sangh and Others
Supreme Court of India

17 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.7511/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard the learned counsel for the parties.
1.  Learned counsel for the petitioners states that after the impugned order petitioners have filed application for modification of the said order.
2.  In our view, as application for modification of the impugned order is filed this petition against the interim order is not required to be entertained.
3. Operation of the impugned order is stayed till the modification application is heard and decided in the light of the judgment rendered by this Court in Steel Authority of India Ltd and Ors etc. vs. National Union Water Front Workers and Ors etc 2001 Indlaw SC 20484.
4.  High Court may decide the writ petition expeditiously preferably within three months.
The special leave petition stands disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
