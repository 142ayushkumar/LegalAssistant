Tamilnadu Industrl.Investment Corporation Ltd v Electric Control Equipment Company and Others
Supreme Court of India

17 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.7419/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
1.  Without going into the merits of the issues involved, counsel for the parties are agreed that the interim order passed by the High Court be vacated, as a result whereof the appellant would not be liable to deposit any money.
2. If, however, a decree is passed and sustained against the appellant, it will be bound by the same subject to its right of appeal.
This appeal is disposed of in the aforesaid terms.
Appeal disposed of.
