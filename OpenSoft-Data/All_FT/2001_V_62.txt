State of Karnataka v Venkatagiri Yappa and Others
Supreme Court of India

7 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.11975-11977/1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  So far as respondent No.1 is concerned, issue notice to the proposed legal representatives. In regard to respondent No.2, inasmuch as, he died before the order of the learned Single Judge was passed, his application cannot be entertained at this stage. The petitioner to approach the High Court to have the cause title amended.
2. Thereafter, he can revive the petition here in regard to Respondent No.2 I.A. with regard to respondent No.1 is allowed and I.A. with regard to respondent No.2 is dismissed.
List the matters after 8 weeks.
Order accordingly.
