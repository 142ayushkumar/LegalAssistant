Ritika Bhatia v Rishi Bhatia and Anr
Supreme Court of India

5 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 2079/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the parties have filed a joint application (CRL MP No.12980/2001) wherein it has been stated that husband and wife have settled their dispute outside the Court and also submitted that the FIRs lodged by the petitioner be set aside and a decree of divorce by mutual consent between the parties be granted.
2. In our view, it would be open to the parties to approach the trial court for decree of divorce by mutual consent and also seek leave to drop the proceedings on the basis of the FIRs. Special leave petition stands disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
