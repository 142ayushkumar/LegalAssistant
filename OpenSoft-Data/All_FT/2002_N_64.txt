Commissioner of Customs (Import), Mumbai v National Organic Chemical Industries Limited
Supreme Court of India

20 February 2002
C.As. No. 6764 of 1999 with Nos. 1098 and 2877 of 2001
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  We have heard learned counsel for the appellant, perused the orders of the Commissioner and the Tribunal.
2. We are satisfied that no interference with the orders of the Tribunal, which are under challenge, is required.
The civil appeals are dismissed.
No order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed.
