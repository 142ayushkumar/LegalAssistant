Ghanshyam Dass Jain and another v State of Uttar Pradesh
Supreme Court of India

27 April 1981
W.P. (Criminal) No. 1921 of 1981
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  It will meet the ends of justice if we dispose of the writ petition as under which we do after hearing counsel on either side.
2.  As soon as petitioners are arrested, we direct that they be forthwith produced before the appropriate Magistrate and be released on bail in the sum of Rs 2000 with two sureties each in the like amount. In view of this it is not necessary to decide the question of constitutionality raised in the petition.
The writ petition is disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
