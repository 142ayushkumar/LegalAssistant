Bhagwan Shri Bhagwan v State of Haryana
Supreme Court of India

30 July 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 813/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  Without expressing any opinion on the merits of the case, it appears to us that it is a fit case in which the appellant, who has been in custody since November 18, 1998, be released on bail pending trial.
2. We, accordingly, direct the learned Sessions Judge to release the appellant on bail on furnishing bail bond in the sum of Rs. 10, 000/- with two sureties of the like amount to the satisfaction of the learned Sessions Judge. The appeal is disposed of.
Appeal disposed of.
