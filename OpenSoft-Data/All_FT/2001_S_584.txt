Dinesh Kumar and Another v State of Bihar
Supreme Court of India

24 September 2001
S.L.P. (Cr.) No. 2511 of 2001
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1.  Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner and Mr. Chowdhary appearing for the complainant - Customs Authorities.
2. Having regard to the nature of the offence for which the petitioner is facing trial and having taken into account the fact that the petitioner is already in custody for more than a year, we direct that the petitioner be released on bail to the satisfaction of the Special Court of Economic Offences, Muzaffar Pur. The Special Leave Petition stands disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
