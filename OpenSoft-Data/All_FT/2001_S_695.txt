State of Haryana and Others v Devender Kumar Malik
Supreme Court of India

30 March 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.12785/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the petitioners refers to and relies upon the decision of this Court in State of Haryana vs. Haryana Veterinary and AHTS Association and Another while counsel for the respondent relies upon the decision of this Court in C.A. Nos. 5740-41/1997 and connected matters, dated 31st October, 2000. Both the decisions are of a Bench presided over by Hon'ble Mr. Justice G.B. Pattanaik.
2.  It would, in our opinion, be more appropriate if this matter is also listed before a Bench of which Hon'ble Mr. Justice G.B. Pattanaik is a Member. Ordered accordingly.
Order accordingly.
