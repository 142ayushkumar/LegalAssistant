Sameer v State of Goa
Supreme Court of India

16 August 2001
Review Petition(Cr.) No. 472/2001 in S.L.P.(Cr.)No. 1979/1997
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There has been an inordinate delay of 1311 days in filing the Review Petition for which there is no sufficient explanation.
2. Even on merits, we do not find any case for our interference.
3.  The Review Petition is dismissed on the ground of delay as well as on merits.
Review dismissed.
