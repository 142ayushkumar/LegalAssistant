Mridul Dhar (Minor) and another v Union of India and others
Supreme Court of India

17 September 2008
IAs Nos. 23-25 and No. ... of 2008 in W.P. (C) No. 306 of 2004
The Order of the Court was as follows:
IA No. 23 of 2008.
1.  Place the application tomorrow i.e. 18-9-2008, at 3.50 p.m. before a Bench comprising of the Hon'ble Mr. Justice B.N. Agrawal, Hon'ble Mr. Justice Harjit Singh Bedi and Hon'ble Mr. Justice G.S. Singhvi.
IAs Nos. 24, 25 and IA No.... of 2008.
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
2.  It appears that the deadline fixed in Mridul Dhar v. Union of India 2005 Indlaw SC 40, could not be adhered to because of unprecedented situation in the State of Jammu and Kashmir.
3.  In view of this, as a special case, we extend the time for completing the process for admission in MBBS and BDS courses within the State of Jammu and Kashmir up to 10-10-2008. Interlocutory applications are, accordingly, disposed of.
Order accordingly.
