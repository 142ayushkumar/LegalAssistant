Bharat Coking Coal Limited v Collector of Central Excise, Patna
Supreme Court of India

18 September 1998
C.A. No. 4973 of 1998 with No. 4975 of 1998 and No. 4974 of 1998
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Delay condoned.
Leave granted.
1.  Without intending this order to be treated as a precedent, and having regard to the Exemption Notifications, we reduce the amount from Rs. 60 lacs (fixed by the CEGAT) to Rs. 5 lacs. CEGAT is directed to restore the appeals and hear them on merits.
2. The time for making the deposit of the amount indicated above is extended by two months from today.
The appeals are disposed of in the manner indicated above.
Appeals disposed of.
