M.Thirunavukkarasu v U.O.I
Supreme Court of India

6 November 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.1333/2001 in S.L.P.(Civil)No.17021/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There is an inordinate delay of 322 days in filing the review petition. The explanation offered in the application seeking condonation of delay is neither reasonable nor satisfactory. The application seeking condonation of delay is dismissed.
2. Even otherwise, we find no merit in the review petition. Accordingly, the review petition is dismissed both on the ground of delay and on merits.
Petition dismissed.
