Chhagan Haribhai Kuchhadia Langdo v State of Gujarat
Supreme Court of India

19 October 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 2352/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  Considering the averments made in paragraph 4 of the affidavit filed on behalf of the State of Gujarat wherein it is averred, inter alia, that the petitioner is a patient of Hypertension and for that he is getting all necessary facilities in the Civil Hospital, Ahmedabad, we are not inclined to grant the petitioner's prayer for bail at present.
2. The High Court is requested to dispose of the Criminal Appeal No.634/98 filed by the petitioner expeditiously, if possible within six months.
3.  If the appeal is not disposed of within the said period it will be open to the petitioner to file a fresh application for bail before this Court stating relevant facts and change in circumstances, if any.
The SLP is dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
