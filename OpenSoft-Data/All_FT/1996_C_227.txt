Commissioner of Income Tax v Apar Limited
Supreme Court of India

20 February 1996
C.A. No........ of 1996 arising out of S.L.P. No. 13353 of 1992
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  In our opinion, the point involved relating to s. 80K of the IT Act, 1961, is covered by the decision in Union of India vs. Coromandel Fertilizers Ltd. 1975 Indlaw SC 361 in favour of the assessee and against the Revenue.
2. We are unable to accept the contention of the Revenue that the decision in Union of India vs. Coromandel Fertilizers Ltd. (supra), requires reconsideration as it is no longer good law because of the subsequent decisions of this Court in CIT vs. Patiala Flour Mills Co. (P) Ltd. 1978 Indlaw SC 5141978 Indlaw SC 514 and Rajapalayam Mills Ltd. vs. CIT 1978 Indlaw SC 2181978 Indlaw SC 218  by a Bench of three learned Judges pertaining to s. 80J of the IT Act, 1961
The appeal is dismissed. No costs.
Appeal dismissed.
