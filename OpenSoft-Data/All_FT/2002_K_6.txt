K. L. Kunjappan v Rafeeque and Another
Supreme Court of India

12 August 2002
Cr.A. No. 821 of 2002 (Arising out of S.L.P. (Cr.) No. 1665 of 2002).
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
1.  It is stated by the learned counsel for the respondent that the money due under the cheque has already been received by the respondent.
2. In this view of the matter, the sentence awarded is reduced to the period already undergone.
This appeal is disposed of in the aforesaid terms.
Appeal disposed of.
