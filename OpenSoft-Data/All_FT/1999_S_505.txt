Arjun Flour Mills v State of Orissa and others
Supreme Court of India

6 October 1999
C.As. No. 8763 of 1994 with No. 8909 of 1994
The Order of the Court was as follows:
We have read the referral order.
1.  We find that the decision of a Constitution Bench in S. Kodar v. State of Kerala 1974 Indlaw SC 81 will also have to be considered.
2. Therefore, a reference to a Bench of seven learned Judges becomes necessary. The papers and proceedings may be placed before the Hon'ble Chief Justice for appropriate directions.
Order accordingly.
