Jai Shakti Traders v State of Uttar Pradesh
Supreme Court of India

8 September 1994
C.A. No. 3938(NT) of 1983
The Order of the Court was as follows:
We are unable to see any substance in this appeal.
1.  It cannot be said that the shoe tips, heels, tip toes, tip nails made of iron or steel are "plates both plain and chequered in all qualities" within the meaning of sub-section (vii) of clause (iv) of Section 14 of the Central Sales Tax Act as amended by the  Central Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1962. The appeal accordingly fails and is dismissed with no order as to costs.
Appeal Dismissed.
