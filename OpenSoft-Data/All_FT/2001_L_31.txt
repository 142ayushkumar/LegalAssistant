Lalita Agrawal v Sawarmal Jhunjhunwala (D) By Lrs. and Others
Supreme Court of India

19 February 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.11445-11446/2000
The order of the court was as follows:
Delay condoned.
1.  In reply to the substitution application, we are informed that the concerned respondent No.5 has died when the matter was pending in the High Court itself and as per counter, the second appeal itself abated in the High Court.
2. On the other hand learned counsel for the petitioner states since his client was not aware, he seeks liberty to move the High Court for moving the substitution and for setting aside the abatement.
3.  With the said observation the present Special Leave Petition is disposed of with liberty to proceed after the said matter is decided in accordance with law.
Petition disposed of.
