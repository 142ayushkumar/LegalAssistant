Mohd. Haleem v State of Madhya Pradesh
Supreme Court of India

14 December 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 2354/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
We have heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  In compliance with our order dated 27.11.2001 the Registrar General of the High Court of Madhya Pradesh has informed the Registry of this Court by the letter dated 10.12.2001 that in compliance with the Court's Order dated 6.9.99 paper book in Criminal Appeal No.274/99 has been prepared and the case has been included in the weekly list of Final Hearing cases for the week commencing 3.12.2001.
2. In view of the information received from the High Court of Madhya Pradesh no further orders is necessary to be passed in this matter.
The special leave petition is disposed of.
Petition disposed of.
