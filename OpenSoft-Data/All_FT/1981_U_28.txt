Ana Carelina D'Souza and another v Union of India and Others
Supreme Court of India

7 April 1981
Writ Petns. Nos. 821 and 822 of 1981
The Judgment was delivered by: CHINNAPPA REDDY, J.
In both these petitions it is admitted by the learned counsel for the respondents that the documents upon which grounds of detention are based were not supplied to the detenus along with the grounds of detention.
1. In view of the long line of decisions of this court, we have no option but to direct the detenus to be set at liberty forthwith. It is so ordered.
Petitions allowed.
