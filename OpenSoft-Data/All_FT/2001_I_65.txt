State of Haryana and Anr v Ishar Dass
Supreme Court of India

5 January 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.17533/1999
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Leave granted.
1.  On 18.11.1999 when the matter came up for preliminary hearing this Court directed to issue of notice to show cause as to why the order made by the High Court should not be remanded to the High Court for fresh consideration in view of the decision in C.A. Nos. 5900-5901/1997- State of Haryana & Anr.Vs.Dr. Nirmala Mittal & Anr. decided on 29.8.1997.
2.  Following the said decision and for the reasons stated therein the order made by the High Court is set aside and the matter is remanded back to the High Court for fresh consideration in accordance with law. The appeal is allowed.
Appeal allowed.
