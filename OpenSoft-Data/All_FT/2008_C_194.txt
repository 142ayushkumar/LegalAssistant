Commissioner Trade Tax, Uttar Pradesh and another v Precise Laboratories Limited
Supreme Court of India

29 April 2008
C.A. No(s). 5856 of 2002 with Transfer Petition(C) No. 501 of 2003
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  It is an admitted position that the point involved in Civil Misc. Writ Petition No.860(MB)/1998 titled M/s Precise Laboratories Ltd. vs. Commissioner Trade Tax, UP 1999 Indlaw ALL 214, pending in the High Court of Allahabad at Lucknow Bench, the transfer of which is sought to this Court by filing the present transfer petition, is inter-linked with the point involved in Civil Appeal No.5856 of 2002 pending in this Court between the same parties.
2. It is also an admitted position that some of the period in the afore-mentioned writ petition pending in the High Court overlaps with the period in question in the Civil Appeal.
3.  In this view of the matter, we are of the opinion that it would be in the interest of justice to transfer the afore-mentioned writ petition from the High Court of Allahabad at Lucknow Bench to this Court and to be heard along with the present civil appeal.
4. Accordingly, Transfer Petition No.501 of 2003 is allowed and Civil Misc. Writ Petition No.860(MB)/1998 titled M/s Precise Laboratories Ltd. vs. Commissioner Trade Tax, UP 1999 Indlaw ALL 214, pending in the High Court of Allahabad at Lucknow Bench is ordered to be transferred to this Court.
5.  Registry is directed to send for the record of the afore-mentioned writ petition from the High Court of Allahabad at Lucknow Bench and on receipt and after assigning the number to the aforesaid case, the same be tagged with Civil Appeal No.5856 of 2002 for hearing and disposal.
Order accordingly.
