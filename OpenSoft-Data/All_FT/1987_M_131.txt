Dinesh Kumar and others v Moti Lal Nehru Medical College, Allahabad and others
Supreme Court of India

30 April 1987
CMP No. 10268 of 1987 in W.P. Nos. 348-352 of 1985
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  It appears that the admission examination held by the applicant Institute of Medical Sciences, Banaras Hindu University, Varanasi, is based on the principle that there is no reservation or limitation in regard to the domicile of the applicant seeking admission to the medical courses of the Institute.
2. The Institute prays for exemption from the Scheme envisaged by the order of this Court dated 21-7-1986. Exemption has already been granted in similar circumstances to the All India Institute of Medical Sciences, New Delhi, the Armed Forces Medical College, Poona and the Post Graduate Institute of Medical Sciences, Chandigarh.
3.  In the circumstances, we grant this application and exempt the applicant from the operation of the Scheme aforesaid.
Application allowed.
