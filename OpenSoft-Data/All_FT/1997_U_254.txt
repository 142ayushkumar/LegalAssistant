Indian Legal and Economic Forum v Union of India
Supreme Court of India

7 April 1997
W.P. (C) No. 151 of 1997
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The questions of law raised in this petition under Article 32 of the a Constitution of India are in the abstract.
2.   It would be more appropriate to examine the same in a case in which the question/questions arise on the facts thereof. There is thus no occasion to entertain this writ petition at the behest of the NGO to consider the validity of an enactment in the abstract.
The writ petition is dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
