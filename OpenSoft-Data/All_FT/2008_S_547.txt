Sunil Kundu & Anr. v State Of Jharkhand
Supreme Court of India

7 July 2008
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Crl) No(s).214/2008
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. Leave granted.
2. Hearing expedited.
3. Original records be called for.
4. The prayer for grant of bail at this stage is refused. In the event, the appeal is not heard within six months from this date, it will be open for the appellants to renew their prayer for grant of bail.
Order accordingly
