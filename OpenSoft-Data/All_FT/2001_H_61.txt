Jaswinder Kaur v Harish Chaudhary
Supreme Court of India

20 April 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.14713/2000
The order of the court was as follows:
Leave granted.
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  The present appeal is directed against the order of the High Court confirming the order passed by the trial Court wherein ex-parte proceedings of eviction have been passed against the appellant.
2. Looking into the facts and circumstances of this case, we feel it appropriate that the matter may be contested on merits and ex-parte order be set aside.
3.  Accordingly, the appeal succeeds and the impugned order passed by the High Court and trial Court is hereby set aside and the case is remanded back to the trial Court for deciding the case on merits.With the abovesaid observations, the appeal is disposed of. Parties to bear their own costs.
Appeal allowed.
