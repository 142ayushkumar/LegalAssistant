State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v Gaya Singh and Others
Supreme Court of India

2 March 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.13571-13574/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
1.  After hearing the learned counsel for the parties, the decision of the High Court is modified to the extent that the appellants shall consider the case of the respondents for promotion in accordance with the rules and take a decision within eight weeks from today.
2.  In case the respondents are not considered fit for promotion, reasons with respect thereof shall be given.
The appeals are disposed of, accordingly.
Appeals disposed of.
