Messrs Hawkins Cookers Limited v Contract Laghu Udyog Kamgar Union and Others
Supreme Court of India

8 October 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal(Civil)...6944/2001
The order of the Court was as follows:
Delay condoned.
1.  Heard Shri Ashok H. Desai learned senior counsel for the petitioner. He contends that in the impugned order direction has also been issued to respondent No.3, the petitioner herein, not to terminate the servicaes of the employees engaged as contract labourers.
2. The said direction affects the petitioner prejudicially as the Company is not the employer of the contract labourers. If that is so then the petitioner is not affected by the direction .
With these observations the SLP is dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
