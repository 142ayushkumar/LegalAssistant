Pgf Limited and Others v Union of India and Others
Supreme Court of India

1 October 2004
Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No(s). 19748/2004 (From the Judgment and Order Dt. 26.7.2004 In W.P. No. 188/2003 of High Court of Punjab and Haryana At Chandigarh : (2004) 4 Comp Lj 288 (P&H)
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Leave granted.
1. We extend time by ten weeks to comply with the direction given in the impugned judgment pertaining to the joint venture scheme.
No orders on the stay at present.
2. We, however, clarify that the above does not mean that the petitioners can stall proceedings under the Securities and Exchange Board of India Act, 1992.
Order accordingly.
