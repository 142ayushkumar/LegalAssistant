Union of India and Others v High Court of Sikkim Through Registrar and Another
Supreme Court of India

13 December 2001
CIVIL APPEAL NO. 941 OF 1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the parties state that the impugned order has to be set aside.
2.  In that view of the aforesaid statement, the judgment under challenge is set aside. The appeal is allowed. There shall be no order as to costs.
3. However, we would like to add here is that the kind of order that has been passed by the learned Judge ought not to have been passed. The suo moto proceedings initiated by the learned Judge stand quashed.
Appeal allowed.
