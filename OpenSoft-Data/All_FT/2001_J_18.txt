J.P.S. Mahla v Union of India and Others
Supreme Court of India

1 October 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.16298/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Mr. M.N. Krishnamani, learned senior counsel, after addresing arguments for some time, submitted that the petitioner would be advised to move an application before the Division Bench of the High Court for an early hearing of the LPA. Mr. Mukul Rohtagi, learned Additional Solicitor General appearing for the Union of India submitted that if any such motion is made, that would not be opposed.
2. In the light of this development, learned senior counsel submits that the special leave petition can be dismissed as withdrawn. It is, accordingly, dismissed as withdrawn.
Petition dismissed.
