Gurdev Singh and Anr v Mohd. Abdul Razak
Supreme Court of India

13 March 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.246/2001 in S.L.P.(Civil)No.14583/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Delay condoned.
Review Petition is dismissed.
1. We have perused the Petition for review and the relevant documents annexed to it. We find no merit in the petition.
2.  Accordingly, the prayer for review of our order dated 22.9.2000 is refused and the petition is dismissed.
Review dismissed.
