Shikarmal Bhansali v S.N. Srinivasa
Supreme Court of India

29 January 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.8710-8711/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We are not inclined to interfere with the matter since the same involves a question of fact. In any event the provisions of Section 21(1)(p) of the Karnataka Rent Control Act are otherwise categorical and is applicable in the contextual facts. As such the petition is dismissed.
2.  The petitioner, however, would be at liberty to continue to stay in the premises in question upto 30.4.2001 subject to his filing an usual undertaking in this Court within a periof of four weeks from the date hereof failing which the extension of time granted shall stand withdrawn.
3.  The respondent/landlord shall be entitled to arrears which should be paid within a period of six weeks from the date hereof as also the current rent.
Petition dismissed.
