Executive Officer, Tirumala Tirupati D and Another v A. Vasudevaiah
Supreme Court of India

13 July 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.10350/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that though the question that the petitioner does not fall under the category of the teaching staff of the Educational Institutions' within the meaning of proviso to sub-rule (1) to Rule 9 of the TTD Employees Service Rules, 1989 was urged but it was not dealt with by the High Court, and therefore he would advise the petitioner to approach the High Court by way of review and on that ground he seeks permission to withdraw the Special Leave Petition.
The Special Leave Petition is dismissed as withdrawn.
Petition dismissed.
