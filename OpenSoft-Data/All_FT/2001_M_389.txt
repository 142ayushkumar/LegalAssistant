Messrs Scarlet Flowers and Agritech Limited v Messrs Oriental Floratech India Limited and Others
Supreme Court of India

16 April 2001
C.A..No.5801/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Mr. L.N. Rao, learned Sr. Counsel appearing for the appellant seeks leave to withdraw this appeal saying that the appellant will take steps for appointment of an arbitrator in pursuance of agreement and in accordance with law.
This appeal is dismissed as withdrawn.
Appeal dismissed.
