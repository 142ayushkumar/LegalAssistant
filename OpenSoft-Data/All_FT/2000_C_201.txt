Commissioner of Income Tax v Panama Chemicals Works
Supreme Court of India

15 September 2000
C.A. No. 5077 of 2000 with C.A. No. 5078 of 2000
The order of the court was as follows:
Delay condoned.
Special leave granted.
1.  After hearing learned counsel for the appellant, we are of the opinion that a question of law did arise. We, therefore, direct the Tribunal to state the case and refer the following question of law to the High Court 1999 Indlaw MP 107,
"Whether, on the facts and in the circumstances of the case, the Tribunal was justified in law in holding that the claim of the assessee under section 80-I is justified even if he had not filed the audit report in Form No. 10CCB along with a return of income?"
The appeals are disposed of.
Appeals disposed of.
