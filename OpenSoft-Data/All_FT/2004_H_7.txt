H. U. D. A v Veena Yadav
Supreme Court of India

18 August 2004
S.L.P. (C)Nos. 15362 of 2003
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There is no proper explanation for the inordinate delay of 447 days in filing the Special Leave Petition. Application for condonation of delay is dismissed.
Accordingly, the Special Leave Petition is dismissed.
SLP dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
