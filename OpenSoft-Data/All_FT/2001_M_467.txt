Moneshwar Borah and Others v State of Assam and Others
Supreme Court of India

19 September 2001
IA No. 9 in C.A. No. 160 of 1992
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There is an inordinate delay of 378 and 565 days in filing the review petition Nos. 1148/2001 and 1149/2001 respectively for which the explanation offered is not found to be satisfactory.
2. That apart, we find no merit in the review petitions which are, accordingly, dismissed.
Review dismissed.
