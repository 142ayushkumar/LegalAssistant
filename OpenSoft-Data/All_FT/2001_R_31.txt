R.C. Lamba v Adarsh Lamba
Supreme Court of India

3 December 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.17363/1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  This special leave petition is directed against the judgment of the High Court of Delhi arising out of a matrimonial proceeding. In view of the findings of fact arrived at, while issuing notice, this Court issued a limited notice to explore the possibility of a settlement. Though the matter has been adjourned a number of times, no settlement has been arrived at.
2.  In this view of the matter, question of examining the merits of the finding does not arise. This special leave petition stands rejected accordingly.
Petition dismissed.
