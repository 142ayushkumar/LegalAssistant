Afzal hussain Haji Abdul Karim and Others v State of Gujarat and Others
Supreme Court of India

7 November 1986
C.A. No. 405 of 1973
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There is an appeal on a certificate granted by the High Court of Gujarat on the question of the views of some of the provisions of the Land Acquisition Act.
2.  This question is no longer open to the appellants as the issues have already been settled by the decisions of this Court.
The appeal is, therefore, dismissed. No costs
Appeal dismissed.
