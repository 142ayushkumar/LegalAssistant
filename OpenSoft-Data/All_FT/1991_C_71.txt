Sri Ramamohan Motor Service v Commissioner of Income Tax
Supreme Court of India

15 January 1991
C.A. No. 1360 of 1978
The Judgment was delivered by: K. RAMASWAMY, J.
1.  Heard learned counsel for the parties. We do not find any error in the impugned order of the High Court dated March 3, 1977. Civil Appeal having no force is, accordingly dismissed. There will be no order as to costs.
Appeal dismissed.
