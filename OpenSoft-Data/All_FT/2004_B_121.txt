Bikas Chatterjee and another v State of West Bengal and another
Supreme Court of India

25 June 2004
W.P. (Cri.) No. D12522 of 2004
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Counsel for the State of West Bengal brought to our notice a communication received from the Government of India addressed to the Secretary to the Government of West Bengal of the department concerned and it appears that the execution of the death sentence of Dhananjay Chatterjee is postponed and some representations also have been submitted to the President of India by certain associations and citizens.
2. As the matter is being examined by the President of India, we do not find any reason to consider this matter at this stage.
The writ petition is disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
