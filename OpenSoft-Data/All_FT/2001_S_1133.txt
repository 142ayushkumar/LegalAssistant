Sunil Surendra v State of Rajasthan
Supreme Court of India

19 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 2647/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  After considering the arguments addressed by learned counsel we have noticed that petitioner stands convicted under Section 307 IPC besides under Section 380 IPC. On the fact situation Section 307 IPC, second limb, would apply and therefore it is an offence punishable with imprisonment for life.
2. The benefits of Probation of Offenders Act cannot be applied in the case of an offence punishable with death or imprisonment for life.
3.  For these reasons we are unable to help the petitioner with the Probation of Offenders Act. Accordingly, Special Leave Petition is dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
