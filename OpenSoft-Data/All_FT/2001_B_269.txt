Bpl Limited v State of Karnataka
Supreme Court of India

19 March 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.8415/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The present Special Leave Petition has been filed against an assessment order. Learned counsel states that despite the judgments of this Court which support the contention of the petitioner on merits, the petitioner has chosen not to file a writ petition in view of a Division Bench decision of the Karnataka High Court.
2. In our opinion, it will be more appropriate for the petitioner to approach the Karnataka High Court and make the submission there. Liberty granted to the petitioner to approach the High Court by way of an appropriate petition and this petition is dismissed as withdrawn.
Petition dismissed.
