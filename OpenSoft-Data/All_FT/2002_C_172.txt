Commissioner of Income Tax v Shaily Engineering Plastic Limited
Supreme Court of India

31 October 2002
C.A. No. 2118 of 2000 (Arising as an appeal from the judgment and order Dt. 16 April 1999, of the Gujarat High Court in Special Civil Application No. 1818 of 1999).
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In the facts and circumstances of the case, we see no reason to interfere with the findings recorded by the High Court.
2.  The civil appeal is accordingly dismissed. There shall be no order as to costs.
Appeal dismissed.
