Hans Raj v State of Punjab
Supreme Court of India

24 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 3015/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard the learned counsel for the parties.
1.  In case of arrest of the petitioner for the alleged offences punishable under Sections 419, 420, 465, 467, 468/471 of the Indian Penal Code on the basis of the FIR No.77 dated 15th February, 2001 at the Police Station Phillaur, District Jalandhar, he be released on bail on furnishing a bail bond for a sum of Rs.5, 000/- with one surety of the like amount on the condition that he would fully cooperate during the investigation.
This petition stands disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
