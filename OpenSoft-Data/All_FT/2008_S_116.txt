Haryana State Agrl.Marketing Board v Sanjiv Kumar
Supreme Court of India

17 July 2008
CIVIL APPEAL NO.157 OF 2007 With C.A.No.3770/2007, C.A.No.4193/2007
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1.  Learned counsel for the respondent, on instruction, submits before us that the award of the Labour Court which was affirmed by the High Court had already been implemented and the back wages of the employee has already been paid.
2.  Learned counsel for the appellant also does not appear before us to press the appeals. In that view of the matter, the appeals are dismissed. There shall be no order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed.
