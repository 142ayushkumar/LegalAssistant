Munnu Lal Seth v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others
Supreme Court of India

26 March 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.4633/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Heard Mr. Gopal Subramanium, learned senior counsel for the petitioner and Mr. R.B.Misra, learned counsel for the respondent No.3.
2.  We are not inclined to interfere with the order passed by the High Court on 23.2.2000. However, we direct that the trial court shall take up the application for interim injunction on 16th April, 2001 without fail and dispose it of expeditiously. We further direct that the parties will appear before the trial court on 16th April, 2001 without further notice.
3. In the meantime, both the parties will maintain status-quo as on date regarding the suit property and will not change the nature and character of the land and structure standing thereon. The special leave petition is disposed of on the above terms.
Petition disposed of.
