Mewar Marbles Limited v State of Rajasthan and Anr
Supreme Court of India

31 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.13872/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  After hearing the counsel for the parties, we are of the view that the Division Bench should have entertained the appeal and decided the same on merits.
2.  We, therefore, allow this appeal, set aside the decision of the High Court and remit the matter back to the High Court.
3. While attachment of the properties of the appellant would continue, there will be no sale of the same pending disposal of the appeal by the High Court.
The appeal be decided, if possible, within six months.
Appeal allowed.
