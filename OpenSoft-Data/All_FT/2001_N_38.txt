Jawahar Sahkari Griha Nirman Samiti Limited v Nageshwari Devi and Others
Supreme Court of India

24 July 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.465/2001 in C.A.No.8784/1995
The order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There is an inordinate delay of 692 days in filing the review petition which has not been explained satisfactorily.
Accordingly, the review petition is dismissed on the ground of delay.
Review dismissed.
