Mahesh Chand Bisht and Another v Union of India and Others
Supreme Court of India

15 December 1997
Writ Petn. (Civil) No. 648 of 1997
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The question whether the petitioners should be permitted to represent the cause which is brought in public interest would be decided on the petitioners furnishing full particulars on an affidavit of their concern and the purpose to agitate the same in the Court. The affidavit would be filed within two weeks.
2. The question of appointment of A. C. would also be considered at that time.
Issue notice returnable on 5-1-98.
Order accordingly.
