Satyawati Devi v Susheela Devi and Others
Supreme Court of India

12 March 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.2054/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the petitioner refers to the affidavit filed by the petitioner on 1st March, 2001 and to the certificate of her advocate dated 19th February, 2001.
2.  Having regard to what is stated in the certificate, learned counsel prays for leave to withdraw the special leave petition so as to enable the petitioner to move the High Court and point out that the two points mentioned in the certificate have not been dealt with in the order that is under challenge.
3. On the application of learned counsel for the petitioner, the special leave petition is dismissed as withdrawn.
Petition dismissed.
