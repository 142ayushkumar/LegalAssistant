Assistant Commercial Taxes Officer v I. C. I. (India) Limited
Supreme Court of India

15 January 2010
Civil Appeal No. 300 of 2010 (Arising out of S.L.P. (C) No. 12329 of 2009)
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Leave granted.
Heard learned advocates on both sides.
1.  We are setting aside the impugned order on the ground that it does not give any reasons whatsoever. We do not express any opinion on the merits of the case. We expressly make it clear that all contentions on both sides are kept open. The High Court will decide the matter de novo in accordance with law.
2.  Accordingly, the civil appeal stands disposed of with no order as to costs.
Appeal Disposed of
