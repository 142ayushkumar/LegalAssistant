Dharam Raj And Another v State of Haryana
Supreme Court of India

17 November 2008
Criminal Appeal No. 1836 of 2008 (Arising out of SLP (Crl.) No.6269/2008)
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Leave granted.
1.  Considering the facts and circumstances of the present case and after hearing the learned counsel for the parties, we modify the order of the trial court as affirmed by the High Court to the extent that the sentence which was awarded to the appellants is reduced to four months.
2.  The appellants shall serve out the remaining sentence if not already undergone.
The appeal is disposed of accordingly.
Appeal disposed of
