Mohd. Rizwan Khan v Director, State Civil Aviation and another
Supreme Court of India

6 December 2004
C.As. Nos. 7897-98 of 2004 (Arising out of S.L.Ps. (C) Nos. 8123-24 of 2004)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  By the impugned order, a Division Bench of the Allahabad High Court referred to some decisions of this Court and held that the facts of the case are covered by the said decisions. It was not indicated as to what was the ratio indicated in those cases and issues which were raised in the writ petition.
2. Without indicating as to how the decisions are applicable to the appellant's case, the matter should not have been disposed of. On that limited ground, we remit the matter to the High Court for a fresh decision in accordance with law. We make it clear that we have not expressed any opinion on the merits of the case.
The appeals are disposed of accordingly.
Appeals disposed of.
