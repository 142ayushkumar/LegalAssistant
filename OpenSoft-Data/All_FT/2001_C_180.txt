Bibhu Ranjan Kumar v Coal India Limited
Supreme Court of India

12 September 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.1135/2001 in C.A.No.1031/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There is a delay of 160 days in filing the petition for review. The explanation offered by the petitioner is not satisfactory.
2. However, on perusal of the petition for review of our judgment dated 5th February, 2001 and the other relevant papers we do not find any merit in the review petition.
It is dismissed accordingly.
Review dismissed.
