Muduganti Goverdhan Reddy v Duvvasi Babu and others
Supreme Court of India

27 November 2015
Civil Appeal Nos. 14022 of 2015 (S. L. P. (C) No. 33829/2015) (S. L. P. (C) No. CC 20699/2015)
(Non-Reportable)
The Judgment was delivered by : Anil R. Dave, J.
1. Delay condoned.
2. Leave granted.
3. Looking at the peculiar facts and circumstances of the case, we are not issuing notice to Respondent No.3, United India Insurance Co. Ltd., who has been saddled with the liability to make payment of compensation to the appellant. Upon perusal of the papers and looking at the facts of the case, in our opinion, a further sum of Rs.2,00,000/- should have been paid to the appellant by way of compensation and, therefore, we enhance the compensation by Rs.2,00,000/-.
4. A copy of this order shall be forwarded to respondent no.3. Respondent No.3 shall make payment of additional compensation but if it is aggrieved by this order, it may approach this Court by filing an application within one month from the date of communication of this order to it so that this Court can reconsider this order.
5. The appeal stands disposed of as allowed subject to aforesaid modification.
No order as to costs.
Appeal disposed of
