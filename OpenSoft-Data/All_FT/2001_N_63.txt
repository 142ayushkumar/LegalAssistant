Narayan Dutt v Subash Chander
Supreme Court of India

20 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.6589/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard the learned counsel for the parties.
1.  The impugned order passed by the High Court does not call for any interference.
The special leave petition is dismissed.
2. However, at the request of the learned counsel for the petitioner time to vacate the suit premises is granted upto 31.8.2002 on usual undertaking to be filed before this Court within a period of four weeks from today and also on the condition that the petitioner would pay Rs.1,000/- p.m.(Rupees One thousand only) as mesne profits to the respondent from the date of the High Court's order.
Petition dismissed.
