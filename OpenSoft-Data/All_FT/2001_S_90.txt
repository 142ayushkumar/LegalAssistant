E. Ravi v Sakunthalammal and Anr
Supreme Court of India

28 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.16203/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the petitioner seeks to withdraw the petition but the petitioner may be given a reasonable time to vacate the premises inasmuch as heavy machinery is installed in the premises. The petitioner is allowed three months time to remove the machinery and deliver vacant and peaceful possession to the respondet and not to cause any hindrances in execution of the decree in the event of possession not being given on or before the expiry of a period of three months from today.
2. An undertaking in the above terms shall be filed by the petitioner in the executing court within a period of three weeks from today. The SLP is dismissed as withdrawn.
Petition dismissed.
