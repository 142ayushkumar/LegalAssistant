Union of India and Others v Manager Messrs Jain and Associates
Supreme Court of India

30 January 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.17531/1999
The order of the court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  Delay in filing the objection application under Section 30/33of the Act is condoned. The impugned judgment and order dated 7-10-99 passed by the High Court in APOT No.858/1998 is set aside andconsequently the judgment and decree dated 28-4-1997 passed by thelearned Single Judge in Award Case No.22/1997 is also quashed and setaside.
2. The appeal is allowed in terms of the signed Judgment. There shall be no order as to costs.
Appeal allowed.
