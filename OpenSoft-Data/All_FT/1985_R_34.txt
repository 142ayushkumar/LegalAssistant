State of Gujarat v Ratilal Hiralal Patel and Others
Supreme Court of India

30 September 1985
S.L.P. (Civil) No. 9469 of 1985 (From the Judgment and Order Dt. 21 February 1985 of the High Court of Gujarat at Ahmedabad in Misc. Civil Application No. 763 of 1984).
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We decline to interfere with the order of the High Court dismissing the revision application, but we direct the State Government to implement all three resolutions within a period of three months.
The Special Leave Petition is accordingly dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
