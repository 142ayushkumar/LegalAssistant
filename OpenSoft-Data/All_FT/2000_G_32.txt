Haryana Vidyut Prasaran Nigam Limited v Gargi Overseas Private Limited
Supreme Court of India

27 November 2000
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.13240/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1. In view of the judgment of this Court in H.V.P.N.L. v. Mahavir, this appeal is allowed and the matter is remanded to the State Commission for fresh disposal in accordance with the law.
Appeal allowed.
