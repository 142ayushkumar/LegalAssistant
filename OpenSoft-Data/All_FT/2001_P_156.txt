Prashant Kumar Nayak v Pravash Kumar Sahoo and Others
Supreme Court of India

9 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.3717/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  By an interim order dated 16th March, 2001 this Court had suspended the impugned order passed by the High Court. It was also made clear that if promotion is given to the petitioner in the meanwhile such promotion would subject to the result of contempt petition pending in the High Court.
2. This order no where directs that the petitioners should be promoted. It does not direct contrary also. In any case, if promotion is to be given to any one, the competent authority is bound to follow the necessary procedure prescribed under the rules and the law laid down by this Court.
3.  To the aforesaid extent the order of the High Court stands modified. Hence, with these observations this petition is disposed of.
Petition disposed of.
