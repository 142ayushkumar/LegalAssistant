Common Cause (A Regd.Society) v Union of India and Anr
Supreme Court of India

17 August 2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned Solicitor General has been awaited even after the list was revised three times. We are now informed by Mrs. Niranjana Singh, learned counsel that the learned Solicitor General is on his legs in a case in Court No.6.
2. So far as this matter is concerned, it is of a vital concern since it concerns spate of railway accidents which have taken place. The petitioner, as a concerned citizen, seeks some directions from the Court for improving the functioning of the railway system so as to prevent such accidents in future.
3.  We are adjourning this matter at the request of learned counsel to 3rd of September, 2001 as assistance of the learned Solicitor General is necessary. It shall come up at 2.00 P.M.
Order accordingly.
