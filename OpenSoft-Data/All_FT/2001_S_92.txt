Messrs Aerocom Private Limited v Sales Tax Officer and Others
Supreme Court of India

31 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.603/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There being no challenge to the validity of Section 5A of the Orissa Sales Tax Act, we must proceed on the basis that levy of surcharge under Section 5A of the Orissa Sales Tax Act is payable.
2.  This surcharge has to be regarded as rate of tax payable under Section 8(2) of the Central Sales Tax Act in view of the decision of this Court in Deputy Commissioner of Sales Tax vs. Aysha Hosiery Factory (P) Ltd.
3.  This special leave petition is dismissed as withdrawn as the petitioner wishes to file an appeal and/or file a writ petition, inter alia, challenging the provisions of Section 5A of the Orissa Sales Tax Act.
Petition dismissed.
