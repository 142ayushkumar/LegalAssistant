Ram Rati Devi v Kamal Kumar Tharad and Others
Supreme Court of India

23 July 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.586/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
Heard the learned counsel for the parties.
1.  Considering the facts and the dispute involved with regard to immovable property wherein it is contended that the property in dispute is a joint family property, parties are directed to maintain status quo qua the said immovable property till the disposal of the suit.
2. It would be open to the parties to approach the trial court for expeditious disposal of the suit.In any case, the trial court would abide by the directions given by the High Court.
The appeal stands disposed of accordingly.
Appeal disposed of.
