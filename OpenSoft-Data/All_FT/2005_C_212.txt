Commissioner of Central Excise, Mumbai II v Messrs Ceat Limited, Mumbai
Supreme Court of India

25 January 2005
Appeal (Civil) 1676-1677 of 2002
The Judgment was delivered by : S. H. Kapadia, J.
1. For the reasons given in our decision in the conjoint Civil Appeals, entitled Commissioner of Central Excise v. M.R.F. Ltd. [Civil Appeal No.1476 of 1999 etc.], both these Civil Appeals filed by the department relating to Dipped Tyre Cord Fabric are also allowed; the impugned judgments and orders of the Tribunal as well as of the Commissioner are set aside; and these appeals are remitted to the Commissioner, Mumbai, for a fresh disposal in accordance with law.
2. In the facts and circumstances of this case, there will be no order as to costs.
Appeal disposed of
