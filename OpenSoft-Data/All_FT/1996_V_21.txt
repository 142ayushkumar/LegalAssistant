Union of India and Others v V.P. Ayyappan and Others
Supreme Court of India

9 September 1996
C.As. Nos. 11984, 11981, 11983 and 11982 of 1996 [Arising out of S.L.Ps. (C) Nos. 10216 of 1990, 2592 of 1994, 1391 and 1392 of 1996]
The Judgment was delivered by: HANSARIA, J.
1. Leave granted. Heard learned counsel for the parties.
2. In view of the judgment of this Court in Union of India v. G. Vasudevan Pillay 1994 Indlaw SC 1421994 Indlaw SC 1421994 Indlaw SC 142 these appeals are allowed by setting aside the impugned judgments, inasmuch as the review petitions concerning those cases have been dismissed today by a separate judgment.
3. The observations made in the judgment relating to non-realisation of the amount already disbursed shall apply to these cases as well.
Appeals allowed.
