Commissioner of Income Tax v Coromandal Cements Limited
Supreme Court of India

12 December 1997
C.A. No. 8741 of 1997
The Judgment was delivered by: SUHAS CHANDRA SEN, J.
1.  Leave granted. No one appears on behalf of the respondent in spite of the notice.
2.  The point in dispute has now been concluded by a decision of this court in Tuticorin Alkali Chemicals and Fertilizers Ltd. v. CIT 1997 Indlaw SC 3320.
3. In view of that the appeal is allowed. The judgment under appeal is set aside. There will be no order as to costs.
Appeal allowed.
