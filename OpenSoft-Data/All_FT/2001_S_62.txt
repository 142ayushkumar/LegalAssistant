S.S. Iyer v Ministry of Water Resources and Anr
Supreme Court of India

21 March 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.314/2001 in S.L.P.(Civil)No.1008/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We have perused the order sought to be reviewed, the order of the High Court dated September 27, 2000, the review petition and the annexures thereto. We do not find any justifiable ground to review our order dated 25.01.2001.
The review petition is therefore dismissed.
Review dismissed.
