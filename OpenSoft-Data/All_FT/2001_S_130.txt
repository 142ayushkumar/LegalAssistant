State of Punjab and Anr v Sant Prakash Singh Gill and Others
Supreme Court of India

21 August 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.766/2001 in S.L.P.(Civil)No.1796/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  From the order dated 29.9.2000, disposing of the special leave petition, filed by the State of Punjab, it is apparent that the counsel had only urged on the question of determination of the emolument of a Government servant, on passing the departmental examination, with reference to Rule 101(2) and the legality of the other directions contained in the judgment of the High Court, had not been assailed.
2. In that view of the matter, question of entertaining the Review Petition does not arise. Besides, the Review Petition is barred by 215 days, for which there is no explanation.
The Review Petition accordingly stands dismissed.
Review dismissed.
