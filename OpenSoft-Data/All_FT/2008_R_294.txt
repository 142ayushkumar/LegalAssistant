Shilpa Shetty v Rikhab Raj Bhandari and others
Supreme Court of India

18  May  2008
Transfer Petition (Cr.) No(s). 165 of 2007
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. Issue notice.
2. Learned Counsel for the petitioner submits that an order police has been passed as part of the investigation, the not go authorities had instructed that the petitioner shall abroad.
3. Petitioner is permitted to go abroad subject to her having appropriate travel documents.
Order accordingly
