Rajkot Municipal Corporation v Lavjibhai Madanbhai Patel and Others
Supreme Court of India

11 September 2001
I.A. Nos. 2-3 In Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.11189/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In I.A.No.2 This is an application for clarification of our order dated 4.8.2000 to the effect that a sum of Rs.15 lacs payable by the petitioner to the respondent relates to relief `A' of the decree. It is not disputed that the aforesaid amount relates to relief `A' of the decree.
2.  We, accordingly, clarify that a sum of Rs.15 lacs which is payable by the petitioner to the respondents relates to relief `A' of the decree. We further modify our contd earlier order and direct that the said sum shall be paid by the petitioner to the respondents within three months from today. I.A. No.2 stands disposed of accordingly.
3.  I.A.No.3 Issue notice. Mr. Anip Sachthey, learned counsel accepts notice on behalf of the respondents.
4.  Learned counsel appearing for the parties are agreed that a sum of Rs.27,88,000/- with interest which is payable by the petitioner to the respondents in respect of relief `D' of the decree, only a sum of Rs. 15 lacs without interest shall be payable by the petitioner to the respondents within three months from today. We order accordingly.
5. Rest of the decree is affirmed. This is in modification of our order dated 4.8.2000. With these directions, I.A.No.3 is disposed of.
Applications disposed of.
