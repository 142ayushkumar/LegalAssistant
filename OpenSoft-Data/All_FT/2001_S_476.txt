Sambasivam v State Rep. By Inspector of Police, T.Nadu
Supreme Court of India

30 July 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal(Cr.).../2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  On 30th April, 2001 this Court passed an order refusing to grant exemption from surrendering. Petitioner has been convicted under Section 302 of the Indian Penal Code and sentenced to life imprisonment and the High Court has confirmed the same in January, 2001. Even today the petitioner has not surrendered.
2. We, therefore, dismiss this Special Leave Petition. At the same time we direct the trial court to take prompt steps to put the petitioner in jail to undergo the sentence passed on him. The trial judge shall report to the Registrar of the Madras High Court who in turn report this court about compliance of this order.
Petition dismissed.
