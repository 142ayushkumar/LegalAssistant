Additional Spl. Tahsildar and Land Acqu.Officer v C. Krishnan
Supreme Court of India

2 February 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.8661/2000
The order of the court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  This appeal is directed against the judgment and order passed by the Madras High Court dismissing the appeal filed by the appellant on the ground of delay of 147 days. We have looked into the explanation given by the appellant herein for condonation of delay and are satisfied that the appellant has made out sufficient cause for condoning the delay.
2. Under such circumstances, we set aside the judgment under appeal and send the case back to the High Court after condoning the delay for deciding the appeal on merits.The appeal is allowed. There shall be no order as to costs.
Appeal allowed.
