Messrs Fancy Handicrafts v Union of India An Others
Supreme Court of India

14 July 1994
W.P. Nos. 170 of 1987, 1498-99, 1101, 1768, 1037, 1661, 1181, 1522-23 and 1729-30 of 1986
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The contentions raised in these writ petitions are covered by the pronouncement of this Court in Writ Petition No. 123 of 1987 (Pankaj Jain Agencies v. Union of India)
2.  For the reasons set out in and following the said pronouncement, these writ petitions are dismissed with costs.
Petitions dismissed.
