Andhra Pradesh State Road Transport Corporation v Income Tax Officer, Hyderabad
Supreme Court of India

9 December 1987
C.A. No. 998 of 1975 (Arising as an appeal by special leave against the judgment and order Dt. 6 December 1974, of the Andhra Pradesh High Court in W.P. No. 888 of 1973)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The question raised in this appeal has now become academic in view of decision of this court in CIT v. Andhra Pradesh State Road Transport Corporation 1986 Indlaw SC 3441986 Indlaw SC 344 and hence the appeal will stand dismissed. There will be no order as to costs.
Appeal dismissed.
