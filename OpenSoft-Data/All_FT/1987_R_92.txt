Roop Lal and Others v Kidarnath
Supreme Court of India

9 January 1987
C.As. Nos. 956-957 of 1985
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1. In view of the judgment of this Court in Jagdish v. Nathimal Kejriwal 1986 Indlaw SC 211 these appeals have to be allowed and are allowed.
2. The suit for pre-emption is dismissed. Shri Rajendra Sachhar contends that the judgment is wrong and that it requires reconsideration. We are not prepared to accede to his request to reconsider the judgment. No costs.
Appeal allowed.
