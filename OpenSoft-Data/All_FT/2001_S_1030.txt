State of Punjab and Others v Harpreet Kaur and Others
Supreme Court of India

13 December 2001
CIVIL APPEAL NOS. 3225-3226/2000
The Order of the court was as follows:
1.  In the facts and circumstances of the case, we do not think that there is any good reason to interfere with the orders made by the High Court under Article 136 of the Constitution.
These appeals are, therefore, dismissed.
2.  The High Court has fixed the period of three months to consider the case of the respondents. The time granted by the High Court shall stand extended further by three months from today. The questions of law arising in this case are left open.
Appeals dismissed.
