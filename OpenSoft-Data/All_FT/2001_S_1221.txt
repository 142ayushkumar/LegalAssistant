State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v Sanjay Singh and Others
Supreme Court of India

17 July 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.755/2001 in S.L.P.(Civil)No.19594/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There is an inordinate delay of 200 days in filing the review petition which has not been explained satisfactorily. Apart from this, we do not find any merit in the review petition.
2. Accordingly, the review petition is dismissed on the ground of delay as well as on merits.
Review dismissed.
