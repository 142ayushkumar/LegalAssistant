Bipil Shantilal Panchal v State of Gujarat and Anr
Supreme Court of India

20 July 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 2523/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1. While granting bail, the court has granted liberty to the petitioner to seek extension of temporary bail, before reasonable period of expiry of 90 days, if the trial is not concluded by the date of making such application.
2. It is further directed that when such an application is made, the same should be decided on merits in accordance with law. Learned counsel submits that he may be permitted to withdraw this SLP and if need be he shall take benefit of the above condition.
3.  The special leave petition is, accordingly, dismissed as withdrawn after recording the statement of learned counsel for the respondent.
Petition dismissed.
