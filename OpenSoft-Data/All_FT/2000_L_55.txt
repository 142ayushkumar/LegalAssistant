Commissioner of Income Tax v Lucas T. V. S. Limited
Supreme Court of India

5 December 2000
C.A. Nos. 4789 & 4790 of 1998
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  We, have heard learned counsel for the Revenue. Despite his very persuasive arguments, we are, on the facts available to us, unable to take a view other than that taken by the Tribunal and the High Court.
The appeals are dismissed. No order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed.
