Haricharan S. Rao and Anr v State of Karnataka
Supreme Court of India

7 January 2002
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 6391/2001
The order of the Court was as follows:
Heard the learned counsel for the parties.
1.  Pending further hearing petitioners have already been granted bail in S.C.No.88/2001 on the file of III Addl.Sessions Judge, Mysore, vide this Court's order dated 9.11.2001. That order shall continue till the trial is over. However, it is ordered that pending trial petitioner No.1 shall report every week on Monday to the police station.
2. He will not leave the State of Karnataka without the prior permission of the trial court.
The special leave petition stands disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
