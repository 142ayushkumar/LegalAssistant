Supreme Court of India

8  May  2013
C.A. Nos. 7045-7046 of 2004 and C.A. Nos. 7539-7540 of 2004
The Judgment was delivered by :
1. It is stated that in Civil Appeal Nos.7045-7046/2004, appellant no.2 (D. K. Audikesavulu) is dead.
2. Learned counsel for the appellants prays for six weeks' time to file the substitution application for bringing on record the legal representatives of deceased appellant no.2 in the aforesaid appeals.
3. Time, as prayed for, is granted. In case, learned counsel for the appellants does not file the said application within the given period, the appeals shall stand dismissed against the appellant no.2. List the matters after six weeks.
Order accordingly
