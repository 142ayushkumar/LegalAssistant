Pariyaram Veetil Narayanan Nair v Kutantakath Abdul Salam and Anr
Supreme Court of India

7 December 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.20923/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
We find no merit in this petition. It is accordingly dismissed.
1.  It was then urged by the learned counsel for the petitioner that in case the petitioner is required to vacate the premises immediately, he will be put to great hardship. On the other hand learned counsel for the respondents submits that in pursuance of the order passed by the Executing Court, the respondents has taken possession on 4/12/2001.
2. However, it was disputed by counsel for the petitioner. Under such circumstances we direct that the petitioner shall not be required to hand over the premises till 30th June, 2002, subject to his filing usual undertaking within four weeks, provided the Executing Court has not already passed an order for delivery of possession of the premises.
Petition dismissed.
