Bhartesh C. Jain and others v Shoaib Ullah and another
Supreme Court of India

30 January 2008
C.A. No. 838 of 2008 (Arising out of S.L.P. (C) No. 15397/2006)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard learned Counsel for the parties.
Leave granted.
1.  Learned counsel for the parties agree that in the light of the fact that the order of the Hon'ble Chief Justice of the Allahabad High Court dated 4th August, 2006 does not meet the issues raised before him, the matter may be remitted to the Chief Justice for fresh decision.
2. We accordingly set aside the order dated 4th August, 2006 and remit the matter to the Hon'ble Chief Justice with a request that the matter be heard and decided expeditiously.
3.  The Civil Appeal is disposed of accordingly with no order as to costs.
Appeal disposed of.
