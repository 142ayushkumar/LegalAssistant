State of Bihar and Another v Usha Martin Industries Limited
Supreme Court of India

25 February 1987
C.A. No. 4534 of 1986
"The Order of the Court is as follows":
1.  The question raised in this appeal stands concluded by the law laid down by this Court in Pournami Oil Mills v. State of Kerala 1986 Indlaw SC 131986 Indlaw SC 13, where the ratio laid down in M/s Motilal Padampat Sugar Mills Co. Ltd. v. State of U.P.  1978 Indlaw SC 561978 Indlaw SC 56 was affirmed.
2.  This is not disputed by learned counsel for the appellant. In the circumstances the appeal is dismissed but without any order as to costs.
Appeal dismissed.
