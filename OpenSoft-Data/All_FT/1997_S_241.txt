Southern Switchgear Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax and Another
Supreme Court of India

11 December 1997
The Judgment was delivered by: SUHAS CHANDRA SEN, J.
1.  We have perused the order of the High Court. We have also seen the agreement.
2. We are not persuaded to hold that the view taken by the High Court is erroneous; the appeals are dismissed.
There will be no order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed.
