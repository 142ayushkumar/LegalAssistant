Sipani Automobiles Limited v T.G. Sheat
Supreme Court of India

2 March 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.11781/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1. Learned counsel for the respondent states that he has advised his client to move the B.I.F.R. It is not disputed that the order under challenge and of the court below cannot stand.
2. Accordingly, the civil appeal is allowed. The orders of the High Court and the court below are set aside.Liberty to the respondent to move the B.I.F.R.No order as to costs.
Appeal allowed.
