Jayendra Awad v Nivedita Sharma and others
Supreme Court of India

30 November 2015
Civil Appeal No. 13935 of 2015 (Arising Out of S. L. P. (Civil) No. 35209 of 2011)
(Non-Reportable)
The Judgment was delivered by : Anil R. Dave, J.
1. Leave granted.
2. In view of the judgment of this court dated 16th October, 2015 in Prakash & Ors. vs. Phulavati & Ors 2015 Indlaw SC 894., the impugned order is set aside and the appeal is remanded to the High Court for a fresh decision on merits in accordance with law.
3. Parties shall appear before the High Court for further proceedings on 14th December, 2015.
4. The appeal is allowed accordingly. There shall be no order as to costs.
5. Pending applications stand disposed of.
Applications disposed of
