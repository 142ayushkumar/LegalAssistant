Prabhakar Baburao Bokde v Bank of Baroda, Nagpur
Supreme Court of India

7 April 2000
C.A. No. 2498 of 2000.
The Order of the court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  We have heard learned counsel for the parties finally in this appeal.
2. The short question is whether the High Court by the impugned order could have disposed of by one word "rejected" the appellant' application dated 14-6-1999 under Section 17-B of the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947.
Order accordingly.
