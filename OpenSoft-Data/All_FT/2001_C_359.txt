Comnr. of Income Tax, Gujarat v Messrs Vijay Estate Corporation
Supreme Court of India

19 January 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.10330/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
None appears for the respondent though the service is complete.
Special leave granted.
1.  After hearing the learned counsel for the appellant, in our opinion the High Court should have directed the Tribunal to state the case.
2. We, accordingly, allow this appeal, set aside the judgment of the High Court and direct the Tribunal to state the case & refer the following question of law to the High Court:
"Whether the Appellate Tribunal is right in law and on facts in directing the A.O. to grant continuation of registration to the assessee firm?"
Appeal allowed.
