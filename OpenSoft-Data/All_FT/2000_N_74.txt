National Seeds Corporation Limited v L.N.Sharma
Supreme Court of India

24 November 2000
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.18477/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the petitioner relies upon the order passed by this Court in Civil Appeal No..... of 1995 SLP(C)No.9102/1989 and submits that the High Court committed an apparent error on the face of the record in deciding the appeal on merits whennon was present for the appellant. He further submitted that the said order is against the expressed provisions of the Code of Civil Procedure.
2. In our view, this petition does not require to be interfered with at this stage. It would be open to the petitioner toapproach the High Court for review, if it is so desired.
In view ofthe aforesaid, this special leave petition is disposed of.
Petition disposed of.
