State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v Messrs Anupam Traders
Supreme Court of India

28 March 1989
Petition for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No. 1220 of 1989 (From the Judgment and Order Dt. 29 July 1988 of the High Court of Judicature at Allahabad in Civil Misc. W.P. No. 14893 of 1988)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  It is reported that the Finance Minister of the Government of India has in the course of his budget speech stated that the cement control has been lifted in India.
2.   In the circumstances, we do not find any reason to entertain this petition since it has become infructuous. The special leave petition is dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
