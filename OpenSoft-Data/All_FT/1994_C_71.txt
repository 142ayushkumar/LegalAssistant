Chetan Metal Supply Corporation v Union of India
Supreme Court of India

1 March 1994
S.L.P. No. 48410 of 1994
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Delay condoned, Learned Counsel for the Petitioner No. 3159/1987 was wrongly placed on board along with other group of petitions where the issue involved was similar to the one which was decided by the Full Bench of the High Court in Bombay Conductor Electrical Limited v. Government of India, 1983 Indlaw DEL 10128. Along with the said group of matters, his writ petition was also dismissed. Therefore he had to come to this Court by way of the present special leave petition. The petitioner should have approached the High Court by way of review pointing out the said order.
2. We, therefore direct the petitioner to approach the High Court in review.
3.  If there is any delay the High Court will condone it. The S.L.P. is disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
