M.R. Vijayan and Others v Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation and Anr
Supreme Court of India

12  May  2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.8271/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the petitioners submits that in a connected matter (SLP(C)No.7477/2001) this Court made the following observations while dismissing the Special Leave Petition;
"Upon hearing the submissions as advanced by Dr.Rajiv Dhawan learned senior counsel appearing for the petitioners in support of the petition, we do not find any infirmity in the order passed by the High Court. In that view of the matter, the special leave petition is dismissed. Time, however, to demolish the structure is extended till the end of July, 2001."
Petition dismissed.
