Comnr. of Central Excise, Surat-I v Messrs Gujarat Insecticides Limited
Supreme Court of India

9 April 2001
I.A.No.3-4 in C.A. No.1849-1850/2000
The order of the Court was as follows:
The operation of the impugned judgment shall remain stayed.
1.  It is, however, clarified that this stay order shall not entitle the applicants to lay any claim for recovery of any arrears of duty on the basis of the circular, which had been struck down by the High Court, as accruing till date.
2. Similarly, the question of refunding of any amount, if already recovered, from any of the members of the respondent shall also not arise. The question with regard to arrears etc shall be decided at the final disposal of the appeals.
Order accordingly.
