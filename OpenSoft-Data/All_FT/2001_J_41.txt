P.S.E.B. and Others v Jagjit S. Sandhu
Supreme Court of India

27 November 2001
CIVIL APPEAL NO. 4302/1999
The Order of the court was as follows:
1.  In the special features arising in these cases, it is not necessary for us to interfere under Article 136 of the Constitution. Leaving the question of law open, these appeals shall stand dismissed.
Appeals dismissed.
