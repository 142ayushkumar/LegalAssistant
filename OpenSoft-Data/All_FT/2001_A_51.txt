Abdul Hai Khan v Subhal C. Ghose and Others
Supreme Court of India

17 July 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.8634/1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In the course of hearing of these cases, Mr.Altaf Ahmad, learned Additional Solicitor General drew our attention to the order passed on 9th July 2001 in SLP(C)No. 5832 of 2001, in which this Court observed that the concerned authority may dispose of the pending application in accordance with law but not to give effect to the same until further orders.
2.  Mr. Altaf Ahmad stated that the concerned authority has not yet passed order on the applications for grant of stage carriage permit and the matter is pending.
3. He suggested that as these cases are similar to the case in which the order was passed; they may be listed after four weeks so that in the meantime the order of the authority will be available and can be placed before the court for consideration.
List all these matters after four weeks.
Order accordingly.
