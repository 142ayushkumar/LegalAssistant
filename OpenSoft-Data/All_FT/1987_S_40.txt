Union of India and Others v Sardar Parduman Singh
Supreme Court of India

14 September 1987
S.L.P. (Civil) No. 7749 of 1987.
The Order of the Court was as follows:
The Special Leave Petition is dismissed with costs.
1.  The Petitioner shall pay a sum of Rs. 3, 000 as costs to the respondent within four weeks.
Petition dismissed.
