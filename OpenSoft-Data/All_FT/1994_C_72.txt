Chetan Metal Supply Corporation v Union of India and Others
Supreme Court of India

7 January 1994
S.L.P. No. 718 of 1993
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Delay condoned.
1.  Learned counsel for the petitioner states that his Writ Petition No. 3159 of 1982 was wrongly placed on board along with other group of petitions where the issue involved was similar to the one which was decided by the Full Bench of the High Court in Bombay Conductors and Electricals Ltd. v. Government of India, 1983 Indlaw DEL 10128Along with the said group of matters, his writ petition was also dismissed.
2.   Therefore, he had to come to this Court by way of the present special leave petition. The Petitioner should have approached the High Court by way of a review pointing out the said error. We, therefore, direct the petitioner to approach the High Court in review. It there is any delay the High Court will condone it. The SLP is disposed of accordingly.
Petition Disposed of.
