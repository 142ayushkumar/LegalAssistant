Rajesh Gandhi v State of Jharkhand
Supreme Court of India

19 October 2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
We have heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  This application has been filed by the appellant for modification of the order of this Court dated 11.10.2001 in which he was granted bail with certain conditions attached therewith.
2.  On perusal of the petition and on consideration of the submissions made by the learned counsel for the parties, the order dated 11.10.2001 is modified to the extent:
(a) that the appellant shall execute a bond for a sum of Rs.2 lakhs (Rupees two lakhs) instead of Rs.25 lakhs (Rupees twenty five lakhs) with two sureties each for the like amount;
(b) the appellant shall be present in Dhanbad by the date of commencement of the trial of the case and shall not go out of Dhanbad without prior permission of the trial court till the trial is concluded. The bail order passed on 11.10.2001 is modified to the above extent only.
The Criminal Miscellaneous Petition No.12618 of 2001 is disposed of.
Petition disposed of.
