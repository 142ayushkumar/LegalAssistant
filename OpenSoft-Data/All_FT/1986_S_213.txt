Aboobacker Alias Kuttitharavan v State of Kerala
Supreme Court of India

8 December 1986
Cr.A. No. 630 of 1986 (Arising out of S.L.P. (Cri.) No. 1463 of 1986)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted limited to the question of sentence.
1.  Having regard to all the facts and circumstances of this case we are satisfied that the sentence undergone by the appellant is adequate.
2. We, therefore, allow the appeal partly and reduce the sentence from one of four years' rigorous imprisonment to one of the period already undergone.
Appeal partly allowed.
