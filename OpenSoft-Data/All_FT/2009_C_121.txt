Commercial Tax Check Post,Ratanpur v M/S.Perfect Thread Mills Ltd.
Supreme Court of India

19 January 2009
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No(s).25047/2008(From the judgment and order dated 27/11/2007 in STR No. 273/2007 of The HIGH COURT OF RAJASTHAN AT JAIPUR) CIVIL APPEAL NO.344 OF 2009 (Arising out of SLP(C) No.25047 of 2008
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Delay condoned.
Leave granted.
1.  The issue is squarely covered by our two judgments in the case of Guljag Industries Vs. Commercial Taxes Officer reported in 2007 (7) SCC 269 2007 Indlaw SC 888 and Assistant Commercial Taxes Officer Vs. M/s Bajaj Electricals Limited reported in 2008 (14) SCALE 380 2008 Indlaw SC 1921, in favour of the Department.
2.  Accordingly, the Civil Appeal filed by the Department stands allowed with no order as to costs.
Appeal allowed
