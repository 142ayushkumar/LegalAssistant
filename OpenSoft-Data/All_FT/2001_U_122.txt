Iqbal Hasan Shaikh Ibrahim Kaskar v Union of India and Anr
Supreme Court of India

20 April 2001
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  The learned Attorney General states that the application of the petitioner for the renewal of his passport shall be considered and decided within four weeks from today.
2.  That being the position, the writ petition does not survive for consideration and it is disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
