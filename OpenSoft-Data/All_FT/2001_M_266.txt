Vijay Gupta v Medicare Systems and Anr
Supreme Court of India

7 February 2001
CIVIL APPEAL NO. 4835/2000  WITH C.A. No. 4836/2000 O R D E R C.A. No. 4835/2000
The Order of the court was as follows:
1.  The short question that is involved in this case is one relating to the interest granted by the State Commission which has been deleted by the National Commission.
2. We do not think that this calls for interference by us. Therefore, this appeal stands dismissed.
3.  C.A.No. 4836/2000 In terms of the order made on 11.1.2001 a sum of Rs.5 lacs had to be deposited by the appellant in the District Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission, Karnal for withdrawal by the respondent failing which the leave granted shall stand revoked.
4.  As the said sum has not been deposited, the leave granted stands revoked and the Special leave petition out of which this appeal arose also stands dismissed.
Appeal dismissed.
