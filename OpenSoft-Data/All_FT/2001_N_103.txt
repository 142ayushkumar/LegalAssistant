National Insurance Company Limited v Bai Kankaben and Others
Supreme Court of India

9 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.18789/2001
The order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Issue notice on special leave petition as well as on the prayer for interim relief.
2.  In the meantime the operation of the order and judgment shall remain stayed, provided the petitioner deposit half of the decretal amount alongwith the interest within four weeks before the Trial Court. In case such an amount is deposited, it will be open to the claimant to withdraw the same without furnishing any security.
3. In the event the amount is not deposited within the stipulated period, then this order shall automatically stand vacated without further reference to the Court.
Tag with SLP (C) No. 7162/2001.
Order accordingly.
