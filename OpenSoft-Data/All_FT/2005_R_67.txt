Kalyan Chandra Sarkar v Rajesh Ranjan Alias Pappu Yadav and another
Supreme Court of India

3 February 2005
Suo Motu Petition No. 5410 of 2004 with Cr. MP No. 1477 of 2005 in Cr.A. No. 324 of 2004 and Cr. MPs Nos. 10422 of 2004 and Nil of 2005 in Cr. A. No. 1129 of 2004
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1. Crl. MP No. 10422 of 2004 in Crl. A. No. 1129 of 2004
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
Judgment reserved.
2. Suo Motu Petition No. 5410 of 2004 in Crl. A. No. 324 of 2004 and Crl. MP No. 1477 of 2005 in Suo Motu Petition No. 5410 of 2004 in Crl. A. No. 324 of 2004 and Crl. MP No. Nil of 2005 in Crl. Appeal No. 1129 of 2004.
3.  We direct the Principal District and Sessions Judge, Madhepura to get order sheet of Sessions Trial No. 93 of 1989 on the file of Additional Sessions Judge, Fast Track Court No. 1, Madhepura photocopied and sent to this Court within two weeks from today.
4.  We further direct CBI to seize the medical records pertaining to the treatment and the place of treatment given to Rajesh Ranjan Yadav @ Pappu Yadav in the Patna Medical College Hospital and produce the same in this Court within two weeks.
5.  Learned counsel appearing for Rajesh Ranjan Yadav @ Pappu Yadav has filed an application alleging that the SLP filed by Kalyan Chandra Sarkar for cancellation of bail is supported by an affidavit not really executed by the said Kalyan Chandra Sarkar.
6. We direct the learned counsel appearing for Kalyan Chandra Sarkar to file his response to the same within two weeks with a prior copy to the applicants also.
Call this matter on 17-2-2005.
Order accordingly.
