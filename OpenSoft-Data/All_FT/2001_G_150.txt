Mohmed Anwar Gharada and Others v Gujarat Housing Board and Others
Supreme Court of India

12  May  2001
I.A.Nos. 2-3 In C.A. No.6051-6052/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We are informed that the petitioner No.3 has died on 7th September, 1999 while the review was decided on 13th December, 1999. Hence the said petitioner has died when the matter was pending before the High Court.
2.  We grant liberty to the petitioner to move the matter before the High Court for the substitution and the effect of the substitution which is prior to the review decided, to be considered by the High Court.
3. List the matter after the decision made by the High Court in case the petitioner move before the High Court within four weeks from today.
Order accordingly.
