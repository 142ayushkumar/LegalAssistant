D.D.A v Bhavanacoop. G.H. Society Limited
Supreme Court of India

24 August 1995
Civil Appellate Jurisdiction : Civil Appeal No. 7932 of 1995
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Leave granted.
1.  Ms. Sangeeta Kalra and Mr. Sudhir Kalra appeared on behalf of the Society. In view of the judgment of this Court in D.D.A. vs. Grihsthapana Cooperative Group Housing Society Ltd. [JT 1995 (2) SC530 1995 Indlaw SC 592], the appeal is allowed and forfeiture to the extent of rupees five lakhs is upheld. The remaining amount would be refunded by the appellant within a periodof four weeks from today, failing which the respondent would be entitled for interest @ 18% per annum from that date till payment. In the facts and circumstances of the case, we make no order as to costs.
Appeal allowed
