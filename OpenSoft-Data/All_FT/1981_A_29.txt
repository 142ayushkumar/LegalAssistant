All India Canara SC/ST Employees' Association and Others v Union of India and Others
Supreme Court of India

27 April 1981
C.A. No. 1501 of 1981.
The Judgment was delivered by: PATHAK, J.
Special leave granted.
1.  It is clear that the order April 2, 1980 made by the Calcutta High Court can operate in respect of those proceedings only which are taken within the jurisdiction of that court.
The order is clarified accordingly.
Order accordingly.
