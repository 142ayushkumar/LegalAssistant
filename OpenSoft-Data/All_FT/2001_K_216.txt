Krishan Gopal v Director, C.B.I., New Delhi
Supreme Court of India

7 December 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 3319/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  During the course of hearing, Mr. Altaf Ahmad, learned Additional Solicitor General produced a letter from the Superintendent of Police, CBI, SCB-I, Delhi, recording therein that CBI has not been involved in the matter in which the notices were issued.
2. Let this intimation sent to Shri P. Parmeswaran, Additional Government Advocate, Central Law Agency, Ministry of Law, be kept on the records and in view of such a statement of the learned Additional Solicitor General, the question of any further inquiry in the matter does not arise.
As such, the special leave petition stands dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
