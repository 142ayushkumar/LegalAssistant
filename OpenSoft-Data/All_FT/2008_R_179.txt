Commissioner of Central Excise Vishakapatnam-II v Ramdas Paper Boards Private Limited
Supreme Court of India

4 February 2008
Civil Appeal No. 1002 of 2008 (D.No. 31023/2007)
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Delay condoned.
1.  Although an appeal involving similar question is pending before this Court but in view of the fact that no revenue is involved in this appeal, we are of the opinion that it is not necessary to admit this appeal as the question involved in this matter shall be decided therein. The appeal is dismissed with the aforementioned observation.
Appeal dismissed
