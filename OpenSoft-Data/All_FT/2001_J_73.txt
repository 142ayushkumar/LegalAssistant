Jangi Ram Kaushal v State of Haryana and Anr
Supreme Court of India

5 October 2001
Review Petition(Cr.) No. 181/2001 in S.L.P.(Cr.) No. 428/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  After hearing the counsel for the parties, the review petition is allowed, the order dated 3rd November, 2000 is recalled and the special leave petition is restored on the file.
The special leave petition is adjourned by two weeks.
2. In the meantime, the respondent-State is at liberty to proceed further in the investigation and in lodging challan in court, but the petitioner shall not be arrested till further orders by this Court Order dated 18.2.2000 is modified in the aforesaid terms.
Petition allowed.
