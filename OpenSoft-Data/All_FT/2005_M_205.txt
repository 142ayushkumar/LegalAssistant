Deepika Alias Tara v Maruthi Kathare
Supreme Court of India

18 April 2005
Transfer Petition (C) No. 852 of 2004
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The petitioner wife has filed this petition under Section 25 of the Code of Civil Procedure for transfer of OP No. 1014 of 2004 instituted by the respondent husband under Section 13(1)(ia)(d) of the Hindu Marriage Act from the Family Court, Chennai to a court of competent jurisdiction at Hubli (Karnataka).
2.  Counsel for the respondent does not oppose the transfer petition but states that the same be not transferred to Hubli as the respondent apprehends danger to his life/bodily harm in travelling to Hubli.
3. After hearing the counsel for the parties and keeping in view the assertions in the transfer petition and the reply filed thereto, we deem it appropriate to direct that OP No.1014 of 2004 titled as Maruti Kathare v. Deepika pending before the Family Court at Chennai be transferred to the Family Court at Gadak, Karnataka. Case record be transmitted forthwith to the Family Court at Gadak, Karnataka.
The transfer petition is allowed accordingly.
Petition allowed.
