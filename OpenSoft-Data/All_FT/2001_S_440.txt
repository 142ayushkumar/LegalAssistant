State (Nct of Delhi) v Sidharth Gupta
Supreme Court of India

17 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 875-876/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  As on today, on the available materials on record we see no infirmity with the impugned order of the High Court to be interfered with by this Court.
The Special Leave Petition is dismissed accordingly.
2.  The trial is already going on. If materials would be available against the respondent - Sidharth Gupta, it will be always open for the learned Judge to exercise power under Section 319 of the Code of Criminal Procedure.
3. We make it clear that this observation of ours is in relation to laying down of law and not in relation to the merits of the case.
Petition dismissed.
