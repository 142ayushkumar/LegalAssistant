Pushpa Jain v Heera Bai
Supreme Court of India

5 January 2001
C.A..No.2543/1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The parties have entered into a settlement and filed I.A.No.4/2000 to record the settlement. I.A. is allowed and the settlement is taken on record.
2.  The appeal is disposed of in terms of the settlement. No costs.
Appeal disposed of.
