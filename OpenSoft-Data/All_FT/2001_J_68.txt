Janab Hayath Badshya (Dead) By Lrs v Krishnaramsa
Supreme Court of India

12  May  2001
I.A. No. 2 in C.A..No.6723/1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  It is agreed by the learned counsel for the parties that in view of the decision of this Court in Gantusa H. Baddi (Dead) By Lrs. VS. Meera Bai G. Pai and Others, the impugned judgment of the HighCourt is to be set aside and the case be remanded to the High Court for decision afresh in accordance with law.
Ordered accordingly. No costs.
Order accordingly.
