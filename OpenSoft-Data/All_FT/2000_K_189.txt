Commissioner of Income Tax v Kumari K. Anita Reddy
Supreme Court of India

15 November 2000
C.A. Nos. 8036 to 8039 of 1995
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Having heard learned counsel, we are of the view that the judgment of this Court by a Bench of two learned Judges in Union of India vs. S. Muthyam Reddy 1999 Indlaw SC 4641999 Indlaw SC 464, requires consideration by a larger Bench.
2.  The civil appeals shall be placed before a Bench of three learned Judges.
Order accordingly.
