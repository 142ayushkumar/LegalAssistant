Indian Council For Enviro-Legal Action v Union of India and others
Supreme Court of India

15 March 2005
I. A. Nos. 27, 34, 35 & 36 in W.P. (Civil) No. 967 of 1989
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. With reference to the order passed by this Court on 9.12.2004 in I.A. Nos.27 & 34, the learned counsel appearing for the Managing Directors of respondent Nos.4 to 8 - Companies states that the counter affidavit filed in I.A. No.27 in response to the prayer for taking action under the Contempt of Courts Act, can be treated as the counter to the notice issued by the Court on 9.12.2004 and no separate counter needs to be filed by them in that behalf. Post the matter on Thursday, 17th March, 2005 for giving necessary directions regarding sale of the properties as a first step.
Order accordingly
