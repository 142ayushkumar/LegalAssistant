English Electric Company Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax
Supreme Court of India

24 July 1997
C.A. No. 3050 of 1982 (Arising as an appeal from the judgment and order Dt. 13 December 1979, of the Madras High Court in T.C. No. 277 of 1976).
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In view of the decision of this court in CIT v. Canara Workshops P. Ltd. 1986 Indlaw SC 347, this appeal is allowed.
2. The judgment under appeal is set aside. There will be no order as to costs.
Appeal allowed.
