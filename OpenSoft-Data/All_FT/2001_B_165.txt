Shyam Kishore Singh and Others v Bharat Coking Coal Limited and Others
Supreme Court of India

22 January 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.10791/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In this matter on 8.1.2001 this Court was pleased to pass an order to the effect:
"An appeal under the Letters Patent lies before the Division Bench of the High Court. Therefore, this special leave petition will be dismissed with liberty to the petitioners to file a Letters Patent Appeal before the High Court.
It appears that the respondents filed an appeal under the Letters Patent before the High Court but withdrew it because this special leave petition was pending here.
Adjourned for two weeks to enable a copy of the order of withdrawal to be produced."
2.  The learned advocate appearing for the respondents produces a copy of the order as passed by the High Court in this matter and the same be kept on record of this Court. In that view of the matter and in view of the earlier order of this Court this SLP is dismissed as withdrawn with liberty to the petitioners to file a Letters Patent Appeal before the High Court.
3.  The respondents would also be at liberty to take appropriate steps in the matter in accordance with law for restoration of the Letters Patent Appeal No.62/2000.
The SLP is dismissed as withdrawn.
Petition Dismissed.
