Sukhdev Sharma v Sanjeev Shriya
Supreme Court of India

21 September 2015
Contempt Petition (C) No. 432 of 2015, in SLP(C) No. 22876 of 2013
(Non-Reportable)
The Judgment was delivered by : Anil R. Dave, J.
1. Learned counsel for the respondent has submitted that in pursuance of an order dated 20th March, 2015 passed by this court, Shri Raghvendra Srivastava, Arbitrator, has already been appointed.
2. In view of the above fact, there is no contempt. The contempt petition stands disposed of.
Petition disposed of
