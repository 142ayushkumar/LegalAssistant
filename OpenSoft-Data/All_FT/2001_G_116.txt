Government of Maharashtra and Others v Messrs Belloi Distillery
Supreme Court of India

8  May  2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal(Civil)......./2001 (CC:3103)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Delay condoned.
1.  The point raised in this matter is actually covered by a two judge bench of this court in Polychem Ltd. and Anr. vs. State of Maharashtra. As per that judgment the two judge bench has approved the decision of a Division Bench of the Bombay High Court in J.E. Billimoria & Sons vs. State of Maharahstra, Mr. Soli J. Sorabjjee, AG submitted that the said decision of the Division Bench of the Bombay High Court has since been overruled by a Full Bench of the Bombay High Court.
2. Learned AG also pointed out that due to some mistake the said decision of the Full Bench has not been brought to the notice of this Court while hearing arguments in Polychem Ltd. vs. State of Maharashtra (supra). Issue notice.
Order accordingly.
