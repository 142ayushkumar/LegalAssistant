Commissioner of Central Excise, Jamshedpur v Tata Iron and Steel Company Limited
Supreme Court of India

16 February 2005
C.As. No. 5807 of 1999 with No. 1067 of 2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Having regard to the concurrent findings that the claim of the respondent was barred by limitation and in addition considering that for the period not covered by the period of limitation, the products in question were exempted from payment of duty.
We dismiss the appeals.
Appeals dismissed.
