Commissioner of Income Tax, West Bengal v Orient Paper Mills Limited
Supreme Court of India

20 January 1989
C.A. No. 1247 of 1975
The Judgment was delivered by: RANGANATH MISHRA, J.
1. Admittedly, the appeal is concluded by the view taken by this court in Textile Machinery Corporation Ltd. v. CIT and CIT v. Indian Aluminium Co. Ltd..
2.   In accordance with that view, the appeal fails and is dismissed. There is no order as to costs.
Appeal dismissed.
