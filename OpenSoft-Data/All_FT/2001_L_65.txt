Leela Krishnan Unni v Blue Chrome Limited and Others
Supreme Court of India

30 April 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.18787/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Heard the learned counsel for the respondent No.1. Learned counsel for the said respondent makes it abundantly clear and states before this Court that apart from the amount which was paid to the petitioner under the Workmen's Compensation Act, respondent would pay in addition her share which is payable from 1.11.1994 with 12 per cent interest within eight weeks from today after duducting the amount already paid on the basis of the orders passed by the High Court. As per the calculation by the petitioner the said amount would come to Rs.1,83,316/-.
The special leave petition stands disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
