Neelima Rani Alias Neelima Rani Palakonda v Srikanth
Supreme Court of India

3 January 2005
Transfer Petition (C) No. 574 of 2004
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard parties.
1.  This is the wife's petition for transfer of Divorce Petition OP No. 844 of 2004 titled Srikanth (Dr.) v. B. Neelima Rani Palakonda from the Family Court at Chennai, Tamil Nadu to the Court of the Family Judge at Warangal District, Andhra Pradesh.
2.  The petitioner is a lady who is unemployed and has no source of income. She also has no other male member to accompany her to Chennai.
3. In any case the respondent has to come from UK to India and therefore it makes no difference to him whether he goes to Chennai or to Andhra Pradesh.
We, therefore, deem it fit and proper to transfer the case.
4. We accordingly direct that Divorce Petition OP No. 844 of 2004 be transferred from the Family Court at Chennai, Tamil Nadu to the Court of the Family Judge at Warangal District, Andhra Pradesh.
The transfer petition is allowed accordingly.
Petition allowed.
