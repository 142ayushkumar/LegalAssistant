S.N. Narayanaswamy v T.S. Puttaswamy
Supreme Court of India

24 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal(Civil).......CC 6412-6413/2001
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  There is a delay of 541 days in filing these petitions. However, learned counsel for the petitioner states that the appeal before the High Court preferred by the opposite party was as to costs only and the judgment of the High Court is to be read in that light.
2. He also submits that if the decree drawn by the Appellate Court is not in confirmity with the judgment, the petitioner would be advised to seek correction of the decree under Section 152 C.P.C. We record the statement and dismiss these petitions as withdrawn.
Petitions dismissed.
