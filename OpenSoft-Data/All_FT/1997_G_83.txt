Commissioner of Income Tax v Gujarat Mineral Development Corporation
Supreme Court of India

30 September 1997
C.As. Nos. 2289 to 2291 of 1981 (Appeals from the judgment and order Dt. 31 July 1980, of the Gujarat High Court in I.T.R. Nos. 145 and 146 of 1975).
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In view of the decision of this court in the case of CIT v. Elecon Engineering Co. Ltd. 1987 Indlaw SC 28763, these appeals are dismissed.
2. There will be no order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed.
