Jhabarmal and Others v State of Rajasthan
Supreme Court of India

19 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 931/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Heard learned counsel for the parties. The order passed by the High Court of Rajasthan rejecting the petition filed by the petitioners under Section 389 of 
 Code of Criminal Procedure for being released on bail during pendency of the appeal is under challenge in this special leave petition.
2. On consideration of the matter we are not inclined to interfere with the order under challenge. It is submitted by the learned counsel for the petitioners that they have already undergone imprisonment for about 3 years. On consideration of the said submission the High Court is requested to dispose of the D.B. Criminal Appeal No.129 of 2000 expeditiously, if possible, within six months from the date of production of the certified copy of this order.
3.  If there is delay in disposal of the appeal it will be open to the petitioner to file a fresh application for bail.
With the above observations, the special leave petition is dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
