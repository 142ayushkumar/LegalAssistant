Yusuf Qureshi and Others v Muslim Minority Front and Others
Supreme Court of India

22 August 2001
CIVIL APPEAL NO. 7960/1996
The Order of the court was as follows:
1.  C.A. No. 7960/1996 Learned counsel for the appellants submits that this matter pertains to appointment of members of the A.P. Waqf Board. Subsequently, elections having been held, this appeal has now become infructuous. It is disposed of as having become infructuous.
2.  C.A. No. 4776/1996 Learned Counsel for the appellants brings to our notice that respondent No. 1 who had filed Waqf Reference No. 5 of 1997 before the Court of the Civil Judge at Lucknow which is the Tribunal under the U.P.Muslims Wakf Act, 1960, has filed an application for withdrawal of the Reference Petition itself.
3. In that view of the matter, this appeal has now become infructuous.
4.  Learned counsel for the respondents states that he has no instructions. Even if that is so, when an affidavit has been filed before us and a copy of the said application for withdrawal of the Reference has been produced before us, we do not think that there is any reason to pursue with the matter any further.
This appeal is disposed of as having become infructuous.
Appeal disposed of.
