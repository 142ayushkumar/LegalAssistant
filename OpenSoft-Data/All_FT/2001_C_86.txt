Bharat Kumar Gupta v Chairman and M.D., Modi Spinn. and Weav.M.Ltd and
Supreme Court of India

8 October 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.10846/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  This Special Leave Petition is filed against the order made by the learned Single Judge of the High Court of Judicature at Allahabad in a petition filed under Article 226 of the Constitution of India. We are prima facie of the opinion that a letters patent appeal against this order lies to a Division Bench of the High Court.
2. In this view of the matter, the counsel for the petitioner is given permission to withdraw this petition and approach the Division Bench. In the event of the appeal being held to be not maintainable the petitioner is at liberty to approach this Court by way another special leave petition.
3.  In view of the above, the Special Leave Petition is dismissed as withdrawn.
Petition dismissed.
