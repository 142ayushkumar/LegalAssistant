Krishi Upaj Mandi Samiti v Messrs Jain Bidi Udyog
Supreme Court of India

14 December 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.21281/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  Printing dispensed with There will be no recovery of arrears due. There will also be no stay of the refund of the tax collected, if any. The amount collected may be refunded within four months from this date. In future there will be no stay of recovery of market fee due and payable from the date of the High Court's judgment.
2. In case ultimately if the respondents succeed then the amount collected will be refunded by the appellants alongwith the interest 12% per annum. In case the appellants succeed then the respondents undertake to pay the arrears of market fee alongwith the interest 12% per annum from the date of the payment.
Order accordingly.
