Rameswarlal Chaudhury v Ram Niranjan Mour
Supreme Court of India

4 January 1994
C.A. No. 2977 of 1991
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In this case in which the appellant-tenant did not tender the rent to the landlord. Without resorting to such tender he has deposited the rent into the Court.
2.   That is not compliance with Section 5(4) of the Assam Urban Areas Rent Control Act, 1972. The High Court is correct in its conclusion. Civil appeal is dismissed. No costs.
Appeal Dismissed.
