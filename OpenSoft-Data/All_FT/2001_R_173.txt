Ram Manorath v Balbhadra and Others
Supreme Court of India

26 November 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.31/2001 in C.A.No.2686/1981
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Having heard Mr.R.B. Mehrotra, the learned senior counsel for the petitioner we are of the opinion that as the point now sought to be urged was not argued at the time of the hearing of the appeal we are not inclined to interfere with the order under review.
The Review Petition is accordingly dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
