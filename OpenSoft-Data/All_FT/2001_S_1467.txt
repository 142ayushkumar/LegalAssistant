Syed Sajidul Islam v State of West Bengal
Supreme Court of India

26 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 3463/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
We have heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  The order passed by the High Court rejecting the petitioner's prayer for anticipatory bail is under challenge in this special leave petition. By the order passed on 28.9.2001 this Court granted interim bail to the petitioner.
2. Considering the facts and circumstances of the case, we dispose of this SLP with the order that the petitioner shall surrender in the trial court and file an application for regular bail, the application will then be disposed of expeditiously.
3.  The interim order passed on 28.9.2001 will continue for a period of two weeks to enable the petitioner to move the trial court for regular bail.
Petition disposed of.
