Hindu v State of Tamil Nadu
Supreme Court of India

3 November 1987
C.A. No. 346 of 1978. Appeal by certificate from the judgment and order Dt. 4 November 1976, of the Madras High Court in T.C. No. 199 of 1972. The judgment of the High Court is reported at [1978] 41 STC 105 (Mad.)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In view of the law laid down by this Court in C.A. No. 1633 of 1974 [Indian Express (P.) Ltd. v. State of Tamil Nadu , there is no force in this appeal.
The appeal is dismissed a accordingly. No costs.
Appeal dismissed.
