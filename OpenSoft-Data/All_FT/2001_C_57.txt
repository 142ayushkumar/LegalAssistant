Messrs Rasiklal Kantilal and Company and Others v Cental Bureau of Investigation and Anr
Supreme Court of India

27 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 469/2001
The order of the Court was as follows:
We have heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  In course of his arguments Mr. Sushil Kumar learned senior counsel appearing for the petitioners sought leave to withdraw the special leave petition saying that since the trial of the case has already been commenced and the High Court in the order under challenge has granted leave to the petitioners to raise all contentions and after completion of the recording of the evidences it is not necessary for the petitioners to pursue this special leave petition.
2. In view of the submissions made, we dismiss this special leave petition as withdrawn.
Petition dismissed.
