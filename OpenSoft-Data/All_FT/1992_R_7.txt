R. Yashod Vardhan v Superintendent Central Excise
Supreme Court of India

14  May  1992
W.P. (Cr.) No. 499 of 1987
The Judgment was delivered by: L. M. SHARMA, J.
This writ petition is not pressed and is, therefore, dismissed.
1.  The period for which the present writ petition has remained pending in this Court shall be excluded for computing the period under Section 110 of the Customs Act.
Petition dismissed.
