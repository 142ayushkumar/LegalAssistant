Syed Baqar Ali and Others v Sardar Khan (D) By Lrs. and Others
Supreme Court of India

2 April 2008
Appeal (Civil) 4959 of 2001
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  This appeal is concluded by the findings of facts rendered by the High Court in Second Appeal No.90 of 1975. We are not inclined to interfere with the same.
2.  The appeal is accordingly dismissed. Interim order, if any, shall stand vacated. There will be no order as to costs.
3.  In view of the order passed in the appeal, the application for impleadment stands disposed of as having become infructuous.
Appeal disposed of
