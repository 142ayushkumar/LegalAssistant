A. S. Impex Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise, New Delhi
Supreme Court of India

29 March 2006
C.A. No. 1631 of 2001 with C.A. Nos. 4530-4532 of 2005
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Doubting the correctness of the judgment of this Court in the case of Thermax Private Limited vs. Collector of Customs reported in 1992 Indlaw SC 765, a three Judge Bench in the case of Commissioner of Central Excise, New Delhi vs. Hari Chand Shri Gopal reported 2005 Indlaw SC 759 has referred the matter to a larger Bench.
2. Since the point involved in the present appeals is regarding the applicability of the law laid down in Thermax's case 1992 Indlaw SC 765, correctness of which has been doubted, we deem it appropriate if these appeals are also tagged with Civil Appeal Nos.1878-1880 of 2004 referred to a larger Bench. We order accordingly.
Order accordingly.
