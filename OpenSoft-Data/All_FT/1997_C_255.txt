ITC Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise, Madras
Supreme Court of India

11 December 1997
C.As. Nos. 11530, 11537 & 11540 of 1995
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We have heard learned counsel for the appellant-assessees and the learned Additional Solicitor General. We are satisfied that the conclusion reached by the Tribunal and its reasons for that conclusion are correct. The conclusion of the Tribunal is stated thus:
"In view of the above discussion we find no force in the appellants contention that the classification of the disputed articles under Chapter 48 of the Central Excise Tariff Act,1985 ."
2.  Accordingly there is no merit in these appeals and the same are dismissed.
Appeals dismissed.
