Maharashtra State Judges' Assn. and another v Hon'ble Bombay High Court through Registrar and others
Supreme Court of India

14 November 2005
W.P. (C) No. 109 of 2005 with IA No. 2
The Order of the Court was as follows:
IA No. 2 is allowed.
1.  List the petition for directions in December 2005. Writ Petition (C) No. 279 of 2004 shall also be listed on that date.
2.  We further direct that information may be obtained from all the High Courts on the question whether the Rules for filling up of the posts to various categories in higher judicial service, as contemplated in All India Judges' Assn. (III) v. Union of India 2002 Indlaw SC 1965 have been framed; if so, copies thereof may be obtained and placed on record.
Order accordingly.
