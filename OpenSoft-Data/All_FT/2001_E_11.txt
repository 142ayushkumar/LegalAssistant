East India Hotels Limited and Anr v Union of India and Anr
Supreme Court of India

9 August 2001
C.A. No. 5086 OF 1989
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Apart from there being a delay of 147 days in filing the review petition for which the explanation offered is not found to be satisfactory, we find no merit in the review petition which is, accordingly, dismissed.
Review dismissed.
