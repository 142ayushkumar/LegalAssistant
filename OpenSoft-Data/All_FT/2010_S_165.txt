Society For Un-Aided P. School of Raj. v Union of India and another
Supreme Court of India

6 September 2010
W.P.(C) No. 95 of 2010 with W.P.(C) No. 98 of 2010, W.P.(C) No. 126 of 2010, W.P.(C) No. 137 of 2010, W.P.(C) No. 228 of 2010, W.P.(C) No. 269 of 2010
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Since the challenge involved raises the question as to the validity of Articles 15(5) and 21-A of the Constitution of India, we are of the view that the matter needs to be referred to the Constitution Bench of five Judges.
Issue Rule Nisi.
2.  Learned Solicitor General waives service of the Rule. All the respondents are before us. Counter affidavits be filed within four weeks.
3.  These Petitions be placed before the Constitution Bench for Directions on a suitable date.
Order Accordingly.
