Hasanuzzaman v State of Assam and Anr
Supreme Court of India

2 January 2001
I.A. No. 3 in C.A..No.4566/2000
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  After hearing learned counsel for the parties we allow this application and in modification of the judgment and order dated 16th August, 2000 passed by this Court in Civil Appeal No. 4566 of 2000, we direct that:
(i) the amount of compensation payable to the appellant shall be Rs.75,000/- wherefrom any amount received or withdrawn by the appellant-applicant whether before and after the judgment and order dated 16th August, 2000 on the basis of compensation granted by this Court alone shall be deducted and not the sum of Rs.15,000/-; and
(ii) interest 9% shall be payable to the appellant-applicant on the balance amount from the date when possession of the land was taken. The amount due to the appellant-applicant, determined in the manner aforesaid, shall be payable on or before 31st of March, 2001.
Application allowed.
