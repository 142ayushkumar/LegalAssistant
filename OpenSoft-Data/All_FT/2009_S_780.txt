National Human Rights Commission v State of Gujarat and others
Supreme Court of India

15  May  2009
W.P. (Cri.) No. 109 of 2003
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  A letter dated 14-5-2009, received from the Chairman, Special Investigating Team has been placed before us. The Chairman has requested for induction of two more members to the existing investigating team.
2. It is stated in the letter, that the induction of additional members has been necessitated because of the increase in the volume of work in the light of the recent orders of this Court and the reluctance of Shri C. B. Satpathy to resume his assignment in the investigating team.
3.  Having heard the learned amicus curiae and the learned counsel for the State of Gujarat, we accede to the prayer made in the letter and direct that S/Shri Paramvir Singh, former Special Director, CBI, New Delhi and A. K. Malhotra, former DIG, CBI, New Delhi shall form part of the Special Investigating Team.
4.  In view of what is mentioned by the Chairman, Shri C. B. Satpathy is relieved of his assignment as member of the investigating team.
Order accordingly.
