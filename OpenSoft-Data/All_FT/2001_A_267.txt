State of Haryana and Others v Ashok Kumar
Supreme Court of India

13 December 2001
CIVIL APPEAL NO. 2594/2000
The Order of the court was as follows:
1.  In the facts and circumstances of this case, we do not think it would be appropriate to interfere with the order made by the High Court. This appeal shall stand dismissed.
2.  Two points were raised before the High Court that the certificate of eligibility which had been produced by the respondent was not only belated but also that it was a fake one.
3. However, here in this Court the fact that allegation that the said certificate is a fake one, is not pursued with.
Appeal dismissed.
