Commissioner of Income Tax v Gujarat Heavy Chemicals Limited
Supreme Court of India

18 October 2001
C.As. Nos. 5428 to 5430 of 1999 (Appeals By Special Leave From the Orders Dt. 8 December 1998, of the Gujarat High Court In I.T.A. Nos. 287, 288 and 289 of 1998)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We have read the Order of the High Court and heard learned counsel for the appellant. We are satisfied that upon the facts, no interference with the order of the High Court is called for.
The civil appeals are dismissed.
No order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed.
