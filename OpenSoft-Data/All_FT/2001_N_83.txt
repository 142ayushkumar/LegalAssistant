Nareshkumar Vishweshwardas Agarwal v State of Gujarat
Supreme Court of India

3 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 3077/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Mr. P.S. Mishra, learned senior counsel after addressing arguments at length requests for permission to withdraw this Special Leave Petition as the party would be advised to surrender and apply for regular bail after interrogation is over and that party will also be advised to request the court to hear and dispose of the bail application on the same day.
2. In view of the course to be adopted, learned senior counsel seeks permission to withdraw this Special Leave Petition. Special Leave Petition is accordingly dismissed as withdrawn.
Petition dismissed.
