Century Cement v Collector of Central Excise
Supreme Court of India

25 July 2001
C.A. No. 5264 of 1992
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In view of the notification dated 25th June, 1996, issued subsequent to the order of the CEGAT, the appellant is clearly entitled to exemption of excise duty on lime stone falling under Heading 25.05 and captively consumed in the manufacture of cement clinkers and cleared during the period 20th March, 1990 to 16th September, 1990 which is the period in question in the present appeal.
2. In view of the said notification having been issued, this appeal is allowed. No costs.
Appeal allowed.
