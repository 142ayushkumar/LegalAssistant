Wild Life Protection Department and Anr v Roshan Lal
Supreme Court of India

2 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.2290/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
We have heard learned counsel for the parties.
Leave is granted.
Preparation of paper books is dispensed with.
Permission is granted to the parties to file additional documents.
1. As an interim measure, it is ordered that there shall be stay of operation of the direction of the High Court regarding payment of back wages.
2.  There shall be no stay so far as the direction for re-instatement of the respondent is concerned.
3.  This order is passed without prejudice to the rights and contentions of the parties and subject to the result of the appeal.
Order accordingly.
