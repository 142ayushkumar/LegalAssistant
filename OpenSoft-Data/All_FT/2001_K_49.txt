Asia Television Network Limited and Others v K.K. Modi Investment and Financl.Ser.P.Ltd
Supreme Court of India

12  May  2001
IA No 2 In Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.4696/2001
The order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Heard Mr. Ashok Desai appearing for the petitioners and Mr. K.K. Venugopal appearing for the respondents. In the facts and circumstances of the present case, we are not inclined to interfere with the impugned order of the Division Bench of Bombay High Court under Article 136 of the Constitution. The Special Leave Petition accordingly stands dismissed.
2. The petitioners, however, may approach the Division Bench of the Bombay High Court, which is stated to be sitting in the Vacation to take up the matter for admission and appropriate orders.
Petition dismissed.
