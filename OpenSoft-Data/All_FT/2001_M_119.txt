Mercury Travels (India) Limited v Mahabir Prasad and Anr
Supreme Court of India

11  May  2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.2287/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the parties submit that the terms of the consent of compromise has already been filed on 8th May, 2001.
2. It is further submitted that both the parties have signed the said terms, counter signed by the counsel and verified their signatures. We are further informed by both the counsel that the parties are also present in the Court today.
3.  In view of this, the aforesaid compromise filed on 8th May, 2001 dated 4th May, 2001 is accepted and the present petition is disposed of in terms of the said compromise.
4.  Accordingly, the impugned order of the High Court stands modified to the extent signed compromise agreed by the parties.
Petition disposed of.
