Kaushlendra Kumar Singh v Commissioner, S.E.C., U.P. and Others
Supreme Court of India

10  May  2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.15569/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
SLP (C) No.15569/2000
We have heard counsel for the parties.
1.  It is stated that an election petition has already been filed by one Manju Lata. In view of the said fact, we are not inclined to interfere in the matter. The special leave petition is dismissed. However, the election petition requires to be decided at an early date preferably within three months, if possible.
SLP (C) No. 16687/2000
Learned counsel states that the petitioner wants to withdraw the petition.
The special leave petition is dismissed as withdrawn.
Petitions dismissed.
