Khurbur v Kallu and Others
Supreme Court of India

31 January 2001
CIVIL APPEAL NO. 2829 OF 1996
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  C.A. No. 2829/96: The memo of compromise filed in the Court is taken on record.
2.  Mr. Attar Singh and Mr. N.P.Midha, learned counsel for the parties states that the parties to this case have compromised the matter in terms of the compromise memo filed today.
3.  In view of the said statement, the appeal is disposed of in terms of the compromise memo referred to above.
4.  C.A. No. 2830/96: We do not find any merit in this appeal. It is accordingly dismissed.
There shall be no order as to costs.
Appeal dismissed.
