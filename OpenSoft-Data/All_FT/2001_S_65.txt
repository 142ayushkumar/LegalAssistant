Assistant Dir., Dist.Survey and Land Records and v S.T. Ganapathi Rao and Others
Supreme Court of India

5 September 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No901-902/2001 in S.L.P.(Civil)No.18058-18059/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There is delay of 186 days in filing of the review petitions for which no satisfactory explanation has been offered. Even otherwise, we do not find any merit in these petitions. The Review Petitions are dismissed on the ground of delay as well as on merits.
Reviews dismissed.
