Commissioner of Customs, Chennai v Yeses International
Supreme Court of India

13 October 2003
I.A. Nos. 7-9 In C.A. Nos. 6408-6410 of l999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  This Court's judgment dated 8-10-2001 in regard to the demur rage is based on the facts of this case as also on a concession made by learned Counsel appearing for the Revenue. The question of law pertaining to the demurrage has not been decided in the said judgment.
2.  With the above clarification, I.A. Nos. 7 to 9 are disposed of.
Applications disposed of.
