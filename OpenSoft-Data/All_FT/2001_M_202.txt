Manik and Others v Director of Social Forestry and Others
Supreme Court of India

30 July 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.7935/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We do not find any ground to interfere with this interim order.
2. However, we are informed that this Court has already directed for the disposal of the writ petition itself within a specified period by means of order dated 18th August, 1999 in SLP(C) No. 11359/99.
3.  In view of the said order, we hope and trust that the High Court will dispose of the matter, if not already disposed of, within a period of three months from today. With the said observation the Special Leave Petition is disposed of.
Petition disposed of.
