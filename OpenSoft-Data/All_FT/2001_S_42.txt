State of Uttar Pradesh and Anr v S.C. Gupta and Others
Supreme Court of India

27 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.11774/2000
The order of the court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  The interim direction made by the High Court is to the effect that the respondents be continued in service till the age of 62 years. That order having been challenged in this petition was stayed by an order made on 11-8-2000.
2. In the circumstances of the case we think it appropriate to dispose of this matter by directing that the interim order passed by this Court on 11-8-2000 shall remain in operation until disposal of the writ petition before the High Court.
3.  This appeal is disposed of accordingly with no order as to costs.
Appeal disposed of.
