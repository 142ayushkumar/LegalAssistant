K. S. Sundaram v Commissioner of Income Tax
Supreme Court of India

19 July 2001
C.A. No. 3655 of 1998 (Arising as an appeal by special leave from the judgment and order Dt. 20 February 1998, of the Madras High Court in T.C. No. 612 of 1986).
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We have heard learned counsel for the appellant and read the judgment and order under challenge.
2. We have also read the rule, which the High Court 1998 Indlaw MAD 192 (Mad), has construed.
In our view, the judgment of the High Court is unexceptionable.
The civil appeal is dismissed with costs.
Appeal dismissed.
