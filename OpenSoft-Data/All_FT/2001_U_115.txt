Associated Cement Cos. Limited and Anr v Union of India and Anr
Supreme Court of India

6 November 2001
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  It is stated by Mr. Rajiv Nanda learned counsel appearing for the Union of India that some proposals have been received from some of the petitioners and they are under active consideration at the highest level by the Government of India. We direct the other petitioners who have not yet given proposals to give the same.
2. Needful be done within three weeks from today. The Government of India will take a final decision within four weeks thereafter. List for directions after seven weeks.
Order accordingly.
