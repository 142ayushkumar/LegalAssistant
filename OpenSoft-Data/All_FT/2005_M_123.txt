Umesh Korga Bhandari v Mahanagar Telephone Nigam Limited and another
Supreme Court of India

25 October 2005
C.As. No. 6462 of 2003 with Nos. 6464 and 6463 of 2003
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In these appeals strong reliance is being placed upon G.M., Telecom v. A. Srinivasa Rao 1997 Indlaw SC 516.
2.   In this case relying upon Bangalore Water Supply and Sewerage Board v. A. Rajappa1978 Indlaw SC 260 the case of Bombay Telephone Canteen Employees' Assn. v. Union of India 1997 Indlaw SC 2466 has been overruled.
3.   The judgment in Bangalore Water Supply and Sewerage Board case has been referred to a larger Bench in State of U.P. v. Jai Bir Singh 2005 Indlaw SC 766.
4.  In our view these appeals must, therefore, await the decision of the larger Bench. To be placed on board after a decision in State of U.P. v. Jai Bir Singh 2005 Indlaw SC 766 is delivered.
Order accordingy.
