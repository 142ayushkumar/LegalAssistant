Messrs Shivalik Agro Ply Products v Collector of Customs, Bombay
Supreme Court of India

13 September 1989
C.A. No. 186 of 1988
The Judgment was delivered by: SABYASACHI MUKHARJI, J.
This is an appeal under Section 130-E of the Customs Act, 1962.
In that view of the matter, we cannot interfere with the order passed by the Tribunal.
1. The appeal is accordingly dismissed. The interim orders are vacated. The amount be paid by the appellant within a period of four months.
Appeal dismissed.
