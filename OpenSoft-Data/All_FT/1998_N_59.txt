Niranjan Lal v District Judge, Bulandshahr and Others
Supreme Court of India

6 February 1998
Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No. 1542 of 1998
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We cannot entertain the factual controversy raised in this matter. The special leave petition is dismissed. The petitioner, if so advised, may move the High Court for review.
Petition dismissed.
