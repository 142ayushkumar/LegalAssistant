Ashwani K. Singh v U.P.Public Service Commission and Others
Supreme Court of India

11 December 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.11503/1998
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In course of hearing, it transpires that by giving seniority to the present petitioners in the rank of Accounts Officer, the persons, who were appointed on the basis of competitive examination held in the next year on the post being carried forward to the next year, their seniority would be affected.
2. It would, therefore, be appropriate to implead them as party in this proceedings. Mr. Sharan prays for four weeks time to file an application for impleading them as party. On such application being filed, notice be issued to them.
3.  Put up after notice is served on the proposed newly added party.
Order accordingly.
