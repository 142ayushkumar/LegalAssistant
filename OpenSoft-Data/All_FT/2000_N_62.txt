Messrs Eveready Industries India Limited v National Carbon Company Cant. Mazo
Supreme Court of India

12 December 2000
CIVIL APPEAL NO. 3289/1997 With C.A. No. 3290/1997
The Order of the court was as follows:
1.  These matters are covered by the decision of this Court rendered in Civil Appeal No.670/1997 titled VST Inds.Ltd. Vs. VST Inds.Canteen Workers Union and Ors. and connected matters.
2. Following the said decision and for the reasons set forth therein these appeals are disposed of.
Appeals disposed of.
