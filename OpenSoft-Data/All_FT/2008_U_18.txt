Makbul Mirasaheb Mulani and Others v Uakub Babasaheb Mahabari (D) Th. Lrs
Supreme Court of India

1 February 2008
Appeal (civil) 902 of 2008
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
Leave granted.
1. The delay in presentation of the appeal is condoned subject to payment of Rs. 8,000/- as costs to be paid by the appellants to the respondents within a period of eight weeks. The receipt showing acknowledgement of the payment shall be filed before the High Court within ten weeks. If the receipt is filed, only then our order directing condonation of delay shall be operative and not otherwise.
The appeal is disposed of accordingly.
Appeal disposed of
