Uttaranchal Transport Corporation Ltd. v Smt. Vimla Devi & Ors.
Supreme Court of India

3 March 2009
CIVIL APPEAL NO. 1068 OF 2009
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. Upon being mentioned in the judgment the dated 16/2/2009 line 'the MACT shall work out the entitlement on the aforesaid basis' is deleted.
2. The para of the judgment shall now be read as follows:
"Keeping in view the parameters indicated above it would be appropriate to fix the multiplier at 10 and the rate of interest @ 6% p.a."
Order accordingly.
