Union of India and Others v A.S. Saharan
Supreme Court of India

25 July 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.17795/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Mr. Altaf Ahmad, learned Additional Solicitor General reported that pursuant to the order passed by this Court on 25.4.2001 directing the Union of India to pass the order in accordance with the directions contained in the impugned judgment the authority concerned considered the matter again and decided to maintain the status of the C.R. dossier. This is recorded. Now, Mr. Altaf Ahmad submits that the SLP filed by the Union of India has become infructuous.
2. We, therefore, dismiss this SLP without prejudice to the right of the respondent to challenge the latest order passed in respect of the C.R.
Petition dismissed.
