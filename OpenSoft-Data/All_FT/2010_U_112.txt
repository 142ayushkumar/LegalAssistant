Union of India v Prasanta Bhattacharjee
Supreme Court of India

19 February 2010
Petition for Special Leave to C.A. Nos. CC 2149-2150 of 2010
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Exemption allowed.
Delay condoned.
1.  Although, the counsel for the Department made the statement that refund will be granted without supporting documents, we are of the view that refund cannot be granted in the absence of relevant documents to show the compliance of Section 27 of the Customs Act, 1962.
Issue notice.
Order accordingly.
