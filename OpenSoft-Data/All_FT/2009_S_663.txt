Sompal Singh & Anr. v State Of U.P.
Supreme Court of India

23 January 2009
Special Leave to Appeal (Crl) No(s).8103/2008
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  Appellant No.1 Sompal Singh has been convicted by the Courts below and is undergoing imprisonment for one year and six months on two counts which shall run concurrently.
2. In the facts and circumstances of the case, his bail application is allowed. Sompal Singh is directed to be released on bail subject to the condition that he shall furnish personal bond in the sum of Rs.5,000/- (Rupees five thousand only) with one surety of the like amount to the satisfaction of the Trial Court.
3.  The bail application of Kunwarpal Singh Appellant No.2 is rejected at this stage.
Order accordingly
