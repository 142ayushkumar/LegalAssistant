Commissioner of Income Tax, West Bengal Ii v Shyama Shankar Lall Singha
Supreme Court of India

24 September 1971
The Judgment was delivered by: HEGDE, J.
1.  The question of law referred to the High Court u/s. 66(1) of the Indian Income-tax Act, 1922, is:
"Whether, on the facts and in the circumstances of the case, the amount of Rs. 2, 344 was rightly included as dividend in the total income of the assessee for the assessment year 1949-50 ?"
2.  This question is concluded by the decision of this court in Commissioner of Income-tax v. Nalin Behari Lall Singha.
Following that decision this appeal is dismissed. No costs.
Appeal dismissed.
