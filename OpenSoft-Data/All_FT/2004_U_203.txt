Pradeep Jain v Union of India
Supreme Court of India

5 January 2004
W.P. (Civil) No. 556/2003
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel appearing for the petitioner seeks leave of the Court to withdraw this petition with liberty to challenge constitution of National Tax Tribunal, if a legislation in this behalf is passed by the Parliament.
Permission granted.
The writ petition is dismissed as withdrawn.
Petition dismissed.
