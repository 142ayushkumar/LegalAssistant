Shubham Bahuuddeshiya Shikshan Sanstha, Waddhamana and Another v Education Officer, Nagpur and Others
Supreme Court of India

30 April 2008
Civil Appeal No. 3380 of 2008 [Arising out of SLP(C) No. 6646/2006]
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Leave granted.
1.  Having heard the learned counsel for the parties, we are of the opinion that interest of justice would be sub-served if this appeal is disposed of with the following directions:
(1) The respondent No.3 shall continue to work in the school in question till an appropriate order of transfer is passed posting him to any other school. Such an order may be passed keeping in view the existing vacancy and/or as and when such vacancy occurs.
(2) Respondent No.2, if not working in the school in question, shall be reinstated forthwith in terms of the order passed by the School Tribunal. He shall be paid the back-wages with effect from the date of order passed by the School Tribunal in his favour but he may not be paid any back-wages for the period 1993 to 2002.
2.  We are passing this order keeping in view the undertaking given by him before the High Court of Judicature at Bombay in Writ Petition No.355/2004.
The appeal is disposed of with the aforementioned directions.
Appeal disposed of
