E.A.A. Charles v P. Mohan Reddy and Others
Supreme Court of India

5 September 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.1100-1102/2001 in C.A.No.3054-3056/1998
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There has been an inordinate delay of 114 and 141 days respectively in the filing the Review Petitions for which there is no sufficient explanation. Even on merits. we do not find a case for our interference.
2.  The Review Petitions are dismissed on the ground of delay as well as on merits.
Review dismissed.
