Sanshin Chemicals Industry v Oriental Carbons and Chemicals Limited and Others
Supreme Court of India

16 January 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.21010/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  None appeared to press this petition when the matter was called. The Special Leave Petition accordingly stands dismissed.
2.  After the aforesaid order was passed, Mr. Ganguli, learned senior counsel appearing for the petitioner, submitted that he was unavoidably held up somewhere and could not reach when the matter was called and prayed that the matter may be restored. He further stated that the necessary submissions will be filed by the petitioner in the case. Mr. Dave, learned senior counsel appearing for the respondents, has no objection to the matter being restored and reviewed.
3. In the circumstances, the matter is restored to file. Put up this matter on 23.01.2001.
Order accordingly.
