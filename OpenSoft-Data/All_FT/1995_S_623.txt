State of Rajasthan v Nandlal Singh and Others
Supreme Court of India

13 January 1995
C.A. No. 695 of 1995 (Arising out of S.L.P. (C) No. 7468 of 1993)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
1.  We have today pronounced judgment in - State of Rajasthan v. Gopaldas. For the reasons and conclusions reached by us in Gopaldas case, we allow the appeal and set aside the impugned judgment of the High Court.
Appeal allowed.
