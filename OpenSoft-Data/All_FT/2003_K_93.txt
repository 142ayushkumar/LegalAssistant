Commissioner of Central Excise v Kartik Steels Limited
Supreme Court of India

24 January 2003
C.A. No. 5323 of 2002 (Arising as an appeal From 2002 (139) E.L.T. 423 (Tribunal), 2002 (146) E.L.T. A314 (S.C.))
The order of the Court was as follows:
1.  As required to re-appreciate and decide the said issue on merits. In the result, the appeal is allowed, the impugned order passed by the Tribunal is set aside and the matter is remitted to the Tribunal for deciding it on merits with regard to the applicability of first proviso to Section 11A(1) of the Customs Act.
The appeal stands disposed of accordingly. No order as to costs.
Appeal disposed of.
