Commnr. of Central Excise, Lucknow v Messrs Uptron India Limited
Supreme Court of India

23 February 2001
C.A. No.5651/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The respondent was given notice that the matter might be disposed of at this stage. It has been served but has not chosen to put in an appearance.
2.  The order under appeal is passed by the Customs, Excise and Gold (Control) Appellate Tribunal based upon the decision of this Court Commissioner of Central Excise vs. Cotspun Ltd.
3. Thereafter, Section 11-A of the Central Excise Act, 1944 has been amended so that the action proposed in a show-cause notice under Section 11-A (i) in cases where the price lists and classification lists have been approved by the Department between the period 17th November, 1980 to 12th May, 2000 has retrospective effect. This amendment will cover the case at hand.
4.  Accordingly, the appeal is allowed. The order under appeal is set aside.
No order as to costs.
Appeal allowed.
