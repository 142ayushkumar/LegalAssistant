Tata Consultancy Services v Municipal Corporation of Greater Bombay and
Supreme Court of India

5 November 2004
W.P. (c) No. 147 of 2002
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Parties agreed that the points raised in this writ petition are covered by the ratio of the judgment pronounced today in Civil Appeal No. 2582 of 1998 etc. [Tata Consultancy Services v. State of Andhra Pradesh 2005 (1) CompLJ 25].
The writ petition accordingly stands dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
