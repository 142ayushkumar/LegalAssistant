J.K. Jain and Others v Ziff-Davis
Supreme Court of India

21 August 2008
Civil Appeal No. 5187-5188 of 2002
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1.  In view of the settlement arrived at between the parties, learned counsel for the parties do not want to press these appeals as the same have become infructuous. The appeals are accordingly disposed of as having become infructuous.
2. However, if the compromise petition is not filed in the suit, it will be open to either of the parties to apply for recall of the order.
Appeals disposed of
