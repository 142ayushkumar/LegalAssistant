Bajaj Auto Limited v R.P. Sawant and Others
Supreme Court of India

8 October 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.16928/2001
The order of the court was as follows:
Issue notice.
1.  Mr. Ashok Desai, learned senior counsel, states, on instructions, that the review petition filed before the High Court shall not be pressed and it will be withdrawn within a period of two weeks from the date hereof. Such a statement is recorded hereby.
2.  Learned advocates who appear for the respondent are present in Court on caveat. The notice issued stands accepted by the advocates on record, who are present.
3. Counter affidavit be filed within a period of four weeks and rejoinder, if any, thereafter. Let the matter appear 12 weeks hence.
4.  Intervention applications are allowed. They would be at liberty to file affidavit if they so desire. Leave is given to file a copy of the judgment.
Order accordingly.
