Kapildeo Prasad v General Manager, Eastern Railway and Others
Supreme Court of India

12 October 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.6596/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Heard Shri Rudreshwar Singh learned counsel for the petitioner and Shri R.N. Trivedi learned Additional Solicitor General for the respondents.
2.  Though a limited notice for remand of the case to the National Commission was issued by the order dated 30.4.2001 learned counsel for the parties submit that the question of law raised in the case may be decided by this Court.
3. Considering the request it is ordered that this case will be disposed of on merits.
It will be listed for final disposal on a non-miscellaneous day after eight weeks.
Counter and rejoinder be filed in the meantime.
Order accordingly.
