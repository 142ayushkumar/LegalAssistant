Vijay Kumar v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others
Supreme Court of India

27 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 530-531/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
SLP 530-531/2001:
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  The State of U.P. has filed the special leave petition challenging the same judgment/order of acquittal.
2.  We do not feel the necessity of continuing this special leave petition.
3.   Acordingly the SLP is dismissed. However, it will be open to the counsel for the petitioner to assist the counsel representing the State of U.P. in connected case.
SLP 2610-2611/2001 :
Issue notice returnable within five weeks.
Learned counsel appearing for the State of U.P. states that he will take steps for issue of notice to the respondents in the course of the day.
Order accordingly.
