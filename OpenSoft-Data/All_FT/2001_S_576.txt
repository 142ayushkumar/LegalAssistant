Babujan Tudu and Others v State of Bihar
Supreme Court of India

1 August 2001
Review Petition(Cr.) No. 698/2001 in S.L.P.(Cr.)No. 3525/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There has been an inordinate delay of 195 days in filing the Review Petition for which there is no satisfactory explanation. Even on merits, we do not find a case for our interference.
2. The Review Petition is dismissed on the ground of delay as well as on merits.
Review dismissed.
