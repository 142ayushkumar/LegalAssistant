Kotha Badhrinath v State of Andhra Pradesh and Anr
Supreme Court of India

17 April 2001
Review Petition(Cr.) No. 350/2001 in S.L.P.(Cr.)No. 2112/2000
The Order of the court was as follows:
Application for bail stands rejected.
1.  There has been an inordinate delay of 213 days for which there is no sufficient explanation. The application for condonation of delay is dismissed.
2. Even otherwise, we do not find any merit in the Review Petition which is accordingly dismissed on the ground of delay as well as on merit.
Petition dismissed.
