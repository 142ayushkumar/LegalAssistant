Trilochan Nath v State of Jammu and Kashmir and Others
Supreme Court of India

15 December 2000
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.143/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
1.  After hearing the learned counsel for the parties, we are of the opinion that the matter should be tried by the High Court once again on the question as to whether the petitioner was senior to the others or not and on that basis to be retained in service.
2. The judgments of the High Court as well as of the Single Judge are set aside and the original writ petition is restored to the file of the Single Judge. The appeal is disposed of.
Appeal disposed of.
