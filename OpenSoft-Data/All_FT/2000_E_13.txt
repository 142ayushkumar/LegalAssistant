Commissioner of Income Tax v Eid Parry Limited
Supreme Court of India

16 November 2000
C.A. Nos.143-144/1997
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The appeals must be dismissed, in view of the fact that the question arising therein has already been answered against the appellant by the decision of this Court in Commissioner of Income Tax, Madras vs. Kasturi & Sons Ltd.
The appeals are dismissed.
Appeals dismissed.
