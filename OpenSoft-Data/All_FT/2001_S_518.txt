State of Assam and Anr v Jibanjyoti Baishya and Others
Supreme Court of India

5 February 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.1672-1675/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
S.L.P.(C) Nos.1672-75/2001: The special leave petitions are dismissed.
1.  S.L.P.(C) No....CC 852-855/2001: Permission to file special leave petitions is granted.
2.  Mr.Sibal, learned counsel for the petitioners, states that four seats from the all India quota have fallen vacant and that the petitioners will move the High Court for clarification. He submits that application should be decided without reference to the order under challenge.
3.  On the application of learned counsel for the petitioners, the special leave petitions are dismissed as withdrawn. The High Court shall consider the application of the petitioners for clarification in accordance with law.
Petitions dismissed.
