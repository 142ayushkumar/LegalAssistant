Commissioner of Wealth Tax, Bihar, Ranchi v Sandhya Rani Dutta
Supreme Court of India

1 March 2001
C.A. Nos.4684-4686 of 1998
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The issue involved in these civil appeals is, counsel fairly stated, covered in favour of the Revenue by the judgment of this Bench delivered on 22nd February 2001 in the assessee's own case in Civil Appeal Nos.5450-5451 of 1997.
2.  Accordingly, the civil appeals are allowed. The order under appeal is set aside.
3.  The questions are answered in favour of the Revenue. No order as to costs.
Appeals allowed.
