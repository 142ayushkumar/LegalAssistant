Shree Durga Glass Private Limited v Collector of Central Excise
Supreme Court of India

18 September 1996
C.A. No. 3661 of 1984 (Arising as an appeal from CEGAT order No. 362/84-D, Dt. 22 June 1984)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Having read the judgment and order under appeal and heard learned Counsel for the appellant, we are satisfied that no interference with the judgment and order of the Tribunal under appeal is called for.
2.  Technicalities cannot be allowed to outweigh the merits. The appeal is, accordingly, dismissed, with no order as to costs.
Appeal dismissed.
