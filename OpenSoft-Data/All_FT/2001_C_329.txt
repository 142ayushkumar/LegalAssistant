Gangabai Charities v Commissioner of Wealth Tax
Supreme Court of India

22 February 2001
C.As. Nos. 5890 to 5895 of 1998 with C.As. Nos. 1495 and 1496 of 2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We have heard learned counsel for the appellant, read the judgments and orders under appeal as also the authorities cited by him.
2.  We are of the view that the judgments and orders under appeal are correct and no interference therewith is required.
The civil appeals are dismissed with costs.
Appeals dismissed.
