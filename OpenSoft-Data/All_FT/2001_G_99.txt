Gouhar Azeez v Inspector of Police, Chennai and Anr
Supreme Court of India

4 April 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 1256/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The High Court by the impugned order has given liberty to the petitioner to approach the learned Magistrate under Section 35 of the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act, 1960 for recovering cost of maintenance.
2.  Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the petitioner shall file the requisite application within one week. In the interest of justice, we stay the operation of the impugned order of the High Court for 10 days. It will be open for the Petitioner to seek such relief as may be available to him in law.
The special leave petition is dismissed with the above observations.
Petition dismissed.
