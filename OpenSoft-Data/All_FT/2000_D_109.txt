United Phosphorous Ltd v Director General (Safeguards)
Supreme Court of India

5  May  2000
S.L.P. (Civil ) No. 6967 of 2000, Dt. 5 May 2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We decline to invoke the jurisdiction under Article 136 of the Constitution in regard to what is only a recommendation.
2. This, in our view, should also have governed the writ petition filed by the petitioners.
The special leave petition is dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
