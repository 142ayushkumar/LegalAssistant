Xing Peng Maritime Incorpration and Anr v R. Piyarelal International Limited and Anr
Supreme Court of India

24 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.13484/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
The respondents are here in caveat.
1. Mr. Banerji appearing for the petitioners and Mr. Sarkar appearing for the respondents say that the matter has been settled on the following terms:
"The parties will nominate their respective Arbitrators afresh within a period of four weeks from date. The parties may also agree to the change of Venue of arbitration. In the absence of any such agreement on the change of venue the original venue shall remain. The two nominated Arbitrators will appoint the third Arbitrator. Parties will also be at liberty to use the pleadings already filed if they are so advised in the Arbitration proceedings."
2.  The impugned order of appointment therefore is substituted by the aforesaid order, which is agreed to by the learned counsel for the parties.
The Special Leave Petition is disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
