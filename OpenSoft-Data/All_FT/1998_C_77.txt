Collector of Central Excise, Bombay v Caprihans India Limited
Supreme Court of India

31 March 1998
C.A. No. 4718 of 1995
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In this appeal the only question that falls for consideration is whether decorative laminated sheets are classifiable under sub-heading 4823.90 of the Central Excise Tariff 1985-86 as held by the Central Excise and Gold (Control) Appellate Tribunal in its judgment dated 21st April, 1994. This matter has been considered by this Court in Collector of Central Excise v. M/s. Wood Polymers Ltd. 1997 Indlaw SC 728 wherein it has been held that decorative laminated sheets are classifiable under sub-heading 3920.21 till February, 1988 and under sub-heading 3920.37 on or after 1st March, 1988.
2. For the reasons given in this judgment the appeal is allowed. The impugned judgment of the Tribunal is set aside. It is held that the decorative laminated sheets manufactured by the respondent are classifiable under sub-heading 3920.21 till February, 1988 and sub-heading 3929.37 on or after March 1, 1988. No order as to costs.
Appeal allowed.
