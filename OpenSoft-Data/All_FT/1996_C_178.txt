Collector of Customs v Shrishma Fine Chemicals and Pharmaceuticals (Karnataka)
Supreme Court of India

17 December 1996
C.As. Nos. 1954-89 of 1990
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1. The respondents imported Phenol USP Grade and claimed the benefit of an exemption notification issued under the provisions of Section 25 of the Customs Act, 1962.
2. The exemption therein applied, inter alia, to
"pharmaceutical chemicals, i.e., chemicals having prophylactic or therapeutic value and used solely or predominantly as drugs, not elsewhere specified"
3. The appeals are dismissed, with no order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed.
