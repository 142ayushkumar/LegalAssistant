Tormal Sharma v Ashoke Kumar Das Adhikary
Supreme Court of India

24 November 2000
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.18485-18487/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  After we heard the matter, we do not find any merit in the petitions. The special leave petitions are dismissed accordingly.
2.  Learned counsel for the petitioner states that in case the petitioner is required to vacate the premises in question immediately, he would be put to irreparable loss and for that reason the petitioner may be granted some time to vacate the premises.
3. Learned counsel for the respondent has no objection. We accordingly direct that the petitioner shall not be dispossessed from the premises till 30th June, 2001 provided he files a usual undertaking to this effect in this Court within four weeks from today.
Petitions dismissed.
