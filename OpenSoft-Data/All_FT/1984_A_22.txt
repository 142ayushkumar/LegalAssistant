Amar Singh and Others v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others
Supreme Court of India

6 April 1984
Special Leave Petn. (Civil) No. 1093 of 1984
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1.  In view of our decision in Hari Singh v. State of U. P., 1984 Indlaw SC 160 Civil Miscellaneous Writ Petition No. 11476 of 1982 on the file of the High Court of Allahabad out of which this special leave petition arises also suffers from laches.
2. Hence this special leave petition which is filed against the order vacating the interim order in that writ petition is also dismissed. The High Court will now proceed to dispose of the writ petition accordingly.
Petition dismissed.
