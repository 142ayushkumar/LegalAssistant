Punjab State Electricity Board and another v Ramesh Kumar Gupta
Supreme Court of India

29 April 2002
C.A. No. 3165 of 2002 (Arising out of S.L.P. (C) No. 4936 of 2002)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  Punjab State Electricity Board is in appeal against the order of the executing court and that of the High Court. The decree in question is not susceptible of being executed in the manner in which the executing court has directed. The executing court has exceeded its jurisdiction in directing promotion of the respondent from a specific date, which is not a part of the decree itself and the decree was only for a consideration of the case of the respondent.
2. In the aforesaid premises, the impugned orders of the executing court as well as that of the High Court are set aside. The appeal is allowed.
Appeal allowed.
