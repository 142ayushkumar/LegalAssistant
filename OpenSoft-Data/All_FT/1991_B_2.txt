B. I. Nagarale v Union of India and Others
Supreme Court of India

9 August 1991
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
1.  In view of our judgment in Civil Appeal No. 1414 of 1993 arising out of Special Leave Petition (Civil) No. 17028 of 1991 dated March 23, 1993 this appeal (Civil Appeal No. 1418 of 1993 [arising out of SLP (C) No. 6136 of 1992]) is allowed and the interim order dated September 9, 1991 in O.A. No. 530 of 1991 pending before the Central Administrative Tribunal, Bombay is quashed. No costs.
Appeal allowed.
