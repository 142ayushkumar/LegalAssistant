Nachhattar Singh and Others v Joint Development Commissioner and Others
Supreme Court of India

3 January 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.17199/1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  We have read the order under challenge and heard learned counsel. We are of the view that the writ petition filed by the present appellants requires consideration and that, therefore, the order dismissing the writ petition in limine requires to be set aside and the writ petition (C.W.P.No.13700 of 1999) restored to the file of the High Court to be heard and disposed of on merits.
Order on the appeal accordingly.
No order as to costs.
Order accordingly.
