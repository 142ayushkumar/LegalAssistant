Ramdas Sopan Bahirat v State of Maharashtra and Others
Supreme Court of India

21 March 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.280/2001 in S.L.P.(Civil)No.11789/1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There is an inordinate delay of 537 days in filing the review petition which has not been explained satisfactorily.
Accordingly, the review petition is dismissed on the ground of delay.
Review dismissed.
