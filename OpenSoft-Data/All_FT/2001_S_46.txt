Urmi Gupta and Anr v S.K. Khaitan and Anr
Supreme Court of India

26 November 2001
The order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the parties submit that the parties have settled their disputes out of the Court and it has been decided that the complainant should move an application before the trial court for withdrawal of the case.
2. In view of the statement these transfer petitions are dismissed. Interim order passed on 4.1.2001 by this Court is vacated.
Petitions dismissed.
