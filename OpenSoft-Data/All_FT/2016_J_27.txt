Gujarat Water Supply and Sewerage Board v Jasubhai Kabhai Harijan Etc.
Supreme Court of India

11 January 2016
Civil Appeal Nos. 272-273 of 2016 (Arising Out of S. L. P. (Civil) Nos. 17642-17643 of 2014)
(Non-Reportable)
The Judgment was delivered by : Anil R. Dave, J.
1. Leave granted.
2. In view of the judgement delivered in Jogendrasinhji Vijaysinghji v. State of Gujarat (2015) 9 SCC 1 2015 Indlaw SC 472, we set aside the judgment of the Division Bench and restore the Letters Patent Appeal to its original number to be decided on its merits afresh.
3. Parties shall appear before the High Court on 15th February, 2016.
4. In view of the above, the appeals are allowed with no order as to costs.
Appeals allowed
