Krishan Lal alias Bada v State (Delhi Administration)
Supreme Court of India

16 January 1981
Cr.A. No. 39 of 1981
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1.  The appellant was convicted u/s. 37 of the Arms Act and also u/s. 397 read with S. 34 of the Penal Code and sentenced to suffer rigorous imprisonment for one and a half years and seven years respectively.
2. He was also directed to pay a fine of Rs. 200/- for the offence u/s. 397 and in default to suffer rigorous imprisonment for one month.
3.  Having considered the facts and circumstances of the case, we think that the second offence really falls u/s. 392 of the Penal Code. We alter the conviction accordingly and reduce the sentence to rigorous imprisonment for three years.
The appeal is allowed to the extent indicated above.
Appeal partly allowed.
