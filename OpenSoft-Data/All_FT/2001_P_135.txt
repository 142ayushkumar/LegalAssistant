Pradeep Kumar Chattopadhyay and Others v Union of India and Others
Supreme Court of India

21 November 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.1469/2001 in S.L.P.(Civil)No.12339/2001
The order of the court was as follows:
Delay in filing the review petition is condoned.
1.  We have perused the review petition and the order sought to be reviewed. We are of the view that no case is made out for review of the said order.
The review petition is accordingly dismissed.
Review dismissed.
