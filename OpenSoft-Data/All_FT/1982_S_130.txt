Digpal Singh v State of Uttar Pradesh
Supreme Court of India

22 January 1982
Cr.A. No. 54 of 1982
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
1.  After hearing counsel on either side on the question of sentence, we feel that some reduction in the sentence should be made. Having regard to the family conditions of the appellant which have been put on record in the form of a certificate from the Pradhan of the village, we reduce the sentence to six months' rigorous imprisonment.
With this modification in the sentence, the appeal is disposed of.
Order accordingly.
