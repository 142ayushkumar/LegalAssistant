State of Himachal Pradesh v Shri Kundan Lal Ahuja and Another
Supreme Court of India

8 April 1997
C.A. Nos. 2584 - 2587 of 1981.
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  These appeals are allowed in view of the principles laid down by a judgment of this Court in the case of Kasinka Trading v. Union of India 1994 Indlaw SC 2183.
2. The judgment under appeal is set aside. Each party will bear their own costs.
Appeals allowed.
