Daya Ram and Anr v Manpal and Anr
Supreme Court of India

21 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.11413/2000
The order of the court was as follows:
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
Leave is granted.
1.  The point raised in this appeal stands covered by the decision of this Court in Kastur Chand and Another Vs Harbilash and Others.
2. This is what is represented by the learned counsel for the parties.
3.  Accordingly, the order of the High Court,under challenge,is set aside. Suit of the respondent is dismissed. The appeal is thus allowed.
No costs.
Appeal allowed.
