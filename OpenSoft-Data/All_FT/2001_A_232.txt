Irappa and Others v Arjun and Anr
Supreme Court of India

16 March 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.14786/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
We have heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  We are not satisfied that the order under challenge warrants interference. However, we direct that the suit filed by the respondents, O.S.No.37 of 1999 which is stated to be pending in the Court of the Principal Civil Judge, Senior Division, Bijapur (Karnataka State) shall be disposed of expeditiously, within six months from the date of receipt of a copy of this order.
2. We further direct that as ordered by this Court on 08th September 2000 status quo as prevailing on that day shall be maintained by the parties till disposal of the suit by the trial Court.
The Special Leave Petition is dismissed accordingly.
No cost.
Petition dismissed.
