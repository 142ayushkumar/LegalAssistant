Mohd.Mahaboob Ali v Kauser Shabana and Others
Supreme Court of India

27 April 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.2210/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The counsel for the respondents states that if the petitioner pays a sum of Rs.30,000/- towards arrears of maintenance, as granted by the Court,for the period from 18.10.94 till 19.12.97, the respondents would not take any proceedings for execution of such order.
The petitioner's counsel is agreeable to that statement.
2.  We, therefore, dispose of this petition with the direction that the petitioner shall pay an amount of Rs.30,000/- within two months from today and shall continue to pay the current maintenance as and when it falls due to the respondents.
Petition disposed of.
