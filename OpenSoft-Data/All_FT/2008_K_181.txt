Kiran Aggarwal v Hari Mohan Gupta
Supreme Court of India

22 January 2008
Appeal (civil) 618 of 2008[Arising out of SLP(C) No.18101/2006]
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Leave granted.
1.  Heard learned counsel for the appellant at great length. We see no reason to interfere with the well-merited concurrent findings of facts recorded by the three Courts below. The appeal, being devoid of merit, is, accordingly, dismissed. The time for vacating is, however, extended by six months from today on an usual undertaking to be filed by the appellant within four weeks.
Appeal dismissed
