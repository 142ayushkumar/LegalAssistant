Dhanraj Mills Limited v Custodian and Others
Supreme Court of India

4 December 2000
C.A.Nos.5813-5814/2000 (for preliminary hearing)(Apollo Tyres Ltd. vs. Custodian & Ors.)(Along with appln.(s) forexemption from filing c/c of the impugned judgment and ex-parte stay)
The Order of the court was as follows:
1.  CA Nos. 5813-5814/2000 Exemption from filing certified copy of the impugned judgment is granted.
2.  Appeals are admitted restricted to the issue whether the shares of Apollo Tyres Ltd. should be sold as a controlling block or in small lots. To be tagged along with Civil Appeal Nos. 7629/99 and 7630/99.
3.  The question of buying-back will also be gone into if need arises.
4.  It is made clear that there is no stay of the applicability of the Scheme as such.
5.  After hearing the learned counsel for the parties, we are in agreement with the decision of the Special Court which, in our opinion, calls for no interference. The appeal is dismissed.
Appeal dismissed.
