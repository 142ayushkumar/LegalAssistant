Income Tax Officer, Shillong, and Others v Hubert Osrick Unger
Supreme Court of India

21 March 1976
C.As. Nos. 304 to 307 of 1976
The Judgment was delivered by: H. R. KHANNA, J.
1.  In view of the decision of this court in the case of Income-tax Officer, Shillong v. N.Takin Roy Rymbal, these appeals are allowed and the writ petitions filed by the respondent are dismissed.
The parties shall bear their own costs throughout.
Appeal allowed
