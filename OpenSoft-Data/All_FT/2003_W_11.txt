U. P. Coop. Cane Unions Federation v West U. P. Sugar Mills Assn. and Others
Supreme Court of India

15 January 2003
C.As. No. 460 of 1997 With Nos. 461, 4685 of 1997, 1639-45, 1727 of 1999, 6065 of 2001, S.L.P. (C) No. 16851 of 2001 and 1363 of 2002
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The matters are placed before us in view of the conflict in the decisions in State of M.P. v. Jaora Sugar Mills Ltd. 1996 Indlaw SC 1356 and State of T.N. v. Kothari Sugars and Chemicals Ltd. 1996 Indlaw SC 785.
2. These two judgments have been rendered by Benches composed of two Judges each. One of the judgments has been approved by a decision in S.K.G. Sugar Ltd. v. State of Bihar 1997 Indlaw SC 760 by a Bench composed of three Judges.
3.  In this view of the matter it would be appropriate to refer these matters to a larger Bench of five Judges. We order accordingly.
4.  A contention is sought to be raised by Mr. Harish N. Salve, learned Senior Advocate that Presidential assent has to be disclosed as per the judgment in Kaiser-I-Hind (P) Ltd. v. National Textile Corpn. (Maharashtra North) Ltd.2002 Indlaw SC 1156. In that regard if appropriate pleadings are filed, the respondents may respond to it.
Issue notice on the application for intervention.
Dasti notice to Respondent 2.
5.  Considering the nature of matters, it would be appropriate to expedite hearing. Liberty to mention for early date of hearing.
Order accordingly.
