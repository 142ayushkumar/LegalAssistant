Narendra Nath Aggarwal v U.O.I. and Another
Supreme Court of India

23 August 2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the writ petitioner states that the writ petition has become infructuous by reason of the fact that the Monopolies and Restrictive Trade Practices Commission is proposed to be wound up. On his application, the writ petition is dismissed as withdrawn accordingly.
No order as to costs.
Petition dismissed.
