Banarsi Dass v State of Himachal Pradesh
Supreme Court of India

22 July 2005
Cr.M.P. No. 12802 of 2004
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard learned counsel for the petitioner.
1.  Perused the affidavit filed by the petitioner on 2.3.2005 enclosing therewith copy of notification issued by the Government of Himachal Pradesh on 10.2.2005 whereby pardon has been accorded to the petitioner who was sentenced to undergo two years' rigorous imprisonment and fine of Rs. 2000/- under Section 16(1-A) read with Section 7(i) of the Prevention of Food Adulteration Act, 1954 passed by the learned Sub Divisional Judicial Magistrate, Ghumwarin, Distt. Bilaspur, H.P. in case No. 237-1 of 1995 / 90 and sentence upheld by the learned Distt. and Sessions Judge, Bilaspur, H.P. in Crl. A. No. 11 / 95 and by the High Court of Himachal Pradesh in Crl. Revision No. 12 / 2000.
2. In view of this Government order, this appeal leave petition has become infructuous. In view of the aforesaid notification, order dated 
2.1.2005 passed by this Court directing the petitioner to surrender is recalled and he is exempted from surrender.
The special leave petition is, thus, dismissed as infructuous.
Petition dismissed.
