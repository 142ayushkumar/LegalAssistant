Messrs Rajasthan Spinning and Weaving Mills Limited v Commr. of Central Excise, Jaipur
Supreme Court of India

18 April 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.308/2001 in C.A.No.6936/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Delay is condoned.
1.  We have carefully gone through the contentions raised in the review petition, the order of the Tribunal dated August 22, 2000 and the order sought to be reviewed. We are of the opinion that no case is made out to review our order dated December 15, 2000. The review petition is therefore dismissed.
Review dismissed.
