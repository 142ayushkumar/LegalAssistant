Manju Lal and another v Ghaziabad Development Authority
Supreme Court of India

18 November 2004
C.A. No. 7566 of 2002
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard learned counsel for the appellants.
1.  In this case, the National Commission has awarded 18% interest. It is stated that the appellant has already received the amount. In Ghaziabad Development Authority v. Balbir Singh 2004 Indlaw SC 941 this Court has held that the amount already paid to the beneficiary, should not be refunded. The only grievance of the appellant is that the amount which he has already received, should not be allowed to be refunded.
In view of the orders passed in Balbir Singh case, 2004 Indlaw SC 941 the amount paid to the appellant shall not be refunded.
The appeal is disposed of in the above terms. No costs.
Appeal Disposed of.
