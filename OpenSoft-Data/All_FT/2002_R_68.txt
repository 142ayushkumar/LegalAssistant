Ram Jharo Devi v Union Of India & Ors
Supreme Court of India

28 October 2002
Petition(s) For Special Leave To Appeal (Civil) No.6890/2002
The Order of court was as follows:
1.  Heard Mr. P.S.Mishra, the learned senior counsel for the petitioner and the learned Additional Solicitor General for the Union of India.
2. Perused the decisions of this Court in Mukund Lal Bhandari and Ors. Vs. Union of India and Ors. as well as the decision of this Court in Gurdial Singh Vs.Union of India and Ors.
3.  Having regard to the fact that the Union of India while enquiring into the entitlement of the petitioner for being granted the Swatantra Sainani Samman pension under the scheme, has examined the relevant materials and came to the conclusion that the petitioner is not entitled to even with the most liberal construction to be given, we are not persuaded to interfere with the order refusing the pension in question.
The Special Leave Petition is accordingly dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
