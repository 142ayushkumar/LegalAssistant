D.N. Singh v State of Bihar and Anr
Supreme Court of India

8 January 2002
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 6469-6473/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Mr. R.K. Jain, learned senior counsel appearing in support of the petition, on instructions, submitted that for expedition he will make over the papers to the learned advocate appearing for the State. Let the paper be made available to the learned advocate for the State within a period of seven days from date hereof.
2. Let the matter stand over two weeks hence. The learned advocate for the State is directed to obtain necessary instructions as well on or before the next date of hearing.
3.  Since the CBI is involved in the matter, the petitioner is directed to serve on Mr. P. Parmeswaran, standing counsel of the CBI. Service to the CBI at Bihar be however effected as well by Dasti.
Order accordingly.
