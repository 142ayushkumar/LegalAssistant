Suresh Prabhu Suresh Prabhakar Prabhu v Kunja Santhalia and Anr
Supreme Court of India

26 March 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 993-994/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Petitioner submits that he is not liable to be proceeded against particularly in view of the statements made by the complainant in the High Court.
2. It is open to the petitioner to raise this contention before the trial court if he is interested in the discharge or quashing the process. If any such move is mode before the trial court the same shall be disposed of untrammelled or uninfluenced by any of the observations contained in the impugned order.
With these observations these Special Leave Petitions are disposed of.
Petitions disposed of.
