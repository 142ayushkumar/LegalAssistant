P Ramanathan v Messrs National Small Inds Corporation Limited
Supreme Court of India

25 July 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.11669-11671/1997
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Having heard the learned counsel for the parties, we consider it just and appropriate on the facts and in the circumstances of the case, to dispose of the Special Leave Petitions with the following directions:
On the petitioner's depositing Rs.1 lakh (rupees one lakh only) within a period of three months from today the respondent shall hand over the machinery which are in its possession within two weeks of the payment. Thereafter, the balance of the amount shall be paid in 10 equal instalments.
The first instalment shall be deposited after one month of the deposit of the said sum of rupees one lakh. In the event of default of payment of rupees 2 one lakh within the said period or in payment of any one of the instalments it would be open to the respondent to seize the machinery again and sell them in accordance with law.
The petitioner shall give an undertaking in this Court that in the event of handing over of machinery he will not dispose of the same till the balance of the amount is paid off by him.
Petitions disposed of.
