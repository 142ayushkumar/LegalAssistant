State of Rajasthan and Others v Krishan Kant Tiwari
Supreme Court of India

13 January 1995
C.A. No. 696 of 1995 (Arising out of S.L.P. (C) No. 13848 of 1994)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
1.  We have today pronounced judgment in  State of Rajasthan v. Gopaldas. For the reasons and conclusions reached by us in Gopaldas case we allow the appeal and set aside the impugned judgment of the High Court.
Appeal allowed.
