Thressiamma and Anr v State Bank of Travancore and Others
Supreme Court of India

7 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.12122/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Delay in filing counter affidavit is condoned. It is submitted by the learned counsel for the petitioners that petitioner No. 1 has not been able to make the further payment of the amount due in view of her son having met with an accident. He prays for last opportunity to make good the payment as per the decree.
2.  In view of the aforesaid submission, we permit the petitioner to make the payment as per the decree within a period of two months from today, namely, on or before 9th October, 2001.
3. If the payment is made, the decree shall stand satisfied and the sale would then, in that event, be regarded as having been set aside, but in the event of payment not being made or if there being any shortfall in payment, this special leave petition shall stand automatically dismissed without further recourse to the Court.
Petition dismissed.
