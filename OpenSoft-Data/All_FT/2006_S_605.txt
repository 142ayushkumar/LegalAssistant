Voice Consumer Care Council v State of Tamil Nadu
Supreme Court of India

30 August 2006
W.P(C) No. 35 of 1996, W.P(C) No. 238 of 1995, W.P(C) No. 259 of 1994
1. One of the points to be examined in this case is about the validity of the Tamil Nadu Backward Classes, Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes (Reservation of Seats in Educational Institutions and of Appointment or Posts in the Services under the State) Act, 1993 which has been included in the Ninth Schedule to the Constitution of India by virtue of the constitutional 76th amendment on and with effect from 31st August 1994. Whether the validity of an Act placed in the Ninth Schedule to the Constitution of India can be examined by the Court is the main question under examination in C.A.Nos.1344-45/1976 etc. titled I.R. Coelho (Dead) By LRs. etc. v. The State of Tamilnadu etc.
2. In these matters there are other points as well. The matters will be listed along with C.A.Nos.1344-45/1976 etc. for the present to hear the submissions on the aforesaid aspect of the impact of the Act being included in the Ninth Schedule to the Constitution of India. The written
3. submissions would be confined only on this legal issue and time schedule mentioned in the order passed today in C.A.Nos.1344-45/1976 etc. would be applicable to the present case as well. List these petitions along with C.A.Nos.1344-45/1976 etc.
Order accordingly
