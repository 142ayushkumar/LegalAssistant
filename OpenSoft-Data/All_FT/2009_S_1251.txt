Superintendent Of Post Offices And Others v K. K. Gopalakrishnan
Supreme Court of India

4  May  2009
Civil Appeal No. 3407 of 2009 (arising out of Special Leave Petition (C) No. 14431 of 2008)
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1.  Delay condoned.
2.  Leave granted.
3.  Heard learned counsel for the parties.
4.  Since the respondent has already been appointed in compliance with the directions made by the Central Administrative Tribunal and the High Court did not interfere with it, we are not inclined to interfere with the impugned orders exercising our jurisdiction under Article 136 of the  Constitution.
5.  The Civil Appeal is accordingly disposed of with no order as to costs. Interim order, if any, stands vacated.
Appeal disposed of
