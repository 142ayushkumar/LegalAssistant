State of Rajashtan and Others v Devi Singh
Supreme Court of India

25 January 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.4742/2000
The order of the court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  Having gone through the judgment of the High Court and after hearing learned counsel on both sides we are satisfied that though there was some lapse on the part of the appellants the matter should not have been disposed of in the manner it has been done by the High Court.
2. We set aside the order made by the High Court and restore Special Appeal No. 508/1998 to its original file.
3.  The requirements which were not complied with by the appellants shall be done within a period of two weeks from today and they shall also pay a sum of Rs.5,000/- by way of cost to the respondent which shall not be costs in the cause.
4.  If any of the conditions are not complied with by the appellants before us, the order made by the High Court shall stand restored. The appeal shall stand disposed of accordingly.
Appeal disposed of.
