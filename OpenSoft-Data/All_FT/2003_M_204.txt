Mohanraj v Messrs Statex Mills
Supreme Court of India

13 October 2003
Petition(s) For Special Leave To Appeal(Cr.)...................../2003
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that he has also moved the High Court by way of review petition. Therefore, in our opinion, this matter should await the decision of the High Court in the said review petition.
2. Learned counsel points out that pursuant to the sentence awarded to him by the judgment of the High Court, he is undergoing imprisonment, therefore he should be enlarged on bail. Since review petition is pending before the High Court, this prayer should be made before the High Court which if made will consider the same in accordance with law.
3.  List after the disposal of the review petition by the High Court.
Order accordingly.
