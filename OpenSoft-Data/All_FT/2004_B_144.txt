Tata Power Company Limited v Bses Limited and Others
Supreme Court of India

9 January 2004
Ias Nos. 5-6 In C.As. Nos. 8362-63 of 2003
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  After hearing the learned counsel for the parties, we clarify that what has been rejected of the judgment dated 17-10-2003 2003 Indlaw SC 871is the argument which is noted in the first sentence of the paragraph.
2. We further direct the words "by itself" to be read as added at the end of the paragraph. The rest of the prayers made in the applications cannot be allowed by way of clarification.
The applications stand disposed of.
Applications disposed of.
