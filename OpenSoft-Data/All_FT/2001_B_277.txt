Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation and Anr v Julie K. John and Others
Supreme Court of India

10 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.5802/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
Leave granted.
1.  Considering the order dated 29th November, 1999 passed by the Division Bench of the High Court in Appeal No.1139 of 1999 in Writ Petition No.1639 of 1999 along with the order dated 18th December, 1999 passed by the Education Officer, as directed by the Division Bench of the High Court, it appears that the High Court has committed an error in relying upon its previous order dated 27th July, 1999.
2. In this view of the matter learned counsel for the parties agree that the matter be remitted back to the High Court for deciding it afresh in accordance with law.
3.  In this view of the matter, the impugned order dated 16th January, 2001 passed in Writ Petition No.779 of 2000 passed by the High Court is set aside. The High Court is directed to decide the matter afresh in accordance with law.
The appeal is allowed to the aforesaid extent.
Appeal allowed.
