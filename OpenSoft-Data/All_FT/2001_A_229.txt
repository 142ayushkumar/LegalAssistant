Aresh v Spl. Tahsildar, Balgaum, Karnataka and Anr
Supreme Court of India

31 October 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.19093/1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard the learned counsel for the parties.
1.  The respondent is directed to file counter affidavit giving particulars of the amount of compensation determined as payable to the petitioner as on the date of vesting. The mode of payment of compensation shall also be indicated; as to how the amount was paid in cash and how much in the form of bonds carrying interest 5-1/2%.
2. Further the recalculation as on and from 1.3.1984 must also be indicated. It should also be specified as to whether the amount of interest found payable, was paid to the petitioner and if so how much? The case is adjourned by four weeks. To be treated as part-heard.
Order accordingly.
