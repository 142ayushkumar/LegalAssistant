Commnr. of Customs (Ports), Chennai v Messrs Sathavahana Ispat Limited
Supreme Court of India

12 April 2001
C.A..No.4558/2000
The order of the court was as follows:
Appeal is admitted.
1.  In view of the decision of this Court in Union of India and Ors. vs. M/s. Ganesh Das Bhojraj, the impugned order of CEGAT is set aside.
2. Counsel for the parties are agreed that the matter should go back now to the adjudicating authorities for a fresh decision in accordance with law. Ordered accordingly.
This appeal is disposed of.
Appeal disposed of.
