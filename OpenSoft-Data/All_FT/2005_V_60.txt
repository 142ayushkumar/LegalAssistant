Manas Bhattacharya v Vinay Cements Limited
Supreme Court of India

18 November 2005
Transfer Petition (C) No. 897 of 2004
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Nobody appears on behalf of the respondent despite service of notice.
1.  Having regard to the fact that the corporate office of the respondent is at Kolkata, we are of the opinion that interest of justice will be subserved if Money Suit No. 3 of 2004, titled Vinay Cements Ltd. v. Manas Bhattacharya pending in the Court of Additional Deputy Commissioner, North Cachar Hills, Haflong (Assam) is transferred to the City Civil Court at Kolkata and we do so.
The transfer petition is allowed.
Petition allowed.
