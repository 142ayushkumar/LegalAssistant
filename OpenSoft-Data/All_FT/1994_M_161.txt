Supreme Court of India

14 July 1994
C.A. No. 3845 of 1989
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We have pronounced judgment in Union of India v. Tushar Ranjan Mohanty 1994 Indlaw SC 844 today.

Appeal dismissed.
