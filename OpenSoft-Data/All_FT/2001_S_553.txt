Man Bahadur Mahto v State of Bihar and Others
Supreme Court of India

23 February 2001
I.A. No. 2 in C.A..No.6757/2000
The order of the court was as follows:
The last paragarh of order dated 20.11.2000 be read as follows:
"Therefore in the facts and circumstances of the case, we are of the view that the learned Single Judge was not right in allowing the writ petition and the Division Bench was right in setting aside the said proceedings. The appeal is dismissed accordingly. No order as to costs."
I.A. No. 2 for clarification is disposed of accordingly.
Appeal dismissed.
