Union of India and another v Indian Rayon and Industries
Supreme Court of India

11 April 2002
C.As. No. 35 of 1995 with Nos. 2567 and 5973 of 1995
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  It is fairly stated that the civil appeals must be dismissed, being covered against the Revenue by the judgment of this court in Govt, of India v. Madras Rubber Factory Ltd. 1995 Indlaw SC 937.
The civil appeals are dismissed. No order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed.
