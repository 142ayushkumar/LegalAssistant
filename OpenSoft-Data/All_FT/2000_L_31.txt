Income Tax Officer, Ahmedabad v Laxmi Corporation
Supreme Court of India

3 November 2000
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.3258/2000
The order of the court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
None appears for the respondent though the service is complete.
1.  After hearing the counsel for the appellant, in our opinion a question of law does arise. We, therefore, direct the Tribunal to state the case and refer the following question of law to the High Court:
"Whether on facts and circumstances of the case the Tribunal was right in coming to the conclusion that the assessee was a genuine firm and is not covered by the decision of McDowell vs. C.T.O.?"
The appeal is disposed of.
Appeal disposed of.
