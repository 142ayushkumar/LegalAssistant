State of Mp v Ramachandra Rao and Others
Supreme Court of India

19 March 2001
C.A..No.3301/1997
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Insofar as the correction of the name of Respondent No.12 is concerned, the respondent shall take steps to have the name corrected in the record of the High Court and thereafter he may move to this Court for correction.
2.  Four weeks time is granted to the appellant to furnish the particulars mentioned in the Office Report of March 16, 2001. In default the appeal shall stand dismissed as against respondent Nos. 7 and 13.
Appeal dismissed.
