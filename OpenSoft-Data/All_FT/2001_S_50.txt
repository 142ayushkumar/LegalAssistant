S.M. Sowayab v Kamala Devi and Anr
Supreme Court of India

19 February 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.11247/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  Considering the facts and circumstances of this case, we direct the Ist Additional District Judge, West Champaran, at Bettiah, before whom Title Appeal No.57 of 1998 is pending, to consider the application of the petitioner afresh herein for production of additional documents at the time of hearing of the said appeal.
2. Learned counsel for the respondents submits that if the petitioner produces such additional documents he would not object to the same, but it would be for the Court to decide whether the said documents can be produced under Order 41 Rule 27 of the Code of Civil Procedure. Learned counsel for the petitioner further submits that the petitioner or his counter part would not ask for any adjournment of the appeal on any ground, including the ground of sickness or strike by the Advocates.
The appeal is disposed of accordingly.
Appeal disposed of.
