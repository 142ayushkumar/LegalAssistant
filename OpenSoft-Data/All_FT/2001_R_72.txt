Raj Kumar Jain and Anr v Kamla Sadgopal (D) By Lrs. and Others
Supreme Court of India

2 January 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal(Civil).....CC 8544-8545/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Heard Mr. M.L. Verma, learned senior counsel appearing for the petitioners and Mr. Arun Bhardwaj, learned counsel appearing for the respondents. We do not find any merit in the petitions. The Special Leave Petitions are dismissed.
2. Learned counsel for the petitioners then urged that in case the petitioners are required to vacate the premises immediately, it shall create great hardship to them. Therefore, some time may be granted to vacate the premises. We, therefore, grant time upto 01.02.2001 to the petitioners to vacate the premises on his filing usual undertaking in this Court within one week from today. The petitioners shall hand over vacant and peaceful possession of the premises to the respondents on or before the expiry of the said period.
Petitions dismissed.
