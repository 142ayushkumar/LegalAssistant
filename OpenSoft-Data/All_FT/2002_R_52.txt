Rajesh v State of Goa
Supreme Court of India

12 August 2002
Cr.A. No. 822 of 2002 (Arising out of S.L.P. (Cr.) No. 1173 of 2002)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
1.  After hearing the counsel for the parties and on the basis of the evidence on record, it is evident that this is not a case of gang rape in view of acquittal of co-accused and the provision of Section 376(2)(g) IPC was not applicable.
2. This would be a case of rape simpliciter and, therefore, the sentence of the accused is reduced to the prescribed minimum of seven years.
This appeal is disposed of in the aforesaid terms.
Appeal disposed of.
