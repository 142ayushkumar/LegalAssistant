Devta Vishveshwar Mahadev v Union of India and Anr
Supreme Court of India

16 April 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.2292/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
1. We have perused the order passed on 27th of September 2000 and the affidavit filed by Mr. C. Dorje, Superintending Archaeologist, Archaeological Survey of India, Chandigarh Circule on 27th October, 2000 in pursuance of the said order. We are satisfied that the dismissal of the contempt petition ordered by the High Court is correct. The Special leave petition is dismissed.
2.  Dismissal of SLP will not prevent the petitioner from approaching the competent Authority for its grievance, if any, in accordance with law.
Petition dismissed.
