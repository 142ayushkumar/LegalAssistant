Union of India and Others v Ramcharan Sharma
Supreme Court of India

6 September 2001
CIVIL APPEAL NO. 8252/1996
The Order of the court was as follows:
1.  In the special facts and circumstances arising in this case, we do not think it necessary to interfere with the order made by the Tribunal and the appellants, as directed by the Tribunal, will have to decide the matter in terms of Rule 54-A of the Fundamental Rules.
2. Let such action be taken within a period of three months from today.
The appeal is disposed of with aforesaid observation.
Appeal disposed of.
