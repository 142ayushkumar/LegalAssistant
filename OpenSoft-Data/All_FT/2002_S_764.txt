Supreme Court Monitoring Committee v Mussoorie Dehradun Development Authority and others
Supreme Court of India

8 April 2002
W.P. (C) No. 749 of 1995 with Suo Motu Contempt Petitions (C) Nos. 14-134 of 1998 in W.P. (C) No. 749 of 1995, S.L.Ps. (C) Nos. 20791-92 of 2001 and W.P. (C) No. 469 of 1996
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The Secretary, Ministry of Urban Development is directed to file an affidavit in response to this Court's order dated 4-3-2002, to the effect that the UOI should consider having a law permitting the Government to take possession of the illegal construction as a receiver till the compliance with the building bye-laws by the owner takes place. The affidavit filed on 18-3- 2002, on behalf of the Ministry of Urban Development refers to the vesting of the property which was mooted on 7-1-2002, Fresh affidavit should address itself to the question of possession being taken as Receiver without depriving the owner of the title to the property, even if illegally constructed.
2. The Chief Secretary, State of Uttaranchal is also directed to file the affidavit in response to this Court's order dated 7-1-2002 and 4-3-2002, in relation to vesting of illegally constructed property with the Government or the local bodies or their taking possession of the illegal construction as a Receiver.
List these cases for hearing on 6-8-2002.
In IA No. 8, issue fresh notice returnable on 6-8-2002.
Dasti is permitted. Response of the respondents be filed before that.
SLPs (C) Nos. 20791-92 of 2001.
Delay condoned.
The SLPs are dismissed.
Petitions dismissed.
