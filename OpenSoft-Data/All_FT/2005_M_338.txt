Babulal and another v Municipal Corporation, Ratlam and others
Supreme Court of India

15 July 2005
C.A. No. 3765 of 2005 (Arising out of S.L.P.(C) No. 5743 of 2004)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  The High Court appears to have proceeded on the basis that it was settled law that a person not a party to the original proceedings cannot be proceeded against in contempt. The issue is a debatable one, particularly having regard to the law on the issue particularly in S.N. Banerjee v. Kuchwar Lime and Stone Co. Ltd. 1938 Indlaw PC 54.
2. The order of the High Court is set aside and the matter is remanded back for reconsideration of the issue.
The appeal is disposed of accordingly.
Appeal disposed of.
