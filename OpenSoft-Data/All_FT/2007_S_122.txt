Municipal Corporation, Hoshiarpur and another v Sewa Das and others
Supreme Court of India

31 August 2007
C.A. No. 4014 of 2007 (Arising out of S.L.P. (C) No. 23886 of 2005)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  By the impugned order, the High Court directed that the writ petitioners were entitled to regularisation of their services. It appears that against the impugned order, when the matter was brought to this Court by the Punjab Water Supply and Sewerage Board in Civil Appeal No. 5632 of 2006 2006 Indlaw SC 940 this Court allowed the appeal and set aside the aforesaid order passed by the High Court.
2. In our view, the present case is squarely covered by the aforesaid order of this Court and the appellants are also entitled to the same relief.
3.  The civil appeal is, accordingly, allowed and the impugned order is set aside. However, there shall be no order as to costs.
Appeal allowed.
