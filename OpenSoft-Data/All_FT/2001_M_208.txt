Manisha Manohar Nimkar v Geeta Gajanan Pagi
Supreme Court of India

27 August 2001
C.A. No.2673/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Counter-affidavit has been filed.
Rejoinder-affidavit, if any, be filed within four weeks.
1.  Printing dispensed with. Additional documents, if any, may be filed within six weeks. List for directions in the matter of hearing thereafter.
2. The order granting stay of further proceeding dated 9.4.2001 is modified to the extent that the High Court may proceed with the hearing of the election petition. When the stage is reached for commencement of trial before the High Court, either party would be at liberty to invite the attention of this Court for suitable directions.
Order accordingly.
