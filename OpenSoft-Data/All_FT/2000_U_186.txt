Niranjan Prasad Sinha and another v Union of India and others
Supreme Court of India

9  May  2000
Civil Appeal No. 10912/1996
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. Hon'ble Mr. Justice S.N. Phukan delivered the judgment of the Court.
2. In terms of the judgment the appeal is allowed with the direction to the respondents to draw up a fresh seniority list placing the appellants above the promotees in the post of Fireman Grade A. The revised seniority list shall be published within a period of 3 months. Considering the facts and circumstances of the case the parties are directed to bear their own costs of this appeal.
Order accordingly
