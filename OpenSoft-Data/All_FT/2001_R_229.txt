Shanti Devi and Anr v Rameshwar Singh and Anr
Supreme Court of India

15 October 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.17197/1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  It is stated that respondent No.2 has already died during the pendency of the revision petition before the High Court and he was substituted by his heirs before the High Court.
2. Therefore, his heirs ought to have been impleaded in this petition. The application for impleading heirs of respondent No.2 is allowed. Let the heirs namely, Raj Narayan Singh, Binod Singh, Prabhawati Kuer, Sushila Devi and Vidyawati Devi be impleaded as respondents in this matter. Notice shall go to the newly added respondents. This order is in modification of our earlier order dated 27.4.2001.
Order accordingly.
