Balan and Others v State of Tamil Nadu and Others
Supreme Court of India

8 October 2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the petitioners states that in view of the State's appeal having been dismissed, the petitioner Nos. 1 and 3 have left the country but the other petitioners are still in the refugee camp and would be moving the detaining authority for leaving the country on their arranging the requisit finances.
2.  We record the statement and dismiss this petition as withdrawn in view of its having been rendered infructuous.
Petition dismissed.
