Brittonia Carmia Club v Chanda Ramji and Anr
Supreme Court of India

13 February 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.130/2001 in S.L.P.(Civil)No.18829/99
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Apart from the fact that the delay of 141 days in filing the review petition was not caused due to any sufficient cause to justify condonation of the same, even on merits we find no case to review our order dated 4.8.2000.
2. No ground whatsoever is made out either in the review petition or the annexures thereto to admit the review petition. It is accordingly dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
