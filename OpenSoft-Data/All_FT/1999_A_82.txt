Commissioner of Central Excise, Mumbai v Amar Dye and Chemical Limited
Supreme Court of India

4 November 1999
C.A. Nos. 6001-02 of 1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We have gone through the judgment of the Tribunal and we are in agreement that reasonable discounts to buyers in different regions, so long as they are not based on other than commercial considerations were permissible in determining the assessable values in view of Section 4(1)(a) proviso (i).
These appeals are dismissed.
Appeals dismissed.
