Collector of Central Excise, Bombay v Parekh Dye Chem (India) Private Limited
Supreme Court of India

27 March 1995
C.A. No. 2153 of 1987
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard counsel for the appellant and the respondent.
1. We are of the opinion that the Tribunal took an unduly harsh attitude in refusing to condone the delay of 15 days in the facts and circumstances of the case.
2.  The order of the Tribunal under appeal is set aside and the delay in filing the appeal is condoned and the matter is remitted to the Tribunal for disposal of the appeal in accordance with law. The appeal is accordingly allowed. No costs.
Appeal allowed.
