State of Tamil Nadu and Others v Govindan and Company
Supreme Court of India

30 October 1990
C.As. Nos. 420, 421 and 882 of 1976 and 2618 and 2619 of 1988. C.A. No. 420 of 1976 was by special leave from the judgment and order Dt. 25 April 1973, of the Madras High Court in Tax Case No. 248 of 1969. The judgment of the High Court is reported at 1974 33 STC 1 (Mad.). C.A. No. 421 of 1976 was by special leave from the judgment and order Dt. 20 February 1974, of the Madras High Court in Tax Case No. 1 of 1974. The judgment of the High Court is reported at 1975 35 STC 50 (Mad.).
The Order of the Court was as follows:
After hearing learned counsel for the parties, we do not find any merit in these appeals.
1. The appeals are accordingly dismissed. There will be no order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed.
