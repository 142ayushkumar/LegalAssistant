State of Haryana and Others v Dhoop Singh
Supreme Court of India

19 February 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.18794/2000
The order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
1.  In view of the decision of this Court in State of Haryana vs. Haryana Veterinary and AHTS Association and Another, this appeal is allowed and the judgment of the High Court is set aside.
No costs.
Appeal allowed.
