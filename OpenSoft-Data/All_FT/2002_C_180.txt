Vijay Kumar Talwar v Commissioner of Income Tax
Supreme Court of India

15 April 2002
S.L.P. (Civil) Nos. 7555-7556 of 2002 (From the judgment and order dated 21/12/2001 and 19.2.2002 in ITA 202/01, and RA 10/02 in ITA 66/01 of the DELHI HIGH COURT)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  It is pointed out by the learned counsel for the petitioner that the authorities below proceeded on the basis that cash amount of Rs.3,49,991 was recovered at the time of the raid. The learned counsel also points out that this is factually incorrect as could be seen from the original order.
2. Issue notice.
Order accordingly
