State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v Champat Singh
Supreme Court of India

3 December 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.5943/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
1.  After hearing the counsel for the parties and in view of the special circumstances of the case, we decline to interfere with the order under challenge except to the extent that the respondent shall not be entitled to any backwages from the date of his termination till the date of the award.
Appeal is disposed of in the aforesaid terms.
Appeal disposed of.
