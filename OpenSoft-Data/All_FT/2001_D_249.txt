Durga Shankar v Bherulal and Anr
Supreme Court of India

7 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.8129/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We are not inclined to interfere in the matter. The special leave petition is dismissed.
2.  Learned counsel for the petitioner then stated that in case the petitioner is required to vacate the premises in question immediately, he would be put to great hardship and for that reason the petitioner may be granted some time to vacate the premises.
3. We, accordingly, direct that the petitioner shall not be dispossessed from the premises till March 31, 2002 subject to filing a usual undertaking in this Court within four weeks' from today. In case the respondents have any grievance to this part of our order, it shall be open to them to apply for vacation of the same.
Petition dismissed.
