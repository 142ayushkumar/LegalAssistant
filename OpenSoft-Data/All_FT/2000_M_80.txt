M.P. Laghu Udyog Nigam Limited v Mohd. Imran and Others
Supreme Court of India

13 November 2000
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.1454/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave is granted.
1.  In view of the fact that the order impugned is an interlocutory order, we are not inclined to interfere in the said order.
2. However, we leave it open to the appellant to move the High Court for expeditious hearing of the Writ Petition.
The appeal is accordingly dismissed.
Appeal dismissed.
