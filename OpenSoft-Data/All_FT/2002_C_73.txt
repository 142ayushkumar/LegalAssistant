Collector Of Central Excise, Jaipur v Associated Stone Industries (Kota) Limited, Kota, Rajasthan
Supreme Court of India

3 October 2002
C.A. No. 1355 (NM) of 1994
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard the learned counsel for the parties.
1.  We have gone through the judgment and order passed by CEGAT.
It cannot be held that cutting, edging, trimming, polishing and other processes on the marble slabs amount to a process of manufacture as it does not bring in a distinct product.
2. Hence, this appeal is dismissed. There shall be no order as to costs.
Appeal dismissed.
