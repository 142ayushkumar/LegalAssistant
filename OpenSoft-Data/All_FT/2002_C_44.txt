Chiranjilal Kesharwani v Messrs Grasim Cement and Others
Supreme Court of India

9 January 2002
The order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We are not inclined to allow the transfer prayed for. However, we permit the petitioner to move an application for exemption from personal appearance in the Court after making the initial appearance. If any such application is made the trial court shall exempt him from physical appearance on the following conditions:
1) A counsel on behalf of the petitioner would be present in the court whenever his case is taken up;
2) The petitioner will not dispute his identity as the accused in the case;
3) The petitioner will be present in the court when ever such presence is imperatively needed.
With these observations this transfer petition is disposed of.
Petition disposed of.
