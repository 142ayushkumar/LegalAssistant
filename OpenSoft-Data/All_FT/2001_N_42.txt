Nagrik Upbhokta M. Manch v U.O.I. and Others
Supreme Court of India

20 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.14945/1999
The order of the Court was as follows:
List for final disposal on a non-miscellaneous day.
1.  The State of Madhya Pradesh shall file a statement, duly supported by an affidavit, giving details of the amount collected under the impugned orders of the Director Food and Civil Supplies and Collector showing how it has been utilised.
2. Consequent upon formation of the State of Chhattisgarh, during the pendency of this petition, on an oral prayer made by the learned counsel for the petitioner, we direct the State of Chhattisgarh to be impleaded as respondent No.4. Notice shall issue to the newly added respondent returnable in four weeks.
Order accordingly.
