Commissioner of Central Excise, New Delhi v I. T. C. Limited and Others
Supreme Court of India

16 January 1999
C.A. No. 6101-6113/98
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  C.A. Nos. 6102/98, 6103/98, 6110/98, 6111/98, 6112/98 and 6113/98. The appeals are dismissed. C.A. Nos. 6104 to 6109 of 1998. The appeals are admitted only restricted to the question relating to Section 11A, main part, liability for six months.
C.A. Nos. 6101/98 and 70/99. The appeals are admitted.
2.  Adjudication proceedings will go on. But no order will be passed. Both parties are at liberty to move in the light of the adjudication proceedings. Liberty to apply for expeditious hearing.
Order accordingly.
