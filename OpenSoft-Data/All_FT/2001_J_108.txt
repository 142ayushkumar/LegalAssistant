Prayag Sangeet Samiti v Jitendra Nath Singh and Others
Supreme Court of India

3 January 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.17815/1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  During the course of hearing this Court was informed about an order passed on 3.11.2000 in SLP(C) No.16720/99 wherein status quo was directed to be maintained, though, however, illegal structures to the extent declared by the High Court could be removed by the authorities.
2. The learned counsel submitted that it is the same judgment which is in appeal in the present petition and the same order should be passed here also.
3.  Delay condoned. Leave granted. Status quo to the same extent as was passed in SLP(C) 16720/99.
Order accordingly.
