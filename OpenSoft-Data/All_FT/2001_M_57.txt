B.C. Nagaraj and Anr v M. Subbaiah(Dead) By LRs
Supreme Court of India

2 March 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.5965/2000
The order of the court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
1.  After hearing the counsel for the parties, we set aside the order of the High Court without expressing any opinion on the merits of the case and direct the High Court to decide R.S.A. No.962 of 1996 afresh in accordance with law.
The appeal is disposed of.
Appeal disposed of.
