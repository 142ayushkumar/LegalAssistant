Union of India and Anr v Rajesh Ranjan Pappu Yadav
Supreme Court of India

19 July 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 243/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  On behalf of petitioner - Union of India a request is made for adjourning this matter to 25.7.2001 due to personal inconvenience of the Solicitor General of India.
2. On 10.5.2001, Mr. Harish Salve, learned Solicitor General of India offered to produce a copy of the entire confession including a copy of the minutes of the Magistrate who recorded the confession of Ranjan Tiwari - the co-accused.
3.  Due to some reasons according to the learned counsel this could not be achieved thus far and it will possibly be produced by the next posting date.
List on 25.7.2001.
Order accordingly.
