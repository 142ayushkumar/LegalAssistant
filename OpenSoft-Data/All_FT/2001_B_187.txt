Union of India and Others v Bheek Singh Rathore and Others
Supreme Court of India

5 February 2001
IA No 3 In C.A..No.5795/2000
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  The L.Rs. of deceased Respondent No.2 are brought on record. The respondents are the employees of the Unit Run Canteen at Jodhpur. The question involved in this case is fully covered by the judgment of this Court in Union of India and Ors. v. M. Aslam and Ors.
2. This Civil Appeal accordingly stands disposed of in terms of the said judgment.
Appeal disposed of.
