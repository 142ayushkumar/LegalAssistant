Allbank Finance Limited v Custodian and Others
Supreme Court of India

4 December 2000
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.7082/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
We have heard the learned counsel for the parties.
1.  Without going into the merits of the case, in our opinion, the Special Court should have allowed the amendment prayed for subject to such objections which may be taken in the amended written statement.
2.  We, accordingly, allow this appeal, set aside the order of the Special Court and direct that the application for amendment filed by the appellant will stand allowed.
No costs.
Appeal allowed.
