Dean, Goa Dental College and Hospital v Taneshwar Singh Chahal and Others
Supreme Court of India

1  May  2001
C.As..2876 and Mr. Chand Kiran, adv. 2877/98 Mr. Vijay Prakash, adv
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  W.P.(C) NO.420/2000 List the writ petition for hearing in the last week of July, 2001. The case need not be treated as part-heard. At the oral request of Mr. D.D. Thakur, learned senior counsel appearing for the petitioner, we permit impleadment of Medical Council of India and direct a notice to be issued to the Medical Council of India.
2. Mr. Maninder Singh, learned counsel is present and accepts notice on behalf of Medical Council of India.
C.A. NOS.2876/98, 2877/98 and 1966/2000 List along with Writ Petition (C) No.420 of 2000.
Order accordingly.
