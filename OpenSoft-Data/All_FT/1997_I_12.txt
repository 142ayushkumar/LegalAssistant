Ilac Limited v Collector Of Central Excise
Supreme Court of India

6  May  1997
C.As. Nos. 4443-4444 of 1985
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1.  Learned counsel for the appellant submits the appellant have given no instructions to the advocate on record. The appeals are, therefore, dismissed for want of prosecution. No order as to costs.
Appeal dismissed.
