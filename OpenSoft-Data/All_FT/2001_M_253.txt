Md. Hafiz Siddique v State of Bihar
Supreme Court of India

9 February 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 301/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Heard Mr. Diwedi for the petitioner and learned counsel appearing for the State of Bihar.
2. Having regard to the facts and circumstances of the case and taking into account that the accused has already been in custody for about 9 months and the investigation is complete and charge-sheet has been filed, we direct that the petitioner be released on bail to the satisfaction of Chief Judicial Magistrate, Patna. While on bail, the petitioner shall regularly appear on the date to which the criminal case is posted and shall not try to tamper with the prosecution evidence in any manner.
The Special Leave Petition is disposed of.
Petition disposed of.
