V.B. Ramesh Gowda v Mgmt.Of Sri A. Shikshana Trust(R) and Anr
Supreme Court of India

29 October 2001
I.A.No.1 in C.A. No.6379/2001
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  Mr. SS Javali, learned senior counsel stated that the respondent-Management is prepared to reinstate the appellant here in provided the Management be not directed to pay the arrears of salary during pendency of appeal. In view of this statement, we direct the respondent-Management to take back the appellant in service.
2. However, the Management shall not be required to pay the arrears of salary. The appellant shall be entitled to receive the salary after the date of reinstatement as and when it falls due.
I.A. No. 1 is disposed of accordingly.
Application disposed of.
