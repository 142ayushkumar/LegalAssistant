M. S. Chidambara Jois and Others v State of Karnataka and Others
Supreme Court of India

18 March 1994
C.A. Nos. 2100-2121 of 1994 (From the Judgment and Order Dt. 1 September 1992 of the Karnataka High Court in W.A. No. 1588 of 1992 etc. etc.)
The Judgments of the Court was delivered by: N. P. SINGH, J.
1.  IA No. 2 of 1994 in Special Leave Petition (C) No. 14290 of 1992 for bringing the Applicant as Legal Representative of the deceased-petitioner, is allowed.
Leave granted in all the above mentioned special leave petitions.
2.  Civil Appeals arising out of the above mentioned special leave petitions are allowed.
3. In terms of the judgment delivered today in Civil Appeal No.2099 of 1994, arising out of Special Leave Petition No.6068 of 1991, and the appeals filed before the High Court on behalf of the appellants, shall be heard afresh and disposed of in the light of the aforesaid judgment of this Court.
Appeals disposed of.
