Kusum Kotnala v Anil Kotnala
Supreme Court of India

22 August 2008
Transfer Petition (Civil) No. 671 of 2007
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1.  This is a wife's transfer petition. We are not concerned with the merits of the case. The petitioner is employed in Delhi. In the circumstances, we pass the following order:
2.  Marriage Suit No.403 of 2006 titled as Anil Kotnala Vs.Kusum Kotnala, pending before the Family Court, Dehradun under Section 13(a) of the Hindu Marriage Act, is directed to be transferred to the District Judge, Tis Hazari, and Delhi, who is requested, in turn, to assign the matter to the concerned Court.
The transfer petition is disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of
