Pramod Kumar Mahajan v Punjab National Bank and Others
Supreme Court of India

17 November 2000
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.10040/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  It is pointed out by the learned senior counsel for the respondents that the subject matter of the criminal case relates only to certain irregularities in the accounts maintained by the respondents in connection, one account, namely the bank account of S.K.Industries whereas the departmental case relates to about twenty one accounts of other companies or enterprises.
2. In the circumstances, we direct that the disciplinary action so far as the accounts relating to S.K. Industries may not be finalised for the present.
3.  It is, however, open to the respondents to proceed with the charges relating to other accounts and pass appropriate orders in accordance with law.
The SLP is disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
