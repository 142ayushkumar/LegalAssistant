Javed Abidi v Union of India and Another
Supreme Court of India

3 October 2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Leave is given to the petitioner, who is appearing in person to file an affidavit as rejoinder to the affidavit filed by the Joint Secretary, Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Shastri Bhawan, New Delhi. Such affidavit be filed by the petitioner within a period of three weeks from the date hereof.
2. In the meantime, Mr. N.N.Goswami, learned counsel appearing for the Union of India, would also make an endeavour to list out the employment percentage of handicapped persons in all the Public Sector Undertakings within the available time frame.
3.  Let the matter appear on 7th December, 2001 after the miscellaneous matters.
Order accordingly.
