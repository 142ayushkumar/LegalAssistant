Commissioner of Income Tax v Ambalal Sarabhai D. Trust
Supreme Court of India

21 March 1996
C.A. No. 5194 of 1996 (From the Judgment and order Dt. 29 March 1989 of the Gujarat High Court in ITA No. 59 of 1989)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted
1.  The impugned order of the High Court is set aside. The High Court is requested to examine the question in the light of the judgment of this Court in the case of CIT vs. Kamalini Khatau 1994 Indlaw SC 1625 : 1994 Indlaw SC 1625 with reference to the facts of the present case.
The appeal is allowed. No costs.
Appeal allowed.
