Sunita Sharma v Kuldeep Sharma
Supreme Court of India

13 October 2008
TRANSFER PETITION (C) NO. 842 OF 2007
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  In the facts and circumstances of the case, HMA No. 32/2007 titled Dr. Kuldeep Sharma vs. Smt. Sunita Sharma, pending before the Chief Judge, Family Court, Indore, Madhya Pradesh is directed to be transferred to District Judge, Jodhpur, Rajasthan who shall deal with and decide the said case himself or assign the same to some other court of competent jurisdiction.
The Transfer Petition is allowed accordingly.
Petition allowed
