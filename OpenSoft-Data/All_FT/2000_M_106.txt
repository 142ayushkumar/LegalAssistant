Mahanagar Taxi Union v State of Maharashtra and Others
Supreme Court of India

20 November 2000
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.18123/2000
The Order of the court was as follows:
Leave is granted.
None appears for the State.
1.  Heard learned counsel for the appellant as well as the contesting respondents.
2.  We are not inclined to interfere with the order under challenge. However, having regard to the peculiar facts and circumstances of the case, we consider it just and appropriate to modify the date mentioned so as to read `from February 1, 2001' for the words `from November 1, 2000'.
Subject to above modification, the Appeal is dismissed.
Appeal dismissed.
