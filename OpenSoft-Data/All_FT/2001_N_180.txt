Nizam Sugar Factory Limited v Collector of Central Excise
Supreme Court of India

11 January 2001
C.A. Nos. 1742-44 and 2619-20 of 1991
The Order of the Court is as follows:-
Civil Appeal Nos. 2619-20 of 1991: In view of the decisions of this Court in Belarpur Sugar & Allied Industries Ltd. v Collector of Central Excise, Aurangabad 1999 Indlaw SC 10711999 Indlaw SC 1071 and Neoli Sugar Products Factory Ltd. v. Commissioner of Central Excise, Kanpur 1999 Indlaw SC 13571999 Indlaw SC 1357.
1.   These appeals are allowed, the order of the Customs, Excise and Gold (Control) Appellate Tribunal is set aside and it is held that the appellant would be entitled to the benefit of the Notification in question with effect from 1st May, 1982 to 30th September, 1982.
No costs.
Civil Appeal Nos. 1742-44 of 1991
2.   In view of the ratio of decisions of this Court in Belarpur Sugar and Allied Industries Ltd, and Neoli Sugar Products Factory Ltd.  These appeals are also allowed, the order of the Customs, Excise and Gold (Control) Appellate Tribunal is set aside and it is held that the appellant would be entitled to the benefit of the Notification dated 27th April, 1983.
Appeals allowed.
