Gurdev Singh v State of Punjab and Others Etc
Supreme Court of India

31 October 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.20928-20929/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  It is reported that sub-Registrar concerned has registered all the 21 documents pursuant to the direction passed by this Court on 7.9.2001. In view of this we are not disposed to continue with the contempt proceedings. They will stand dropped.
2.  We make it clear that the registration of the documents is subject to the final result of Suit No.211 of 20.10.1999 pending before Addl.Civil Judge (Senior Division), Pathankot, Punjab. We also wish to add that the dispute regarding title or possession of the property has not been determined by us as they are to be determined by the court before which the suit is pending.
3.  No alienation of the properties involved in the lis will be made without permission of the court concerned so long as the suit is pending.
With these observations these Special Leave Petitions are disposed of.
Petitions disposed of.
