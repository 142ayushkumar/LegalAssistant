L. M. L. Fibres Limited v Deputy Commissioner of Income Tax and Others
Supreme Court of India

26 August 1991
C.A. No. 3424 of 1991 (Arising out of S.L.P. C No. 9124 of 1991)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Mr. Manoj Arora, Advocate, takes notice on behalf of all the respondents. Special leave granted. Heard counsel on both sides.
2.  It appears to us that both order of the CIT dt. 8th January, 1991 made under s. 26 of the IT Act, 1961, and the judgment of the High Court dt. 16th April, 1991 impugned in this civil appeal require to be and are hereby set aside.
3.  The appellant's revision petition before the CIT under s. 264 of the IT Act is remitted to the Commissioner for a fresh disposal in accordance with law after affording to the appellant an opportunity of being heard. Ordered accordingly.
Order accordingly.
