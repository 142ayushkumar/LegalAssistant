Commissioner of Central Excise, Meerut-I v Wazir Industries and Another
Supreme Court of India

11 September 2001
CIVIL APPEAL NOS.3645-3646 OF 2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The point in issue is covered by the decision of this Court in Commissioner of Central Excise & Customs Vs. Venus Castings (P) Ltd..
2. In view of the same, the appeals are allowed and the judgment of the Tribunal is set aside.
Appeals allowed.
