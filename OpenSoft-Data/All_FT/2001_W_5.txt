Waris Ali v Shohrab Vali Mohamed and Others
Supreme Court of India

24 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.9375/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  Learned counsel for the parties stated that they have no objection if the order under challenge is set aside and the case is sent back to the trial court for decision on merits. In view of the aforesaid statement, the judgment under challenge is set aide and the matters are remitted to the trial court for decision on merits, including on the question of fraud on judicial side.
2. The appeals are allowed. There shall be no order as to costs.
Appeals allowed.
