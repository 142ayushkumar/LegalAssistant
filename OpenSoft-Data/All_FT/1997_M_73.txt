Collector Of Central Excise Indore v M/S. Orient Paper Mills
Supreme Court of India

7  May  1997
Civil Appeal Nos. 3596-97 of 1989
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. The principle laid down in the judgment delivered by us today in Civil Appeal Nos. 3596-97 of 1989 Collector of Central Excise Patna vs. Tata Engineering & Locomotive Co 1987 Indlaw CEGAT 742., Jamshedpur, being applicable, these appeals are also dismissed, with no order as to costs.
