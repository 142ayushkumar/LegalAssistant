Chatterjee Petrochem (India) Private Limited v Haldia Petrochemicals Limited and others
Supreme Court of India

27 August 2008
Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No(s). 22655-22658/2007, S.L.P.(C) Nos. 24737-24740 of 2007, S.L.P.(C) No. 21536 of 2007
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. Learned senior counsel appearing for the petitioner states that petitioner had made huge investments and petitioner's petition filed u/s. 397 and 398 of the Companies Act, 1956 has been held to be not maintainable by the High Court.
2. Keeping in view the stakes and the points of law involved in this case, we grant leave in this matter and direct the office to place the papers before Hon ble the Chief Justice for listing this matter for final disposal somewhere in the month of November, 2008. Liberty to mention. The Application for intervention/impleadment is allowed as learned senior counsel for the appellant has no objection.
Application allowed
