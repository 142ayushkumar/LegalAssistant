Mohd. Hussain v State Of AP
Supreme Court of India

4 March 2013
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Crl) No(s).1693/2013
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1. Dismissed.
2. However, we direct the Superintendent, Central Prison, Cherlapalli, Ranga Reddy District, to inform this Court by filing an appropriate report as to when the petitioner was taken into custody.
3. We also intend to know that if there was no interim order passed by this Court, how the authorities allowed the petitioner to remain outside in spite of order of conviction passed by the High Court.
4. The aforementioned officer shall file his report within two weeks' time from today. The Registry is directed to inform the contents of this order to the Superintendent of Prison, Cherlapalli, Ranga Reddy District through FAX. List the matter after two weeks.
Order accordingly
