Darshan Singh and Anr v Gram Panchayat Rajni Devi
Supreme Court of India

20 November 2000
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.4689/2000
The Order of the court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1. We are of opinion that until the suit is heard and decided, status quo as on to-day should be maintained. We therefore, order the arrangement made by the District Court to revive and be in force during the pendency of the suit.
2.  It shall not be misunderstood that we have considered the case on merits and made any observation in favour or against either of the parties. This arrangement is only for the temporary period. We direct the suit to be expedited and disposed of as early as possible. The appeal is disposed in the above terms. No costs.
Appeal disposed of.
