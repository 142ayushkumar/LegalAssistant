State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v Aditya Prakash Pandey and Others
Supreme Court of India

2 January 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal(Civil)...CC 8540/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  This Special Leave Petition has been filed after a delay of 496 days. It is filed against an order of the High Court of Judicature at Allahabad which had dismissed the Special Appeal on the ground that that was delayed by one year and 268 days. Over and above, the counsel had not disputed that the matter was concluded against the petitioner.
2. In such state of affairs to have recommended the filing of present S.L.P. is a waste of public money.
3.  We impose a cost of Rs.1,000/- on the person who had recommended to file the present S.L.P. The cost be paid to the Supreme Court Legal Services Committee. The Special Leave Petition is dismissed with the above observations.
Petition dismissed.
