Messrs Swaraj Ashram, Kanpur v Industrial Tribunal (Iii) U. P. Kanpur and Others
Supreme Court of India

29 November 1978
C.A. Nos. 1274 to 1276 of 1973
The Judgment was delivered by: KRISHNA IYER, J.
1.  In the light of the decision of this Court in counsel on both sides agree that the appellant comes within the definition of 'industry.'
2. The appeals, on this point, therefore, fail. The consequence is that the Industrial Tribunal has to take up the proceedings and continue to adjudicate on the dispute and give its award. No costs.
Appeals dismissed.
