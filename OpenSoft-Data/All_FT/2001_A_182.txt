Sunil Advani v Anil Advani and Anr
Supreme Court of India

17 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.13161/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  This appeal is directed against the order of the High Court dismissing the application for condonation of delay of 178 days. We have perused the record and are satisfied that the appellant has made out sufficient cause for condonation of delay.
2. We, accordingly, condone the delay and set aside the judgment under challenge.
3.  The appeal is allowed. The matter is remitted to the High Court for deciding the appeal on merits. There shall be no order as to costs.
Appeal allowed.
