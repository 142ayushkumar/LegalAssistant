Commissioner of Customs, Meerut-Ii v Messrs Modi Xerox Limited, Modipur
Supreme Court of India

13 March 2001
C.A. No.2094/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  It is common ground that the appeal is covered by the decision of this Court in Union of India vs. Solar Pesticides Pvt.Ltd.
2. Therefore, following that judgment, the appeal is allowed. The order under appeal is set aside. The matter is remanded to the Assistant Commissioner to decide the refund claim afresh, as to whether or not the incidence of the duty on the imported raw material had been passed on by the respondent to any other person. Liberty to the assessee to produce evidence in this behalf.
No order as to costs.
Appeal allowed.
