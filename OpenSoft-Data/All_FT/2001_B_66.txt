State of Punjab and Others v Bakhshish Singh Roy and Others
Supreme Court of India

1 August 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.760/2001 in S.L.P.(Civil)No.3650/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There has been an inordinate delay of 458 days in filing the Review Petition for which there is no satisfaction explanation. Even on merits, we do not a case for our interference.
2. The Review Petition is dismissed on the ground of delay as well as on merits.
Review dismissed.
