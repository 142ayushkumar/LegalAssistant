S.T. Sevrins High School & Anr v State of Bihar & Ors
Supreme Court of India

3  May  2001
C.A. No.2135 of 1997
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  After hearing learned counsel for the parties, we find no reason to disagree with the decision of the High Court.
The civil appeal is dismissed.
2. Now that the school has been recognised, it will be open to the appellants to make a representation to the State Government to stop any further collection from them for the relevant period.
No costs.
Appeal dismissed.
