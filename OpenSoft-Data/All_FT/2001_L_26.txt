Lal Chand and Anr v Nitya Nand
Supreme Court of India

12 October 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.8043/2001
The order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We are not inclined to interfere in the matter. The special leave petition is dismissed.
2.  Learned counsel for the petitioners then stated that in case the petitioners are required to vacate the premises in question immediately, they shall be put to great hardship and for that reason the petitioners may be granted some time to vacate the premises.
3. We, accordingly, direct that the petitioners shall not be dispossessed from the premises till 30th September, 2002 subject to filing a usual undertaking in this Court within four weeks' from today. In case the respondent has any grievance to this part of our order, it shall be open to him to apply for vacation of the same.
Petition dismissed.
