T.R. Sankarakkutty and Others v Director of Municipal Admn. and Others
Supreme Court of India

7 February 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.150/2001 in S.L.P.(C) No.17905/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Delay condoned.
1.  Except for reiteration, no fresh grounds have been made out in the petition and we have also carefully gone through the Review Petition and the connected papers.
2. We do not find any merit in this petition. This petition is accordingly dismissed.
Review dismissed.
