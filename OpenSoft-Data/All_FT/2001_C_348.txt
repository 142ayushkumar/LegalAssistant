Commnr. of Income Tax, Jodhpur v Messrs Ajay Vijay Traders
Supreme Court of India

25 January 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.6442-6447/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
1.  After hearing the counsel for the parties, we are of the opinion that the High Court should have admitted the appeals under Section 260A of the Income-tax, Act, 1961 and decide the cases on merit.
2. We, accordingly, allow the appeals, set aside the judgments of the High Court and restore the appeals filed by the appellant on the board of the High Court. We make it clear that we are not expressing any opinion on the merits of these cases.
Appeals allowed.
