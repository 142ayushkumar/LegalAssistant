Commissioner Of Central Excise, Calcutta v Suntrack Electronics (P) Ltd
Supreme Court of India

18 November 2002
C.A. No. D11677 of 2002, Dt. 18 November 2002
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The Tribunal in the impugned order has relied upon its earlier order made in A-1824 CAL/2000, dated 8-11-2000 which the Revenue has not chosen to challenge. Therefore, we are not inclined to entertain this appeal.
The appeal is dismissed accordingly.
Appeal dismissed.
