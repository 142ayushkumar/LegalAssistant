Commissioner of Income Tax v P. V. A. L. Kulandagan Chettiar
Supreme Court of India

1 November 2007
Review Petition (Civil) D. No. 11645 of 2007 In C.As. Nos. 5752, 5754 to 5756, 5760, 5761, 5746 and 6229 of 1997 and Nos. 2006 and 2451 of 2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There is inordinate delay of 1027 days in filing of the review petitions and no satisfactory explanation has been offered.
Even otherwise, we do not find any ground, whatsoever, to entertain the same.
2. The review petition is dismissed on the ground of delay as well as on the merits.
Review dismissed.
