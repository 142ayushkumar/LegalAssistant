Anganwadi Workers and Helpers Union v State of Manipur and Another
Supreme Court of India

1 December 2008
Civil Appeal No.7064 of 2008
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Leave granted.
1.  This appeal is directed against an interim order dated 03.10.2007 passed by the High Court in W.A.No.69 of 2007 directing the State Government not to fill up 24 posts of Supervisors.
2.  It has been brought to our notice that excepting these 24 posts as directed by the High Court, all other posts have already been filled up. Without going into all this, we dispose of this appeal by requesting the High Court to dispose of the Writ Petitions, pending before it, on merits within two months positively from the date of supply of a copy of this Order.
3.  We make it clear that all the appointments that may be made or have been made will abide by the result of the Writ Petitions. The appeal is disposed of accordingly.
There shall be no order as to costs.
Appeal disposed of
