Commercial Tax Check Post, Ratanpur v Perfect Thread Mills Limited
Supreme Court of India

19 January 2009
Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No(s). 25047/2008
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Delay condoned.
Leave granted.
1.  The issue is squarely covered by our two judgments in the case of Guljag Industries Vs. Commercial Taxes Officer reported in 2007 (7) SCC 269 2007 Indlaw SC 888 and Assistant Commercial Taxes Officer Vs. M/s Bajaj Electricals Limited reported in 2008 (14) SCALE 380 2008 Indlaw SC 1921, in favour of the Department.
2. Accordingly, the Civil Appeal filed by the Department stands allowed with no order as to costs.
Appeal allowed
