NTC (IDA) Employees Assn. v Union of India
Supreme Court of India

27 September 2002
S.L.P. (C) No. 16732 of 1997 with TPs (C) Nos. 409 and 490 of 1997
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Intervention application is dismissed.
1.  We have been informed that BIFR has already formulated eight schemes which stand approved by all concerned and agencies. Let the scheme as sanctioned by BIFR be implemented. The special leave petition and the transfer petitions stand disposed of accordingly.
Petitions disposed of.
