Municipal Corporation of Gr.Bombay and Others v St. Joseph's Home Society and Anr
Supreme Court of India

27 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.6708-6709/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Delay condoned.
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
Leave granted.
1.  Considering the circular dated 26th September, 1968 issued by the Superintendent of Aided Schools the impugned order passed by the High Court on 15th November, 1999 shall continue till the petition is decided. This shall be without prejudice to the rights and contentions of the parties.
2. In view of this order the clarificatory order passed by the High Court on 18th January, 2000 would not survive. However, it is made clear that total three peons are permitted to be appointed in view of the above said circular.
3.  The appeals stand disposed of accordingly with no order as to costs.
Appeal disposed of.
