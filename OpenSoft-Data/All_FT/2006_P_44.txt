State of Rajasthan and another v Partap Singh and others
Supreme Court of India

22 September 2006
Civil Appeal No(s). 6292 of 2004, CA Nos. 6306, 6307, 6318, 6293, 6302, 6305, 6321, 7678/2004 and CA No. @ SLP(C) No. 16359/2003
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. Leave granted in SLP(C)No. 16359/03.
2. Appeals are allowed setting aside the impugned judgments of the Punjab and Haryana High Court. However, whatever benefits, if any, received by the respondents would not be recovered and they will not be entitled to fixation under Rule 4.4 of the Rules. No order as to costs.
Order accordingly
