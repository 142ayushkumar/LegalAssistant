Prasanta Kumar Sukul and Others v Damodar Valley Corporation and Others
Supreme Court of India

12 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.19268/2000
The order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The High Court made a direction that the absorption of the writ petitioners who are petitioners before us shall abide by the decision of the larger Bench of this Court in Steel Authority of India Ltd. and Ors. Vs. National Union Water Front Workers and Ors.
2.  Now as this question has already been decided by this Court, it would be appropriate for the parties to work out their rights in terms of the decision referred to above.
The special leave petition is disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
