Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams and Anr v P Bhaskar and Others
Supreme Court of India

14 September 2001
IA Nos. 11-12, 13-14 and 15-16 in C.A. No.1030-1031/1998
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Having heard Mr. M.N. Rao, learned counsel for the applicants, Mr. P.P.Rao, learned senior counsel for the Devasthanams and the learned counsel appearing for the State of Andhra Pradesh, notwithstanding the circular dated 2 4.11.1996 which was placed before us on behalf of the Devasthanam, and particularly clause (iv) thereof, we clarify that the employees concerned will get the minimum salary in the minimum scale of pay and the dearness allowance permissible thereon as per clause (i) of the said circular.
2. Devasthanams would do well in making these payments within a period of three months from today, after adjusting the payments already made. The amount which has been lying in deposit of this Court pursuant to interim order may be refunded to Devasthanams.
I.As. stand disposed of accordingly.
Applications disposed of.
