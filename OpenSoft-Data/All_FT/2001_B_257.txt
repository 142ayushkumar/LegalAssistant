State of Bihar and Others v Bokaro and Ramgur Limited and Others
Supreme Court of India

10 December 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.10793/2000
The order of the court was as follows:
I.A. No. 2 is allowed.
1. Let the State of Jharkhand be substituted in place of State of Bihar.
I.A. No. 3 is allowed.
2. Let the name of Inder Jeetender Narain Singh be substituted in place of Respondent Nos. 4(b) and 4(c).
3.  It was brought to our notice that respondent has filed a review petition before the High Court, but the same could not be heard on account of the fact that the State of Bihar/State of Jharkhand has filed the present petition.
4. We may clarify that the pendency of this petition shall not debar the High Court to decide the review petition filed by the respondent.
Applications allowed.
