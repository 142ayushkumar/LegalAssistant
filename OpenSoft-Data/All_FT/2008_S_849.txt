G. S. F. Medical and Para Medical Asson v State of Gujarat and Another
Supreme Court of India

25 November 2008
Civil Appeal No. 6871 of 2008 (Arising out of SLP(C) No. 11930/2004) With Civil Appeal No. 5285/2005
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Heard learned counsel for both the parties. Leave granted.
1. The matters relate to the admission of students for medical course in the private colleges of Medical Association in the State of Gujarat. This Court passed an interim order on 16.9.2004 by which the admissions for that year were regulated. Now we are told that the students got admission already.
2. The interim order passed on 16.9.2004 is made absolute. The appeals are disposed of accordingly.
Appeals disposed of
