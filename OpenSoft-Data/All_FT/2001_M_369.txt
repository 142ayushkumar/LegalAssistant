Messrs New Raj Theatre, Punjab and Anr v Tirath Ram
Supreme Court of India

30 April 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.231/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard.
1.  We are not inclined to interfere with the impugned order passed by the High Court in its revisional jurisdiction as we do not find any error therein. The SLP accordingly stands dismissed.
2. The learned counsel for the tenant-petitioner however prays that some time be given to deliver the vacant possession of the premises in question.
3.  Having heard the learned counsel for the parities and in the facts and circumstances, we direct that the vacant premises in question should be delivered to the landlord-respondent by 30th of September, 2001 subject to the usual undertaking being given in this Court within four weeks from today.
Petition dismissed.
