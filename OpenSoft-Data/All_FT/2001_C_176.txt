Mahavirchand G. Mehta v City Indstrl.Dev.Corpn., Maharashtra and Others
Supreme Court of India

5 February 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.18189/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
The order appealed against is a cryptic order.
1.  Without setting out any reasons the matter has been disposed of by the High Court. In the circumstances, we set aside the order made by the High Court and remit the matter back to the High Court for fresh consideration in accordance with law.
2.  The appeal is accordingly allowed. Let the interim order made by this Court continue till disposal of the matter before the High Court. The High Court may dispose of the matter as expeditiously as possible.
Appeal allowed.
