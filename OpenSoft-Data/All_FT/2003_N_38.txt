Supreme Court of India

28 July 2003
S.L.Ps. (Cr.) Nos. 4734-35 of 2002
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  Having regard to the conflict in the decisions of this Court in Abdul Rashid Ibrahim Mansuri v. State of Gujarat,2000 Indlaw SC 705 and Sajan Abraham v. State of Kerala,2001 Indlaw SC 20048, both rendered by a three-Judge Bench, relating to the provisions of Section 42 of the NDPS Act, these appeals are referred to a larger Bench.
Tag with Crl. Appeal No.36 of 2003.
2.  Papers be placed before the Hon'ble the Chief Justice of India for appropriate orders.
Order accordingly.
