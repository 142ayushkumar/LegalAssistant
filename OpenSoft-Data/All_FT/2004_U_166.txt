Ramdas Athawale (2) v Union of India and others
Supreme Court of India

13 September 2004
W.P. (C) No. 86 of 2004
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Issue rule.
1.  This writ petition involves a substantial question of law as to the interpretation of Articles 85 and 87 of the Constitution and thus, we are of the opinion that having regard to clause (3) of Article 145 thereof it should be heard by a five-Judge Bench.
Order accordingly.
