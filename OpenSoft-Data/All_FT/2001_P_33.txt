P.J.Khobragade v State of Maharashtra and Others
Supreme Court of India

7 December 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.9453/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1. Notice was issued on 16.7.2001 to the respondents as to why the order under appeal should not be set aside and the matter be heard along with Writ Petition No. 2834/1999 and connected matters.
2.  Now having heard learned counsel on both sides, we are satisfied that this matter should be heard along with the said Writ Petition and connected matters by the High Court.  Therefore, the order under appeal is set aside and the matter is remitted to the High Court for consideration in accordance with law along with Writ Petition No. 2834/1999 and connected matters.
This appeal is allowed accordingly.
3.  In the meanwhile, status quo shall be continued so far as this matter is concerned.
Appeal allowed.
