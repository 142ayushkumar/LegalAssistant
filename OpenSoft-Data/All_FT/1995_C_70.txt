Collector Of Central Excise, Patna v Indian Tube Co. Ltd
Supreme Court of India

2 March 1995
C.A. No. 442 of 1989, Dt. 2 March 1995
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The Tribunal is right in saying that the diluted sulphuric acid, that is the liquid which remains after user, cannot be said to have been manufactured by the respondent and, therefore, no duty can be levied thereon.
The appeal is dismissed accordingly.
Appeal dismissed.
