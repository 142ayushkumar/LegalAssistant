Harbhajan Singh v Union of India and Anr
Supreme Court of India

26 March 2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Heard Mr. Goodwill Indeevar, learned counsel for the petitioner and Mr. Altaf Ahmad, learned Additional Solicitor General for the respondents.
2.  This writ petition is disposed of with the order that the respondents will send another copy of the prescribed form in which a freedom fighter is to apply/represent for getting pension under the Central scheme, within two weeks.
3. On receipt of the same the petitioner will furnish the necessary informations and particulars and take steps for submitting the said form according to the procedure prescribed under the scheme for grant of pension.
4.  The competent authority on receipt of the application/representation will dispose of the same as expeditiously as possible.
Petition disposed of.
