Vijay Khanna and Anr v State of Punjab
Supreme Court of India

26 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 6694/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We are not inclined to interfere with the impugned order, however, it is open to the petitioners to surrender before the court concerned. If they do so within two weeks from today and apply for bail the court concerned shall release them on bail on such conditions as the court deem fit.
2. One of the conditions shall be that the petitioners would report to the investigating officer on a specified date for the purpose of investigation. With these observations this Special Leave Petition is disposed of.
Petition disposed of.
