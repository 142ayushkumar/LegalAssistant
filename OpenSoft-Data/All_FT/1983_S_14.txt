Commissioner of Income Tax, Pune v Sandvik Asia Limited
Supreme Court of India

11 November 1983
C.A. No. 10279 of 1983
The Judgment was delivered by: V. Balakrishna Eradi, J.
1. This is a matter where, in our opinion, the four questions arising out of the order of the Tribunal which are set out in the judgement of the High Court dated 6th September, 1979 should have been referred to the High Court for its opinion.
2. We accordingly allow the appeal, set aside the judgment of the High Court and direct that these four questions will be referred by the Tribunal to the High Court together with a statement of case under s. 256(2) of the I.T. Act, 1961 .
Appeal allowed.
