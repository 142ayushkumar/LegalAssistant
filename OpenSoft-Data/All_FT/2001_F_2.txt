S.S. Patnaik v F.U. Choudhary and Anr
Supreme Court of India

15 January 2001
C.A..No.6358/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The impugned order of the High Court directing issue of contempt notice was by reason of the fact that there was an earlier order of the High Court in favour of the respondent herein. That order was stayed by this Court on 10th July, 2000 in S.L.P.(C) No. 1130/2000.
2.  In view of this order, the question of the High Court continuing with the contempt proceedings does not arise. This appeal is, accordingly, allowed and the impugned order of the High Court is set aside.
Appeal allowed.
