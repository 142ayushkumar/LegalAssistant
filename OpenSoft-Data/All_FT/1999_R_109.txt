Ramchandra Govind Dongre v Indian Hume Pipe Company Limited
Supreme Court of India

29 November 1999
S.L.P. (Civil) No. 9860 of 1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Leave granted. Learned counsel for the appellant has inter alia, cited a decision of this Court in U. P. State Coop. Land Development Bank Ltd. v. Chandra Bhan Dubey, 1998 Indlaw SC 2070 and the passage on which specific reliance is placed is quoted in our order dated 9-8-1999.
2. In our opinion, the view expressed in the said passage requires consideration by a larger Bench especially as it involves the interpretation of a constitutional provision article 226 of the Constitution.
3. We, therefore, direct that the papers be placed before the Hon'ble the Chief Justice of India for appropriate orders.
Order accordingly.
