Appasaheb Tatya Ugare and Anr v M.G. Joshi and Others
Supreme Court of India

23 July 2001
C.A..No.5096-5097/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The report of the tehsildar as directed has been placed on record. It is placed on record further that both the learned counsel in the mattter accepts the report, as filed by the tehsildar. There will be no further order at this stage. Advocates will be at liberty to apply for an early date of hearing.
2.  In the meantime, however, the appellant is directed to make deposit in terms of the report of the tehsildar.
Such deposit shall be made within a period of six months.
3.  The amount so deposited can however be withdrawn upon furnishing a bank guarantee and bank guarantee shall be kept alive till the disposal of these appeals.
Order accordingly.
