Amita v Union of India and Others
Supreme Court of India

10 August 2001
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  Learned Additional Solicitor General appearing for the Union of India prays for some time to file a detailed Report. He submits that a copy of the Report was given to learned counsel for the petitioner but the Report could not be filed in this Court.
2.  Learned counsel for the petitioner has filed an affidavit by way of a short response to the Report dated 31st May, 2001. Learned Additional Solicitor General submits that he shall take the points raised in the counter affidavit and see what remedial steps are possible in this behalf.
3. We grant five weeks' time to the Union of India to file the Report as also response to the counter affidavit, filed by the petitioner.
List after five weeks.
Order accordingly.
