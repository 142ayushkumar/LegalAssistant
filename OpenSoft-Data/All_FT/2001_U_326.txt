Murti Devi and Anr v Union of India
Supreme Court of India

11 December 2001
C.A.No.6572/2001-(Along with appln.(s) for c/delay in filing S.L.P.)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  It is not disputed that these appeals stand concluded in view of Constitution Bench decision of this Court in Sunder Vs. Union of India . We, therefore, hold that the appellants are entitled to interest under Sections 23(1-A) and 23(2) of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894.
2. However, such an interest is subject to the decision by this Court in C.A.No.6573/2001 titled as Union of India - Vs. Puran Singh.
The appeals shall stand disposed of in the aforesaid terms.
Appeals disposed of.
