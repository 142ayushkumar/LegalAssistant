Palak Jayantibhai Patel (Minor) and another v State of Gujarat and others
Supreme Court of India

22 August 2005
Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No(s). 15644-15645/2005
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  In so far as the eligibility to participate in the common entrance test is concerned, the direction of the High Court is varied to the extent that instead of 'aggregate 70% marks', for the present Academic Year it would be taken as 'aggregate 50% marks'.
2. To be heard along with S.L.P.(C) No.11930 of 2004 G.S.F. Medical and Para Medical Association Vs. State of Gujarat and Anr. 2004 Indlaw SC 505.
Order accordingly.
