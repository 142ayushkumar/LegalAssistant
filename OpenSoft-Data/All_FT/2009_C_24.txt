C.Nandini & Ors. v J. Harsha.
Supreme Court of India

13 July 2009
TRANSFER PETITION (C.) NO.198 OF 2008
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Heard both sides.
1.  The petitioner-wife is seeking transfer of a case pending before the Court of Asst. District & Sessions Judge, Tis Hazari Courts, Delhi in H.M.A.No.18/2008 titled as 'J.Harsha Vs. C. Nandini & Ors. to the Court at Asst. District & Sessions Judge, Mysore.
2.  The petitioner-wife is now staying with her parents at Mysore. Under the above said circumstances, we direct that H.M.A.No.18/2008 titled as 'J. Harsha Vs. C. Nandini & Ors. Be transferred from the Court of Asst. District & Sessions Judge, Tis Hazari Courts, and Delhi to the Court at Asst. District & Sessions Judge, Mysore. The transfer petition is allowed accordingly.
3.  Asst. District & Jussions Judge, Tis Hazari, Delhi is directed to transfer all the records to the Court of Asst. District & Sessions Judge, Mysore.
Petition allowed
