C. Subramaniam v Tamil Nadu Housing Board
Supreme Court of India

25 January 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.17862-17863/2000
The Order of the court was as follows:
1.  On 13th November, 2000, a limited notice was ordered by this Court on the special leave petition in the following terms:
"Issue notice limited to the question as to why it should not be clarified that the Trial Court should dispose of the case without being influenced by any observation made or any finding recorded on merits by the Trial Court as well as the High Court in the impugned order."
2. The learned counsel who entered appearance for the respondent has no objection for passing an order in the above terms.
3.  Accordingly, the special leave petitions are disposed of in the above terms.
Petitions disposed of.
