Safal Kumar Sood and Others v Varinder Lal Sood and Others
Supreme Court of India

27 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.18407/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the parties are agreed that this case be disposed of at this stage.
Leave granted.
2.  The sole question that arises in this appeal is whether the revision filed by the appellants is maintainable in law or not. We have heard counsel for the parties and perused the record. We are of the view that the revision was maintainable.
3. Under such circumstances, we set aside the order under challenge and send the case back to the High Court, to decide the same on merits.
4.  The appeal is allowed. There shall be no order as to costs.
Appeal allowed.
