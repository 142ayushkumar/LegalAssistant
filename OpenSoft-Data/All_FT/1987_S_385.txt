Hashmatullah v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others
Supreme Court of India

9 March 1987
W.P. (Civil) No. 1541 of 1986
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1. The question involved in this case is covered by the decision of this Court in Ishwar Singh Bagga v. State of Rajasthan 1986 Indlaw SC 225. No officer of the State Road Transport Corporation can be appointed to exercise powers under Section 129-A of the Motor Vehicles Act.
2. We therefore quash the impugned notification dated July 16, 1986 issued by the State of Uttar Pradesh appointing 8 classes of officers of the U.P. State Road Transport Corporation to exercise the powers under Section 129-A of the Motor Vehicles Act. The writ petition is allowed accordingly. No costs.
Petition allowed.
