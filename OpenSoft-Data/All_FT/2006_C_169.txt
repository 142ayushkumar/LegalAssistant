Pearl Polymers Limited v Commissioner of Central Excise, Navi Mumbai
Supreme Court of India

4  May  2006
C.A. No. 1666-1668 of 2005
The Order of the case was as follows:
Heard.
1.  The findings recorded by the Customs, Excise and Service Tax Appellate Tribunal, New Delhi that the duty was not paid by the assessee-appellant under protest is a finding of fact, which does not call for our interference in this batch of appeals.
2.  In view of this, without going into any other detail, the appeals are dismissed with no order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed.
