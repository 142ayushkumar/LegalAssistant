State of Gujarat v Mirzapur Moti Kureshi Kassab Jamat and others
Supreme Court of India

19 July 2005
C.A. No(s). 4937-4940 of 1998, C.A. Nos. 4941-4944 of 1998, C.A. No. 4945 of 1998
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. I.A. Nos. ...../2005 filed in Court by the appellant-State of Gujarat in C.A. Nos. 4937-4940/1998 are allowed. The appellant is permitted to refer to and rely upon the documents filed in connected C.A. Nos. 4941- 4944/1998.
2. Heard the learned counsel for the parties for some time.
3. It is pointed out that the present cases may call for re-consideration of some of the earlier decisions of this Court which are by Constitution Benches and hence it will be in the fitness of things if the matters are placed for hearing before a Bench of seven Judges.
4. It is also prayed that looking to the nature of the questions arising for decision, the matters may be heard at an early date.
5. Be listed accordingly.
6. The parties propose to file additional documents.
7. The appellants are allowed one week's time and the respondents one week thereafter for the purpose.
Order accordingly
