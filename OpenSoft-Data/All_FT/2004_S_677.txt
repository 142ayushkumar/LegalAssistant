Vikas v State of Maharashtra and others
Supreme Court of India

26 March 2004
I.A. Nos. 2-3 in Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No. 20799/2003
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  Printing dispensed with. The appeal shall be heard on the SLP paperbook. Parties may file additional documents, if any, within six weeks. Original record need not be requisitioned.
2. The application seeking stay of the Judgment of the High Court is rejected for the present.
Order accordingly.
