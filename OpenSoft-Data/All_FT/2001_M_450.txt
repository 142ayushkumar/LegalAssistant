Mohd. Islam v State of Bihar
Supreme Court of India

8  May  2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 1305/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  Petitioner is involved in an offence under Section 376 Indian Penal Code, 1860. He has been arrested on 8.4.2000. We do not find any reason to continue to keep the appellant as under trial prisoner.
2. We, therefore, order him to be released on bail on his executing a bond for such amount as to the satisfaction of the trial Judge with two solvent sureties. Appeal is disposed of.
Appeal disposed of.
