Sunder v State of Maharashtra
Supreme Court of India

3 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 2577/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  As the appellant is in jail as an undertrial prisoner for offence under Section 307 IPC, we as ascertained from the learned counsel for the State of Maharashtrra whether there are any strong reasons in not releasing him on bail.
2. We have not come across any such strong reason. We, therefore, order him to be released on bail on his executing a bond for such an amount as the trial judge may fix with two solvent sureties to his satisfaction.Appeal is disposed of accordingly.
Appeal disposed of.
