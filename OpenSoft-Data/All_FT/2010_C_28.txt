Transmission Corporation of Andhra Pradesh v Ch. Prabhakar and others
Supreme Court of India

29 September 2010
C.A. No. 6131 of 2002
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In view of the subsequent legislation, namely, Electricity Act, 2003, learned counsel appearing on behalf of the parties say that the matter has become academic.
2. Accordingly, we need not answer the reference made to the Constitution Bench. Consequently, nothing survives in this appeal and the same is accordingly dismissed.
Appeal dismissed.
