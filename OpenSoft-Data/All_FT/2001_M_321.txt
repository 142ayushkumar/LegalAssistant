Messrs Ghagra Sugar Limited v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others
Supreme Court of India

16 February 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.2179/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  Considering the dispute involved in this matter we remit thecase to the High Court with a direction that the matter may be disposed of as early as possible, preferably within a period of two weeks from today. In the meantime, it would be open to the petitioner and respondent No.5 to purchase the sugarcane from the disputed area.
2.  Learned counsel for the parties further state that they willnot ask for adjournement of the matter before the High Court on anyground. If they ask for the adjournment, it would be open to the HighCourt to refuse the same.
3. It would be open to the petitioner to file his counter-affidavit within three days from today before the HighCourt and the rejoinder, if any, may be filed within seven days thereafter. The petition stands disposed with the modification of the impugned order in the above terms.
Petition disposed of.
