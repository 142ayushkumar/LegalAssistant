N.C. John and Sons Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax
Supreme Court of India

22 January 2003
C.A. Nos. 1842 to 1845 of 2002
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1. We have heard learned counsel for the parties.
2.We see no reason to interfere with the judgment under appeal.
3.  The civil appeals are accordingly dismissed without any order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed.
