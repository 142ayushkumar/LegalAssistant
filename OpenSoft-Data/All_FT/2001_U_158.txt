Union of India and Others v Pravin Chandra Mehta and Anr
Supreme Court of India

7 December 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 2624-2625/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Mr. U.U. Lalit, learned counsel appearing for the petitioner fairly states that he does not press the special leave petition filed against respondent No.1, Praveen Chandra Mehta Praveen Bhai Mehta for the reason that from the counter affidavit, it is clear that the case of this respondent is similar to that of Vinod Kumar Jain, who was granted bail under Section 167 Cr.P.C.
Therefore, the SLP stands dismissed against Shri Praveen Chandra Mehta.
2. Shri Lalit prays for and is granted three days time to peruse the counter affidavit filed by respondent No. 2 and make his submissions so far as the said respondent is concerned.
The case will be listed on 10.12.2001.
Order accordingly.
