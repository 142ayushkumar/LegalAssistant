Union of India and Anr v R.B. Jodhmal and Company Private Limited
Supreme Court of India

15 January 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.3680/2000
The order of the court was as follows:
Delay condoned.
1.  Considering the totality of circumstances we think it appropriate to set aside the impugned order made by the High Court and restore the proceeding pending before it to its original file subject to the condition of petitioners paying a sum of Rs.25, 000/- within a period of two weeks from today, irrespective of the result of the case in the High Court this cost shall be paid.
2.  Undisputed amount of compensation shall be disbursed within three months from today. The matter shall be disposed of by the High Court as expeditiously as possible.
The special leave petition is disposed of as aforesaid.
Petition disposed of.
