State Of Assam v Union Of India And Ors
Supreme Court of India

28 February 2005
I.A. No. 9-12 in ORIGINAL SUIT 2/1988 with I.A. NO. 4 in Original Suit No. 1/1989
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. During the course of hearing it was pointed out that consistently with the First Schedule to the Constitution of India, the Surveyor General of India must have drawn up a map defining the boundaries and the same must have been accepted by the Government of India and the States. Let that map be produced before the Court.
2. List on 7th March, 2005.
Order accordingly
