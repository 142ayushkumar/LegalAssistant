State of Uttar Pradesh and Anr v Lala and Others
Supreme Court of India

29 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.10353/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
1.  After hearing the counsel for the parties, we modify the award to the extent that the respondents would not be entitled to full backwages. They will be entitled to receive only the backwages which is so far stated to have been paid i.e. Rs. 1,12,600/-. No further amount as backwages is, therefore, payable by the appellants to the respondents.
With this modification this appeal stands disposed of.
Appeal disposed of.
