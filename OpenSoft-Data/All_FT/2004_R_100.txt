Ram Das Athawale (1) v Union of India and others
Supreme Court of India

8 March 2004
W.P. (C) No. 86 of 2004
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Issue notice. Issue notice to learned Attorney General also. Learned Attorney General is present in court. He seeks and is allowed four weeks' time to obtain instructions.
List after four weeks.
2.  However, it is clarified that the question shall have prospective bearing and not retrospective in regard to the dissolution of Parliament.
Order accordingly.
