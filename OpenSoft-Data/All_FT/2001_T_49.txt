State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v Tapan Kumar Banerjee and Others
Supreme Court of India

21 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.4452-4455/2001
The order of the court was as follows:
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  Learned counsel for the respondents rightly pointed out that against the impugned judgment and order passed by the learned Single Judge in Writ Petition No.1134/98 and connected matters, the special appeal is maintainable before the Division Bench of the High Court.
2. Learned counsel appearing for the petitioners, after considering the rules, agreed that the appeal is maintainable before the Division Bench of the High Court.
3.  In this view of the matter, this special leave petition is rejected. It would be open to the petitioners to approach the High Court for filing special appeal.
Petition dismissed.
