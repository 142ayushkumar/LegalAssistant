Supreme Court of India

12 February 1987
C.As. Nos. 1856 to 1859 (NT) of 1973 with 1382-87 and 2338 of 1980
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Having regard to the view taken by this Court in Sunil Siddharthbhai v. CIT and Kartikeys V. Sarabhai v. CIT 1985 Indlaw SC 3351985 Indlaw SC 3351985 Indlaw SC 335 these appeals must be dismissed.
