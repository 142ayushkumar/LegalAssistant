Aaron Pharmaceuticals Pvt. Ltd v Collector Of Central Excise, Bombay-I
Supreme Court of India

4 December 2002
C.A. No. 1354 of 1995, Dt. 4 December 2002
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard the learned Counsel for the parties.
1. In our view, the order passed by the Customs, Excise and Gold (Control) Appellate Tribunal in Final Order No. 372/1994-D holding that the product "Aprot" manufactured by the appellant-Company cannot be held to be 'prepared and preserved food' under Tariff Item No. 1B eligible for exemption under Notification No. 17/70 cannot be said to be, in any way, illegal or erroneous.
Hence, this appeal is dismissed.
Appeal dismissed.
