P. K. Baby John v State of Kerala and another
Supreme Court of India

13 November 2002
Cr.As. Nos. 1016-17 of 2002
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The appellant has been convicted for an offence punishable under Section 138 of the Negotiable Instruments Act and sentenced to undergo simple imprisonment for three months.
2.  It is now brought to our notice that the parties have settled their dispute outside the court.
Bearing this aspect of the matter in mind, we reduce the sentence that has been imposed to a fine in a sum of Rs. 1,000/- which shall be payable within four weeks from today. In default, the appellant to undergo imprisonment for a further period of one month.
The appeals are disposed of accordingly.
Appeals disposed of.
