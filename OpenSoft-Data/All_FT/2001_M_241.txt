Mathai Cyriac v State of Kerala and Others
Supreme Court of India

20 April 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.15500/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
1.  In view of the decision of this Court in Civil Appeal Nos. 6326-6329 of 1999 (T.J. Baby and Others vs. State of Kerala and Others) dated 10th August, 2000, this appeal is allowed, the decision of the Division Bench is set aside and that of the Single Judge restored. No costs.
Appeal allowed.
