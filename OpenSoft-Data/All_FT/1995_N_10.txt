Special Land Acquisition Officer v N.S. Takkalaki And Ors
Supreme Court of India

16 August 1995
Civil Appeal No. 7588 of 1995
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Leave granted.
1.  Notification under Section 4(1) of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (for short, 'the Act') was published on January 23, 1980 and the Land Acquisition Officer gave his award on March 28, 1980. Consequently, the respondents are not entitled to the additional amount @ 12 per cent per annum of the enhanced compensation under Section 23 (1-A) of the Act as amended by Amendment Act 68 of 1984.
2.  The appeal is accordingly allowed and the grant of additional amount under Section 23 (1-A) is set aside. In other respects, the award of the Reference Court, as confirmed 7588 the High Court, stands confirmed. No costs.
