Messrs Harvard House and Anr v Messrs Ceat Financial Services Limited
Supreme Court of India

11 September 2001
Review Petition(Cr.) No. 1121/2001 in T.P.(Cr.) No. 83/2001
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  There has been an inordinate delay of 103 days in filing the Review Petition for which there is no sufficient explanation. Even on merits, we do not find a case for our interference.
2. The Review Petition is dismissed on the ground of delay as well as on merits.
Review Dismissed.
