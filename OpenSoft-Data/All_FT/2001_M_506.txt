Uttar Singh v Municipal Corporation, Chandigarh and Others
Supreme Court of India

16 July 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.232/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  There will be stay of the construction of the urinal just adjoining to the house of the petitioner. It would be open to the respondent to construct the urinal at some reasonable distance. Learned counsel for the petitioner agreed that there would be no encorachment by the petitioner over this property/land. Learned counsel for the respondent submits that it would be open to the respondents to use the said land for some other purpose without causing any nuisance to the petitioner.
The prayer is allowed.
Order accordingly.
