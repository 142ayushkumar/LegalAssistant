Vijay Pal Sharma and Others v R. P. Kalra and Others
Supreme Court of India

13 September 1991
Contempt Petition No. 262 of the 1990 (Arising out of W.P. No. 818 of 1989), in W.P. No. 818 of 1989
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Heard Learned counsel in part. Compliance report be submitted us within two weeks with regards to the treatment meted out to the petitioners, similar to that meted out to 1200 employees who were under the scheme.
List the matter after two weeks.
Order accordingly.
