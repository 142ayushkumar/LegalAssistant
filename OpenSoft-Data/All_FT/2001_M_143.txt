Sudesh Kumar Sharma v Mahendra Development Sharma and Others
Supreme Court of India

29 November 2001
The Order of the court was as follows:
1.  After hearing both sides we feel that the case can be transfered to a Court at Ghaziabad. Learned counsel for the respondents opposed the prayer for transfer to Delhi. That is why we have decided to transfer the case to the Court at Ghaziabad. We allow this petition.
2. The case bearing No.450/87 titled Mahendra Dev. Sharma Vs. Baijnath Sharma and; Ors. pending in the Court of Judicial Magistrate Baghpat (Meerut) to the Chief Judicial Magistrate, Ghaziabad. It is open to the Chief Judicial Magistrate to try the case in his own court or to make it over to any other court within his domain.
The Transfer Petition is disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
