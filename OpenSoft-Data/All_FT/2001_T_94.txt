Union of India v Thakhat Singh and Anr
Supreme Court of India

8 November 2001
CIVIL APPEAL NO. 1032 OF 1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  It is conceded that this appeal stands covered by the decision of this Court in Sunder Vs. Union of India.
2. In view of the above cited decision, we do not find any merit in this appeal. It is, accordingly, dismissed. There shall be no order as to costs.
Appeal dismissed.
