Supreme Court of India

11 April 2001
C.A. No.3191/95 (Along with office report)C.A. 3192/95 (Along with office report)&S.L.P.(C) 6099/92 (Along with office report)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted in SLP(C) No.6099/1992.
Civil Appeal Nos.3190, 3191 and 3192 of 1995 are dismissed.
1.  Civil Appeal No.2873/20001 SLP(C) No.6099 of 1992 is allowed and the impugned judgment and order passed by the High Court is setaside in terms of the Judgment.
2. As the High Court has not decided the matter on merits, it is remitted back to the High Court for decision in accordance with law.
There will be no order as to costs.
Appeal allowed.
