Manvendra Tiwary v State of Bihar
Supreme Court of India

23 April 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal(Cr.)......./2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Delay condoned.
1.  Heard Shri P.S. Mishra, learned senior counsel for the petitioner. We are not satisfied that it is a fit case for grant of anticipatory bail.
2. Considering the submissions made by Shri Mishra, it is ordered that the petitioner will surrender in the Court of the Magistrate at Giridih in which the criminal case arising out of FIR Case No.129/2000 is pending and seek regular bail on or before April 30, 2001. Till 30.4.2001, the petitioner will not be arrested in connection with the aforesaid case.
The special leave petition is dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
