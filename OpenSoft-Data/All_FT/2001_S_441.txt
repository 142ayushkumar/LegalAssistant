Kamal Kumar and Others v State (Nct of Delhi)Th.Home Secretary
Supreme Court of India

19 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 3687/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.Leave granted.
2.  Having considered the submissions of the learned counsel appearing on the either side, we are of the view that in the peculiar circumstances of the case one more opportunity may be granted to the appellants to cross-examine P.Ws.10 and 14.
3. If the appellants fails to avail of this final and last opportunity no further indulgence will be shown. The appeal is accordingly allowed.
Appeal allowed.
