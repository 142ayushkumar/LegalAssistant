G. Govinda Rajulu v Andhra Pradesh State Construction Corporation
Supreme Court of India

26 November 1986
W.P. No. 6081/83,
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We have carefully considered the matter and after hearing learned counsel for the parties, we direct that the employee of the Andhra Pradesh State Construction Corporation Limited whose service were sought to be terminated on account of the closure of the Corporation shall be continued in service on the same terms and conditions either in the Government Departments or in the Government Corporations.
2.  The writ petition is disposed of accordingly. There is no order as to costs.
Petition disposed of.
