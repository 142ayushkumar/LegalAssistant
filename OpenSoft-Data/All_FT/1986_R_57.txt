Ratan M. R. Tara v Assam Railway and Trading Company Limited
Supreme Court of India

9 December 1986
C.A. No. 811 of 1973.
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The termination of appellant's service was in accordance with clause 8 of the contract. Three months' salary in lieu of notice has been paid. There is no question of breach of contract or of damages.
2.  We, therefore, agree with the reasoning and conclusion of the High Court. The appeal is, accordingly, dismissed with no order as to costs.
Appeal dismissed.
