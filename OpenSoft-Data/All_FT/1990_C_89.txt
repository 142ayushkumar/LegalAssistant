Commissioner of Income Tax v Indian Telephone Industries Limited
Supreme Court of India

13 July 1990
C.A. No. 3306-3308 of 1984
The Judgment was delivered by: DR. T. KOCHU THOMMEN, J.
1.  These appeals by the Revenue are against the judgment of the Karnataka High Court in CIT v. Indian Telephone Industries Ltd..
2. In view of the subsequent amendment of section 35 of the Income-tax Act with retrospective operation, the appeals have to be allowed. We do so.
Appeals allowed.
