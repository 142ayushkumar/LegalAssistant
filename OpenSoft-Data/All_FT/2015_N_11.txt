1. Heard the learned counsel.
2. Leave granted.
3. Crl.M.P.No.17416/2015 for correction is allowed.
4. The learned counsel appearing for the State has fairly stated that the State has no objection if the petitioner is heard by the High Court without being impleaded as a party before the High Court.
5. In the circumstances, the impugned order passed by the High Court is modified to the effect that the petitioner shall be permitted to appear and make his submissions without being impleaded as a party.
6. The appeal is allowed to the above extent.
Appeal allowed
