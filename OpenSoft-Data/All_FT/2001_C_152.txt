Corporation Bank v Chief Secretary of Karnataka and Others
Supreme Court of India

26 July 2001
C.A. No.5549/1997
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  It is conceded on behalf of the appellant that this matter stands concluded by a decision of this Court in Dena Bank vs. Bhikhabhai Prabhudas Parekh and Co. and Ors. reported in 2000 Indlaw SC 740. Consequently, the appeal fails and is, accordingly, dismissed.
There shall be no order as to costs.
Appeal dismissed.
