Commissioner of Sales Tax, U. P v India Aluminium Cables Limited
Supreme Court of India

16 September 1998
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Civil Appeal No. 10580 of 1996 with C.A. Nos. 10402 - 10405 and 10581 of 1996 (Appeals from the judgment and order dated March 4, 1994 of the Allahabad High Court in S.T.R. Nos. 730-733, 815 and 816 of 1993), decided on September 16, 1998.
2.  We have read the judgment and order under appeal. We have also read the circular of the Director. Export Assistance dated January 7, 1970. We are quite satisfied that the conclusion of the High Court is correct having regard to the facts of these particular cases.
The appeals are dismissed. No order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed
