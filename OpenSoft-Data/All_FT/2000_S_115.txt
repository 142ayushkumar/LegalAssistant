Saurashtra Cement and Chemicals
Supreme Court of India

7 November 2000
CIVIL APPEAL NO.5576 OF 1998
The Order of the court was as follows:
1.  This appeal is directed against an interlocutory order passed by the High Court and it has been brought to our notice that the main matter out of which these proceedings arise is still pending consideration of the High Court.
2. In these circumstances, we think it appropriate to dispose of the matter by directing that the interim order granted, will enure to the benefit of the parties during the pendency of the main matter before the High Court. The appeal is disposed of accordingly.
Appeal disposed of.
