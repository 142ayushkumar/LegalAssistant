Jang Bahadur v Shanti Rani
Supreme Court of India

7 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.14490/2001

The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Heard learned counsel for the petitioner. We are not satisfied that any interference with the order under challenge is warranted. The special leave petition is dismissed.
2.  Then learned counsel for the petitioner prayed for three months further time to vacate the premises.
3.  The prayer is granted subject to the condition that the petitioner files an undertaking on usual terms in this Court within three weeks from today failing which it will be open to the respondent to execute the order of eviction forthwith. If the respondent has any objection to the order granting extension of time for vacating the premises it will be open to her to file proper application for modification of the order.
Petition dismissed.
