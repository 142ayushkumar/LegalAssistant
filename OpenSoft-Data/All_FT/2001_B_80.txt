Balbir Singh v Punjab State Civil Supplies Corporation and Anr
Supreme Court of India

26 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.13679/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the petitioner states that considering the financial position of the petitioner it is difficult for him to deposit Rs.3 lakhs as ordered by this Court.
2.  He has filed IA No.1 for modification of the order in terms of the prayers 1-3 as under:
"1. The petitioner will give the aforesaid 3 Demand Drafts Nos.950420, 950421 and 950422 drawn by State Bank of Patiala for sum of Rs.1 lakh to the respondent;
2. That the undertaking of petitioner that would not withdraw amount from GPF Account be accepted;
3. That the respondent will have lien on amount of gratuity and pension benefits during the pendency of Regular First Appeal before the High Court."
3. The prayer Nos.1-3 as above are granted. Demand Draft to be deposited with the respondent-Corporation within one week from today.
Order accordingly.
