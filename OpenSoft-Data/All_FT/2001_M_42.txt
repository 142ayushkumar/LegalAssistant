M. P. Poddar (Huf) and Another v Appropriate Authority and Others. (And Another
Supreme Court of India

26 July 2001
C.As. Nos. 2114 of 2000 and 5084 of 2000 (C.A. No. 2114 of 2000 is by special leave from the judgment and order Dt. 17 September 1999 of the Delhi High Court in C.W.P. No. 1145 of 1998).
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the appellants fairly state that the issue involved in these appeals is covered against the appellants by reason of the judgment of this court in Ramesh Bhai J. Patel v. Union of India 2000 Indlaw SC 2741.
The civil appeals are accordingly dismissed.No order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed.
