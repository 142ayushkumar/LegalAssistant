Commnr. of Income Tax, Bangalore and Anr v Messrs Seema International
Supreme Court of India

7 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.12418/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
1.  In our opinion, questions of law do arise in this case. We, therefore, direct the Tribunal to state the case and refer the following questions of law to the High Court:
(1) Whether the process of extracting/mining of granite rock from the earth amounts to the manufacture or production of any article or thing as per Section 32-A of the Income tax Act ?
(2) Whether the assessee is entitled to claim deduction under Section 32-A of the Income tax Act on the machinery utilized for the purpose of extracting/mining of granite ?
The appeal is allowed accordingly.
Appeal allowed.
