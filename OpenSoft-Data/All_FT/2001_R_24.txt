Management, State Bank of India v R. Sundaram and Others
Supreme Court of India

4 December 2001
CIVIL APPEAL NOS. 86-88 OF 1999
The Order of the court was as follows:
1.  On the special features arising in these cases, the High Court disposed of the same by granting certain limited relief to the respondents.
2. In that view of the matter, we do not think we should interfere in exercise of our power under Article 136 of the Constitution.
3.  In as much as the High Court disposed of the cases considering the special features arising in these matters, we do not think the same should be applicable to all the matters.
The appeals are disposed of accordingly.
Appeals disposed of.
