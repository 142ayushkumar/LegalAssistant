Union of India and Anr v State of Orissa and Others
Supreme Court of India

26 July 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.702/2001 in C.A.No.2235/1996
The Order of the court was as follows:
1.  There has been an inordinate delay of 136 days for which there is no sufficient explanation. The application for condonation of delay is dismissed.
2. Even on merit, we do not find a case for our intereference. The Review Petition is dismissed on the ground of delay as well as on merits.
Petition dismissed.
