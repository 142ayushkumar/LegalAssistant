Shripal Bhati And Others v State Of Uttar Pradesh And Others
Supreme Court of India

21 January 2009
Interlocutory Application No.6 of 2009
In
Civil Appeal No.1390 of 2007
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1.  By filing the present interlocutory application, the applicants/appellants seek permission to withdraw their Civil Appeal No.1390/2007. Permission sought for is granted and the Civil Appeal is dismissed as withdrawn. However, there will be no order as to costs and interim order, if any, stands vacated. I.A. is accordingly disposed of.
Application disposed of
