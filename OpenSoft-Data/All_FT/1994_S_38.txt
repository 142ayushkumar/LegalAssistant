Messrs Orissa Steel Corporation v Sales Tax Officer and Others
Supreme Court of India

18 July 1994
S.L.P. (C) No. 14571 of 1992
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We have today pronounced judgments in State of Orissa v. Minerals and Metals Trading Corpn. of India Ltd. and also in Industrial Minerals and Metals v. Sales Tax Officer.
2.  For the reasons recorded and the conclusions reached in the above judgments, we dismiss the special leave petition.
Petition dismissed.
