Messrs Sardar Estates v Atma Ram Properties Private Limited and Others
Supreme Court of India

9 April 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal(Civil).......CC 1853-1854/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Delay condoned.
1.  We find no merit in this petition and it is accordingly dismissed.
However, after hearing learned counsel for the parties, we feel it appropriate and accordingly direct the petitioner to vacate and hand over the physical possession of the portion in his physical possession to the respondent by or before the 31st October, 2001 subject to the usual undertaking to be filed within four weeks from today.
2. We also make it clear, so far the legal possesion of other portions, it stands concluded in favour of the respondents in view of this order.
Petition dismissed.
