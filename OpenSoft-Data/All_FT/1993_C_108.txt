Chettinad Corporation Private Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax
Supreme Court of India

6 January 1993
C.A. No. 4326 of 1984
The Judgment was delivered by: M. N. VENKATACHALAIAH, J.
1.  This appeal arises out of and is directed against the judgment dated April 8, 1983, of the High Court of Madras in Tax Case No. 1545 of 1977, Reference No. 1090 of 1977.
2. Learned counsel on both sides submit that the point raised in the appeal is covered by the pronouncement of this court in CIT v. Sun Engineering Works P. Ltd. in which the judgment under appeal in the present case is referred to and approved For the reasons contained in and following the said pronouncement, CIT v. Sun Engineering Works P. Ltd. this appeal is dismissed. No costs
Appeal dismissed.
