Karur Ghee Stores Rep.By V.Periasamy v N.Palaniappan and Anr
Supreme Court of India

23 July 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.11157/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  Considering the findings given by the courts below the impugned order passed by the High Court does not call for any interference, hence, this petition is dismissed. However, learned counsel for the petitioner seeks reasonable time to vacate the premises.
2.  Considering the fact that the petitioner-tenant is in possession of the disputed shop since years time to vacate the premises is granted upto 31st January, 2002 on filing of usual undertaking before this Court as well as before the trial court within two weeks from today.
Petition dismissed.
