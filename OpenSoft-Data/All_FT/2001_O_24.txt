Gautam Dhara and Anr v Om Prakash Thakur and Others
Supreme Court of India

26 November 2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the respondents on instructions states that at present only the matter is at invetigation stage. Chargesheet is not submitted and no case is pending before the court.
2. Hence this pettion at this stage is not maintainable and is rejected.
Petition dismissed.
