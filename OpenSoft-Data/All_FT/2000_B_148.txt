Brij Mohan Singh and Company v Commissioner of Income Tax
Supreme Court of India

14 November 2000
C.A. No. 2253 of 1998
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The question has been rightly answered by the High Court in the order under challenge.
2.  The decision of the High Court accords with the view taken by this Court in Biharilal Jaiswal vs. CIT 1995 Indlaw SC 646 and followed in Motilal Chunnilal Tak vs. CIT 1997 Indlaw SC 16701997 Indlaw SC 1670. The appeal is dismissed. No order as to costs.
Appeal dismissed.
