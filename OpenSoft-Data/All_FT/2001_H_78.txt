Haryana Seeds Development Corporation Limited v Satyapal and Anr
Supreme Court of India

9 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.958/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In SLP 958/2001 Learned counsel for the respondent No.1, prays for and is granted four weeks' further time to file counter affidavit. It is made clear that no further time will be granted for the purpose.
2. In the meantime counsel for the petitioner will also take steps for service of notice on respondent No.2.
3.  In SLP 21373/2000 Learned counsel appearing for the sole respondent prays for and is granted four weeks' time to file counter affidavit. It is made clear that no further time will be granted for the purpose.
4.  In SLP 21382/2000 Learned counsel for the petitioner prays for and is granted four weeks' time to take steps for fresh service on the sole respondent.
List all the matters together after service is complete.
Order accordingly.
