Messrs Kalaimagal Account Book Shop v P.K. Vijayan and Others
Supreme Court of India

9 February 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.10088/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the parties have filed a compromise today and according to the learned counsel for both sides, the petitioner and respondent Nos.1 to 4 have signed the same.
2. Under this, the petitioner has undertaken to vacate the premises by or before 16th April 2001 and has agreed to pay    Rs.1,000/- regularly every month till handing over the possession to the respondents.
3.  In view of the said compromise, the petitioner does not want to press this petition and is accordingly dismissed as such. The original compromise is taken on record.
Petition dismissed.
