Pawan Kumar Bhalotia v State of West Bengal
Supreme Court of India

28 February 2005
Cr.A. No. 360 of 2005
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
Heard appellant's counsel and counsel for the respondent.
1.  In view of the disposal of of Criminal Appeal No. 359 of 2005 arising out of SLP (Crl) No. 3254/2004, the reasons given in the judgment equally applies to the appellant Pawan Kumar Bhalotia.
We quash the criminal proceedings initiated against the appellant and accordingly allow the appeal.
Appeal allowed.
