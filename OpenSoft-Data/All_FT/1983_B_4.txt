Dhanraj Jain and Another v B. N. Biswas and Another
Supreme Court of India

2 February 1983
Cr.A. No. 389 of 1976 (Arising as an appeal by special leave from the Judgment and Order Dt. 14 January 1976 of the Calcutta High Court in Criminal Revision Case No. 187 of 1975).
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1.  This appeal is directed against the order framing charges and at this stage we are not inclined to interfere because it will be open to the accused to make all his submissions during the trial.
The appeal is accordingly dismissed.
Appeal dismissed.
