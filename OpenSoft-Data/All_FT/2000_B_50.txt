H.S.M.I.T.C. Thro. Chief Engg. v Balwant Kaur and Others
Supreme Court of India

7 December 2000
CIVIL APPEAL NO. 2232/1998
The Order of the court was as follows:
1.  This appeal is directed against an interlocutory order granted by the High Court. However, that order has been stayed by this Court while granting leave to appeal.
2. In the circumstances, of the case we direct that the interlocutory order granted by this Court shall ensure to the benefit of the appellants during the pendency of the proceedings before the High Court. The appeal shall stand disposed of accordingly.
Appeal disposed of.
