Assistant Commercial Taxes Officer v M/Swipro Limited
Supreme Court of India

5 January 2010
CIVIL APPEAL NO.16 OF 2010 (Arising out of S.L.P. (C) No.3329 of 2008)
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Leave granted.
None appears for the respondent, though served.
1.  The impugned order has been passed without considering the two judgements of this Court in the cases of Guljag Industries vs. Commercial Taxes Officer, reported in 2007 (7) S.C.C.269 2007 Indlaw SC 888 and Assistant Commercial Taxes Officer vs. Bajaj Electricals Limited, reported in 2009 (1) S.C.C. 308 2008 Indlaw SC 1921. In the circumstances, impugned order is set aside and the matter is remitted to the High Court for de novo consideration in the light of the afore-stated judgements of this Court.
The civil appeal, accordingly, stands disposed of.
Appeal Disposed of
