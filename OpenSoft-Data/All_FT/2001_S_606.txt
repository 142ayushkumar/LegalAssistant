Pranav Kumar Pranava Kumar and Others v State of Bihar
Supreme Court of India

3 December 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 6847-6849/2001
The order of the court was as follows:
Heard learned counsel for the petitioners.
1.  We are not inclined to interfere with the order passed by the High Court rejecting the prayer for anticipatory bail.
2.The Special leave petitions are dismissed.
3.  If the petitioners or any of them surrenders in Court and files application for regular bail, the same would be considered and disposed of, if possible, on the same day.
Petitions dismissed.
