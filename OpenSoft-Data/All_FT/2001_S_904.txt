Lakhkha Lakhan v State of Madhya Pradesh
Supreme Court of India

19 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal(Cr.)......../2001
The order of the court was as follows:
Delay condoned.
1.  Along with the special leave petition an application under Section 374 of the Code of Criminal Procedure 
has also been filed on behalf of the petitioner to compound the offence.
2. Heard learned counsel for the petitioner.
3.  We are of the view that the proper course would be to approach the trial court with such an application and giving liberty to the petitioner to pursue such remedy before the trial court, the special leave petition as well as the application for compounding the offence is disposed of.
4. The trial court shall deal with the application as and when it is moved in accordance with law.
Petition disposed of.
