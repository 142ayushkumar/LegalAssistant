U. Palaniappan and another v Sub-Inspector of Police
Supreme Court of India

14 March 2005
Cr.A. No. 417 of 2005
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
Leave granted.
1.  On the facts and circumstances of this case, the condition imposed by the High Court while granting anticipatory bail that (he first appellant should deposit Rs.10/- lakhs and the second appellant should deposit Rs.5/- lakhs before getting the benefit of anticipatory bail in our opinion is onerous.  Hence, in modification of the said order while affirming the grant of anticipatory bail, we direct the appellants to furnish a self-bond of Rs.50,000/- each and one surety for the like sum each to the satisfaction of the Court or the arresting authority as the case may be.
2.  The appellants shall abide by the conditions enumerated in Section 438(2) of the  Criminal Procedure Code.
The appeal is allowed.
Appeal allowed.
