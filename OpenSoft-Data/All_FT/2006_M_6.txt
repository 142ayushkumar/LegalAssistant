M. C. Mehta v State of Tamil Nadu and others
Supreme Court of India

1 February 2006
W.P. (Civil) No. 465 of 1986 with W. P. (C) No. 318/2005
The Order of the Court was as follows :
W.P.(C) NO.465/1986:
1. Fresh affidavit be filed indicating number of children working in hazardous/non- hazardous employment within a period of four weeks.
W.P.(C) NO.318/2005:
2. Issue notice to the States/Union Territories of Andhra Pradesh, Assam, Bihar, Gujarat, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, Karnataka, Kerala, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Chhattisgarh, Manipur, Meghalaya, Jharkhand, Uttaranchal, Nagaland, Orissa, Punjab, Rajasthan, Sikkim, Tamil Nadu, Tripura, Uttar Pradesh, West Bengal, Andaman & Nicobar Island, Arunachal Pradesh, Chandigarh, Dadra & Nagar Haveli, Delhi, Daman and Diu, Goa, Lakshadweep, Mizoram and Pondicherry through their respective Chief Secretaries/Administrators.
3. Dasti, in addition, is permitted.
4. Let a copy of this order be also communicated to the Registrar General (Census and Operations), Govt. of India, 2A, Man Singh Road, New Delhi.
5. List both the matters after six weeks.
Order accordingly
