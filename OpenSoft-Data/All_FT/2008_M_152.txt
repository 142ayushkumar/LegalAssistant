State Bank Of Bikaner & Jaipur v M/S.Modern Syntax (India) Ltd
Supreme Court of India

14 February 2008
Appeal (civil) 6759 of 2001
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1.  Learned counsel appearing on behalf of the appellant submits on instruction that this appeal has become infructuous in view of the consent decree passed and therefore, he does not want to proceed with the appeal.
2.  The appeal is accordingly dismissed for non-prosecution. Interim order, if there be any, stands vacated. There will be no order as to costs.
Appeal dismissed
