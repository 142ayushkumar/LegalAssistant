Madhuri v Kurukshetra University
Supreme Court of India

14 February 2001
Review Petition(Civil) Nos.140-141/2001 in S.L.P.(Civil)Nos. 6422-6423/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There is an inordinate delay of 188 days in filing the review petitions which has not been explained satisfactorily.
2.  Apart from this, we do not find any merit in the review petitions. Accordingly, the petitions are dismissed on the ground of delay as well as on merits.
Petitions dismissed.
