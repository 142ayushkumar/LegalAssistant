P.S. Govinda Hebber v Mahin Sherule
Supreme Court of India

23 March 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.4384/2001
The order of the Court was as follows:
We do not find any merit in this petition.
1.  The special leave petition is dismissed Learned counsel for the petitioner states that in case the petitioner is required to vacate the premises in question immediately, he would be put to great hardship and for that reason the petitioner may be granted some time to vacate the premises.
2. We, accordingly, direct that the petitioner shall not be dispossessed from the premises till 31st August, 2001 provided he files an usual undertaking to this effect in this Court within four weeks from today.
3.  In case the respondent has any grievance to this part of our order, it shall be open to him to apply for vacation of the same.
Petition dismissed.
