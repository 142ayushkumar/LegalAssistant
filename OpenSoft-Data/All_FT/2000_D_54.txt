U.O.I. and Others v Delhi Housing and Finance and Others
Supreme Court of India

17 November 2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In our earlier Order dated 7th November, 2000, in SLP(C) 10371/97 and batch, the last sentence at page 11 may be read as under:
"For the aforesaid reason we find that all the special leave petitions filed by the Union of India and the review petitions filed by the DDA in the aforesaid group of cases are also devoid of any merit and are accordingly dismissed."
The aforesaid Order stands corrected accordingly.
Order accordingly.
