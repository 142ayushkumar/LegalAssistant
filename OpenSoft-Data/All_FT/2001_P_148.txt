Prakashak-Mohit Printers v Messrs Ashok Prakahsan(Regd) and Anr
Supreme Court of India

23 March 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal(Civil)...CC.6586/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Delay is condoned.
1.  It is represented by the learned counsel for the petitioner that the question of punishing the petitioner in contempt in a case of representative decree was argued before the High Court. It is further submitted that in execution of the same decree and for its violation no person can be punished twice in view of Section 58(2) of CPC. was also argued before the High Court.
2. We do not find from a perusal of the order of the High Court that such contentions were raised there. However, the learned counsel seeks permission to withdraw the S.L.P. with liberty to file a review petition in the High Court, we permit him to withdraw the same.
The special leave petition is accordingly dismissed as withdrawn.
Petition Dismissed.
