Allahabad Distt. Co-Operative Bank Limited v Vidya Varidhi Mishra
Supreme Court of India

15 October 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.16141/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The General Manager of the petitioner. Company shall file an affidavit indicating as to whether the Resolution dated May 13, 1985 was acted upon and if so, what steps were taken pursuant there to.
2. If, however, that Resolution was not acted upon as asserted before us now, then deponent must also state whether the suspension allowance was continued to be paid to the respondent from the date of the said Resolution till passing of the impugned order dated April 6, 1989.
List the matter after four weeks.
Order accordingly.
