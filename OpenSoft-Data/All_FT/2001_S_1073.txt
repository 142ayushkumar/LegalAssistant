Prem Kumar Agarwal and Anr v State of Punjab
Supreme Court of India

11  May  2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 1799/2001
The order of the Court was as follows:
We have heard Mr. N.C.Kochhar, learned senior counsel for the petitioners.
1.  We are not inclined to interfere with the order of the High Court declining to grant anticipatory bail to the petitioners. However, if the petitioners surrender and file application for regular bail by 25th May, 2001, the same will be considered on merit in accordance with law and disposed of expeditiously.
2. The petitioners will not be arrested till 25th May, 2001, in connection with the case F.I.R.No.158 dated 29th August, 2000 registered at Police Station, Phillaur, Punjab.
The S.L.P. is dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
