v Niraj Upadhyaya and Others
Supreme Court of India

29 January 2001
I.A. Nos. 1-5 in Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.13704-13708/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Heard learned counsel for the parties in I.A. Nos. 1-5 of 2001. We have also perused the Office Report. According to the Office Report, the connected batch of matters is being listed on 23rd February, 2001. This petition shall also be tagged with that batch.
2.  In the meanwhile, we direct that the operation of the order dated 19.1.2001, passed by the High Court of Allahabad in Writ No. 1716 of 1998, shall stay.
3.  In case the batch of petitions is not heard on 23rd February, 2001, it shall be open to the petitioners to seek extension of stay or an order for staying the operation of the impugned judgment, in the special leave petitions, dated 10th May, 2000.
Order accordingly.
