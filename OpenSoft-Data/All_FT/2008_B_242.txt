Bishwanath Sao And Anr v State Of Bihar
Supreme Court of India

18 January 2008
CRIMINAL APPEAL NO. 124 OF 2008 [Arising out of SLP(Crl.) No. 6979/2007]
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Leave granted.
1.  Having heard the learned counsel for the parties and keeping in view the fact that the appellants are already in jail for a period of about 10 months, they are directed to be released on bail on furnishing bail bonds of Rs. 10,000/- each with two sureties for the like amount, to the satisfaction of the Trial Court.
The appeal is allowed with the aforementioned direction.
Appeal Allowed
