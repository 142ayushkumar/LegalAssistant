Messrs Neelkanth Chemical Works v State of Rajasthan and Others
Supreme Court of India

6 August 2001
IA Nos.10-12 in C.A..No.5068-5070/1997
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The learned counsel for the appellants prays that the I.A.Nos.10 to 12 for withdrawal of the appeals may be allowed.
2. They are accordingly allowed and consequently the appeals are dismissed as withdrawn. No costs.
Appeals dismissed.
