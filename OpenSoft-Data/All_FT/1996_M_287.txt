Collector of Central Excise v Modella Steels and Alloys
Supreme Court of India

22 April 1996
S.L.P. (Civil) ...../96 (C.C. 1740/96)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Delay Condoned.
1.  The impugned order by the High Court is to be construed as giving the direction to the Department to decide the matter in accordance with law, that is, according to the law as interpreted in binding precedents.
The S.L.P. is disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
