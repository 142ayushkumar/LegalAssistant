Dengsailova and others v Union of India and others
Supreme Court of India

16 March 2016
Civil Appeal No. 5565 of 2014
(Non-Reportable)
The Judgment was delivered by : Kurian Joseph, J.
1. Heard learned counsel for the appellants and the learned counsel appearing for the respondents.
2. The question of law raised in this appeal on limitation is covered against the appellants by the judgment of this Court in Civil Appeal No. 3536 of 2008 titled as 'J.Thansiama v. State of Mizoram & Ors.' [2015 (8) JT 159] 2015 Indlaw SC 611.
3. The appeal is, accordingly, dismissed.
Order accordingly
