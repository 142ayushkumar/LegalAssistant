Kantappa and Others v State of Karnataka
Supreme Court of India

4  May  2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.17889-17890/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  The appeal itself was dismissed as the learned counsel for the appellants appearing before the High Court had no file.
2. After hearing learned counsel for the parties, on the facts of this case, we feel in the interests of justice that the appellants should be granted an opportunity to place their case before the High Court.
3.  Accordingly, the appeals are allowed, the impugned orders of the High Court dated 15.3.2000 and 12.6.2000 are hereby set aside and the case is remanded back to the High Court for deciding the matter afresh, in accordance with law, after giving an opportunity to the parties. Costs on the parties.
Appeals allowed.
