Vishwa Nath v State of Haryana
Supreme Court of India

21 August 2001
Review Petition(Cr.) No. 706/2001 in Cr.A.No. 2769/99
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There has been an inordinate delay of 534 days in filing of the review petition for which no satisfactory explanation has been offered.
2.  Even on merits, having carefully gone through the review petition, we do not find that a case for review has been made out.
3. The review petition accordingly stands dismissed on the ground of delay as well as on merits.
Review dismissed.
