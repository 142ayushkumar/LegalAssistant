Sukumar Dutta v Chairman, Aseb and Others
Supreme Court of India

7 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.9291/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard counsel for the parties.
1.  The claim of the petitioner is that he is further entitled to Pay and Allowance (leave period) a sum of Rs.1,03873.80 and further three months salary - Rs.14,640.00 and T.A. - Rs.500.00. The claim is disputed by the respondents.
2. In view of the fact that the said claim is disputed, it would be open to the petitioner to take such fresh proceeding for recovery of the amount, as it may be available under law.
With this observation, the special leave petition is dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
