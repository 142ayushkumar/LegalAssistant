Collector of Central Excise, Jaipur v Modi Alkalies and Chemicals Limited
Supreme Court of India

19 February 1997
C.A. No. 4173 of 1995 (Arising as an appeal from 1994 (74) E.L.T. 924 (Tribunal)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1. The Tribunal followed its judgment in the case of Laxmi Chemicals v. Collector of Central Excise, Jaipur and held that hydrated lime is not an excisable commodity.
The civil appeal filed against the judgment in the case of Laxmi Chemicals was dismissed by this Court on 12th January, 1996 (Civil Appeal No. 11994 of 1995).
The appeal is, therefore, dismissed with no order as to costs.
Appeal dismissed.
