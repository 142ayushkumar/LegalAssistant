Ghaziabad Development Authority v Dhanesh Chand Goel
Supreme Court of India

12 January 2001
C.A. No. 11315 of 2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The judgment in Ghaziabad Development Authority v. Union of India 2000 Indlaw SC 5362000 Indlaw SC 536 only opines that interest @ 18% can be awarded on equitable grounds in appropriate cases.
2. Given the facts recorded in the order under challenge, the rate of 18% per annum is an award at a reasonable rate.
The special leave petition is dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
