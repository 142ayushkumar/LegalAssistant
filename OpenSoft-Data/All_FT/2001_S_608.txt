Radha Raman Prasad v State of Bihar
Supreme Court of India

12 March 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 528/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Exemption from filing O.T. is allowed.
1.Despite service of notice none appears for the respondent.
2.  Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner. Considering the facts in Sp.Case No.11/95 pending in the court of Special Judge Vigilance South Bihar, Patna, if petitioner files an application for releasing him on bail before the trial court, the court shall, release him on his furnishing a bond for a sum of Rs.10,000/- on a condition that the petitioner would fully cooperate in further investigation.
3.The special leave petition stands disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
