National Insurance Co. Ltd. v Bhagwani & Ors.
Supreme Court of India

5 January 2004
Special Leave Petition (Civil) 9215 Of 2003
The Judgment was delivered by : S. B. Sinha, J.
1.  This Special Leave Petition is covered by a judgment of this Court in SLP (Civil) No. 9027 of 2003, National Insurance Co. Ltd. Vs. Swaran Singh & Ors 2004 Indlaw SC 14, delivered today where it has been held that the learner's licence is also a valid licence.
It is accordingly dismissed.
Appeal dismissed
