St. Pauls' Teachers Training Institute (Men and Women) and Others v Director of School Education, Madras and Others
Supreme Court of India

15 September 1987
C.A. Nos. 1914, 1915, 1916, 1917, 1918, 1919, 1920, 1922, 1923, 1924 and 1925 of 1987 and S.L.P. (Civil) Nos. 10652, 10653 and 10654 of 1987
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We think that in the circumstances of the case the interests of justice require that the students of the appellant/petitioners should be permitted to appear in the ensuing supplemental examination to be conducted by the Director of Government Examination and the Director of School Education and the results declared thereafter.
2.  The appeals and the special leave petitions are accordingly disposed of. No cost.
Appeals disposed of.
