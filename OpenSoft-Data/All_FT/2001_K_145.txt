Y.N. Gangadhara Setty and Others v Karnataka Milk Federation and Others
Supreme Court of India

3 December 2001
I.A.Nos.1-2, 3 and 4 in No.514/1971 (for directions and exemption fromfiling records and impleadment and intervention)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Delay condoned.
Heard the learned counsel for the parties.
1.  In our view, the impugned interim order passed by the High Court does not call for any interference. Hence the special leave petition is dismissed. However, trial court is directed to expedite the hearing of the suit if possible within six months. Any interim order passed by this Court stands vacated.
2.  In view of the aforesaid order, no orders are required to be passed on the I.As. I.As. stand disposed of accordingly.
3. Learned senior counsel, Mr. Shanti Bhushan states that the government has executed the reconveyance deed.
Petition dismissed.
