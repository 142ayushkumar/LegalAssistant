Jahanara Begum v Rustom Ali Bhuyan
Supreme Court of India

3 December 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 590/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  In view of the decision of the Constitution Bench in 2001 (7) SCC-740 Daniel Latifi and Anr. vs. Union of India the matter has to be considered by the trial court afresh. For that purpose we set aside the order passed by the trial magistrate and the High Court and remit the case to the trial court for disposal of the claim afresh in accordance with law.
The appeal is disposed of accordingly.
Appeal disposed of.
