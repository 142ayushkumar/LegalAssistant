Manoj and Anr v Shriram Transport Finance Company Limited and Others
Supreme Court of India

5 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 96/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Dasti affidavit filed cannot be taken to be a proof of service on Respondent No. 4. Learned counsel, therefore, prays that he may be permitted to have the service effected by giving notice in the newspaper.
This prayer is allowed.
2. The notice in question may be published in the newspaper Loksatta, newspaper being published from Nagpur, as well as The Hindustan Times, Lucknow Edition which is stated to be widely circulated in Faizabad. Respondent No. 2 is permitted to file additional documents, if any, within four weeks.
Order accordingly.
