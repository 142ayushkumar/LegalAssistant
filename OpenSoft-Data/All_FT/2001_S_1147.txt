State of Tamil Nadu and Others v J.Mohanlal and Others
Supreme Court of India

23 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.14354/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Delay condoned.
1.  In view of the statement contained in paragraph 23 of the counter affidavit of the respondents we do not find any scope for interference with the impugned order.
2. Learned counsel for the petitioners earlier sought for time to got instructions in the matter and time was granted on more than one occasion but till now no instruction is forthcoming. It means that the Government is also not persuaded to take a different stand from what has been stated in paragraph 23 of the counter affidavit.
Accordingly, Special Leave Petition is dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
