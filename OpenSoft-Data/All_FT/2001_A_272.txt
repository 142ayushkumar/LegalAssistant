Ashok Sirpal v State of Delhi and Anr
Supreme Court of India

21 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 3355/2001
The order of the Court was as follows:
Heard learned counsel for the petitioner.
1.  Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that in view of the non bailable warrants issued by the trial court petitioner would surrender before the trial court. His only request is that the trial court may be directed to hear the application of bail at the earliest which may be filed by him after he surrenders.
2.  Considering the facts and the contentions raised in this petition, trial court to decide the application for bail which may be filed by the petitioner for releasing him on bail on the same day or within two-three days of the filing.
Petition stands disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
