N.S. Anandappa v Tara Mangalavedhe
Supreme Court of India

6 November 2001
CIVIL APPEAL NO. 2473 OF 2000
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel appearing for the respondent has handed over Bank Draft No.059296 dated 20th October, 2001 for 
Rs.1,50,000/- (Rupees one lakh fifty thousand only) drawn on Canara Bank, Bangalore, to the learned counsel appearing for the appellant as compensation for the litigation and for finding out alternative accommodation.
2.  After hearing learned counsel for the parties and in view of the fact of payment of rupees one lakh fifty thousand to the appellant, we are not inclined to interfere in this matter.
3. The appeal is dismissed keeping the question of law open. No order as to costs.
Appeal dismissed.
