Parminder Singh v Atma Singh
Supreme Court of India

3 December 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 2619/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Mr. R.P. Gupta, learned senior counsel appearing for the respondent states, on instructions, that by reason of certain averments in the rejoinder affidavit, it has become inescapable to file an additional affidavit as regards the statements contained in the rejoinder affidavit.
2. Learned counsel for the petitioner does not have any serious objection thereto and we also deem it fit to grant such an opportunity to file the same.
3.  It is, however, made clear that no new facts would be pleaded therein. As such, affidavit be filed within one week after reopening in the New Year.
4.  Let the matter appear three weeks after reopening in the New Year for final disposal on a non-miscellaneous day.
Order accordingly.
