Arjun Mahto v State of Bihar
Supreme Court of India

30 July 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 915/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
In SLP (Crl.)915/2001:
1.  Heard learned counsel for the parties. Leave granted. Permission to file addiltional documents is granted.
2.  The appellant Arjun Mahto shall be relased on bail on execution of a bond of Rs.10,000/- with two sureties each for the like amount to the satisfation of the trial court (4th Additional Sessions Judge, Gaya, Bihar).
In SLP(Crl.)462/2001:
3.  Learned counsel for the petitioners prays for and is granted two weeks' time to file an affidavit stating the period of sentence undergone by the petitioners who have been in custody.
In SLP(Crl.)No.1185/2001:
Leave granted. Permission to file additional documents is granted.
4. The appellant Bindeshwari Sao shall be released on bail on execution of a bond of Rs.10,000/- with two sureties each for the like amount to the satisfaction of the trial court.
Order accordingly.
