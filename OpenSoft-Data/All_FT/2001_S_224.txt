Shama Kakkar v Dara Ghandhi and Others
Supreme Court of India

10  May  2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.8714/2001
The Order of the court was as follows:
Taken on board.
1.  Learned counsel relies on Section 15A and states that the petitioner has become a tenant. Admittedly, the peititoner did not get a licence from the owner The petitioner claims to have got a licence from respondent No. 3 who was not the owner of the property. Section 15 A does not apply to such persons who get possession not from the landlord but from a tenant or a sub-tenant.
2. The High Court is right in deciding the case against the petitioner. The Special Leave Petition is dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
