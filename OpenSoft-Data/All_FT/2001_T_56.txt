Div.Manager, United India Insur. Company Limited v Tarla Laxman and Anr
Supreme Court of India

29 October 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.18115/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  It is urged that the insurance policy does not cover a cleaner in the event of an accident. Issue notice on the special leave petition as well as on prayer for interim relief.
2.  In the meantime, there shall be stay of the impugned judgment provided the petitioner deposits the entire compensation awarded by the Commissioner within four weeks' from today before the Commissioner. In case, such an amount is deposited, it will be open to the claimant to withdraw half of the amount without furnishing any security and rest half on furnishing security to the satisfaction of the Commissioner. In the event of default, this order shall stand automatically vacated.
Order accordingly.
