Commissioner of Income Tax v Harrison Crossfield (India) Limited
Supreme Court of India

2 August 2000
C.A. Nos. 15498 & 15499 of 1996 (From the judgment and order Dt. 25 January 1996 of the Kerala High Court in IT Ref. Nos. 136 & 137 of 1989, reported as CIT vs. Harrisons Crossfield (India) Ltd. 1996 132 CTR (Ker) 45 : 1996 220 ITR 494 (Ker) : TC S24.2483).
The Order of the court was aas follows:
1.  Given the facts and circumstances of the case, we think that no interference is called for with the orders of the CIT(A), the Tribunal and the High Court. We may say that it is the CIT(A) who has put the reasoning correctly.
The appeals are dismissed. No order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed.
