Dalmia Cement (Bharat) Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax
Supreme Court of India

26 July 2000
Tax Ref. Case No. 15 of 1992
The Judgment was delivered by: BHARUCHA, J.
1.  The question that the Court is called upon to answer in this reference at the behest of the assessee reads thus:
"Whether, on the facts and in the circumstances of the case, the Tribunal is right in law in holding that the assessee-company is not entitled to depreciation allowance in respect of 11th and 12th floors of Hansalaya Building at Barakhamba Road, New Delhi ?"
2.  The answer to the question is covered by the decision of this Court in Mysore Minerals Ltd. vs. CIT 1999 indlaw sc 11831999 Indlaw SC 1183. Following that judgment, the question is answered in the negative and in favour of the assessee. No order as to costs.
Appeal allowed.
