Vijay Kuma v Government of N.C.T. of Delhi
Supreme Court of India

9 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 6549/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard Mr. D.N.Goburdhan, learned counsel for the petitioner.
1.  We are not inclined to interfere with the order passed by the High Court rejecting the petitioner's prayer for anticipatory bail.
2. However, in the facts and circumstances of the case we grant him protection against arrest in connection with the criminal case for ten day to enable the petitioner to surrender in Court and file application for regular bail. If such application is filed the same will be disposed of expeditiously.
3. With this observation the special leave petition is disposed of.
Petition disposed of.
