Raichurmatham Prabhakar and Anr v Rawatmal Dugar
Supreme Court of India

6 November 2001
CIVIL APPEAL NOs. 2152-2153 OF 1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  None appears for the appellants. The appeals are directed to be dismissed for non-prosecution at 3.35 p.m.
2.  The Order of stay passed on 1/2/1999 and continued on 5/4/1999 is directed to be vacated.
Appeals Dismissed.
