State of Uttar Pradesh and Others v Ram Khelawan and Others
Supreme Court of India

17 August 1995
Civil Appellate Jurisdiction : Civil Appeal No. 7873 of 1995
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Leave granted.
1.  Heard thecounsel on both sides. The High Court per-judged the issue by directing to pay as if the respondent is a regular employee. Since thisissueis pending adjudication, the High Court is not justified in giving such direction.
2.  Accordingly, the direction is set aside. Since the matter is pending in the High Court, we request it to dispose of the same as expeditiously as possible preferably within six months, giving preference to the writ petition.
The appeal is accordingly allowed.
Appeal allowed
