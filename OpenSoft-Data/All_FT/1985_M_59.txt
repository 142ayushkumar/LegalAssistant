Bihari Lal v Mst. Kalyani
Supreme Court of India

2 December 1985
S.L.P. to Appeal (Criminal) No. 488 of 1985 (From the Judgment and Order Dt. 7 November 1984 of the High Court of Madhya Pradesh at Jabalpur in Cr. Revision No. 25 of 1983), with application for ex parte stay and Criminal Miscellaneous Petition No. 1059 of 1985.
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Special leave petition is directed against an order of maintenance awarded by the High Court.
We are surprised that special leave petitions are still being filed against such orders.
1. We think that it is high time that such petitions are not filed and not entertained. SLP is dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
