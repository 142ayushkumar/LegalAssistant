State of Haryana v Veer Parkash and Another
Supreme Court of India

24 March 2008
C.A. No.2147 of 2008 (Arising Out of S.L.P.(C)No.10310 of 2007)
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Leave granted.
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
1. On 15.05.2007, notice was issued limited to the question as to whether back wages should be paid or not. Considering the facts and circumstances of the case, we are of the view that instead of 50% of back wages as directed by the Labour Court and as affirmed by the High court, 25% of back wages shall be paid to the respondent within a period of two months from this date. We order accordingly. With this modification, the order of the High Court is affirmed. The appeal is disposed of accordingly. There shall be no order as to costs.
Appeal disposed of.
