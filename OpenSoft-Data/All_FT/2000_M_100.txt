Madhyamik Vidyalaya Prabandhak Assn. and Anr v State of Uttar Pradesh and Others
Supreme Court of India

5 December 2000
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.19502/1998
The order of the court was as follows:
Leave is granted.
1.  After arguing the matter for quite some time Mr.R.P. Gupta learned counsel appearing for the appellants submits that inasmuch as many contentions are to be urged before the High Court, he seeks permission to withdraw the appeal with liberty to approach the High Court for appropriate relief.
The appeal is, therefore, dismissed as withdrawn.
Appeal dismissed.
