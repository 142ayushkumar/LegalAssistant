State of Haryana and Others v Darshan Kumar and Others
Supreme Court of India

2 January 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.1800-1802/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  Learned counsel for the appellants submits that the respondents would be regularised as per the new policy and appropriate orders would be passed within two months. It is clarified that it would be open to the Competent Authority to take action, if any inquiry is pending against some of the parties.
2.  As against this, learned counsel for the respondents vehemently submits that respondents are entitled to the benefits of the old Policy of 1994 and that they ought to have been regularised under the said Policy.
Considering the limited dispute hearing is expedited.
Order accordingly.
