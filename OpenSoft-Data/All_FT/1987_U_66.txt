Mrs. Louise Fernandes and Others v Union of India and Another
Supreme Court of India

2 March 1987
W.P. (Criminal) No. 149 of 1987
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1. By this petition, the petitioners challenge the constitutional validity of certain provisions of the Terrorists and Disruptive Activities (Prevention) Act, 1985.  It is not disputed that the question can be raised before the High Court inasmuch as it involves the constitutional validity of the provisions of the Act.
2. In the circumstances, the writ petition is allowed to be withdrawn with liberty to the petitioners to apply to the High Court for relief.
Petition allowed.
