Commissioner of Wealth Tax v Sheo Kumar Gupta
Supreme Court of India

24 February 1993
C.As. Nos. 1544 and 1545 of 1977
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the appellant fairly states that the point involved in this appeal is covered against the appellant by the judgment of this court in CWT v. B. K. Sharma 1990 Indlaw SC 911
2.  In view of the judgment in B. K. Sharma's case 1990 Indlaw SC 911 and for the reasons recorded therein, we dismiss this appeal. No costs.
Appeal dismissed.
