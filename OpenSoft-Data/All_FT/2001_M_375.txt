Commissioner of Central Excise, Mumbai-Iii v Messrs Philips India Limited
Supreme Court of India

13 December 2001
C.A. Nos. 2083-2085 of 1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Having heard the counsel for the parties, we are not satisfied that there is any material on record which can persuade this Court to come to a conclusion different from the one arrived at by the Tribunal, namely, that the item in question, viz., stem of a bulb, is a marketable commodity.
While affirming the order of the Tribunal, these appeals are dismissed.
Appeals dismissed.
