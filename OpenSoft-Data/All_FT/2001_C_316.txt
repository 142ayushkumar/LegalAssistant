Tamil Nadu State Transport Corporation Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax
Supreme Court of India

11 October 2001
C.As. Nos. 4383 and 4384 of 1999 (Appeals by special leave from the judgment and order Dt. 2 March 1998, of the Madras High Court in T.C. Nos. 1820 and 1821 of 1986).
The Order of the Court was as follows:
We have heard learned counsel for the appellant.
There is no merit in the appeals.
The civil appeals are dismissed with costs.
Appeals dismissed.
