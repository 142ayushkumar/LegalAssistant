Union of India and Anr v Messrs Kash Ind-Roller Flour Mills
Supreme Court of India

16 April 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.18107/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard counsel for the parties.
Leave granted.
1.  Learned Counsel for the respondent stated that the respondent is prepared to furnish security to the satisfaction of the Registrar, Delhi High Court and on furnishing such security, the amount deposited be released in its favour.
2.  Counsel for the appellants has no objection. We, therefore, direct that the amount deposited by the appellants be released in favour of the respondent on the respondent's furnishing security to the satisfaction of the Registrar, Delhi High Court.
3.  The appeal is disposed of in the aforesaid terms. There shall be no order as to costs.
Appeal disposed of.
