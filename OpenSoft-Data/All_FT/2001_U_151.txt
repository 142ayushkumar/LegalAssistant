Union of India and Others v Mannalal Hiralal Agrawal and Others
Supreme Court of India

7 November 2001
C.A. No.4581/1995
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Ms. Rachna Gupta, learned counsel filed an application for substitution to bring the legal representative of one of the deceased respondents. She states that the applicant is no longer interested in the matter. In view of this statement, the application filed on 8.9.2001 is dismissed.
2.  Mr. NN Goswami, learned senior counsel started his arguments at 11.10 a.m. and concluded at 2.20 p.m.
3.  Thereafter Mr. PS Mishra, learned senior counsel made his arguments till 4.00 p.m., when the Court rose for the day, leaving the matter as part-heard.
Order accordingly.
