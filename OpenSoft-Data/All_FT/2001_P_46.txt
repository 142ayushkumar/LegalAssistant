Vivek Goenka v Padam Sambhav Jain and Another
Supreme Court of India

24 July 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 399/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The special leave having been granted on the identical issue in the case of K.M. Mathew v. K.A. Abraham and Ors.
we think it appropriate to hear and dispose of both the matters together, if the other matter is ready. Even in the other matter while granting leave, liberty had been given to the counsel to mention for early hearing.
2.  We accordingly direct that this SLP and the aforesaid Criminal Appeal be posted together on Tuesday, the 21st of August, 2001.
Order accordingly.
