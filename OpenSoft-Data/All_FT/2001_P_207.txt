Project and Equipment Corporation, of India v Messrs Mandavi Marine Private Limited and Anr
Supreme Court of India

4 January 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.20876/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  After going through the order carefully, we are not, in any way doubt that in the event an application is filed by the respondent and objections with regard to its maintaibility or to the acceptance of arbitration clause or with regard to the appointment of arbitrator, is raised by the petitioner, the same shall be decided first before proceeding any further with the matter.
2.  In this view of the matter, we are not inclined to interfere with the impugned order. The special leave petition is, accordingly, dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
