Shaikh Mohd. Shamsuddin v State of Gujarat
Supreme Court of India

27 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal(Cr.)......./2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Delay condoned.
Issue notice returnable in six weeks.
1.  Meantime, Registry to call for information as to how many NDPS appeals with year-wise break up are pending for disposal and to find out whether following direction issued by this Court in Maktool Singh vs. State of Punjab is complied with:
"As a temporary measure to lessen the problem we direct the Registry of each High Court to include every appeal (against conviction of offences under the Act) in the hearing list as soon as such appeal becomes ripe for hearing."
Order accordingly.
