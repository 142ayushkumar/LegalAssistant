Kamala Devi and Others v Hinch Lal Tiwari and Others
Supreme Court of India

20 November 2001
Review Petition(Civil) No.1448/2001 in C.A.No.4787/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We have perused the order under review, the Review Petition and Rules 115-M, 115-N, 115-O and 115-P of the U.P.
Zamindari Abolition and Land Reforms Rules, 1952 (Annexure R-6).
2. After careful examination of the contention raised, we are of the view that no case for review of our order dated 25th July, 2001 is made out. The Review Petition is, therefore, dismissed.
Review dismissed.
