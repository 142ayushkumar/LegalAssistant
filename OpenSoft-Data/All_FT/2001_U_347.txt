United India Insurance Company Limited v Patricia Jean Mahajan and Others
Supreme Court of India

7 December 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.20874/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard the learned Attorney General for the petitioners.
Issue notice to the respondents.
1.  Shri Ashok K. Mahajan, learned counsel, who has appeared on caveat for respondents 1 to 6 accepts notice for the said respondents.
2. No further step be taken in respect of respondents 1 to 6. Steps for service of notice on the remaining respondents be taken within six weeks.
3.  As an interim measure, the petitioner is directed to deposit a sum of Rupees Five Crores with the Tribunal within two months from today. On the amount being deposited, the same shall be kept in a fixed deposit in a nationalised Bank for a term of six months, renewable for the similar period after its expiry.
Order accordingly.
