R.K. Singh v M.C.D. and Others
Supreme Court of India

13 November 2000
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.2759/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
1.  After hearing the appellant in-person and counsel for the respondents, we are of the opinion that the High Court should go into the case afresh keeping in view the averments which have been made in the affidavits filed by the State of Bihar as well as the appellant in this Court.
2. We, accordingly, allow this appeal, set aside the impugned judgment of the High Court and restore Civil Writ Petition No.372/1994 on the board of the High Court.
Appeal allowed.
