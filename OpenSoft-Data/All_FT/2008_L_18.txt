Badesaheb M.S.T.(D)By Pros.L.Rs. &Ors. v Lalasaheb F. Tehsildar (Dead)By Lrs.&Ors
Supreme Court of India

17 July 2008
CIVIL APPEAL NO.5396 OF 1998
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1.  In view of the law laid down by this Court in Abubakar Abdul Inamdar (Dead) by Lrs. & Ors. vs. Harun Abdul Inamdar and Ors., (1995) 5 SCC 612 1995 Indlaw SC 1200, we do not find any ground to interfere with the impugned judgment. The appeal is accordingly dismissed. There shall be no order as to costs.
Appeal dismissed
