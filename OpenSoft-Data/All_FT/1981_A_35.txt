Amathabhai Arjanbhai v C. D. Patel and Another
Supreme Court of India

2 March 1981
S.L.P. (Cr.) No. 3467 of 1979
The Judgment was delivered by: BAHARUL ISLAM, J.
1.  The Public Analyst found the sample of milk deficient in fact content submits that it was not possible to detect 0.5% deficiency of fat content in the milk.
It was not put to the Food Inspector or to the Public Analyst who would have been the proper person to answer the question. The point also does not appear to have been taken in any of the three courts below
The petition is rejected
Petition dismissed.
