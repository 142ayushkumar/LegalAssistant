State of Rajasthan and Others v Mahendra Kanwar and Another
Supreme Court of India

6 July 2009
Civil Appeal No. 4135 of 2009 (Arising out of SLP(C) No. 17346 of 2008)
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Leave granted.
1.  The High Court by the impugned order has only directed the authorities to consider the case of the respondents for grant of family pension if payable to them. In these circumstances, we are not inclined to interfere with the impugned order.
The appeal is accordingly dismissed.
Appeal dismissed
