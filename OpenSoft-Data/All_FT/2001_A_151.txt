Commissioner of Income Tax v Ambur Co-Operative Sugar Mills Limited (And
Supreme Court of India

28 August 2001
C.A. No. 2499 of 1998 With Other Appeals and a S.L.P.
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Delay condoned.
1.  The civil appeals and special leave petition are not pressed by Mr. M.L.Verma, learned counsel for the Revenue, for the reason that the special leave petition against the judgment followed by the High Court in the order under appeal was dismissed as also special leave petitions against the judgments of various High Courts taking a similar view.
2. The civil appeals and special leave petition are dismissed. No order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed.
