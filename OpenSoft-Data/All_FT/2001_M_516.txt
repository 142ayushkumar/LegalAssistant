A.K. Muniyappa (Dead) By Lrs v Munniappa and Others
Supreme Court of India

5 January 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.8344-8345/1998
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Mr.Sanjay R.Hegde, learned counsel for the State of Karnataka submitted under insutructions that Government would be considering granting an alternative site of land to the petitioner as he belongs to a weaker section. We direct the petitioner to make an applicatioin before the Deputy Commissioner, Kolar within one month from today for the purpose of allotment of the said land.
2. Mr.Hegde submits that if any such application will be filed the alternative land will be allotted pursuant to the said application within 2 months thereafter. (A copy of the application shall be forwarded by the petitioner to the Revenue Department Karnataka).
The SLP is disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
