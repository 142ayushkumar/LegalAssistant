Suresh Kumar v State of Haryana and Others
Supreme Court of India

9 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.18716/2001
The order of the Court was as follows:
1.  It is pointed out to us by the learned counsel for the petitioner that the concerned Note to regulation dated March 2001 provides:
NET shall remain the compulsory requirement for appointment as Lecturer even for candidates having Ph.D. degree. However, the candidate who have completed M.phil.degree or have submitted Ph.D. thesis in the concerned subject up to 31st December, 1993, are exempted from appearing in the NET examination makes a invidious discrimination between the candidates based on date of qualification which discrimination according to the counsel for the petitioner is unsustainable.
Issue notice.
Order accordingly.
