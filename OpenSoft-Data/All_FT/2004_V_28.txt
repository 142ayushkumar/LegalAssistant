J. Durga Debi v Venkata Bala Subramani
Supreme Court of India

29 November 2004
Transfer Petition (C) No. 748 of 2003
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  For the reasons mentioned in the transfer petition such as that the wife is unemployed with a two-year-old son and the distance at which the proceedings are pending, we consider it a fit case to be transferred.
2. Matrimonial Suit No.8 of 2003 entitled Venkata Bala Subramani v. J.Durga Debi pending before the Additional District Judge, Alipurduar, West Bengal is, therefore, ordered to be transferred to District Dharmapuri with a direction to the District Judge to either hear the same himself or place it before a court of competent jurisdiction.
The transfer petition is allowed accordingly.
Petition allowed.
