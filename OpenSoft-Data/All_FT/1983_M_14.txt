Mahendra Kumar Shastri v Union of India and Another
Supreme Court of India

10 January 1983
Write Petn. No. 1675 of 1979
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1.  We do not find any merit in the contentions urged by the petitioner in the writ petition. The disability which is imposed u/s. 62(5) of the Representation of the People Act is equally applicable to all persons similarly situate mentioned therein and they are even prevented from contesting the election or offering themselves as candidates for such election.
2. The provision is reasonable and in public interest to maintain purity in electing peoples' representatives and there is no. arbitrariness or discrimination involved. Rule is discharged and the writ petition is dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
