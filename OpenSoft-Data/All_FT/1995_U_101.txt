Supreme Court Legal Aid Committee Representing Undertrial Prisoners v Union of India and Another
Supreme Court of India

17 April 1995
W.P. (Cr.) No. 307 of 1993
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  After this Court delivered the judgment on 7-10-1994 in Supreme Court Legal Aid Committee v. Union of India, notices were issued to ten State Government calling for information in a proforma appended at the foot of the judgment/order. The information which has been supplied has been found in some cases to be incomplete and defective. But it is not necessary to detain ourselves any further because that also affects the undertrials in those States.
2.   We, therefore, direct that the decision of this Court in the aforesaid matter , Supreme Court Legal Aid Committee v. States, namely, States of Andhra Pradesh, Assam, Kerala, Karnataka, Gujarat, Orissa, Bihar, West Bengal, Uttar Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh. With the said observations we dispose of this petition finally.
Petition disposed of.
