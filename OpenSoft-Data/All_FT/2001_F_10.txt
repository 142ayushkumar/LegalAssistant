Ram Lal and Anr v Financial Commnr. Revenue, Haryana and Others
Supreme Court of India

14 December 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.14037/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There is a letter on record on behalf of the petitioners to say that the arguing counsel of the petitioners will seek an adjournment for two weeks on the grounds of demise in his family. The application is opposed on the ground that adjournments have been taken in the past.
2.  We are concerned with the advocate on record and would grant an adjournment if there had been some difficulty with him.
we do not grant adjournments for the convenience of counsel.
The application for adjournment is refused.
3.  The advocate who had made the application for the petitioners is not present nor is anyone else. The special leave petition is dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
