Commissioner of Central Excise, Delhi v R. T. Packaging Limited
Supreme Court of India

24 February 2003
C.A. No. 5148 of 2002
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Delay is condoned.
1.  Since the Revenue has accepted the judgment of the Customs, Excise and Gold (Control) Appellate Tribunal in CCE v. India Foils Ltd. (2000) 39 RLT 304 : (2001) 132 ELT 737 2000 Indlaw CEGAT 2387 (CEGAT) and that judgment has been followed in this case by the Tribunal, the civil appeal is dismissed.
No costs.
Appeal dismissed.
