C.M. Lakshmanna v M. Satyanarayana Reddy
Supreme Court of India

2 August 2001
CIVIL APPEAL NO. 1367 OF 1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel appearing for the appellant states that due to passage of time and also in view of the ensuing elections, these appeals are rendered infructuous. Accordingly, they are dismissed as such, leaving the question of law open to be decided in an appropriate case.
Appeals Dismissed.
