Bharat Kumar Manilal Dalal v Controller of Estate Duty, Gujarat
Supreme Court of India

27 November 1986
Civil Miscellaneous Petition No. 30393 of 1986 in C.A. No. 930 of 1975.
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Having regard to the decision of this court in M. Ct. Muthiah v. Controller of Estate Duty 1986 Indlaw SC 359, this appeal is allowed.
2.   For-the appellant is allowed to withdraw the relief No. (ii) in C.M.P. NO. 30393 of 1986 as he proposes to claim that relief in another forum.
3.  The civil miscellaneous petition and the civil appeal are disposed of accordingly. There will be no order as to costs.
Appeal allowed.
