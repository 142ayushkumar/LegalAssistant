Bochna Nageswar Rao v State of Orissa
Supreme Court of India

19 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 3283-3284/2001
The Order Of the Court was as follows:
Heard the learned counsel for the parties.
1.  The condition imposed by the Assistant Sessions Judge, Koraput vide order dated 19th March,2001,for releasing the petitioner on bail to deposit a sum of Rs.2 lakhs which was modified by the High Court to Rs.1 lakh is set aside. Instead of that, the petitioenr shall execute a bail bond for a sum of Rs.1 lakh (rupees one lakh) with two solvent sureties of the like amount. Rest of the conditions imposed by the learned Judge shall be complied with by the petitioner.
Petitions stand disposed of accordingly.
Petitions Disposed of.
