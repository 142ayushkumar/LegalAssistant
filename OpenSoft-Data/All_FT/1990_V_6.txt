V. Subramanian v Union of India and Another
Supreme Court of India

9 April 1990
W.P. (Civil) No. 328 of 1990
The Order of the Court is as follows
1. This petition has been received by post alleging air pollution by the cement factory at Ariyalur (Tamil Nadu). We are of the view that this petition should be transmitted to the Madras High Court for disposal in accordance with law by treating the same as one u/art. 226 of the Constitution.
The notice shall be issued to the respondent and on the basis of the counter-affidavit, filed by them, the matter should be proceeded with.
Order accordingly
