Ranjit Chowdhury and Others v Dilip K. Roy and Others
Supreme Court of India

12 April 2001
IA No 2 In C.A..No.6350/1998
The Order of the court was as follows:
1.  Mr. Sanghi submits that this petition be adjourned because the appellants have filed a Special Leave Petition against an order of eviction and that petition is yet to be numbered and likely to be listed shortly. We cannot agree.
2. After hearing learned counsel for the parties, we are of the opinion that in the established facts and circumstances of this case, the interim order made on 31st August, 1998 needs to be vacated and we make an order accordingly.
3.  The Special Leave Petition referred to by Mr.Sanghi shall come up for consideration on its own merits, as and when listed.
I.A. No.2 is disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
