Omveer v Government of N.C.T. of Delhi
Supreme Court of India

24 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 3174/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  We are not inclined to interfere with the order passed by the High Court refusing the petitioner's prayer for bail during the pendency of the appeal, at this stage. The High Court is requested to dispose of the appeal, filed by the petitioner, expeditiously. If the appeal is not disposed of by the end of June,2001,for any reason,it will be open to the petitioner to file application for bail before the High Court.
The special leave petition is disposed of.
Petition disposed of.
