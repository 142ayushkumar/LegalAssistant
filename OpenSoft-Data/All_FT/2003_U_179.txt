Union of India v Zila Singh and others
Supreme Court of India

15 January 2003
C.A. No. 300 of 2003 (Arising out of S.L.P. (C) No. 8668 of 2000)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Despite service, none appears on behalf of the respondents.
IA No. 2 for substitution is allowed.
Delay condoned.
Special leave granted.
1.  In view of a three-Judge Bench decision of this Court in Union of India v. Ram Phool dated 28-11-2002, this appeal is allowed and the judgment under challenge is set aside.
There shall be no order as to costs.
Appeal allowed.
