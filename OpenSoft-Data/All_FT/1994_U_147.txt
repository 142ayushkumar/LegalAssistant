Mohan Chand v Union of India and Others
Supreme Court of India

14 November 1994
W.P. (Cr.) No. 326 of 1994
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  This petition under Article 32 of the Constitution of India relates to incident at Khatima on 1-9-1992 in connection with movement for Uttarakhand. It appears that some matters are pending in the Allahabad High Court regard to the same incident and other connected incidents.
2.   In these circumstances, we consider it more appropriate that this matter be dealt with by the Allahabad High Court as a petition under Article 226 of the Constitution of India.
3.  It is, therefore, directed that this writ petition may be transferred to the Allahabad High Court for disposal by treating it as a petition under Article 226 of the Constitution of India. Liberty to mention before the High Court. The writ petition is disposed of accordingly.
Petition disposed of.
