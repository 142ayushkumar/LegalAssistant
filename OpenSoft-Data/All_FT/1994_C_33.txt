Card Board Box Manufacturing Company v Collector of Central Excise, Calcutta
Supreme Court of India

14 July 1994
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We have today delivered judgment in Rollatainers Ltd. v. Union of India.
2.  For the reasons and the conclusions reached by us in the said appeal, this civil appeal is dismissed and the order dated 27-3-1984 of the Central Excise and Gold (Control) Appellate Tribunal, New Delhi is upheld. No costs.
Appeal dismissed.
