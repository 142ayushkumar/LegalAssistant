Bihar State Credit & Investment Corporation Ltd. & Ors v Prakash Roller Flour Mills Ltd
Supreme Court of India

25 January 2008
Appeal (civil) 749 of 2008
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. Leave granted.
2. The Appeal stands allowed in terms of the additional affidavit filed on behalf of the respondent-M/s. Prakash Roller Flour Mills Ltd.
One more point needs to be clarified. Undertaking has been given by the respondent herein through its Director Shri Raman Prakash Saraogi. The affidavit is taken on record. Undertaking is accepted.
3. Appeal is disposed of in terms of the said undertaking.
Appeal disposed of
