Ram Chandra Kumar v State of Bihar
Supreme Court of India

31 July 2001
Review Petition(Cr.) No. 790/2001 in S.L.P.(Cr.)No. 2256/1997
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There is an inordinate delay of 1298 days in filing the review petition which has not been explained satisfactorily.
Accordingly, the review petition is dismissed on the ground of delay.
Review dismissed.
