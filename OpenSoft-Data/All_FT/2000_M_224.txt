Shyamarao (Dead) By LRs v Messrs Raghavendra Const.Co. and Others
Supreme Court of India

3 November 2000
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.6100/1999
The Order of the court was as follows:
Leave granted.
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
1. We do not find any error in the impugned order which require any interference, except to modify the condition No. (b) which requires the appellant to get audited on every 5th of each month his accounts which he has to submit. Instead of audited accounts to be filed on 5th of each month, it could be filed twice in a year after every six months. With the said modifications, the present appeal stands disposed of.
Appeal disposed of.
