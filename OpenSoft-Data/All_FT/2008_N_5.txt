N. Kannadasan v Ajoy Khose and others
Supreme Court of India

19 December 2008
C.A. No. 7360 of 2008 with C.A. No. 7368 of 2008 C.A. No. 7371 of 2008 C.A. No. 7372 of 2008
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. Taken on Board.
2. Issue notice.
3. Ms. Kamini Jaiswal, Advocate accepts notice on behalf of caveator-respondent No.1
4. Let notice be served upon the other non-appearing respondents.
5. Let this matter appear for hearing on 22nd January, 2009.
6. It is accepted at the Bar that no counter affidavit need be filed. The pleadings used/filed by the parties before the High Court shall be treated to be the pleadings before this Court. Additional documents, if any, may be filed by 15th January 2009.
7. The Registrar General of the Madras High Court shall send, in sealed cover, the entire file of the writ petitions and original records of this case for our perusal on the next date fixed.
8. In view of the statements made by Mr. Ranjit Kumar and Mr. Lalit, learned senior counsel appearing for Mr. N. Kannadasan, that till the disposal of these appeals, Mr. N. Kannadasan, Presiding Officer would go on leave, the operation of the impugned judgment shall remain stayed until further orders.
9. We request the learned Solicitor General of India to assist us in this matter.
Order accordingly
