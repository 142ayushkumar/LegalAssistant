Travancore Tea Estates Company Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax
Supreme Court of India

17 December 1997
C.A. No. 11369 of 1995
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  This case is about writing off of bad debts It is well settled that whether a debt has become bad or the point of time when it became bad are pure questions of fact
There is no question of law involved in this appeal.
We, therefore, decline to go into the controversy raised.
2.  The appeal is dismissed. There will be no order as to costs
Appeal dismissed.
