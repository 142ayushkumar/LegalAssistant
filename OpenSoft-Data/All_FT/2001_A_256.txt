Rup Chand Daftary v Ashim Ranjan Modak and Anr
Supreme Court of India

9 July 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.8083/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We find no merit in this petition and it is accordingly dismissed.
2.  However, after hearing learned counsel for the parties, on the facts and circumstances of the case, we observe, the petitioner shall vacate the premises in question by or before the 31st December, 2001 subject to the usual undertaking to be filed within four weeks from today.
3. He shall pay all the arrears, if any, within four weeks from today and will continue to pay the amount which he has been paying, till he hands over possession on or before 31st December, 2001.
Petition dismissed.
