Baldev Singh and Anr v State of Madhya Pradesh
Supreme Court of India

9 November 2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Heard learned counsel for the parties. Considering the averments made in the petition for temporary bail filed by appellant No.1, and the submissions made by the learned counsel for the parties, the prayer for temporary bail is granted.
2.  The appellant No.1, Baldev Singh shall be released on bail for two months on executing a bond of Rs.10,000/- with two sureties each of the like amount to the satisfaction of the trial court. He shall surrender to custody by 15th January, 2002, without fail.
Order accordingly.
