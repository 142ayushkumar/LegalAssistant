Municipal Corporation, Faridabad v Maan Singh and Others
Supreme Court of India

26 February 2001
C.A..No.6411/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  After hearing the learned counsel for the parties, we are of the opinion that inasmuch as the award of the tribunal to the effect that the respondents were appointed as Tubewell Operators has become final. The same not being challenged by the appellant herein the decision of the High Court directing the implementation of the award cannot be assailed.
The civil appeal is dismissed.
Appeal dismissed.
