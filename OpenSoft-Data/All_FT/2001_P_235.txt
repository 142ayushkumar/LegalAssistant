Ranjit Singh Bath v Punjabi University and Others
Supreme Court of India

25 April 2001
C.A. No. 5180 OF 1998
The order of the Court was as follows:
1.  There is an inordinate delay of 854 days in filing the review petition for which the explanation offered is not found to be satisfactory. Hence the review petition is dismissed on the ground of delay.
Petition dismissed.
