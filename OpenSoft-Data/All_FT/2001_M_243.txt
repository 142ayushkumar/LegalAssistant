Matkan Singh and Anr v Secretary To Government Haryana, Reh.Deptt. and
Supreme Court of India

19 January 2001
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  Notice was issued on the writ petition limited to the question question as to why appropriate observation should not be made to the effect that the decision rendered in the proceedings in the absence of the petitioners would not be treated as binding on the petitioners as it may be open to the petitioners to ventilate their grievance in accordance with law.
2. Having heard learned counsel for the parties, who are agreeable to such an observation, we order that observation in the above terms be issued as it will meet the ends of justice.
Accordingly this writ petition is disposed of. No costs.
Petition disposed of.
