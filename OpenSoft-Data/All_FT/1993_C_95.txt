Commissioner of Income Tax v Singh Transport Company
Supreme Court of India

7 January 1993
C.As. Nos. 3642 and 3643 of 1983 (Arising as an appeal from the judgment and order of Gauhati High Court is in Income-tax Reference No. 16 of 1978)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  These appeals are covered by the pronouncement of this court in Garden Silk Weaving Factory v. CIT 1991 Indlaw SC 922. For the reasons stated in and following the said pronouncement, these appeals are dismissed, but without any order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed.
