State of Punjab and Others v Amarjit Singh Grewal and Others
Supreme Court of India

1 October 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.14642-14643/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  SLP(C)No.14642/2001: Learned counsel for respondents 1 to 3 prays for and is granted four weeks time for filing counter affidavit.
2. Steps for fresh service on respondent No.4 will also be taken within 4 weeks. List after service is complete.
3.  SLP(C)No.14643/2001: Learned counsel appearing for respondent No.3 prays for and is granted four weeks' time for filing counter affidavit. Steps for fresh service on 2 respondent No.4 will also be taken within four weeks.
4.  The other respondents who have been served with notice may file counter affidavit within four weeks. List after service is complete.
Order accordingly.
