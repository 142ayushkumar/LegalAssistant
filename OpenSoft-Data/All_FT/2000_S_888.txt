Mohammed Mahmoob v Syed Yousufuddin and Others
Supreme Court of India

15 December 2000
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.20097/2000
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  The special leave petition is dismissed Learned counsel for the petitioner states that in case the petitioner is required to vacate the premises in question immediately, he would be put to great hardship and for that reason the petitioner may be granted some time to vacate the premises.
2. We accordingly direct that the petitioner shall not be dispossessed from the premises till 30th June, 2001, subject to filing a usual undertaking to this effect in this Court within four weeks from today.
3.  In case the landlords are aggrieved against this part of the order, it will be open to them to apply for vacation of this order.
Petition dismissed.
