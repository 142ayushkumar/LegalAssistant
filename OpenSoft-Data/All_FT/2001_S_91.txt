Delhi Development Authority v Salek Chandra Garg
Supreme Court of India

14 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.15157/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Mr. Rohtagi, learned Additional Solicitor General submits that in view of para 3.1 of the Office Memorandum dated 14.09.1992 and the direction issued by the Division Bench on April 10, 2001 in the appeal against order of the learned Single Judge to the effect that the petitioner may act in accordance with law, the petitioner is advised to pass appropriate orders and in that view of the matter, he seeks to withdraw this SLP.
2. We record the statement of the learned Additional Solicitor General and dismiss the special leave petition as withdrawn.
Petition dismissed.
