Bank of Baroda v Messrs Mathura Tractor and Auto.Pvt.Ltd. and Others
Supreme Court of India

21 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.18278/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
1.  I.A. No. 3 has been filed under Order 23 Rule 3, CPC, for recording settlement between the parties.
2.  Essential terms of the settlement are contained in paragraph 3 of the application. The settlement, inter alia, contemplates the suit filed by the Bank being decreed as prayed for, but if respondents 1 to 5 pay a sum of Rs.20 lakhs in four instalments, the last instalment being paid by 15th September, 2001, then the decree in Suit No. 510 of 1991 shall stand satisfied.
3.  This application is supported by affidavits on behalf of the Bank as well as of respondents 1 to 5.
4.  We, accordingly, allow this application and pass a decree in terms thereof. The application itself shall form part of the decree.This appeal stands disposed of.
Appeal disposed of.
