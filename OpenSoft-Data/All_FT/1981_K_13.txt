Administrator, Ranchi Municipal Corporation v Kamakhya Narain Singh and Others
Supreme Court of India

24 April 1981
Transfer Petition (Civil) No. 1 of 1981.
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1.  We have heard Mr Attorney-General for the petitioner and Mr B.C. Ghose for the respondents.
2.  In view of the points made in the transfer petition and in view of the fact that Civil Appeal No. 2889 of 1980 pending in this Court in the case of Kamakhya Narain Singh raises certain questions which would have an impact on the decision of C.W.J.Cs. Nos. 802 and 805 of 1980 pending in the High Court of Patna, Ranchi Bench, Ranchi and in order to avoid any conflict of decisions in these cases, we direct that C.W.J.Cs. Nos. 802 and 805 of 1980 pending in the High Court of Patna, Ranchi Bench, Ranchi, be withdrawn and stand transferred to this Court and be heard along with C.A. No. 2889 of 1980 pending in this Court.
3. The connected petitions for amendment of Writ Petitions Nos. 802 and 805 of 1980 shall also stand transferred to this Court for disposal.There shall be no order as to costs.
Matter remanded to high court- Order Accordingly.
