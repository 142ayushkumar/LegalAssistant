Prithish Lala Prema Lala and Others v Pushpa Rani Devi and Others
Supreme Court of India

10 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.14962/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
We are not inclined to interfere with the matter.
1.  The Special Leave Petition is dismissed. After we dismissed the matter, Mr. M.P. Varma, learned senior counsel for the petitioners stated that in case the petitioners are required to vacate the premises immediately, it shall create great hardship to them.
2.  Therefore, six months' time may be granted to vacate the premises. Learned counsel appearing for the respondents has no objection.
3. We, therefore, grant time till 31.3.2002 to the petitioners to vacate the premises on their filing usual undertaking in this Court within four weeks from today. In case of any default, this order shall stand automatically vacated.
Petition dismissed.
