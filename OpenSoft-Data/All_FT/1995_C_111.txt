Ferro Alloys Corporation Limited v Collector of Customs and Central Excise
Supreme Court of India

21 August 1995
C.A. No. 6928 of 1995, (Arising as an appeal from 1995 (77) E.L.T. 302 (Tribunal)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Since the ultimate decision is in favour of the appellant and is neither relevant on the rate of duty applicable nor on the question of value of goods for the purpose of assessment, this appeal is not maintainable under Section 35L of the Central Excise Act.
2. On this ground alone, the appeal is dismissed without going into the merits of the question decided by the Full Bench of the Tribunal.
Appeal dismissed.
