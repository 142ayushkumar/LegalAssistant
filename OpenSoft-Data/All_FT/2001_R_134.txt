Rajni Duggal v Hari Om Maheshwari
Supreme Court of India

27 July 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.9993/2000
The order of the court was as follows:
Leave granted. Heard learned counsel for the parties.
1. The present appeal is directed against the order of revision by setting aside the order of Rent Controller by which it rejected the application of the tenant-respondent for grant of leave to defend.
2.  We have perused the order passed by the High Court. We do not find any merit which calls for our interference. The High Court has given good reasons for setting aside.The appeal is accordingly dismissed.
3. Looking to the facts and circumstances of the case, we do observe that the Rent Controller will dispose of the matter expeditious preferably by the end of this year.
Appeal dismissed.
