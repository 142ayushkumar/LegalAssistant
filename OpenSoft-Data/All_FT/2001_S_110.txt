Executive Director(Personnel)Lic and Anr v Sangamesh and Others
Supreme Court of India

3 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal(Civil).....(cc.5733/2001)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Delay condoned.
1.  It is contended by the learned Solicitor General that in view of the directions issued by this Court in the judgment in Director of Tribal Welfare, Govt. of A.P. vs. Leveti Giri and Anr. the employer is left with no option but to cancel the appointment of the respondent even if the same is in contravention of Article 311 of the Constitution of India or other allied service rules applicable to the employees of the instrumentalities of the States. Issue notice.
Order accordingly.
