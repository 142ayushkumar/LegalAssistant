Collector of Central Excise Indore v Messrs Orient Paper Mills
Supreme Court of India

7  May  1997
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. The principle laiddown in the judgment delivered by us today in Civil Appeal Nos. 3596-97 of 1989 Collector of Central Excise, Patna vs. Tata Engineering & Locomotive Co 1997 Indlaw SC 152., Jamshedpur, being applicable, these appeals are also dismissed, with no order as to costs.
