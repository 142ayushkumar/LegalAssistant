N. M. Abraham and another v State of Kerala
Supreme Court of India

2 February 1983
Cr.As. Nos. 298 and 299 of 1976 (Appeals by special leave from the Judgment and Order Dt. 27 June 1976 of the Kerala High Court in Cr.As. Nos. 413 and 414 of 1974).
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Heard counsel for the parties.
1.  The appellant were convicted as far back as in 1973 and were released on bail in 1976. Having regard to the very peculiar facts and circumstances of this case while upholding the conviction of the appellants, we reduce the sentence of both the appellants to the period already served and in lieu of the sentence remitted we impose a fine of Rs 500 on each of the appellants. In default of the payment of fine the appellants shall suffer rigorous imprisonment for six months. The fine imposed will be deposited in the trial court within a month from today.
The appeals are accordingly disposed of.
Appeals disposed of.
