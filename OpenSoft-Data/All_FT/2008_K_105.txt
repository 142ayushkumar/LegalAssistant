Kamlesh Mittal v Board of Revenue Uttar Pradesh and Others
Supreme Court of India

21 February 2008
Civil Appeal No. 8266 of 2004
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
1.  Considering the nature of the controversy, we direct that the claim of the appellant which is for about 7 Biswas, shall be allotted to her in Plot No. 4323 instead of Plot No. 4324 on the Western side.
The appeal is accordingly disposed of. No costs.
Appeal disposed of
