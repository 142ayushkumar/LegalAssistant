Innovative Capital Strategies Pvt.Ltd v Dhirajbala P.Sanghvi and Others
Supreme Court of India

3 September 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.4879/2000
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  Having heard the learned senior counsel for the petitioner at length and having considered the matter, we do not find that it is a fit case for our interference under Article 136 of the Constitution of India except to the extent of directing that the plaint which has been rejected under Order 7 Rule 11(d) ought to have been returned for presentation to the appropriate Court, if necessary, by appropriately drawing up as many plaints as necessary arising out of that plaint.
The special leave petition is disposed of with the aforesaid observation.
Petition disposed of.
