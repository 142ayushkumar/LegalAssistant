Putli G. Dadlani v Pisu Rijumal Mirani and Others
Supreme Court of India

24 November 2000
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.18538/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  After we heard the matter, we do not find any merit in the petition. The special leave petition is dismissed accordingly.
2.  Learned counsel for the petitioner states that in case the petitioner is required to vacate the premises in question immediately, she would be put to irreparable loss and for that reason she may be granted time till 30th June, 2001 to vacate the premises. We find the request is reasonable.
3. We accordingly direct that the petitioner shall not be dispossessed from the premises till 30th June, 2001 provided she files a usual undertaking to this effect in this Court within four weeks from today. In case the respondents are aggrieved against this part of the order, it will be open to them to move this Court for recall of this part of the order.
Petition dismissed.
