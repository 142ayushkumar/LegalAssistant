Ramesh Kumar Sharma v Union of India and Others
Supreme Court of India

5 January 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal(Civil)......./2000 (CC8935/2000)
The Order of the court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the petitioner states that the High Court has wrongly recorded that:
"no argument with respect of non-fulfilment of the requirements of Rule 17 (supra) was urged"
2.  Learned counsel states that he has ascertained this from Mr.Wazir, who appeared for the petitioner before the High Court. In the circumstances, learned counsel prays for leave to withdraw this special leave petition so as to enable the petitioner to move the High Court in review.The special leave petition is dismissed as withdrawn.
Petition dismissed.
