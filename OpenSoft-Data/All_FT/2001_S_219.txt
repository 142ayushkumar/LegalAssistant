Maniya Narendra v Shakuntala Devi and Others
Supreme Court of India

20 July 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.10936/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We are not inclined to interfere in the matter. The special leave petition is dismissed.
2.  Learned counsel for the petitioner then stated that in case the petitioner is required to vacate the premises in question immediately, he shall be put to great hardship and for that reason the petitioner may be granted some time to vacate the premises. Learned counsel for the respondents has no objection. Otherwise also we think that the request is reasonable.
3.  We, accordingly, direct that the petitioner shall not be dispossessed from the premises till 30th November, 2001 subject to filing a usual undertaking in this Court within four weeks' from today, failing which this order shall automatically stand vacated.
Petition dismissed.
