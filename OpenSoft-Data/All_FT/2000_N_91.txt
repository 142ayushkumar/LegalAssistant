Ravinder Pal Singh v Nazar Singh
Supreme Court of India

27 November 2000
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.11141/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Having heard Mr. Ranjit Kumar, the learned counsel for the petitioner and Mr. Gopal Subramanium, the learned senior counsel for the respondent, we are not inclined to interfere with the conclusion of the High Court. The Special Leave Petition accordingly stands dismissed.
2.  The petitioner, however, prayed for some time to deliver the vacant possession of the premises to which Mr. Subramanium strongly objects.
3. Having regard to the facts and circumstances, we direct that the petitioner shall deliver vacant possession of the premises in question by 31st of May, 2001, subject to the usual undertaking being given in this Court within four weeks from today.
Petition dismissed.
