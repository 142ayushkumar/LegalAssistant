Indian Smelting and Refining Company Limited v Commissioner of Income Tax
Supreme Court of India

28 November 2000
C.A. No. 2756 of 1998
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  We have heard learned counsel and seen the relevant documents. We are of the opinion that the view taken by the High Court, in the facts and circumstances of the case, is correct and no interference is called for.
The civil appeal is dismissed.
Appeal dismissed.
