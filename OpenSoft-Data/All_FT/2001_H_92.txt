Hem Chand and Others v Hari Kishan Rohtagi and Others
Supreme Court of India

28 November 2001
Appeal (Civil) 9116-17 of 1996
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  After going through the contentions raised in the review petition and the Annexures thereto, we do not find any justifiable ground to review our Order dated 25th September, 2001. The Review Petitions are, therefore dismissed.
Review dismissed.
