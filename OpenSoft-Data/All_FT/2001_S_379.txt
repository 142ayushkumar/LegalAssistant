Municipal Committee, Thanesar v Sona Rani
Supreme Court of India

12 February 2001
I.A. No.2 In C.A..No.6383/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We made an order on 10.11.2000 disposing of the matter and setting aside the order made by the High Court and it is brought to our notice that appeal is pending before the Additional District Judge, Kurukshetra.
2. It would be appropriate to modify the order to the extent that the direction to the High Court to register the same shall be deleted and the appeal shall be restored to the file of Additional District Judge, Kurukshetra who shall dispose it now in accordance with law.
I.A. No. 2 is allowed accordingly.
Application allowed.
