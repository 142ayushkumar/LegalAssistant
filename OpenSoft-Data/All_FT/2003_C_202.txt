Commissioner of Central Excise, Chennai v Twenty First Century Pharmaceutical P. Limited
Supreme Court of India

17 September 2003
C.A. No. 4102 of 2000 (Arising From Cegat's Final Order Reported In 2003 (58) Rlt 660 (Cegat-Che.)) With C.A. No. 4993 of 2003, Dt. 17 September 2003.
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The issue raised in these appeals has been decided in favour of the assessee by this Court while dismissing the appeal in the appeal in the matter of Sanat Products Ltd. v. Commissioner of Central Excise, Meerut 1999 Indlaw CEGAT 255.
2.  The order of this Court affirming the view of the Tribunal is reported in 2000 (121) ELT (A225) (reported in 2000 RLT F5 (SC)) in Commissioner of Central Excise v. Sanat Products Ltd.
The appeals are accordingly dismissed without any order as to cost.
Appeals dismissed.
