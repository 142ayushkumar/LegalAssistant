Land Acquisition Collector and Others v Hans Raj Gupta and Anr
Supreme Court of India

29 October 2001
C.A. No.4700/1997
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel prays for and is granted six weeks' time to serve the unserved respondent.
2. In the event, the steps are not taken, this appeal shall stand dismissed against respondent No.2 without reference to the Court.
Appeal dismissed.
