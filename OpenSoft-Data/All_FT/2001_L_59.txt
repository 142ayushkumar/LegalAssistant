State of Gujarat and Others v Laxmiben Jayantilal Sikligar
Supreme Court of India

23 April 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.19560/2000
The order of the Court was as follows:
Delay condoned.
Leave granted.
Printing of paperbooks is dispensed with.
Permission to file additional documents is granted.
1.  The stay order passed on 24.11.2000 is modified to the effect that further proceedings in the Execution application No.26 of 1998 will remain stayed subject to the condition that the appellant deposits the entire decretal amount in the executing court within four weeks failing which the stay order shall stand vacated without further reference of the Court.
2. The decretal amount on being deposited will be invested with a nationalised bank subject to further orders of this court.
Order accordingly.
