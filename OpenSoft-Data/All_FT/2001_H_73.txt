Harpal Singh v Delhi Administraion and Others
Supreme Court of India

19 February 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Cr.) No. 617/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We are not inclined to quash the F.I.R. as it is open to the investigating officer to come to his own conclusion regarding the allegnations made against the petitioner in the said F.I.R. All the same, on the fact situation we are inclined to grant one relief to this petitioner.
2. He shall be released on bail in case he is arrested on his executing a bond with two solvent sureties to the satisfaction of the arresting officer. With these observations this Special Leave Petition is disposed of.
Petition disposed of.
