N.Nagalaxmi Devi v Distt.Judge, Distt.Ct., Karnool, A.P. and Others
Supreme Court of India

7 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.18356/1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In response to the notification dated 1.10.1996, the petitioners submitted applications for appointment to the Class III posts within the jurisdiction of the District Judge, Karnool.
2. After conducting written test and interview by the District Judge, a panel was prepared. The candidates who have touched the roster point were given appointment on regular basis and the candidates who have not touched the roster point were given appointment on ad hoc basis and were accommodated in the existing vacancies till the said vacancies are notified.
3.  We do not find any error in the judgment of the High Court.
The S.L.Ps are, accordingly, dismissed.
Petitions dismissed.
