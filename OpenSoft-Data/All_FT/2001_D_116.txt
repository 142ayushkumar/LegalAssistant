Messrs Balarama Rice and Flour Mills Cont.Co v Deputy Commnr. of Commercial Taxes and Anr
Supreme Court of India

7 December 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.20909/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In our opinion, the High Court was right in not entertaining the writ petition against a show cause notice, especially, when it is open to the petitioner to agitate all the contentions before the authority concerned.
2.  Short-circuiting the procedure under the Act should oridinarily not be permitted. While dismissing this Special Leave Petition, four weeks' time is given to the petitioner to file reply to the show cause notice.
3. Assessing authority will consider all the contentions which are raised by the petitioner. Copy of the order be sent to the Registrar, High Court.
Petition dismissed.
