Nicco Corporation Limited v Prysmian Cavie Sistemi Energia S. R. L. and
Supreme Court of India

15 September 2009
SLP (CIVIL) NO. 22053 OF 2009
(Non-Reportable)
The Order of the Court was as follows :
Issue notice.
1.  Counter affidavit may be filed by the respondents within three weeks. Rejoinder, if any, within two weeks thereafter.
2.  List the matter for final disposal on a non-miscellaneous day in the last week of October, 2009.
3.  In the meanwhile, operation of the impugned order dated July 29, 2009 shall remain stayed.
Order accordingly
