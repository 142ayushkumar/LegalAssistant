Commissioner of Income Tax v Abhijit Iron Processors Private Limited
Supreme Court of India

13 February 2000
C.A. No. 380 of 2000
The order of the court was as follows:
Leave granted.
1.  After hearing learned counsel for the parties we direct the Tribunal to state the case and refer the following question of law to the High Court.
"Whether on the facts and in the circumstances of the case, the Tribunal was justified in law upholding the order of the CIT(A) in directing the AO to include interest income in the gross total income while computing the deduction under s. 80-I of the IT Act, 1961?"
The appeal is allowed in the aforesaid terms. No costs.
Appeal allowed.
