Abdul Wahab v Union of India and Others
Supreme Court of India

25 July 2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The petitioner got selected under the All India Quota for Post Graduate Medical Degree and since he was insisted upon to undergo one year Pre-Post Graduate Rural Service, has under gone that period also.
2. In the circumstances, this petition has become infructuous and stands dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
