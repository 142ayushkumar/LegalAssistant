Collector of Cent.Excise, Delhi v Messrs Pratap Steel Limited
Supreme Court of India

18 January 2001
C.A.No.1064/1999 (Along with office report) C.A.No.7468/1999 (Along with office report) C.A.No.53/2001 (Along with appln. for ex-Parte stay and office report)
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We have heard learned counsel. On the facts of these cases, we think that the Tribunal was right in following the judgment of this Court in Philips India Ltd. vs. Collector of Central Excise, Pune.
The civil appeals are dismissed. No order as to costs.
Appeals dismissed.
