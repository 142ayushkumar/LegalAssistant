Maharashtra General Kamgar Union v Solid Containers Limited Krantikari Kamgar Union
Supreme Court of India

17 August 2000
C.A. Jurisdiction I.A. No. 1 In C.A. No. 1824 of 1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Having heard learned senior counsel for the applicant as well as learned counsel for the respondent we clarify our order of March 30,2000 by observing that once the orders of the learned single Judge and the Division Bench of the High Court were set aside the reasoning given in the said judgments also would not survive.
We keep the question of law open.
Order accordingly.
