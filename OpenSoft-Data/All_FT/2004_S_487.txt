Krishna Kumar Singh and others v State of Bihar and another
Supreme Court of India

23 November 2004
Civil Appeal No. 5875 of 1994 with Nos. 3533-95 of 1995, 5876-90 of 1994, 580 of 1995 and IA No. 3
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. During the course of hearing, Mr P.P. Rao, learned Senior Counsel for the appellants placed reliance on the decisions of this Court in State of Orissa v. Bhupendra Kumar Bose1961 Indlaw SC 395 and T. Venkata Reddy v. State of A.P.  1985 Indlaw SC 490 The learned Solicitor General brought to the notice of this Court the decision in State of Punjab v. Sat Pal Dang1968 Indlaw SC 429.
All these decisions are Constitution Bench decisions. Mr Rakesh Dwivedi, learned Senior Counsel for the respondent State of Bihar, however, relied on a nine-Judge Bench decision of this Court in S.R. Bommai v. Union of India 1994 Indlaw SC 2206 and in particular thereof.
2. We are of the opinion that these matters call for hearing by a seven- Judge Bench of this Court. Be listed accordingly.
Order accordingly
