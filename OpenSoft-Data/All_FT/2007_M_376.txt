Municipal Council, Panipat v Randhir
Supreme Court of India

30 August 2007
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No(s). 9286/2006
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
Leave granted.
1.  Printing dispensed with. The appeal shall be heard on the SLP paperbook. Parties may file additional documents from the record, if any, within six weeks. Original record shall be requisitioned.
2.  Learned counsel for the appellant submits that the respondent has already been reinstated. Since the respondent has already been reinstated, there is no question of staying the operation of the impugned judgment and order.
However, the direction to pay back wages is stayed.
Order accordingly
