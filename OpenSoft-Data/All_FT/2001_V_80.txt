Vijay Kumar Vijay Sah v State of Bihar
Supreme Court of India

2 November 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal(Cr.)......../2001
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  Heard Shri A.K. Ganguli learned senior counsel for the petitioner.
We are not inclined to interfere with the order of the High Court refusing the petitioner's prayer for anticipatory bail.
2. If the petitioner surrenders and files an application for regular bail, the same will be considered on merits withing being influenced by the observations made by the High Court on merits of the case and the application for regular bail will be disposed of as expditiously as possible.
The special leave petition is dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
