State of Haryana and Anr v Messrs Deep Mayank and Associates and Others
Supreme Court of India

10 November 2000
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.11720/1999
The Order of the court was as follows:
Leave granted.
Heard learned counsel for the parties.
We do not find any merit in the present appeal.
1.  Accordingly, the appeal is dismissed. However, we have no hesitation to say in case appellant approaches High Court, the High Court will consider it sympathetically for early hearing.
Appeal dismissed.
