Dsm Idemitsu Company Limited v Designated Authority, Ministry of Commerce
Supreme Court of India

8 January 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.12049/2000
The order of the court was as follows:
1.  On the application of learned counsel for the petitioner, the special leave petition is dismissed as withdrawn.
2.  The petitioner shall be at liberty to challenge the notification that has been issued by the Government on 9th May, 2000. In its reply to such challenge, it would be open to the Government to rely upon the order of the Tribunal upon which the notification is based.
3. If this is done, it would be open to the petitioners to challenge in those proceedings the correctness of such order.
Petition dismissed.
