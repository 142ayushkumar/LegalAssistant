Messrs Reliance Industries Limited v Murli Krishna of Hyderabad and Others
Supreme Court of India

3 December 1986
C.A. No. 4386 of 1986 (Arising out of Petition for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No. 14937 of 1986).
The Order of the Court was as follows:
Special leave granted.
1.  Having regard to the particular facts and circumstances of this case placed before us, we vacate the ex parte interim injunction granted by the High Court and communicated to the Controller of Capital Issues and the Reliance Industries Limited, Bombay, by the High Court by its telegram dated December 2, 1986, and direct the High Court to dispose of the application for interim injunction after notice to the Controller of Capital Issues and after permitting a period of four days to the respondents before it to file their respective affidavits.
2.  The appeal is disposed of accordingly and there will be no order as to costs.
Appeal disposed of.
