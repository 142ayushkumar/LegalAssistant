Commissioner of Customs, Kandla v Messrs Nirma Limited
Supreme Court of India

3 November 2000
C.A. Nos.3566-3567/2000
The Order of the Court was as follows:
The respondent has been served but has not entered appearance.
1.  The notice on the appeals stated that they might be disposed of at this stage, having regard to the judgment of this court in Union of India vs. Solar Pesticide Pvt. Ltd.
The appeals being so covered, they are allowed and the orders under appeal are set aside. The order of the Assistant Commissioner, Kandla, is restored.
No order as to costs.
Appeals allowed.
