National Insurance Company Ltd v Bhukya Tara and Others
Supreme Court of India

8  May  2008
CIVIL APPEAL NO. 7805 OF 2002 WITH C.A.NO.7804 OF 2002 & C.A. NO.8332 OF 2002
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1. These appeals have been filed by the insurance company against the award passed by the tribunal and affirmed by the High Court. It is not disputed that the deceased was travelling in a goods vehicle.
Therefore, the cases in hand are squarely covered by the decision of three-Judge Bench of this Court rendered in New India Assurance Co. Ltd. Vs Asha Rani & Ors., (2003) 2 SCC 223 2002 Indlaw SC 1460.
2. The appeals are allowed. The orders of the tribunal and of the High Court are set aside. However, if any compensation has actually been paid to the respondent as of today, the same shall not be recovered. We further clarify that if any amount deposited before the tribunal is still not paid to the claimant, the same shall be refunded to the appellant.
Appeal Allowed
