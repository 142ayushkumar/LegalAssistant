Ram Narayan Gupta (D) By Lrs v Babulal and Others
Supreme Court of India

28 September 2001
I.A. No. 3 In Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.3771/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the petitioners states that the petitioners, who are in occupation of the premises, have filed necessary undertaking before this Court for vacating the premises on or before 30th September, 2001. However, because of the unavoidable circumstances it has become difficult for the petitioners to vacate the premises.
2. Therefore, time to vacate the premises is extended upto 31st January, 2002 on a usual undertaking to be filed within 15 days from today with a specific understanding that the petitioners would not seek any further extension of time.
Order accordingly.
