Chung Chiang Yi v Janakidevi Anantlal Gupta and Others
Supreme Court of India

6 August 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.12429/2001
The order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Having heard Mr.RF Nariman, the learned senior counsel for the petitioner, in our view, no case is made out to interfere with the order under challenge.
2. However, having regard to the fact that the petitioner is in profession of a dentist for quite a long time, we grant time to him upto the end of December, 2001 to vacate and hand over the peaceful possession of the suit premises to the respondents on furnishing a usual undertaking to this Court within four weeks from today.
The S.L.P. is accordingly dismissed.
Petition Dismissed.
