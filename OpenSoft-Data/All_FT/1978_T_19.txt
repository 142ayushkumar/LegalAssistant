Employers In Relation To Messrs Bharat Heavy v Their Workmen
Supreme Court of India

11 December 1978
C.A. No. 1124 of 1978
The Order of the Court was as follows :
1.  It is represented by counsel for the appellant that the subject matter of the appeal has been settled, and so he seeks leave to withdraw this appeal. He is allowed to do so. The appeal is dismissed as wihtdrawn with no. order as to costs.
Appeal dismissed.
