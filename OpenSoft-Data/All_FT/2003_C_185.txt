Commissioner of Agricultural Income Tax v P. V. Balakrishnan Nair and Others
Supreme Court of India

4 September 2003
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  The same issue appears to have been decided against the Revenue and in favour of the assessee in Progressive Financers v. CIT 1997 Indlaw SC 32711997 Indlaw SC 3271, in connection with section 184(2) of the Income-tax Act, 1961, which is in pari materia with section 27(2) of the Kerala Agricultural Income-tax Act, 1950. We accordingly dismiss these appeals.
Appeals dismissed.
