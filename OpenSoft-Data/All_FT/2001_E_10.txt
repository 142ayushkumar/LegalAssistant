Eadi Shankaraiah v State of Andhra Pradesh
Supreme Court of India

8 October 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.16336-16337/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  Learned counsel for the petitioner contended that a specific plea was raised before the Special Court under the Andhra Pradesh Land Grabbing Act that petitioner has perfected his title by adverse possession and that Special Court has no jurisdiction to enter upon a finding on such a plea.
2. If that is a stand of the petitioner his remedy is to file a suit for declaration of his title and establish that the Special Court has no jurisdiction to decide the question of adverse possession and that he has perfected his title by adverse possession. Without prejudice to such right, if any, of the petitioner we dispose of this Special Leave Petition.
Petition disposed of.
