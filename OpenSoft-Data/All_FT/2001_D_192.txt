Director of Income Tax v Gopal Srinivasan Trust
Supreme Court of India

17 October 2001
C.A. No. 4982 of 1999
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1. We have heard learned counsel for the appellant.
2. On the record of the case, no interference is called for.
3. The civil appeal is dismissed. No order as to costs.
Appeal dismissed.
