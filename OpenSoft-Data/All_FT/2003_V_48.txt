Commissioner of Central Excise, Bhopal v Vindhachal Process Corporation
Supreme Court of India

4 September 2003
C.A. No. 6215 of 2000 (Arising From Cegat's Final Order Reported In 2000 (39) Rlt 1032 (Cegat)) With C.A. No. 3010 of 1999, Dt. 4 September 2003.
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  In both these appeals the Tribunal had relied upon the decision reported in 1993 Indlaw CEGAT 519. That decision was impugned by way of an appeal by the Revenue authorities. This appeal has Since been rejected by this Court in Collector of Central Excise, New Delhi v. Universal Electrical Industries reported in 2003 Indlaw SC 265 : 2003 Indlaw SC 265.
2. In this view of the matter, the appeals are dismissed. No order as to costs.
Appeal dismissed.
