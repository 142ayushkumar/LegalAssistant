Commissioner of Income Tax v Foramer France
Supreme Court of India

16 January 2003
C.A. Nos. 5269 to 5271 of 2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  We have heard the learned counsel for the parties and considered the facts of the case. We see no reason to interfere with the decision of the High Court.
Accordingly, the civil appeals are dismissed with costs.
Appeals dismissed.
