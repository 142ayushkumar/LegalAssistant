Dharamchand v Suganchand Jain and Others
Supreme Court of India

27 July 2001
Petition(s) for Special Leave to Appeal (Civil) No.10670/2001
The Order of the Court was as follows:
1.  After hearing the learned counsel for the petitioner at length we do not find any valid ground to interfere with the order under challenge.
2. However, having regard to the facts and circumstnaces of the case we consider it just and appropriate to grant time to the petitioner to vacate and hand over the peaceful vacant possession of the premises in question to the respondent/landlord on or before 30th of November, 2001. This is subject to the petitioner's filing a usual undertaking within four weeks from today. The S.L.P. is accordingly dismissed.
Petition dismissed.
