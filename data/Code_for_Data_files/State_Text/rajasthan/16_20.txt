Rajasthan Private Universities' Laws (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan An Act to amend the Private Universities' Laws. Be enacted by the Rajasthan State Legislature in the Sixty third Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Rajasthan Private Universities' Laws (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan (1) This Act may be called the Rajasthan Private Universities' Laws (Amendment) Act, 2012. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Rajasthan Private Universities' Laws (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan In this Act, unless the context otherwise requires,- (a) "Private Universities Laws" means the Private Universities' Acts specified in the Schedule; and (b) "Schedule" means the Schedule appended to this Act.
Rajasthan Private Universities' Laws (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 3--> State(s): Rajasthan In clause (y) of Section 5 of the Private Universities' Laws, the existing expression "or without" shall be deleted.
