Rajasthan Appropriation (No. 3) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan An Act to authorize the appropriation of certation further sums from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State for the services of the financial year 2006-2007 ended on the 31st March, 2007 in excess of the amount granted for those services and for that Year. Be it enacted by the Rajasthan State Legislature in the Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Rajasthan Appropriation (No. 3) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan This Act may be called the Rajasthan Appropriation (No. 3) Act, 2011.
Rajasthan Appropriation (No. 3) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan Certain further sums not exceeding those specified in Column 2 of the Schedule amounting in the aggregate to the sums of Rs. 42,36,656/-(Rupees Forty two lacs thirty six thousand six hundred fifty six) only paid from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State fotwards defraying the several charges which come in course of payment during the year ended on the 31st day of March, 2007 in respect of the services specified in Column 1 of the Schedule are hereby authorized to be appropriated for such services in relation to the year ended on the 31st March, 2007 in excess of the amount granted for those services and for the said year.
Rajasthan Appropriation (No. 3) Act, 2011_Section Sch.--> State(s): Rajasthan Schedule Principal Secretary to the Government.
