Rajasthan Appropration (No. 3) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan An Act to authorise the appropriation of certation further sums from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State for the services of the financial year 2009-2010 ended on the 31st March, 2010 in excess of the amount granted for those services and for that year. Be it enacted by the Rajasthan State Legislature in the Sixty-fourth year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Rajasthan Appropration (No. 3) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan This Act may be called the Rajasthan Appropration (No. 3) Act, 2013.
Rajasthan Appropration (No. 3) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan Certain further sums not exceeding those specified in Column 2 of the Schedule amounting in the aggregate to the sums of Rs. 35,72,41,069/-(Rupees Thirty five crore seventy two lacs fourty one thousand sixty nine) only paid from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State towards defraying the several charges which come in course of payment during the year ended on the 31st day of March, 2010 in respect of the services specified in Column 1 of the Schedule are hereby authorized to be appropriated for such services in relation to the year ended on the 31st March, 2010 in excess of the amount granted for those services and for the said year.
Rajasthan Appropration (No. 3) Act, 2013_Section Sch.--> State(s): Rajasthan Schedule Principal Secretary to the Government.
