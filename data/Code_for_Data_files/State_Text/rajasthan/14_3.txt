National Law University, Jodhpur (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan An Act to amend the National Law University, Jodhpur Act, 1999. Be enacted by the Rajasthan State Legislature in the Sixty third Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
National Law University, Jodhpur (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan (1) This Act may be called the National Law University, Jodhpur (Amendment) Act, 2012. (2) It shall come into force at once.
National Law University, Jodhpur (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan For the existing sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the National Law University, Jodhpur Act, 1999 (Act No. 22 of 1999), the following shall be substituted, namely:- "(1) The Chief Justice of India or his nominee who is, for the time being, a Judge of the Supreme Court of India, shall be the visitor of the University.".
