Rajasthan Stamp (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan An Act further to amend the Rajasthan Stamp Act, 1998. Be it enacted by the Rajasthan State Legislature in the Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Rajasthan Stamp (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan (1) This Act may be called the Rajasthan Stamp (Amendment) Act, 2011. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Rajasthan Stamp (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan In proviso of sub-section (1) of Section 65 of the Rajasthan Stamp Act, 1998 (Act No. 14 of 1999), hereinafter referred to as the principal Act, for the existing expression "fifty percent", the expression "twenty five percent" shall be substituted.
Rajasthan Stamp (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 3--> State(s): Rajasthan In proviso of Section 67 of the principal Act, for the existing expression "fifty percent", the expression "twenty five percent" shall be substituted.
