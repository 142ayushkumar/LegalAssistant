Code of Civil Procedure (Rajasthan Amendment) Act, 1958_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan Be it enacted by the Rajasthan State Legislature in the Ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Code of Civil Procedure (Rajasthan Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan This Act may be called the Code of Civil Procedure (Rajasthan Amendment) Act, 1958.1 Received the assent of the President on the 12th day of April, 1958.
Code of Civil Procedure (Rajasthan Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan In clause (b) of the proviso to sub-sec. (1) of Sec. 60 of the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 (Central Act V of 1908) in its application to the State of Rajasthan after the word, "agriculturist" the words "his milch cattle and those likely to calve within two years" shall be inserted.
