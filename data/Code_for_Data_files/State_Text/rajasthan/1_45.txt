Rajasthan Appropriation (No. 1) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan An Act to authorise payment and appropriation of certain furtuer sums from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State for the services of the financial year 2010-2011. Be it enacted by the Rajasthan State Legislature in the Sixty-Second Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Rajasthan Appropriation (No. 1) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan This Act may be called the Rajasthan Appropriation (No. 1) Act, 2011.
Rajasthan Appropriation (No. 1) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan From and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State there may be paid and applied the sums not exceeding those specified in Column 2 of the Schedule and amounting in the aggregate to the sums of Rs. 48,70,28,87,000/- (Ruppees Four thousand eight hundred seventy crore twenty eight lacs and eighty seven thousand) only towards defraying the several charges which will come in course of payment during the financial year 2010-2011 in respect of the services specified in Column 1 of the Schedule.
Rajasthan Appropriation (No. 1) Act, 2011_Section 3--> State(s): Rajasthan The sums authorised to be paid and applied from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State by this Act shall be appropriated for the services and purposes expressed in the Schedule in relation to the said year.
Rajasthan Appropriation (No. 1) Act, 2011_Section Sch.--> State(s): Rajasthan Schedule Principal Secretary to the Government.
