Rajasthan Appropration (No. 2) Act, 2012_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan An Act to authorize the appropriation of certation further sums from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State for the services of the financial year 2011-2012. Be it enacted by the Rajasthan State Legislature in the Sixty-third year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Rajasthan Appropration (No. 2) Act, 2012_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan This Act may be called the Rajasthan Appropration (No. 2) Act, 2012.
Rajasthan Appropration (No. 2) Act, 2012_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan From and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State there may be paid and applied the sums not exceeding those specified in Column 2 of the Schedule and amounting in the aggregate to the sums of Rs. 67,95,20,95,000/- (Rupees Six thousand seven hundred ninety five crore twenty lacs and ninty five thousand) only towards defraying the several charges which will come in course of payment durng the financial year 2011-2012 in respect of the services specified in Column 1 of the Schedule.
Rajasthan Appropration (No. 2) Act, 2012_Section 3--> State(s): Rajasthan The sums authorized to be paid and applied from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State by this Act shall be appropriated for the services and purposes expressed in the Schedule in relation to the said year.
