Rajasthan Appropration (No. 4) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan An Act to authorise payment and appropriation of certain further sums from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State for the services of the financial year 2013-2014. Be it enacted by the Rajasthan State Legislature in the Sixty-fourth year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Rajasthan Appropration (No. 4) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan This Act may be called the Rajasthan Appropration (No. 4) Act, 2013.
Rajasthan Appropration (No. 4) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan From and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State there may be paid and applied the sums not exceeding those specified in Column 2 of the Schedule and amounting in the aggregate to the sums of Rs. 39,19,86,19,000/-(Rupees three thousand nine hundred nineteen crore eighty six lacs and nineteen thousand) only towards defraying the several charges which come in course of payment during the financial year 2013-2014 in respect of the services specified in Column 1 of the Schedule.
Rajasthan Appropration (No. 4) Act, 2013_Section Sch.--> State(s): Rajasthan Schedule Principal Secretary to the Government.
