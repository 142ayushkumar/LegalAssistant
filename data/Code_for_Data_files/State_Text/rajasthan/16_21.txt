Rajasthan Private Universities' Laws (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan An Act further to amend the Private Universities Laws. Be it enacted by the Rajasthan State Legislature in the Sixty-fourth Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Rajasthan Private Universities' Laws (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan (1) This Act may be called the Rajasthan Private Universities Laws (Amendment) Act, 2013.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on and from 12th November, 2012. Received the assent of the Governor on 23rd day of March, 2013.
Rajasthan Private Universities' Laws (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan In this Act, unless the context otherwise requires,- (a) Private University Law" means a Private University Act specified in the Schedule; and (b) "Schedule" means the Schedule to this Act.
Rajasthan Private Universities' Laws (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 3--> State(s): Rajasthan In sub-section (1) of section 12 of each of the Private Universities Laws, the existing expression "and shall not be eligible for re-appointment" shall be deleted.
Rajasthan Private Universities' Laws (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 4--> State(s): Rajasthan (l) The Rajasthan Private Universities Laws (Amendment) Ordinance, 2012 (Ordinance No. 12 of 2012) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, all things done, actions taken or orders made under the principal Act as mentioned by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done, taken or made under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
