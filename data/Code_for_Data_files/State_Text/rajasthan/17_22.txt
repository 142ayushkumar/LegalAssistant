Rajasthan Review and Validation of Rent-Rates (Revival) Repeal Act, 1957_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan An Act to repeal the Rajasthan Review and Validation of Rent-Rates (Revival) Act 1956. Be it enacted by the Rajasthan State Legislature in the Eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Rajasthan Review and Validation of Rent-Rates (Revival) Repeal Act, 1957_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan This Act may be called the Rajasthan Review and Validation of Rent-Rates (Revival) Repeal Act, 19571 . Received the assent of the Governor on the 30th day of November 1957
Rajasthan Review and Validation of Rent-Rates (Revival) Repeal Act, 1957_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan The Rajasthan Review and Validation of Rent-Rates (Revival) Act, 1956 is hereby repealed.
