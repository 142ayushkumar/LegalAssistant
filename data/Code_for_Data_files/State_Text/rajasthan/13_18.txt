Rajasthan Ministers' Salaries (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan An Act further to amend the Rajasthan Ministers' Salaries Act, 1956. Be it enacted by the Rajasthan State Legislature in the Sixty-first Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Rajasthan Ministers' Salaries (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan (1) This Act may be called the Rajasthan Ministers' Salaries (Amendment) Act, 2010. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Rajasthan Ministers' Salaries (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan In section 3 of the Rajasthan Ministers' Salaries Act, 1956 (Act No. 43 of 1956), hereinafter referred to as the principal Act,- (i) for the existing expression "the date of commencement of the Rajasthan Ministers' Salaries (Second Amendment) Act, 2005 (Act No. 12 of 2005)", the expression "1st April, 2010" shall be substituted; (ii) in clause (a), for the existing expression "twenty thousand rupees", the expression "twenty five thousand rupees" shall be substituted; (iii) in clause (b), for the existing expression "seventeen thousand rupees", the expression "twenty two thousand rupees" shall be substituted; and (iv) in clause (c), for the existing expression "fifteen thousand rupees", the expression "twenty thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Rajasthan Ministers' Salaries (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 3--> State(s): Rajasthan In section 4 of the principal Act,- (i) in sub-section (1),- (a) for the existing expression "the date of commencement of the Rajasthan Ministers' Salaries (Amendment) Act, 2006 (Act No. 9 of 2006)", the expression "1st April, 2010" shall be substituted; and (b) for the existing expression "twenty thousand rupees", the expression "twenty five thousand rupees" shall be substituted; (ii) in sub-section (2),- (a) for the existing expression "the date of commencement of the Rajasthan Ministers' Salaries (Amendment) Act, 2006 (Act No. 9 of 2006)", the expression "1st April, 2010" shall be substituted; and (b) for the existing expression "fourteen thousand rupees", the expression "twenty thousand rupees" shall be substituted; (iii) in sub-section (3),- (a) for the existing expression "the date of commencement of the Rajasthan Ministers' Salaries (Amendment) Act, 2006 (Act No. 9 of 2006)", the expression "1st April, 2010" shall be substituted; and (b) for the existing expression "fourteen thousand rupees", the expression "twenty thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Rajasthan Ministers' Salaries (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 4--> State(s): Rajasthan In section 7B of the principal Act,- (i) in sub-section (1),- (a) for the existing expression "the date of commencement of the Rajasthan Ministers' Salaries (Second Amendment) Act, 2005 (Act No. 12 of 2005)", the expression "1st April, 2010" shall be substituted; and (b) for the existing expression "fifteen thousand rupees", the expression "twenty thousand rupees" shall be substituted; (ii) in sub-section (3),- (a) for the existing expression "the date of commencement of the Rajasthan Ministers' Salaries (Second Amendment) Act, 2005 (Act No. 12 of 2005)", the expression "1st April, 2010" shall be substituted; and (b) for the existing expression "ten thousand rupees", the expression "twenty thousand rupees" shall be substituted; Principal Secretary to the Government.
