University of Rajasthan (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan An Act further to amend the University of Rajasthan Act, 1946. Be it enacted by the Rajasthan State legislative in the Twenty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
University of Rajasthan (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan This Act may be called the University of Rajasthan (Amendment) Act, 1974.1 Received the assent of the Governor on the 11th day of September, 1974.
University of Rajasthan (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan In sub-section (1) of section 18 of the University of Rajasthan Act, 1946 hereinafter referred to as the principal Act,- (i) in sub division I, after the existing clause (xiii) the following new clause shall be inserted, namely:- (xiii-A) The President of the Central Union (Students) of the University,"; and (ii) in sub-division III, sub-clause (b) of clause (xxv) shall be omitted.
University of Rajasthan (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 3--> State(s): Rajasthan In section 21 of the principal Act,- (i) in sub-section (1) after clause (x), the following new clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(xi) one person elected by the Senate from amongst the students of the University teaching departments and constituent colleges who are elected as other members of the Senate under clause (xxviii) of sub-division III of sub-section (1) of section 18 and the ex-officio member of the Senate under clause (xiii-A) of sub- division I of sub-section (1) of the said section; (xii) one person elected by the Senate from amongst the students of the affiliated colleges who are elected as other members of the Senate under clause (xxviii) of sub-division III of sub- section (1) of section 18."; (ii) after sub-section (2), the following new sub-section shall be inserted, namely:- "(3) Persons who are members under clauses (xi) and (xii) of subsection (1) above, shall not be associated with the appointment of examiners or with the committees of selection for university employees, the finance committee or with such other committee as the State Government may, by notification in the official Gazette, specify."
University of Rajasthan (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 4--> State(s): Rajasthan In the principal Act, in section 22, after the existing clause (f), the following new clause shall be inserted, namely: "(ff) On the security of the property of the University or on any other security and subject to the prior concurrence of the State Government to borrow money or so raise public loans for the purposes of the University;".
University of Rajasthan (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 5--> State(s): Rajasthan (1) 1 The University of Rajasthan (Amendment) Ordinance, 1974 and the University of Rajasthan (Second Amendment) Ordinance, 1974 2 made and promulgated by the Governor on 20th day of April, 1974 and 25th day of July, 1974 respectively are hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinances so repealed shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act as amended by this Act. Published in Rajasthan Gazette Exty., Pt. IV-B, dated 20-4-1974 Published in Rajasthan Gazette Exty., Pt. IV-B, dated 26-7-1974
