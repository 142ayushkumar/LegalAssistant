Rajasthan Appropration Vote on Account (No. 2) Act, 2014_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan An Act to authorise payment and appropriation of certation further sums from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State for the services of the financial year 2012-2013. Be it enacted by the Rajasthan State Legislature in the Sixty-fifth year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Rajasthan Appropration Vote on Account (No. 2) Act, 2014_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan This Act may be called the Rajasthan Appropration Vote on Account (No. 2) Act, 2014.
Rajasthan Appropration Vote on Account (No. 2) Act, 2014_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan From and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State there may be paid and applied the sums not exceeding those specified in Column 2 of the Schedule and amounting in the aggregate to the sums of Rs. 4,28,86,77,67,000 (Rupees Forty two thousand eight hundred eighty six crore seventy seven lacs and sixty seven thousand) only towards defraying the several charges which will come in course of payment during the financial year 2014-2015 in respect of the services specified in Column 1 of the Schedule.
Rajasthan Appropration Vote on Account (No. 2) Act, 2014_Section 3--> State(s): Rajasthan The sums authorized to be paid and applied from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State by this Act shall be appropriated for the services and purposes expressed in the Schedule in relation to the said year.
