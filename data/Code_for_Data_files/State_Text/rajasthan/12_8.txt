Rajasthan Land Revenue (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1966_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan An Act further to amend the Rajasthan Land Revenue Act, 1956 , and to validate the establishment and functioning of the Board of Revenue for Rajasthan. Be it enacted by the Rajasthan State Legislature in the Seventeenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Rajasthan Land Revenue (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1966_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan This Act may be called the Rajasthan Land Revenue (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1966.
Rajasthan Land Revenue (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1966_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan Amendments contained in section 2 has been carried out in the principal Act.
Rajasthan Land Revenue (Amendment and Validation) Act, 1966_Section 3--> State(s): Rajasthan Notwithstanding anything contained in section 4 of the principal Act prior to its amendment by this Act, and notwithstanding any judgment, decision, decree or order of any court, civil or revenue, and notwithstanding any omission or defect of form or procedure, all persons who were, from time to time, appointed as Chairman or Members of the Board of Revenue for Rajasthan after the coming into force of the principal Act shall be deemed to have been legally and properly appointed, and notwithstanding the absence of any order or notification under sub-section (1), or sub-section (2), or sub-section (3) of section 4 of the principal Act, and notwithstanding the absence of any rules made under sub-section (4) of the said section, the Board shall be, and shall be deemed always to have been, legally and properly constituted and to have legally functioned as such.
