Rajasthan Appropration (No. 1) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan An Act to authorise payment and appropriation of certation further sums from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State for the services of the financial year 2012-2013. Be it enacted by the Rajasthan State Legislature in the Sixty-fourth year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Rajasthan Appropration (No. 1) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan This Act may be called the Rajasthan Appropration (No. 1) Act, 2013.
Rajasthan Appropration (No. 1) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan From and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State there may be paid and applied the sums not exceeding those specified in Column 2 of the Schedule and amounting in the aggregate to the sums of Rs. 1,04,21,17,60,000 (Rupees Ten thousand four hundred twenty one crore seventeen lacs and sixty thousand) only towards defraying the several charges which will come in course of payment during the financial year 2012-2013 in respect of the services specified in Column 1 of the Schedule.
Rajasthan Appropration (No. 1) Act, 2013_Section 3--> State(s): Rajasthan The sums authorized to be paid and applied from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State by this Act shall be appropriated for the services and purposes expressed in the Schedule in relation to the said year.
