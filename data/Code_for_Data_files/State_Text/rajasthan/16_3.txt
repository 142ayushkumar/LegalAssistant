Rajasthan Panchayati Raj (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan An Act further to amend the Rajasthan Panchayati Raj Act, 1994. Be it enacted by the Rajasthan State Legislature in the Sixty-first Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Rajasthan Panchayati Raj (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan (1) This Act may be called the Rajasthan Panchayati Raj, (Amendment) Act, 2010. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Rajasthan Panchayati Raj (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan In Section 89 of Rajasthan Panchayati Raj Act, 1994 (Act No. 13 of 2004), hereinafter referred to as the principal Act,- (a) in sub-section(1), for the existing punctuation mark ":", appearing at the end, the punctuation mark "." shall be substituted; (b) the existing proviso of sub-section (1) shall be deleted; (c) in sub-section (6), the existing expression "on the posts specified in clause (i), (ii) and (iv) of sub-section (2) and on the posts encadred under sub-section (3)" shall be deleted; and (d) the existing sub-section (6-A) shall be deleted.
Rajasthan Panchayati Raj (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 3--> State(s): Rajasthan In clause (a) of sub-section (2) of Section 90 of the principal Act, for the existing expression "except the posts specified in clause (iii) and (v) of sub-section (2) of Section 89", the expression "except the posts specified in clause (v) of sub-section (2) of Section 89" shall be substituted.
