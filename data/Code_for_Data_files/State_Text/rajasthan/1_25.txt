Ajmer Shamlat Deh (Regulation) Repeal Act, 1959_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan An Act to repeal The Ajmer Shamlat Deh (Regulation) Act, 1956 Be it enacted by the Rajasthan State Legislature in the Tenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Ajmer Shamlat Deh (Regulation) Repeal Act, 1959_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan (1) This Act may be called the Ajmer Shamlat Deh (Regulation) Repeal Act, 1959.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the 16th day of May, 1959
Ajmer Shamlat Deh (Regulation) Repeal Act, 1959_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan The Ajmer Shamlat Deh (Regulation) Act, 1956 (Ajmer Act VI of 1956) is hereby repealed.
