Rajasthan Ministers' Salaries (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan An Act further to amend the Rajasthan Ministers' Salaries Act, 1956. Be it enacted by the Rajasthan State Legislature in the Sixty-third Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Rajasthan Ministers' Salaries (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan (1) This Act may be called the Rajasthan Ministers' Salaries (Amendment) Act, 2012. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Rajasthan Ministers' Salaries (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan In Section 3 of the Rajasthan Minister's Salaries Act, 1956 (Act No. 43 of 1956), hereinafter referred to as the principal Act,- (i) for the existing expression "1st April, 2010", the expression "1st April, 2012" shall be substituted; (ii) in clause (a), for the existing expression "twenty five thousand rupees", the expression "thirty five thousand rupees" shall be substituted; (iii) in clause (b), for the existing expression "twenty two thousand rupees", the expression "thirty thousand rupees" shall be substituted; (iv) in clause (c), for the existing expression "twenty thousand rupees", the expression "twenty seven thousand rupees" shall be substituted; and (v) in clause (d), for the existing expression "eight thousand two hundred fifty rupees", the expression "twenty four thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Rajasthan Ministers' Salaries (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 3--> State(s): Rajasthan In Section 4 of the principal Act,- (i) in sub-section (1),- (a) for the existing expression "1st April, 2010", the expression "1st April, 2012" shall be substituted; and (b) for the existing expression "twenty five thousand rupees", the expression "thirty five thousand rupees" shall be substituted; (ii) in sub-section (2),- (a) for the existing expression "1st April, 2010", the expression "1st April, 2012" shall be substituted; and (b) for the existing expression "twenty thousand rupees", the expression "thirty thousand rupees" shall be substituted; (iii) in sub-section (3),- (a) for the existing expression "1st April, 2010", the expression 1st April, 2012" shall be substituted; and (b) for the existing expression "twenty thousand rupees", the expression "thirty thousand rupees" shall be substituted; and (iv) in sub-section (4),- (a) for the existing expression "the date of commencement of the Rajasthan Ministers' Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1998 (Act No.4 of 1998)", the expression "1st April, 2012" shall be substituted; and (b) for the existing expression "two thousand five hundred rupees", the expression "twenty thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Rajasthan Ministers' Salaries (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 4--> State(s): Rajasthan In Section 7-B of the principal Act,- (i) in sub-section (1),- (a) for the existing expression "1st April, 2010", the expression "1st April, 2012" shall be substituted; and (b) for the existing expression "twenty thousand rupees", the expression "twenty seven thousand rupees" shall be substituted; and (ii) in sub-section (3),- (a) for the existing expression "1st April, 2010", the expression "1st April, 2012" shall be substituted; and (b) for the existing expression "twenty thousand rupees", the expression "thirty thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
