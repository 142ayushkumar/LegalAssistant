Rajasthan Appropriation (No. 4) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan An Act to authorize payment and appropriation of certain further sums from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State for the services of the financial year 2011-2012. Be it enacted by the Rajasthan State Legislature in the Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Rajasthan Appropriation (No. 4) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan This Act may be called the Rajasthan Appropriation (No. 4) Act, 2011.
Rajasthan Appropriation (No. 4) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan From and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State there may be paid and applied the sums not exceeding those specified in Column 2 of the Schedule and amounting in the aggregate to the sums of Rs. 2,24,47,01,000/- (Rupees two hundred twenty four crore forty seven lacs and one thousand) only towards defraying the several charges which will come in course of payment during the financial year 2011-2012 in respect of the services specified in Column 1 of the Sechedule.
Rajasthan Appropriation (No. 4) Act, 2011_Section 3--> State(s): Rajasthan The sums authorized to be paid and applied from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State by this Act Shall be appropriated for the Services and purposes expressed in the Schedule in relation to the said year.
