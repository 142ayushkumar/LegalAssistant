Rajasthan Scheduled Debtors (Moratorium on Debts) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 6 of the Rajasthan Scheduled Debtors (Moratorium on Debts) Act, 1976 (Rajasthan Act 15 of 1976) the State Government hereby makes the following rules, namely:-
Rajasthan Scheduled Debtors (Moratorium on Debts) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan (1) These rules may be called the Rajasthan Scheduled Debtors (Moratorium on Debts) rules, 19761 . (2) The rules shall come into force on the date of their publication in the Rajasthan Rajpatra.2 Notification No. F. 2(1) Rev./Gr. IV/76 dated June 30, 1976. Published in Rajasthan Gazette, Part IV-C, dated 8-7-1976
Rajasthan Scheduled Debtors (Moratorium on Debts) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan In these rules, unless there is anything repugnant in the subject or context,- (i) "Act" shall mean the Rajasthan Scheduled Debtors (Moratorium on Debts) Act, 1976; and (ii) "Form" means a form appended to these rules.
Rajasthan Scheduled Debtors (Moratorium on Debts) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Rajasthan Application for obtaining a certificate of being an agricultural labourer, a marginal farmer, a rural artisan or a small farmer shall be in Form I.
Rajasthan Scheduled Debtors (Moratorium on Debts) Act, 1976_Section 4--> State(s): Rajasthan The Tehsildar/Naib-Tehsildar/Inspector of Land Records shall get the particulars given in each application verified with the entries existing in the annual register and any other Revenue record and may after making such further enquiry as be deems fit, issue a certificate that the debtor is an agricultural labourer, a marginal farmer, a rural artisan or a small farmer in Form II.
