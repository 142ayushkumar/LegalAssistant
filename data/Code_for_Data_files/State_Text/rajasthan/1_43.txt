Rajasthan Appropration (No. 6) Act, 2012_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan An Act to authorize the appropriation of certain further sums from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State for the services of the financial year 2012-2013. Be it enacted by the Rajasthan State Legislature in the Sixty-third year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Rajasthan Appropration (No. 6) Act, 2012_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan This Act may be called the Rajasthan Appropration (No. 6) Act, 2012.
Rajasthan Appropration (No. 6) Act, 2012_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan Certain futher sums not exceeding those specified in Column 2 of the Schedule amounting in the aggregate to the sums of Rs. 3,52,64,16,000/- (Rupees Three hundred fifty two crore sixty four lacs and sixteen thousand) only towards defraying the several charges which will come in course of payment during the financial year 2012-2013 in respect of the services specified in Column 1 of the Schedule.
Rajasthan Appropration (No. 6) Act, 2012_Section 3--> State(s): Rajasthan The sums authorized to be paid and applied from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State by this Act shall be appropriated for the services and purposes expressed in the Schedule in relation to the said year.
Rajasthan Appropration (No. 6) Act, 2012_Section Sch.--> State(s): Rajasthan Schedule Principal Secretary to the Government.
