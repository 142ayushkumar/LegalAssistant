Code of Civil Procedure (Rajasthan Amendment) Act, 1982_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan An Act further to amend the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908, in its application to the State of Rajasthan. Be it enacted by the Rajasthan State Legislature in the Thirty-third Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Code of Civil Procedure (Rajasthan Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan (1) This Act may be called the Code of Civil Procedure (Rajasthan Amendment) Act, 1982.1 (2) It shall extend to the whole of the State of Rajasthan. (3) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the President on the 8th day of May, 1983
Code of Civil Procedure (Rajasthan Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan In Sec. 137 of the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 (Central Act V of 1908), in its application to the State of Rajasthan, for sub-sec. (3), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely:- "(3) Wherever this Code requires or allows anything other than the recording of evidence to be done in writing in any such court, such writing shall be in Hindi in Devnagri Script with the international form of Indian numerals: Provided that the court may in its discretion accept such writing in English on the undertaking of the party filing such writing, to file a Hindi translation of the same, within such time as may be granted by the court and the opposite party shall have a right to have a copy of such writing in Hindi."
