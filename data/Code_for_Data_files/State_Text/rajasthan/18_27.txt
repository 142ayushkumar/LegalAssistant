Rajasthan Special Courts (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Rajasthan An Act to amend the Rajasthan Special Courts Act, 2012. Be it enacted by the Rajasthan State Legislature in the Sixty-fourth Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Rajasthan Special Courts (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Rajasthan (1) This Act may be called the Rajasthan Special Courts (Amendment) Act, 2013. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Rajasthan Special Courts (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Rajasthan In clause (b) of section 2 of the Rajasthan Special Courts Act, 2012 (Act No. 38 of 2012), for the existing expression "section 3", the expression "section 13" shall be substituted. Principal Secretary to the Government.
