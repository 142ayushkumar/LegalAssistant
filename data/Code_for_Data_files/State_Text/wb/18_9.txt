West Bengal Settled Estates (Repealing) Act, 1973_Section Preamble--> State(s): West Bengal An Act to repeal the Bengal Settled Estates Act, 1904. Whereas it is expedient to repeal the Bengal Settled Estates Act, 1904; It is hereby enacted in the Twenty-fourth Year of the Republic of India, by the Legislature of West Bengal, as follows:-
West Bengal Settled Estates (Repealing) Act, 1973_Section 1--> State(s): West Bengal This Act may be called the West Bengal Settled Estates (Repealing) Act, 1973.
West Bengal Settled Estates (Repealing) Act, 1973_Section 2--> State(s): West Bengal The Bengal Settled Estates Act, 1904, is hereby repealed.
