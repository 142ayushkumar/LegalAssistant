West Bengal Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section Preamble--> State(s): West Bengal An Act to amend the West Bengal Municipal Corporation Bill. 2006. WHEREAS it is expedient to amend the West Bengal Municipal Corporation Bill, 2006, for the purposes and in the manner hereinafter appearing: It is hereby enacted in the Fifty-eighth Year of the Republic of India by the Legislature of West Bengal, as follows:-
West Bengal Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 1--> State(s): West Bengal (1) This Act may be called the West Bengal Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2007. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
West Bengal Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 2--> State(s): West Bengal Clause 314 of the West Bengal Municipal Corporation Bill, 2006 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Bill), shall be omitted.
West Bengal Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 3--> State(s): West Bengal In clause 325 of the principal Bill, the words and figures "or section 328, as the case may be" shall be omitted.
West Bengal Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 4--> State(s): West Bengal For clause 326 of the principal Bill, the following clause shall be substituted:- "Information about births and deaths 326, Births and deaths occur in the Corporation area shall be reported for registration as per provisions of sub-section (1) of section 8 of the Registration of Births and Deaths Act, 1969.".
West Bengal Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 5--> State(s): West Bengal In clause 327 of the principal Bill, for the words and figures", to the officer empowered under section 343, within twenty-one days", the words and figures ", to the officer empowered under section 326, within such period as prescribed in the rules framed by the State Government under the Registration of Births and Deaths Act. 1969." shall be substituted.
West Bengal Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 6--> State(s): West Bengal Clause 328 of the principal Bill shall be omitted.
West Bengal Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 7--> State(s): West Bengal In clause 329 of the principal Bill, after the words ", sign and forward", the words ", without charging any fee." shall be inserted. By order of the Governor. Anindya Bhattacharyya, Secretary in charge to the Government of West Bengal Law Department.
