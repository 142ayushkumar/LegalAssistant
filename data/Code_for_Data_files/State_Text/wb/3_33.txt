West Bengal Cruelty to Animals (Repeal of Laws) Act, 1974_Section Preamble--> State(s): West Bengal An Act to repeal certain laws relating to the prevention of cruelty to animals in West Bengal. WHEREAS it is expedient to repeal certain laws relating to the prevention of cruelty to animals in West Bengal; It is hereby enacted in the Twenty-fifth Year of the Republic of India, by the Legislature of West Bengal, as follows;-
West Bengal Cruelty to Animals (Repeal of Laws) Act, 1974_Section 1--> State(s): West Bengal This Act may be called the West Bengal Cruelty to Animals (Repeal of Laws) Act, 1974.
West Bengal Cruelty to Animals (Repeal of Laws) Act, 1974_Section 2--> State(s): West Bengal The following enactments, namely;- (a) the Bengal Cruelty to Animals Act, 1869; (b) the Bengal Cruelty to Animals (Arrest) Act, 1869; (c) the Bengal Cruelty to Animals Act, 1900; and (d) the Bengal Cruelty to Animals Act, 1920 are hereby repealed.
