West Bengal Legislature (Members' Pension) (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section Preamble--> State(s): West Bengal 1 An Act to amend the West Bengal Legislature (Members' Pension) Act, 1986. Whereas it is expedient to amend the West Bengal Legislature (Members' Pension) Act, 1986 (West Ben. Act XVIII of 1986), for the purposes and in the manner hereinafter appearing; It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-first Year of the Republic of India, by the Legislature of West Bengal, as follows:- Published in the Kolkata Gazette, Extra-ordinary, Part III, No. 1313-L, dated 3rd September, 2010.
West Bengal Legislature (Members' Pension) (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 1--> State(s): West Bengal (1) This Act may be called the West Bengal Legislature (Members' Pension) (Amendment) Act, 2010. (2) It shall come into force on the 1st day of August, 2010.
West Bengal Legislature (Members' Pension) (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 2--> State(s): West Bengal In section 3 of the West Bengal Legislature (Members' Pension) Act, 1986 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (1),- (a) for the words "four thousand rupees", the words "eight thousand rupees" shall be substituted; (b) in the proviso, for the words "eight thousand rupees", the words "twelve thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
West Bengal Legislature (Members' Pension) (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 3--> State(s): West Bengal In section 3B of the principal Act, for the words "at the rate of rupees four thousand per month", the words "at the rate of rupees six thousand per month" shall be substituted.
