West Bengal Legislative Assembly Proceedings (Protection of Publication) Act, 1978_Section Preamble--> State(s): West Bengal An Act to protect the publication of the reports of proceedings of the West Bengal Legislative Assembly. WHEREAS it is proper lo protect the publication of the reports of proceedings of the West Bengal Legislative Assembly; It is hereby enacted in the Twenty-ninth Year of the Republic of India, by the Legislature of West Bengal, as follows:-
West Bengal Legislative Assembly Proceedings (Protection of Publication) Act, 1978_Section 1--> State(s): West Bengal (1) This Act may be called the West Bengal Legislative Assembly Proceedings (Protection of Publication) Act, 1978.1 (2) It extends to the whole of West Bengal. (3) It shall come into force at once. For Statement of Objects and Reasons, sec the Calcutta Gazette, Extraordinary. Part IV, of the 9th September, 1978: for proceedings of the West Bengal Legislative Assembly, see the proceedings of the meeting of that Assembly held on the 13th December, 1978.
West Bengal Legislative Assembly Proceedings (Protection of Publication) Act, 1978_Section 2--> State(s): West Bengal In this Act, "newspaper" means any printed periodical work containing public news or comments on public news and includes news- agency supplying material for publication in a newspaper.
West Bengal Legislative Assembly Proceedings (Protection of Publication) Act, 1978_Section 3--> State(s): West Bengal (1) Save as otherwise provided in sub-section (2), no person shall be liable to any proceedings, civil or criminal, in any court in respect of publication in a newspaper of a substantially true report of any proceedings of the West Bengal Legislative Assembly, unless the publication is proved to have been made with malice. (2) Nothing in sub-section (1) shall be construed as protecting the publication of any matter which is not Tor the public good.
West Bengal Legislative Assembly Proceedings (Protection of Publication) Act, 1978_Section 4--> State(s): West Bengal The Act shall also apply in relation to reports or matters broadcast by means of wireless telegraphy as part of any programme or service provided by means of a broadcasting station as it applies in relation to reports or matters published in a newspaper.
