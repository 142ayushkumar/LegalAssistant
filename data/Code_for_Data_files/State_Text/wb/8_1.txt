West Bengal Heritage Commission (Amendment) Amending Act, 2010_Section Preamble--> State(s): West Bengal 1 An Act to amend the West Bengal Heritage Commission (Amendment) Act, 2006 and the West Bengal Heritage Commission (Amendment) Act, 2007. Whereas it is expedient to amend the West Bengal Heritage Commission (Amendment) Act, 2006 (West Ben. Act XXXIII of 2006) and the West Bengal Heritage Commission (Amendment) Act, 2007 (West Ben. Act VII of 2007), for the purposes and in the manner hereinafter appearing; It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-first Year of the Republic of India, by the Legislature of West Bengal, as follows:- Published in the Kolkata Gazette, Extra-ordinary, Part III, No. 1410-L, dated 5th October, 2010.
West Bengal Heritage Commission (Amendment) Amending Act, 2010_Section 1--> State(s): West Bengal (1) This Act may be called the West Bengal Heritage Commission (Amendment) Amending Act, 2010. (2) It shall come into force at once.
West Bengal Heritage Commission (Amendment) Amending Act, 2010_Section 2--> State(s): West Bengal For sub-section (2) of section 1 of the West Bengal Heritage Commission (Amendment) Act, 2006, the following sub-section shall be substituted:- "(2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 29th January, 2007.".
West Bengal Heritage Commission (Amendment) Amending Act, 2010_Section 3--> State(s): West Bengal For sub-section (2) of section 1 of the West Bengal Heritage Commission (Amendment) Act, 2007, the following sub-section shall be substituted:- "(2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 19th July, 2007.".
West Bengal Heritage Commission (Amendment) Amending Act, 2010_Section 4--> State(s): West Bengal (1) The amendments made in the West Bengal Heritage Commission Act, 2001 (West Ben. Act IX of 2001), (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), by the West Bengal Heritage Commission (Amendment) Act, 2006 (West Ben. Act XXXIII of 2006) shall be deemed to have been made with effect from the 29th January, 2007 and accordingly anything done or any action taken on or after the said date and before the commencement of this Act shall be deemed to have been validly done or taken. (2) The amendments made in the principal Act, by the West Bengal Heritage Commission (Amendment) Act, 2007 (West Ben. Act VII of 2007) shall be deemed to have been made with effect from the 19th July, 2007 and accordingly anything done or any action taken on or after the said date and before the commencement of this Act shall be deemed to have been validly done or taken.
