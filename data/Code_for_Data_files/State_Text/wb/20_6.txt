West Bengal University Laws Amendment (Repealing) Act, 1977_Section Preamble--> State(s): West Bengal An Act to repeal the West Bengal University Laws Amendment Act, 1976. WHEREAS it is expedient to repeal the West Bengal University Laws Amendment Act, 1976; It is hereby enacted in the Twenty-eighth Year of the Republic of India, by the Legislature of West Bengal, as follows:-
West Bengal University Laws Amendment (Repealing) Act, 1977_Section 1--> State(s): West Bengal This Act may be called the West Bengal University Laws Amendment (Repealing) Act, 1977.
West Bengal University Laws Amendment (Repealing) Act, 1977_Section 2--> State(s): West Bengal The West Bengal University Laws Amendment Act, 1976, is hereby repealed.
