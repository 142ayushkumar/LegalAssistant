West Bengal Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes (Reservation of Vacancies in Services and Posts) (Amendment) Act, 2009_Section Preamble--> State(s): West Bengal An Act to Amend the West Bengal Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes (Reservation of Vacancies in Services and Posts) Act, 1976. Whereas it is expedient to amend the West Bengal Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes (Reservation of Vacancies in Services and Posts) Act, 1976, for the purpose and in the manner hereinafter appearing; It is hereby enacted as follows:- Statement of Object As per clause (b) of section 5 of the West Bengal Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes (Reservation of Vacancies in Services and Posts) Act, 1976 (Wesl Ben. Act XXVII of 1976), the provision of reservation does not apply to the post carrying the scale of pay exceeding Rs. 18,300. Consequent upon the revision of pay and allowances of the employees of the State Government by virtue of the West Bengal Services (Revision of Pay and Allowances) Rules, 2009, retrospectively with effect from the 1st day of January, 2006, it has become necessary to bring corresponding change in the aforementioned provisions of the Act from the date of commencement of the said rules [Vide statement of objects and reasons, published in the West Bengal, Gazette, Extraordinary dated 26th November, 2009
West Bengal Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes (Reservation of Vacancies in Services and Posts) (Amendment) Act, 2009_Section 1--> State(s): West Bengal (1) This Act may be called the West Bengal Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes (Reservation of Vacancies in Services and Posts) (Amendment) Act, 2009. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st day of January, 2006.
West Bengal Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes (Reservation of Vacancies in Services and Posts) (Amendment) Act, 2009_Section 2--> State(s): West Bengal In section 5 of the West Bengal Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes (Reservation of Vacancies in Services and Posts) Act, 1976, for clause (b), the following clause shall be substituted:- "(b) there shall be no reservation in any post carrying the grade pay exceeding Rs.8,700;".
