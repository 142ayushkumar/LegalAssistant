West Bengal Medical and Dental Colleges (Regulation of Admission) (Repealing) Act, 1977_Section Preamble--> State(s): West Bengal An Act to repeal the West Bengal Medical and Dental Colleges (Regulation of Admission) Act, 1973. WHEREAS it is expedient to repeal the West Bengal Medical and Dental Colleges (Regulation of Admission) Act, 1973; It is hereby enacted in the Twenty-eighth Year of the Republic of India, by the Legislature of West Bengal, as follows:-
West Bengal Medical and Dental Colleges (Regulation of Admission) (Repealing) Act, 1977_Section 1--> State(s): West Bengal This Act may be called the West Bengal Medical and Dental Colleges (Regulation of Admission) (Repealing) Act, 1977.
West Bengal Medical and Dental Colleges (Regulation of Admission) (Repealing) Act, 1977_Section 2--> State(s): West Bengal The West Bengal Medical and Dental Colleges (Regulation of Admission) Act, 1973 is hereby repealed.
