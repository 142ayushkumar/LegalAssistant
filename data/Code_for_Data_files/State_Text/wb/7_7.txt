West Bengal Gramdan (Repealing) Act, 1979_Section Preamble--> State(s): West Bengal An Act to repeal the West Bengal Gramdan Act, 1964. WHEREAS it is expedient to repeal the West Bengal Gramdan Act, 1964; It is hereby enacted in the Thirtieth Year of the Republic of India, by the Legislature of West Bengal, as follows:-
West Bengal Gramdan (Repealing) Act, 1979_Section 1--> State(s): West Bengal This Act may be called the West Bengal Gramdan (Repealing) Act. 1979.
West Bengal Gramdan (Repealing) Act, 1979_Section 2--> State(s): West Bengal The West Bengal Gramdan Act, 1964 is hereby repealed.
