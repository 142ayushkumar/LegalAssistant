Punjab Passengers and Goods Taxation (Haryana Amendment) Act, 2007_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana, Punjab An Act further to amend the Punjab Passengers and Goods Taxation Act, 1952, in its application to the State of Haryana. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Fifty-eight Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Punjab Passengers and Goods Taxation (Haryana Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana, Punjab This Act may be called the Punjab Passengers and Goods Taxation (Haryana Amendment) Act, 2007.
Punjab Passengers and Goods Taxation (Haryana Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana, Punjab In section 14B of the Punjab Passengers and Goods Taxation Act, 1952, the words and sign "for a period not exceeding three months, and also additional interest upto two percent per month for the period of default exceeding three months" shall be omitted and shall be deemed to have been omitted with effect from the 1st day of March, 2007. (M.S Sullar), Secretary to Government, Haryana. Legislative Department.
