Punjab Departmental Enquiries (Powers) Act, 1955_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act to confer certain powers on the officers conducting enquiries under the Punjab Civil Service (Punishment and Appeal) Rules. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Punjab Departmental Enquiries (Powers) Act, 1955_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab This Act may be cited as the Punjab Departmental Enquiries (Powers) Act, 1955.1 Received the assent of the Governor of Punjab on the 3rd May, 1955, and was first published in the Punjab Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 7th May, 1955
Punjab Departmental Enquiries (Powers) Act, 1955_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab For the purposes of an enquiry under the Punjab Civil Services (Punishment and Appeal), Rules, 1 (or the Punjab Police Rules), for the time being in force, the officer conducting such an enquiry shall be competent to exercise the same powers for the summoning of witnesses and for compelling the production of documents as are exercisable by a commission appointed for an enquiry under the Public Servants (Inquiries) Act, 1850 (Act (XXXVII of 1850), and all persons disobeying any process issued by such officer in this behalf shall be liable to the same penalties as if the same had issued from a Court. Inserted by Punjab Act 17 of 1958, section 2
