Punjab Regional and Town Planning and Development (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act further to amend the Punjab Regional and Town Planning and Development Act, 1995. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Sixty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Punjab Regional and Town Planning and Development (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab (1) This Act may be called the Punjab Regional and Town Planning and Development (Amendment) Act, 2016. (2) It shall come into force on and with effect from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.
Punjab Regional and Town Planning and Development (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In the Punjab Regional and Town Planning and Development Act, 1995, in section 79, in the proviso thereunder, for the words "ten years", the words "fifteen years" shall be substituted. (Vivek Puri) Secretary to Government of Punjab, Department of Legal and Legislative Affairs.
