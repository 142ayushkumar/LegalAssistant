Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales) Haryana Amendment Act, 1982_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act to amend the Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales) Act, 1939, in its application to the State of Haryana. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Thirty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows :
Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales) Haryana Amendment Act, 1982_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab This Act may be called the Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales) Haryana Amendment Act, 1982.
Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales) Haryana Amendment Act, 1982_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales) Act, 1939, for the words "fifteen paise per litre", the words "seven per centum ad valorem" shall be substituted.
Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales) Haryana Amendment Act, 1982_Section 3--> State(s): Punjab The Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales), Haryana Amendment Ordinance, 1982 (Haryana Ordinance No. 3 of 1982) (See page 67 supra.), is hereby repealed.
