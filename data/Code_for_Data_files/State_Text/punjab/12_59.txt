Punjab Lokpal (Repeal) Act, 1996_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act to repeal to Punjab Lokpal Act, 1995. Be it enacted by Legislature of State of Punjab in the Forty-Seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Punjab Lokpal (Repeal) Act, 1996_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab (1) This Act may be called the Punjab Lokpal (Repeal) Act, 1996. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the 10th day of December, 1996.
Punjab Lokpal (Repeal) Act, 1996_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab The Punjab Lokpal Act, 1995 (Punjab Act 9 of 1995) is hereby repealed.
Punjab Lokpal (Repeal) Act, 1996_Section 3--> State(s): Punjab The Punjab Lokpal Act (Repeal) Ordinance, 1996 (Punjab Ordinance No. 3 of 1996) is hereby also repealed.
