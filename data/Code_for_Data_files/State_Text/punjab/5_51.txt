Evacuee Interest (Separation) Supplementary Act, 1953_Section Preamble--> State(s): Haryana, Punjab An Act to supplement certain provisions of the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Act, 1953
Evacuee Interest (Separation) Supplementary Act, 1953_Section 1--> State(s): Haryana, Punjab (1) This Act may be called the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Supplementary Act, 1953.1 (2) It shall extend to the whole of the [Principal Territories]2 . (3) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 15th day of December, 1952. Received the assent of the Governor of Punjab on the 28th April, 1953, and was first published in the Punjab Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 2nd May, 1953. Substituted for "State of Punjab" vide Haryana Adoption of Laws Order 1968.
Evacuee Interest (Separation) Supplementary Act, 1953_Section 2--> State(s): Haryana, Punjab The Evacuee Interest (Separation) Act, 1951, shall, so far as it relates to any matter enumerated in List II in the Seventh Schedule of the Constitution be as valid in the [Principal Territories]1 as if it had been passed by the Legislature [for such Territories]2 . Substituted for "State of Punjab" vide Haryana Adoption of Laws Order 1968. Substituted for "Legislature of the State" by Haryana Adoption of Laws Order 1968.
