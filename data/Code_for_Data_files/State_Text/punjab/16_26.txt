Punjab Pre-emption (Repeal) Act, 1973_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act to repeal the Punjab Pre-emption Act, 1913. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Twenty-Fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Punjab Pre-emption (Repeal) Act, 1973_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab This Act may be called the Punjab Pre-emption (Repeal) Act, 1973.1 Received the assent of the Governor of Punjab on the 6th April, 1973 and was published in the Punjab Gazette, (Extra.), Legislative Supplement, Part I. dated 9th April. 1973.
Punjab Pre-emption (Repeal) Act, 1973_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab The Punjab Pre-emption (Repeal) Act, 1973.
Punjab Pre-emption (Repeal) Act, 1973_Section 3--> State(s): Punjab On and from the date of commencement of the Punjab Pre-emption (Repeal) Act. 1973, no court shall pass a decree in any suit for pre-emption.
Punjab Pre-emption (Repeal) Act, 1973_Section 4--> State(s): Punjab The Punjab Pre-emption (Repeal) Ordinance, 1973 (Punjab Ordinance No. 1 of 1973) is hereby repealed.
