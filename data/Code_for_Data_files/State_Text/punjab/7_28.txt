Punjab Gram Panchayat (Common Purposes Land) Eviction and Rent Recovery Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act to provide for the eviction of unauthorized occupants from Land reserved for common purposes under the East Punjab Holdings (Consolidation and Prevention of Fragmentation) Act, 1948 the management and control whereof vests in the Gram Panchayat and for certain incidental matters. Be it enacted by the legislature of the State of Punjab in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows-
Punjab Gram Panchayat (Common Purposes Land) Eviction and Rent Recovery Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab (1) This act may be called the Punjab Gram Panchayat (Common Purposes Land) Eviction and Rent Recovery Act, 19761 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the President of India on the 22nd April, 1976.
Punjab Gram Panchayat (Common Purposes Land) Eviction and Rent Recovery Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In the act, unless the context otherwise requires: (a) "common purpose land II means land reserved for the common purposes of a village under section 18 of the East Punjab Holdings(Consolidation and Prevention of Fragmentation)Act, 1948, the management and control whereof vests in the Gram Panchayat under section 23-A of the aforesaid Act: (b) "Government" means the Government of the State of Punjab.
Punjab Gram Panchayat (Common Purposes Land) Eviction and Rent Recovery Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Punjab Notwithstanding anything contained in any law for the time being in force, the Punjab Public Premises and Land (Eviction and Rent Recovery Act, 1973 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) shall apply to common purposes land which shall be deemed to be public premises for the purposes of the principal Act.
Punjab Gram Panchayat (Common Purposes Land) Eviction and Rent Recovery Act, 1976_Section 4--> State(s): Punjab Notwithstanding anything in the principal Act an appeal under section 9 of that Act in relation to common purposes land shall lie to an officer not below the rank of a Joint Director of Panchayats appointed by the Government for the said purpose.
