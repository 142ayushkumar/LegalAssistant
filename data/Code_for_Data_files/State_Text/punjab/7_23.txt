Punjab General Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act further to amend the Punjab General Sales Tax Act, 1948. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Fifty-one Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Punjab General Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab (1) This Act may be called the Punjab General Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 2000. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Punjab General Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In the Punjab General Sales Tax Act, 1948 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in section 13, in sub-section (2), - (i) in clause (a), for the words "a cash memorandum or bill serially numbered", the words "a cash memorandum or bill from a duly bound and serially numbered cash memorandum or bill book" shall be substituted; and (ii) for the existing proviso, the following provisions shall be substituted, namely :- "Provided that the provisions of cash memorandum or bill referred to in clause (a) shall not be applicable to the computerised cash memo or bill : Provided further that the State Government may, by notification, exempt any class of registered dealers from the provisions of this sub-section.".
Punjab General Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 3--> State(s): Punjab In the principal Act, in section 20, for sub-section (5), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely :- "(5) No appeal shall be entertained by an appellate authority unless such appeal is accompanied by satisfactory proof of the prior minimum payment of twenty-five per cent of the tax, penalty, if any, imposed and the interest accrued thereon.".
Punjab General Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 4--> State(s): Punjab In the principal Act, sections 24 and 24A shall be omitted.
Punjab General Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 5--> State(s): Punjab In the principal Act, in section 26, in sub-section (3), for the words "the Regional Computer Centre, Chandigarh" the words "an agency with whom the State Government have entered into a contract" shall be substituted.
