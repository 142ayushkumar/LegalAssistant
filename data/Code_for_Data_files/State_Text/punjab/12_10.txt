Land Acquisition Punjab Second Amendment Act, 1956_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act to amend the Land Acquisition Act, 1894, as amended by Punjab Act II of 1954, in its application to the State of Punjab. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Seventh Year of Republic of India as follows:-
Land Acquisition Punjab Second Amendment Act, 1956_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab This Act may be called The Land Acquisition (Punjab Second Amendment) Act, 1956.1 Received the assent of the President on the 29th October, 1956, and was first published in the Punjab Government Gazette, Extraordinary, dated the 31st October, 1956
Land Acquisition Punjab Second Amendment Act, 1956_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In sub-section (2) of section 17 of Land Acquisition Act, 1894, as amended by Punjab Act II of 1954, in its application to the State of Punjab, the following clause shall be inserted after the words" any public road occurring in clause (b) :- "(c) Whenever land is acquired for a public purpose which in the opinion of the appropriate Government is of urgent importance."
Land Acquisition Punjab Second Amendment Act, 1956_Section 3--> State(s): Punjab The Land Acquisition (Punjab Amendment) Ordinance, 1956 (Ordinance 4 of 1956), is hereby repealed.
