Punjab Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act further to amend the Punjab Value Added Tax Act, 2005. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Sixty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Punjab Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab (1) This Act may be called the Punjab Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2016. (2) It shall come into force on and with effect from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.
Punjab Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In the Punjab Value Added Tax Act, 2005, in section 68, in sub-section (7), for the words "shall not be stayed", the words and signs "may be stayed, for the reasons to be recorded in writing after hearing the State," shall be substituted.
Punjab Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 3--> State(s): Punjab (1) The Punjab Value Added tax (Amendment) Ordinance, 2016 (Punjab Ordinance No. 5 of 2016) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the Ordinance referred to in sub-section (1), shall be deemed to have been done or taken under this Act. (Vivek Puri) Secretary to Government of Punjab, Department of Legal and Legislative Affairs.
