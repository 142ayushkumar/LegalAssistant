Punjab Entertainments Duty (Amendment and Validation) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act further to amend the Punjab Entertainments Duty Act, 1955. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Fifty-one Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Punjab Entertainments Duty (Amendment and Validation) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab (1) This Act may be called the Punjab Entertainments Duty (Amendment and Validation) Act, 2000. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on and with effect from the 12th day of August, 1994.
Punjab Entertainments Duty (Amendment and Validation) Act, 2000_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In the Punjab Entertainments Duty Act, 1955 (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act), in section 2, in clause (d), for the words' "for exhibition of films", the words "or exhibition of films" shall be substituted.
Punjab Entertainments Duty (Amendment and Validation) Act, 2000_Section 3--> State(s): Punjab (1) Notwithstanding any judgment, decree or order of any court or other authority, any duty levied or collected or purported to have been levied or collected under the principal Act from the 12th August, 1994 to the date of publication of the Punjab Entertainments Duty (Amendment and Validation) Act, 2000 in the Official Gazette, shall, for all purposes be deemed to be and to have always been levied or collected in accordance with law, and accordingly, - (a) no suit or other proceeding shall be maintained or continued in any court for the refund of any duty so paid; (b) no court shall enforce any decree or order directing the refund of any duty so paid; (c) any duty levied or purported to have been levied under the principal Act from the 12th August, 1994 to the date of publication of the Punjab Entertainments Duty (Amendment and Validation) Act, 2000 in the Official Gazette, but not collected, may be recovered in the manner provided in the principal Act; and (d) any duty under the principal Act from the 12th August, 1994 to the date of publication of the Punjab Entertainments Duty (Amendment and Validation) Act, 2000 in the Official Gazette, but not levied and collected, may be levied and collected in the manner provided in the principal Act and the Rules made thereunder notwithstanding the period of limitation provided therein. (2) For the removal of doubts, it is hereby declared that nothing in sub-section (1) shall be construed as preventing any person - (a) from questioning in accordance with the provisions of the principal Act and the Rules made thereunder, the levy or collection of such duty; and (b) from claiming refund of any duty paid by him in excess of the amount due from him under the principal Act as amended by the Punjab Entertainments Duty (Amendment and Validation) Act, 2000.
Punjab Entertainments Duty (Amendment and Validation) Act, 2000_Section 4--> State(s): Punjab (1) The Punjab Entertainments Duty (Second Amendment and Validation) Ordinance, 1999 (Punjab Ordinance No. 10 of 1999), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the Ordinance referred to in sub-section (1), shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
