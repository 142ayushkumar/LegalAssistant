Punjab General Sales Tax (As in force in the Union territory of Chandigarh) Repeal Act, 2005_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act to repeal the Punjab General Sales Tax Act, 1948, as in force in the Union territory of Chandigarh. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Punjab General Sales Tax (As in force in the Union territory of Chandigarh) Repeal Act, 2005_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab This Act may be called the Punjab General Sales Tax (As in force in the Union territory of Chandigarh) Repeal Act, 2005.
Punjab General Sales Tax (As in force in the Union territory of Chandigarh) Repeal Act, 2005_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab The Punjab General Sales Tax Act, 1948, as in force in the Union territory of Chandigarh, is hereby repealed. T. K. VISWANATHAN, Secy. to the Govt, of India.
