Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales) Amendment Act, 1986_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act to amend the Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales) Act, 1939. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Thirty-seventh Year of the Republic of India, as follows :-
Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales) Amendment Act, 1986_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab (1) This Act may be called the Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales) Amendment Act, 1986. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the Government may by notification appoint.
Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales) Amendment Act, 1986_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In the Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales) Act, 1939, in section 3, for sub-section (1) excepting the proviso, the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely :- "(1) There shall be levied and paid to the Government on ad valorem basis a tax on all sales of - (a) diesel oil, at the rate of four paise in a rupee; and (b) other motor spirit, at the rate of six paise in a rupee;".
