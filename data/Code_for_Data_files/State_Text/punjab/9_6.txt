Indian Forest (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1954_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act to amend the Indian Forest Act, 1927 (Act XVI of 1927) in its application to Punjab. It is hereby enacted as follows :-
Indian Forest (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1954_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab (1) This Act may be called The Indian Forest (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1954. Punjab Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 9th March, 1954.1 (2) It extends to the whole of Punjab. Received the assent of the Governor of Punjab on the 5th May, 1954, and was first published in the Punjab Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 11th May, 1954.
Indian Forest (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1954_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In clause (c) of section 76 of the Indian Forest Act, 1927, between the word "timber" and the word "belonging" the words "or other forest produce" shall be inserted, and the words "not grown on lands belonging to or in the occupation of private persons" shall be omitted.
