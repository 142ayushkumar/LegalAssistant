Punjab General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act to amend the Punjab General Sales Tax Act, 1948. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Thirty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Punjab General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab (1) This Act may be called the Punjab General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1982. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 29th day of January, 1982, except section 3 which shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st day of April, 1978.
Punjab General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In the Punjab General Sales Tax Act, 1948 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in section 10, for sub-section (3), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely :- "(3) Such dealers as may be required so to do by the assessing authority by notice served in the prescribed manner and every registered dealer shall, - (i) furnish such returns by such dates and to such authority as may be prescribed; and (ii) file a declaration in respect of the goods which have been purchased from a registered dealer within the State of Punjab in such manner and form and to such authority as may be prescribed."
Punjab General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 3--> State(s): Punjab In the principal Act, in section 26, in sub-section (3), after the words "State Government", the words "or for the purpose of preparing data by the officials of the Regional Computer Center, Chandigarh" shall be added.
Punjab General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 4--> State(s): Punjab (1) The Punjab General Sales Tax (Amendment) Ordinance, 1982 (Punjab Ordinance No. 1 of 1982) (See 1982 49 STC Statutes 153.), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the Ordinance referred to in sub-section (1), shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act, as amended by this Act. The Statement of Objects and Reasons appended to the Punjab General Sales Tax (Amendment) Bill, 1982 (Bill No. 7-PLA of 1982) (Punjab Government Gazette, Extraordinary, dated 19th March, 1982, page 266.), runs as follows :- "Amendments of sub-section (3) of section 10 and sub-section (3) of section 26 of the Punjab General Sales Tax Act, 1948, are aimed at plugging loopholes and checking the evasion of sales tax by unscrupulous dealers. Under the proposed amendments it shall be enjoined on the dealers to supply a list of their purchases from registered dealers, etc. This data is proposed to be utilised through computerisation for cross checking the accounts of dealers both in respect of their sales to an purchases from other registered dealers. Without this provision the computerisation work was incomplete. This bill is designed to replace the Punjab General Sales Tax (Amendment) Ordinance, 1982 (See 1982 49 STC Statutes 153.), promulgated on 29th January, 1982."
