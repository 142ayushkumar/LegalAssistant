Punjab Public Service Commission (Additional Functions) Act, 1955_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act to provide for the recruitment of certain local bodies officers through the Punjab Public Service Commissions. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Sixth Year of the Republic of India is follows:-
Punjab Public Service Commission (Additional Functions) Act, 1955_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab (1) This Act may be called the Punjab Public Service Commission (Additional Functions) Act, 19551 (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Punjab. (3) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor of Punjab on the 8th May, 1955.
Punjab Public Service Commission (Additional Functions) Act, 1955_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In this Act, unless the context otherwise requires,- (a) "Local Authority" means a Municipal Committee, Notified Area Committee, District Board or any other authority entrusted with their functions under any law for the time being in force. (b) "Commission" means the Punjab Public Service Commission. 1 [(c) "Recruitment" means direct appointment or appointment of an official by transfer or promotion other than transfer or promotion in the cadre of the same service.] Added by Punjab Act 10 of 1957, Section 2
Punjab Public Service Commission (Additional Functions) Act, 1955_Section 3--> State(s): Punjab Except in the case of an appointment of an Executive Officer of a Municipal Committee under the Punjab Municipal (Executive officer) Act (Punjab Act II of 1931), 1 [or the Patiala Municipal (Executive Officer) Act, 2003] recruitment of persons for all posts under a local authority, carrying an initial pay of 2 [three hundred rupees] or more, shall be made through the Commission. Inserted by Punjab Act 23 of 1957, Schedule. Substituted for "Rs.150" by Punjab Act 9 of 1972, Section 2
