Punjab General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act to amend the Punjab General Sales Tax Act, 1948. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Thirty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Punjab General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab This Act may be called the Punjab General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1986.
Punjab General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In the Punjab General Sales Tax Act, 1948, in section 5-A for the words "two per centum", the words "ten per centum" shall be substituted. The Statement of Objects and Reasons appended to the Punjab General Sales Tax (Amendment) Bill, 1986 (Bill No. 10-PLA of 1986) (Punjab Government Gazette, Extraordinary, dated 21st March, 1986, page 308.), runs as follows : "This legislation aims at mobilisation of additional resources to rehabilitate and reconstruct the State's economy and accelerate the tempo of development of the State."
