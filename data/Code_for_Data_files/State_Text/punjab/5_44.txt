Punjab Entertainments Duty (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act further to amend the Punjab Entertainments Duty Act, 1955. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Forty-firth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Punjab Entertainments Duty (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab (1) This Act may be called the Punjab Entertainments Duty (Amendment) Act, 1994. (2) It shall come into force on and with effect from the first day of June, 1994.
Punjab Entertainments Duty (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In the Punjab Entertainments Duty Act, 1955 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), after section 3, the following section shall be inserted, namely :- "3-A. Entertainment duty is not leviable in case tax is paid under Punjab Act 8 of 1954. - Notwithstanding anything contained in this Act, no entertainment duty shall be leviable on the proprietor who is liable to pay entertainment tax under the Punjab Entertainments Tax (Cinematograph Shows) Act, 1954.".
Punjab Entertainments Duty (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 3--> State(s): Punjab In the principal Act, in section 11, sub-section (5) shall be omitted.
Punjab Entertainments Duty (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 4--> State(s): Punjab (1) The Punjab Entertainments Duty (Amendment) Ordinance, 1994 (Punjab Ordinance No. 3 of 1994 (See 1994 94 STC Statutes 44)), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the Ordinance referred to in sub-section (1), shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
