Punjab Rural Development (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act further to amend the Punjab Rural Development Act, 1987. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Sixty-seventh Year of Republic of India as follows :-
Punjab Rural Development (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab (1) This Act may be called the Punjab Rural Development (Amendment) Act, 2016. (2) It shall come into force on and with effect from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.
Punjab Rural Development (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In the Punjab Rural Development Act, 1987, after section 6, the following section shall be inserted, namely:- "6-A. Audit of Fund - Subject to the control of the Secretary to Government of Punjab, Department of Finance, who shall be the final audit authority, the accounts of the Fund shall be audited, as far as possible once in a year or half yearly, as the Government may direct, by or under the orders of the Examiner, Local Fund Accounts, Punjab".
Punjab Rural Development (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 3--> State(s): Punjab (1) The Punjab Rural Development (Amendment) Ordinance, 2016 (Punjab Ordinance No. 2 of 2016), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the Ordinance referred to in sub-section (1), shall be deemed to have been done or taken under this Act. Vivek Puri Secretary to Government of Punjab, Department of Legal and Legislative Affairs.
