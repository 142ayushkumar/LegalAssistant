Punjab Value Added Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 2008_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act further to amend the Punjab Value Added Tax Act, 2005. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the fifty-ninth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Punjab Value Added Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 2008_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab (1) This Act may be called the Punjab Value Added Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 2008. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on and with effect from the 21st day of November, 2007.
Punjab Value Added Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 2008_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab Section 13A, inserted by the Punjab Value Added Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 2007 (Punjab Act No. 3 of 2008), in the Punjab Value Added Tax Act, 2005, shall be deemed to have been inserted on and with effect from the 21st day of November, 2007.
