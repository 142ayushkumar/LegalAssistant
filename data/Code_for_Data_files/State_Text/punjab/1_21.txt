Punjab Agricultural Universities (Haryana Amendment) Act, 1997_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act to amend the Haryana and Punjab Agricultural Universities Act, 1970, in its application to the State of Haryana. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Forty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Punjab Agricultural Universities (Haryana Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab This Act may be called the Haryana and Punjab Agricultural Universities (Haryana Amendment) Act, 1997.
Punjab Agricultural Universities (Haryana Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In sub-section (4) of section 34 of the Haryana and Punjab Agricultural Universities Act, 1970, for the words and sign "Examiner, Local Fund Accounts", the words and signs "Director, Local Audit, Haryana" shall be substituted.
