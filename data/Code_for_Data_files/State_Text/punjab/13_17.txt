Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales) Amendment Act, 1969_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act to amend the Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales) Act, 1939. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Twentieth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales) Amendment Act, 1969_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab This Act may be called the Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales) Amendment Act, 1969.
Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales) Amendment Act, 1969_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab For sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales) Act, 1939 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), the following sub-section shall be and shall be deemed to have been substituted with effect from the first day of November, 1967, namely :- "(1) There shall be levied and paid to the Government of Punjab, on all retail sales of - (a) diesel oil, a tax at the rate of seven paise for each litre; and (b) other motor spirit, a tax at the rate of nine paise for each litre."
Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales) Amendment Act, 1969_Section 3--> State(s): Punjab Notwithstanding the cesser of operation of the Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales) Amendment Act, 1966 (hereinafter referred to as the President's Act of 1966) and anything contained in any judgment, decree or order of any court or other authority to the contrary, any assessment, levy or collection of any tax on retail sale of motor spirit made or purporting to have been made or anything done or purporting to have been done or any action taken or purporting to have been taken under the principal Act, as amended by the President's Act of 1966, whether before or after such cesser of operation up to the date of commencement of this Act, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act, as amended by this Act, as if the principal Act as so amended had been in force at all relevant times and accordingly - (a) all acts, proceedings or things done or action taken by the State Government or by any other officer of the State Government or by any other authority in connection with the assessment, levy or collection of such tax shall, for all purposes, be deemed to be, and to have always been done or taken in accordance with law; (b) no suit or other proceedings shall be maintained or continued against the State Government or any person or authority whatsoever in any court or before any authority for the refund of any such tax; and (c) no court shall enforce any decree or order directing the refund of any such tax.
Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales) Amendment Act, 1969_Section 4--> State(s): Punjab (1) The Punjab Motor Spirit (Taxation of Sales) Amendment Ordinance, 1969 (Punjab Ordinance No. 9 of 1969), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under this Act, as if this Act had commenced on the thirteenth day of August, 1969.
