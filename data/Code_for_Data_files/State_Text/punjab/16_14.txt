Payment of Wages (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1964_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An act to amend the Payment of Wages Act, 1936, in its application to the State of Punjab. Be it enacted by the Legislative of the State of Punjab in the Fifteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows :- STATEMENT OF OBJECT :
Payment of Wages (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1964_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab (1) This Act may be called the Payment of Wages (Punjab Amendment) Act, 19641 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the1st day of April, 1963. Notes :-The Act is enacted to neutralise the effects of the amendments made in Section 6 of the principal Act from the day the persons become liable to compulsory deposit under the Compulsory Deposit Scheme Act. 1963 Received the assent of the President of India on February 6, 1964, and was published in the Punjab Gazette, (Extra), Legislative Supplement. Part I, dated February 14, 1964.
Payment of Wages (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1964_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In section 6 of the Payment of Wages Act, 1936, in its application to the State of Punjab, as amended by the Payment of Wages (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1962, the whole of the Proviso and Explanation shall be omitted.
