Punjab Land Preservation Act, 1900_Section 17--> State(s): Punjab (1) Upon the publication of a notification issued under any of the provisions of this Act, the Deputy Commissioner shall cause public notice of the substance thereof to be given at convenient places in the locality to which which such notification relates. (2) The procedure prescribed in sections 20, 21 and 22 of the Punjab Land Revenue Act, 1887 (XVII of 1887), shall be followed, as far as may be, in proceedings under this Act.
Punjab Land Preservation Act, 1900_Section 18--> State(s): Punjab Every order passed and award made by a Deputy Commissioner under this Act, shall, for the purposes of appeal, review and revision, respectively, be deemed to be the order of a Collector within the meaning of sections 13,14,15,and 16 of the Punjab Land Revenue Act, 1887 : Provided that nothing in this Act contained shall be deemed to exclude the jurisdiction of any Civil Court to decide any dispute arising between the persons interested in any compensation awarded as to the apportionment or distribution thereof amongst such persons or any of them.
