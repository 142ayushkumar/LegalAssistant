Suits Valuation (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1942_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act further to amend the Suits Valuation Act, 1887, in its application to the Punjab. It is hereby enacted as follows:-
Suits Valuation (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1942_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab This Act may be called the Suits Valuation (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1942.1 Received the assent of His Excellency the Governor General on the 2nd day of November, 1942, and was first published in the Punjab Government Gazette, dated the 4th December, 1942.
Suits Valuation (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1942_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab Section 2 of the Suits Valuation (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1938, shall be omitted.
Suits Valuation (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1942_Section 3--> State(s): Punjab The Suits Valuation Act, 1887, shall, in its application to1 [Punjab] be amended as follows:- (a) in Part I the heading "Suits relating to land" shall be deemed to be ommitted; (b) in Part II the heading "Other suits" shall be deemed to be omitted; (c) in Part III- (i) the heading "Supplemental provisions" shall be deemed to be omitted; and (ii) in section 11 to sub-section (1), the following proviso shall be deemed to be added, namely: "Provided that in a suit for accounts the value for purposes of jurisdiction as determined by the Court at any stage of the trial shall be final and shall not be liable to be contested in appeal or revision." Substituted for the words "East Punjab" by the Adaptation of Laws Order, 1950.
