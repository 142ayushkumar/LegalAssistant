Press and Registration of Books (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1942_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act to amend the Press and Registration of Books Act, 1867, in its application to the Punjab. It is hereby enacted as follows:-
Press and Registration of Books (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1942_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab This Act may be called the Press and Registration of Books (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1942.1 Received the assent of his Excellency the Governor General on the 31st December, 1942, and was first published in the Punjab Government Gazette, Part I, dated the 1st January, 1943.
Press and Registration of Books (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1942_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab Section 4 of the Press and Registration of Books Act, 1867 (hereinafter referred to as the said Act), shall be renumbered as sub-section (1) of Section 4 and to the section so re-numbered, the following sub-sections shall be deemed to be added, namely:- 1 [(2)] As often as a press for the printing of books and papers having ceased to function is restarted, a new declaration shall be necessary unless the cessation is due to non-compliance with an order under sub-section (3) of Section 3 or sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the 2 [Indian Press (Emergency Powers) Act, 1931,] or any other law for the time being in force. 3 [(4)] For the purposes of this Act a printing press shall be deemed to be have ceased to function if no books or papers are printed therein for a period of six consecutive months. Renumbered as sub-section (3) by Punjab Act No. 15 of 1957, Section 2. Now repealed by Central Act No. XVI of 1951. Renumbered as sub-section (4) by Punjab Act 15 of 1957, Section 2
