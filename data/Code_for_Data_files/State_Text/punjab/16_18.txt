Pepsu Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act further to amend the Pepsu Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act, 1955. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Sixty-fourth Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Pepsu Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab (1) This Act may be called the Pepsu Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Amendment) Act, 2013. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Pepsu Tenancy and Agricultural Lands (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In the Pepsu Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act, 1955, for section 7-B, the following section shall be substituted, namely:- "7-B. One time concession - The concession given under sections 7 and 7-A to the land owner, who is a Non-Resident Indian, shall be available only in respect of his ancestral property and the property, which has been purchased by him at least five years before from the date he files the ejectment application.". H. P. S. Mahal Secretary to Government of Punjab, Department of Legal and Legislative Affairs.
