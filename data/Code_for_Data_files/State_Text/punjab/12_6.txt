Land Acquisition (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1956_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act to amend the Land Acquisition Act, 1894, in its application to the State of Punjab. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Land Acquisition (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1956_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab (1) This Act may called the Land Acquisition (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1956.1 (2) It shall come into force at once Received the assent of the President on the 15th July, 1956, and was first published in the Punjab Government Gazette Extraordinary dated the 14th July, 1956
Land Acquisition (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1956_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In clause (b) of sub-section (1) of section 17 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894, as amended by Punjab Act II of 1954, after the words "labour colonies" the words "or houses for any other class of people" shall be inserted.
