Registration (Punjab Amendment) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act further to amend the Registration Act, 1908, in its application to the State of Punjab. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Fifty-one year of the Republic of India as follow:-
Registration (Punjab Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab (1) This Act may be called the Registration (Punjab Amendment) Act, 20001 . (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the President of India on the 20th November, 2000
Registration (Punjab Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In the Registration Act, 1908, in this application to the State of Punjab (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in Section 30,- (i) In sub-section (1), the brackets and figure (1) shall be omitted; and (ii) Sub-section (2) shall be omitted
Registration (Punjab Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 3--> State(s): Punjab In the principal Act, Section 67 shall be omitted.
