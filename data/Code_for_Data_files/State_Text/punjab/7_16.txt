Punjab General Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act further to amend the Punjab General Sales Tax Act, 1948. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Fifty-one Year of the Republic of India, as follows :-
Punjab General Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab (1) This Act may be called the Punjab General Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 2000. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Punjab General Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In the Punjab General Sales Tax Act, 1948, section 5-A shall be omitted.
