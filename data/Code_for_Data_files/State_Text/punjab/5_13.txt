East Punjab Holdings (Consolidation and Prevention of Fragmentation) Validation Act, 1969_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act to validate certain schemes made under the East Punjab Holdings (Consolidation and Prevention of Fragmentation) Act, 1948. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Twentieth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
East Punjab Holdings (Consolidation and Prevention of Fragmentation) Validation Act, 1969_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab This Act may be called the East Punjab Holdings (Consolidation and Prevention of Fragmentation) Validation Act, 1969.1 Received the assent of the President of India on the 7th June, 1969.
East Punjab Holdings (Consolidation and Prevention of Fragmentation) Validation Act, 1969_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab Notwithstanding anything contained in any judgment, decree or order of any court or other authority to the contrary, where, in any scheme made at any time before the commencement of this Validation Act, land had been assigned or reserved for a common purpose as defined in clause (bb) section 2 of the East Punjab Holdings (Consolidation and Prevention of Fragmentation) Act, 1948, in accordance with the executive instructions issued by the Government from time to time, such scheme shall be, and shall be deemed always to have been, valid and no such scheme shall be questioned merely on the ground that the scale for such assignment or reservation had not been laid down by rules framed under the said Act.
