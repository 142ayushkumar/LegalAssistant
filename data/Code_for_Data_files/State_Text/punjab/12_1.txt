Punjab Labour Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act further to amend the Punjab Labour Welfare Fund Act, 1965. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Sixty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Punjab Labour Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab (1) This Act may be called the Punjab Labour Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 2010. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Punjab Labour Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In the Punjab Labour Welfare Fund Act, 1965, in section 2, in clause (10), for the words "three years", the words "one year" shall be substituted. Harbans Singh Additional Secretary to Government of Punjab, Department of Legal and Legislative Affairs
