Punjab Registration (Validating) Act, 1973_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act to validate certain registrations and matters appurtenant thereto. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Twenty- fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Punjab Registration (Validating) Act, 1973_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab This Act may be called the Punjab Registration (Validating) Act, 1973.1 Received the assent of the Governor of Punjab on the 17th April, 1973 and was published in the Punjab Gazette. (Extra). Legislative Supplement, Part I dated April 19.1973.
Punjab Registration (Validating) Act, 1973_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab All acts performed by the General Assistant, Amritsar, puporting to act as the Sub-Registrar of Amritsar City during the period commencing from the 1st September, 1964, and ending with the 13th of February, 1973, shall be valid and deemed to have been validly performed as if the appointment of the aforesaid official had been duly made as Sub-Registrar under the Indian Registration Act, 1908 (Act XVI of 1908), for the purposes of the performance of such acts.
