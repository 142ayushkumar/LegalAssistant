Punjab Municipal Infrastructure Development Fund (Amendment) Act, 2014_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act further to amend the Punjab Municipal Infrastructure Development Fund Act, 2011. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Sixty-fifth Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Punjab Municipal Infrastructure Development Fund (Amendment) Act, 2014_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab (1) This Bill may be called the Punjab Municipal Infrastructure Development Fund (Amendment) Act, 2014. (2) It shall come into force with immediate effect.
Punjab Municipal Infrastructure Development Fund (Amendment) Act, 2014_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In the Punjab Municipal Infrastructure Development Fund Act, 2011, in section 4, in sub-section (1), after the words "financial institutions", the words "or State Government Undertakings" shall be added. H. P. S. Mahal Secretary to Government of Punjab, Department of Legal and Legislative Affairs.
