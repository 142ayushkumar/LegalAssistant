Punjab General Sales Tax (Haryana Amendment) Act, 1970_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act to amend the Punjab General Sales Tax Act, 1948. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Haryana in the Twenty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Punjab General Sales Tax (Haryana Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab (1) This Act may be called the Punjab General Sales Tax (Haryana Amendment) Act, 1970. (2) Section 2 shall come into force at once, clause (a) of section 3, section 4 and sub-clause (a) of clause (i) of section 5 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 14th day of November, 1967 and the remaining provisions of this Act shall be deemed to have come into force on the 19th November, 1969.
Punjab General Sales Tax (Haryana Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In section 3A of the Punjab General Sales Tax Act, 1948 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), - (a) after sub-section (1), the following new sub-section shall be inserted, namely :- "(1A) The headquarters of the Tribunal shall be at such place as the State Government may by notification specify."; (b) in sub-section (2), for clause (ii), the following clause shall be substituted, namely :- "(ii) a Commissioner of a Division or Secretary to the State Government in the rank of Commissioner; or"; and (c) in sub-section (9), for the words "procedure, including the place or places of its sitting,", the word "procedure" shall be substituted.
Punjab General Sales Tax (Haryana Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 3--> State(s): Punjab In section 5 of the principal Act, - (a) in the second proviso sub-section (1) and in sub-clause (vi) of clause (a) of sub-section (2), for the words, letters and figures "30th day of December, 1967" wherever occurring, the words, letters figures "13th day of November, 1967" shall be substituted; and (b) in sub-clause (ii) of clause (a) of sub-section (2), - (i) for the words and letters "other than the sales of goods specified in Schedules C and D", the words, brackets and letters "other than the sales of goods (not being cotton seeds) specified in Schedules C and D" shall be substituted; and (ii) for the words "or of goods specified in his certificate of registration", the words and brackets "or of goods (not being cotton seeds) specified in his certificate of registration" shall be substituted.
Punjab General Sales Tax (Haryana Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 4--> State(s): Punjab In sub-section (1) of section 11-AA of the principal Act, for the words, letters and figures "30th day of December, 1967", the words, letters and figures "14th day of November, 1967" shall be substituted.
Punjab General Sales Tax (Haryana Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 5--> State(s): Punjab In Schedule D to the principal Act, - (i) against Serial No. 1, - (a) in column 2, for the word "manufactured", the word "un-manufactured" shall be substituted; and (b) in entry (ii) of column 4, for the word "First", the word "Last" shall be substituted; and (ii) against Serial No. 2, for the entries in columns 3 and 4, the following entries shall be substituted, namely :- ----------------------------------------------------------------- 3 4 ----------------------------------------------------------------- (i) In the case of cotton seeds, Last sale within the State in all circumstances of Haryana by a dealer liable to pay tax under this Act. (ii) In the case of other oil-seeds, - (a) if imported by a dealer First sale within the State from outside the State of of Haryana by a dealer liable Haryana or otherwise to pay tax under this Act. received by him in the State of Haryana for sale; and (b) if purchased in the State First purchase within the of Haryana State of Haryana by a dealer liable to pay tax under this Act.". -----------------------------------------------------------------
Punjab General Sales Tax (Haryana Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 6--> State(s): Punjab (1) The Punjab General Sales Tax (Haryana Amendment) Ordinance, 1969 (Haryana Ordinance No. 11 of 1969), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding any such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act. The Statement of Objects and Reasons appended to the Punjab General Sales Tax (Haryana Amendment) Bill, 1970 (Haryana Government Gazette, Extraordinary, dated 2nd March, 1970, page 293.) runs as follows : "With a view to rectifying the errors which had crept in the parent Act as amended by Haryana Act No. 15 of 1969, and also to make a certain changes in the stage of taxation on cotton and cotton seeds for the convenience of the trade and administration, the Punjab General Sales Tax (Haryana Amendment) Ordinance was promulgated. This Bill seeks to convert the said Ordinance into an Act. It also seeks to empower the State Government to fix the headquarters of the Tribunal at such place as the State Government may by notification specify and make slight modification in the qualifications for appointment as Member of the Tribunal."
