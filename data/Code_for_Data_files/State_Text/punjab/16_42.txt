Provincial Small Cause Courts (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1966_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act to amend the Provincial Small Cause Courts Act, 1887, in its application on the State of Punjab. Be it enacted by the legislature of the State of Punjab in the Seventeenth Year of the Republic of India as follows In view of such amendment it is not considered necessary to retain sub- section (3) of section 15 which is being omitted.
Provincial Small Cause Courts (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab This Act may be called the Provincial Small Cause Courts (Punjab Amendment) Act, 19661 Received the assent of the Governor of Punjab on the 20th March, 1966, and was first published in the Punjab Government Gazette Extraordinary, dated the 23rd February, 1966.
Provincial Small Cause Courts (Punjab Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In section 15 of the Provincial Small Cause Courts Act, 1887, in its application to the State of Punjab - (1) In sub-section (2) for the words five hundred the words two thousand shall be substituted; and (2) sub-section (3) shall be omitted.
