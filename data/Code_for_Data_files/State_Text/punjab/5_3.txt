East Punjab Cement Control (Repealing) Act, 1962_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act to repeal the East Punjab Cement Control Act, 1948. Be it enacted by the legislature of the State of Punjab in the Thirteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:
East Punjab Cement Control (Repealing) Act, 1962_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab This Act may be called the East Punjab Cement short title. Control (Repealing) Act, 19621 Received the assent of Governor of Punjab on the 23rd May, 1962.
East Punjab Cement Control (Repealing) Act, 1962_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab On such day as the State Government may, by Repeal of Punjab Act notification in the Official Gazette, specify in this XXXIX of behalf, the East Punjab Cement Control Act, 1948, 1948, shall stand re-appealed.
