Punjab General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section Preamble--> State(s): Punjab An Act further to amend the Punjab General Sales Tax Act, 1948. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Punjab in the Forty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Punjab General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 1--> State(s): Punjab (1) This Act may be called the Punjab General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1994. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Punjab General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 2--> State(s): Punjab In the Punjab General Sales Tax Act, 1948 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in section 5, in sun-section (2), in clause (a), in sub-clause (ii), in the first proviso, for the words "such sales", the words "such sales other than those made on commission basis by a commission agent to the registered dealer" shall be substituted.
Punjab General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 3--> State(s): Punjab In the principal Act, in section 11-D, in sub-section (3), in clause (i), the words "any any amounts less than one hundred rupees as one hundred rupees" shall be omitted.
Punjab General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 4--> State(s): Punjab (1) The Punjab General Sales Tax (Amendment) Ordinance, 1994 (Punjab Ordinance No. 5 of 1994), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the Ordinance referred to in sub-section (1), shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
