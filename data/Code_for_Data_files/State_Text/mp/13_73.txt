Madhya Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Mandal (Pathya Vivaranon Tatha Pathya Pustakon Ka Vihit Kiya Jana) Act, 1974_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh 1 An Act to lay down syllabi and to prescribe text books according to such syllabi for language subjects for examinations conducted by the Board of Secondary Education. Be it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the Twenty-fifth year of the Republic of India as follows :- Received the assent of the Governor on 29-3-1974; assent first published in the Madhya Pradesh Gazette (Extraordinary), dated 30-3-1974, dated 30-3-1974 at pp. 609-610.
Madhya Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Mandal (Pathya Vivaranon Tatha Pathya Pustakon Ka Vihit Kiya Jana) Act, 1974_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Madhya Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Mandal (Pathya Vivaranon Tatha Pathya Pustakon Ka Vihit Kiya Jana) Adhiniyam, 1974. (2) It extends to the whole of Madhya Pradesh. (3) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 20th April, 1959.
Madhya Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Mandal (Pathya Vivaranon Tatha Pathya Pustakon Ka Vihit Kiya Jana) Act, 1974_Section 2--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In this Act, unless the context otherwise requires :- (a) "Act of 1973" means the Madhya Pradesh Middle School Tatha Madhyamik Shiksha (Pathya Pustakon Sambandhi Vyavastha) Adhiniyam, 1973 (No. 13 of 1973); (b) "language subject" means English, Hindi, Marathi, Urdu, Gujarati, Telugu, Tamil, Sindhi, Punjabi, Sanskrit, Bengali, Kannad, Malayalam, Persian, Arabic or French; (c) "Prospectus" means the prospectus by the Board till the commencement of the Act of 1973 or any examination conducted by the Board; (d) "relevant year" means the year of examination tor which syllabi is contained in the prospectus; (c) "syllabi" means a document or a part thereof containing the courses of instructions in language subjects for each standard of secondary education; (f) expressions used in this Act but not defined shall have the meanings respectively assigned to them in the Act of 1973.
Madhya Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Mandal (Pathya Vivaranon Tatha Pathya Pustakon Ka Vihit Kiya Jana) Act, 1974_Section 3--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh Prescription of syllabi for language subjects and text books therefor. It is hereby enacted that the syllabi for language subjects and text books therefor shall be as specified in the prospectus for the relevant year and it shall and shall always be deemed that the syllabi and text books were in force from the date of commencement of this Act and accordingly on the commencement of the Act of 1973, the syllabi and text books in force in language subjects immediately before such commencement shall be, deemed to be laid down and continued under Sections 3 and 4 of the Act of 1973, notwithstanding any judgment, decree or order of any Court or anything contained in any other law for the time being in force to the contrary.
Madhya Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Mandal (Pathya Vivaranon Tatha Pathya Pustakon Ka Vihit Kiya Jana) Act, 1974_Section 4--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh For removal of doubt it is hereby declared that all examinations conducted by the Board on the basis of the syllabi and text books laid down or prescribed under Section 3 and certificates or diplomas granted on the basis of the result of such examinations shall be and shall always be deemed to be validly conducted and granted as if Section 3 were in force at all material times when such examinations were conducted and certificates and diplomas were granted and the validity of such examinations or certificates and diplomas shall not be called in question in any Court of law merely on the ground that no syllabi was laid down or text books were prescribed for such examinations.
