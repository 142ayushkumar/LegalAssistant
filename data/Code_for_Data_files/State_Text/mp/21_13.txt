Madhya Pradesh Vas-sthan Dakhalkar (Bhumiswami Adhikaron Ka Pradan Kiya Jana) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2012_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh 1 An Act further to amend the Madhya Pradesh Vas-sthan Dakhalkar (Bhumiswami Adhikaraon Ka Pradan Kiya Jana) Adhiniyam, 1980. Be it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the Sixty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:- The following Act of the Madhya Pradesh Legislature received the assent of the Governor on 25th April, 2012 and was published in the Madhya Pradesh Gazette, Extra-ordinary, dated 30th April, 2012.
Madhya Pradesh Vas-sthan Dakhalkar (Bhumiswami Adhikaron Ka Pradan Kiya Jana) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2012_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh This Act may be called the Madhya Pradesh Vas-sthan Dakhalkar (Bhumiswami Adhikaron Ka Pradan Kiya Jana) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2012.
Madhya Pradesh Vas-sthan Dakhalkar (Bhumiswami Adhikaron Ka Pradan Kiya Jana) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2012_Section 2--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In Section 2 of the Madhya Pradesh Vas-sthan Dakhalkar (Bhumiswami Adhikaron Ka Pradan Kiya Jana) Adhiniyam, 1980 (No. 4 of 1980) (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act), for the figures and word "14th April, 2003", the figures and words "31st December, 2011" shall be substituted.
Madhya Pradesh Vas-sthan Dakhalkar (Bhumiswami Adhikaron Ka Pradan Kiya Jana) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2012_Section 3--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In Section 3 of the Principal Act, in clauses (ii) and (iii), for the figures and word "14th April, 2003", the figures and word "31st December, 2011" shall be substituted.
Madhya Pradesh Vas-sthan Dakhalkar (Bhumiswami Adhikaron Ka Pradan Kiya Jana) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2012_Section 4--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the Principal Act, for the figures and word "14th April, 2003", the figures and word "31st December, 2011" shall be substituted.
