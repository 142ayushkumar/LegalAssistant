Madhya Pradesh Vidhan Sabha Sadasya Vetan, Bhatta Tatha Pension (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh 1 An Act further to amend the Madhya Pradesh Vidhan Sabha Sadasya Vetan, Bhatta Tatha Pension Adhiniyam, 1972. Be it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the Sixty second Year of the Republic of India as follows:- The following Act of the Madhya Pradesh Legislature received the assent of the Governor on 23rd April, 2011 and was published in the Madhya Pradesh Gazette, Extra-ordinary, dated 27th April, 2011.
Madhya Pradesh Vidhan Sabha Sadasya Vetan, Bhatta Tatha Pension (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh (1) This Act may be called Madhya Pradesh Vidhan Sabha Sadasya Vetan, Bhatta Tatha Pension (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2011. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force from the 27th April, 2010.
Madhya Pradesh Vidhan Sabha Sadasya Vetan, Bhatta Tatha Pension (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh For Section 5 of the Madhya Pradesh Vidhan Sabha Sadasya Vetan, Bhatta Tatha Pension Adhiniyam, 1972 (No. 7 of 1973), the following Section shall be substituted, namely:- "5. Bus Travelling Allowance:- There shall be paid to every member and every person entitled to pension under section 6-A Bus Travelling Allowance at the rate of five hundred rupees per mensem.".
