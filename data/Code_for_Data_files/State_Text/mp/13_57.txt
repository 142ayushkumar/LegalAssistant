Madhya Pradesh Rajkoshiya Uttardayitva Avam Budget Prabandhan (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2017_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh An Act further to amend the Madhya Pradesh Rajkoshiya Uttardayitva Avam Budget Prabandhan Adhiniyam, 2005. Be it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the Sixty-Eighth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Madhya Pradesh Rajkoshiya Uttardayitva Avam Budget Prabandhan (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2017_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh This Act may be called the Madhya Pradesh Rajkoshiya Uttardayitva Avam Budget Prabandhan (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2017.
Madhya Pradesh Rajkoshiya Uttardayitva Avam Budget Prabandhan (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2017_Section 2--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In Section 9 of the Madhya Pradesh Rajkoshiya Uttardayitva Avam Budget Prabandhan Adhiniyam, 2005 (No. 18 of 2005), after sub-section (2), the following new sub-section shall be added, namely:- "(3) Notwithstanding any limit contained in clause (b) of sub-section (2), for the financial year ending 31st March, 2017, the borrowings under Ujwal Discom Assurance Yojana (UDAY), for financial reconstruction of the Companies of the Energy Department, shall not be reckoned against the normal permissible net borrowing ceiling of the State.".
