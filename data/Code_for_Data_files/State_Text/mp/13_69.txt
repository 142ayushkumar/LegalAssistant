Madhya Pradesh Vritti Kar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2016_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh An Act Further to amend the Madhya Pradesh Vritti Kar Adhiniyam, 1995. Be it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the Sixty-seventh year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Madhya Pradesh Vritti Kar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2016_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Madhya Pradesh Vritti Kar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2016. (2) It shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Madhya Pradesh Gazette.
Madhya Pradesh Vritti Kar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2016_Section 2--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In Section 8 of the Madhya Pradesh Vritti Kar Adhiniyam, 1995 (No. 16 of 1995), in sub-section (3), for the words "within sixty days", the words "within one working day" shall be substituted.
