Madhya Pradesh Appropriation (No. 5) Act, 2016_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh An Act to Provide for the authorization of appropriation of money out of the Consolidated Fund of the State of Madhya Pradesh to meet the amounts spent on certain services during the financial year ended on the 31st day of the March, 2010 in excess of the amounts granted for those services and for that year. Be it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the Sixty-Seventh year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Madhya Pradesh Appropriation (No. 5) Act, 2016_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh This Act may be called the Madhya Pradesh Appropriation (No. 5) Act, 2016.
Madhya Pradesh Appropriation (No. 5) Act, 2016_Section 2--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh From and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State of Madhya Pradesh the sums specified in column (3) of the Schedule amounting in the aggregate to the sum of Rs. One Hundred Twenty Three Crores Ninety Five Lakhs Thirty Two Thousands Five Hundred Fourty Rupees shall be deemed to have been authorised to be paid and applied to meet the amount. Spent on defraying the charges in respect of the services specified in column (2) of the said Schedule during the Financial Year ended on the 31st day of March, 2010 in excess of the amounts granted for those services and that year.
Madhya Pradesh Appropriation (No. 5) Act, 2016_Section 3--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh The sums deemed to have been authorized to be paid and applied from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State of Madhya Pradesh under this Act Shall be deemed to have been appropriated for the services and purposes expressed in the Schedule in relation to the financial year ended on the 31st day of March, 2010.
