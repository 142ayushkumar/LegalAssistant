Madhya Pradesh Gramo Me Ki Dakhalrahit Bhoomi (Vishesh Upabandh) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2017_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh An Act further to amend the Madhya Pradesh Gramo Me Ki Dakhalrahit Bhoomi (Vishesh Upabandh), Adhiniyam, 1970. Be it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the sixty-eighth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Madhya Pradesh Gramo Me Ki Dakhalrahit Bhoomi (Vishesh Upabandh) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2017_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh This Act may be called the Madhya Pradesh Gramo Me Ki Dakhalrahit Bhoomi (Vishesh Upabandh) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2017.
Madhya Pradesh Gramo Me Ki Dakhalrahit Bhoomi (Vishesh Upabandh) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2017_Section 2--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In Section 3 of the Madhya Pradesh Gramo Me Ki Dakhalrahit Bhoomi (Vishesh Upabandh) Adhiniyam, 1970 (No. 26 of 1970) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for the figures, letters and words "31st day of December, 2011", the figures, letters and words "31st day of December, 2014", shall be substituted.
Madhya Pradesh Gramo Me Ki Dakhalrahit Bhoomi (Vishesh Upabandh) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2017_Section 3--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In Section 5 of the principal Act, for the figures, letters and words "31st day of December, 2011", the figures, letters and words "31st day of December, 2014", shall be substituted.
