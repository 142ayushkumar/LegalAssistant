Madhya Pradesh Evacuee Interest (Separation) Supplementary Act, 1960_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh 1 An Act further to supplement certain provisions of the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Act, 1951, in their application to the State of Madhya Pradesh. Be it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the Eleventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:- Received the assent of the Governor on the 4th November, 1960: assent first published in the Madhya Pradesh Gazette on the 11th November, 1960
Madhya Pradesh Evacuee Interest (Separation) Supplementary Act, 1960_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Madhya Pradesh Evacuee Interest (Separation) Supplementary Act, 1960. (2) It shall extend to the whole of Madhya Pradesh.
Madhya Pradesh Evacuee Interest (Separation) Supplementary Act, 1960_Section 2--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh The Evacuee Interest (Separation) Amendment Act, 1960 (No. 27 of 1960), shall, in so far as it relates to any matter enumerated in List II of the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution, be as valid in the State of Madhya Pradesh as if the provisions contained therein had been enacted by the Legislature of the State.
Madhya Pradesh Evacuee Interest (Separation) Supplementary Act, 1960_Section 3--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh The Madhya Pradesh Evacuee Interest (Separation) Supplementary Ordinance, 1960 (5 of 1960), is hereby repealed.
