Madhya Pradesh Anugrahik Tatha Sevarth Nagad Anudan Sampati (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1984_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh 1 An Act further to amend the Madhya Pradesh Anugrahik Tatha Sevarth Nagad Anudan Samapti Adhiniyam, 1982. Be it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the Thirty fifth year of the Republic of India as follows :- Received the assent of the Governor on the 19th November, 1984; assent first published in the Madhya Pradesh Gazette (Extraordinary), dated the 19th November, 1984.
Madhya Pradesh Anugrahik Tatha Sevarth Nagad Anudan Sampati (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1984_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh This Act may be called The Madhya Pradesh Anugrahik Tatha Sevarth Nagad Anudan Sampati (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1984.

Madhya Pradesh Anugrahik Tatha Sevarth Nagad Anudan Sampati (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1984_Section 3--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh Amendments incorporated in the Principal Act.
Madhya Pradesh Anugrahik Tatha Sevarth Nagad Anudan Sampati (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1984_Section 4--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh The statement referred to in Section 5 of the Madhya Pradesh Anugrahik Tatha Sevarth Nagad Anudan Samapti (Sanshodhan) Adhyadesh, 1984 (No. 9 of 1984) shall be dealt with and disposed of by the appropriate competent authority as if it was a statement filed before it in pursuance of a notice served on grantee in accordance with the provisions of sub-section (1) of Section 6 of the Principal Act as amended by Section 3 of this Act.
Madhya Pradesh Anugrahik Tatha Sevarth Nagad Anudan Sampati (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1984_Section 5--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh The Madhya Pradesh Anugrahik Tatha Sevarth Nagad Anudan Samapti (Sanshodhan) Adhyadesh, 1984 (No. 9 of 1984) is hereby repealed.
