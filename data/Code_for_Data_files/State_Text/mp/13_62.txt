Madhya Pradesh Vas-Sthan Dakhalkar (Bhumiswami Adhikaron Ka Pradan Kiya Jana) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2017_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh An Act further to amend the Madhya Pradesh Vas-sthan Dakhalkar (Bhumiswami Adhikaron Ka Pradan Kiya Jana) Adhiniyam, 1980. Be it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the Sixty-eighth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Madhya Pradesh Vas-Sthan Dakhalkar (Bhumiswami Adhikaron Ka Pradan Kiya Jana) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2017_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh This Act may be called the Madhya Pradesh Vas-Sthan Dakhalkar (Bhumiswami Adhikaron Ka Pradan Kiya Jana) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2017.
Madhya Pradesh Vas-Sthan Dakhalkar (Bhumiswami Adhikaron Ka Pradan Kiya Jana) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2017_Section 2--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In Section 2 of the Madhya Pradesh Vas-sthan Dakhalkar (Bhumiswami Adhikaron Ka Pradan Kiya Jana) Adhiniyam, 1980 (No. 4 of 1980) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in clause (d), for the figures and word "31st December, 2011", the figures and word "31st December, 2014", shall be substituted.
Madhya Pradesh Vas-Sthan Dakhalkar (Bhumiswami Adhikaron Ka Pradan Kiya Jana) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2017_Section 3--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In Section 3 of the principal Act, in clause (ii) and (iii), for the figures and word "31st December, 2011", the figures and word "31st December, 2014", shall be substituted.
Madhya Pradesh Vas-Sthan Dakhalkar (Bhumiswami Adhikaron Ka Pradan Kiya Jana) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2017_Section 4--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In Section 4 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), for the figures and word "31st December, 2011", the figures and word "31st December, 2014", shall be substituted.
