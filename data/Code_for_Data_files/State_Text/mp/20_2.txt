Madhya Pradesh Uchcha Nyayalaya (Khand Nyaypeeth Ko Appeal) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh An Act to amend the Madhya Pradesh Uchcha Nyayalaya (Khand Nyapeeth Ko Appeal) Adhiniyam, 2005. Be it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the sixty-fourth year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Madhya Pradesh Uchcha Nyayalaya (Khand Nyaypeeth Ko Appeal) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh This Act may be called the.1 Received the assent of the Governor on 16th April, 2013 and was published in the Madhya Pradesh Gazette, Extra-ordinary, dated 17th April, 2013.
Madhya Pradesh Uchcha Nyayalaya (Khand Nyaypeeth Ko Appeal) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In Section 2 of the Madhya Pradesh Uchcha Nyayalay (Khand Nyapeeth Ko Appeal) Adhiniyam, 2005 (No. 14 of 2006), in sub-section (2), for the figure and word "45 days", wherever they occur, the figure and word "90 days" shall be substituted.
