Madhya Pradesh Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2008_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh An Act further to amend the Madhya Pradesh VAT Act, 2002. BE it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the Fifty-ninth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Madhya Pradesh Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2008_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh This Act may be called the Madhya Pradesh VAT (Amendment) Act, 2008.
Madhya Pradesh Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2008_Section 2--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In section 57 of the Madhya Pradesh VAT Act, 2002 (No. 20 of 2002) in sub-section (17), for the words "five times", the words "three times", shall be substituted.
Madhya Pradesh Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2008_Section 3--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh (1) The Madhya Pradesh VAT (Amendment) Ordinance, 2008 (No. 1 of 2008) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding the repeal of the said Ordinance, anything done or any action taken under the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the corresponding provision of this Act.
