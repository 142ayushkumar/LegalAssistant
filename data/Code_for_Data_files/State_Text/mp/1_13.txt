Madhya Pradesh Agrakraya Vidhi Nirasan Adhiniyam, 1968_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh 1 An Act to repeal the Kanoon Hakshafa Riyasat Gwalior, Samvat 1992, the Bhopal Pre-emption Act, 1934 and the Rewa State Pre-emption Act, 1946 Be it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the Nineteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows :- Received the assent of the President on the 19th June, 1968; assent first published in the Madhaya Pradesh Gazette, Extraordinary dated the 28th June, 1968.
Madhya Pradesh Agrakraya Vidhi Nirasan Adhiniyam, 1968_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh This Act may be called The Madhya Pradesh Agrakraya Vidhi Nirasan Adhiniyam, 1968.
Madhya Pradesh Agrakraya Vidhi Nirasan Adhiniyam, 1968_Section 2--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh The Kanoon Hakshafa Riyasat Gwalior, Samvat 1992, the Bhopal Pre-emption Act, 1934 (III of 1934) and the Rewa State Pre-empton Act, 1946 are hereby repealed.
