Madhya Pradesh Nagariya Kshetron Ke Bhoomihin Vyakti (Pattadhriti Adhikaron Ka Pradan Kiya Jana) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2017_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh An Act further to amend the Madhya Pradesh Nagariya Kshetron Ke Bhoomihin Vyakti (Pattadhriti Adhikaron Ka Pradan Kiya Jana) Adhiniyam, 1984. Be it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the sixty-eighth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Madhya Pradesh Nagariya Kshetron Ke Bhoomihin Vyakti (Pattadhriti Adhikaron Ka Pradan Kiya Jana) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2017_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh This Act may be called the Madhya Pradesh Nagariya Kshetron Ke Bhoomihin Vyakti (Pattadhriti Adhikaron Ka Pradan Kiya Jana) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2017.
Madhya Pradesh Nagariya Kshetron Ke Bhoomihin Vyakti (Pattadhriti Adhikaron Ka Pradan Kiya Jana) Sanshodhan Adhiniyam, 2017_Section 2--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In Section 3 of the Madhya Pradesh Nagariya Kshetron Ke Bhoomihin Vyakti (Pattadhriti Adhikaron Ka Pradan Kiya Jana) Adhiniyam, 1984 (No. 15 of 1984) (hereinafter reffered to as the principal Act),- (i) in sub-section (1), for the figures and words "31st day of December, 2012", the figures and words "31st day of December, 2014" shall be substituted; (ii) in sub-section (2), for the figures and words "31st December, 2012" wherever they occur, the figures and words "31st December, 2014" shall be substituted.
