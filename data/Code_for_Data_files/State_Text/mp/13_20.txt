Madhya Pradesh Appropriation (No. 3) Act, 2016_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh An Act to authorise payment and appropriation of certain further sums from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State of Madhya Pradesh for the services of the Financial Year 2016-2017. Be it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the Sixty-Seventh year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Madhya Pradesh Appropriation (No. 3) Act, 2016_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh This Act may be called the Madhya Pradesh Appropriation (No. 3) Act, 2016.
Madhya Pradesh Appropriation (No. 3) Act, 2016_Section 2--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh From and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State of Madhya Pradesh there may be paid and applied sums not exceeding those specified in column (3) of the Schedule amounting in the aggregate to the sums of ? Fourteen Thousand Three Hundred Sixty Seven Crore Fifty Four Lakh Twenty Four Thousand Nine Hundred only towards defraying the several charges which will come in the course of payment during the Financial Year 2016-2017 in respect of services and purposes specified in column (2) of the Schedule.
Madhya Pradesh Appropriation (No. 3) Act, 2016_Section 3--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh The sums authorised to be paid and applied from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State of Madhya Pradesh by this Act, Shall be appropriated for the services and purposes expressed in the Schedule in relation to the said year.
