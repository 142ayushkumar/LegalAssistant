Madhya Pradesh Upkar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2016_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh An Act Further to amend the Madhya Pradesh Upkar Adhiniyam, 1981. Be it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the sixty-seventh year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Madhya Pradesh Upkar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2016_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Madhya Pradesh Upkar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2016. (2) It shall come into force from 1st April, 2016.
Madhya Pradesh Upkar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2016_Section 2--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In Section 9 of the Madhya Pradesh Upkar Adhiniyam, 1981 (No. 1 of 1982), (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for sub-section (6), the following sub-section shall be substituted namely:- "(6) The proceeds of the cess shall be used for rural development specially for maintenance of infrastructure of schools in rural areas.".
Madhya Pradesh Upkar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2016_Section 3--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In Schedule to the principal Act, against serial number 1, in column (4), for the figure "2.5", the figure "10" shall be substituted.
