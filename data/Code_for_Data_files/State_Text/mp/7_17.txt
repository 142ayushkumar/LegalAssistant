Madhya Pradesh Gram Swachhta Tatha Lok Prabandh Nirsan Adhiniyam, 1965_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh As Act to repeal The Madhya Pradesh Village Sanitation and Public Management Act, 1920 Be it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the Sixteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Madhya Pradesh Gram Swachhta Tatha Lok Prabandh Nirsan Adhiniyam, 1965_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Madhya Pradesh Gram Swachhta Tatha Lok Prabandh Nirsan Adhiniyam, 1965.1 (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint. Received the assent of the Government on the 08-10-1965.
Madhya Pradesh Gram Swachhta Tatha Lok Prabandh Nirsan Adhiniyam, 1965_Section 2--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh The Madhya Pradesh Village Sanitation and Public Management Act, 1920 (No. 11 of 1920), is hereby repealed.
