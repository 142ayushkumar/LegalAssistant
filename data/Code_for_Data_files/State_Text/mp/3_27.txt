Madhya Pradesh Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh 1 An Act further to amend the Madhya Pradesh Contingency Fund Act, 1957. Be it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the Sixty second Year of the Republic of India as follows:- The following Act of the Madhya Pradesh Legislature received the assent of the Governor on 8th August, 2011 and was published in the Madhya Pradesh Gazette, Extra-ordinary, dated 12th August, 2011.
Madhya Pradesh Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh This Act may be called Madhya Pradesh Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 2011.
Madhya Pradesh Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In Section 3 of the Madhya Pradesh Contingency Fund Act, 1957 (No. 7 of 1957) for the words "one hundred crores rupees", the words "two hundred crores rupees" shall be substituted.
