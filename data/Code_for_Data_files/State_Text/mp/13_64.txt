Madhya Pradesh Vat (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh An Act further to amend the Madhya Pradesh Vat Act, 2002. Be it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the Sixty-eighth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Madhya Pradesh Vat (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Madhya Pradesh Vat (Amendment), Act 2017. (2) It shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Madhya Pradesh Gazette.
Madhya Pradesh Vat (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 2--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In Section 34 of the Madhya Pradesh Vat Act, 2002 (No. 20 of 2002) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (2), the word "registered" shall be omitted.
Madhya Pradesh Vat (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 3--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In Section 46 of the principal Act,- (a) in sub-section (8),- (i) In clause (a), for the words "twelve calendar months", the words "twenty four calendar months" shall be substituted; (ii) in clause (b), for the words "The Appellate Board shall dispose of every appeal within two calendar years from the date of filing of appeal", the words "The Appellate Board shall make an endeavor to pronounce its order in writing within one calendar year from the date of filing of appeal" shall be substituted; (b) sub-section (8B) shall be omitted; (c) in sub-section (9), for the words "twelve calendar months", the words "twenty four calendar months" shall be substituted.
