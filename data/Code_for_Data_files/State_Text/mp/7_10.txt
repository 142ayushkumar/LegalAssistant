Madhya Pradesh General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1983_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh An Act further to amend the Madhya Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1958. BE it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the thirty-fourth year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Madhya Pradesh General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh This Act may be called the Madhya Pradesh General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1983.
Madhya Pradesh General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 2--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In sub-section (1) of section 7-AA of the Madhya Pradesh General Sales Tax Act, 1958 (No. 2 of 1959) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), - (a) the words, figures and letter "As from the 1st day of April, 1983" shall be omitted; and (b) for the word "five", the word "seven" shall be substituted.
Madhya Pradesh General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 3--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In clause (c) of sub-section (1) of section 8 of the principal Act, for the word "five", the word "seven", shall be substituted.
Madhya Pradesh General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 4--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh The Madhya Pradesh General Sales Tax (Amendment) Ordinance, 1983 (No. 6 of 1983) (See (1983) 54 STC Statutes 101.) is hereby repealed.
