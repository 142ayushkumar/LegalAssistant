Madhya Pradesh Vilasita, Manoranjan, Amod Evam Vigyapan Kar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2012_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh An Act to amend the Madhya Pradesh Vilasita, Manoranjan, Amod Evam Vigyapan Kar Adhiniyam, 2011 . Be it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the Sixty Third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Madhya Pradesh Vilasita, Manoranjan, Amod Evam Vigyapan Kar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2012_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Madhya Pradesh Vilasita, Manoranjan, Amod Evam Vigyapan Kar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2012. (2) (a) The provisions of Section 2 of this amending Act shall be deemed to have come into force from 1st April, 2012. (b) The remaining provisions the this amending Act shall come into force from 1st April, 2012.
Madhya Pradesh Vilasita, Manoranjan, Amod Evam Vigyapan Kar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2012_Section 2--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In Section 2 of the Madhya Pradesh Vilasita, Manoranjan, Amod Evam Vigyapan Kar Adhiniyam, 2011 (No. 11 of 2011) (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act), in sub-section (1) for clause (1), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:- "(1) "marriage hall" includes lawn, garden a building or part of a building where accommodation or space is provided for more than one hundred persons, for any event including seminar or convention or banquet or meeting or exhibition-cum-sale;".
Madhya Pradesh Vilasita, Manoranjan, Amod Evam Vigyapan Kar (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2012_Section 3--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In Section 3 of the Principal Act, in sub-section (2), for the words "rupees five lacs", the words "rupees ten lacs" shall be substituted.
