Madhya Pradesh Rajya Vitta Ayog (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2017_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh An Act further to amend the Madhya Pradesh Rajya Vitta Ayog Adhiniyam, 1994. Be it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the sixty-eighth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Madhya Pradesh Rajya Vitta Ayog (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2017_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh This Act may be called the Madhya Pradesh Rajya Vitta Ayog (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2017.
Madhya Pradesh Rajya Vitta Ayog (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2017_Section 2--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In section 3 of the Madhya Pradesh Rajya Vitta Ayog Adhiniyam, 1994 (No. 3 of 1994), (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act),- (i) for the words "four other members", the words "upto four other members" shall be substituted; (ii) for full stop, colon shall be substituted and thereafter the following proviso shall be added, namely:- "Provided that no act or proceeding of the Commission shall be invalidated merely by reason of any vacancy in, or any defect in the constitution of, the Commission.".
Madhya Pradesh Rajya Vitta Ayog (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2017_Section 3--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In Section 4 of the principal Act, the word "four" shall be omitted.
