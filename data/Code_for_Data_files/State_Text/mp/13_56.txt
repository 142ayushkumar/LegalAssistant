Madhya Pradesh Panchayat Raj Avam Gram Swaraj (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2016_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh An Act further to amend the Madhya Pradesh Panchayat Raj Avam Gram Swaraj Adhiniyam, 1993. Be it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the Sixty-seventh Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Madhya Pradesh Panchayat Raj Avam Gram Swaraj (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2016_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Madhya Pradesh Panchayat Raj Avam Gram Swaraj (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2016. (2) It shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Madhya Pradesh Gazette.
Madhya Pradesh Panchayat Raj Avam Gram Swaraj (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2016_Section 2--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh In Section 36 of the Madhya Pradesh Panchayat Raj Avam Gram Swaraj Adhiniyam, 1993 (No. 1 of 1994), in sub-section (1), after clause (cd), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(ce) does not have flush latrine or pour flush latrine in his residential premises; or".
