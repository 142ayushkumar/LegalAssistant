Madhya Pradesh Vidhan Mandal Sadasya Nirhata Nivaran (Sanshodhan) Ahiniyam, 2017_Section Preamble--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh An Act further to amend the Madhya Pradesh Vidhan Mandal Sadasya Nirhata Nivaran Adhiniyam, 1967. Be it enacted by the Madhya Pradesh Legislature in the sixty-eighth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Madhya Pradesh Vidhan Mandal Sadasya Nirhata Nivaran (Sanshodhan) Ahiniyam, 2017_Section 1--> State(s): Madhya Pradesh This Act may be called the Madhya Pradesh Vidhan Mandal Sadasya Nirhata Nivaran (Sanshodhan) Ahiniyam, 2017.
