Mizoram University (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section Preamble--> State(s): Mizoram An Act to amend the Mizoram University Act, 2000. BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Mizoram University (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 1--> State(s): Mizoram This Act may be called the Mizoram University (Amendment) Act, 2007.
Mizoram University (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 2--> State(s): Mizoram In the Mizoram University Act, 2000 , after section 9, the following section shall be inserted, namely:- "9A.The Chief Rector:-The Governor of the State of Mizoram shall be the Chief Rector of the University." K.N.CHATURVEDI, Secy, to the Govt, of India.
