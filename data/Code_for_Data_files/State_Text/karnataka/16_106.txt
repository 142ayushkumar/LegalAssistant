Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1973_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) Act, 1959 in its application to the State of Karnataka. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) Act, 1959 (Central Act 29 of 1959), in its application to the State of Karnataka; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-fourth Year of Republic of India as follows:-
Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1973_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 19731 . (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Karnataka. (3) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of January, 1971. First published in the Karnataka Gazette on the Seventh day of June, 1973, Received the assent of the President on the Thirtieth day of May, 1973
Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1973_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) Act, 1959 (Central Act 29 of 1959), for the words, figures and letters "the 31st day of December, 1970", the words, figures and letters "the 31st day of December, 1975" shall be substituted.
