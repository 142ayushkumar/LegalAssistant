Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods Into Local Areas for Consumption, Use Or Sale Therein (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, Use or Sale Therein Act, 1979. Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, Use or Sale WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Tax on Therein Act, 1979, (Karnataka Act 27 of 1979) for the purpose hereinafter appearing; BE it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods Into Local Areas for Consumption, Use Or Sale Therein (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, Use of Sale Therein (Amendment) Act, 1987. (2) It shall come into force on the first day of April, 1987.
Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods Into Local Areas for Consumption, Use Or Sale Therein (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In section 2 of the Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Area for Consumption, Use or Sale Therein Act, 1979 (Karnataka Act 27 of 1979) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in clause (A), for sub-clause (9), the following sub-clause shall be substituted, namely :- "(9) 'year' means the year commencing on the first day of April.".
Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods Into Local Areas for Consumption, Use Or Sale Therein (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In section 3 of the principal Act, after sub-section (1), the following proviso shall be inserted, namely :- "Provided that no tax shall be leviable on 'gunny bags' falling under item (v) of entry 16A of the Schedule for the period from the first day of April, 1983 to the twenty-seventh day of October, 1986.".
Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods Into Local Areas for Consumption, Use Or Sale Therein (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka After section 30 of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely :- "30A. Assessment to tax in certain cases- Notwithstanding anything contained in this Act, every registered dealer and every dealer liable to get himself registered under this Act whose assessment year commenced on a date after the first day of April, 1986 shall complete his accounts and close them of thirty-first day of March, 1987. He shall be assessed to the period ending thirty-first March, 1987 in accordance with the procedure laid down in section 5 of this Act.".
Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods Into Local Areas for Consumption, Use Or Sale Therein (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In the Schedule to the principal Act, in explanation II, for the words "Agricultural produce" the words "Agricultural produce including cotton both ginned and unginned" shall be and shall be deemed to have been substituted with effect from the first day of April, 1983.
