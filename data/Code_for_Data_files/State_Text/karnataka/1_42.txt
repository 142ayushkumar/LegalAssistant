Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1970_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1966. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1966 (Karnataka Act 27 of 1966); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1970.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Seventeenth day of October, 1969. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-first day of February, 1970
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1966 (Karnataka Act 27 of 1966) (hereinafter referred to as the " Principal Act") for clause (2), the following clause shall be substituted, namely - "(2) "Agriculturist" means a person cultivating any agricultural land, who is an occupant of such land, or a tenant of an occupant; Explanation -For purposes of this clause, the expressions occupant and tenant shall have the meaning assigned to them in the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964);".
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In clause (VI) of sub-section (1) of Section 10 of the principal Act, for the words "the Taluk Marketing Society", the words "a Taluk Marketing Society" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 11 of the principal Act - (1) in clause (iv), for the words "the Taluk Marketing Co-operative Society", the words "a Taluk Marketing Society" shall be substituted; (2) for the first proviso, the following proviso shall be substituted, namely - "Provided that if there is more than one Taluk Marketing Society of the class specified in clause (iv) or more than one Producers Society of the class specified in clause (v), a representative of each class of such societies, who is not disqualified under Section 16, elected by the members of the committees of management of the respective class of societies, shall be the member of the market committee"; (3) in the last proviso to clauses (iv) and (v), the words "functioning in the market area", where they occur for the first time, shall be omitted; (4) in the Explanation to the sub-section, the words "first or" shall be omitted.
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In the proviso to sub-section (1) of Section 13 of the principal Act, for the word "yard", the words "market yard" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka (1) (a) Section 19 of the principal Act shall be re-numbered as sub-section (1) of that section and the existing sub-sections (1), (2) and (3) of the said section shall be re-numbered as clauses (i), (ii) and (iii) respectively of the said sub-section (1); (b) in clause (iii) as so re-numbered, for the word, brackets and figure "sub-section (1)", the word, brackets and figure "clause (i)" shall be substituted; (c) in the Explanation, for the words "this section", the words "this sub-section" shall be substituted. (2) After sub-section (1) of the said Section 19, as so re-numbered, the following sub-sections shall be added, namely - "(2) No person shall be a member of two or more market committees, and if a person is chosen as a member of two or more market committees, then, at the expiration of such period as may be prescribed, that persons seat in all market committees shall become vacant, unless he has previously resigned his seat in the market committees of all but one of such market committees. (3) If a person who is already a member of one market committee and whose term of office as member of such committee has commenced, is chosen as a member of another market committee, his seat in the former market committee shall on the date on which he is so chosen become vacant.".
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Second Amendment) Ordinance, 1969 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 7 of 1969) is hereby repealed: Provided that anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
