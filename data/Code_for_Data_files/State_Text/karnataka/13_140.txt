Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Municipalities Act, 1964. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Municipalities Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1964), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 1984.1 2 (2) Sections 5 and 7 shall be deemed to have come into force on the seventh day of April, 1964 and the remaining provisions shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-third day of June, 1984 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-sections (3) and (5) of Section 43 of the Karnataka Municipalities Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1964) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for the word "Commissioner" the words "Municipal Commissioner", shall be substituted.
Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 47 of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely - "47-A. Participation by Legislators - A member of the State Legislative Assembly representing a part or whole of a municipality and member of the State Legislative Council residing in the area shall be entitled to attend the meetings of the municipal council and the standing committees thereof and take part in the proceedings thereat but shall not have the right to vote on any subject or to contest for any elected office in the municipal council or the standing committees."
Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 51 of the principal Act, after sub-section (3), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(4) Notwithstanding anything contained in the foregoing sub-sections, at any meeting of the municipal council, while any resolution expressing want of confidence in the President is under consideration, the President, or while any resolution expressing want of confidence in the Vice-President is under consideration, the Vice-President, shall not, though he is present, preside, but shall have the right to speak and otherwise to take part in the proceedings of the municipal council while any such resolution is under consideration. The provisions of sub-section (2) shall mutatis mutandis apply for such meeting."
Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 94 of the principal Act, in item (viii) of clause (b) of sub-section (1), for the words "which may be imposed in the form of a tax, assessed on buildings and lands, or in any other form including that of charges for such supply" the words "imposed in one or more of the following forms that is to say, in the form of a tax assessed on buildings and lands or in any other form including in the form of charges for such supply" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (3) of Section 138 of the principal Act, for the words "twenty-five naye paise" the words "five rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka Notwithstanding anything contained in any decree or order or judgment of any court or any other authority, any levy or collection of water rate, levied in the form of tax assessed on buildings and lands as well as in the form of charges for the supply of water shall be deemed to be as valid and effective as if such levy or collection had been made, or done under the principal Act, as amended by this Act and accordingly - (a) all acts, proceedings or things done or action taken by any municipal council in connection with the levy and collection of such water rates shall, for all purposes be deemed to be, or to have always been, done or taken in accordance with the law; (b) no suit or other proceedings shall be maintained or continued in any court or before any authority for the refund of any such rates; and (c) no court shall enforce any decree or order directing the refund of any such rates.
