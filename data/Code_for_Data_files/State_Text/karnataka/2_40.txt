Karnataka Bhoodan Yagna (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Bhoodan Yagna Act, 1963. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Bhoodan Yagna Act, 1963 (Karnataka Act 34 of 1963) for the purpose hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Bhoodan Yagna (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Bhoodan Yagna (Amendment) Act, 1978.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-ninth day of September, 1978
Karnataka Bhoodan Yagna (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka The provisos to sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the Karnataka Bhoodan Yagna Act, 1963 (Karnataka Act 34 of 1963) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) shall be omitted.
Karnataka Bhoodan Yagna (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the principal Act - (1) the proviso to sub-section (1) shall be omitted; (2) in sub-section (2), the words "on the recommendation of Shri Acharya Vinoba Bhave" shall be omitted;
Karnataka Bhoodan Yagna (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 13 of the principal Act- (1) the words "in consultation with Sri Acharya Vinoba Bhave" shall be omitted; (2) for the words "recommended by Sri Acharya Vinoba Bhave" the words "appointed by the State Government" shall be substituted.
