Payment of Wages (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Payment of Wages Act, 1935 in its application to the State of Karnataka; Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Payment of Wages Act, 1936 (Central Act 4 of 1936) in its application to the State of Karnataka; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Payment of Wages (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Payment of Wages (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1981.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the President on the Eighteenth day of January, 1982
Payment of Wages (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 7 of the Payment of Wages Act, 1936 (Central Act 4 of 1936) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), clause (r) shall be omitted.
Payment of Wages (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 13 of the Principal Act, for the words, letters and brackets "clause (q) and clause (r)", the words, letters and brackets and "clause (q)" shall be substituted.
