Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) Act, 1974. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act No. 36 of 1974); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1977.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the eighteenth day of December, 1976. Received the assent of the Governor on the Eighteenth day of April, 1977
Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 13 of the Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act No. 36 of 1974), for the words "Two years" the words "three years" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Ordinance, 1977 (Karnataka Ordinance No.2 of 1977) is hereby repealed.
