Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1988_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Legal Aid Board Act, 1981. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Legal Aid Board Act, 1981 (Karnataka Act 32 of 1981) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1988.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-second day of February, 1989 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the Karnataka Legal Aid Board Act, 1981 (Karnataka Act 32 of 1981), in clause (k) - (a) for the words "nine persons" the words "ten persons", shall be substituted; (b) in sub-clause (ii), after the words "of labour", the words "other than agricultural labour", shall be inserted; (c) after sub-clause (ii), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(iia) one shall be a representative of agricultural labour".
