Karnataka High Court (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka High Court Act, 1961. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka High Court Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1962) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-fourth year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka High Court (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka High Court (Amendment) Act, 1993.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Eighth day of March, 1994
Karnataka High Court (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka For Section 5 of the Karnataka High Court Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1962) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), the following section shall be substituted, namely - "5. First appeals -Save as otherwise provided in this Act - (i) all First Appeals against a decree or order passed in a suit or other proceedings, the value of subject matter of which is rupees three lakhs or more shall be heard by a Bench consisting of not less than two Judges of the High Court and other First Appeals shall be heard by a Single Judge of the High Court. (ii) All Criminal Appeals against Judgments in which sentence of death or imprisonment for life is passed and against Judgments of acquittal in cases in which offences are punishable with death or imprisonment for life shall be heard by a Bench consisting of not less than two Judges of the High Court and other Criminal Appeals shall be heard by a Single Judge of the High Court".
Karnataka High Court (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka Clause (xiii) of Section 9 of the principal Act, shall be omitted.
Karnataka High Court (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka Notwithstanding anything contained in any law, all First Appeals and Criminal Appeals and all applications and other proceedings relating to the matters specified in Section 5 of the principal Act as amended by this Act pending in the High Court on the date of commencement of this Act shall be disposed of in accordance with the provisions of the principal Act as amended by this Act: Provided that where First Appeals and Criminal Proceedings and all other applications are part-heard and pending, such appeals, criminal proceedings and all other applications shall be continued and disposed of under the principal Act as if the provisions of this amendment Act are not made.
