Indian Partnership (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1961_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Indian Partnership Act, 1932 (Central Act IX of 1932) as in force in the whole of the State of Karnataka. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Indian Partnership Act, 1932 (Central Act IX of 1932) as in force in the whole of the State of Karnataka; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twelfth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Indian Partnership (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Indian Partnership (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1961.1 (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Karnataka. Received the assent of the President on the Sixth day of September, 1961
Indian Partnership (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 59-A of the Indian Partnership Act, 1932 (Central Act IX of 1932), in sub-section (1), for the words "by reason of the reorganisation of States", the words, figures and brackets "by reason of the addition of the Bellary District to the State of Karnataka under the Andhra State Act, 1953 (Central Act XXX of 1953), or of the reorganisation of States under the States Reorganisation Act, 1956 (Central Act 37 of 1956)" shall be substituted.
