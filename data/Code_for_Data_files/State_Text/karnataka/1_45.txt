Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1966. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1966 (Karnataka Act 27 of 1966) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1976.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Twenty-fourth day of January, 1976. Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixth day of March 1976
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka Section 60 of the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1966 (Karnataka Act 27 of 1966) (hereinafter referred to as the " Principal Act") shall be omitted.
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 63 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), after item (vi) of clause (b), the following item shall be inserted, namely - "(vii) provide facilities for processing and packaging".
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 65 of the principal Act, to sub-section (1), the following proviso shall be inserted, namely - "Provided that the State Government may, by order, in public interest exempt any market committee from such levy and collection in respect of any agricultural produce".
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka For Section 109 of the principal Act, the following section shall be substituted, namely - "109. Grants by the State Government -The State Government shall for every financial year make a grant to the Board of an amount equal to one per cent of the gross receipts of the market committees during that year by way of market fee and licence fee".
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Ordinance, 1976 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 5 of 1976) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
