Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1966. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1966 (Karnataka Act 27 of 1966), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1987.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Tenth day of August, 1987. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-fourth day of October, 1987
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 8 of the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1966 (Karnataka Act 27 of 1966) (hereinafter referred to as the " Principal Act"), for sub-section (2), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely- "(2) No place except the market yard, market sub-yard or sub-market yard, as the case may be, shall be used for purchase or sale of notified agricultural produce; (3) Nothing in sub-section (2) shall apply to- (a) the purchase or sale of notified agricultural produce by- (i) a Taluk Agricultural Produce Co-operative Marketing Society; (ii) a Primary Agricultural Co-operative Credit Society; and (iii) any other Co-operative Society permitted by the State Government; (b) the sale of notified agricultural produce by a retail trader".
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-clause (ba) of clause (2) of Section 16 of the principal Act, for the figures "114", the word and figures "114, 117 and 118" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka After sub-section (1) of Section 44 of the principal Act, the following proviso shall be inserted, namely - "Provided that a motion of no-confidence shall not be moved within a period of one year after the failure of a similar motion of no-confidence moved earlier."
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 65-A of the principal Act, for the words "market committee may", the words "market committee shall" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 73 of the Principal Act, after clause (d), the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(dd) if the holder of the licence fails to pay the market fee due along with the penalty specified in the bye-laws;".
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka For Section 85 of the principal Act, the following section shall be substituted, namely- "85. Security by traders -(1) No trader shall buy or take delivery of any goods from any commission agent on credit and no traders licence shall be granted to any person who intends to buy or take delivery of goods from commission agents on credit unless he has deposited with or furnished to, the market committee cash security or a bank guarantee of not less than rupees one thousand. (2) No licensed trader shall buy or take delivery of goods from licensed commission agents in the yard so as to remain indebted to such agents on account of the purchases of goods to such an extent as the security or guarantee deposited or furnished by him falls short of such percentage as shall be provided in the bye-laws which shall not be less than one per cent of the aggregate amount of the indebtedness arising out of such credit purchases".
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 117 of the principal Act, for the words "with fine which may extend to three thousand rupees", the words "with fine which may extend to rupees five thousand but shall not be less than rupees five hundred" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka For Section 117-A of the principal Act, the following section shall be substituted, namely- "117-A. Penalty for contravention of Section 66- If any person carrying on business in notified agricultural produce, fails without any reasonable cause- (i) to produce or caused to be produced accounts and documents; or (ii) to furnish or caused to be furnished information, which he is required to produce or as the case may be, To furnish under Section 66, he shall, on conviction, be punishable with fine which may extend to one thousand rupees".
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 10--> State(s): Karnataka For Section 118 of the principal Act, the following section shall be substituted namely- "118. Penalty for contravention of Sections 78, 79 and 80 -Whoever contravenes the provisions of Sections 78, 79 and 80, shall, on conviction, be punished with imprisonment for a term which may extend to six months or with fine which may extend to five thousand rupees or with both: Provided that- (i) in the case of a first offence, the Court may, for reasons to be recorded impose a sentence of fine only; (ii) in the case of a second or subsequent offence, the Court shall impose a sentence of imprisonment which shall not be less than one month".
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 11--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 129 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), in clause (a), after the words "if he thinks fit", the words "either suo motu or" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 12--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Ordinance, 1987 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 5 of 1987) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
