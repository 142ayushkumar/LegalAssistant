Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1962); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Eighteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1967.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of January, 1967. Received the assent of the Governor on the Fifteenth day of April, 1967
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 14 of the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1962), after sub-section (7), the following sub-section shall be added, namely - "(8) In the districts of Chickmagalur, Dharwar, Gulbarga, Hassan, Karnataka, Shimoga, South Kanara and Tumkur the provisions of this section shall have effect as if in sub-sections (2), (4) and (6) for the words "fifteen months" the words "nineteen months" have been substituted."
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Ordinance, 1967 is hereby repealed.
