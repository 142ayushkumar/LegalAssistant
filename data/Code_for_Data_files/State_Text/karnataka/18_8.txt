Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1964_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Fifteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1964_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1964. 1 2 (2) Section 4 shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of April, 1964, and 1 Sections 2 and 3 shall come into force on such date as the State Government may by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint. Repealed by Act 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Fourteenth day of July, 1964
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1964_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Second Schedule to the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in the third column of Serial No. 59, for the words "Seven per cent", the words "Ten per cent" shall be substituted. Sections 2 and 3 have come into force on 1st October, 1964 (vide S.O. 1158, dated 14th August, 1964)
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1964_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In the Fourth Schedule to the principal Act, for the entry relating to Serial No. 3, the following entry shall be substituted, namely - "3. Hides and skins, whether in a raw or dressed state. Purchase by the last dealer in the State liable to tax under this Act. One per cent".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1964_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In the Fifth Schedule to the principal Act - (1) in the entry relating to Serial No. 22, after item (ii), the following item shall be added, namely - "(iii) by canteens recognised by the Commissioner which are run in Central or State Government Offices as an amenity provided for the members of the staff employed in such offices on a 'no profit' basis when such articles are sold, supplied or distributed to such member"; (2) after the entry relating to Serial No. 34, the following entry shall be and shall be deemed to have been inserted, with effect from the 1st day of September, 1963, namely - "34-A. Goods sold to the Indian Aid Mission, Nepal, certified by the Director of the said Mission as being required for Indian aided projects in Nepal"; and (3) after the entry relating to Serial No. 39, the following entries shall be inserted, namely - "40. Foodgrains when sold by the Central Government. 41. Foodgrains when sold by a wholesale Co-operative Society to a Primary Co-operative Society."
