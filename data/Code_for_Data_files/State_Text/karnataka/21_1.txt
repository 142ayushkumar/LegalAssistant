Karnataka Vacant Lands in Urban Areas (Prohibition of Alienation) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Vacant Lands in Urban Areas (Prohibition of Alienation) Act, 1975 . And, whereas, the said Act was enacted with a view to prevent speculation pending the enactment of law fixing ceiling on vacant lands in urban areas and for other purposes. And, whereas, the Urban Land (Ceiling and Regulations) Act, 1976 enacted by Parliament has come into force in certain areas of the State. And, whereas, the said Act empowers the State Government to extend with the approval of the Central Government the provisions of the Act to other areas in the State having regard to the factors specified therein. Now, therefore, it is considered necessary to provide that the provisions of the Karnataka Vacant Lands in Urban Areas (Prohibition of Alienation) Act, 1975 shall cease to have effect in the areas in which the Central Act remains in force and for this purpose to amend the Karnataka Vacant Lands in Urban Areas (Prohibition of Alienation) Act, 1975 . Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Vacant Lands in Urban Areas (Prohibition of Alienation) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Vacant Lands in Urban Areas (Prohibition of Alienation) (Amendment) Act, 1976.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-ninth day of May, 1976 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Vacant Lands in Urban Areas (Prohibition of Alienation) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 1 of the Karnataka Vacant Lands in Urban Areas (Prohibition of Alienation) Act, 1975 (Karnataka Act 36 of 1975), after sub-section (3), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(4) Notwithstanding anything in sub-section (3), the State Government may, by notification, declare that the provisions of this Act other than this section shall cease to be in force in such areas and on such date as may be specified in the said notification and thereupon the provisions of Section 6 of the Karnataka General Clauses Act, 1899 shall apply as if this Act had been repealed in the areas specified in the said notification."
