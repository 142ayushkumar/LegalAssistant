Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1970_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964). Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-first year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1970.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Twenty-third day of October, 1969. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-first day of February, 1970 Karnataka Act No. 5 of 1970 repealed by the Karnataka Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Sections 73, 77, 78, 83, 87, 88, 100, 104, 105, 110, 130, 140, 142, 147, 154, 163, 164, 165, 171, 179, 188 and 189 of the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for the words "Deputy Commissioner" wherever they occur, the word "Tahsildar" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 49 of the principal Act, for clauses (c) and (d), the following clauses shall be substituted, namely - "(c) if such an order is passed by the Deputy Commissioner - (i) to the Divisional Commissioner, in the case of an order made in respect of grant of lands; and (ii) to the Tribunal, in all other cases; (d) if such an order is passed by the Divisional Commissioner - (i) to the State Government, in the case of an order made in respect of grant of lands; and (ii) to the Tribunal, in all other cases."
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 50 of the principal Act, for clause (b), the following clauses shall be substituted, namely - "(b) if such an order is passed by the Deputy Commissioner - (i) to the Divisional Commissioner, in the case of an order made in respect of grant of lands; and (ii) to the Tribunal, in all other cases; "(bb) if such an order is passed by the Divisional Commissioner - (i) to the State Government, in the case of an order made in respect of grant of lands; and (ii) to the Tribunal, in all other cases."
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 56 of the principal Act - (i) before the Explanation to sub-section (1), the following proviso shall be inserted, namely - "Provided that the Tribunal shall not have power to call for and examine the record of any inquiry or proceedings relating to grant of lands;" (ii) after sub-section (1), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(1-A) The State Government may call for and examine the record of any inquiry or proceedings relating to grant of lands of any subordinate officer under this Act for the purpose of satisfying itself as to the legality or propriety of the proceedings of such officer;" (iii) in sub-section (2), after the words "the Tribunal", in the two places where they occur, a comma and the words "the State Government" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka For Section 69 of the principal Act, the following section shall be substituted, namely - "69. Disposal of lands or other property belonging to State Government under Section 67 -Subject to such rules as may be made in this behalf, the State Government, the Divisional Commissioner, the Deputy Commissioner, the Assistant Commissioner in-charge of a Taluk or Taluks and the Tahsildar, may dispose of land or other property belonging to the State Government under Section 67 or otherwise, for purposes of agriculture, industry or any public utility and subject to the provisions of Chapter XII for the construction of buildings."
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 94 of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely - "94-A. Regularisation of certain cases of unauthorised occupation -Nothing in Section 94 shall prevent the Deputy Commissioner if the person liable to be evicted under that section who has unauthorisedly occupied the land prior to the first day of July, 1968 and who satisfies such other conditions (including the extent of land held by him) as may be prescribed, so desires, to grant the land or any portion thereof to such person in accordance with such rules relating to restrictions terms and conditions (including the sum payable not exceeding twice the market value of such land) as the State Government may make."
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka All appeals and proceedings connected therewith and all proceedings in revision under the principal Act relating to grant of lands other than those relating to orders of the Deputy Commissioner, where such order is made by him with the previous sanction of the Divisional Commissioner or the State Government, pending before the Tribunal immediately before the date of commencement of this Act, shall, with effect from that date, stand transferred to the Divisional Commissioner or the State Government, as the case may be, and the said appeals and proceedings shall be disposed of by the Divisional Commissioner or the State Government as if the said appeals or proceedings had been preferred or commenced before the Divisional Commissioner or the State Government, as the case may be.
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka Land Revenue (Second Amendment) Ordinance 1969 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 8 of 1969) is hereby, repealed: Provided that anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
