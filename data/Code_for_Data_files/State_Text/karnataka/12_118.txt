Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Second Amendment) Act, 1957_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1956. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Second Amendment) Act, 1957_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Second Amendment) Act, 1957.1 2 (2) Section 3 shall come into force at once and Section 2 shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of June, 1957. Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixteenth day of October, 1957. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Second Amendment) Act, 1957_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12 of the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1956 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (1) at the end of clause (a), the following words shall be added, namely - "and such members shall also be provided with fully furnished accommodation, without payment of rent, at the place at which their attendance is required". (2) for clause (b), the following clause shall be substituted, namely - "(b) there shall be paid - (i) to all members daily allowance for attending the meetings and for the prescribed number of days of holidays, intervals or absence between meetings at the rate of sixteen rupees per diem; (ii) to all members entitled to travelling allowance under clause (a) daily allowance for the day before and the day after the day of the meetings, and when a meeting extends for more than one day, for the day before the day of commencement of the meetings and for the day after the day of conclusion of the meetings;" (3) for clause (d), the following clause shall be substituted - "(d) members entitled to travelling allowance under clause (a) may, during the days of halt in the City of Bangalore, other than during the period when their attendance is required in connection with the meetings, be provided with fully furnished accommodation on payment of one half of the rent payable for such accommodation;".
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Second Amendment) Act, 1957_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka All members who were entitled to be provided with fully furnished accommodation without payment of rent at the place at which their attendance was required under sub-clause (1) of clause (b) of Section 12 of the principal Act, and who, during the period between the first day of June, 1957, and the date of commencement of this section, did not, whether by election or otherwise, have such accommodation, shall, in addition to the daily allowance paid or payable under the second paragraph of the said sub-clause, be paid, in respect of the said period additional daily allowance at the rate of three rupees and eight annas per diem.
