Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) Act, 1974. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 36 of 1974); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1978.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixteenth day of September, 1978
Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 13 of the Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 36 of 1974), for the words "three years", the words "five years" shall be and shall always be deemed to have been substituted.
