Karnataka Stamp (Second Amendment) Act, 1997_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 34 of 1957), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-eights year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka Stamp (Second Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Stamp (Second Amendment) Act, 1997.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-seventh day of September, 1997
Karnataka Stamp (Second Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In schedule to the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 34 of 1957). (i) In clause (d) of Article 5, after the brackets, words and figures "(Karnataka Act 11 of 1959)", a comma, the words, figures and brackets "or the allotment of industrial sheds and plots by the Karnataka Industrial Area Development Board established under the Karnataka Industrial Area Development Act, 1966 (Karnataka Act 18 of 1966), the Karnataka Small Scale Industrial Development Corporation, the Karnataka State Industrial Investment and Development Corporation and the Karnataka State Electronics Development Corporation, registered as a company under the Companies Act, 1956 (Central Act 1 of 1956), or the allotment of land or site with or without building to the market functionaries in the yard by the Agricultural Produce Market Committees constituted under the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1966 (Karnataka Act 27 of 1966)" shall inserted. (ii) In the proviso to Article 20 after the words "insofar as it relates to allotment of building sites to the members", a comma, the words, figures and brackets "or conveyance effected by the Karnataka Industrial Areas Development Board established under the Karnataka Industrial Area Development Act, 1966 (Karnataka Act 18 of 1966), the Karnataka Small Scale industrial Development Corporation, the Karnataka State Electronics Development Corporation registered as a company under the Companies Act, 1956 (Central Act 1 of 1956), so far as it relates to industrial sheds and plots allotted by them, or conveyance effected by the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Market Committee Consultuted under the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1966 (Karnataka Act 27 of 1966) so far as it relates to sale or lease-cum sale of land or site with or without building, to market functionaries in the yard" shall be inserted.
