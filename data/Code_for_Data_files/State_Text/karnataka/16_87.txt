Karnataka Public Libraries (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Public Libraries Act, 1965. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Public Libraries Act, 1965 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1965); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Public Libraries (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Public Libraries (Amendment) Act, 19981 . (2) It shall come into force at once. First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, on Twenty-fourth day of November, 1998, Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-fourth day of November, 1998
Karnataka Public Libraries (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 31 of the Karnataka Public Libraries Act, 1965 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1965), in sub-section (1), for the words "three per cent", the words "six per cent" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Public Libraries (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Public Libraries (Amendment) Ordinance 1998 (Karnataka Ordinance 7 of 1998) is hereby repealed.
