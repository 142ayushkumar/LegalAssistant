Karnataka Revision of State Laws Act, 1955_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to repeal certain enactments and to amend certain other enactments. Whereas, for the purpose of bringing into accord with the Constitution of India and Central Acts extended to the State of Karnataka, and for other purposes, it is expedient to repeal certain enactments and to amend certain other enactments. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Revision of State Laws Act, 1955_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Karnataka Revision of State Laws Act, 1955.1 Received the assent of the President on the Thirty-first day of December, 1955
Karnataka Revision of State Laws Act, 1955_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka The enactments specified in the First Schedule are hereby repealed.
