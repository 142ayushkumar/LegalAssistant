Karnataka Public Service Commission (Conduct of Business and Additional Functions) (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Public Service Commission (Conduct of Business and Additional Functions) Act, 1959. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Public Service Commission (Conduct of Business and Additional Functions) Act, 1959 (Karnataka Act 20 of 1959); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Seventeenth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Public Service Commission (Conduct of Business and Additional Functions) (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Public Service Commission (Conduct of Business and Additional Functions) (Amendment) Act, 19661 . (2) It shall come into force at once. First published in the Karnataka Gazette on the First day of September, 1966, Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-fifth day of August, 1966
Karnataka Public Service Commission (Conduct of Business and Additional Functions) (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Public Service Commission (Conduct of Business and Additional Functions) Act, 1959 (Karnataka Act 20 of 1959) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), clause (a) shall be re-lettered as clause (aa), and before the clause as so re-lettered, the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(a) "Assistant Secretary" means the Assistant Secretary to the Commission, or in his absence such other person discharging the duties of the Assistant Secretary for the time being";
Karnataka Public Service Commission (Conduct of Business and Additional Functions) (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 11 of the principal Act - (i) in the heading, for the words "by Secretary", the words "by the Secretary or the Assistant Secretary" shall be substituted; (ii) after the words "by the Secretary", the words "or by the Assistant Secretary" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Public Service Commission (Conduct of Business and Additional Functions) (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 17 of the Principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely - "17-A. Commission to furnish returns, etc., to Government - The Commission shall furnish such returns, records and information as may be required by the Government".
