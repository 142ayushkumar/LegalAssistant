Karnataka Appellate Tribunal (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Appellate Tribunal Act, 1976. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Appellate Tribunal Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1976) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in Thirtieth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Appellate Tribunal (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Karnataka Appellate Tribunal (Amendment) Act, 1979.1 Received the assent of the Governor on the Twentieth day of February, 1979
Karnataka Appellate Tribunal (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (3) of Section 3 of the Karnataka Appellate Tribunal Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1976) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (1) for the words "one shall be" occurring in two places, the words "at least one shall be" shall be substituted; (2) for the words "the Commercial Taxes Member" and "the Co-operation Member", the words "a Commercial Taxes Member" and "a Co-operation Member" shall respectively be substituted.
Karnataka Appellate Tribunal (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6 of the principal Act, in sub-section (3) - (a) for sub-clause (ii) of clause (a), the following shall be substituted, namely - "(i) a Co-operation Member;" (b) for sub-clause (ii) of clause (b), the following shall be substituted, namely:- "(ii) a Commercial Taxes Member."
