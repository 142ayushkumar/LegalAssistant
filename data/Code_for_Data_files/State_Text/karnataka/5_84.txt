Karnataka Excise (Second Amendment) Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Excise Act, 1965. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Excise Act, 1965 (Karnataka Act 21 of 1966); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Excise (Second Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Excise (Second Amendment) Act, 1993.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the eighth day of October, 1993. Received the Assent of the Governor On the Tenth Day of February, 1994
Karnataka Excise (Second Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka For Section 24 of the Karnataka Excise Act, 1965 (Karnataka Act 21 of 1966), (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) the following shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted, namely:- "24. Payment of fees for grant of lease:-Instead of or in addition to any excise duty or countervailing duty leviable under Sections 22 and 23, the State Government may, accept payment of a sum or levy such licence fee or privilege fee as maybe prescribed, in consideration of grant of a lease or licence or both, by or under this Act."
Karnataka Excise (Second Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka Sub-rule (1) of Rule 8 of the Karnataka Excise (Sale of Indian and Foreign Liquors) Rules, 1968 made in Notification No. GSR 7, dated 4th January, 1968 and published in the Karnataka Gazette dated 11th January, 1968 and as amended from time to time (hereinafter referred to as the said sub-rule) shall, notwithstanding anything contained in any judgment, decree or order of any Court or other authority be deemed to be as valid and effective for all purposes as if, the said sub-rule has been made or amended under the principal Act as amended by this Act, and accordingly,- (a) levy and collection of any licence fee made or purporting to have been made during a period of three years next before the second day of August, 1971 and any action or thing taken or done (including any notice or orders issued and all proceedings held or any amount purported to have been collected by way of licence fee) in relation to such levy and collection under the said sub-rule shall for all purposes be, deemed to be and to have always been made, taken or done in accordance with law; (b) no suit or other proceeding shall be maintained or continued in any Court or Tribunal or before any authority for the refund of any such licence fee; (c) no Court shall enforce any decree or order directing the refund of any such licence fee".
Karnataka Excise (Second Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Excise (Second Amendment) Ordinance, 1993 (Karnataka Ordinance 11 of 1993) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act".
