Karnataka Command Areas Development (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Command Areas Development Act, 1980. And, whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Command Areas Development Act, 1980 (Karnataka Act 6 of 1980) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-sixth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Command Areas Development (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Command Areas Development (Amendment) Act, 1995.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Eighteenth day of November, 1995
Karnataka Command Areas Development (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the Karnataka Command Areas Development Act, 1980 (Karnataka Act 6 of 1980) - (i) for clause (c), the following shall be substituted, namely - "(c) the Deputy Secretary to Government (Command Area Development Authority) Irrigation Department." (ii) in clause (d), after the words "Director of Agriculture", the words "or his nominee not below the rank of Joint Director of Agriculture" shall be inserted; (iii) in clause (e), before the words "The Chief Engineers", the words "the Engineer-in Chief and" shall be inserted; (iv) in clause (f), after the words "Registrar of Co-operative Societies", the words "or his nominee not below the rank of Joint Registrar of Co-operative Societies" shall be inserted.
