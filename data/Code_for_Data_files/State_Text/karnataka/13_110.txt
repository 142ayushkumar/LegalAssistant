Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Municipal Corporations Act, 1976. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Municipal Corporations Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1977), for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty Fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1983.1 2 (2) All sections of this Act except clause (b) of Section 6 shall be deemed to have come into force on the twenty-fourth day of February, 1983 and clause (b) of Section 6 shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of March, 1983. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-sixth day of April, 1983 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 7 of the Karnataka Municipal Corporations Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1977) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (2), for clause (b) the following clause shall be substituted, namely - "(b) for women shall be, as nearly as may be twenty per cent of the total number of councillors."
Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 21 of the principal Act - (1) after sub-section (1), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(1-A) No notification under sub-section (1) shall be called in question in any court of law." (2) after Section (2), the following proviso shall be inserted, namely:- "Provided that the reference in this sub-section to the last preceding census shall until the relevant figures for the first census taken after the year 2000 have been published be construed as reference to the 1971 census."
Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 22 of the principal Act for the words, "electoral roll" the words, "list of voters" and for the words, "roll pertains" the words "list pertains" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 23 of the Principal Act - (1) in sub-section (1), for the words, "shall for the purposes of this Act be deemed to be electoral roll" the words, figures and brackets "along with the additional list of voters prepared under sub-section (1-A) and relatable to such division shall for the purposes of this Act, be deemed to be the list of voters", shall be substituted; (2) in the proviso to sub-section (1), for the words, "electoral roll" the words "list of voters", shall be substituted; (3) after sub-section (1), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(1-A) There shall be prepared and revised by the prescribed authority in the prescribed manner and at prescribed intervals an additional list of voters for the city. Every person who is ordinarily resident within the city and is not less than eighteen years of age on the First day of January of the year in which it is so prepared or revised but excluding a person who was not less than twenty one years of age on the First day of January of the year in which the electoral roll referred to in sub-section (1) was last revised shall be eligible to be included in the additional list of voters so prepared. The provisions of Section 16 of the Representation of the People Act, 1950 (Central Act 43 of 1950) shall, mitatis mutandis, apply for the purpose of this sub-section."
Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 421 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1) - (a) the words "after previous publication" shall be omitted; (b) after the word "may" the words, "after previous publication" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Ordinance, 1983 (Karnataka Ordinance 2 of 1983) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
