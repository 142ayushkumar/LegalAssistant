Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Second Amendment) Act, 2002_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An act further to amend the Karnataka Municipal Corporations Act, 1976. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Municipal Corporations Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1977) for the purpose hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the fifty-third year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Second Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Second Amendment) Act, 2002. 1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Published in the Karnataka Gazette Part IV-A Extra Ordinary No. 312 dated 27-3-2003.
Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Second Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 7 of the Karnataka Municipal Corporations Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1977), in sub-section (1), in clause (b), for the words "not more than five persons" the words "not more than ten persons in the case of Bangalore City Corporation and not more than five persons in the case of other City Corporations" shall be substituted. By Order and in the name of the Governor of Karnataka, M.R. Hegde Secretary to Government, Department of Parliamentary Affairs and Legislation
