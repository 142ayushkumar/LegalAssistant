Karnataka Land Revenue (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964). Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty- seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Land Revenue (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Revenue (Second Amendment) Act, 1976.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Twenty-fourth day of January, 1976. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twelfth day of March, 1976 Karnataka Act No. 23 of 1976 repealed by the Karnataka Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Land Revenue (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6 of the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964), (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), after the words "the State Government shall", the words "except in cases where it considers not necessary so to do" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Land Revenue (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Ordinance, 1976 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 6 of 1976) is hereby, repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
