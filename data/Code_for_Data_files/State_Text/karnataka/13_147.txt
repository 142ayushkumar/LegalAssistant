Karnataka Municipalities (Second Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Municipalities Act, 1964. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Municipalities Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1964), for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty- second Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Municipalities (Second Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Municipalities (Second Amendment) Act, 1981.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixth day of August, 1982 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Municipalities (Second Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 321 of the Karnataka Municipalities Act, 1964, (Karnataka Act 22 of 1964) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), after the words "the Commissioner", the words "or the Deputy Commissioner" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Municipalities (Second Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 342 of the principal Act, in clause (a) of sub-section (1), for the words "sixty rupees", the words "such amount as may be prescribed" shall be substituted.
