Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1965_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Sixteenth Year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1965_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1965.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Fourth day of November, 1965
Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1965_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 10 of the Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1957) - (1) clause (a) shall be omitted. (2) for item (ii), the following item shall be substituted, namely - "(ii) different in respect of water supplied, made available or used from different irrigation works, and when water is supplied, made available or used for irrigation of any land, with or without reference to the crop or crops grown or which may be grown on such land."
