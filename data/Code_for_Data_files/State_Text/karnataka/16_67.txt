Karnataka Prohibition (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Prohibition Act, 1961. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Prohibition Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 17 of 1962); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Eighteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Prohibition (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Prohibition (Amendment) Act, 19671 . (2) It shall come into force at once. First published in the Karnataka Gazette, dated the Twenty-fourth day of August, 1967, Received the assent of the Governor on the Seventeenth day of August, 1967
Karnataka Prohibition (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 1 of the Karnataka Prohibition Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 17 of 1962) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), after sub-section (4), the following sub-section shall be added, namely - "(5) Notwithstanding anything contained in sub-sections (3) and (4), the State Government may, by notification, declare that the provisions of this Act other than this section and Sections 2 and 15 shall cease to be in force in any area on such date as may be specified in such notification, and thereupon the Karnataka Excise Act, 1965 (Karnataka Act 21 of 1966), and the rules, orders and notifications made or issued or deemed to be made or issued thereunder shall extend to such area and shall come into force therein with effect on and from such date, and this Act other than this section and Sections 2 and 15, shall stand repealed in such area and the provisions of Section 6 of the Karnataka General Clauses Act, 1899, shall apply accordingly: Provided that the issue of a notification under this sub-section shall not preclude the State Government from issuing a notification under clause (b) of sub-section (4) bringing the provisions of this Act into force in such local area".
Karnataka Prohibition (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the principal Act - (i) clause (1) shall be renumbered as clause (1-A) and before the clause as so renumbered, the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(i) "Alcohol" means ethyl alcohol of any strength and purity having the chemical composition C2 H5 CH"; (ii) for clause (18), the following clause shall be substituted, namely - "(18) "Intoxicating drugs" means - (i) the leaves, small stalks and flowering or fruiting tops of the Indian hemp plant (Cannabis Sativa or Indica) including all forms known as bhang, siddi or ganja; (ii) charas, that is, the resin obtained from the Indian hemp plant, which has not been submitted to any manipulations other than those necessary for packing and transport; (iii) any other intoxicating or narcotic drug or substance, which the State Government may, by notification, declare to be an intoxicating drug, for the purposes of this Act, such drug or substance not being opium, cocoa leaf or manufactured drug, as defined in Section 2 of the Dangerous Drugs Act, 1930 (Central Act 2 of 1930); (iv) any mixture with or without neutral materials of any of the above forms of intoxicating drug or any drink prepared therefrom;"
Karnataka Prohibition (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 17 of the principal Act - (i) at the end of clause (b), the word "or" shall be added; and (ii) after clause (b), the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(c) members of the Armed Forces of the Union or of any other Armed Forces raised or maintained by the Union or attached to or operating with any of its Armed Forces;"
Karnataka Prohibition (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka Section 44 of the principal Act shall be renumbered as sub-section (1) of that section and after the sub-section as so renumbered, the following sub-section shall be added, namely - "(2) The conditions prescribed under sub-section (1) may include provisions of accommodation by the licensee to Prohibition Officers at the licensed premises or the payment of rent or other charges for such accommodation at or near the licensed premises, and the payment of the costs, charges and expenses (including the salaries and allowances of the Prohibition Officers) which the State Government may incur in connection with supervision to ensure compliance with the provisions of the licence and the provisions of this Act, the rules, regulations and orders made thereunder."
Karnataka Prohibition (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 51 of the principal Act, for the words, figures and brackets "the Sea Customs Act, 1878 (Central Act VIII of 1878)", the words, figures and brackets "the Customs Act, 1962 (Central Act 52 of 1962)", shall be substituted.
Karnataka Prohibition (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka Section 56 of the principal Act shall be omitted.
Karnataka Prohibition (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 129 of the principal Act, for the figures "1898", the figures "1878" shall be and shall always be deemed to have been substituted.
Karnataka Prohibition (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka In Item 3 of the Schedule A to the principal Act, for the figures "1938", in the two places where they occur, the figures "1937" shall be and shall always be deemed to have been substituted.
