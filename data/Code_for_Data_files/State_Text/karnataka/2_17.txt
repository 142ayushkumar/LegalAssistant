Bangalore Development Authority (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Bangalore Development Authority Act, 1976. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Bangalore Development Authority Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1976) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the fifty-sixth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bangalore Development Authority (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Bangalore Development Authority (Amendment) Act, 2005. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Bangalore Development Authority (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10 of the Bangalore Development Authority Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1976) in sub-section (1) and (2), for the words "twenty lakhs", the words "fifty lakhs" shall be substituted. By Order and in the name of the Governor of Karnataka, G. K .Boregowda Secretary to Government (I/c), Department of Parliamentary Affairs and Legislation
