Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Municipalities Act, 1964. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Municipalities Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1964), for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty- second year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 1981.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Thirtieth day of September, 1980. Received the assent of the Governor on the Seventh day of April, 1981 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka After sub-section (2) of Section 63 of the Karnataka Municipalities Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1964), (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), the following sub-sections shall be inserted, namely - "(2-A) (a) In addition to the committee or committees specified in sub-section (1), in every municipal council, there shall be a social justice committee consisting of - (i) the councillors of the municipal council belonging to the Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes; (ii) women councillors of the municipal council; and (iii) not more than two other councillors nominated by the Commissioner in the case of city municipal councils and by the Deputy Commissioner in the case of town municipal councils. (b) The President of the municipal council shall, ex-officio be a member and be the Chairman of the social justice committee. The said committee, shall subject to such instructions as the municipal council may give from time to time perform such functions as may be prescribed for securing social justice to the Scheduled Castes, the Scheduled Tribes, women and weaker sections of the society."
Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Ordinance, 1980 (Karnataka Ordinance 10 of 1980) hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
