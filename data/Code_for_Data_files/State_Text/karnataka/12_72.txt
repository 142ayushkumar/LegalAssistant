Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964), for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Fifty-first year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2000.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the 27th day of April, 2000
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 94-B of the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964), for the words, brackets and figures "within one year from the date of commencement of the Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1997", the words, brackets and figures "within three years from the date of commencement of the Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2000" shall be substituted.
