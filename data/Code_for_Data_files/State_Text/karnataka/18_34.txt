Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1961_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957). Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twelfth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1961.1 2 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the First day of December, 1961
Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 2 of the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (i) after clause (f), the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(ff) Assistant Commissioner" means any person appointed to be an Assistant Commissioner of Commercial Taxes under Section 3"; (ii) clause (n) shall be omitted.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the principal Act, for the words "Deputy Commissioners of Commercial Taxes, Commercial Tax Officers, Assistant Commercial Tax Officers and Inspecting Officers", the words "Deputy Commissioners of Commercial Taxes, Assistant Commissioners of Commercial Taxes, Commercial Tax Officers and Assistant Commercial Tax Officers" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (4) of Section 5 of the principal Act - (i) in the first proviso, for the words and figures "shall be refunded to the dealer on production of proof that the subsequent sale has been subjected to tax under the Central Sales Tax Act, 1956", the words "shall be refunded to such person in such manner and subject to such condition as may be prescribed" shall be substituted; (ii) after the first proviso, the following proviso shall be added, namely - "Provided further that where tax has been paid by any dealer under this Act in respect of the purchase of goods mentioned in Serial Numbers 5(a) and 6 of the Fourth Schedule and such goods are subsequently sold to any other dealer in the State, liable to the payment of tax under this Act, the tax paid by the earlier dealer in respect of such goods shall be refunded to him on production of proof that the purchase by the later dealer has been subjected to the tax under this Act".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 18 of the principal Act, (i) for sub-section (3), the following sub-sections shall be substituted and shall be deemed always to have been substituted, namely - (3) (a) If any person collects any amount by way of the tax in contravention of the provisions of sub-section (1) or (2) or if any registered dealer or licensed dealer collects any amount by way of the tax in excess of the amount payable by him, the amounts so collected shall, without prejudice to any prosecution that may be instituted against such person or dealer for any offence under this Act, stand forfeited to the State Government, and such person or dealer, as the case may be, shall, within the prescribed period, pay such amount into a Government treasury and in default of such payment, the amount shall be recovered as an arrear of land revenue. (b) Where any amount forfeited to the State Government is either paid into a Government treasury or is recovered as an arrear of land revenue under clause (a), a refund of such amount or any part thereof can be claimed from the Government only by the person from whom the person, registered dealer or licensed dealer, as the case may be, has actually realised such amount by way of the tax provided that such claim is made within one year from the date of payment of the amount into a Government treasury or recovery thereof as arrear of land revenue"; (ii) after sub-section (3) as substituted by clause (i), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(4) Notwithstanding anything contained in clause (b) of sub-section (3), in respect of refund of any amount forfeited to the State Government and paid or recovered by it, before the date of commencement of the Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1961, the claim may be made by the person entitled to refund within one year from the date of such commencement".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 20 of the principal Act, for the words, brackets and figures "sub-sections (2) and (3) of Section 12", the words "any provision of this Act" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In clause (ii) of sub-section (1) of Section 21 of the principal Act, the words "to him" shall be omitted.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 22 of the principal Act, after the words "order relating to assessment passed", the words "by the Assistant Commissioner on appeal under Section 20 or" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka In the Second Schedule to the principal Act - (i) in Entry 38, for the words "All liquor including beer other than country liquor", the words "All liquor other than country liquor" shall be substituted and shall be deemed always to have been substituted; (ii) in Explanation II, for the words "other than liquor", the words "other than beer, or any other liquor" shall be substituted and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 10--> State(s): Karnataka Anything done or any action taken or purported to be done or taken (including any notices or orders issued or assessment, payments, or recoveries made and all proceedings held for the levy and collection of tax) under the principal Act before the commencement of this Act, shall, notwithstanding any judgment, decree or order of any Court, be deemed to have been validly done, taken, issued, made or held and shall have effect for all purposes, as if it had been done, taken, issued, made or held by or under the provisions of the principal Act as amended by Sections 5 and 9 of this Act and accordingly no suit or other legal proceeding shall be entertained or continued in any Court or other authority on the ground that any such thing was done or action taken under or in pursuance of provisions which were not valid or not in force at that time.
