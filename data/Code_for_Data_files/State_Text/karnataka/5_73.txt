Karnataka Excise (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Excise Act, 1965. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Excise Act, 1965 (Karnataka Act 21 of 1966), for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-ninth year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka Excise (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Excise (Amendment) Act, 1998.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-seventh day of May, 1998
Karnataka Excise (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Excise Act, 1965 (Karnataka Act 21 of 1966) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (1) in clause (10), after the words "Excise Commissioner", the words "a Joint Commissioner of Excise" shall be inserted; (2) after clause (17), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(17-A) "Joint Commissioner of Excise" means a Joint Commissioner of Excise appointed under Section 4-A."
Karnataka Excise (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4-A of the principal Act - (i) in the heading, for the words "Deputy Commissioner of Excise", the words "Joint Commissioner of Excise and Deputy Commissioner of Excise" shall be substituted; (ii) in sub-section (1), for the words "Deputy Commissioners of Excise", the words "Joint Commissioners of Excise or Deputy Commissioners of Excise" shall be substituted; (iii) in sub-sections (2) and (3), for the words "a Deputy Commissioner of Excise" wherever they occur, the words "a Joint Commissioner of Excise or a Deputy Commissioner of Excise" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Excise (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5-A of the Principal Act, for the words "Deputy Commissioner of Excise" the words "Joint Commissioner of Excise or a Deputy Commissioner of Excise" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Excise (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6 of the principal Act, in sub-section (4), after the words "Excise Commissioner", the words "Joint Commissioner" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Excise (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 7 of the principal Act in sub-section (2), after the words "Excise Commissioner", the words "or the Joint Commissioner of Excise" shall be inserted.
