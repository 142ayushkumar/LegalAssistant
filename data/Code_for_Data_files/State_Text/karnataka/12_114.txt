Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1956. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1957) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-fifth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1974.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-seventh day of May, 1974
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (2), for the words "three hundred and fifty rupees", the words "five hundred rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6 of the principal Act, in clause (b) of sub-section (1), for the words "seventy-five rupees", the words "one hundred and twenty five rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 8 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2) - (i) in clause (a), for the words "one and half times", the words "two times" shall be substituted; (ii) in clause (b), for the words "forty paise", the words "seventy-five paise" shall be substituted (iii) in clause (d) - (a) for the words "rupees twenty", the words "thirty rupees" shall be substituted; (b) in the first proviso, for the words "forty rupees", the words "fifty rupees" shall be substituted;
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 9 of the principal Act, after the words "medical attendance and treatment," the following shall be inserted, namely - "They shall also be entitled, subject to rules made by the State Government, to reimbursement of the expenses incurred by them for medical attendance and treatment obtained at any other place."
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka Section 10 of the principal Act shall be renumbered as sub-section (1) of that section and - (i) in the said sub-section (1), for the words "eight hundred rupees", the words "one thousand rupees" shall be substituted; (ii) after the said sub-section (1), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely: "(2) There shall be paid to the Deputy Chairman and the Deputy Speaker a conveyance allowance of five hundred rupees per mensem."
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 11 of the principal Act, for the words "one hundred and fifty rupees", the words "two hundred and fifty rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12 of the principal Act - (i) in clause (a) - (a) for the words "one and half times" the words "two times" and for the words "forty paise", the words "seventy-five paise" shall respectively be substituted; (b) for the words "and such members shall also", the words "and all the members" shall be substituted. (ii) in clause (b) - (a) in sub-clause (i), for the words "twenty five rupees", the words "thirty one rupees" shall be substituted; (b) in sub-clause (ii), for the words "for the day before and the day after the day of the meetings, and when a meeting extends for more than one day, for the day before the day of commencement of the meetings and for the day after the day of conclusion of the meetings", the words "for two days before the commencement of meetings and for two days after the day of conclusion of the meetings" shall be substituted;
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 12 of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely - "12-A. Constituency allowance to members - There shall be paid to the members of the Legislative Assembly and the Legislative Council an allowance of one hundred rupees per mensem towards meeting expenditure incurred by them in their constituencies for the discharge of their duties as members".
