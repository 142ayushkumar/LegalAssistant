Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Third Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1966. Whereas it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1966 (Karnataka Act 27 of 1966); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Third Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Third Amendment) Act, 1976.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Nineteenth day of June, 1976
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Third Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 41 of the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1966 (Karnataka Act 27 of 1966) (hereinafter referred to as the " Principal Act") - (i) for sub-section (2), the following sub-sections shall be substituted, namely - "(2) As soon as may be after the constitution of a market committee under Section 11 or on its reconstitution, a meeting thereof shall be called by the prescribed officer who shall himself preside over the meeting but shall have no right to vote. At such meeting a member representing an agriculturists constituency shall first be elected as the Chairman and thereafter another member representing another agriculturists constituency shall be elected as Vice-Chairman in the manner hereinafter provided. (2-A) At the meeting - (a) candidates for the office of the Chairman shall be proposed by one member and seconded by another. The names of all members proposed and seconded shall be read out by the Presiding Officer; (b) if any candidate desires to withdraw, he may do so in writing. The names of the contesting candidates shall then be read out by the Presiding Officer; (c) if there is only one candidate, he shall be declared to have been elected as the Chairman. If there are two or more candidates, votes of the members present at the meeting shall be taken by ballot and the candidate securing the highest number of votes shall be declared to have been elected as the Chairman; (d) in case of equality of votes, the result shall be decided by drawing lots. (2-B) The election of the Vice-Chairman shall be conducted by the Chairman and the provisions of sub-section (2-A) shall mutatis mutandis apply. (2-C) If for any reason the Chairman or the Vice-Chairman is not elected at the meeting referred to in sub-section (2) such election shall be held, at an adjourned meeting, by the - (i) prescribed officer for the election of the Chairman; and (ii) Chairman, for the election of the Vice-Chairman and after following the procedure laid down in sub-sections (2-A) and (2-B)."
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Third Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 133 of the principal Act, the words "of which all the members are either agriculturists or the primary producers of any notified agricultural produce" shall be omitted.
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Third Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 153 of the principal Act - (1) in the heading, for the word "Rules", the words "Rules, orders" shall be substituted; (2) after the words "under this Act", the words and figures "every notification issued under Section 133" shall be inserted; (3) for the words "rule or order" wherever they occur, the words "rule, notification or order" shall be substituted
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Third Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka Notwithstanding anything contained in the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1966, if any proceedings taken under the said Act or the rules made thereunder for the election of the Chairman to any market committee are, on the date of the commencement of this Act, incomplete and a Chairman has not been elected, all such proceedings shall abate and the Chairman for such market committee shall be elected in accordance with the provisions of the principal Act as amended by this Act.
