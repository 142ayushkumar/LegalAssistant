Karnataka Contingency Fund (Temporary Amendment) Act, 1961_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to provide for a temporary increase of the amount in the Karnataka Contingency Fund. Whereas, it is expedient to provide for a temporary increase of the amount in the Karnataka Contingency Fund; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twelfth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Contingency Fund (Temporary Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Contingency Fund (Temporary Amendment) Act, 1961.1 (2) It shall remain in force up to the first day of April, 1962. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-ninth day of July, 1961
Karnataka Contingency Fund (Temporary Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka Notwithstanding anything contained in Section 2 of the Karnataka Contingency Fund Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 11 of 1957), during the period this Act remains in force, a sum of four crores of rupees shall be paid from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State into the Contingency Fund of the State of Karnataka.
