Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka State Universities Act, 1976. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka State Universities Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1976), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1980.1 2 (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixth day of August, 1980
Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka State Universities Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1976) (hereinafter referred to as the  principal Act), clause (1) shall be renumbered as clause (1-A), and before clause (1-A) as so renumbered, the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(1) Amendment Act means the Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1980".
Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the principal Act - (1) after sub-section (1), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(1-A) Notwithstanding anything contained in sub-section (1), on and from the date of commencement of the Amendment Act - (a) the Karnataka University shall cease to have territorial jurisdiction over the area comprising the districts of Bellary, Bidar, Gulbarga and Raichur; (b) the Mysore University shall cease to have territorial jurisdiction over the area comprising the districts of Dakshina Kannada and Kodagu; (c) there shall be established the following Universities, namely - (i) the Gulbarga University with headquarters at Gulbarga and territorial jurisdiction over the area comprising the districts of Bellary, Bidar, Gulbarga and Raichur; (ii) the Mangalore University with headquarters at Konaie and territorial jurisdiction over the area comprising districts of Dakshina Kannada and Kodagu." (2) in sub-section (2), after the words, brackets and figures "in subsection (1)", the words, brackets, figure and letter "and sub-section (1-A)", shall be inserted.
Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the principal Act, after sub-section (4), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(5) Notwithstanding anything contained in sub-sections (3) and (4) - (a) all educational institutions - (i) situated in Bellary, Bidar, Gulbarga and Raichur districts and admitted to the privileges of or affiliated to the Karnataka University; (ii) situated in Dakshina Kannada and Kodagu districts and admitted to the privileges of or affiliated to the Mysore University, immediately before the date of commencement of the Amendment Act shall, with effect on and from the said date and subject to the provisions of Chapter IX be deemed to be institutions admitted to the privileges of or affiliated to the Gulbarga University or the Mangalore University, as the case may be, established under this Act: Provided that any application for affiliation made in respect of an institution situated in any of the aforesaid districts and pending with the Karnataka University or the Mysore University or the State Government on the date of commencement of the Amendment Act shall be deemed to be an application made for affiliation to the appropriate University established under sub-section (1-A) of Section 3 and shall be dealt with and disposed of by the Gulbarga University or the Mangalore University, or the State Government, as the case may be, on this basis; (b) all colleges, attached hostels, other buildings together with the articles of furniture, libraries, books, laboratories, stores, instruments, apparatus, appliances and equipment and all other property both movable and immovable - (i) at the postgraduate centres at Gulbarga and at Nandi-halli in Bellary District and belonging to the Karnataka University; and (ii) at the postgraduate centre at Konaje and belonging to the Mysore University, shall, with effect on and from the date of commencement of the Amendment Act stand transferred to the Gulbarga University or the Mangalore University, as the case may be, established under sub-section (1-A) of Section 3; (c) all proceedings, including legal proceedings, pending immediately before the date of commencement of the Amendment Act relating to the affairs of - (i) the Karnataka University and arising out of the University Area of the Gulbarga University; (ii) the Mysore University and arising out of the University Area of the Manglore University, shall, with effect on and from that date, stand transferred to or be continued by or against, the Gulbarga University or the Mangalore University, as the case may be, established under sub-section (1-A) of Section 3".

(3) in sub-section (3), after the word "inspection", the words "or inquiry" shall be inserted.

Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 14--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 51 of the principal Act, the following new sections shall be inserted, namely - "51-A. Appointments to part-time posts -(1) There shall be a Board of Appointment for selecting persons for appointment as part-time Lecturers, Assistant Professors, Readers and Professors in the University. (2) The Board shall consist of - (i) the Vice-Chancellor-Ex officio Chairman; (ii) the two experts nominated by the Chancellor; (iii) the Head of the Department concerned; (iv) the Secretary to the Government of Karnataka in the Education Department or a person nominated by him: Provided that for appointment to any post in the Law College the two experts shall be the Advocate General in Karnataka and the Secretary to Government of Karnataka in the Department of Law and Parliamentary Affairs. (3) The Registrar shall act as the Secretary of the Board. (4) Every part-time post shall be duly and widely advertised with all necessary particulars and shall be filled by selection after interviewing the candidates: Provided that the Board may invite eminent persons in the field to fill any post without such advertisement and interview. No part-time appointment shall be made for a period exceeding one year. (5) The quorum for a meeting of the Board shall be three. (6) The Board shall prepare a list of persons selected and forward the list to the Syndicate which shall make the appointment. 51-B. Temporary appointment -(1) Notwithstanding anything in Sections 49, 50, 51 and 51-A, the Vice-Chancellor may make temporary appointments of not more than six months duration to posts of Lecturers and such non-teaching staff as may be specified in the Statutes provided such posts are either temporary or appointments to such posts cannot be made in accordance with Sections 49, 50, 51 and 51-A without delay. (2) The appointments under sub-section (1) shall be made in the manner prescribed by the Statutes. 51-C. Reservation of promotional vacancies for Scheduled Castes etc -Where appointment to any post in the University is made by promotion the authority empowered to make such appointment shall comply with the orders issued by the State Government from time to time for reservation of posts in promotional vacancies for the Scheduled Castes, the Scheduled Tribes and other socially and educationally backward class of citizens."

Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 17--> State(s): Karnataka Section 71 of the principal Act shall be renumbered as sub-section (1) thereof and after sub-section (1) as so renumbered, the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(2) Notwithstanding anything contained in this Act - (a) the first Vice-Chancellor of the Gulbarga University and the Mangalore University shall be appointed by the Chancellor in consultation with the State Government for a period of three years; (b) the first Registrar, Controller of Examinations, Finance Officer and Librarian of the Gulbarga University and the Mangalore University shall be appointed by the Chancellor on contract basis for a period not exceeding three years after ascertaining and considering the opinion of the Vice-Chancellor; (c) subject to clauses (d), (e) and (f); (i) appointment of Professors in the Gulbarga University and the Mangalore University shall, during the first three years after the commencement of the Amendment Act be made by the Chancellor on contract basis in consultation with the Vice-Chancellor; (ii) appointment of other ministerial and non-ministerial staff shall be made by the Vice-Chancellor by appointing such of the persons in the office of the Special Officer as are willing to be so appointed and by deputation of officers of equivalent grade in the service of the Karnataka University or the Mysore University or by direct recruitment; Explanation -In this clause "Special Officer" means the officer appointed as officer on special duty in Government Order No. DPAR 51 SAS 80, dated 12th February, 1980. (d) every teacher and other employee - (i) of the Karnataka University and serving in or attached to the Postgraduate Centre at Gulbarga and at Nandihalli immediately before the date of commencement of the Amendment Act, shall, as from such date, stand transferred to the appropriate cadre or category of posts in the Gulbarga University; (ii) of the Mysore University and serving in or attached to the Postgraduate Centre at Konaje immediately before the date of commencement of the Amendment Act, shall, as from such date stand transferred to the appropriate cadre or category of posts in the Mangalore University: Provided that any teacher or other employee so transferred may, within ninety days or within such further period not exceeding six months as the State Government may by order direct, from the date of commencement of the Amendment Act apply to the Chancellor for retransfer to the University of which he was an employee prior to such transfer and the decision of the Chancellor thereon shall be final; (e) any teacher or other employee of the Karnataka University or the Mysore University who desires to get transferred to the Gulbarga University or the Mangalore University may, within ninety days or within such further period not exceeding six months as the State Government may by order direct, from the date of commencement of the Amendment Act apply to the Chancellor for such transfer and the decision of the Chancellor thereon shall be final; (f) the Chancellor may, in consultation with the Vice-Chancellor of the Karnataka University and the Mysore University determine the excess teaching, ministerial and other staff, if any, in these Universities and transfer the staff so in excess to the Gulbarga University or the Mangalore University, as the case may be, after following such principles and procedure as he may consider appropriate; (g) the financial estimates for the year 1980-81 in respect of the Gulbarga and the Mangalore Universities shall be determined by the State Government".
Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 18--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 72 of the principal Act, after sub-section (3), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(4) Until Statutes, Ordinances, Regulations and Rules are made by the Gulbarga University and the Mangalore University, the Statutes, Ordinances, Regulations and Rules applicable to - (a) the Karnataka University as in force on the date of commencement of the Amendment Act shall be deemed to be the Statutes, Ordinances, Regulations and Rules made under this Act by the Gulbarga University; (b) the Mysore University as in force on the date of commencement of the Amendment Act shall be deemed to be Statutes, Ordinances, Regulations and Rules made under this Act by the Mangalore University: Provided that the Vice-Chancellor may, with the approval of the Chancellor obtained through the State Government, make such modifications and adaptations therein as he may consider necessary".
Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 19--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 73 of the principal Act, insert the following at the end, namely - "and in the case of Universities established under sub-section (1-A) of Section 3 not later than one year from the date of commencement of the Amendment Act".
Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 20--> State(s): Karnataka Section 74 of the principal Act shall be renumbered as sub-section (1) thereof and after sub-section (1) as so renumbered, the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(2) Notwithstanding anything contained in this Act, the Statutes, the Ordinances, the Regulations, and the Rules, any student who immediately prior to the commencement of the Amendment Act was studying for a degree of the Karnataka University or the Mysore University in an educational institution situated within the territorial jurisdiction of the Gulbarga University or the Mangalore University, in accordance with the Regulations in force, may, until such examination is provided by the Gulbarga University or the Mangalore University, as the case may be, be admitted to the examination of the Karnataka University or the Mysore University, as the case may be, and be conferred the degree of the Karnataka University or the Mysore University, as the case may be, for which he qualifies on the results of such examination."
Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 21--> State(s): Karnataka (1) Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Ordinance, 1980 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 4 of 1980) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
