Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Panchayat Raj Act, 1993. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Panchayat Raj Act, 1993 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1993) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Fifty-first year of the Republic of India as follows:
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 2000.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on First day of April, 2000
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 44 of the Karnataka Panchayat Raj Act, 1993 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1993) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (1), after the words, figures and brackets "under sub-section (8) of Section 5", the words "or immediately before the expiry of term of office of Adhyaksha and Upadhyaksha" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 45 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1) after the words, figures and brackets "under sub-section (8) of Section 5" the words "and immediately before the expiry of term of office of Adhyaksha and Upadhyaksha" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 46 of the principal Act, for sub-section (1), the following shall be substituted, namely - "(1) The term of office of every Adhyaksha and every Upadhyaksha of the Grama Panchayat shall, save as otherwise provided in this Act, be thirty months from the date of his election or till he ceases to be a member of Grama Panchayat, whichever is earlier".
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 61 of the principal Act, after sub-section (1), the following sub-sections shall be inserted, namely - "(1-A) Election of members of Standing Committee shall be held as soon as may be after the general election of members of Grama Panchayat or on its reconstitution or establishment under this Act or immediately before the expiry of term of office of the members of the Standing Committee. (1-B) The term of office of every member of the Standing Committee shall, save as otherwise provided in this Act be thirty months from the date of the election or till he ceases to be a member of the Grama Panchayat, whichever is earlier".
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Sections 121, 122 and 123 of the principal Act, for the words "State Election Commission" wherever they occur, the word "Government" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 124 of the principal Act, for the words "subject to the general or special order of the State Election Commission, the Deputy Commissioner" the words "the Deputy Commissioner" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 133 of the principal Act, for the words "notified by the State Election Commission" the words "reported to the Commissioner who shall notify the names" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 138 of the principal Act in sub-section (2), for the words "State Election Commission" the word "Government" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 10--> State(s): Karnataka In Sections 160, 161 and 162 of the principal Act, for the words "State Election Commission" wherever they occur, the word "Government" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 11--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 163 of the principal Act, for the words "subject to the general or special order of the State Election Commission, the Deputy Commissioner", the words "The Government" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 12--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 172 of the principal Act for the words "notified by the State Election Commission" the words "reported to the Government which shall notify the names" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 13--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 177 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), for the words "State Election Commission", the word "Government" shall be substituted.
