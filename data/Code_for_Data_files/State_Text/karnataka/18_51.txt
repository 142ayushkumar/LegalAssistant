Karnataka Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1976.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of April, 1976. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-eighth day of April, 1976
Karnataka Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6-B of the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), after the words "whose total turnover is ten lakh rupees or more in a year", the words "and from every dealer liable to pay tax under Section 25-B" shall be and shall always be deemed to have been inserted with effect from 1st April, 1975.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka For sub-section (1) of Section 25-B of the principal Act, the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely - "(1) Notwithstanding anything in Section 5, there shall be levied and collected a tax on the last purchase of sugarcane in the State at the rate of - (i) rupees nine per tonne, when purchased by the manufacturer of jaggery; and (ii) rupees sixteen per tonne, in other cases".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In the Second Schedule to the principal Act - (1) in Column 3 of Serial No. 44, for the words "Six per cent", the words "Eight per cent" shall be substituted; (2) in Serial No. 61 - (a) in column 2, after the words "and all other accessories", the words "but excluding pumpsets with electric motors of not more than 10 H.P." shall be added; (b) in Column 3, for the words "Eight per cent", the words "Ten per cent" shall be substituted; (3) after Serial No. 61-A, the following new entry shall be inserted, namely - "61-B. Pumpsets with electric motors of not more than 10 H.P - Eight per cent"; (4) in Column 3 of Serial No. 79, for the words "Six per cent", the words "Eight per cent" shall be substituted; (5) in Column 3 of Serial No. 89, for the words "Six per cent", the words "Eight per cent" shall be substituted; (6) in Column 3 of Serial No. 119, for the words "Six per cent", the words "Eight per cent" shall be substituted; (7) in Column 3 of Serial No. 121, for the words "Six per cent", the words "Eight per cent" shall be substituted; (8) in Column 3 of Serial No. 123, for the words "Four per cent", the words "Six per cent" shall be substituted; (9) in Column 3 of Serial No. 125, for the words "Six per cent", the words "Seven per cent" shall be substituted; (10) after Serial No. 135, the following entry shall be added, namely - "136. Articles of food and drink sold, supplied or distributed to a person while witnessing a cabaret Forty per cent".
