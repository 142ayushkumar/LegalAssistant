Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Seventeenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1966.1 2 (2) It shall come into force on the First day of January, 1967. Received the assent of the Governor on the Fourteenth day of December, 1966 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
