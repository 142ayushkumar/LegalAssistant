Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1972_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1957), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1972_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1972.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of July, 1972. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1972_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Part 'A' of the Schedule to the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1957) - (1) In Item 4, for the words "Motor vehicles plying for hire", the words, figure and letter "Motor vehicles other than those mentioned in Item 4-A plying for hire" shall be substituted; (2) After Item 4, the following Item shall be inserted, namely:- "4-A. Motor vehicles plying for hire or reward used for transport of passengers in respect of which contract carriage permits have been issued under the Motor Vehicles Act, 1939 and permitted to carry more than five persons excluding the driver for every passenger which the vehicle is permitted to carry. 100.00 150.00"
