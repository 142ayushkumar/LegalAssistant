Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 34 of 1957) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirtieth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1979.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Twenty-seventh day of December, 1978. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twentieth day of February, 1979 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Schedule to the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 34 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act), after the entries relating to item "20 the conveyance", the following proviso shall be inserted, namely:- "Provided that the duty in respect of any instrument of conveyance effected by the Bangalore Development Authority Constituted under the Bangalore Development Authority Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1976), the City Improvement Trust Board, Karnataka constituted under the City of Karnataka Improvement Act, 1903 (Karnataka Act 3 of 1903), the Karnataka Housing Board constituted under the Karnataka Housing Board Act, 1962 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1963), the Improvement Boards constituted under the Karnataka Improvement Boards Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 11 of 1976) and such other authorities as may be specified by the Government shall be payable at the rates specified above on the amount or value of consideration for such conveyance as set forth in the instrument."
Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Ordinance, 1978 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 14 of 1978) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal any action taken of any appointment, notification, order, scheme, rule, form or bye-law made or issued under the Principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been taken, made or issued under the Principal Act as amended by this Act and shall continue in force accordingly unless and until superseded by any action taken or any appointment, notification, order, scheme, rule, form or bye-law made or issued under the Principal Act as amended by this Act.
