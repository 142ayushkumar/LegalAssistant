Karnataka Private Nursing Homes (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Private Nursing Homes (Regulation) Act, 1976. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Private Nursing Homes (Regulation) Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 75 of 1976); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-eighth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Private Nursing Homes (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Private Nursing Homes (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1977.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Thirtieth day of December, 1976. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-first day of April, 1977 Repealed by Karnataka Act No. 22 of 2000, dated 29-11-2000
Karnataka Private Nursing Homes (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the proviso to Section 3 of the Karnataka Private Nursing Homes (Regulation) Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 75 of 1976), for the words "two months", the words "eight months" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Private Nursing Homes (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka Private Nursing Homes (Regulation) (Amendment) Ordinance, 1976 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 37 of 1976) is hereby repealed.
