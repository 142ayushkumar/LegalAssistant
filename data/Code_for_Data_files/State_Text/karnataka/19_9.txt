Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, Use or Sale Therein (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, Use or Sale Therein Act, 1979. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, Use or Sale Therein Act, 1979, (Karnataka Act 27 of 1979) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be, it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, Use or Sale Therein (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, Use or Sale Therein Act, 19811 2 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Eighteenth day of February, 1981. Received the assent of the Governor on the Fourth day of April, 1981. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000.
Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, Use or Sale Therein (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 1 of the Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, Use or Sale Therein Act, 1979 (Karnataka Act 27 of 1979) (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act) - (1) In the heading for the words "and extent" the words "extent and commencement" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted; (2) After sub-section (2) the following sub-section shall be and shall be deemed always to have been inserted, namely:- "(3) It shall come into force on the First day of October, 1980."
Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, Use or Sale Therein (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Principal Act, for the words "as may be specified by the State Government", the words "and from such date as may be specified, whether prospectively or retrospectively, by the State Government and different dates" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, Use or Sale Therein (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka For Item 1 of the Schedule, the following Item shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted, namely:- "(1) All varieties of textiles, namely cotton, woollen, or artificial silk including rayon or nylon whether manufactured in mills, powerlooms or hand-looms and hosiery cloth in lengths but excluding khadi, cotton hand-loom fabrics, silk fabrics, and artificial silk hand-loom fabrics."
Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, Use or Sale Therein (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, Use or Sale Therein (Amendment) Ordinance, 1981 (Karnataka Ordinance 3 of 1981) is hereby repealed. (2) The Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, Use or Sale Therein (Repeal) Ordinance, 1980 (Karnataka Ordinance 11 of 1980) is hereby repealed with effect from Twenty-fifth day of October, 1980. (3) Notwithstanding such repeal any action taken or any appointment, notification, order, scheme, rule, form or bye-law made or issued under the Principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance (Ordinance 3 of 1981) shall be deemed to have been taken, made or issued under the Principal Act as amended by this Act and shall continue in force accordingly unless and until superseded by any action taken or any appointment, notification, order, scheme, rule, form or bye-law made or issued under the Principal Act as amended by this Act. (4) Any amount collected or deemed to have been collected by any dealer by way of tax or purporting to be by way of tax under the Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods into Local Areas for Consumption, Use or Sale Therein Act, 1979 (Karnataka Act 27 of 1979) (hereinafter referred to as the 1979 Act) or the Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods into a local Area for Consumption, Use or Sale Therein Act, 1980 (Karnataka Act 21 of 1980) hereinafter referred to as the 1980 Act) - (i) during the period between the First day of June, 1979 and the First day of October, 1980; or (ii) during the period between the First day of October, 1980 and the 18th day of February, 1981 in excess of one per cent. Notwithstanding anything contained in the said 1979 Act or the 1980 Act or in any other law for the time being in force, be determined in the prescribed manner and the amount so determined shall, after giving such dealer a reasonable opportunity of being heard, be collected from him by the assessing authority as if it is a tax due from such a dealer under the said 1979 Act and the said amount shall not be adjusted towards any tax due under the 1979 Act for the period subsequent to the First day of October, 1980.
