Madras Hindu Religious and Charitable Endowments (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1966_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Madras Hindu Religious and Charitable Endowments Act, 1951, as in force in the Madras Area. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Madras Hindu Religious and Charitable Endowments Act, 1951 (Madras Act 19 of 1951), as in force in the Madras Area; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Seventeenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Madras Hindu Religious and Charitable Endowments (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Madras Hindu Religious and Charitable Endowments (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1966.1 Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixteenth day of December, 1966
Madras Hindu Religious and Charitable Endowments (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 39 of the Madras Hindu Religious and Charitable Endowments Act, 1951 (Madras Act 19 of 1951), as in force in the Madras Area, for the words "five persons", the words "nine persons" shall be substituted.
