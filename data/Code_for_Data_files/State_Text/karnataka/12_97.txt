Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Legal Aid Board Act, 1981. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Legal Aid Board Act, 1981 (Karnataka Act 32 of 1981) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1983.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Twelfth day of September, 1983
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4 of the Karnataka Legal Aid Board Act, 1981 (Karnataka Act 32 of 1981) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (1) in clause (k), for the words "eight persons" the words "nine persons" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 9 of the principal Act, for the words, "and the ex-officio members" the words "and the officers of the Government" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 14 of the principal Act, after clause (1), the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(1a) to undertake strategic and preventive legal service programme;".
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 16 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), after clause (r), the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(ra) constitution of legal aid committees and centres in the districts and taluks, their functions and other matters, including the allowances payable to the members thereof for attending the meetings of the committee or sub-committees thereof."
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 18 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), in clause (a), the words, "including women and children" shall be added at the end.
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 31 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), clause (f) shall be omitted.
