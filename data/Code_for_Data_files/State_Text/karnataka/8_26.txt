Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners Act, 1961. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1961) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1976.1 Received the assent of the Governor on the Fourth day of March, 1976
Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 16 and Section 37-A of the Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1961) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for the words "Director of Health and Family Planning Services in Karnataka", the words "Director of Indian Systems of Medicine and Homeopathy in Karnataka" shall be and shall be deemed to have been substituted with effect from the 16th day of April, 1975.
Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka Notwithstanding anything contained in the principal Act, no act of the court of examiners constituted by the State Government under Section 16 or the committee appointed by the State Government under Section 37A of the principal Act, with the Director of Indian Systems of Medicine and Homeopathy in Karnataka as the Chairman shall be deemed to the invalid by only that the said Director was not at the time of his appointment as Chairman competent to the appoint as such.
Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Ordinance, 1975 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 16 of 1975) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
