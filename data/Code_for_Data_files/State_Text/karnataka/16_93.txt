Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1974. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 32 of 1974), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Amendment) Act, 19811 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Thirtieth day of September, 1980. First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, on the Fifteenth day of April, 1981, Received the assent of the Governor on the Tenth day of April, 1981
Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 32 of 1974) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in clause (e), after item (iv) and before the Explanation, the following items shall be inserted, namely - "(v) a wakf under the management of the Karnataka State Board of Wakfs; (vi) the State Government and the Government of Andhra Pradesh jointly, and under the management or administrative control of the Tungabhadra Board constituted by the Government of India under sub-section (4) of Section 66 of the Andhra State Act, 1953 (Central Act 30 of 1953)".
Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the principal Act, in clause (a),- (a) in sub-clause (ii), the word "and" at the end shall be omitted; (b) in sub-clause (iii), at the end, a semicolon and the word "and" shall be inserted; and (c) after sub-clause (iii), the following sub-clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(iv) in respect of any other premises, an officer of the State Civil Services or of any other authority".
Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Amendment) Ordinance, 1980 (Karnataka Ordinance 9 of 1980) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
