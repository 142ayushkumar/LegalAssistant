Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty- seventh year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1976.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the twenty-fourth day of December, 1975. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twelfth day of March, 1976 Karnataka Act No. 22 of 1976 repealed by the Karnataka Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 49 of the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in clause (a), after the word "invested", the words "or delegated" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka Section 94-A of the principal Act shall be omitted.
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka All applications pending before the Deputy Commissioner under Section 94-A and all proceedings by way of appeal or revision pending before any authority against the orders of the Deputy Commissioner under the said section shall abate.
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Land Revenue (Second Amendment) Ordinance, 1975 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 30 of 1975) is hereby, repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
