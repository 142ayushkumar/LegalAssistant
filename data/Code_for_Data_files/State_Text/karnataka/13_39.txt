Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Second Amendment) Act, 1979_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances Act, 1956. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirtieth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Second Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Second Amendment) Act, 1979.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the thirtieth day of June, 1979. Received the assent of the Governor on the Fourteenth day of October, 1979 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Second Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In clause (b) of sub-section (2) of Section 12 of the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) for the words "seventy-five paise" the words "one rupee" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Second Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Second Amendment) Ordinance, 1979 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 3 of 1979) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
