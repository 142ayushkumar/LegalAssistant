Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1957). Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Nineteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1968.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Fifth day of October, 1968.
Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1957) in the first proviso to sub-section (1) of Section 4, for the words "three hundred rupees", the words "five hundred rupees" shall be substituted and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
