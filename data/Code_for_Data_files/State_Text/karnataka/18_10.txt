Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Seventeenth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1966.1 2 (2) It shall come into force on the First day of April, 1966. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Eleventh day of March, 1966
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 2 of the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (a) in sub-clause (i) of clause (k), for the words "The Central Government and a State Government other than the Government of Karnataka", the words "an industrial, commercial or trading undertaking of the Government of Karnataka, the Central Government, a State Government of any State, other than the State of Karnataka" shall be substituted; (b) in clause (t), explanation (2) shall be omitted; (c) in clause (v), item (i) of the explanation shall be omitted.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the principal Act - (1) in sub-section (1) - (a) for the words "two per cent", the words "three per cent", shall be substituted; (b) in the first proviso, for the words "one and a half per cent", the words "two per cent" shall be substituted; (c) in the second proviso - (i) for the words "parched rice and beaten rice", the words "parched rice, beaten rice and bran" shall be substituted; (ii) for the words "one per cent", the words "one and a half per cent" shall be substituted; (2) in clause (a) of sub-section (5), for the words "seven thousand and five hundred rupees", the words "ten thousand rupees" shall be substituted; (3) sub-sections (5-A) and (5-B) shall be omitted.

Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 18 of the principal Act - (1) in sub-section (1) - (a) after the words "by way of tax", the words "or purporting to be by way of tax", shall be inserted; (b) the second and third provisos shall be omitted; (2) in sub-section (2), after the words "by way of tax", the words "or purporting to be by way of tax" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 18 of the Principal Act, the following section shall be and shall always be deemed to have been inserted, namely - "18-A. Penalty for collection in contravention of Section 18 - If any person collects any amount by way of tax or purporting to be by way of tax in contravention of the provisions of sub-section (1) or sub-section (2) of Section 18, or if any registered dealer or licensed dealer collects any amount by way of tax or purporting to be by way of tax in excess of the amount payable by him, whether or not any tax is due from such person or dealer under this Act in respect of the transaction in which he collects such amount, the assessing authority may, after giving such person or dealer a reasonable opportunity of being heard, by order in writing, impose upon him by way of penalty a sum not exceeding one and a half times such amount: Provided that no proceedings under this section shall be commenced after a period of five years from the expiry of the year in which the amount has been collected: Provided further that no prosecution for an offence under Section 29 shall be instituted in respect of the same facts on which a penalty has been imposed under this section."
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 10--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 26 of the principal Act, the proviso shall be omitted.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 11--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 29 of the principal Act - (1) in sub-section (1), clause (c) shall be omitted; (2) in sub-section (2), after clause (c), the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(cc) collects any amount by way of tax or purporting to be by way of tax in contravention of sub-section (1) or sub-section (2) of Section 18; or."
