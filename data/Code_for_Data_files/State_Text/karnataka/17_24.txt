Karnataka Relief Undertakings (Special Provisions) (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Relief Undertakings (Special Provisions) Act, 1977 Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Relief Undertakings (Special Provisions) Act, 1977 (Karnataka Act 24 of 1977) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Fortieth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Relief Undertakings (Special Provisions) (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Relief Undertakings (Special Provisions) (Amendment) Act, 19891 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Ninth day of January, 1989. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-ninth day of May, 1989
Karnataka Relief Undertakings (Special Provisions) (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Karnataka Relief Undertakings (Special Provisions) Act, 1977 (Karnataka Act 24 of 1977) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in the proviso, for the words "ten years", the words "twelve years" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted
Karnataka Relief Undertakings (Special Provisions) (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Relief Undertakings (Special Provisions) (Amendment) Ordinance, 1989 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 1 of 1989) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken or purporting to have been done or taken under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
