Karnataka Prevention of Cow Slaughter and Cattle Preservation (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Prevention of Cow Slaughter and Cattle Preservation Act, 1964. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Prevention of Cow Slaughter and Cattle Preservation Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1964); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Seventeenth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Prevention of Cow Slaughter and Cattle Preservation (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Karnataka Prevention of Cow Slaughter and Cattle Preservation (Amendment) Act, 1966.1 2 Received the assent of the Governor on the Nineteenth day of August, 1966 Repealed by Karnataka Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Prevention of Cow Slaughter and Cattle Preservation (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 16 of the Karnataka Prevention of Cow Slaughter and Cattle Preservation Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1964), after clause (b), the following clause shall be added, namely - "(c) any cow or animal belonging to the Central Government in the Ministry of Defence, slaughter of which is certified by a Veterinary Officer of the Indian Army to be necessary on the ground that it is suffering from an incurable disease or injury."
