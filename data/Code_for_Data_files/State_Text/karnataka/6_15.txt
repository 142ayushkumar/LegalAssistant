Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Forest Act, 1963. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Forest Act, 1963 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1964) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1983.1 (2) It shall come into force on the First day of April, 1983. Received the assent of the Governor on the First day of April, 1983
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 98-A of the Karnataka Forest Act, 1963 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1964), in sub-section (1), the following proviso shall be inserted, namely - "Provided that the tax levied and paid under this sub-section on the disposal of forest produce specified in column (2) of the Table below to the categories of persons or industries specified in column (3) thereof shall be twelve per cent on the amount of consideration. Table Sl. No. Forest produce When disposed of to (1) (2) (3) 1. Timber, Firewood, Grass, Charcoal and Eucalyptus Industries 2. Bamboo, reeds and canes Pulp and Paper Industries except Cottage Industries; 3. Sandalwood (a) Sandalwood Oil Factories; and  (b) Others, except;  (i) Artisans  (ii) Religious Institutions; and  (iii) Cottage Industries 4. Minor Forest produce as defined in the rule but not falling under Serial numbers 1 and 2 Industries except Large scale Multipurpose Co-operative Societies (LAMPS)".
