Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1957_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1956. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1957_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1957.1 2 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000. Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixth Day of July, 1957
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1957_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka After the proviso to Section 11 of the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1956, the following further proviso shall be added and shall always be deemed to have been added, namely - "Provided further that - (i) in the case of a member of a new Legislative Assembly constituted after a general election, the salary shall be paid only from the date of commencement of the duration of that Assembly or if the old Legislative Assembly has been dissolved before the aforesaid date, then from the date of such dissolution; and (ii) in the case of a member of the Legislative Council, the salary shall be paid only from the date on which his term of office as a member of the said Council commences."
