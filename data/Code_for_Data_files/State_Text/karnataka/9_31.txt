Industrial Disputes (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1962_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, in its application to the State of Karnataka. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 (Central Act 14 of 1947), in its application to the State of Karnataka; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Industrial Disputes (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1962_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Industrial Disputes (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1962.1 (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Karnataka. (3) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the President on the Twenty-second day of January, 1963
Industrial Disputes (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1962_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (3) of Section 7-A of the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 (Central Act 14 of 1947) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), after clause (a), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(aa) he is, or has been a District Judge, or".
Industrial Disputes (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1962_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In the First Schedule to the principal Act, after item No. 10, the following item shall be added, namely:- "11. Oxygen and Acetylene".
