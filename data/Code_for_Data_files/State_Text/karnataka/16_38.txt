Karnataka Preservation of Trees (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Preservation of Trees Act, 1976. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Preservation of Trees Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 76 of 1976). Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirtieth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Preservation of Trees (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Preservation of Trees (Amendment) Act, 1979.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Twenty-second day of September, 1978. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-second day of February, 1979 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Preservation of Trees (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 27 of the Karnataka Preservation of Trees Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 76 of 1976), (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) the following section shall be inserted, namely: "27-A. Exemption of Certain Areas from the Operation of this Act - The State Government may, by notification and subject to such restrictions and conditions as may be specified in such notification exempt any area or areas from the provisions of this Act."
Karnataka Preservation of Trees (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 29 of the principal Act, for sub-section (2), the following sub-section shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted, namely:- "(2) Notwithstanding such repeal any action taken or any appointment, notification, order, scheme, rule, form or bye-law made or issued under the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been taken, made or issued under this Act and shall continue in force accordingly unless and until superseded by any action taken or any appointment, notification, order, scheme, rule, form or bye-law made or issued under this Act."
Karnataka Preservation of Trees (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Preservation of Trees (Amendment) Ordinance, 1978 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 10 of 1978) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal any action taken or any appointment notification, order, scheme, rule, form or bye-law made or issued under the principal Act as amended by the said ordinance shall be deemed to have been taken, made or issued under the principal Act as amended by this Act and shall continue in force accordingly unless and until superseded by any action taken or any appointment, notification, order, scheme, rule, form or bye-law made or issued under the principal Act as amended this Act.
