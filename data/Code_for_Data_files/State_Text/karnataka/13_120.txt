Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 2006_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Municipal Corporations Act, 1976. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Municipal Corporations Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1977) for the purposes here in after appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the fifty- seventh year of Republic of India, as follows:
Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 2006_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 2006. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 2006_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Karnataka Municipal Corporations Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1977), in section 7, in sub-section (1), in clause (a), for the word "hundred", the words "one hundred fifty" shall be substituted.
