National Law School of India (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the National Law School of India Act, 1986. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the National Law School of India Act, 1986 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1986) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows -
National Law School of India (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the National Law School of India (Amendment) Act, 1992.1 2 (2) The provisions of Section 4 shall come into force at once and the rest of the provisions shall be deemed to have come into force on the Fourteenth day of May, 1992. Received the assent of the Governor on the First day of February, 1993 Repealed by Karnataka Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
National Law School of India (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 9 of the National Law School of India Act, 1986 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1986) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for the words "supreme authority" the words "Chief Advisory Body" shall be substituted.
National Law School of India (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In the Schedule to the principal Act, for clause 5, the following shall be substituted, namely - "5. Powers of the General Council - The General Council shall have the following powers namely - (1) To review from time to time the broad policies and programme of the School and suggest measures for the improvement and development of the School. (2) To consider and pass the resolution on the Annual Report, financial estimates and the audit reports on such accounts. (3) To perform such other functions as it may deem necessary for the efficient functioning and administration of the School".
National Law School of India (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The National Law School of India (Amendment) (No. 2) Ordinance, 1992 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 9 of 1992) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under principal Act, as amended by this Act.
