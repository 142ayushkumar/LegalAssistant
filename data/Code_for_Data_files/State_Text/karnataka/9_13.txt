Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka (Religious and Charitable) Inams Abolition Act, 1955, the Karnataka (Sandur Area) Inams Abolition Act, 1976 and the Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition Act, 1977. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka (Religious and Charitable) Inams Abolition Act, 1955 (Karnataka Act 18 of 1955), the Karnataka (Sandur Area) Inams Abolition Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 54 of 1976) and the Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition Act, 1977 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1978) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Act, 1987.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Twenty-seventh day of November, 1986. x x x x x Received the assent of the Governor on the Seventh day of March, 1987
Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10 of the Karnataka (Sandur Area) Inams Abolition Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 54 of 1976), for the words and figures "31st March, 1984", the words and figures "30th day of June, 1987" shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 11 of the Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition Act, 1977 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1978) - (i) for the words and figures "31st March, 1984" the words and figures "30th day of June, 1987" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted; and (ii) in the proviso, for the words and figures "Thirty-first day of December, 1984", the words and figures "30th day of June, 1987" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Ordinance, 1986 (Karnataka Ordinance 16 of 1986) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the Karnataka (Religious and Charitable) Inams Abolition Act, 1955, the Karnataka (Sandur Area), Inams Abolition Act, 1976 and the Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition Act, 1977 as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the said Acts, as amended by this Act.
