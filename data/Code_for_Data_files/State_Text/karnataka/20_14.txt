Karnataka Urban Water Supply and Drainage Board (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Urban Water Supply and Drainage Board Act, 1973. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Urban Water Supply and Drainage Board Act, 1973 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1974). Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Urban Water Supply and Drainage Board (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Urban Water Supply and Drainage Board (Amendment) Act, 1977.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-eighth day of July, 1977 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Urban Water Supply and Drainage Board (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 29 of the Karnataka Urban Water Supply and Drainage Board Act, 1973 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1974), after clause (b) of sub-section (4), the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(bb) any Scheduled Bank; or."
