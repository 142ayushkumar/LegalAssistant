Karnataka Municipal Laws (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend certain laws relating to Municipal Councils and Municipal Corporations as in force in the State of Karnataka. Whereas, it is expedient to amend certain laws relating to Municipal Councils and Municipal Corporations as in force in the State of Karnataka. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Municipal Laws (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Municipal Laws (Amendment) Act, 1976.1 2 (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Karnataka. (3) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the twentieth day of October, 1975. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-third day of February, 1976 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Municipal Laws (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 99 of the Karnataka Municipalities Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1964), for the words "the amount of the consideration for the sale, the value of the property in the case of a gift or the amount secured by the mortgage, the value of the property of the greater value in the case of exchange", the words "the market value of the property which is the subject matter of sale or gift or the amount secured by the mortgage, the market value of the property of greatest value which is the subject matter of exchange" shall be substituted.
