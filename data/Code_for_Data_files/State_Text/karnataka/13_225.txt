Mysore Subversive Activities (Repealing) Act, 1946_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka Whereas, it is expedient to repeal the Mysore Subversive Activities Act, 1944 (III of 1944). Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by Section 30 of the Government of Mysore Act, 1940 (XVIII of 1940). It is hereby enacted as follows -
Mysore Subversive Activities (Repealing) Act, 1946_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Mysore Subversive Activities (Repealing) Act, 1946.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of His Highness the Maharaja on the 28th day of May, 1946
Mysore Subversive Activities (Repealing) Act, 1946_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka The Mysore Subversive Activities Act, 1944, is hereby repealed.
