Registration (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1975_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Registration Act, 1908 in its application to the State of Karnataka. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Registration Act, 1908 (Central Act 16 of 1908), in its application to the State of Karnataka; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Registration (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1975_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Registration (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 19751 . (2) It shall come into force at once. First published in the Karnataka Gazette Extraordinary on Twenty-fourth day of July, 1975, Received the assent of the President on the Eighteenth day of July, 1975
Registration (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1975_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 80 of the Registration Act, 1908 (Central Act 16 of 1908), the following section shall be inserted, namely:- "80-A Deficient amounts of fees payable and their recovery - (1) If the value of the property has been increased under Section 45-A of the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 34 of 1957), consequential increase in the fee for the registration of documents under this Act shall be paid by the person liable to pay the same within a period of thirty days from the date, the order determining the market value of the property is communicated to him. (2) The fee payable under sub-section (1) may be recovered as an arrear of land revenue".
