Karnataka (Sandur Area) and Certain Inams Abolition (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka (Sandur Area) Inams Abolition Act, 1976 and the Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition Act, 1977. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka (Sandur Area) Inams Abolition Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 54 of 1976) and the Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition Act, 1977 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1978) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirtieth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka (Sandur Area) and Certain Inams Abolition (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka (Sandur Area) and Certain Inams Abolition (Amendment) Act, 1979.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Thirteenth day of July 1979. Received the assent of the Governor on the Seventeenth day of October, 1979
Karnataka (Sandur Area) and Certain Inams Abolition (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Karnataka (Sandur Area) Inams Abolition Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 54 of 1976) - (1) for sub-section (4) of Section 1, the following sub-section shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted, namely - "(4) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, in the official Gazette appoint." (2) in Section 10, for the words "within six months from the appointed day", the words and figures "on or before 31st March, 1980" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
Karnataka (Sandur Area) and Certain Inams Abolition (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 11 of the Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition Act, 1977 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1978), for the words "within six months from the appointed date" the words and figures "on or before 31st March 1980" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
Karnataka (Sandur Area) and Certain Inams Abolition (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka (Sandur Area) and Certain Inams Abolition (Amendment) Ordinance, 1979 is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the Karnataka (Sandur Area) Inams Abolition Act 1976 (Karnataka Act 54 of 1976) and the Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition Act, 1977 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1978) as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the said Acts as amended by this Act.
