Karnataka Laws (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend certain Karnataka Laws. Whereas it is expedient to amend certain Karnataka Laws for the purpose hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty- ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Laws (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Laws (Amendment) Act, 1978.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Thirteenth day of September, 1978
Karnataka Laws (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka (1) In the Acts mentioned in column 2 of the Schedule I, except to the extent specified in column 3 thereof for the word "Mysore" wherever it occurs, the word "Karnataka" shall be and shall always be deemed to have been substituted. (2) The Act mentioned in column 2 of Schedule II, shall be amended as specified in column 3 thereof.
Karnataka Laws (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section Sch.1--> State(s): Karnataka (See Section 2) Sl. No. Acts Exception 1 2 3 The Mysore Agricultural Debtors (Temporary Protection) Act, 1973 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1973).
