Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1972_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1961. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 9 of 1962); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1972_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1972.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-eighth day of December, 1972
Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1972_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act of 9 of 1962) hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), the words "at such time and place and" shall be omitted.
Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1972_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In clause (b) of sub-section (2) of Section 30 of the principal Act, the words "time at which and the place and" shall be omitted.
