Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances Act, 1956. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1957) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-eight Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1987.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of April, 1987. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-third day of April, 1987
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (i) in sub-section (1), for the words "one thousand seven hundred and fifty rupees", and "six thousand rupees", the words "two thousand and five hundred rupees" and "ten thousand rupees" shall respectively be substituted; (ii) in sub-section (2), for the words "one thousand seven hundred and fifty rupees" and "ten thousand rupees", the words "two thousand and five hundred rupees" and "fifteen thousand rupees" shall respectively be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Sections 4, 10-B, 10-F and 10-K of the principal Act, for the words "five hundred rupees" wherever they occur, the words "one thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), for the words "nine hundred and fifty rupees", the words "two thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 8 of the principal Act - (a) in sub-section (2) - (i) in clause (b), for the words "one rupee and fifty paise", the words "two rupees and fifty paise" shall be substituted; (ii) in clause (d), for the words "rupees fifty" and "seventy-five rupees", the words "rupees one hundred" and "one hundred and fifty rupees" shall respectively be substituted; (b) in sub-section (3) - (i) in clause (a), for the words "to such terms in regard to travelling and other expenses as the Governor may; in each case, by order, determine", the words "to the same terms in regard to travelling and other expenses as a Minister of the State Government" shall be substituted; (ii) clause (b) shall be omitted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10 of the principal Act - (i) in sub-section (1), for the words "one thousand and five hundred rupees", the words "two thousand two hundred and fifty rupees" shall be substituted; (ii) in sub-section (2), for the words "nine hundred and fifty rupees", the words "two thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10-A of the principal Act, for the words "one thousand seven hundred and fifty rupees" wherever they occur, the words "two thousand and five hundred rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10-E of the principal act, for the words "one thousand seven hundred and fifty rupees", the words "two thousand and five hundred rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10-G of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), for the words "nine hundred and fifty rupees", the words "two thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 10--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10-J of the principal Act, for the words "one thousand and five hundred rupees", the words "two thousand two hundred and fifty rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 11--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10-L of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), for the words "nine hundred and fifty rupees", the words "two thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 12--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 11 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), for the words "seven hundred rupees", the words "one thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 13--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 11-A of the principal Act - (i) in sub-section (1), after the first proviso, the following further proviso shall be inserted, namely - "Provided further that where any person has served as aforesaid for not less than fifteen years there shall be paid to him a pension of seven hundred and fifty rupees per mensem"; (ii) sub-section (3) shall be omitted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 14--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 11-C of the principal Act - (i) in sub-section (1), for the words "two thousand and four hundred rupees", the words "five thousand rupees" shall be substituted; (ii) in sub-section (2), for the words "one thousand eight hundred", the words "four thousand" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 15--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12 of the principal Act - (i) in clause (a), for the words "one rupee and twenty-five paise", the words "two rupees and fifty paise" shall be substituted; (ii) in clause (b), in sub-clause (1), for the words "fifty one rupees", the words "seventy-five rupees" shall be substituted; (iii) in clause (ccc), for the words "two thousand rupees", wherever they occur, the words "five thousand rupees" shall be substituted; (iv) in clause (h), for the words "one thousand and three hundred rupees", the words "two thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
