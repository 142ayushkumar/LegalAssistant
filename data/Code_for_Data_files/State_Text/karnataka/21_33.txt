Visveswaraiah Technological University (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Visveswaraiah Technological University Act, 1994. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Visveswaraiah Technological University Act, 1994 (Karnataka Act 39 of 1994), for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka Legislature in the Fiftieth year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Visveswaraiah Technological University (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Visveswaraiah Technological University (Amendment) Act, 1999.1 (2) It shall be come into force of such date as the State Government may, by Notification in the Official Gazette, appoint. Received the assent of the Governor on the ninth day of August, 1999
Visveswaraiah Technological University (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4 of the Visveswaraiah Technological University Act, 1994 (Karnataka Act 39 of 1994) after sub-section (3), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(4) Notwithstanding anything contained in this Act or the Karnataka State University Act, 1976, the affiliation of the Karnataka Regional Engineering College, Surathkal, to the University, with effect from such date as the Government may by notification specify, shall discontinue and shall be deemed to have been affiliated to the Mangalore University from the said date and be governed by the provisions of the Karnataka State Universities Act, 1976".
