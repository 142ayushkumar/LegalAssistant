Karnataka Economic Offences (Inapplicability of Limitation) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to provide for the inapplicability of the provisions of chapter XXXVI of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 , to certain economic offences. BE it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Karnataka Economic Offences (Inapplicability of Limitation) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Economic Offences (Inapplicability of Limitation) Act, 1981. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Karnataka Economic Offences (Inapplicability of Limitation) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka Nothing in chapter XXXVI of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (Central Act 2 of 1974), shall apply to, - (i) any offence punishable under any of the enactments specified in the schedule; or (ii) any other offences which under the provisions of that Code, any be tried along with such offences, and every offence referred to in clause (i) or clause (ii) may be taken cognizance of by the court having jurisdiction as if the provisions of that chapter were not enacted.
