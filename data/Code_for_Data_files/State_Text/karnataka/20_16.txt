Karnataka Urban Water Supply and Drainage Board (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Urban Water Supply and Drainage Board Act, 1973. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Urban Water Supply and Drainage Board Act, 1973 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1974) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the forty-fourth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Urban Water Supply and Drainage Board (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Urban Water Supply and Drainage Board (Amendment) Act, 1993.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor of Karnataka on the Eighteenth day of May, 1993 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Urban Water Supply and Drainage Board (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4 of the Karnataka Urban Water Supply and Drainage Board Act, 1973, (Karnataka Act 25 of 1974), in sub-section (1), in clause (c), - (i) for the words "three Directors" the words "four Directors of whom one shall be the Director of Municipal Administration and three others" shall be substituted; and (ii) for clause (ii), the following shall be substituted, namely:- "(ii) Housing and Urban Development".
