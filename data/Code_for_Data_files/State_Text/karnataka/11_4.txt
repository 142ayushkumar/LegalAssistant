Kannada University (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Kannada University Act, 1991. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Kannada University Act, 1991 (Karnataka Act 23 of 1991), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-third year of the Republic of India as follows -
Kannada University (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Kannada University (Amendment) Act, 1992.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-third day of April, 1992
Kannada University (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Sections 44 and 45 of the Kannada University Act, 1991 (Karnataka Act 23 of 1991) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), after the words "shall be appointed", where they occur the words "by the State Government" shall be and shall always be deemed to have been inserted.
Kannada University (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 46 of the Principal Act - (i) in the heading for the words "Librarian", the words "Finance Officer" shall be and shall always be deemed to have been substituted; and (ii) after the words "shall be appointed", the words "by the State Government" shall be and shall always be deemed to have been inserted.
