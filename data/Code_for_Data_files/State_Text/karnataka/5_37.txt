Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act 30 of 1958) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-fifth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1974.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of April, 1974. Received the assent of the Governor on the Eighteenth day of May, 1974
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka For Section 3-A of the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act 30 of 1958), (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), the following section shall be substituted, namely - "3-A. Levy of Surcharge -In addition to the Entertainments Tax payable under Section 3, a surcharge of one hundred per cent on the rate of such Entertainments Tax shall be levied and the entire proceeds of the surcharge so levied shall be credited to the Consolidated Fund of the State."
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In the table below Section 4 of the principal Act, for items (i) to (iv), the following items shall be substituted, namely - "(i) Held in the City of Bangalore - (a) In Class I Theatres Twenty rupees (b) In Theatres other than Class I Theatres Ten rupees (ii) Held in cities of towns with a population of fifty thousand and above Ten rupees (iii) Held in places with a population of twenty thousand and above but less than fifty thousand Six rupees (iv) Held in other places Four rupees."
