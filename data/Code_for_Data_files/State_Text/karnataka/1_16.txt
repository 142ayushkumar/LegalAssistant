Karnataka Agricultural Debtors Relief (Amendment and Repeal) Act, 1985_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Agricultural Debtors Relief Act, 1966. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Agricultural Debtors Relief Act, 1966 (Karnataka Act 29 of 1966) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Agricultural Debtors Relief (Amendment and Repeal) Act, 1985_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Agricultural Debtors Relief (Amendment and Repeal) Act, 1985.1 (2) Section 2 shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of April, 1969 and the other provisions shall come into force from 27th day of December, 1984. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-fifth day of April, 1985
Karnataka Agricultural Debtors Relief (Amendment and Repeal) Act, 1985_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Karnataka Agricultural Debtors Relief Act, 1966 (Karnataka Act 29 of 1966), after clause (vii), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(vii-a) any sum due to the Karnataka Agro-Industries Corporation Limited".
Karnataka Agricultural Debtors Relief (Amendment and Repeal) Act, 1985_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Agricultural Debtors Relief Act, 1966 (Karnataka Act 29 of 1966) (hereinafter referred to as the repealed Act), is hereby repealed. (2) All proceedings under the repealed Act pending in the courts on the date of commencement of this section shall stand abated: Provided that the suits, appeals, applications and proceedings transferred to the Court under Section 18 of the repealed Act shall stand re-transferred to the civil or revenue Court, as the case may be. (3) In computing the period of limitation for the institution of any suit or proceedings in respect of any debt due from any person, the period during which the proceedings in respect of such debt were before the Court or Court of appeal under the repealed Act shall be excluded.
Karnataka Agricultural Debtors Relief (Amendment and Repeal) Act, 1985_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Agricultural Debtors Relief (Amendment and Repeal) Ordinance, 1984 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 21 of 1984) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the repealed Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the repealed Act as amended by this Act.
