Karnataka Rent Control (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Rent Control Act, 1961. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Rent Control Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1961) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-ninth Year of Republic of India, as follows:-
Karnataka Rent Control (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Rent Control (Amendment) Act, 1978.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Twenty- fourth day of December, 1977. Received the assent of the President on the Thirtieth day of March, 1978 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Rent Control (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (4) of Section 1 of the Karnataka Rent Control Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1961), for the words and figures "Thirty-first December, 1977", the words and figures "Thirty-first December, 1982" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Rent Control (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Rent Control (Second Amendment) Ordinance, 1977 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 15 of 1977) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the Karnataka Rent Control Act, 1961 as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the said Act as amended by this Act.
