Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1961. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 9 of 1962); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Eighteenth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1967.1 Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-ninth day of December, 1967
Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 16 of the Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 9 of 1962) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (i) for the words "two years", the words "two and a half years", shall be substituted and shall be deemed always to have been substituted; and (ii) after the words "preceding the date on which this Chapter comes into force", the words "and that he has completed twenty-five years of age on the said date", shall be inserted and shall be deemed always to have been inserted.
Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 18 of the principal Act - (i) for the words "two years", the words "two and a half years", shall be substituted and shall be deemed always to have been substituted; and (ii) after the words "prior to the commencement of this Chapter in the Ayurvedic or Unani System of Medicine, or any of their branches", the words "and that he has completed twenty years of age on the date of commencement of this Chapter", shall be inserted and shall be deemed always to have been inserted.
Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 21 of the principal Act, for the words "two years", the words "two and a half years" shall be substituted and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Ordinance, 1967 (Karnataka Ordinance 6 of 1967), is hereby repealed.
