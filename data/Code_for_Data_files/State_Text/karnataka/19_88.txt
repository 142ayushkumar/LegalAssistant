Tunga Bhadra Sugars (Deve Sugars) Limited (Acquisition and Transfer of Undertaking) Act, 1998_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to provide for acquisition and transfer of undertaking of the Tunga Bhadra Sugars (Deve Sugars) Limited, Shimoga, in public interest and in order to secure proper management of the said undertaking so as to serve the interest of general public by ensuring continued manufacture, production and distribution of sugar which is essential to the needs of economy of the country and for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto. Whereas, the cessation of manufacture, production and distribution of sugar by the said undertaking has resulted in great hardship to the sugarcane growers and the workers employed therein and accordingly it is necessary to acquire the said undertaking for proper management in order to ensure that the interest of the general public is served by continuance of the manufacture, production and distribution of sugar which is essential to the needs of economy and providing relief against unemployment and preventing hardship to the growers of sugarcane and for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-ninth year of the Republic of India as follows -
