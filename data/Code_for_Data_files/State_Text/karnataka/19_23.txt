Karnataka Tax on Luxuries (Hotels and Lodging Houses) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Tax on Luxuries (Hotels and Lodging Houses) Act, 1979. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Tax on Luxuries (Hotels and Lodging Houses) Act, 1979 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1979), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty second Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Tax on Luxuries (Hotels and Lodging Houses) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Karnataka Tax on Luxuries (Hotels and Lodging Houses) (Amendment) Act 1981.1 2 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000. Received the assent of the Governor on the Fourth day of April, 1981
Karnataka Tax on Luxuries (Hotels and Lodging Houses) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Tax on Luxuries (Hotels and Lodging Houses) Act, 1979 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1979) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in clause (5), for the words "thirty rupees", the words "twenty rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Tax on Luxuries (Hotels and Lodging Houses) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka For sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the principal Act, excluding the provisos, the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely - "(1) Subject to the provisions of this Act, there shall be levied and collected from every person a tax (to be known as Luxury Tax) in respect of any luxury provided in a hotel to a person at the following rates, namely - (a) Where the charges for lodging per person per day are twenty rupees or more, but do not exceed fifty rupees. Five per cent of such charges. (b) Where the charges for lodging per person per day exceed rupees fifty, but do not exceed rupees one hundred. Seven and a half per cent of such charges. (c) Where the charges for lodging per person per day exceed rupees one hundred. Ten percent of such charges"
