Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-ninth year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1998.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-sixth day of April, 1999
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 18 of the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964) (hereinafter referred to as the  Principal Act), the following shall be inserted, namely - "18-A. Appointment of Licensed Surveyors -(1) The Director of Survey Settlement and Land Records may, for the purposes of the third proviso to Section 128 and of clause (c) of Section 131, issue, with the prior approval of the State Government and subject to such conditions and restrictions and in such manner as may be prescribed, a licence to any person (hereinafter referred to as the Licensed Surveyor) possessing the prescribed qualifications and experience. (2) The fee payable to a Licensed Surveyor shall be as may be prescribed".
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 128 of the Principal Act, after the second proviso, the following proviso shall be inserted, namely - "Provided also that any person reporting under this sub-section the acquisition by him of a right in partition in respect of the land shall annex with the report a sketch showing the metes and bounds and other prescribed particulars of such land and such person shall get the sketch prepared by a Licensed Surveyor".
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 131 of the Principal Act, after the clause (b), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(c) any person while reporting acquisition by him of a right in accordance with the third proviso to sub-section (1) of Section 128 or any person alienating any land that is part of the survey or sub-number shall get a sketch of the said property prepared by a licensed surveyor, for the purpose of presenting the deed before the registering authority".
