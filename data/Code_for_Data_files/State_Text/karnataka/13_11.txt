Malnad Area Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Malnad Area Development Board Act, 1991. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Malnad Area Development Board Act, 1991 (Karnataka Act 36 of 1991) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-fifth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Malnad Area Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Malnad Area Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1994.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on thirtieth day of September, 1994 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Malnad Area Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Schedule to the Malnad Area Development Board Act, 1991 (Karnataka Act 36 of 1991),- (i) in the entries relating to the District of Chickmagalur at serial number 2, in Column 3, after the word "Tarikere", the word "Kadur" shall be inserted; (ii) in the entries relating to the District of Shimoga at serial number 8, in Column 3, after the word "Bhadravathi", the word "Honnali" shall be inserted.
