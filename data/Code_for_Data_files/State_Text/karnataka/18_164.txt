Karnataka Stamp and Certain Other Laws (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957, the Karnataka Municipalities Act, 1964, the Karnataka Municipal Corporations Act, 1976 and the Karnataka Panchayat Raj Act, 1993. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 34 of 1957), the Karnataka Municipalities Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1964), the Karnataka Municipal Corporations Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1977) and the Karnataka Panchayat Raj Act, 1993 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1993), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by Karnataka State Legislature in the Fifty-fourth year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Karnataka Stamp and Certain Other Laws (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Stamp and Certain Other Laws (Amendment) Act, 2003. 1 (2) It shall come into force on the first day of April, 2003. Published in the Karnataka Gazette Part IV-A Extra Ordinary No. 333 dated 31-3-2003.

Karnataka Stamp and Certain Other Laws (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka If any difficulty arises in giving effect to the provisions of the Karnataka Stamp and Certain Other Laws (Amendment) Act, 2003, the Government may, by notification in the official Gazette make such provisions as appear to it to be necessary or expedient for removing the difficulty.
