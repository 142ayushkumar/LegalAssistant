Karnataka Sheep and Sheep Products Development (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Sheep and Sheep Products Development Act, 1973 . Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Sheep and Sheep Products Development Act, 1973 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1974). Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-ninth Year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka Sheep and Sheep Products Development (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Sheep and Sheep Products Development (Amendment) Act, 19781 2 . (2) It shall come into force at once. First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, on the Twenty-second day of September, 1978, Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixteenth day of September, 1978 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Sheep and Sheep Products Development (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Karnataka Sheep and Sheep Products Development Act, 1973 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1974) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) for the expression "Karnataka" wherever it occurs, including the long title and preamble, the expression "Karnataka" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Sheep and Sheep Products Development (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka For sub-section (3) of Section 1 of the principal Act, the following sub-section shall be and shall always be deemed to have been substituted, namely - "(3) It shall come into force on the First day of March, 1975."
Karnataka Sheep and Sheep Products Development (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka Anything done or any action taken or purported to have been done or taken under the principal Act before the commencement of this Act, shall be deemed to have been validly done or taken and shall have effect for all purposes, as if it had been done or taken by or under the provisions of the principal Act as amended by Section 3 of this Act and accordingly no legal proceedings shall be entertained or continued in any Court or other authority on the ground that such thing was done or action was taken under or in pursuance of the provisions which were not valid or not in force at that time.
