Karnataka Weights and Measures (Enforcement) (Amendment) Act, 1975_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Weights and Measures (Enforcement) Act, 1958 . Whereas it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Weights and Measures (Enforcement) Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1959); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Weights and Measures (Enforcement) (Amendment) Act, 1975_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Weights and Measures (Enforcement) (Amendment) Act, 1975.1 (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint. Received the assent of the Governor on the Third day of May, 1975
Karnataka Weights and Measures (Enforcement) (Amendment) Act, 1975_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 15 of the Karnataka Weights and Measures (Enforcement) Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1959) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (i) in sub-section (1), after the words "Controller of Weights and Measures", the words "and a Joint Controller of Weights and Measures" shall be inserted; (ii) in sub-section (3), for the words "all Deputy Controllers", the words "the Joint Controller, all Deputy Controllers", and for the words "Deputy Controllers" the words "the Joint Controller, Deputy Controllers" shall respectively be substituted; (iii) in sub-section (4), after the words "The Controller", the words "the Joint Controller", shall be inserted.
Karnataka Weights and Measures (Enforcement) (Amendment) Act, 1975_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 20 of the principal Act, for the word "Appeals" occurring in the heading, the words "Appeals and Revision" shall be substituted, and - (i) in sub-section (1), the words "Controller, or any" shall be omitted; (ii) for sub-section (2), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely - "(2) Any person aggrieved by an original decision of the officer specified in column (1) of the Table below may within sixty days from the date of such decision prefer an appeal to the authority specified in the corresponding entries in column (2) of the said Table". Table (1) (2) (i) Controller Government (ii) Joint Controller Controller (iii) Deputy Controller Joint Controller (iv) Assistant Controller Deputy Controller." (iii) after sub-section (3), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(4) The Controller or the Joint Controller specially authorised by the State Government in this behalf may call for and examine the record of any inquiry or proceedings of any Deputy Controller, Assistant Controller or Inspector of Weights and Measures in respect of which no appeal has been preferred under sub-section (1) or sub-section (2), for the purpose of satisfying himself as to the correctness, legality or propriety of the proceedings of such officer and if in any case it shall appear to the Controller or the Joint Controller, as the case may be, that any decision or order or proceedings so called for should be modified, annulled or reversed he may pass such order thereon as he may deem fit: Provided that no order shall be modified, annulled or reversed unless notice has been served on the parties interested and opportunity given to them of being heard".
Karnataka Weights and Measures (Enforcement) (Amendment) Act, 1975_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 37 of the principal Act, after the words "made by the Controller", the words "or the Joint Controller" shall be inserted.
