Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Forest Act, 1963. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Forest Act, 1963 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1964), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1987.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on 22nd day of February, 1989
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Forest Act, 1963 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1964), (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (1) in clause (6), for the words "Chief Conservator" the words "Principal Chief Conservator, Chief Conservator, Additional Chief Conservator", shall be substituted; (2) in clause (7), in sub-clause (a), after the word "resin" the words "rubber latex, cocobeans or pods" shall be inserted; (3) in clause (18), after the words "flakes or pulp" the words "but does not include sandalwood manufactured in the form of sandalwood handicraft articles" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Sections 16 and 22 of the principal Act, for the words "Karnataka Revenue Appellate Tribunal" at the two places where they occur, the words "Karnataka Appellate Tribunal" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 24 of the principal Act, after clause (g), the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(gg) unauthorisedly occupies land for any purpose; or"
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 33 of the principal Act - (1) in sub-section (2), after clause (iii), the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(iiia) prohibit unauthorised occupation of land for any purpose";
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 41 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), for the words, figures and brackets "Section 32 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1898 (Central Act V of 1898)" the words, figures and brackets "Section 29 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (Central Act II of 1974)", shall be substituted.
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 46 of the principal Act, in sub-section (3), for the words and figures " Indian Limitation Act, 1908", the words and figures " Limitation Act, 1963", shall be substituted.
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 77 of the principal Act, for the words, figures and brackets, " Code of Criminal Procedure, 1898 (Central Act V of 1898)" the words, figures and brackets " Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973" (Central Act II of 1974)", shall be substituted.
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka In section of the principal Act, in sub-section (1) - (1) for the words "refine or sell", the words "refine, possess, store or sell or attempt to refine, store or sell" shall be substituted; (2) in the second proviso, for the words "seven pounds" the words "three kilograms and sandalwood oil upto one hundred grams" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 10--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 98-A of the principal Act - (1) in sub-section (1) - (a) after the words "disposed of by the State Government" the words "or by a corporation, owned or controlled by or a body notified by the State Government" shall be and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from the Fourteenth day of February, 1978; (b) after the proviso, the following proviso shall be and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from the First day of October, 1983, namely - "Provided further that the tax under the above proviso in respect of Eucalptus and Bamboos supplied as raw material for manufacture of news print shall be fifty per cent of the rates specified therein or a period of five years". (2) after sub-section (1), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(1-A) Notwithstanding anything contained in sub-section (1), no tax shall be payable to the State Government by a corporation, owned or controlled by the State Government to the extent of tax not levied and collected by it during the period from fourteenth day of February, 1978, till the commencement of the Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1988".
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 11--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 98-B of the principal Act, for sub-section (2), the following sub-sections shall be substituted, namely - "(2) The following shall form part of Karnataka Forest Development Fund, namely - (a) the tax levied and collected under Section 98-A; (b) the money recovered for raising compensatory plantation in lieu of the forest area made over for non-forestry purposes; (c) sandal surcharges collected for the development of sandalwood resources; (2-A) The amounts referred to in sub-section (2) shall first be credited to the Consolidated Fund of the State and under appropriation duly made by law in this behalf, be entered in and transferred to the Karnataka Forest Development Fund".
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 12--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 99 of the principal Act - (1) in sub-section (1), in clause (c), for the words, figures and brackets, " Code of Criminal Procedure, 1898 (Central Act V of 1898)" the words, figures and brackets " Code of Criminal Procedure 1973 (Central Act II of 1974)" shall be substituted; (2) in sub-section (2), in the proviso, for the words, figures and brackets, "Section 355, Section 356 or Section 357 of the Code Criminal Procedure, 1898 (Central Act V of 1898)" he words, figures and brackets, "Section 274 or Section 275 of the Code of Criminal Procedure 1973 (Central Act II of 1974) shall be substituted.
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 13--> State(s): Karnataka To sub-section (1) of Section 101-A of the principal Act, the following provisos shall be deemed to have been added, with effect from the Twenty-third day of February, 1981, namely - "Provided that no seigniorage value shall be payable in respect of bark for the period from the Nineteenth day of February, 1981 to the Thirty-first day of August, 1983: Provided further that the State Government may, for a period of ten years from the First day of October, 1983, and on payment of fifty per cent of such seigniorage value, grant to any person Eucalyptus or Bamboos for the manufacture of news print".
