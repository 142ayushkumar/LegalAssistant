Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances Act, 1956. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1981.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Eighth day of April, 1981
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances Act, 1956 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (2), for the words "seven hundred and fifty rupees", the words "nine hundred and fifty rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 8 of the principal Act, in clause (b) of sub-section (2), for the words "one rupee", the words "one rupee and fifty paise", shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), for the words "seven hundred and fifty rupees", the words "nine hundred and fifty rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10-G of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), for the words "seven hundred and fifty rupees" the words "nine hundred and fifty rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10-L of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), for the words "seven hundred and fifty rupees", the words "nine hundred and fifty rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12 of the principal Act - (1) in clause (a) - (i) the words "not ordinarily resident within prescribed distances from the place at which their attendance is required in connection with the meetings" shall be omitted; (ii) for the words "seventy-five paise", the words "one rupee and twenty-five paise" shall be substituted; (2) in sub-clause (ii) of clause (b), the words, brackets and letter "entitled to travelling allowance under clause (a)" shall be omitted; (3) in clause (cc) for the words "and his spouse" the words "and his companion" shall be substituted; (4) in clause (ccc) - (a) for the words "be provided with railway coupons which shall subject to such rules as may be prescribed, entitled such member and a member of his family to travel by first-class, by any railway in India, for a distance of ten thousand kilometers in the aggregate during a period of twelve calendar months", the words "for every financial year, be provided with railway coupons of the value of two thousand rupees which shall, subject to such rules as may be prescribed, entitle such member to travel either singly or with a companion in one or more journeys by any class, by any railway in India" shall be substituted; (b) in the proviso - (i) for brackets and words "(including a member of his family)", the words "along with a companion" shall be substituted. (ii) for the words "the amount equivalent to the first class railway fare admissible under this section for the journey between such places", the words "two thousand rupees in a financial year", shall be substituted; (c) the Explanation shall be omitted; (5) in clause (d), for the words "five rupees", the words "two rupees" shall be and shall be deemed to have been substituted with effect from 1st January, 1980. (6) in clause (h), for the words "three hundred rupees", the words "five hundred and fifty rupees" shall be substituted.
