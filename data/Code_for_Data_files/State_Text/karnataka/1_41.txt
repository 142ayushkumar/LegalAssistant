Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1966. Whereas it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1966 (Karnataka Act 27 of 1966); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twentieth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 19691 . (2) Sections 2 to 11 (both inclusive), shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of May, 1968, and the remaining provisions shall be deemed to have come into force on the Eighteenth day of July, 1969. (First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, on the Eighteenth day of September, 1969) (Received the assent of the President on the Twelfth day of September, 1969)
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1966 (Karnataka Act 27 of 1966) (hereinafter referred to as the  Principal Act), in clauses (18), (22), (23), (45) and (46) - (i) after the word declared, the words or deemed to be declared shall be inserted; and (ii) for the words, brackets and figures sub-section (2) of Section 6, the words this Act shall be substituted.
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6 of the principal Act - (1) in sub-section (2) - (i) the words and brackets (including any structure, enclosure, open place or locality) wherever they occur shall be omitted; (ii) in clause (b), for the words any other specified area or areas, the words any other specified place or places, shall be substituted; (iii) after the proviso, the following further proviso shall be inserted, namely - Provided further that if in respect of any market area, the Chief Marketing Officer is of the opinion that a suitable place for the location of the market yard or sub-market yard cannot be easily secured within the limits of the area of a city, town or village, proposed to be declared as a market or sub-market, he may declare an area including one or more villages beyond the limits of a city, town or village to be a market or sub-market and declare a place in any such village to be a market yard or sub-market yard for such market or sub-market.; (2) after sub-section (2), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - (2-A) In respect of any market area declared under any of the enactments repealed by sub-section (1) of Section 154, the Chief Marketing Officer may, as soon as possible after the commencement of this Act, and subject to the provisos to sub-section (2), declare by notification, any specified area in such market area to be a market, any other specified area in the market area to be a sub-market, a specified place in the market to be a market yard, any other place or places to be a market sub-yard or market sub-yards, and a specified place in the sub-market yard.; (3) in sub-section (3), after the words at any time by a notification, the words alter any market or sub-market or any market yard, market sub-yard or sub-market yard or shall be inserted and for the word proviso, the word provisos shall be substituted; (4) after sub-section (3), the following Explanation shall be added, namely - Explanation - In this section, the expression place shall include any structure, enclosure, open place, locality or street, whether vested in the market committee of the market area or not.
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka To Section 7 of the principal Act, the following proviso shall be added, namely - Provided that in the case of a market or sub-market notified under sub-section (2-A) of Section 6, the market or sub-market shall be deemed to be established on the date of the notification of the Chief Marketing Officer.
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 8 of the principal Act, for the words and figure the date specified in the declaration made under Section 7, the words the market is established shall be substituted.
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 10 of the principal Act, for the words a market committee constituted for the first time for any market area, the words and figure the first market committee constituted for a market area declared after the commencement of this Act, under Section 4 shall be substituted.
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 63 of the principal Act - (i) in sub-clause (i) of clause (a), after the word yards, the words vesting in the market committee shall be added; (ii) in sub-clause (i) of clause (b), after the words the yard, the words vesting in the market committee shall be added.
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 112 of the principal Act, for the words The Board shall be consulted, the words After the establishment of the Board, it shall be consulted shall be substituted.
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 149 of the principal Act, for the words the first market committee of a market area, the words and figures the first market committee constituted under Section 10 for a market area declared after the commencement of this Act, under Section 4 shall be substituted.
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 10--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 154 of the principal Act - (1) in sub-section (1) - (a) in the first proviso - (i) in clause (c), for the words until the market committees are constituted under the provisions of this Act, and on the date of the first meeting of any market committee after such constitution under this Act, the market committee constituted under the repealed enactments shall be deemed to have been dissolved, the words and figures until the 19th day of July, 1969 shall be substituted; (ii) in clause (d), for the words until the market committees are constituted under the provisions of this Act, the words and figures until the 19th day of July, 1969 shall be substituted. (b) the second proviso shall be omitted; and (2) in sub-section (2), for the word provisos, the word proviso shall be substituted.
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 11--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 154 of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely - 154-A. Transitory provisions - (1) Notwithstanding anything contained in this Act or any of the enactments repealed by sub-section (1) of Section 154 or any other law - (a) on the 19th day of July, 1969, the term of office of the members of every market committee and of the Chairman and Vice-Chairman thereof continuing to function under clause (c) of the proviso to sub-section (1) of Section 154 shall expire, and with effect from the said date, the powers conferred and the duties imposed on the market committee, the Chairman, the Vice-Chairman and any sub-committee or other body constituted by the market committee shall be exercised and performed by the Administrator referred to in the Explanation to this sub-section; (b) within a period of one year from the 19th day of July, 1969, the market committees shall be constituted in accordance with the provisions of Section 11; (c) the Administrator shall hold office until the date immediately preceding the date of the first meeting of the market committee constituted under Section 11. Explanation - For the purposes of this sub-section, the Administrator means - (i) the Deputy Commissioner, in respect of a market committee of a market area, the market yard of which is situated in the headquarters of the district; (ii) the Assistant Commissioner of the revenue sub-division, in respect of a market committee of a market area, the market yard of which is situated in the headquarters of the revenue sub-division other than the headquarters of the district; (iii) the Tahsildar of the taluk, in respect of a market committee of a market area, the market yard of which is situated in any place in such taluk, other than the headquarters of the district or the headquarters of the revenue sub-division. (2) Notwithstanding anything contained in sub-section (1), the State Government may, at any time after the 19th day of July, 1969, by notification, appoint, from time to time, any officer not below the rank of a Tahsildar, to be the Administrator for any market committee and the said officer shall be deemed to be the Administrator of the said market committee for purposes of this section.
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 12--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The notifications specified in the Schedule to this Act, issued by the Chief Marketing Officer declaring in respect of any market area, any area to be a market or sub-market, or any place to be a market yard, market sub-yard or sub-market yard, shall, notwithstanding anything contained in the principal Act or any of the enactments repealed by Section 154 of the principal Act or any other law shall be and shall always be deemed to have been validly issued and no such notification shall be called in question merely on the ground that the power therefor was not duly conferred on the Chief Marketing Officer. (2) Anything done or any action taken or purported to be done or taken (including market fees levied and collected under Section 65 of the principal Act) by any market committee consequent upon the issue of any notification specified in the schedule shall notwithstanding any judgment, decree or order of any Court be deemed to be and to have always been validly done or taken in accordance with law; and accordingly no suit or other proceeding shall be entertained or continued in any Court on the ground that the thing done or action taken was done or taken in accordance with the notification which was outside the scope or inconsistent with any of the provisions of the principal Act or any other law.
Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 13--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) (Amendment) Ordinance, 1969 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 2 of 1969) is hereby repealed: Provided that anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amnded by this Act.
