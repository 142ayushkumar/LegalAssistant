Karnataka High Court (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka High Court Act, 1961. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka High Court Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1962). Be, it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twentieth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka High Court (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka High Court (Amendment) Act, 1969.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Seventeenth day of September, 1969
Karnataka High Court (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka Section 3 of the Karnataka High Court Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1962), shall be renumbered as sub-section (1) thereof and after sub-section (1) as so re-numbered, the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(2) The High Court may also have as many Additional Registrars, Joint Registrars and Assistant Registrars as may be determined by the Governor in consultation with the High Court."
