Mysore Duty on Gold (Repealing) Act, 1949_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to repeal the Mysore Duty on Gold Act, 1940. Whereas, it is expedient to repeal the Mysore Duty on Gold Act, 1940 (XIX of 1940). It is hereby enacted as follows -
Mysore Duty on Gold (Repealing) Act, 1949_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Mysore Duty on Gold (Repealing) Act, 1949.1 (2) It shall come into force on such date as the Government may by notification in the Mysore Gazette appoint. Received the assent of His Highness the Maharaja on the 27th day of July, 1949
Mysore Duty on Gold (Repealing) Act, 1949_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka The Mysore Duty on Gold Act, 1940 (XIX of 1940), is hereby repealed.
