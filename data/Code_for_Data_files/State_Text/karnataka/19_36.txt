Karnataka Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments Act, 1976. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1976) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State legislature in the Thirty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments (Amendment) Act, 1987.1 2 (2) It shall come into force with effect from the First day of April, 1987. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Second day of April, 1987
Karnataka Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka in Section 2 of the Karnataka Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1976) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for clause (j), the following shall be substituted, namely,- "(j) 'Salary' or 'Wage' includes pay or wages, dearness allowance and all other remunerations received or receivable by any person, whether payable in cash or in kind and also includes perquisites or profits in lieu of salary, as defined in Section 17 of the Income Tax Act, 1961";
Karnataka Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In the Schedule to the principal Act, for item 1, the following shall be substituted, namely:- "1. Salary or wage earners whose salary or wage or both, as the case may be, for a month is Rs. 2,000 or more. Rupees Twenty per month"
