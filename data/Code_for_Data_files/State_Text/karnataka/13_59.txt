Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1957), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1980.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of October, 1979. (3) It shall remain in force upto and inclusive of the Thirty-first day of March, 1980 and upon its expiry by the operation of this sub-section the provisions which are in force prior to the commencement of this Act shall stand revived but such revival shall not affect the previous operation of the amendments made by this Act, in respect of any action taken during the period the amendment made by this Act remained in force. Received the assent of the Governor on the Eighth day of April, 1980 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Schedule to the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1957), (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in Part A, in Item 3, under the heading "Goods Vehicles." (1) for clauses (h), (i), (j), (k), (1), (m), (n) and (o) of sub-item (i), the following clauses shall be substituted, namely:- "(h) Vehicles exceeding 5,500 Kgs. but not exceeding 7,000 Kgs. in weight laden. 650.00 (i) Vehicles exceeding 7,000 Kgs. but not exceeding 8,500 Kgs. in weight laden. 790.00 (j) Vehicles exceeding 8,500 Kgs. but not exceeding 9,500 Kgs. in weight laden. 860.00 (k) Vehicles exceeding 9,500 Kgs. but not exceeding 10,500 Kgs. in weight laden. 935.00 (1) Vehicles exceeding 10,500 Kgs. but not exceeding 12,000 Kgs. in weight laden. 1,100.00 (m) Vehicles exceeding 12,000 Kgs. but not exceeding 13,500 Kgs. in weight laden. 1,275.00 (n) Vehicles exceeding 13,500 Kgs. but not exceeding 15,000 Kgs. in weight laden. 1,450.00 (o) Vehicles exceeding 15,000 Kgs. in weight laden. 1,450.00 plus Rs. 50 for every 250 Kgs. or part thereof in excess of 15,000 Kgs." (2) for clauses (g), (h), (i), (j), (k), (1), (m) and (n) of sub-item (ii), the following clauses shall be substituted, namely:- "(g) For each trailer, exceeding 5,500 Kgs. but not exceeding 7,000 Kgs. in weight laden. 550.00 (h) For each trailer, exceeding 7,000 Kgs. but not exceeding 9,000 Kgs. in weight laden. 650.00 (i) For each trailer, exceeding 9,000 Kgs. but not exceeding 9,500 Kgs. in weight laden. 700.00 (j) For each trailer, exceeding 9,500 Kgs. but not exceeding 10,500 Kgs. in weight laden. 800.00 (k) For each trailer, exceeding 10,500 Kgs. but not exceeding 12,000 Kgs. in weight laden. 950.00 (1) For each trailer, exceeding 12,000 Kgs. but not exceeding 13,500 Kgs. in weight laden. 1,100.00 (m) For each trailer, exceeding 13,500 Kgs. but not exceeding 15,000 Kgs. in weight laden. 1,250.00 (n) For each trailer, exceeding 15,000 Kgs. in weight laden. 1,250.00 plus Rs. 50 for every 250 Kgs. or part thereof in excess of 15,000 Kgs."
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Third Amendment) Ordinance, 1979 (Karnataka Ordinance 9 of 1979), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
