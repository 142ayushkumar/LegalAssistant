Kudala Sangama Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Kudala Sangama Development Board Act, 1994. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Kudala Sangama Development Board Act, 1994 (Karnataka Act 37 of 1994) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Kudala Sangama Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Kudala Sangama Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1996.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-seventh day of September, 1997
Kudala Sangama Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (4) of Section 3 of the Kudala Sangama Development Board Act, 1994 (Karnataka Act 37 of 1994) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act). (i) in clause (a) for the word "Chairman", the words "ex-officio Chairman" shall be substituted. (ii) in clause (g), in the proviso - (a) in clause (i), for the word "or" the word "and" shall be substituted. (b) in clause (ii), for the word "Civil, P.W.D. (C & B)", the words "Civil" shall be substituted.
Kudala Sangama Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the principal Act - (i) in clauses (a), (b), (d) and (e) at the end, the word "or" shall be inserted. (ii) in the proviso to clause (f), for the words "any interest", the words "any share or interest" shall be substituted.
Kudala Sangama Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 6 of the principal Act, in the proviso to clause (c), for the words "non-official", the words "ex-officio" shall be substituted.
Kudala Sangama Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 8 of the principal Act, in clause (c), after the word "appropriate", the words "or apply" shall be inserted.
Kudala Sangama Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 12 of the principal Act, in the proviso, after the words "not below the rank of Group A Officer", the words "not below the rank of Group A Junior Scale Officer, on deputation" shall be inserted.
Kudala Sangama Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 14 of the principal Act, in sub-section (5), for the words "by regulations prescribe the procedure", the words "by regulations specify the procedure" shall be substituted.
Kudala Sangama Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 18 of the principal Act, for the words "trust created or subsisting", the words "trust created and subsisting" shall be substituted.
