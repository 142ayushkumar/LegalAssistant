Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 2009_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Forest Act, 1963. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Forest Act, 1963 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1964), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Sixtieth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 2009_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 20091 . (2) It shall come into force at once. First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, on the Seventeenth day of September, 2009, Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixteenth day of September, 2009
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 2009_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 98-A of the Karnataka Forest Act, 1963 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1964) (hereinafter referred to as the  principal Act), after sub-section (3), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(4) There shall be levied and collected interest at the rate of eighteen per cent per annum till the date of payment or recovery of all tax dues".
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 2009_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 98-B of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), in clause (a), after the word "tax", the words "and interest" shall be inserted.
