Registration (Karnataka) (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Registration (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1976. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Registration (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 55 of 1976); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Registration (Karnataka) (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Registration (Karnataka) (Second Amendment) Act, 19761 . (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Karnataka. (3) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Twenty-fifth day of October, 1976. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-seventh day of November, 1976
Registration (Karnataka) (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka For sub-section (3) of Section 1 of the Registration (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 55 of 1976), the following sub-section shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted, namely:- "(3) This section and Sections 3, 13, 14, 16, 17 and 19 shall come into force at once and the other sections shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint and different dates may be appointed for different sections and areas."
Registration (Karnataka) (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Registration (Karnataka) (Second Amendment) Ordinance, 1976 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 30 of 1976) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the Registration Act, 1908 as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the Registration Act, 1908 as amended by this Act.
