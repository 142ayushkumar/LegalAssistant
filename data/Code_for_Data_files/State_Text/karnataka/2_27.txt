Bangalore Water Supply and Sewerage (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Bangalore Water Supply and Sewerage Act, 1964. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Bangalore Water Supply and Sewerage Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 36 of 1964), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bangalore Water Supply and Sewerage (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Bangalore Water Supply and Sewerage (Amendment) Act, 1983.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Seventeenth day of April, 1984
Bangalore Water Supply and Sewerage (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 25 of the Bangalore Water Supply and Sewerage Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 36 of 1964) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act),- (1) in sub-section (1), the words "in consultation with the Controller of State Accounts" shall be omitted; (2) in sub-section (2), for the words "the Controller of State Accounts or by such person as he may authorise in this behalf", the words "such auditors as may be appointed by Government", shall be substituted and the words "by him" and "to the Controller of State Accounts" shall be omitted; (3) in sub-section (3), for the words "Controller of State Accounts and any person authorised by him in connection with the audit of the accounts of the Board" the words "auditor appointed by the Government" shall be substituted; (4) in sub-section (4), for the words "Controller of State Accounts or any other person authorised by him in this behalf", the words "auditor" shall be substituted.
Bangalore Water Supply and Sewerage (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 33 of the principal Act, the following shall be and shall be deemed always to have been omitted, namely:- "but shall be deemed to include a supply - (i) for flushing latrines, or drains; and (ii) for all baths other than swimming baths or public baths."
Bangalore Water Supply and Sewerage (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka Notwithstanding anything contained in any decree, order or judgment of any Court or other authority, charges levied and collected for the supply of water to hotels, boarding houses and residential clubs,- (i) for flushing latrines or drains; and (ii) for all baths other than swimming baths, or public baths, At the rate applicable to non-domestic purposes by or under the provisions of the principal Act before the date of commencement of this Act, shall be deemed to be as valid and effective as if such levy or collection had been made under the principal Act as amended by this Act and accordingly - (a) all acts, proceedings or things done or action taken by the Bangalore Water Supply and Sewerage Board in connection with the levy and collection of such charges for supply of water shall, for all purposes be deemed to be or to have always been done, or taken in accordance with law; (b) no suit or other proceedings shall be maintained or continued in any Court or before any authority for refund or adjustment of any such charges; and (c) no Court shall enforce any decree or order directing the refund of adjustment of any such charge towards the amount due from him.
