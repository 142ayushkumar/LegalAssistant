Karnataka Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Contingency Fund Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Contingency Fund Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 11 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Karnataka Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 1958.1 Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-first day of March, 1958
Karnataka Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Contingency Fund Act, 1957, for the words "one crore of rupees", the words "two crores of rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka Contingency Fund (Amendment) Ordinance, 1958, is hereby repealed.
