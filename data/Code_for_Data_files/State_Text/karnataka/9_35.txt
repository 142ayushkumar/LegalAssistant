Industrial Disputes (Mysore Amendment) Act, 1953_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act amend the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, in its application to 1 [the State Mysore except Bellary District]. Where is, it is expedient to amend the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 (Central Act XIV of 1947), in its application 2 [to the State of Mysore except Bellary District]; It is hereby enacted as follows - The Mysore Adaptation of Laws Order, 1953 The Mysore Adaptation of Laws Order, 1953
Industrial Disputes (Mysore Amendment) Act, 1953_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Industrial Disputes (Mysore Amendment) Act, 1953.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the President on the Sixteenth day of July, 1953
Industrial Disputes (Mysore Amendment) Act, 1953_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Chapter III after Section 10 of the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 (Central Act XIV of 1947), the following section shall be inserted, namely - "10-A. Power to transfer cases - The State Government may, by order in writing and for reasons to be stated therein, at any stage transfer any industrial dispute pending before a Tribunal constituted by the State Government to any other Tribunal constituted by the State Government for adjudication, and the Tribunal to which the dispute is so transferred may, subject to any special directions in the order of transfer, proceed either de novo or from the stage at which it was transferred".
