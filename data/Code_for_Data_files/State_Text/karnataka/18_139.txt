Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An act further to amend the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act No. 34 of 1957) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-eighth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1997.1 2 (2) It shall come into force with effect from First day of April, 1997. Received the assent of the Governor on the Thirty-first day of March, 1997 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Clause (1) of sub-section (1) of Section 2 of the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act No. 34 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for the words "immoveable property", the words "immoveable or movable property or both" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 45-A of the principal Act, the Explanation shall be numbered as (1) and after Explanation (1) as so numbered, the following shall be inserted, namely:- "(2) The provisions of this section shall not apply in respect of instrument of partition where the maximum duty has been paid in accordance with Article 39 of the Schedule".
