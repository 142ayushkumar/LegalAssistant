Karnataka Industrial Areas Development (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Industrial Areas Development Act, 1966. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Industrial Areas Development Act, 1966 (Karnataka Act 18 of 1966): Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Industrial Areas Development (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Industrial Areas Development (Amendment) Act, 1978 (2) It shall come into force at once. [Section 2 incorporated in the Principal Act]
