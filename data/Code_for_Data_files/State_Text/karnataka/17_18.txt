Karnataka Regularisation of Unauthorised Constructions in Urban Areas (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Regularisation of Un-authorised Constructions in Urban Areas Act, 1991. Whereas it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Regularisation of Unauthorised Constructions in Urban Areas Act, 1991 (Karnataka Act 29 of 1991) for the purposes hereinafter appearing: Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the forty-fourth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Regularisation of Unauthorised Constructions in Urban Areas (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Regularisation of Unauthorised Constructions in Urban Areas (Amendment) Act, 19931 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the First day of August, 1992. First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, on the eighth day of March, 1994, Received the assent of the Governor on the eighth day of March, 1994
Karnataka Regularisation of Unauthorised Constructions in Urban Areas (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Regularisation of Unauthorised Constructions in Urban Areas Act, 1991 (Karnataka Act 29 of 1991) (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act), in clause (7), the following shall be inserted at the end, namely - "and includes land appurtenant thereto not exceeding maximum extent as may be prescribed".
Karnataka Regularisation of Unauthorised Constructions in Urban Areas (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Principal Act, for the words "sixty days", the words "two years" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Regularisation of Unauthorised Constructions in Urban Areas (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4 of the principal Act, after clause (vii), the following shall be inserted, namely;- "(viia) unauthorised constructions on land belonging to the State Government and appurtenant to any building belonging to the State Government: (viib) unauthorised constructions used for commercial purposes; Explanation - For the purpose of this clause, the word commercial shall have the meaning assigned to it in clause (lb) of Section 2 of the Karnataka Town and Country Planning Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 11 of 1963). (viic) unauthorised constructions on land appurtenant to any building owned by the Central Government or by any company owned or controlled by the State Government or Central Government".
Karnataka Regularisation of Unauthorised Constructions in Urban Areas (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6 of the Principal Act - (1) in sub-section (2), after clause (v), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(va) four persons nominated by the State Government of whom two persons shall be the members of the State Legislature... members Provided that out of the persons so nominated one shall be a woman and one shall be a person belonging to the Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes." (2) for sub-section (3), the following shall be substituted, namely - "(3) Screening Committee in respect of Bangalore District excluding the areas within the limits of the Corporation of the City of Bangalore shall consist of the following members:- (i) Commissioner, Bangalore Development Authority Chairman (ii) Deputy Commissioner, Bangalore District Member (iii) Assistant Commissioner of the concerned Revenue Sub-division Member (iv) Joint Director of Town Planning, Bangalore Metropolitan Region Development Authority Member (v) Four persons nominated by the State Government of whom two persons shall be the members of the State Legislature Members Provided that out of the persons so nominated one shall be a woman and one shall be a person belonging to the Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes. (vi) Assistant Commissioner in charge of prevention Member-Secretary of unauthorised constructions (3-A) Screening Committee in respect of the area within the limits of the Corporation of the City of Bangalore shall consist of the following Members,- (i) Commissioner, Corporation of the City of Bangalore Chairman (ii) Deputy Commissioner, Bangalore District Member (iii) Town Planning Member Bangalore Development Authority Member (iv) Assistant Commissioner of the concerned Revenue Sub-Division Member (v) Assistant Commissioner in charge of prevention of unauthorised constructions Member (vi) Assistant Metropolitan Commissioner, Bangalore Metropolitan Region, Development Authority Member (vii) four persons nominated by the State Government of who two persons shall be the members of the State Legislature and two persons shall be the Councillors of the Corporation of the City of Bangalore Members Provided that out of the persons so nominated, one shall be a woman and one shall be a person belonging to the Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes. (viii) Deputy Director, Town Planning, Corporation of the City of Bangalore Member-Secretary (3) in sub-section (4), for the words "order the regularisation" and "recommend for grant of land appurtaining to the construction" the words "provisional order the regularisation" and "recommend for grant of the appurtenant land included, in the said construction" shall respectively be substituted.
Karnataka Regularisation of Unauthorised Constructions in Urban Areas (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 7 of the Principal Act,- (1) in sub-section (1), for the words "an order of regularisation" the words "a provisional order of regularisation" shall be substituted: (2) in sub-section (2) - (a) in clause (i), for the words "regularisation has been ordered" and "shall be deemed to have been regularised", the words "provisional regularisation has been ordered" and "shall be regularised" shall respectively be substituted; (b) in clause (ii), for the words "shall be deemed to have been regularised" the words "shall be regularised" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Regularisation of Unauthorised Constructions in Urban Areas (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 8 of the Principal Act, after the words, "shall be liable for demolition and" the words "the supply of water or electricity shall be liable to be disconnected without notice and" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Regularisation of Unauthorised Constructions in Urban Areas (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 8 of the Principal Act, the following Section shall be inserted, namely - "8-A. Revision by the State Government - The State Government may of its own motion or on the application of any person at any time call for and examine the order passed or proceeding recorded by the Screening Committee for the purpose of satisfying itself as to the legality or propriety of the order or the regularity of such proceeding and may pass such order with respect thereto as it may deem fit: Provided that no such order shall be made except after giving the person affected a reasonable opportunity of being heard".
Karnataka Regularisation of Unauthorised Constructions in Urban Areas (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 9 of the Principal Act, in sub-section (2), clauses (b) and (c) shall respectively be renumbered as clauses (e) and (f) and before the clauses (e) and (f) as so renumbered the following clauses shall be inserted, namely - "(b) the powers and duties of the Screening Committee. (c) the quorum for the meetings of the Screening Committee. (d) the period within which the sale deed under sub-section (2) of Section 7 shall be executed".
