Karnataka Irrigation (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Irrigation Act, 1965. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Irrigation Act, 1965 (Karnataka Act 16 of 1965); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Nineteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Irrigation (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Irrigation (Amendment) Act, 1968.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the President on the Fifth day of June, 1969
Karnataka Irrigation (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 16 of the Karnataka Irrigation Act, 1965 (Karnataka Act 16 of 1965) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (i) in sub-section (1), for the words "the Deputy Commissioner shall, on being satisfied", the words "the Deputy Commissioner shall issue notices to the owner of such land and other persons interested in it to show cause why such land should not be acquired and after giving them a reasonable opportunity of being heard, if satisfied", shall be substituted; (ii) in sub-section (2), the words commencing from "and compensation to be awarded" and ending with "in respect of the land acquired" and Explanation I and Explanation II shall be omitted.
Karnataka Irrigation (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka Sections 18 and 19 of the principal Act shall be omitted.
Karnataka Irrigation (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 20 of the principal Act - (1) in the heading, for the words "person having a right to use" the words "user of" shall be substituted; (2) in sub-section (1) - (a) for the words "person having a right to use" the words "user of" shall be substituted; (b) clause (b) shall be omitted. (3) in sub-section (2), the words "and every person duly authorised under the provisions hereinafter contained to use a field channel" shall be omitted.
Karnataka Irrigation (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka Sections 21, 22 and 23 of the principal Act shall be omitted.
Karnataka Irrigation (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka Sub-section (2) of Section 24 of the principal Act shall be omitted.
Karnataka Irrigation (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka Section 25 of the principal Act shall be omitted.
Karnataka Irrigation (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka For Section 26 of the principal Act, the following section shall be substituted, namely - "26. Construction of field channel in lands acquired - After any land has been acquired under Section 16 and has vested in the State Government under Section 17, the Irrigation Officer shall construct the field channels necessary in the area."
Karnataka Irrigation (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 28 of the principal Act - (1) in sub-section (1), the words "not assessed as a wet land or" and the words "assessed as wet lands or" shall be omitted; (2) in sub-section (2), the words "to convert the land into wet or garden or", shall be omitted; (3) in sub-section (3), the words commencing from "when such rate is levied separately" and ending with "the neighbouring wet lands" shall be omitted; (4) in sub-section (4), the words "assessed as wet lands or" shall be omitted.
Karnataka Irrigation (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 10--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 31 of the principal Act - (i) in the heading, the word "wet" shall be omitted; (ii) in sub-section (1), the words "or portions of wet lands" shall be omitted.
Karnataka Irrigation (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 11--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 32 of the principal Act, for sub-section (4), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely - "(4) In all cases in which the person who has sown or grown any unauthorised crop or allowed any land to be grown or sown with such unauthorised crop cannot be found, the holder of the land, in addition to such other person concerned, shall - (a) be liable for contravening the provisions of this section; and (b) also be liable to pay such water rate, as may be determined by the Irrigation Officer, not being less than five times and not exceeding ten times the water rate which he would otherwise have been required to pay: Provided that if no water is utilised either directly or indirectly from the irrigation work for growing any crop, the provisions of sub-sections (3) and (4) shall not be applicable."
Karnataka Irrigation (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 12--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 36 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), the words and figures "except in cases of acquisition under Section 16" and the words "as far as possible", shall be omitted.
Karnataka Irrigation (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 13--> State(s): Karnataka Section 39 of the principal Act shall be omitted.
Karnataka Irrigation (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 14--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 55 of the principal Act - (i) clause (xii) shall be omitted; and (ii) the word, brackets and figures "and (xii)" shall be omitted.
