Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1962). Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Seventeenth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1966.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Twenty-ninth day of September, 1966. Received the assent of the President on the Twenty-sixth day of December, 1966
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1962) (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act), for the words "one year", the words "fifteen months" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 14 of the Principal Act - (1) in sub-section (2.) - (a) in clause (a), for the words "one year", the words "fifteen months" shall be substituted; (b) for clause (c), the following proviso shall be and shall always be deemed to have been substituted, namely: "Provided further that in the case of a landlord who is subject to any disability, the statement under sub-section (1) shall be filed within the period specified in clause (a) or (b), as the case may be, by the guardian, manager or other person in charge of the property of such person." (2) in sub-section (4), for the words "one year", the words "fifteen months" shall be substituted; (3) in sub-section (6), for the words "within one year", the words "within fifteen months from the appointed day or one year" shall be substituted; and after clause (d), the words "whichever is later" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 81-A of the Principal Act, for the word "mortgage", the words "mortgage with possession" shall be and shall always be deemed to have been substituted.
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In the first proviso to sub-section (1) of Section 142 of the Principal Act, after the words "provided that", the words "save as otherwise provided in this Act" shall be and shall always be deemed to have been inserted.
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka For the explanation to Part A of Schedule I to the Principal Act, the following explanation shall be and shall always be deemed to have been substituted, namely - "Explanation -For purposes of this Part, the average annual rainfall of any area means the average annual rainfall, in each taluk within which such area is situated, during the period of twenty-five years prior to the First day of October, 1965 as notified by the Prescribed Officer."
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Ordinance, 1966 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 2 of 1966s), is hereby repealed.
