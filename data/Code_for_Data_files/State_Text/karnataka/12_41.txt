Karnataka Land Reforms (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1962) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Land Reforms (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Reforms (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1976.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Thirtieth day of June, 1976. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-seventh day of November, 1976
Karnataka Land Reforms (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 48-A of the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1962), for the figures, letters and words "30th day of June, 1976", the figures, letters and words "31st day of December, 1976" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Land Reforms (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Land Reforms (Fourth Amendment) Ordinance, 1976 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 12 of 1976) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal any action taken under the Principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been taken under the Principal Act as amended by this Act.
