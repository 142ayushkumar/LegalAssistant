Karnataka Agricultural Lands (Prohibition of Alienation) (Repealing) Act, 1974_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to repeal the Karnataka Agricultural Lands (Prohibition of Alienation) Act, 1973. Whereas, the Karnataka Agricultural Lands (Prohibition of Alienation) Act, 1973 (Karnataka Act 24 of 1973) was enacted pending enactment of the Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Bill, 1973; And whereas, the Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Bill, 1973 has become an Act; And whereas, it is considered necessary to repeal the Karnataka Agricultural Lands (Prohibition of Alienation) Act, 1973 (Karnataka Act 24 of 1973); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Agricultural Lands (Prohibition of Alienation) (Repealing) Act, 1974_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Agricultural Lands (Prohibition of Alienation) (Repealing) Act. 1974.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-fifth day of March, 1974
Karnataka Agricultural Lands (Prohibition of Alienation) (Repealing) Act, 1974_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka Agricultural Lands (Prohibition of Alienation) Act, 1973 (Karnataka Act 24 of 1973) is hereby repealed: Provided that such repeal shall not affect the previous operation of the said Act or anything done or any action taken thereunder.
