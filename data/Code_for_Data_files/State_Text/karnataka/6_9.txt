Karnataka Fishing Harbour Terminals Authority (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Fishing Harbour Terminals Authority Act, 1986. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Fishing Harbour Terminals Authority Act, 1986 (Karnataka Act 15 of 1990), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Fishing Harbour Terminals Authority (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Fishing Harbour Terminals Authority (Amendment) Act, 1993.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the 10th day of May, 1993
Karnataka Fishing Harbour Terminals Authority (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Fishing Harbour Terminals Authority Act, 1986 (Karnataka Act 15 of 1990) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in clause (d) for the words "the State Government" the words "the State Government but excluding the area within the limits of a major port as defined in clause (8) of Section 3 of the Ports Act, 1908 (Central Act 15 of 1908)" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Fishing Harbour Terminals Authority (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4 of the principal Act, after clause (1), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(1-a) a representative of the Navy nominated by the State Government in consultation with the Central Government".
