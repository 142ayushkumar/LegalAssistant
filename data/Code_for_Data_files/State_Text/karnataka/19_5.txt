Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods (Third Amendment) Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods Act, 1979. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods Act, 1979 (Karnataka Act 27 of 1979) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be, it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the forty-fourth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods (Third Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods (Third Amendment) Act, 19931 2 . (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Eighth day of March, 1994 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000.
Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods (Third Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods Act, 1979 (Karnataka Act 27 of 1979) (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act), in sub-section (A) :- (1) for clause (2), the following shall be substituted, namely:- "(2) "Assessing Authority" means any officer empowered to make an assessment under the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957"; (2) in clause (5), the following shall be deemed to have been inserted at the end with effect from the tenth day of May, 1993, namely:- "and panchayat area under the Karnataka Panchayat Raj Act, 1993 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1993)".
Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods (Third Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Principal Act, after sub-section (6), the following sub-section shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from the first day of May, 1992, namely:- "(6-A). No tax shall be levied on a defence unit which causes entry of any goods liable to tax under the Act, into a local area for use by it in the manufacture, repair or research and development of defence and defence related goods."
Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods (Third Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3-A of the Principal Act, in sub-section (1), the words, figure and letter "or Section 5-C", shall be omitted.
Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods (Third Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka Section 5-C of the Principal Act shall be omitted.
Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods (Third Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12 of the Principal Act,- (1) for sub-section (1), the following shall be substituted, namely:- "(1) The officers exercising powers, discharging duties and performing functions under the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 in any area or in respect of any dealer or classes of dealer, shall exercise power, discharge duties and perform functions under this Act in respect of such area and such dealer or classes of dealers." (2) sub-sections (2) and (3) shall be omitted.
Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods (Third Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 12-A of the Principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely:- "12-B. Change of incumbent of an office - Whenever in respect of any proceeding under this Act, an assessing authority or any other officer ceases to exercise jurisdiction and is succeeded by another who has, and exercises, jurisdiction, the authority or officer so succeeding may continue the proceeding from the stage at which the proceeding was left by his or its predecessor: Provided that the assessee concerned may demand that before the proceeding is so continued the previous proceeding or any part thereof be re-opened or that before any order of assessment is passed against him, he be re-heard."
Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods (Third Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka Section 30-A of the principal Act, shall be re-numbered as sub-section (1) thereof and after sub-section (1) as so re-numbered the following sub-section shall be inserted namely:- "(2) Notwithstanding anything contained in this Act, where a dealer who is permitted to pay tax under Section 5-C has caused entry of goods into, local area for use, sale or consumption therein and has not paid tax on such goods under that section up to the date of commencement of the Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods (Third Amendment) Act, 1993 shall pay tax under Section 3 at the rates prevailing on the date immediately prior to the date of such commencement."
Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods (Third Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka (1) Notwithstanding anything contained in any judgment, decree or order of any court or other authority to the contrary, any assessment, re-assessment, levy or collection of any tax or penalty levied or purporting to have been levied, any action or thing taken or done in relation to such assessments, re-assessments, levy or collection made under the provisions of the Principal Act before the commencement of the Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods (Second Amendment) Act, 1993 (Karnataka Act 8 of 1993) hereinafter referred to as the said Amendment Act) shall be deemed to be as valid and effective as if such assessment, re-assessment, levy or collection or action or thing had been made, taken or done under the Principal Act as amended by the said amendment Act and accordingly:- (a) all acts, proceedings or things done or taken by the Government or by any officer of the Government or by any authority in connection with the assessment, re-assessment, levy or collection of such tax or penalty shall for all purposes be deemed to be and to have always been done or taken in accordance with law; (b) no suit or other proceedings shall be maintained or continued in any court or before any authority for the refund of any such tax; (c) no court shall enforce any decree or order directing the refund of any such tax. (2) for the removal of doubts, it is hereby declared that nothing in sub-section (1) shall be construed as preventing any person,- (a) from questioning in accordance with the provisions of the Principal Act, as amended by the said Amendment Act, any assessment, re-assessment levy, or collection of tax or penalty referred to in sub-section (1), or (b) from claiming refund of any tax or penalty paid by him in excess of the amount due from him by way of tax under the Principal Act as amended by the said Amendment Act.
Karnataka Tax on Entry of Goods (Third Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 10--> State(s): Karnataka (1) Where entry of goods specified in serial number 1 of the Schedule to the Principal Act as it stood prior to the first day of May, 1992 has been caused into a local area for consumption, use or sale therein during the period between the Nineteenth day of April, 1988 and Thirty-first day of March, 1990 and the dealer causing such entry has not collected any tax under the Principal Act on the ground that no such tax could have been levied or collected in respect of the entry of such goods into a local area and no such tax could have been levied or collected if the amendment made in the Principal Act by the said Amendment Act, had not been made, then, notwithstanding anything contained in the said Amendment Act the dealer shall not be liable to pay any tax under the Principal Act as amended by the said Amendment Act in respect of the entry of such goods into a local area. (2) Where entry of any goods specified in serial number 2 of the Schedule to the Principal Act as it stood prior to the first day of May, 1992 had been caused into a local area for consumption, use or sale therein during the period between the Eleventh day of February, 1991, and Thirty-first day of March, 1991 and the dealer causing such entry has not collected any tax under the Principal Act on the ground that no such tax could have been levied or collected if the amendment made in the Principal Act by the said Amendment Act, had not been made, then notwithstanding anything contained in the said Amendment Act, the dealer shall not be liable to pay any tax under the Principal Act, as amended by the said Amendment Act in respect of the entry of such goods into a local area. (3) For purpose of sub-section (1) and (2) the burden of proving that no tax was collected under the Principal Act in respect of the entry of such goods into a local area referred to in sub-sections (1) (2) shall be on the dealer causing entry of such goods into a local area.
