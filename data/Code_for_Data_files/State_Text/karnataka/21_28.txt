Karnataka Village Panchayats (Postponement of Elections) (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Village Panchayats (Postponement of Elections) Act, 1975 . Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Village Panchayats (Postponement of Elections) Act, 1975 (Karnataka Act 6 of 1975); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-eighth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Village Panchayats (Postponement of Elections) (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Village Panchayats (Postponement of Elections) (Amendment) Act, 1977.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Thirtieth day of December, 1976. Received the assent of the Governor on the Second day of May, 1977 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Village Panchayats (Postponement of Elections) (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Karnataka Village Panchayats (Postponement of Elections) Act, 1975 (Karnataka Act 6 of 1975), in sub-section (3) of Section 1, for the figures and words "31st December, 1976", the figures and words "31st December, 1977" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Village Panchayats (Postponement of Elections) (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka Village Panchayats (Postponement of Elections) (Second Amendment) Ordinance, 1976 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 36 of 1976) is hereby repealed.
