Mysore Palace (Acquisition and Transfer) (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Mysore Palace (Acquisition and Transfer) Act, 1998. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Mysore Palace (Acquisition and Transfer) Act, 1998 (Karnataka Act 32 of 1998), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Sixty-fourth year of the Republic of India, as follows-
Mysore Palace (Acquisition and Transfer) (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Mysore Palace (Acquisition and Transfer) (Amendment) Act, 2013.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Seventh day of March, 2013.
Mysore Palace (Acquisition and Transfer) (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 24 of the Mysore Palace (Acquisition and Transfer) Act, 1998 (Karnataka Act 32 of 1998), in sub-section (1), for item number (6) and entries relating thereto the following shall be substituted, namely- "(6) The Commissioner for Archaeology Museum and Heritage Department Member".
