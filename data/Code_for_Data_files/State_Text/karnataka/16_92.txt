Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1974. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 32 of 1974), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Amendment) Act, 19761 . (2) It shall come into force at once. First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, on the Twenty-first day of February 1977, Received the assent of the President on the Fourteenth day of February, 1977
Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1974, in sub-clause (iii) of clause (e), after the words "established by or under" the words "a Central Act or" shall be inserted.
