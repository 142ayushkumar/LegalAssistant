Advocate-General (Designation) Act, 1941_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka Whereas, it is expedient to change the designation of the Government Advocate in Mysore; it is hereby enacted as follows -
Advocate-General (Designation) Act, 1941_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (a) This Act may be called the Advocate-General (Designation) Act, 1941.1 (b) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of His Highness the Maharaja on the Twenty-seventh day of January, 1941
Advocate-General (Designation) Act, 1941_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka For the words "Government Advocate" wherever they occur in any Regulation, Act or other law for the time being in force, or any Rules, Order or Notification made under any such Regulation, Act or other law, the words "Advocate-General" shall be substituted.
