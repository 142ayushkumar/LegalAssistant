Enemy Agents (Repealing) Act, 1946_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka Whereas, it is necessary to repeal the Enemy Agents Act, 1944 (XX of 1944), with effect from the 1st day of October, 1946. Now, therefore, in exercise of the powers conferred by Section 30 of the Government of Mysore Act, 1940 (XVIII of 1940). It is hereby enacted as follows -
Enemy Agents (Repealing) Act, 1946_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Enemy Agents (Repealing) Act, 1946.1 Received the assent of His Highness the Maharaja on the 30th day of September, 1946
Enemy Agents (Repealing) Act, 1946_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka The Enemy Agents Act, 1944 (XX of 1944), is repealed with effect from the 1st day of October, 1946.
