Karnataka Municipalities (Second Amendment) Act, 1995_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Municipalities Act, 1964. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Municipalities Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1964), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-sixth year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka Municipalities (Second Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Municipalities (Second Amendment) Act, 1995.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the twenty-sixth day of September, 1995 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Municipalities (Second Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Municipalities Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1964) (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act) - (1) for clause (1-A) the following shall be substituted, namely - "(1-A) "Backward Classes" means such class and classes of citizens as may be classified as categories "A" and "B" and notified by the Government from time to time for the purposes of reservation of seats and offices of President and Vice-President in a Municipal Council or Town Panchayat;" (2) in clause (6), the following shall be inserted, at the end, namely - "or Town Panchayat;" (3) after clause (28), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(28-A) "Town Panchayat" means a Town Panchayat established under this Act;"
Karnataka Municipalities (Second Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 11 of the principal Act, after sub-section (3), the following shall be inserted, namely:- "Provided that out of the seats reserved under this sub-section, eighty per cent of the total number of such seats shall be reserved for the persons falling under category "A" and the remaining twenty per cent of the seats shall be reserved for the persons falling under category "B" Provided further that if no person falling under category "A" is available, the seats reserved for that category shall also be filled by the persons falling under the category "B" and vice versa. Explanation - For the purpose of this sub-section, sub-section (2-A) of Section 42 and sub-section (5) of Section 352, categories "A" and "B" shall mean categories "A" and "B" referred to in clause (1-A) of Section 2."
Karnataka Municipalities (Second Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 38 of the principal Act, the following shall be inserted, namely - "38-A. Returning Officer, presiding officer etc. deemed to be on deputation to State Election Commission - Notwithstanding anything contained in this Act or any other law for the time being in force, the returning officer, assistant returning officer, presiding officer, polling officer and any other officer appointed by or under this Act and any police officer designated for the time being by the Government, for the conduct of any election of Councillors under this Act shall be deemed to be on deputation to the State Election Commission for a period commencing on and from the date of notification calling for such election and ending with the date of declaration of the result of such election and accordingly, such officer shall, during that period, be subject to the control of superintendence and discipline of the State Election Commission."
Karnataka Municipalities (Second Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 42 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2-A), after clause (b) the following shall be inserted, namely:- "Provided that out of the offices reserved under this clause, eighty per cent of the total number of such offices shall be reserved for the persons falling under category "A" and the remaining twenty per cent of the offices shall be reserved for the persons falling under category "B": Provided further that if no person falling under category "A" is available, offices reserved for that category shall also be filled by the persons falling under category "B" and vice versa."
Karnataka Municipalities (Second Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 352 of the principal Act, after sub-section (5), the following shall be inserted, namely:- "Provided that out of the seats reserved under this sub-section eighty per cent of the total number of such seats shall be reserved for the persons falling under category "A" and the remaining twenty per cent of the seats shall be reserved for the persons falling under category "B": Provided further that if no persons falling under category "A" is available, the seats reserved for that category shall also be filled by the persons falling under category "B" and vice versa."
