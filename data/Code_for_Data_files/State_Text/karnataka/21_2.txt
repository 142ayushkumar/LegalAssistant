Karnataka Vacant Lands in Urban Areas (Prohibition of Alienation) (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Vacant Lands in Urban Areas (Prohibition of Alienation) Act, 1975 . Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Vacant Lands in Urban Areas (Prohibition of Alienation) Act, 1975 (Karnataka Act 36 of 1975) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Vacant Lands in Urban Areas (Prohibition of Alienation) (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Vacant Lands in Urban Areas (Prohibition of Alienation) (Amendment) Act, 1980.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Twenty-sixth day of July, 1979. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twentieth day of August, 1980 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Vacant Lands in Urban Areas (Prohibition of Alienation) (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Karnataka Vacant Lands in Urban Areas (Prohibition of Alienation) Act, 1975 (Karnataka Act 36 of 1975) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (1) in clause (b), for the words "one thousand square metres", the words "two thousand square metres" shall be substituted; (2) for clause (f), the following shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted, namely - "(f) "urban area" means any area which is comprised for the time being in any City or Town Municipality or notified area and the area within one kilometre from the limits of such City or Town Municipality or notified area."
Karnataka Vacant Lands in Urban Areas (Prohibition of Alienation) (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4 of the principal Act, in the first proviso to sub-section (1), for the words "one thousand square metres", the words "two thousand square metres" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Vacant Lands in Urban Areas (Prohibition of Alienation) (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the principal Act, in sub-clause (i), of clause (a), for the words "one thousand square metres", the words "two thousand square metres" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Vacant Lands in Urban Areas (Prohibition of Alienation) (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Vacant Lands in Urban Areas (Prohibition of Alienation) (Second Amendment) Ordinance, 1979 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 15 of 1979) is hereby, repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the said Act as amended by this Act.
