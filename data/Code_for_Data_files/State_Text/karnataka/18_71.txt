Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act, further to amend the Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board Act, 1966. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board Act, 1966 (Karnataka Act 16 of 1966) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-sixth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board (Amendment) Act, 19951 2 . (2) It shall come into force at once. First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, on the Third day of June, 1995, Received the Assent of the Governor on the 31st day of May, 1995 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 20 of the Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board Act, 1966 (Karnataka Act 16 of 1966), sub-section (2) shall be omitted.
