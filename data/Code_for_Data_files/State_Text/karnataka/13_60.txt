Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1981.1 2 (2) Sections 7 and 9 shall be deemed to have come into force on the Thirtieth day of May, 1981 and the remaining provisions shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Seventh day of August, 1981 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1957), (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (1), after the Explanation, the following note shall be and shall be deemed always to have been inserted, namely:- "Note - For the purpose of the above Explanation the certificate of registration shall, notwithstanding anything contained in Section 38 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1939, be deemed to be current even if the certificate of fitness is not effective provided such certificate of fitness has not been cancelled."
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the principal Act, for the words, "ten days" the words "fifteen days" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10 of the principal Act, after clause (f), the following proviso shall be inserted, namely:- "Provided that the fleet owner shall be entitled to a proportionate reduction in the amount of tax finally determined, in respect of any motor vehicle which is certified by the Commissioner for Transport as not used for a period of one calendar month or more subject to the condition that the fleet owner has intimated the fact of non-user of the motor vehicle to the Commissioner for Transport within seven days of the date of commencement of the period of non-user."
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10-A of the principal Act, the words, brackets and figures "under reciprocal arrangement referred to in sub-section (3) of Section 3 or", shall be omitted.
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 15-A of the principal Act, the words, "either of his own motion or on an application made by any aggrieved person", shall be omitted.

Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka If any difficulty arises in giving effect to the provisions of the principal Act, as amended by this Act, the State Government may, by notification in the official Gazette, make such provisions (including the provision for payment of tax for a period shorter than a quarter) as may appear to it be necessary or expedient for removing the difficulty: Provided that no such order shall be made after the expiry of a period of two years from the date of commencement of this Act.
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Ordinance, 1981 (Karnataka Ordinance 13 of 1981), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
