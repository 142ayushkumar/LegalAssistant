Karnataka High Court (Amendment) Act, 1973_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka High Court Act, 1961. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka High Court Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1962) for purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka High Court (Amendment) Act, 1973_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka High Court (Amendment) Act, 1973.1 (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may by notification appoint. Received the assent of the Governor on the Fifth day of June, 1973
Karnataka High Court (Amendment) Act, 1973_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 9 of the Karnataka High Court Act, 1961 (Karnataka Ad 5 of 1962) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) after Clause (xi), the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(xii) exercise of powers under - (a) Clause (1) of Article 226 of the Constitution of India except where such power relates to the issue of a writ in the nature of habeas corpus; and (b) Articles 227 and 228 of the Constitution of India".
Karnataka High Court (Amendment) Act, 1973_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka For Clause (iv) of Section 10 of the principal Act, the following clauses shall be substituted, namely:- "(iv) exercise of powers under Clause (1) of Article 226 of the Constitution of India where such power relates to the issue of a writ in the nature of habeas corpus; (iv-a) an appeal from any original judgment, order or decree passed by a single Judge in exercise of the powers under Clause (1) of Article 226, Article 227 and Article 228 of the Constitution of India".
Karnataka High Court (Amendment) Act, 1973_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka Notwithstanding anything contained in any law, all applications and other proceedings relating to the matters specified in Section 2 pending in the High Court on the date of commencement of this Act shall be disposed of in accordance with the provisions of the principal Act as amended by this Act.
