Karnataka Gift Goods (Unlawful Possession) (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Gift Goods (Unlawful Possession) Act, 1963. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Gift Goods (Unlawful Possession) Act, 1963 (Karnataka Act 20 of 1963); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twentieth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Gift Goods (Unlawful Possession) (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Gift Goods (Unlawful Possession) (Amendment) Act, 1969.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the first day of May, 1969
Karnataka Gift Goods (Unlawful Possession) (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 5-B of the Karnataka Gift Goods (Unlawful Possession) Act, 1963 (Karnataka Act 20 of 1963), the following section shall be inserted namely - "5-C. Payment of a portion of fine to Police Officers and others - The Magistrate trying any case under this Act may award any portion not exceeding one-half of any fine which may be levied under Section 3 to the police officers and other persons who may have given assistance in the detection or investigation of the offence."
