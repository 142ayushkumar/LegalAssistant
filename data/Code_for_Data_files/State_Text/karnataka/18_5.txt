Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1961_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twelfth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1961.1 2 (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may by notification in the Official Gazette appoint. Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixth day of June, 1961 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-1-2000
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Third Schedule to the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957), after the entries relating to Serial No. 11, the following entries shall be inserted, namely - "11-A Sugarcane Purchase by the last dealer in the State liable to tax under this Act Fifteen percent".
