Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka (Religious and Charitable) Inams Abolition Act, 1955, the Karnataka (Sandur Area) Inams Abolition Act, 1976 and the Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition Act, 1977. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka (Religious and Charitable) Inams Abolition Act, 1955, (Karnataka Act 18 of 1955), the Karnataka (Sandur Area) Inams Abolition Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 54 of 1976) and the Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition Act, 1977 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1978) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty- second Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Act, 1981.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Tenth day of September, 1980. Received the assent of the President on the Twenty-fourth day of April, 1981
Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10 of the Karnataka (Sandur Area) Inams Abolition Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 54 of 1976), for the words, letters and figures "on or before 31st March, 1980", the words, letters and figures "on or before 30th June, 1981" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 11 of the Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition Act, 1977 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1978), for the words, letters and figures "on or before 31st March, 1980" the words, letters and figures "on or before 30th June, 1981" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Ordinance, 1980 (Karnataka Ordinance 7 of 1980) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the Karnataka Act 18 of 1955, the Karnataka Act 54 of 1976 and the Karnataka Act 10 of 1978 as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the said Acts as amended by this Act.
