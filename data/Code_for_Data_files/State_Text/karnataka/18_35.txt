Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act No. 25 of 1957), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1976.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on Sixth day of December, 1976
Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (1) for the proviso to sub-section (1), the following proviso shall be substituted, namely - "Provided that if and to the extent to which such turnover relates to gur (jaggery), gunny bags, bardans (including batars) and hessian cloth, the tax shall be calculated at the rate of two per cent of such turnover"; (2) in sub-section (3-A) - (a) the words "mentioned in the Second Schedule" and "or the rate specified in the Second Schedule in respect of such goods whichever is lower" shall be and shall be deemed to have been omitted with effect from 1st April, 1972; and (b) for the words "mentioned in that Schedule", the words "mentioned in the Second Schedule" shall be and shall be deemed to have been substituted with effect from 1st April, 1972.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In the proviso to Section 6 of the principal Act, for clause (i), the following clause shall be substituted, namely - "(i) in respect of sale or purchase of goods specified in the Fourth Schedule - (a) which are taxable at the point of purchase; and (b) which have already been subject to tax under sub-section (4) of Section 5."
Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka For sub-section (5) of Section 28-A of the principal Act, the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely - "(5) The penalty leviable under sub-section (4) shall not exceed double the amount of tax leviable in respect of the goods under transport: Provided that in the case of goods specified in the Second, Third and Fourth Schedules, if the owner or the person in charge of the goods vehicle or boat produces proof to show that the goods in question have already suffered the single point tax under this Act, no penalty shall be leviable."
Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In the Second Schedule to the principal Act, for the entries relating to Serial Numbers 137 and 138, the following entries shall be substituted, namely - "137. Cereals (whether with or without husk) other than those mentioned in Serial No. 9 of Fourth Schedule Four per cent 138. Flour and husks of pulses; atta, maida, soji and bran of wheat; parched rice, beaten rice and rice bran; soji, maida, atta, grits, flakes and bran of maize Four per cent 139. Pulses (whether whole or separate and whether with or without husk) other than those mentioned in Serial No. 10 of Fourth Schedule Four per cent 140. Chunni of pulses Four per cent."
Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In the Fourth Schedule to the principal Act, for the entries relating to Serial Numbers 8, 9 and 10 the following entries shall be substituted, namely - "8. Crude oil as specified in clause (ii-c) of Section 14 of the Central Sales Tax Act, 1956 Sale by the first or earliest of successive dealers in the State liable to tax under this Act Four per cent 9. Cereals, that is to say, rice, wheat, jowar or milo, bajra, maize, ragi, kodon, kutki and barley Sale by the first or earliest of successive dealers in the State liable to tax under this Act Four per cent 10. Pulses (whether whole or separated and whether with or without husk), that is to say, gram or gulab gram, tur or arhar, mong or green gram, masur or lentil, urad or blackgram, moth, lakh or khesari Sale by the first or earliest of successive dealers in the State liable to tax under this Act Four per cent".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In the Fifth Schedule to the principal Act - (1) for Serial Number 28, the following shall be substituted, namely - "28. Products of prescribed village industries which satisfy the prescribed conditions and limitations when sold by a bona fide producer recognised by the Commissioner"; (2) Serial Numbers 40 and 41 and entries relating thereto shall be and shall be deemed to have been omitted on and with effect from 8th September, 1976; (3) in explanation (1), clause (a) shall be omitted.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Ordinance, 1976 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 22 of 1976) is hereby repealed.
