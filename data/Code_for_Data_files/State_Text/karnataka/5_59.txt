Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1987_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act 30 of 1958) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1987.1 (2) It shall come into force on the First day of April, 1987. Received the assent of the Governor on the Second day of April, 1987
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act 30 of 1958) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (1), for the table, the following table shall be substituted, namely - "Table Sl. No. Payment for admission (excluding the amount of the tax) Rate of tax (a) Does not exceed one rupee and twenty five paise; Thirty five per cent of such payment (b) Exceeds one rupee and twenty five paise but does not exceed two rupees and fifty paise; Forty five per cent of such payment (c) Exceeds two rupees and fifty paise. Fifty five per cent of such payment.
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In the third proviso, to principal Act, for the words "one thousand", the words and figures "Thirty-first December, 1986", the words and figures "Thirty-first December, 1987" shall be and shall be deemed to have been substituted with effect from Thirty-first December, 1986.
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4-B of the principal Act, for the words "one thousand", the words "two thousand five hundred" shall be substituted.
