Mysore Short Titles Act, 1949_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to facilitate the citation of certain Acts. Whereas, it is expedient to facilitate the citation of certain Acts. It is hereby enacted as follows -
Mysore Short Titles Act, 1949_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Mysore Short Titles Act, 1949.1 Received the assent of His Highness the Maharaja on the 26th day of January, 1949
