Mysore Cutchi Memon Act, 1943_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka Whereas, it is expedient that all Cutchi Memons be governed, in matters of succession and inheritance, by the Mohammedan Law. It is hereby enacted as follows -
Mysore Cutchi Memon Act, 1943_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called 1 [the Mysore Cutchi Memon Act, 1943].2 (2) It shall come into force on the first day of July, 1943. Substituted for the words and figures "the Cutchi Memon Act, 1943" by Act No. 1 1956" by Act No. 1 of 1956 Received the assent of His Highness the Maharaja on the Thirty-first day of December, 1942
Mysore Cutchi Memon Act, 1943_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka Subject to the provisions of Section 3, all Cutchi Memons shall, in matters of succession and inheritance, be governed by Mohammadan Law.
Mysore Cutchi Memon Act, 1943_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka Nothing in this Act shall affect any right or liability acquired or incurred before its commencement, or any legal proceeding or remedy in respect of any such right or liability and any such proceeding or remedy may be continued or enforced as if this Act had not been passed.
