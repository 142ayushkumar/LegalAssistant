Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2006_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the fifty-seventh year of the Republic of India, as follows: -
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2006_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2006. 1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the thirteenth day of November, 2006
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2006_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In clause (iii) of sub-section (1) of section 94B of the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964), for the words "within six years" the words "within eight years" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted. By Order and in the name of the Governor of Karnataka G.K. BOREGOWDA Secretary to Government, Department of Parliamentary Affairs and Legislation
