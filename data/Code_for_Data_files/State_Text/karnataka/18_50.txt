Karnataka Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1975_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957, Whereas, it is necessary and expedient further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-sixth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1975_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1975.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have been come into force on the Fifteenth day of July, 1975. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twelfth day of August, 1975 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1975_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Fourth Schedule to the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957) - (1) in Column 4 of Serial Number 1, for the words "three per cent", the words "four per cent" shall be substituted; (2) in Column 4 of Serial Number 2, for the words "three per cent", the words "four per cent" shall be substituted; and (3) in Column 4 of Serial Number 4, for the words "three per cent", the words "four per cent" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1975_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Ordinance, 1975 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 6 of 1975) is hereby repealed: Provided that anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under principal Act as amended by this Act.
