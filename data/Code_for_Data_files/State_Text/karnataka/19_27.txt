Karnataka Tax on Luxuries (Hotels and Lodging Houses) (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Tax on Luxuries (Hotels and Lodging Houses) Act, 1979. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Tax on Luxuries (Hotels and Lodging Houses) Act, 1979 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1979) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Tax on Luxuries (Hotels and Lodging Houses) (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Tax on Luxuries (Hotels and Lodging Houses) (Amendment) Act, 1987.1 2 (2) It shall come into force on the First day of April, 1987. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000. Received the assent of the Governor on the Second day of April, 1987
Karnataka Tax on Luxuries (Hotels and Lodging Houses) (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Tax on Luxuries (Hotels and Lodging Houses) Act, 1979 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1979) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in clause (5), for the words "fifty rupees", the words "thirty five rupees", shall be substituted.
Karnataka Tax on Luxuries (Hotels and Lodging Houses) (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the principal Act - (1) for sub-section (1), excluding the provisos, the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely - "(1) Subject to the provisions of this Act, there shall be levied and collected a tax on the luxury provided in a hotel in respect of every room (to be known as 'Luxury Tax') at the following rates, namely - (a) Where the charges for lodging per room per day are not less than thirty five rupees but less than one hundred rupees - Five per cent of charges (b) Where the charges for lodging per room per day are not less than rupees one hundred but less than two hundred rupees Seven and half per cent of such charges (c) Where the charges for lodging per room per day are not less than two hundred Ten percent of such charges (2) after sub-section (1), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(1-A) Notwithstanding anything contained in sub-section (1), no tax shall be levied and collected from a hotelier to the extent of tax not collected by him during the period from the 1st day of August, 1985 to the 31st day of March, 1986".
