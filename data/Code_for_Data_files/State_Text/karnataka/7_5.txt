Government Grants (Karnataka Extension) Act, 1972_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to provide for the extension of the Government Grants Act, 1895 to the whole of the State of Karnataka. Whereas, it is expedient to extend the Government Grants Act, 1895 (Central Act 15 of 1895) to the whole of the State of Karnataka; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Government Grants (Karnataka Extension) Act, 1972_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Government Grants (Karnataka Extension) Act, 1972.1 (2) It shall extend to the whole of the State of Karnataka. (3) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the President on the Second day of June, 1973
Government Grants (Karnataka Extension) Act, 1972_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Government Grants Act, 1895 (Central Act 15 of 1895) (hereinafter referred to as the extended Act) is hereby extended to, and shall be in force, in the whole of the State of Karnataka. (2) Subject to the provisions of sub-section (1), after the words and letters "in Part B States" in Sub-section (2) of Section 1 of the extended Act, the words, brackets, figures and letters "other than the territories specified in Clause (a) and Clause (c) of sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the States Re-organisation Act, 1956 (Central Act 37 of 1956)" shall be added.
