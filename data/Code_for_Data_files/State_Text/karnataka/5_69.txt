Karnataka Excise (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Excise Act, 1965. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Excise Act, 1965 (Karnataka Act 21 of 1966) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Excise (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Excise (Amendment) Act, 1976.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Seventh day of September, 1976. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-seventh day of November, 1976
Karnataka Excise (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-clause (i) of clause (a) of Section 67 of the Karnataka Excise Act, 1965 (Karnataka Act 21 of 1966) for the words "the Armed Forces" the words "the Armed Forces (including Para military Units)" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Excise (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Excise (Amendment) Ordinance, 1976 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 21 of 1976) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
