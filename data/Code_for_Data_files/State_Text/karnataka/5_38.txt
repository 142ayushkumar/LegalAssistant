Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act 30 of 1958) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1976.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of April, 1976. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-ninth day of April, 1976
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act 30 of 1958) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (1) to sub-section (2), the following proviso shall be added, namely - "Provided that where the seat or accommodation which the holder of such a ticket is entitled to occupy or use is different from the classes of seat or accommodation inside the auditorium or place of entertainment and for admission to the said seat or accommodation no payment is fixed, the holder of such ticket shall be deemed to be entitled to occupy or use the highest class of seat or accommodation and shall for purposes of this Act, be deemed to have been admitted on payment of the charges for such highest class of seat or accommodation." (2) sub-section (3) shall be omitted.
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 3-A of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely - "3-B. Levy of additional tax on payment for admission to entertainments -In addition to the entertainment tax and surcharge leviable under Sections 3 and 3-A there shall be levied and paid to the State Government on each payment for admission to an entertainment, an additional tax at the following rates, namely - Where the payment (including the amount of tax and surcharge) Rate of additional tax (a) exceeds one rupee but does not exceed one rupee and fifty paise 10 paise (b) exceeds one rupee and fifty paise but does not exceed two rupees and fifty paise 20 paise (c) exceeds two rupees and fifty paise but does not exceed three rupees and fifty paise 30paise (d) exceeds three rupees and fifty paise 40 paise."
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6-A of the principal Act - (1) after sub-section (1), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(1-A) Before any proprietor submits the returns required by sub-section (1), he shall, in the prescribed manner, pay into a Government Treasury the full amount of tax, surcharge and additional tax or other taxes, if any, payable by him on the basis of such returns and shall along with the return furnish a receipt from the Treasury evidencing such payment." (2) for sub-section (2), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely - "(2) If the prescribed authority is satisfied that any return submitted finder sub-section (1) is correct and complete and that the tax under the provisions of this Act due thereon is paid in full, it shall assess the proprietor on the basis thereof." (3) in sub-section (3) for the words, figures and letter "Sections 3, 3-A and 4" the words, figures and letters "Sections 3, 3-A, 3-B and 4" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6-B of the principal Act - (i) in sub-section (1) after the words, figure and letter "or Section 3-A" wherever they occur, the words, figure and letter "or Section 3-B" shall be inserted; (ii) in sub-section (2) for the words "one and a half times" the word "thrice" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka After sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the principal Act, the following sub-sections shall be inserted, namely - "(2) An order made under sub-section (1) may be subject to such restrictions and conditions (including a condition that the payment for admission to the entertainments shall not be enhanced) as may be specified in the order. (3) The State Government may, by order, cancel or vary any order issued under sub-section (1) (4) If any restriction or condition specified in an order under sub-section (2) is contravened or is not observed, the proprietor shall be liable to the payment of the entertainments tax as if the provisions of the order under sub-section (1) did not apply to the said entertainment. Explanation -For the purposes of this section entertainments tax includes additional tax also."
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka Section 8 of the principal Act, shall be renumbered as sub-section (1) thereof and after the said sub-section, the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(2) Subject to such rules as may be made, where the State Government is satisfied that any performance of a cinematograph show could not be completed on account of failure of electric power or mechanical break down and that the proprietor has returned the payments for admission to the persons admitted to such performance, it may, by order, direct repayment to the proprietor the amount of entertainment tax paid by him in respect of such performance."
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 8 of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely - "8-A. Rounding off of tax etc -The amount of tax in respect of each payment for admission, surcharge, additional tax, fine, penalty, or any other amount payable and the amount of refund due, under the provisions of this Act, shall be rounded off to the nearest multiple of five paise."
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka For Sections 9 and 9-A of the principal Act, the following section shall be substituted, namely - "9. Payment and recovery of tax -(1) The tax, surcharge or additional tax payable under this Act shall be paid in such manner and in such instalments, if any, and within such time, as may be prescribed. (2) If default is made in making payment in accordance with sub-section (1)- (i) the whole of the amount outstanding on the date of default shall become immediately due and shall be a charge on the properties of the proprietor liable to pay such tax, surcharge or additional tax; and (ii) the proprietor liable to pay such tax, surcharge or additional tax under this Act shall be liable to pay a penalty equal to - (a) one per cent of the amount of tax, surcharge or additional tax remaining unpaid for each month for the first three months after the expiry of the time prescribed under sub-section (1); and (b) two and a half per cent of such amount for each month subsequent to the first three months as aforesaid. Explanation -For purposes of clause (ii), the penalty payable for a part of a month shall be proportionately determined. (3) Any amount of tax, surcharge or additional tax or any other amount including penalty, due under this Act may, without prejudice to any other mode of collection be recovered - (a) as if it were an arrear of land revenue; or (b) on application to any Magistrate, by such Magistrate as if it were a fine imposed by him. (4) The High Court may, either suo motu or on any application by the prescribed authority or any person aggrieved by the order, revise any order made by a Magistrate under clause (b) of sub-section (3)".
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 10--> State(s): Karnataka After clause (a) of sub-section (1) of Section 10 of the principal Act, the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(aa) If any such Officer has reason to suspect that the proprietor is evading or is attempting to evade payment of any tax, surcharge or additional tax payable under this Act, he may, for reasons to be recorded in writing, seize such accounts, registers, ticket books or other documents of the proprietor as he may consider necessary and shall give the proprietor a receipt for the same. Thereafter he shall forthwith forward the accounts, registers, ticket books and other documents so seized to the prescribed officer concerned who shall retain the same only for so long as may be necessary for their examination and for any enquiry or proceedings under this Act: Provided that such accounts, registers, ticket books or other documents shall not be retained for more than thirty days at a time except with the permission of the State Government."
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 11--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 12 of the principal Act - (i) in sub-clause (ii) of clause (b), for the words, "be liable in respect of each such offence to a fine which may extend to one thousand rupees", the words "be punishable with simple imprisonment which may extend to six months or with fine which may extend to two thousand rupees or with both" shall be substituted; (ii) in the proviso to clause (b), for the words, "five hundred rupees", the words, "one thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 12--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 13 of the principal Act, for the words "one thousand", in the two places where they occur, the words "two thousand" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 13--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 17 of the Karnataka Cinemas (Regulation) Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 23 of 1964) for the word and figures "Section 12", the words and figures "Section 12 or has compounded an offence under Section 13" shall be substituted.
