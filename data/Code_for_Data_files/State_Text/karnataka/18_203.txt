Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka State Universities Act, 1976. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka State Universities Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1976); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1977.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Fourth day of March, 1977. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e. f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Eighteenth day of April, 1977
Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 73 of the Karnataka State Universities Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1976), for the words "eighteen months", the words "twenty-four months" shall be substituted.
Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Ordinance, 1977 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 4 of 1977) is hereby repealed.
