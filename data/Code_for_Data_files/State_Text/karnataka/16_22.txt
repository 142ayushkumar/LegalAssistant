Karnataka Pawn Brokers (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Pawn Brokers Act, 1961. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Pawn Brokers Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 13 of 1962), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-eighth year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Karnataka Pawn Brokers (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Pawn Brokers (Amendment) Act, 1997.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the 16th day of April, 1998
Karnataka Pawn Brokers (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Pawn Brokers Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 13 of 1962) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for clause (12), the following shall be substituted, namely - "(12) "Year" means the year commencing on the First day of April."
Karnataka Pawn Brokers (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the principal Act - (i) in Explanation 2, for the words and figures "from the date on which it is granted to the 31st December following but may be renewed from year to year", the words "for a term of five years, but may be renewed from term to term" shall be substituted. (ii) after Explanation 2, the following shall be inserted, namely - "Explanation 3 - Where a licence has been granted in the middle of a year, for the purpose of computing the term of licence, the remaining part of the year shall be deemed to be a year."
Karnataka Pawn Brokers (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4 of the principal Act, for sub-section (5) excluding the proviso, the following shall be substituted, namely - "(5) Every application for the grant or renewal of a licence, shall be accompanied by a fee at the following rates - (a) if the place at which the business is to be carried on is not more than one Rupees two thousand five hundred. (b) if the business is to be carried on at more than one place Rupees two thousand five hundred for the principal place of business and rupees one thousand two hundred and fifty for each of the other places."
Karnataka Pawn Brokers (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4-A of the principal Act - (a) for sub-section (1), the following shall be substituted, namely - "(i) Every person making an application for grant of a licence under Section 4 shall, at the time of making such application, pay security deposit as provided under sub-section (2) and in the case of an application for renewal of such licence the security deposit shall be paid two months before the expiry of such licence." (b) in sub-section (2), in the table for the word "lends" wherever it occurs, the word "invests" shall be substituted. (c) in sub-section (3) - (i) for the words "aggregate amount lent by him during the previous year", the words "the amount invested by him in the business during the previous year" shall be substituted. (ii) the words "and such security deposit shall not carry any interest" shall be deemed to have been inserted at the end with effect from 31st May, 1985. (iii) after the proviso, the following shall be inserted, namely - "Provided further that in the case of a new licensee or a person who has a licence only for a portion of the preceding year the amount of security shall be determined on the basis of a declaration in the prescribed form as to the amount which he is likely to invest during the year."
Karnataka Pawn Brokers (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka Notwithstanding anything contained in any judgment, decree or order of any Court, the provisions of sub-section (4-A) of the principal Act as amended by sub-clause (ii) of clause (c) of Section 5 of this Act, shall be deemed always to have been in force and no person who has paid or deemed to have paid the security deposit under Section 4-A of the principal Act on or after 31st May, 1985 and before the commencement of this Act shall be entitled to claim interest on such security deposit and accordingly - (a) no suit or other proceedings shall be entertained or continued in any Court, Tribunal or before any Authority for the payment of interest on such security deposit; and (b) no Court shall enforce any decree or order directing the payment of interest on such security deposit.
