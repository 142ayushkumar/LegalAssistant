Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Sixty-second year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2011. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 95 of the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964), in sub-section (2-A), for the words, brackets and figures "within the period of one year from the date of commencement of the Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2009", the words, brackets and figures "within the period of one year from the date of commencement of the Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2011" shall be and shall always be deemed to have been substituted.
