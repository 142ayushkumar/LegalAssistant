Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1962) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the fifty-fourth year of Republic of India, as follows:-
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 2003. 1 (2) It shall come into force on such 2 [date] as the State Government may, by notification appoint. Published in the Karnataka Gazette Part IV-A Extra Ordinary No. 977 dated 20-8-2003. The Act has come into force on 1st day of November 2003, vide Notification No. RD 8 LRA 2001 (Part) dated 30.10.2003.
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of section 77 of the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961,- (i) for clause (iv), the following shall be substituted, namely:- "(iv) landless persons or other persons residing in villages in the same Panchayat area whose gross annual income does not exceed rupees twenty thousand and ex-military personnel whose gross annual income does not exceed rupees twenty-two thousand." (ii) clause (vi) shall be omitted. By Order and in the name of the Governor of Karnataka M.R. Hegde Secretary to Government, Department of Parliamentary Affairs and Legislation.
