Karnataka Government Parks (Preservation) (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Government Parks (Preservation) Act, 1975. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Government Parks (Preservation) Act, 1975 (Karnataka Act 23 of 1975) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Government Parks (Preservation) (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Government Parks (Preservation) (Amendment) Act, 1982.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Seventh day of August, 1982
Karnataka Government Parks (Preservation) (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka Section 5 of the Karnataka Government Parks (Preservation) Act, 1975 (Karnataka Act 23 of 1975) shall be and shall be deemed always to have been omitted.
