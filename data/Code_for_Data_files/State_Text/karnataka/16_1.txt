Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Panchayat Raj Act, 1993. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Panchayat Raj Act, 1993 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1993), for the purposes hereinafter appearing: Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-sixth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1995.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the thirteenth day of January, 1995. Repealed by the Karnataka Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the Assent of the Governor On the Thirtieth Day of April, 1995
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Panchayat Raj Act, 1993 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1993) (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act) for clause (2), the following shall be substituted, namely - "(2) "Backward Classes" means such class or classes of citizens as may be classified as category "A" and "B" and notified by the Government from time to time for the purpose of reservation of seats and offices of Chairperson in Zilla Panchayat, Taluk Panchayat and Grama Panchayat."
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the Principal Act,- (1) proviso to sub-section (2), shall be omitted: (2) after sub-section (3), the following shall be inserted, namely: "Provided that out of the seats reserved under this sub-section, eighty percent of the total number of such seats shall be reserved for the persons falling under category "A" and the remaining twenty percent of the seats shall be reserved for the persons falling under category "B": Provided further that if no person falling under category "A" is available, the seats reserved for that category shall also be filled by the persons falling under category "B" and vice versa. Explanation - For the purpose of this sub-section, proviso to clause (b) of sub-section (2) of Section 44, sub-section (2) of Section 123, clause (b) of sub-section (2) of Section 138, sub-section (2) of Section 162 and clause (b) of sub-section (2) of Section 177, categories "A" and "B" shall mean category 'A' and 'B' referred to in clause (2) of Section 2."
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 44 of the Principal Act, in sub-section (2),- (1) proviso to clause (a) shall be omitted; (2) after clause (b), the following shall be inserted, namely - "Provided that out of the offices reserved under this clause eighty percent of the total number of such offices shall be reserved for the persons falling under category 'A' and the remaining twenty percent of the offices shall be reserved for the persons falling under category 'B. Provided further that if no person falling under category 'A' is available, the offices reserved for that category shall also be filled by the persons falling under category 'B' and vice versa."
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 123 of the Principal Act,- (1) proviso to sub-section (1) shall be omitted; (2) after sub-section (2), the following shall be inserted, namely: "Provided that out of the seats reserved under this sub-section eighty percent of the total number of such seats shall be reserved for the persons falling under category 'A' and the remaining twenty percent of the seats shall be reserved for the persons falling under category 'B."
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 138 of the Principal Act, in sub-section (2),- (1) proviso to clause (a), shall be omitted; (2) after clause (b), the following shall be inserted, namely: "Provided that out of the offices reserved under this clause, eighty percent of the total number of such offices shall be reserved for the persons falling under category 'A' and the remaining twenty percent of the offices shall be reserved for the persons falling under category 'B: Provided further that if no person falling under category 'A' is available, the offices reserved for that category shall also be filled by the persons falling under category 'B' and vice versa."
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka For Section 160 of the Principal Act, the following shall be substituted, namely - "160. Elected Members - The elected members of a Zilla Panchayat shall consist of persons elected from taluks in the District, the number of members to be elected from each taluk being fixed in accordance with the scale of one member for forty thousand or part thereof of the population: Provided that in the case of Uttara Kannada and Chickmagalur districts, it shall be one member for thirty thousand or part thereof of the population and in the case of Kodagu district, it shall be one member for eighteen thousand or art thereof of the population."
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 162 of the Principal Act,- (1) proviso to sub-section (1) shall be omitted; (2) after sub-section (2), the following shall be inserted, namely: "Provided that out of the seats reserved under this sub-section, eighty percent of the total number of such seats shall be reserved for the persons falling under category 'A' and the remaining twenty percent of the seats shall be reserved for the persons falling under category 'B."
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 177 of the Principal Act, in sub-section (2),- (1) proviso to clause (a), shall be omitted; (2) after clause (b), the following shall be inserted, namely - "Provided that out of the offices reserved under this clause, eighty percent of the total number of such offices shall be reserved for the persons falling under category 'A' and the remaining twenty percent of the offices shall be reserved for the persons falling under category 'B."
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 10--> State(s): Karnataka (1) Nothing in this Act shall affect the election held immediately before the commencement of this Act to fill the seats and offices of Adhyaksha and Upadhyaksha in a Grama Panchayat. (2) Election to fill the seats in any Grama Panchayat which have fallen vacant on the date of commencement of this Act shall be held in accordance with the provisions of the principal Act as amended by this Act.
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 11--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Ordinance, 1995 (Karnataka Ordinance 1 of 1995), is hereby, repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the Principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the Principal Act as amended by this Act.
