Karnataka Forest and Certain Other Law (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Forest Act, 1963 and the Karnataka Preservation of Trees Act, 1976. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Forest Act, 1963 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1964) and the Karnataka Preservation of Trees Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 76 of 1976) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-eighth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Forest and Certain Other Law (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Forest and Certain Other Law (Amendment) Act, 1997.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Eighth day of May, 1998
Karnataka Forest and Certain Other Law (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Karnataka Forest Act, 1963 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1964) - (1) in Section 2, (i) in clause (7), (a) in sub-clause (a), the words "cocoa beans or pods" shall be omitted; (b) in sub-clause (b), after item (iv), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(iv-a) cocoa beans or pods, garcinia fruits, thornless bamboos, Halmaddi, Raldhupa and Kaidhupa"; (ii) in clause (18), after the words "flakes or pulp", the words "and sandalwood oil" shall be inserted. (iii) after clause (22), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(23) "Village Forest Committee" means a Village Forest Committee constituted under Section 31-A". (2) after Section 31, the following shall be inserted, namely - "31-A. Constitution of Village Forest Committee for Joint Forest Planning and Management of Forest -(1) For the purpose of Joint Forest Planning and Management of Forest, the State Government may, by notification constitute a Village Forest Committee in respect of a village or group of villages. (2) The Government may by rules provide for - (i) the powers, duties and responsibilities of Village Forest Planning Committee; (ii) the conduct of elections to the Committee; (iii) qualification and disqualifications of Members of Village Forest Committee; (iv) the powers and duties of Forest Officer; (v) preparation and execution of management plans; (vi) protection, development and management of forest by the Committee; (vii) sharing mechanism, audit and accounts; (viii) appointment of administrators or administrative Committees; (ix) settlement of disputes; (x) monitoring and evaluation; and (xi) any other matter incidental thereto"; (3) in Section 41, sub-section (2) shall be omitted; (4) in Section 50, in sub-section (2), (i) in clause (k-a), (i) in the brackets, after the word "licence", the words "or to get it renewed" shall be inserted; (ii) after the words "existing saw pits", the words "starting new saw pits" shall be inserted; (ii) after clause (k-a), the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(k-b) provide for levy of fee for grant or renewal of licence for saw mills, saw pits and other sawing contrivances". (5) in Section 62, (i) in sub-section (1), for the words "cattle used", the words "cattle or any other property used" shall be substituted; (ii) in sub-section (3), (a) the words starting with "shall place" and ending with the words "so seized and" shall be omitted;. (b) in clause (a), after the word "ivory", the words "Gulmavu (machilus macrantha) bark, Dalchini bark, Halmaddi (exudation of Ailanthus malabaricum), canes" shall be inserted (6) in Section 63, after the words "release the same on", the words "production of a Bank guarantee equal to the value as estimated by such officer (which shall be renewable from time to time till the final disposal of the criminal proceedings instituted in respect of the alleged offence) and on" shall be inserted; (7) in Section 64-A, after the words "reserved forest" in the two places where they occur, the words "district forest, village forest, protected forest and any other land under the control of the Forest Department" shall be inserted; (8) in Section 71-A - (i) in sub-section (1), after the word "ivory", the words and brackets "Gulmavu (Machilus marantha) bark, Dalchini bark, Halmaddi (exudation of Ailanthus malabaricum), canes" shall be inserted; (ii) in sub-section (2), after the word "fire wood", the words and brakets "Gulmavu (machilus marantha) bark, Dalchini bark, Halmaddi (exudation of Ailanthus Malabaricum), canes" shall be inserted; (9) in Section 71-B, in sub-sections (1) and (2), after the word "firewood", the words and brackets "Gulmavu (Machilus marantha) bark, Dalchini bark, Halmaddi (exudation of Ailanthus malabaricum), canes" shall be inserted; (10) in Section 71-G, after the word "ivory", the words and brackets "Gulmavu (Machilus marantha) bark, Dalchini bark, Halmaddi (exudation of Ailanthus malabaricum), canes" shall be inserted; (11) in Section 79, in sub-section (1) - (i) in clause (a), for the words "five thousand rupees", the words "fifty thousand rupees" shall be substituted; (ii) in clause (b), for the words "as estimated by such officer", the words "as may be prescribed" shall be substituted; (12) after Section 82, the following shall be inserted, namely - "82-A. Criminal liability of licensee for acts of servants -Where any offence under this Act or rules made thereunder, is committed by any person in the employment and acting on behalf of the holder of a licence or permit granted under this Act, such holder shall also be punishable with a fine which may extend to rupees fifteen thousand as if he had committed the said offence unless he establishes that all due and reasonable precautions were exercised by him to prevent the commission of such offence: Provided that no person other than the actual offender shall be punishable with imprisonment except in default of payment of fine. 82-B. Offence by companies, etc -(1) If the person committing an offence under this Act is a company, the company as well as every person incharge of and responsible to the company for the conduct of its business at the time of the commission of the offence shall be liable to be proceeded against and punished accordingly: Provided that nothing contained in this sub-section shall render any such person liable to any punishment if he proves that the offence was committed without his knowledge or that he exercised all due diligence to prevent the commission of such offence. (2) Notwithstanding anything contained in sub-section (1) where an offence under this section has been committed by a company and it is proved that the offence has been committed with the consent or connivance of, or that the commission of the offence is attributable to any neglect on the part of any Director, Manager, Secretary or other officer of the Company, such Director, Manager, Secretary or other officer shall also be deemed to be guilty of that offence and shall be liable to be proceeded against and punished accordingly. Explanation -For the purposes of this section - (a) "Company" means anybody corporate and includes a firm or other association of individuals; and (b) "Director" in relation to a firm, means a partner in the firm"; (13) in Section 86, in the proviso, in item (i), for the words "ten thousand rupees", the words "five thousand rupees", shall be substituted. (14) in Section 87, in sub-section (2), in the proviso, in item (i), for the words "ten thousand rupees", the words "five thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
