Madras Commercial Crops Markets (Karnataka Amendment and Validation of Levy of Cess) Act, 1958_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Madras Commercial Crops Markets Act, 1933 (Madras Act XX of 1933) as in force in the Madras Area. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows: An Act to amend the Madras Commercial Crops Markets Act, 1933 as in force in the Madras Area.
Madras Commercial Crops Markets (Karnataka Amendment and Validation of Levy of Cess) Act, 1958_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Madras Commercial Crops Markets (Karnataka Amendment and Validation of Levy of Cess) Act, 1958.1 Received the assent of the Governor on the Thirtieth day of November, 1958
Madras Commercial Crops Markets (Karnataka Amendment and Validation of Levy of Cess) Act, 1958_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka For sub-section (1) of Section 11 of the Madras Commercial Crops Markets Act, 1933, as in force in the Madras Area (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), the following sub-section shall be substituted and shall be deemed to have been substituted with effect from the Twenty-third day of November, 1955, namely - "(1) Notwithstanding anything contained in the general sales tax law for the time being in force, the market committee shall levy a cess by way of sales tax on any commercial crop bought or sold in the notified area at the rates specified hereunder - I. ARECA-NUT 1. For every thousand raw undried areca-nuts in husk or part thereof Six pies or Three naye paise 2. For every hundred weight of cured areca-nuts (including whole dry nuts and dried nut in slice) or part thereof Two and a half annas or Fifteen naye paise II. COCONUT 1. For every thousand coconuts (with husk or dehusked) or part thereof Two annas or Twelve naye paise 2. For every candy of seven hundred pounds of copra or part thereof Eight annas or Fifty naye paise".
Madras Commercial Crops Markets (Karnataka Amendment and Validation of Levy of Cess) Act, 1958_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10 of the Madras Commercial Crops Markets (Amendment) Act, 1955, as in force in the Madras Area - (i) in sub-section (1), for the words "shall be deemed always to have been levied under the principal Act as amended by this Act as if this Act was in force at all relevant times", the words "and all amounts representing the fees payable or purporting to be payable to market committees, collected by any authority or officer as deposit or security from any person, shall be deemed always to have been validly levied or collected by the market committee concerned or such authority or officer under the principal Act, as amended by this Act, as if this Act were in force at all relevant times and all such amounts collected by any authority or officer shall be paid to the market committee concerned", shall be substituted and shall always be deemed to have been substituted; (ii) in sub-section (2), for the words "any fee so paid", the words "any fees or cess or amounts representing such fees or cess so collected or paid" shall be substituted and shall always be deemed to have been substituted.
Madras Commercial Crops Markets (Karnataka Amendment and Validation of Levy of Cess) Act, 1958_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka (1) Any amount collected - (i) by way of fee or cess by any market committee; (ii) by a customs authority or any other authority or officer from any person as deposit or security representing the fee or cess payable or purporting to be payable to any market committee under any bye-law made by such market committee before the commencement of this Act, shall, notwithstanding anything contained in any law, or any judgment, decree or order of any Court, be deemed to have been validly collected under Section 11 of the principal Act as amended by this Act as if this Act were in force on the day when the collection was made by the market committee or a customs authority or any other authority or officer aforesaid and any amount collected by a customs authority or any other authority or officer aforesaid as deposit or security shall be paid to the market committee concerned. (2) No suit or other proceeding shall lie or be continued in any Court for the refund of any fee or cess or amount representing such fee or cess so collected or paid and no Court shall enforce any decree or order directing the refund of any fee or cess or amount representing such fee or cess so collected or paid.
Madras Commercial Crops Markets (Karnataka Amendment and Validation of Levy of Cess) Act, 1958_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka The Madras Commercial Crops Markets (Karnataka Amendment and Validation of Levy of Cess) Ordinance, 1958 is hereby repealed and it is hereby declared that the provisions of Sections 6 and 24 of the Mysore General Clauses Act, 1899 (Mysore Act III of 1899) shall apply to the repeal as if that Ordinance were an enactment.
