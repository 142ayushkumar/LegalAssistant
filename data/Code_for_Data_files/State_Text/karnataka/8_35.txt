Hyderabad Abolition of Inams (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1964_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Hyderabad Abolition of Inams Act, 1955 Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Hyderabad Abolition of Inams Act, 1955 (Hyderabad Act VIII of 1955); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Fifteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Hyderabad Abolition of Inams (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1964_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Hyderabad Abolition of Inams (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1964.1 Received the assent of the President on the Thirty-first day of August, 1964
Hyderabad Abolition of Inams (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1964_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 15 of the Hyderabad Abolition of Inams Act, 1955 (Hyderabad Act VIII of 1955), to clause (ii) of sub-section (2), the following proviso shall be added, namely:- "Provided that the amount payable under the bonds issued under this clause may be paid in such number of instalments not exceeding ten as may be prescribed."
