Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation Act, 1958. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act 16 of 1958), for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-ninth year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 19981 . (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-seventh day of May, 1998 (First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, on the Twenty-eighth day of May, 1998)
Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 48 of the Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act 16 of 1958), for the word applicant the word appellant shall be substituted.
