Hyderabad Karnataka Area Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Hyderabad Karnataka Area Development Board Act, 1991 Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Hyderabad Karnataka Area Development Board Act, 1991 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1991) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the forty-ninth year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Hyderabad Karnataka Area Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Hyderabad Karnataka Area Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1998.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Twentieth day of August, 1998. Received the assent of the Governor on Thirtieth day of November, 1998
Hyderabad Karnataka Area Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In clause (e) of Section 2 of the Hyderabad Karnataka Area Development Board Act, 1991 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1991) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for the words "and Raichur", the words "Raichur and Koppal and Harapanahalli Taluk of Davanagere revenue District" shall be substituted.
Hyderabad Karnataka Area Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (3) of Section 3 of the principal Act, in clause (b), after the word "Gulbarga", the words "Koppal, Davanagere" shall be substituted.
Hyderabad Karnataka Area Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10 of the principal Act, in clause (c), after the word "Gulbarga", the words "Koppal, Davanagere" shall be inserted.
Hyderabad Karnataka Area Development Board (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Hyderabad Karnataka Area Development Board (Amendment) Ordinance, 1998 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 5 of 1998) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
