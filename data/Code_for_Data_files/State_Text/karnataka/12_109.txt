Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1959_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1956. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Tenth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1959_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1959.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of April, 1959. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Fifteenth day of August, 1959
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1959_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 8 of the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1956 - (1) in sub-section (2), for the words "While touring on official business" the words "while touring inside India" shall be substituted; and (2) after sub-section (2), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(3) While touring outside India, the Chairman or the Speaker shall be entitled - (a) for journeys by air, train or sea, to single fare of the class by which he travels; (b) to daily allowance at rupees fifty per day for the days of journey and for the days of halt at any place: Provided that the State Government may, in special circumstances, authorise by order payment of daily allowance at such rate not exceeding rupees seventy-five per day as may be specified in the order."
