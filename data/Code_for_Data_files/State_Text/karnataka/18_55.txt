Karnataka Sales Tax and Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 and the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957) and the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act 30 of 1958); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Nineteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Sales Tax and Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Sales Tax and Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1969.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the President on the Seventh day of November, 1969
Karnataka Sales Tax and Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958, after Section 12, the following section shall be inserted, namely - "12-A. Summary disposal of certain cases -(1) A Court taking cognizance of an offence under sub-clause (ii) of clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 12 shall state upon the summons to be served on the accused person that he may, by a specified date prior to the hearing of the charge, plead guilty to the charge by registered letter and remit to the Court such sum not exceeding two hundred rupees as the Court may specify. (2) Where an accused person pleads guilty and remits the sum specified under sub-section (1), the Court may proceed to hear and dispose of the case in the absence of the accused, whether or not the prosecutor is also absent in like manner as if both parties had appeared and the accused had pleaded guilty."
