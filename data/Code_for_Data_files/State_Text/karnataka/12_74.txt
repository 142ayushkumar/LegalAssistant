Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964) for the purpose hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the fifty-fifth year or the Republic of India, as follows:-
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2004. 1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Published in the Karnataka Gazette Part IV-A Extra Ordinary No. 368 dated 19-3-2004.
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In clause (iii) of sub-section (1) of section 94B of the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964), for the words "within three years" the words "within five years" shall be and shall be deemed to have been substituted. By order and in the name of the Governor of Karnataka, M.R. Hegde Secretary to Government, Department of Parliamentary Affairs and Legislation.
