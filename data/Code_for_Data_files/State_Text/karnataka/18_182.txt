Karnataka State Commission for Backward Classes (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka State Commission for Backward Classes Act, 1995. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka State Commission for Backward Classes Act, 1995 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1995) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka State Commission for Backward Classes (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka State Commission for Backward Classes (Amendment) Act, 1997.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-seventh day of September, 1997 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka State Commission for Backward Classes (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Karnataka State Commission for Backward Classes Act, 1995 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1995) (hereinafter referred to as the "principal Act"), in sub-section (2) - (i) in clause (a), after the words "High Court", the words "or who is eligible to be appointed as a Judge of the High Court or a Social Scientist" shall be inserted; (ii) clause (b) shall be omitted; (iii) for clause (c), the following shall be substituted, namely - "(c) Five persons who have special knowledge in matters relating to backward classes of whom one shall be a social scientist".
Karnataka State Commission for Backward Classes (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), the words "or until he attains the age of sixty-seven years whichever is earlier" shall be omitted.
