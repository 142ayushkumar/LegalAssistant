Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act No. 25 of 1957) for purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1977. 1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of April, 1977. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-second day of July, 1977 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka For sub-section (1), excluding the proviso thereto, of Section 6-B of the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act No. 25 of 1957), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely - "(1) There shall be levied and collected from every dealer liable to pay tax under this Act, an additional tax at the rates shown below, namely - (a) In the case of a dealer liable to pay tax under Section 5 or under Section 6 -  (i) whose total turnover does not exceed five lakhs of rupees in a year; NIL (ii) whose total turnover exceeds five lakhs of rupee but does not exceed ten lakhs of rupees in a year; Seven and half per cent of the sales tax or purchase tax or both payable by such dealer. (iii) whose total turnover exceeds ten lakhs of rupees in a year; Ten per cent of the sales tax or purchase tax or both payable by such dealer. (b) In the case of a dealer liable to pay tax under Section 25-B; Ten per cent of the tax payable by such dealer."
