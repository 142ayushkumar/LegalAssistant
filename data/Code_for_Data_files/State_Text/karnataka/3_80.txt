Karnataka Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Contingency Fund Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Contingency Fund Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 11 of 1957) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 1976.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the fifteenth day of September, 1976. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-seventh day of November, 1976
Karnataka Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Contingency Fund Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 11 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) for the words "ten crores of rupees", the words "twenty crores of rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Contingency Fund (Temporary Amendment) Ordinance, 1976 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 23 of 1976) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
