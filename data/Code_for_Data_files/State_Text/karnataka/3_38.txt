Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Co-operative Societies Act, 1959. Whereas, it is expedient-further to amend the Karnataka Co-operative Societies Act, 1959 (Karnataka Act 11 of 1959) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-first year of the Republic of India as follows:
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1980.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Eleventh day of August, 1977. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twelfth day of March, 1980
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Co-operative Societies Act, 1959 (Karnataka Act 11 of 1959), (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), after clause (a), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(a-1) "Chief Auditor" means a person appointed to perform the functions of the Chief Auditor of co-operative societies under this Act and includes the Additional Chief Auditor of co-operative societies, a Joint Chief Auditor of co-operative societies, a Deputy Chief Auditor of co-operative societies and an Assistant Chief Auditor of co-operative societies, appointed to assist the Chief Auditor when exercising all or any of the powers of the Chief Auditor".
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2-A of the principal Act,- (1) in sub-section (4), after the words "administration of this Act", the words "except relating to the audit" shall be inserted; (2) in sub-section (6), the words "audit and" shall be omitted.
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 2-A of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely:- "2-AA. Chief Auditor, Additional Chief Auditor, Joint Chief Auditors, Deputy Chief Auditors and Assistant Chief Auditors -(1) The State Government may appoint a person to be the Chief Auditor of co-operative societies for the State. (2) The State Government may appoint an Additional Chief Auditor of Co-operative Societies and as many Joint Chief Auditors, Deputy Chief Auditors and Assistant Chief Auditors, of co-operative societies, as it thinks fit for the purpose of assisting the Chief Auditor. (3) The State Government may appoint such other officers with such designations as it deems fit to assist the Chief Auditor. (4) All officers and persons employed in the administration of this Act relating to audit shall be subject to the superintendence, direction and control of the State Government and the Chief Auditor and the officer or officers to whom each officer appointed under this section shall be subordinate shall be determined by the State Government: Provided that the power vested in the State Government and the Chief Auditor under this sub-section shall not be exercisable so as to interfere with the discretion of any authority in exercise of any quasi-judicial function whether as original or Appellate Authority. (5) The State Government may, by general or special order confer on any person appointed as Additional Chief Auditor, Joint Chief Auditor, Deputy Chief Auditor or Assistant Chief Auditor, of co-operative societies, all or any of the powers of the Chief Auditor under this Act. (6) Notwithstanding anything contained in this Act, matters relating to audit of every co-operative society shall be subject to the supervision and control of the Chief Auditor".
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 63 of the principal Act,- (1) for the word "Registrar", wherever it occurs, the words "Chief Auditor" shall be substituted; (2) for sub-section (5), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely:- "(5) The Chief Auditor shall communicate the result of the audit to the society, the Registrar and to the financing Bank or Credit Agency and, if the society is affiliated to any other society, to such society".
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 65-A of the principal Act,- (1) in the heading for the word "Audit", the word "inquiry" shall be substituted; (2) for the words "the defects noticed in any audit inquiry", the words "the defects noticed in any inquiry" shall be substituted; (3) for the words "such audit or inquiry report", the words "such inquiry or inspection report" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 104 of the principal Act,- (1) in sub-section (1), after the word "Registrar", the words "or the Chief Auditor" shall be inserted; (2) after sub-section (2), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely:- "(3) Notwithstanding anything contained in the preceding sub-sections, the audit fee due from a co-operative society may also be recovered in such manner as may be prescribed".
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 111 of the principal Act, for sub-section (2), the following sub-sections shall be substituted, namely:- "(2) No prosecution shall be instituted under this Act without the previous sanction of,- (a) the Chief Auditor in respect of matters arising out of audit; (b) the Registrar in respect of other matters. (3) The sanction under sub-section (2) shall not be given,- (i) without giving to the person concerned an opportunity to represent his case; (ii) if the Chief Auditor or the Registrar, as the case may be is satisfied that the person concerned has acted in good faith".
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 127 of the principal Act, after the words "acting on his authority" the words "or the Chief Auditor or any other person subordinate to him acting on his authority" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 10--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Ordinance, 1979 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 20 of 1979), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
