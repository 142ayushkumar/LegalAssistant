Karnataka Shops and Commercial Establishments (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka (First published in the Kamataka Gazette, Extraordinary, on the Fourth dny of September, 1982) (Received the assent of the Governor on the Second day of September, 1982) An Act further to amend the Karnataka Shops and Commercial Establishments Act, 1961. WHEREAS, it is expedient further to amend the Kamataka Shops and Commercial Establishments Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 8 of 1962), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; BE it enacted by the Kamataka State Legislature in the Thirty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows.-
Karnataka Shops and Commercial Establishments (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Kamataka Shops and Commercial Establishments (Amendment) Act, 1982. (2) It shall come into force on such date as may be notified in the Official Gazette.
Karnataka Shops and Commercial Establishments (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Kamataka Shops and Commercial Establishment Act, 1961 (Kamataka Act 8 of 1962) (hereinafter referred to as the "principal Act"), after clause (b), the following clause shall be inserted, namely "(ba) "Banking company" means.- (i) the Reserve Bank of India; (ii) the Banking Company as denned under the Banking Regulations Act, 1949; (iii) the State Bank of India constituted under the State Bank of India Act, 1955; (iv) a Subsidiary Bank as defined in the State Bank of India (Subsidiary Bank) Act, 1959; (v) a corresponding new Bank constituted under the Banking Companies (Acquisition and Transfer of Undertakings) Act, 1970; (vi) a corresponding new bank constituted under the Banking Companies (Acquisition and Transfer of Undertakings) Act, 1980".
Karnataka Shops and Commercial Establishments (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), in clause (f), for the words "the Reserve Bank of India", the words "a banking company" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Shops and Commercial Establishments (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4 of the principal Act, after sub-section (3), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely:- "(4) A registration certificate issued under sub-section (2), shall be valid for one year and shall be renewed from year to year on payment of such fees and in such manner as may be prescribed. (5) The registration of every establishment registered before the commencement of the Kamataka Shops and Commercial Establishments (Amendment) Act, 1982 shall, on such commencement, expire and the employer of every such establishment shall renew his licence within ninety days of such commencement in accordance with the provisions of sub-section (4)".
