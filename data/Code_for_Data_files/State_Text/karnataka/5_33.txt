Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) Act, 1974. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 36 of 1974) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-sixth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1995.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force, with effect from Seventeenth day of January, 1995. Received the assent of the Governor on the Thirtieth day of April, 1995
Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 7 of the Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 36 of 1974) (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act), the following section shall be and shall be deemed always to have been inserted, namely - "7-A. Application to the Tribunal -Any person disputing - (i) the amount determinable and payable under Section 5; or (ii) as to which property, rights, liabilities and obligations of the companies vest in the Government under Section 6; or (iii) the amount liable to be deducted under Section 7; may within three months from the date of commencement of the Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1995, apply to the Tribunal: Provided that the tribunal may entertain such application after the period specified above, if it is satisfied that the person making the application had sufficient cause for not making the application within that period".
Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 14 of the Principal Act, in sub-section (2), for the words and figures "under Sections 10 and 13" the words, figures and letter "under Sections 5, 6, 7, 7-A, 10 and 13" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Ordinance, 1995 (Karnataka Ordinance 2 of 1995) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the Principal Act, as amended by the said ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the Principal Act as amended by this Act.
