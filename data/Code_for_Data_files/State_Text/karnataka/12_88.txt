Karnataka Land Revenue (Surcharge) (Amendment) Act, 1962_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue (Surcharge) Act, 1961. Whereas it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue (Surcharge) Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 13 of 1961). Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Land Revenue (Surcharge) (Amendment) Act, 1962_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Revenue (Surcharge) (Amendment) Act, 1962.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the first day of April, 1962. Repealed by the Karnataka Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Seventh day of January, 1963.
Karnataka Land Revenue (Surcharge) (Amendment) Act, 1962_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In clause (4) of Section 2 of the Karnataka Land Revenue (Surcharge) Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 13 of 1961) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), the words "and additional surcharge" shall be omitted.
Karnataka Land Revenue (Surcharge) (Amendment) Act, 1962_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka For sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the principal Act, the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely - "(1) Notwithstanding anything contained in any contract, grant or other instrument or in the Karnataka Land Revenue Code, 1888 (Karnataka Act IV of 1888), or any other corresponding law or orders having the force of law in any area of the State, every landholder liable to pay land revenue to the Government in respect of any land held by him shall pay for the revenue years 1962-63 and 1963-64 surcharge on the land revenue payable by him for each revenue year in respect of the class of land specified in column (1) of the Table below at the rate specified in the corresponding entry of column (2) of the said Table - TABLE Class of land Rate of Surcharge (1) (2) Wet land One hundred percent of the land revenue payable in respect of the land. Garden land One hundred percent of the land revenue payable in respect of the land. Dry land Seventy-five percent of the land revenue payable in respect of the land: Provided that where any landholder satisfies the Tahsildar of the Taluk in which any dry land held by such landholder is situated that the dry land held by him in the whole State does not exceed five acres, no surcharge shall be payable on the land revenue payable in respect of such dry land. Explanation - Land Revenue remitted shall not be deemed to be land revenue for the purposes of this sub-section".
Karnataka Land Revenue (Surcharge) (Amendment) Act, 1962_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka Sections 8, 9 and 10 of the principal Act shall be omitted.
Karnataka Land Revenue (Surcharge) (Amendment) Act, 1962_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka For the removal of doubts it is hereby declared - (1) that the surcharge and the additional surcharge payable under the principal Act as originally enacted shall be payable only on the land revenue payable for the revenue year 1961-62; (2) that the provisions of Section 6 of the Karnataka General Clauses Act, 1899 (Karnataka Act 11 of 1899), shall be applicable in respect of the repeal of the provisions of the principal Act as originally enacted by this Act; and that the provisions of the principal Act as originally enacted and the rules and orders made and notifications issued thereunder shall continue to be applicable for purposes of assessment, levy and collection of the said surcharge and additional surcharge.
