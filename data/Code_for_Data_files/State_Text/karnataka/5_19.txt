Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) Act, 1959. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) Act, 1959 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1959) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) (Amendment) Act, 1998.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the twenty-fourth day of October, 1997. Received the assent of the Governor on the First day of April, 1998
Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4 of the Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) Act, 1959 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1959), in sub-section (5), in clause (ii), for the words "five kilowatts", the words "ten kilowatts" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) (Amendment) Ordinance, 1997 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 4 of 1997) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
