Karnataka Sheep and Sheep Products Development (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Sheep and Sheep Products Development Act, 1973 . Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Sheep and Sheep Products Development Act, 1973 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1974) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-first year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Sheep and Sheep Products Development (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Sheep and Sheep Products Development (Amendment) Act, 19801 2 . (2) It shall come into force at once. First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, on the Twentieth day of June, 1980, Received the assent of the Governor on the Eighteenth day of June, 1980 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Sheep and Sheep Products Development (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4 of the Karnataka Sheep and Sheep Products Development Act, 1973 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1974) in sub-section (4) for the words The term of office of the Chairman the words Subject to the pleasure of the State Government, the term of office of the Chairman shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
