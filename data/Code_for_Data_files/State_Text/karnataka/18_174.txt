Karnataka State Civil Services (Regulation of Promotion, Pay and Pension) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka State Civil Services (Regulation of Promotion, Pay and Pension) Act, 1973. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka State Civil Services (Regulation of Promotion, Pay and Pension) Act, 1973 (Karnataka Act 11 of 1974), for the purposes hereinafter appearing: Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in Thirty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka State Civil Services (Regulation of Promotion, Pay and Pension) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka State Civil Services (Regulation of Promotion, Pay and Pension) (Amendment) Act, 1981.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-second day of July, 1982
Karnataka State Civil Services (Regulation of Promotion, Pay and Pension) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4 of the Karnataka State Civil Services (Regulation of Promotion, Pay and Pension) Act, 1973 (Karnataka Act 11 of 1974) in sub-section (3), after the words, "for the period prior to the date of the actual promotion", the following shall be and shall be deemed always to have been inserted, namely:- "Where, on such review he is found eligible for promotion to a higher class or grade of service from a date subsequent to the date of his actual promotion to such class or grade of service, his pay on the date of eligibility shall be refixed as if he had been promoted on such date, but he shall not be liable to refund the excess pay and allowances drawn by him upto the date of issue of the order fixing the date of eligibility."
Karnataka State Civil Services (Regulation of Promotion, Pay and Pension) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In the Karnataka Service Examinations Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 40 of 1976) in Section 1, for sub-section (2), the following sub-section shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted, namely:- "(2) This section and all the other sections except Section 8 shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of November, 1956. Section 8 shall be deemed to have come into force on the eleventh day of April, 1974."
