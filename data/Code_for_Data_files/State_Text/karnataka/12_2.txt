Karnataka Labour Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Labour Welfare Fund Act, 1965. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Labour Welfare Fund Act, 1965 (Karnataka Act 15 of 1965) for the purpose hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-fourth year of the Republic of India of follows -
Karnataka Labour Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Labour Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1993.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from First day of December, 1993. Received the assent of the Governor on the 10th day of March, 1994
Karnataka Labour Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 7-A of the Karnataka Labour Welfare Fund Act, 1965 (Karnataka Act 15 of 1965). (i) in sub-section (2) for the words "one rupee, two rupees and one rupee" the words "three rupees, six rupees and three rupees" shall respectively be substituted; (ii) in sub-section (3) for the words "money order or in cash" the words "or by crossed demand draft" shall be substituted.
