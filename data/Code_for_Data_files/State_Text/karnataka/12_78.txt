Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2009_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964), for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Fifty-ninth year of Republic of India, as follows.-
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2009_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2009. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2009_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In clause (iii) of sub-section (1) of Section 94-B of the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964), for the words "within eight years", the words "within eleven years" shall be and shall always be deemed to have been substituted.
