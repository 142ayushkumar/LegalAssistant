Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Second Amendment) Act, 1995_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1957) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-sixth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Second Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Second Amendment) Act, 1995.1 (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may by notification appoint. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-fifth day of September, 1995
Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Second Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10 of the Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) Act, 1957. (1) after sub-section (1), the following proviso shall be inserted, namely - "Provided that if in any area or areas water is supplied or made available from any irrigation work by or on behalf of the Krishna Bhagya Jala Nigam Limited (hereinafter called as Nigam the water rates shall be levied and collected by the said Nigam in such area or areas." (2) in sub-section (1-A) - (a) for the words "Government may supply", the words "the Government or Nigam, as the case may be, may supply" shall be substituted; (b) for the words "Government shall levy", the words "Government or the Nigam, as the case may be, shall levy" shall be substituted.
