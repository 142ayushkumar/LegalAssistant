Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1972_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners Act, 1961. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1961; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1972_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1972.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Eleventh day of October, 1972. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-eighth day of December, 1972
Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1972_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1961) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), the words "at such time and at such place and" shall be omitted.
Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1972_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Clause (a) of sub-section (2) of Section 35 of the principal Act, the words "the time and place at which and" shall be omitted.
Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1972_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka Homoeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Ordinance, 1972 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 3 of 1972) is hereby repealed: Provided that anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
