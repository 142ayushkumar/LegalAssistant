Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964). Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Seventeenth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1966.1 2 [(2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.] Received the assent of the Governor on the Eleventh day of February, 1966 This Act has come into force on 1st March, 1966, vide S.O. 3968, dated 26th February 1966 and subsequently repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 202 of the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), after sub-section (2), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(2-A) Notwithstanding anything contained in sub-section (1) or sub-section (2) or in any enactment or law repealed by sub-section (1) or any other law, in respect of any order made or proceedings disposed of by any officer subordinate to the Divisional Commissioner under any enactment or law, repealed by sub-section (1) or any rule or order made under such enactment or law, no appeal shall lie to the Divisional Commissioner, but an appeal shall lie to the Tribunal as if the Tribunal were the Appellate Authority under such enactment, law, rule or order, and such appeal shall be disposed of by the Tribunal in accordance with the provisions of such enactment, law rule or order."
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka All the appeals and proceeding connected therewith pending on the date of commencement of this Act, before the Divisional Commissioner from any order made or proceedings disposed of by any officer subordinate to such Divisional Commissioner under any enactment or law repealed by sub-section (1) of Section 202 of the principal Act or any rule or order made thereunder, shall on the said date stand transferred to the Tribunal and shall be disposed of by the Tribunal as if the said appeals were appeals preferred to the Tribunal under sub-section (2-A) of Section 202 of the principal Act. Explanation -In this section, the expressions "Divisional Commissioner" and "Tribunal" shall have the same meanings as in the principal Act.
