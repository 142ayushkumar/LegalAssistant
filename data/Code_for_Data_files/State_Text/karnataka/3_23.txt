Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Civil Courts Act, 1964. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Civil Courts Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 21 of 1964) for the purpsoes hereinfter appearing; Be, it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1996.1 . (2) It shall come into force at once. (Received the assent of the Governor on the Twentieth day of September, 1996) (First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, on the Twenty-first day of September, 1996
Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Karnataka Civil Courts Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 21 of 1964) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in the Sections, 2, 3, 6, 7, 8, 9, 13, 16, 17, 19, 20, 21, 22, 23, 23-A, 24, 25, 26, 27 and 28 - (i) for the words Civil Judge, the words Civil Judge (Senior Division) and for the words Civil Judges Court andCourt of a Civil Judge, the words Court of Civil Judge (Senior Division) shall be substituted. (ii) for the words Munsiff and Munsiffs Court, the words Civil Judge (Junior Division) and Court of a Civil Judge (Junior Division) shall respectively be substituted. And such other consequential amendments as the rules of grammar may require shall also be made.
Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 30, of the principal Act, the following sections shall be inserted, namely - 30-A. Substitution of the expression Civil Judge (Senior Division), Civil Judge (Junior Division) Court of Civil Judge (Senior Division) Court of Civil Judge (Junior Division) for the expressions in certain laws in force - Unless the context otherwise requires, in any law in force or rule, notification or order made thereunder, for the expressions in column (1) below, the expressions in the corresponding entry in column (2) shall be substituted, namely - Column Column (1) (2) Court of Civil Judge or Civil Judges Court Court of Civil Judge (Senior Division) Civil Judge Civil Judge (Senior Division) Munsiff Civil Judge (Junior Division) Munsiffs Court Court of Civil Judge (Junior Division) 30-B. Construction of references to Civil Judge, Court of the Civil Judge, Munsiff and Munsiffs Court in any judgment and decree, etc - Unless the context otherwise requires, any reference made to Civil Judge, Court of the Civil Judge, Munsiff or Munsiffs Court in any judgment, decree, order or other instrument, prior to the date of commencement of the Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1996, shall respectively be construed as reference to Civil Judge (Senior Division), Court of the Civil Judge (Senior Division), Civil Judge (Junior Division) and Court of the Civil Judge (Junior Division).
