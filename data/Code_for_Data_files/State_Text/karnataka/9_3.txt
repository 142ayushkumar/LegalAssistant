Karnataka Improvement Boards (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Improvement Boards Act, 1976. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Improvement Boards Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 11 of 1976) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Improvement Boards (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Improvement Boards (Amendment) Act, 1981.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixth day of April, 1981
Karnataka Improvement Boards (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 2 of the Karnataka Improvement Boards Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 11 of 1976) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) at the end of clause (o), the following shall be inserted, namely:- "and includes such other area adjacent to the limits of the Local Authority, as the Government may, from time to time, by notification, specify".
Karnataka Improvement Boards (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the principal Act, after sub-section (2) the following sub-section shall be inserted namely:- "(3) The jurisdiction of the Board constituted in respect of any urban area shall stand extended to such other areas as may be notified under clause (o) of sub-section (1) of Section 2 in respect of such urban area, with effect from the date of such notification".
