Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1984.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of April, 1984. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Thirty-first day of March, 1984.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (1) in the proviso to sub-section (1), the words and brackets "Gur (Jaggery)" shall be omitted; (2) in sub-section (5) - (a) in clause (a), for the words "forty thousand rupees", the words "one lakh rupees" shall be substituted; (b) in clause (b), for the words "specified in the Third Schedule or the Fourth Schedule", the words "other than those specified in the Fifth Schedule" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6-C of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), after the first proviso - (1) the following proviso shall be and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from 1st day of July, 1980 and shall be and shall be deemed to have been omitted with effect from 1st day of January, 1983, namely - "Provided further that no such increase shall be made of the tax payable under Section 25-B by Khandasari Units"; (2) the following proviso shall be and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from 15th day of April, 1983 and shall be and shall be deemed to have been omitted with effect from 1st day of July, 1983, namely - "Provided further that, no such increase shall be made of the tax payable under Section 25-B by sugar factories employing Vacuum-Pan-Process".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 6-C of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely - "6-D. Levy of cess - (1) The tax payable under Sections 5,6 and 25-B shall be increased by a cess calculated at the rate of ten per cent of such tax: Provided that, if in the case of the declared goods specified in the Fourth Schedule, the tax payable under Sections 5 and 6 together with the surcharge payable under Section 6-C and the cess payable under this section exceeds four per cent of the sale or purchase price thereof, the rate of cess in respect of such goods shall be reduced to such an extent that the tax, surcharge and cess together shall not exceed four per cent of the sale or purchase price of such goods. (2) The provisions of this Act and the rules made thereunder including those relating to refund or exemption from tax shall, so far as may be, apply in relation to the levy, assessment and collection of the cess payable under sub-section (1) as they apply in relation to the levy, assessment and collection of sales tax or purchase tax under this Act."

Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 16--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 43 of the principal Act, after sub-section (8), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(9) Notwithstanding anything contained in this Act, the assessment of a dealer whose total turnover for the year which ends on a date after the First day of April, 1984 is more than forty thousand rupees but is less than one lakh rupees, shall be regulated as follows, namely - (a) in the case of a dealer permitted to pay any amount by way of composition under Section 17, such dealer shall pay the amount due in monthly instalments for the month immediately prior to the First day of April, 1984 and shall not be liable to pay monthly instalments for the remaining months thereafter; and (b) in case of any other dealer, the turnover during the period from the commencement of the year upto the First day of April, 1984 shall be liable to tax and his turnover during the remaining period from the First day of April, 1984 shall not be liable to tax."
