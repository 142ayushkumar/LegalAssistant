Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation Act, 1958. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act No. 16 of 1958). Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twentieth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 19691 . Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixteenth day of May, 1969 (First published in the Karnataka Gazette on the Twenty-second day of May, 1969)
Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 48 of the Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act 16 of 1958) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (a) In the heading, for the word order, the words decision, award or order shall be substituted; (b) For the words an order, the words a decision or an award or order shall be substituted.
Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 49 of the principal Act, for the words the fee payable, the words and figures save as provided in Section 48, the fee payable shall be substituted.
Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Article 3 of Schedule II to the principal Act, for the words from an order, the words from a decision or an award or order shall be substituted.
