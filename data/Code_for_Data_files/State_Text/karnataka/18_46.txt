Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1987_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1987) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1987.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-fourth day of October, 1987
Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (1) in sub-section (3), after clause (b), the following clause shall be and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from the Twenty-first day of April, 1987, namely - "(c) in respect of silk fabrics, at the rate of four per cent, at the point of last sale in the State by the dealer liable to tax under the Act on his taxable turnover of sales relating to such goods in each year: Provided that the sale by a handloom or a powerloom weaver (other than one whose establishment falls within the definition of a "Factory" under the Factories Act, 1948 (Central Act 63 of 1948) of silk fabrics manufactured by him in Karnataka shall not be deemed to be a sale for purposes of this Act. Explanation - For the purpose of this clause - (1) "Silk fabrics" means all varieties of fabrics manufactured either wholly or partly from silk but does not include cotton fabrics, woollen fabrics and man-made fabrics as defined in the Central Excise and Salt Act, 1944. (2) The term "Weaver" means an individual carrying on business as owner but does not include a corporate entity or society (including a co-operative society), firm, association of persons or a club. (3) The expression "Manufactured by him" means manufacture - (i) by one's own labour, or (ii) by the labour of one's own family, or (iii) by servants on wages payable in cash or by hired labour under one's personal supervision or the personal supervision of any member of one's family; (2) in sub-section (4), after the second proviso, the following proviso shall be and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from the First day of April, 1987, namely: "Provided also that where tax has been paid under this sub-section on the purchase of paddy and such paddy is either subsequently sold to or is hulled and the resultant rice is sold to a procurement agent appointed by the Government of Karnataka or to any sub-agent of such procurement agent in pursuance of the Karnataka Rice Procurement (Levy) Order, 1984 or any other Foodgrains Procurement (Levy) Order of the Government of Karnataka for the time being in force, the tax paid under this Act on the purchase of such paddy shall be reimbursed to the person making such sale to such procurement agent or his sub-agent, as the case may be, in such manner and subject to such conditions as may be prescribed".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6-B of the principal Act - (1) in the first proviso, after clause (x), the following clause shall be and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from the first day of April, 1986, namely - "(xi) such amounts towards labour charges and other like charges not involving any transfer of property in goods, actually incurred in connection with the execution of works contract, or calculated at the rates prescribed"; (2) after the second proviso, the following proviso shall be and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from the 1st day of April, 1986 and shall be omitted with effect from the 1st day of October, 1987, namely: "Provided also that no tax under this section shall be payable by a dealer on his turnover relating to sale of cars with less than 900 c.c. engine capacity".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In the Third Schedule to the principal Act, the entries relating to Serial Number 15 shall be and shall be deemed to have been omitted with effect from the Twenty-first day of April, 1987.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In the Fifth Schedule to the principal Act, after Explanation II, the following explanation shall be and shall be deemed always to have been inserted, namely - "Explanation III - In Item 7, 'Fresh Milk' shall not include milk powder, condensed milk and milk with additives such as flavours, colours, preservatives, cereals, spices and dry fruits".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka Notwithstanding anything in any judgment, decree or order of any Court, any tax levied under this Act on the sale of milk powder, condensed milk and milk with additives such as flavours, colours, preservatives, cereals, spices and dry fruits before the commencement of this Act shall be and shall be deemed always to have been validly levied or collected in accordance with law.
