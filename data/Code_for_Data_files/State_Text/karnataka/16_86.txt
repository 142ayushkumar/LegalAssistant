Karnataka Public Libraries (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Public Libraries Act, 1965. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Public Libraries Act, 1965 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1965), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-fourth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Public Libraries (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Public Libraries (Amendment) Act, 19841 . (2) It shall come into force at once. First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, dated on the Twenty-second day of May, 1984, Received the assent of the Governor on the Twelfth day of May, 1984
Karnataka Public Libraries (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Karnataka Public Libraries Act, 1965 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1965), for the expression "State Librarian" wherever it occurs, the expression "Director of Public Libraries" shall be substituted.
