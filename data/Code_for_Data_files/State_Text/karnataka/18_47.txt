Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Fifty-first year of the Republic of India as follows:
Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2000.1 (2) Section 2 shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the First day of April, 2000, sub-sections (1) and (2) of Section 3 and sub-section (2) of Section 4 shall be deemed to have come into force on the Fourteenth day of August, 2000; sub-section (3) of Section 3 and sub-section (1) of Section 4 shall come into force on such date as the State Government may by notification appoint and the remaining provisions shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Third day of December, 2000
Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5-A of the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for clause (4) of Explanation I, the following shall be substituted, namely - "(4) The expression "consumables" does not include petroleum products falling under Serial Number 11-A of Part "F", Serial Number 12 of Part "M" and Serial Number 5 of Part "P" of the Second Schedule".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In the Second Schedule to the principal Act - (1) in the entries relating to Serial Number 4 of Part "M", in column (2), after the words "Marble slabs", the punctuation mark and the word "tiles" shall be inserted; (2) in the entries relating to Serial Number 2 of Part "O", in column (2), for the words "and Bhang", the punctuation mark and the words "Bhang and other Narcotics" shall be substituted; (3) after Serial Number 22 of Part "S", the following shall be inserted, namely - "22-A. Silk yam twisted or thrown silk yam, spun silk yam and noil silk yam. Four per cent"; (4) after Serial Number 6 of Part "T", the following shall be inserted, namely - "6-A. Teleprinters and their parts Twelve per cent"
Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In the Fifth Schedule to the principal Act - (1) in the entries relating to Serial Number 42-B, the words "and silk yam twisted or thrown silk yam, spun silk yam and noil silk yam" shall be omitted; (2) after the entries relating to Serial Number 47, the following entries shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from the First day of April, 1999 and shall be deemed to have been omitted with effect from the Seventh day of October, 1999, namely - "47-A. Silk Fabrics on which no tax is collected under this Act by a dealer on its sales".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Ordinance, 2000 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 5 of 2000), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
