Karnataka Local Authorities (Prohibition of Defection) (Second Amendment) Act, 1995_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Local Authorities (Prohibition of Defection) Act, 1987. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Local Authorities (Prohibition of Defection) Act, 1987 (Karnataka Act 20 of 1987) for the purpose hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-sixth year of the Republic of India, as follows, -
Karnataka Local Authorities (Prohibition of Defection) (Second Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Local Authorities (Prohibition of Defection) (Second Amendment) Act, 1995.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the fifth day of June, 1995. Received the assent of Governor on eighteenth day of September, 1995 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Local Authorities (Prohibition of Defection) (Second Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4 of the Karnataka Local Authorities (Prohibition of Defection) Act, 1987 (Karnataka Act 20 of 1987) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), - (1) in sub-section (2), - (a) for clause (i), the following shall be substituted, namely - "(i) in the case of Zilla Panchayat to the State Election Commissioner." (b) for clause (iv), the following shall be substituted namely - "(iv) in the case of a Taluk Panchayat, to the State Election Commissioner." (c) the proviso shall be omitted; (2) in the explanation, after clause (2) the following shall be inserted, namely - "(3) State Election Commissioner means the State Election Commissioner appointed under Section 308 of the Karnataka Panchayat Raj Act, 1993."
Karnataka Local Authorities (Prohibition of Defection) (Second Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Local Authorities (Prohibition of Defection) Amendment) Ordinance, 1995 (Karnataka Ordinance 4 of 1995) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the Principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
