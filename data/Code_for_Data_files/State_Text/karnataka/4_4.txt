Karnataka Debt Relief (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Debt Relief Act, 1980 Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Debt Relief Act, 1980 (Karnataka Act 29 of 1980) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty- second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Debt Relief (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This act may be called the Karnataka Debt Relief (Amendment) Act, 1981.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the third day of January, 1981. Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixth day of April, 1981
Karnataka Debt Relief (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4 of the Karnataka Debt Relief Act, 1980 (Karnataka Act 29 of 1980) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act)- (1) in sub-section (1), for the words "within one year from the date of commencement of this Act", the words, figures and letters "on or before the 31st December, 1981" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted; (2) in sub-section (2), for the words "may also make an application", the words, figures and letters "may, on or before the 31st December, 1981, make an application" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
Karnataka Debt Relief (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the principal Act, for the words, "may make an application", the words, figures and letters, "may on or before the 31st December, 1981, make an application", shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
Karnataka Debt Relief (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 13 of the principal Act,- (1) in sub-section (1) - (a) for the words, "within one year from the date of the commencement of this Act", the words, figures and letters, "on or before the 31st December, 1981"; and (b) for the words, "through whom he inherited it was a transfer in the mortgage", the words, "through whom he inherited the immovable property was a transfer in the nature of a mortgage"; and (c) for the words "showed it", the words "showed the transfer", shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted; (2) for sub-section (2), the following sub-section shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted, namely:- "(2) When the Assistant Commissioner makes any such declaration, if the transfer was made prior to the date of the commencement of this Act, the debt covered under the transfer shall deemed to be wholly discharged and the provisions of sub-section (2) of Section 5, shall mutatis mutandis apply for recording the redemption of the mortgage and delivering possession of the property".
Karnataka Debt Relief (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Debt Relief (Amendment) Ordinance, 1981 (Karnataka Ordinance 1 of 1981) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
