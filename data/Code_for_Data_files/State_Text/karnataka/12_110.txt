Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1960_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1956. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Eleventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1960_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1960.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of January, 1960. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-third day of April, 1960.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1960_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10 of the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1957), (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for the words "three hundred and fifty rupees," the words "six hundred rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1960_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 11 of the principal Act - (1) after the words "one hundred and fifty rupees per mensem", the words "and a special allowance of one hundred rupees per mensem" shall be inserted; (2) in the first and second provisos, after the word salary wherever it occurs, the words and special allowance shall be inserted; (3) after the second proviso, the following further proviso shall be added, namely - "Provided also that where a person who is already a member of the Legislative Assembly or of the Legislative Council, is elected or nominated as a .member of the Legislative Council or of the Legislative Assembly, he shall be paid salary and special allowance as a member of the Legislative Council or of the Legislative Assembly, as the case may be, only from the date on which he ceases to be a member of the Legislative Assembly or of the Legislative Council, as the case may be". (4) in the heading for the word salaries, the words salary and special allowance shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1960_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12 of the principal Act, for the words "one anna", "six annas" and "annas eight", the words "six naye paise", "thirty seven naye paise" and "fifty naye paise" shall, respectively, be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1960_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka For sub-section (2) of Section 15 of the principal Act, the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely - "(2) Every rule made under this section shall be laid as soon as may be after it is made, before each House of the State Legislature while it is in session for a total period of thirty days which may be comprised in one session or in two successive sessions, and if before the expiry of the session in which it is sp laid or the session immediately following both Houses agree in making any modification in the rule or both Houses agree that the rule should not be made, the rule shall thereafter have effect only in such modified form or be of no effect, as the case may be; so however that any such modification or annulment shall be without prejudice to the validity of anything previously done under that rule".
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1960_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka Notwithstanding anything contained in sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the Karnataka Legislature (Prohibition of Simultaneous Membership) Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 3 of 1957) or any other law, in the case of members of the Legislative Council of the State of Karnataka who were chosen as members of the legislative Assembly of the State of Karnataka, and the declarations that they had been so chosen were published in the Karnataka Gazette, dated the 16th day of March, 1957, their seats in the said Council shall be deemed to have become vacant on the 20th day of March, 1957, and they shall be paid the salaries and allowances in accordance with the provisions of the principal Act until the said 20th day of March, 1957.
