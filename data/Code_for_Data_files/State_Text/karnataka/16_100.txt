Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Second Amendment) Act, 1999_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1974. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 32 of 1974), for the purposes of hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Fiftieth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Second Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Second Amendment) Act, 19991 . (2) It shall come into force at once. First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, on the Fourth day of August, 1999, Received the assent of the Governor on the Thirty-first day of July, 1999
Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Second Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 32 of 1974), in clause (e) for sub-clause (v), the following shall be substituted, namely - "(v) a Wakf, registered with the Karnataka State Board of Wakfs".
