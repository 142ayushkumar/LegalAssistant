Karnataka Land Revenue (Surcharge) (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue (Surcharge) Act, 1961. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue (Surcharge) Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 13 of 1961). Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Fourteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Land Revenue (Surcharge) (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Revenue (Surcharge) (Amendment) Act, 1963.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of April, 1962. Repealed by the Karnataka Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the First day of November, 1963.
Karnataka Land Revenue (Surcharge) (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In clause (2) of Section 2 of the Karnataka Land Revenue (Surcharge) Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 13 of 1961) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), the words "where such cess or rate does not exceed fifteen rupees per acre" shall be omitted.
Karnataka Land Revenue (Surcharge) (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the principal Act - (a) in the Table, after the entries relating to "dry land", the following entries shall be inserted, namely - "other lands... Seventy-five percent of the land revenue payable in respect of the land"; (b) after the proviso, the following provisos shall be inserted, namely - "Provided further that where water cess, water rate or irrigation cess is less than thirty rupees per acre per annum, the surcharge shall be limited to such amount as to make the aggregate amount payable as surcharge and water cess, water rate or irrigation cess, Thirty rupees per acre per annum: Provided also that no surcharge shall be payable - (i) where water cess, water rate or irrigation cess is not less than thirty rupees per acre per annum; or (ii) in respect of any land for which non-agricultural assessment is payable."
