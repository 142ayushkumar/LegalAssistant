Karnataka Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments Act, 1976. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1976) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State legislature in the Thirty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments (Amendment) Act, 1985.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of August, 1985. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixth day of September, 1985
Karnataka Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1976) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for clause (j), the following shall be and shall be deemed to have been substituted with effect from the First day of April, 1983, namely:- "(j) "Salary" or "Wage" means the basic salary or wage received by any person on regular basis whether in cash or kind but does not include any other allowance or remuneration, or any perquisites or profits."
Karnataka Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In clause (iii) of sub-section (1) of Section 14 of the principal Act, after the words "Deputy Commissioners of Profession Tax", the words "Assistant Commissioners of Profession Tax" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 18 of the principal Act, after the words "The Commissioner", the words "or the Joint Commissioner" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Tax on Professions, Trades, Callings and Employments (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In the Schedule to the principal Act,- (1) for item 1, the following shall be and shall be deemed to have been substituted with effect from the First day of April, 1983, namely:- "1. Salary or wage earners whose salary or wage or both, as the case may be, for a month is- (a) not less than Rs. 1,200 but less than Rs. 1,500 Rs. 15 per month. (b) Rs. 1,500 or more Rs. 20 per month". (2) for item 9, the following shall be substituted, namely:- "9. Dealers registered or liable to be registered under the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 whose total turnover in any year is not less than rupees seventy-five thousand. Rs. 250 per annum" (3) after item 20-A, the following shall be inserted, namely:- "20-B "Photo laboratories, film processing laboratories and photo studios. Rs. 250 per annum" 20-C. Nursing homes, X-ray clinics, pathological testing laboratories and hospitals, other than those run by the State or Central Government. Rs. 250 per annum 20-D. Beauty parlours, dry cleaners and interior decorators. Rs. 250 per annum 20-E. Film distributors and travel agents. Rs. 250 per annum 20-F. Journalists. Rs. 250 per annum"
