Karnataka Evacuee Interest (Separation) Supplementary Act, 1961_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to supplement certain provisions of the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Act, 1951, in its application to the State of Karnataka. WHEREAS it is expedient to supplement certain provisions of the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Act, 1951 (Central Act LXIV of 1951), in its application to the State of Karnataka; BE it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twelfth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Evacuee Interest (Separation) Supplementary Act, 1961_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the 1 [Karnataka] Evacuee Interest (Separation) Supplementary Act, 1961. (2) It extends to the whole of the 2 [State of Karnataka]. (3) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 15th day of October 1960. Adapted by the Karnataka Adaptations of Laws Order, 1973 with effect from . 1.11.1973 Adapted by the Karnataka Adaptations of Laws Order, 1973 with effect from . 1.11.1973
Karnataka Evacuee Interest (Separation) Supplementary Act, 1961_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka The amendments made to the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Act, 1951 (Central Act LXIV of 1951), by the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Amendment Act, 1960 (Central Act 27 of 1960), shall, in so far as they relate to any matter enumerated in List II of the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution, be as valid in the 1 [State of Karnataka] as if the provisions contained therein had been enacted by the Legislature of the State. Adapted by the Karnataka Adaptations of Laws Order, 1973 with effect from . 1.11.1973
Karnataka Evacuee Interest (Separation) Supplementary Act, 1961_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka The 1 [Karnataka] Evacuee Interest (Separation) Supplementary Ordinance, 1960, is hereby repealed. Adapted by the Karnataka Adaptations of Laws Order, 1973 with effect from . 1.11.1973
