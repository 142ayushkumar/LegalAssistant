Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1976. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 81 of 1976); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1978.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Fifteenth day of December, 1977. Received the assent of the Governor on the First day of April, 1978
Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka For sub-section (3) of Section 1 of the Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 81 of 1976) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely - "(3) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint and different dates may be appointed for different provisions of the Act."
Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In the principal Act, Sections 4, 5 and 10 shall be omitted.
Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment) Ordinance, 1977 (Karnataka Ordinance 12 of 1977) is hereby repealed.
