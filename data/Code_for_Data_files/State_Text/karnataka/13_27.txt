Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances Act, 1956. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1957), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1974.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-seventh day of May, 1974 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (2), for the words "three hundred and fifty rupees", the words "five hundred rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6 of the principal Act, in clause (b) of sub-section (1) for the words "seventy-five rupees", the words "one hundred and twenty-five rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6-C of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), for the words "three hundred and fifty rupees", the words "five hundred rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6-D of the principal Act, in clause (b) of sub-section (1), for the words "seventy-five rupees", the words "one hundred and twenty-five rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12 of the principal Act - (i) in sub-section (2) - (a) in clause (a), for the words "one and half times", the words "two times" shall be substituted; (b) in clause (b), for the words "forty paise", the words "seventy-five paise" shall be substituted; (c) in clause (d) - (i) for the words "twenty rupees" the words "thirty rupees" shall be substituted; (ii) in the first proviso, for the words "forty rupees", the words "fifty rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 13 of the principal Act, after the words "medical attendance and treatment", the following shall be inserted, namely - "They shall also be entitled, subject to rules made by the State Government, to reimbursement of the expenses incurred by them for medical attendance and treatment obtained at any other place".
