Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act No. 34 of 1957), for the purpose hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-first Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1990.1 2 (2) It shall come into force on the First day of April, 1990. Received the assent of the Governor on the First day of April, 1990 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
