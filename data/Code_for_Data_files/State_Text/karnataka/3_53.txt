Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Third Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Co-operative Societies Act, 1959. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Co-operative Societies Act, 1959 (Karnataka Act 11 of 1959) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Third Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Third Amendment) Act, 1976.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the third day of November, 1976. Received the assent of the Governor on the First day of December, 1976
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Third Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 16 of the Karnataka Co-operative Societies Act, 1959 (Karnataka Act 11 of 1959) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (1), in clause (c-1), the word "and" at the end shall be omitted and after the said clause, the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(c-2) a firm or society registered under the Karnataka Societies Registration Act, 1960 (Karnataka Act 17 of 1960), in the case of such co-operative societies as may be notified by the State Government; and".
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Third Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 17 of the principal Act- (i) after clause (b) of sub-section (1), the following clauses shall be inserted, namely:- "(c) carries on business of the kind carried on by such co-operative society; (d) is already a member of a co-operative society carrying on business of the same kind as itself."; (ii) in sub-section (2), for the word, figure and bracket "sub-section (1)," the words, figure, letters and brackets "clauses (a) to (c) of sub-section (1)" shall be substituted; (iii) for sub-section (3), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely - "(3) Where on the date of the commencement of the Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Third Amendment) Act, 1976, a person is a member of more than one co-operative society carrying on business of the same kind, then unless he resigns his membership in all but one of such co-operative societies within a period of sixty days from the date of commencement of the said Act he shall, on the expiration of the said period cease to be a member of all such co-operative societies."
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Third Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka For Section 20 of the principal Act, the following section shall be substituted, namely:- "20. Votes of members -(1) No member of a society shall have more than one vote in the affairs of the co-operative society. (2) The following shall not have the right to vote at a meeting of the co-operative society in which they are members, namely:- (a) a nominal or associate member, or a member who is a defaulter, (b) a co-operative society- (i) the committee of which stands superseded or to which a special officer is appointed under Section 30-A of the Act; (ii) which is wound up under Section 72; (iii) which has not commenced working or has ceased to work; (iv) whose principal object is to advance loans and whose percentage of recovery is less than fifty per cent of the total demand for the co-operative year immediately preceding the co-operative year during which the meeting is held; (v) which is under loss continuously for the three years immediately prior to the year in which the meeting is held; (c) an agricultural credit society which is a member of the concerned District Central Co-operative Bank, but has been permitted by the Registrar to raise loans from other financing agency for the purpose of financing its members. Explanation -A member shall be deemed to be a defaulter if he has failed to pay the arrears of any kind due by him to the society whether as borrower or as surety or in any other capacity, at least fifteen days before the date of such meeting after a notice of not less than one month in this behalf has been issued to him."
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Third Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka For sub-section (4) of Section 29-A of the principal Act, the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely:- "(4) The Secretary of the co-operative society shall within fifteen days from the date of election, convene a meeting in the prescribed manner of all the members of the Committee (including nominated and ex officio members if any) for the purpose of electing the President, the Vice- President, the Chairman, the Vice-Chairman and such other office-bearers as are required to be elected under the bye-laws of the co-operative society".
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Third Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 69 of the principal Act, the word "willful" shall be omitted.
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Third Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 79 of the principal Act, for the words "on the security of mortgage, or mortgages, to be acquired or partly on mortgages held and partly to be acquired, and properties and other assets of the Land Development Banks", the following shall be substituted, namely:- "on the security of- (a) mortgage or mortgages already held; or (b) mortgage or mortgages to be acquired; or (c) partly on mortgage held and partly to be acquired; or (d) the guarantee of the State Government guaranteeing the full amount of principal and interest on debentures until the date of complete discharge of the debentures; or (e) partly on mortgages held or to be acquired and partly on the Government guarantee for the principal and interest till the complete discharge of the debentures; and (f) properties and other assets of the Land Development Banks."
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Third Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 109 of the principal Act,- (i) in sub-section (6)- (a) in clause (i), for the words, figure and brackets "under sub-section (2) of that section acts as a member," the words, figure and brackets "under sub-section (2) of that section acts as or exercises any rights or privileges of a member of any such co-operative society" shall be substituted; (b) for the words "two hundred rupees", the words "one thousand rupees" shall be substituted; (ii) after sub-section (6), the following sub-section shall be inserted namely:- "(7) if any person who is not eligible to vote under sub-section (2) of Section 20 exercises such vote, he shall be punishable with a fine which may extend to two thousand rupees".
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Third Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka In section 111-A of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), for the words "Chief Ministerial Officer", the words "a whole time salaried officer of the co-operative society" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Third Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 10--> State(s): Karnataka Section 113 of the principal Act shall be renumbered as sub-section (1) of that section and after the said sub-section (1), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(2) All the meetings including the general body meetings of a co-operative society shall be held in its registered office: Provided that any such meeting may, with the previous permission of the Registrar, be held at any other place within the same city, town or village where the registered office is situated if there is no sufficient accommodation to hold such meeting in the registered office."
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Third Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 11--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Fourth Amendment) Ordinance, 1976 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 34 of 1976) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
