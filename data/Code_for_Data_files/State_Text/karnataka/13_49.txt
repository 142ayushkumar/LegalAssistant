Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1958.1 Received the assent of the Governor on the Nineteenth day of December, 1958
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 16 of the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (i) after the words "may by notification", the words "and subject to such restrictions and conditions as may be specified in the notification" shall be inserted and shall be deemed always to have been inserted; and (ii) for clause (a), the following clause shall be substituted, and shall be deemed always to have been substituted, namely:- "(a) exempt or reduce whether prospectively or retrospectively the tax payable in respect of - (i) any class of motor vehicles, or (ii) motor vehicles not used on roads."
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 19 of the principal Act, - (i) after the words "or in any Union Territory in India," the words "and any law authorising any such levy shall cease to be in force," shall be added; and (ii) the following Explanation shall be added, namely:- "Explanation - For the purposes of this section tax means the tax referred to in Entries 52 and 57 of List II of the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution of India."
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In the Schedule to the principal Act - (1) in Part A - (i) in Item 4, in paragraph (i), the brackets and words "(other than the driver)" shall be inserted at the end of each sub-paragraphs (a), (b) and (c); and (ii) after Item 6, the following item shall be inserted, namely:- "6-A. Omnibuses (other than those liable to tax under the foregoing provisions of this Schedule)-for every person (other than the driver) which the vehicle is constructed or adapted to carry. 10.00 15.00" (2) in Part B after Item 3, the following item shall be inserted, namely:- "3-A. Omnibuses (other than those liable to tax under the foregoing provisions of this Schedule) - for every person (other than the driver) which the vehicle is constructed or adapted to carry. 2.00 5.00"
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka The Indian Tolls Act, 1851 (Central Act VII of 1851), the Indian Tolls Act, 1864 (Central Act XV of 1864) and the Indian Tolls Act, 1888 (Central Act VII of 1888), as in force in any Area of the State of Karnataka and the Bombay Tolls on Roads and Bridges Act, 1875 (Bombay Act III of 1875), as in force in the Bombay Area, are hereby repealed.
