Karnataka Industrial Areas Development (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Industrial Areas Development Act, 1966. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Industrial Areas Development Act, 1966 (Karnataka Act 18 of 1966) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Industrial Areas Development (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Industrial Areas Development (Amendment) Act, 1987.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-ninth day of April, 1987
Karnataka Industrial Areas Development (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6 of the Karnataka Industrial Areas Development Act, 1966 (Karnataka Act 18 of 1966), (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (1) for clause (c), the following clauses shall be substituted, namely - "(c) the Secretary to Government, Housing and Urban Development Department; (ca) the Commissioner for Industrial Development and Director of Industries and Commerce; (cb) the Chairman and Managing Director, Karnataka State Industrial Investment and Development Corporation Limited; (cc) the Chairman, Karnataka State Pollution Control Board; (cd) the Director of Town Planning; (ce) the Managing Director, Karnataka State Small Industries Development Corporation Limited." (2) for clause (e), the following clause shall be substituted, namely - "(e) two nominees of the Industrial Development Bank of India."
Karnataka Industrial Areas Development (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka Sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the principal Act shall be omitted.
Karnataka Industrial Areas Development (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka Sections 9 and 10 of the principal Act shall be omitted.
Karnataka Industrial Areas Development (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In the heading to Chapter VI of the principal Act, for the words, brackets and figures " Mysore Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1961", the words, brackets and figures " Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1974", shall be substituted.
Karnataka Industrial Areas Development (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 25 of the principal Act - (1) in the heading, for the words and figures "Karnataka Act 3 of 1962", the words and figures "Karnataka Act 32 of 1974" shall be substituted; (2) in sub-section (1), for the words, brackets and figures " Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1961", the words, brackets and figures " Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1961", the words, brackets and figures " Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauhorised Occupants) Act, 1974" shall be substituted; (3) in sub-section (2), in clause (b), for the figures and word "11 and 12", the figures and word "14, 15, 16 and 17" shall be substituted.
