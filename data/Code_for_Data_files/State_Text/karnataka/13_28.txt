Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances Act, 1956. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1957) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1976.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the first day of July, 1974. Received the assent of the Governor on the Nineteenth day of June, 1976 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka For sub-section (2-A) of Section 12 of the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1957), the following sub-sections shall be substituted, namely - "(2-A) (a) Every Minister, Minister of State and Deputy Minister touring outside India on duty shall be entitled to such terms in regard to travelling and other expenses as the Governor may, in each case, by order, determine. (b) Every order issued under clause (a) shall, as soon as may be, after it is issued, be laid before each House of the State Legislature".
