Karnataka Command Areas Development (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Command Areas Development Act, 1980. And, whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Command Areas Development Act, 1980 (Karnataka Act 6 of 1980), for the purpose hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Command Areas Development (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Command Areas Development (Amendment) Act, 1987.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Tenth day of November, 1987
Karnataka Command Areas Development (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka After clause (c) of sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the Karnataka Command Areas Development Act, 1980 (Karnataka Act 6 of 1980), the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(cc) The Chief Secretaries of the concerned Zilla Parishadsh."
