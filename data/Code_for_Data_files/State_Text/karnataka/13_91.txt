Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Second Amendment) Act, 1979_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirtieth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Second Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Second Amendment) Act, 1979.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on 27th day of March, 1979. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-sixth day of April, 1979 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Second Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Schedule to the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1957), in Part 'A', in Item 3, - (1) for clauses (h), (i), (j), (k), (1), (m), (n) and (o) of sub-item (i), the following clauses shall be substituted, namely:- "(h) Vehicles exceeding 5,500 Kgs. but not exceeding 7,000 Kgs. in weight laden. 900.00 (i) Vehicles exceeding 7,000 Kgs. but not exceeding 8,500 Kgs. in weight laden. 1,040.00 (j) Vehicles exceeding 8,500 Kgs. but not exceeding 9,500 Kgs. in weight laden. 1,110.00 (k) Vehicles exceeding 9,500 Kgs. but not exceeding 10,500 Kgs. in weight laden. 1,185.00 (l) Vehicles exceeding 10,500 Kgs. but not exceeding 12,000 Kgs. in weight laden. 1,350.00 (m) Vehicles exceeding 12,000 Kgs. but not exceeding 13,500 Kgs. in weight laden. 1,525.00 (n) Vehicles exceeding 13,500 Kgs. but not exceeding 15,000 Kgs. in weight laden. 1,700.00 (o) Vehicles exceeding 15,000 Kgs. in weight laden. 1,700.00 plus Rs. 80 for every 250 Kgs. or part thereof in excess of 15,000 Kgs." (2) for clauses (g), (h), (i), (j), (k), (1), (m) and (n) of sub-item (ii), the following clauses shall be substituted, namely:- "(g) for each trailer exceeding 5,500 Kgs. but not exceeding 7,000 Kgs. in weight laden. 800.00 (h) for each trailer exceeding 7,000 Kgs. but not exceeding 9,000 Kgs. in weight laden. 900.00 (i) for each trailer exceeding 9,000 Kgs. but not exceeding 9,500 Kgs. in weight laden. 950.00 (j) for each trailer exceeding 9,500 Kgs. but not exceeding 10,500 Kgs. in weight laden. 1,050.00 (k) for each trailer exceeding 10,500 Kgs. but not exceeding 12,000 Kgs. in weight laden. 1,200.00 (1) for each trailer exceeding 12,000 Kgs. but not exceeding 13,500 Kgs. in weight laden. 1,350.00 (m) for each trailer exceeding 13,500 Kgs. but not exceeding 15,000 Kgs. in weight laden. 1,500.00 (n) for each trailer exceeding 15,000 Kgs. in weight laden. 1,500.00 plus Rs. 80 for every 250 Kgs. or part thereof in excess of 15,000Kgs."
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Second Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka Where in respect of a motor vehicle, the tax payable under this Act for a year, half-year, or quarter has been paid before the date of commencement of the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Second Amendment) Act, 1979, then, notwithstanding that a taxation card has been granted therefor, the revised tax payable under Section 2 for the said year, half-year or quarter shall be paid within 60 days from the date of publication of this Act in the official Gazette.
