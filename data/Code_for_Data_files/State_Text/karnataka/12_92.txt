Karnataka Land Revenue (Third Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964), for the purpose hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Sixty-second year of the Republic of India, as follows-
Karnataka Land Revenue (Third Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Revenue (Third Amendment) Act, 2011.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the President on the Twenty-second day of January, 2013.
Karnataka Land Revenue (Third Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964) (hereinafter referred to as the 'Principal Act'), in Section 2, in clause (20), in the explanation for the words "landholder in the Coorg District", the words "landholder including Jammabane privileged and unprivileged, umbli land in the Coorg District" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Land Revenue (Third Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 80 of the Principal Act, for the words "wherever situate", the words "wherever situate, including unalienated Jammabane land held by the occupant in Coorg District" shall be substituted.
