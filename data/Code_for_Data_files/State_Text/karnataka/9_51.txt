Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Fourteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1963.1 (2) Sections 2, 3, 4, 5 and 6 shall be deemed to have come into force on the Thirty-first day of October, 1957, and Sections 7, 8 and 9 shall come into force on the date of publication of this Act in the official Gazette. Received the assent of the Governor on the Eighteenth day of February, 1964
Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act - (1) for clause (b), the following clauses shall be substituted, namely - "(b) Betterment Levy Officer means such officer as the Government may appoint to be the Betterment Levy Officer in respect of any area or irrigation work; (ba) contribution means the tax payable as basic contribution and special benefit contribution under Sections 3 and 4;" (2) after clause (f), the following clauses shall be inserted, namely - "(fa) major irrigation work means an irrigation work costing more than rupees five crores; (fb) medium irrigation work means an irrigation work other than a minor irrigation work costing more than rupees ten lakhs and not more than rupees five crores; (fc) minor irrigation work means a tank or other reservoir (including the canals, channels, embankments, structures and installations connected therewith or auxiliary thereto) costing in respect of construction, restoration, expansion or alteration, more than rupees one lakh which is or has been declared by the Government or the prescribed officer to be capable of irrigating not more than one thousand acres of land;" (3) after clause (h), the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(ha) prescribed officer means the officer specified in rules made under this Act and different officers may be specified for different areas, irrigation works or purposes;"
Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the principal Act - (1) the said section shall be renumbered as sub-section (1) and in the sub-section as so renumbered - (i) for the words "The Government shall be entitled to levy a betterment contribution, in accordance with the provisions of this Act, from the landholder or holder of any land which, in its opinion, is benefited", the words and figure "There shall be levied a tax called betterment contribution, at the rates specified in Section 4 in accordance with the provisions of this Act on land, which, in the opinion of the prescribed officer, is benefited" shall be substituted; (ii) after Explanation II, the following explanation shall be inserted, namely - "Explanation III -In this sub-section, the expression "Government" shall be deemed to include the authority established under Section 66 of the Andhra State Act, 1953 (Central Act 30 of 1953)." (2) after the said sub-section (1), the following sub-sections shall be inserted, namely - "(2) For the purposes of levy of betterment contribution, the prescribed officer shall, by notification, specify - (i) the dates of commencement and completion of the construction, restoration, expansion or alteration of any irrigation work; (ii) the nature of the irrigation work, whether major irrigation work, medium irrigation work or minor irrigation work; and (iii) the lands which, in his opinion, are benefited by such irrigation work; and different dates of commencement and completion of any irrigation work may be specified in respect of different lands benefited by the same irrigation work depending upon the dates on which water is made available to such lands. (3) The Government may either suo motu or on application by any landholder affected by a notification issued under sub-section (2), if satisfied that there is any error in any such notification, direct such modification of the notification as it deems fit and the notification shall be modified accordingly by the prescribed officer."
Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka For Section 4 of the principal Act, the following section shall be substituted, namely - "4. Amount of contribution payable -(1) The amount of contribution payable in respect of lands specified under Section 3 shall consist of a basic contribution and a special benefit contribution payable at the rates specified in sub-section (2). (2)(a) The rate of basic contribution shall be, where the land is, or has been, benefited by - (i) a major irrigation work or medium irrigation work, rupees five hundred per acre; and (ii) a minor irrigation work, rupees four hundred per acre. (b) The rate of special benefit contribution shall be, where water is made available or is available from the work for the land - (i) for not less than eight months in a year rupees twenty-five per acre per annum; and (ii) for not less than three months but less than eight months in a year, rupees twelve and a half per acre per annum. (3)(a) The basic contribution shall be payable by the landholder in accordance with the provisions of Section 6. (b) The special benefit contribution shall be payable by the landholder for a period of twenty years in respect of the benefited land, and the said contribution payable in respect of any year shall be paid in the following year. (4) After the issue of a notification under sub-section (2) of Section 3 - (a) The Betterment Levy Officer shall, after making such enquiry, if any, as he considers necessary, by order determine, the amount of basic contribution payable in respect of different lands; (b) the Tahsildar shall, after making such enquiry, if any, as he considers necessary, by order determine, every year, the amount of special benefit contribution payable in respect of different lands. (5)(a) Any person aggrieved by an order made under clause (a) of sub-section (4) may, within sixty days from the date of the order, appeal to the Deputy Commissioner, whose decision thereon shall be final and shall not be liable to be questioned in any Court of law. (b) Any person aggrieved by an order made under clause (b) of sub-section (4) may, within thirty days from the date of the order, appeal to the Assistant Commissioner of the Revenue Sub-Division, whose decision thereon shall be final and shall not be liable to be questioned in any Court of law."
Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the principal Act - (1) in sub-section (1) - (i) for the word "Contribution", the words "The basic contribution" shall be substituted; (ii) for the words "an officer authorised by the Government in this behalf", the words "the Betterment Levy Officer" shall be substituted; (2) in sub-section (2), for the word "contribution", the words "basic contribution" shall be substituted; (3) after sub-section (2), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(3) The special benefit contribution payable for any year shall become payable when the land revenue due on such land for that year becomes payable and shall be paid along with such land revenue in the same manner as such land revenue is payable."
Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6 of the principal Act, for the word "contribution" wherever it occurs the words "basic contribution", shall be substituted.
Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10 of the principal Act - (1) in sub-section (1) - (i) in clause (b), for the words "water is supplied or used for purposes of irrigation", the words "water is supplied, made available or used for purposes of irrigation or any other purpose" shall be substituted; (ii) after clause (c), the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(d) water supplied or made available to the Government by the authority established under Section 66 of the Andhra State Act, 1953, is supplied, made available or used for purposes of irrigation or any other purpose." (iii) for the words "the Government may prescribe the rates at which, the manner in which and the authority by which such water rates shall be fixed and the water rate so fixed may be in lieu of the water rate, if any, payable under any other law in force", the words, figures and brackets "the Government may prescribe the rates at which such water rates shall be levied, which may be - (i) different in respect of water supplied, made available or used for different purposes; (ii) different in respect of water supplied, made available or used with reference to different crops grown or which may be grown; (iii) in lieu of water rate, if any, payable under any other law, and the manner in which and the authority by which the amount of water rate levied shall be determined." (2) sub-section (2) shall be omitted.
Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 11 of the principal Act, for sub-section (3), the following sub-sections shall be substituted, namely - "(3) A rule made under this Act may be made with retrospective effect and when such a rule is made the reasons for making the rule shall be specified in a statement laid before both Houses of the State Legislature. Subject to any modification made under sub-section (4), every rule made under this Act shall have effect as if enacted in this Act. (4) Every rule made under this Act shall be laid as soon as may be after it is made before each House of the State Legislature while it is in session for a total period of thirty days which may be comprised in one session or in two or more successive sessions, and if before the expiry of the session in which it is so laid or the sessions immediately following, both Houses agree in making any modification in the rule or both Houses agree that the rule should not be made, the rule shall thereafter have effect only in such modified form or be of no effect, as the case may be; so however that any such modification or annulment shall be without prejudice to the validity of anything previously done under that rule."
Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka Notwithstanding anything contained in the Madras Irrigation Cess Act, 1865 (Madras Act 7 of 1865) as in force before its repeal by Section 12 of the Principal Act, all amounts levied and collected by way of irrigation cess in purported exercise of any power under the said Madras Irrigation Cess Act, 1865, shall be deemed to be validly levied and collected, and may continue to be levied and collected accordingly until water rates are levied in accordance with the provisions of the principal Act, and any such levy or collection shall not be called in question merely on the ground that such levy or collection was not in accordance with the provisions of the said Madras Irrigation Cess Act, 1865.
