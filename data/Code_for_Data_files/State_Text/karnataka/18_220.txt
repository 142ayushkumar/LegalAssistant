Karnataka State Universities (Second Amendment) Act, 1977_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka State Universities Act, 1976. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka State Universities Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1976), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka State Universities (Second Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka State Universities (Second Amendment) Act, 1977.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-fifth day of July, 1977
Karnataka State Universities (Second Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka State Universities Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1976) (hereinafter referred to as the  principal Act) for clause (2-A) the following clause shall be and shall always be deemed to have been substituted, namely - "(2-A) "Department", "Department of Studies", "Postgraduate Department" and "Postgraduate Department of Studies" means the Department, Department of studies, Postgraduate Department and Postgraduate Department of Studies in the University run and maintained by the University: Provided that where the University is not running and maintaining any Department, Department of Studies, Postgraduate Department or Postgraduate Department of Studies, such teachers in an affiliated college or colleges or institutions as the Chancellor may, in consultation with the State Government, nominate shall be deemed to be the Department, Department of Studies, Postgraduate Department or Postgraduate Department of Studies".
Karnataka State Universities (Second Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 16 of the principal Act, the following new section shall be inserted, namely - "16-A. Temporary vacancy in the Office of the Registrar, Controller of Examinations or Finance Officer -In the event of death, suspension or temporary absence of the Registrar, Controller of Examinations or the Finance Officer, the Chancellor may empower any other officers of the University to perform the duties of the Registrar, Controller of Examinations or the Finance Officer, as the case may be."
Karnataka State Universities (Second Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 21 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1) - (1) for clause (19), the following clause shall be substituted, namely - "(19) One Professor or Director in the Postgraduate Departments from each of the faculties, elected by Professors and Directors in Postgraduate Departments of that faculty"; (2) in clause (22), after the second proviso, the following proviso0 shall be inserted, namely - "Provided also that when representatives of students are elected, the term of office of the students nominated under the proceeding proviso shall come to an end;" (3) after clause (23), the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(24) The Director of Health and Family Planning Services."
Karnataka State Universities (Second Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka For clause (ix) of Section 24 of the principal Act, the following clauses shall be substituted, namely - "(ix) One Member of the Academic Council elected by its members. (x) The Director of Health and Family Planning Services."
Karnataka State Universities (Second Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 26 of the principal Act, after clause (xiv), the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(xv) The Director of Health and Family Planning Services"
Karnataka State Universities (Second Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka For item (iii) of clause (b) of sub-section (2) of Section 49 of the principal Act, the following item shall be substituted, namely - "(iii) The Head of the Department concerned if he is a Professor, and if he is not a Professor, a Professor in the concerned Department of any other University within the State nominated by the Chancellor and where no such Professor is available in any University within the State such Professor in the concerned department from a Central Institute within the State or from a University in any other State nominated by the Chancellor."
Karnataka State Universities (Second Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka For clause (iii) of sub-section (2) of Section 50 of the principal Act, the following clause shall be substituted, namely - "(iii) One person supervising the work of the section for which recruitment is made nominated by the Vice-Chancellor and where there is no such person the Head of the Department of the concerned section".
Karnataka State Universities (Second Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka For Section 64 of the principal Act, the following section shall be substituted, namely - "64. Acts and proceedings not invalidated, merely on the ground of defect in constitution, vacancy etc -(1) Notwithstanding that the Senate, the Syndicate or the Academic Council or any other authority or body of the University is not duly constituted or there is a defect in its constitution or reconstitution at any time and notwithstanding that there is a vacancy in the membership of any such authority or body, no act or proceeding of such authority or body shall be invalidated merely on any such ground or grounds. (2) No resolution of any Authority or body of the University shall be deemed invalid on account of any irregularity in the service of notice upon any member provided that the proceedings of such authority or body were not prejudicially affected by such irregularity."
Karnataka State Universities (Second Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 10--> State(s): Karnataka After sub-section (2) of Section 72 of the principal Act, the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(3) The persons holding the office of the Librarian of the Bangalore University, Karnataka University and the Mysore University on the date of the commencement of this Act shall continue to hold office till the date they would have held that office if this Act had not been passed."
Karnataka State Universities (Second Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 11--> State(s): Karnataka Notwithstanding anything in any judgment or order of any Court, elections to the various authorities of the University shall be held in accordance with the provisions of the principal Act as amended by this Act and any election held either before or after the commencement of this Act contrary to the said provisions shall be null and void.
