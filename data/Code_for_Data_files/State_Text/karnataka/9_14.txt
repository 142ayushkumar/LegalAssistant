Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka (Sandur Area) Inams Abolition Act, 1976 and the Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition Act, 1977. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka (Sandur Area) Inams Abolition Act, 1976 (Karntaka Act 54 of 1976) and the Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition Act, 1977 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1978), for the purposes hereinafter appearing: Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Act, 1990.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Second day of February, 1991
Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10 of the Karnataka (Sandur Area) Inam Abolition Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 54 of 1976), for the words and figures "30th day of June, 1987", the words and figures "31st day of March, 1991", shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 11 of the Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition Act, 1977 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1978), for the words and figures "30th June, 1987", in two places where they occur, the words and figures "31st day of March, 1991" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
