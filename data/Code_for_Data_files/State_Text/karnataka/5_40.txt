Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act 30 of 1958) for the purpose hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1978.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the President on the Twelfth day of January, 1979
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In clause (b) of sub-section (3) of Section 9 of the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act 30 of 1958) for the words "on application to any Magistrate", the words, figures and brackets "notwithstanding anything contained in the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 (Central Act 2 of 1974), on application to any Magistrate" shall be substituted.
