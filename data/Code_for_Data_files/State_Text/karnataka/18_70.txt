Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board Act, 1966. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board Act, 1966 (Karnataka Act No. 16 of 1966). Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board (Amendment) Act, 19771 2 . (2) It shall come into force at once. First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, on the Twenty eighth day of July, 1977, Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-fifth day of July, 1977 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In clause (A) of sub-section (4) of Section 4 of the Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board Act, 1966, (Karnataka Act No. 16 of 1966)- (i) in sub-clause (a), for the words "Divisional Deputy Directors" the words "Divisional Joint Directors" shall be substituted; (ii) in sub-clause (b), for the words "District Educational Officer" the words "Deputy Director of Public Instruction" shall be substituted;
