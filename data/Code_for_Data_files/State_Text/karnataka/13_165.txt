Mysore Abolition of Whipping Act, 1949_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to provide for the abolition of the sentence of whipping. Whereas, it is expedient to provide for the abolition of the sentence of whipping. It is hereby enacted as follows -
Mysore Abolition of Whipping Act, 1949_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called 1 [the Mysore Abolition of Whipping Act, 1949]2 . (2) It extends to the 3 [whole of the State of Mysore except Bellary District.] (3) It shall come into force at once. Substituted for the words and figures "the Abolition of Whipping Act, 1949" by Act No. 1 of 1956 Received the assent of His Highness the Maharaja on the 26th day of January, 1949 The Mysore Adaptation of Laws Order, 1953
Mysore Abolition of Whipping Act, 1949_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka Notwithstanding anything contained in any law for the time being in force, no person shall be punished with a sentence of whipping by any Court or other authority for any offence under any law whatsoever.
Mysore Abolition of Whipping Act, 1949_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka The Act specified in the Schedule are hereby repealed to the extent mentioned in the fourth column thereof.
