Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances Act, 1956. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1957) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1984.1 2 (2) Section 3 shall come into force at once and other sections shall be deemed to have come into force on the 26th day of December, 1978. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-third day of April, 1984
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 11-A of the Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (1) in sub-section (1) - (i) for the words and figures "to every person who has served after the First day of January, 1952", the words, letter, brackets and figures "to every person who has served - (a) after the First day of January, 1952", shall be substituted. (ii) in the fifth proviso for the words "and was not able to serve the full term of five years", the words "or is nominated as a member in the middle of a term and was not able to serve the full term, he" shall be substituted; (2) after sub-section (1), at the end, after the provisos and the explanation, the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(b) before the First day of January, 1952 as a member of - (a) the Constituent Assembly; (b) the Karnataka Representative Assembly; (c) the Karnataka Legislative Assembly; (d) the Legislative Assembly or the Legislative Council of any State (including an Indian State) or Provincey, which or any area of which now forms part of the State of Karnataka, a pension of three hundred rupees per month for the remaining period of the life of such member irrespective of the period of his service: Provided that no such pension shall be paid to a person unless - (i) in the case of a person who served as a member representing a territorial Constituency, he represented; and (ii) in the case of any other member, he permanently resided during such membership in, any area which now forms part of the State of Karnataka." (3) in sub-section (3) - (a) for the words "that to which he is entitled under sub-section (1)", in the two places where they occur, the words "one thousand rupees" shall be substituted; and (b) for the words "only the difference as pension under sub-section (1)", the words "pension under that sub-section, so however, that the aggregate amount shall not exceed one thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In clause (a) of Section 12 of the principal Act, the words "or not availed" shall be omitted.
