Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Legal Aid Board Act, 1981. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Legal Aid Board Act, 1981 (Karnataka Act 32 of 1981) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1985.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force from the Twenty-sixth day of April, 1985. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixth day of September, 1985
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Legal Aid Board Act, 1981 (Karnataka Act 32 of 1981) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act - (a) in clause (4), in sub-clause (c), for the word "documents," the words "petitions and other documents" shall be substituted; (b) after clause (5), the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(5A) 'legal proceedings' includes proceedings before a quasi-judicial or an administrative authorit"; (c) in clause (6), after the word "writing", the following shall be inserted, namely - "and includes spreading of legal knowledge and creating legal awareness and the like amongst people".
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the principal Act - (a) for clause (h), the following clauses shall be substituted, namely - "(h) the Secretary to Government, Home Department, ex officio; (ha) the Director-General of Police, ex officio; (hb) the Director of Prosecutions and Government Litigation, ex officio"; (b) in clause (j), for the words "one person", the words "two persons" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka For Section 9 of the principal Act, the following shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted, namely - "9. Allowances payable to the members of the Board - The sitting fees and other allowances payable to members other than the President and the Executive Chairman for attending the meetings of the Board or of any Committee thereof shall be such as may be prescribed".
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In clause (e) of Section 10 of the principal Act, for the words "exercise such powers", the words "exercise all powers necessary for the proper administration of the Board and to achieve the objects thereof and such other powers" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 13 of the principal Act, for the words "for the custody", the words "for the proper management of the affairs of the Board and the custody" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka After sub-section (2) of Section 15 of the principal Act, the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(3) The Board may delegate any of its powers to the Executive Committee or the Executive Chairman with or without any condition".
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka In the heading to Section 18 of the principal Act, for the words "Test Litigation", the words "Public Interest Litigation" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 21 of the principal Act - (a) in clause (b), for the word "him", the words "him; or" shall be substituted; (b) after clause (b), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(c) by executing the decree or order in a civil Court".
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1985_Section 10--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Ordinance, 1985 (Karnataka Ordinance 8 of 1985) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken or any rule or order or notification made or issued under the principal Act; as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
