Karnataka State Legislature Proceedings (Protection of Publication) Repeal Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to repeal the Karnataka State Legislature proceedings (Protection of Publication) Act, 1968. Whereas, it is expedient to repeal the Karnataka State Legistature Proceedings (Protection of Publication) Act, 1968 (Karnataka Act 20 of 1968); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka State Legislature Proceedings (Protection of Publication) Repeal Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka State Legislature Proceedings (Protection of Publication) Repeal Act, 1976.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Ninth day of January, 1976. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-third day of February, 1976
Karnataka State Legislature Proceedings (Protection of Publication) Repeal Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka During the period the proclamation of Emergency issued under clause (1) of Article 352 of the Constitution of India on the 25th day of June, 1975 is in operation the Karnataka State Legislature Proceedings (Protection of Publication) Act, 1968 (Karnataka Act 20 of 1968) shall be deemed to be repealed: Provided that such repeal shall not affect any proceedings, civil or criminal (whether pending immediately before the commencement of this Act or instituted or taken after such commencement) in respect of - (a) any publication referred to in sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the said Act, made before such commencement; or (b) any report or matter broadcast, before such commencement, by the means referred to in Section 4 of the said Act, and accordingly any such proceedings shall be disposed of as if the said Act had continued in force and this Act has not been enacted.
Karnataka State Legislature Proceedings (Protection of Publication) Repeal Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka State Legislature Proceedings (Protection of Publication) Repeal Ordinance, 1976 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 1 of 1976) is hereby repealed.
