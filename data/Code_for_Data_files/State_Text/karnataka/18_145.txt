Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 2008_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act No. 34 of 1957), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by Karnataka State Legislature in the Fifty-ninth year of the Republic of India, as follows.-
Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 2008_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 2008. (2) It shall come into force with effect from the first day of August, 2008.
