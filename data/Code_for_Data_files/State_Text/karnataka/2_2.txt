Karnataka (Bombay and Hyderabad Areas) Religious and Charitable Inams Abolition (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka (Bombay and Hyderabad Areas) Religious and Charitable Inams Abolition Act, 1973. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka (Bombay and Hyderabad Areas) Religious and Charitable Inams Abolition Act, 1973. Be, it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka (Bombay and Hyderabad Areas) Religious and Charitable Inams Abolition (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka (Bombay and Hyderabad Areas) Religious and Charitable Inams Abolition (Amendment) Act, 1976.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the President on the Sixteenth day of August, 1976
Karnataka (Bombay and Hyderabad Areas) Religious and Charitable Inams Abolition (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Karnataka (Bombay and Hyderabad Areas) Religious and Charitable Inams Abolition Act, 1973 (Karnataka Act 26 of 1973), (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) for the expressions "Mysore", "Bombay and Hyderabad Areas", "Bombay Area" and "Hyderabad Area" wherever they occur the expressions "Karnataka", "Belgaum and Gulbarga Areas", "Belgaum Area" and "Gulbarga Area" shall be substituted.
Karnataka (Bombay and Hyderabad Areas) Religious and Charitable Inams Abolition (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 16 of the principal Act, for the words "twenty-five", the word "ten" shall be substituted.
