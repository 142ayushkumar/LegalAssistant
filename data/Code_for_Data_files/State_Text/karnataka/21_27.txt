Karnataka Village Panchayats (Postponement of Elections) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Village Panchayats (Postponement of Elections) Act, 1975 . Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Village Panchayats (Postponement of Elections) Act, 1975 (Karnataka Act 6 of 1975) for the purpose hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Village Panchayats (Postponement of Elections) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Village Panchayats (Postponement of Elections) (Amendment) Act, 1976.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the nineteenth day of December, 1975. Received the assent of the Governor on the Second day of March, 1976
Karnataka Village Panchayats (Postponement of Elections) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Karnataka Village Panchayats (Postponement of Elections) Act, 1975 (Karnataka Act 6 of 1975), in sub-section (3) of Section 1, for the figures and words "31st December, 1975", the figures and word "31st December, 1976" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Village Panchayats (Postponement of Elections) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka Village Panchayats (Postponement of Elections) (Amendment) Ordinance, 1975 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 25 of 1975) is hereby repealed.
