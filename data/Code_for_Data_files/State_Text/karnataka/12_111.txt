Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1956. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Eighteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1967.1 2 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Ninth day of August, 1967
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act No. 2 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for the words "nine hundred" at the two places where they occur, the words "one thousand and two hundred" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 4, for the words "two hundred and fifty", the words "three hundred and fifty" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the principal Act, for the words "two hundred" the words "three hundred and fifty" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In clause (b) of sub-section (1) of Section 6 of the principal Act, for the word "thirty-five", the word "seventy-five" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 8 of the principal Act - (i) in clause (a), for the word "one-fourth", the word "half" shall be substituted; (ii) in clause (b), for the words "six annas per mile", the words "forty paise per kilometre" shall be substituted; (iii) in clause (d) - (a) for the word "ten", the word "twenty" shall be substituted; (b) in the first proviso, for the word "twenty-five", the word "forty" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10 of the principal Act, for the words "six hundred", the words "eight hundred" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka (i) In Section 11 of the principal Act, for the words "one hundred and fifty", the words "two hundred and fifty" and for the words "one hundred", the words "one hundred and fifty" shall respectively be substituted. (ii) Section 11 of the principal Act shall be renumbered as sub-section (1) of that -section and after the sub-section as so renumbered, the following sub-section shall be added, namely - "(2) Any member entitled to any salary or special allowance under subsection (1) may relinquish the whole or any portion thereof at any time by intimating in writing to the Speaker in the case of a member of the Legislative Assembly and to the Chairman in the case of a member of the Legislative Council: Provided that any relinquishment made by a member in respect of any salary or special allowance may be cancelled by him at any time with prospective effect by writing to the Speaker or Chairman accordingly."
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1967_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12 of the principal Act - (i) in clause (a), for the words and brackets "one single first class fare (irrespective of the class by which the member actually travels) with incidental charges calculated at the rate of six naye-paise per mile, and for journeys by road at thirty-seven naye-paise per mile", the words and brackets "one and half times the single first class fare (irrespective of the class by which the member actually travels) and for journeys by road at forty paise per kilometre" shall be substituted; (ii) in sub-clause (i) of clause (b), for the word "sixteen", the word "twenty-five" shall be substituted; (iii) for clause (c), the following clauses shall be substituted, namely - "(c) every member shall be entitled free of charge to such accommodation in hospitals and dispensaries maintained by the State Government and to such medical attendance and treatment as may be prescribed; (cc) every member shall be provided with one free non-transferable pass which shall entitle him at any time to travel by road transport services of the Karnataka State Road Transport Corporation in any part of the State of Karnataka in which those services operate in such class of accommodation therein as may be prescribed."; (iv) in clause (d), for the words "one half of the rent payable for such accommodation", the words "such rent as may be prescribed" shall be substituted; (v) in clause (e), for the words "rupees twelve and fifty naye-paise", the words "ruees fifteen" shall be substituted; (vi) after clause (e), the following clause shall be added, namely - "(f) there shall be paid to a member who is the Chairman of any Committee of the Legislative Assembly or of the Legislative Council or of both in respect of journeys outside the State in India in connection with their duties as Chairman of the Committee, travelling allowance for journeys by train and journeys by road at the rates specified in clause (a) and for journeys by air at the rate of one and one-fifth times the single air fare paid for such journeys; and daily allowance at rupees twenty-five per diem: Provided that where one-fifth of the single air fare payable under this clause exceeds rupees thirty, the amount so payable shall be limited to rupees thirty".
