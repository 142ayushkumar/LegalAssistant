Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) (Amendment) Act, 1970_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) Act, 1959. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) Act, 1959 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1959) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) (Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) (Amendment) Act, 1970.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Tenth Day of April, 1970
Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) (Amendment) Act, 1970_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) Act, 1959 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1959),- (i) for the words "not exceeding three naye paise", the words "not exceeding six paise" shall be substituted; and (ii) in the proviso, in clause (b) - (a) for the words "Ten naye paise", the words "Fifteen paise" shall be substituted; (b) for the words "Thirteen naye paise" the words "Twenty paise" shall be substituted; (c) for the words "Twenty naye paise", the words "Thirty paise" shall be substituted; (d) for the words "Thirty-three naye paise", the words "Fifty paise" shall be substituted; and (e) for the words "Three naye paise", the words Five paise" shall be substituted.
