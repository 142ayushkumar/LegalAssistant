Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 34 of 1957), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-ninth Year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1998.1 (2) It shall come into force with effect from the First day of April, 1998. Received the assent of the Governor on the Thirtieth day of March, 1998
Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3-B of the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 34 of 1957), (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (a) in sub-section (1) - (i) for the words "Bangalore Mass Rapid Transit System", the words "investment in the Karnataka Infrastructural Development and Finance Corporation shall be substituted; (ii) the words "situated within the limits of Bangalore City Planning Area" shall be omitted; (iii) for the words "shall be chargeable", the words and figures "shall for a period of two years effective from the First day of April, 1998, be chargeable" shall be substituted; (b) the Explanation at the end shall be omitted.
Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 9 of the principal Act - (1) in sub-section (1) - (i) in clause (a), for the words "reduce or remit whether prospectively or retrospectively", the words "reduce up to fifty per cent" shall be substituted; (ii) after clause (a), the following proviso shall be inserted, namely - "Provided that the State Government may in public interest reduce or remit the stamp duty payable on an instrument executed to obtain loan - (i) by any person for an agricultural purpose as defined in the Karnataka Agricultural Credit Operations Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1975); and (ii) by a public sector undertaking, for the purpose for which it is established"; (2) after sub-section (1), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(1-A) Notwithstanding anything contained in any other law for the time being in force, no reduction or remission of stamp duty shall be allowed unless it is notified in accordance with sub-section (1)."
Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 45-A of the principal Act, for the words "or dissolution of partnership", wherever they occur, a comma, words, brackets, figures and letters "dissolution of partnership, an agreement to sell covered under sub-clause (i) of clause (e) and (f) of Article 5, a lease covered under item (iv) of sub-clause (a), item (ii) of sub-clause (b), item (ii) of sub-clause (c) of clause (1) of Article 30 or a power of attorney covered under clause (ea) or (eb) of Article 41" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In the Schedule to the principal Act - (1) in Article 5 - (a) in clause (f), for the entries in Column 3, the following shall be substituted, namely - "Four rupees for every hundred rupees or part thereof on the market value of the property which is the subject-matter of agreement, subject to a maximum of rupees four lakhs." (b) Explanation (I) shall be omitted. (2) in Article 6, in clause (2), in Column 3, the following proviso shall be inserted at the end; namely - "Provided that where a fresh instrument of hypothecation, pawn or pledge of movable property is executed for securing repayment money already advanced by way of loan between the same parties and for the same purpose and for the same amount and the duty in respect of earlier instrument has been paid, then the duty chargeable on such fresh instrument is chargeable as per clause (i) of Article 5 of this Schedule." (3) in Article 30 - (i) in clause (1) - (a) in sub-clause (a), for items (iv) to (viii), the following shall be substituted, namely - "(iv) Where the lease purports to be with or without a renewal clause, contingent or otherwise, for a period in excess of ten years or in perpetuity, or does not purport to be for any definite period Rupees live for every hundred or part thereof on the market value of the property which is the subject-matter of lease."; (b) for sub-clauses (b) and (b), the following shall be substituted, namely - "(b) Where the lease is granted for a fine or a premium or for money advanced and where no rent is reserved -  (i) Where the lease purports to be for a term not exceeding ten years The same duty, as a conveyance (clause (1) of Article 20) for a market value equal to the amount or value of such fine or premium or advance as setforth in the lease. (ii) Where the lease purports to be with or without a renewal clause contingent or otherwise for a period in excess of ten years, or in perpetuity, or does not purport to be for any definite period Rupees five for every hundred or part thereof of the market value of the property which is the subject-matter of lease. (c) Where the lease is granted for a fine or premium or for money advanced in addition to rent reserved -  (i) Where the lease purports to be for a term not exceeding ten years The same duty as a conveyance (clause (1) of Article 20) for a market value equal to the amount or value of such fine or premium or advance as setforth in the lease, in addition to the duty which would have been payable on such lease if no fine or premium or advance had been paid or delivered. (ii) Where the lease purports to be with or without a renewal clause contingent or otherwise for a period in excess of ten years, or in perpetuity, or does not purport to be for any definite period. Rupees five for every hundred or part thereof on the market value of the property which is the subject-matter of lease:  Provided that in any case when an agreement to lease is stamped with advalorem stamp required for a lease and a lease in pursuance of such agreement is subsequently executed the duty on such lease shall not exceed rupees fifteen."; (ii) in clause (2), in Column 3 - (a) in the proviso, for the words "Provided that", the words "Provided further that" shall be substituted; (b) before the proviso so amended, the following proviso shall be inserted, namely - "Provided that in respect of lease of industrial machinery the maximum duty chargeable shall be rupees ten thousand." (4) in Article 34, in clause (b), for the entries under Column 3, the following shall be substituted, namely - "Rupees three for every hundred rupees or part thereof for the amount secured by such deed subject to a maximum of rupees three lakhs."; (5) in Article 41, after clause (ea), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(eb) When given to a person other than the father, mother, wife or husband, son or daughter in relation to the executant authorising such person to sell immovable property Two rupees for every one hundred rupees or part thereof on the market value of the property which is the subject-matter of power of attorney:  Provided that the duty paid on such instrument is adjustable towards the duty payable on the instrument of sale or transfer executed subsequently in favour of either the attorney holder or any other person."
