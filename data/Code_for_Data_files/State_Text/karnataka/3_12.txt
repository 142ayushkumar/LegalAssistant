Karnataka Children (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Children Act, 1964. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Children Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 19 of 1964); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Children (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Children (Amendment) Act, 1976.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixth day of December, 1976
Karnataka Children (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 10 of the Karnataka Children Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 19 of 1964) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for the full-stop occurring at the end of the sub-section, a semi-colon shall be substituted and after the semi-colon, the following words, brackets and figures shall be added, namely - "if a juvenile Court has been constituted for the area, the child shall be tried by such Court, and if no such juvenile Court has been constituted, the child and the adult shall be tried separately, the child by the Court competent to exercise the powers of a juvenile Court in the area under sub-section (2) of Section 8 and the adult by the Court having jurisdiction to try the offence".
Karnataka Children (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 10 of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely - "10-A. Adult to be committed to sessions in a case triable by a Session Court -In the trial of a case in which a child is being tried together with an adult in accordance with the provisions of this Act if it appears to the Magistrate trying the case that offence is triable exclusively by a Court of Session, he shall separate the case in respect of the child from that in respect of the adult and shall commit the case in respect of the adult alone to the Court of Sessions for trial and shall proceed with the trial of the case in respect of the child, notwithstanding anything in the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973, or any other law for the time being in force".
Karnataka Children (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 26 of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely - "26-A. Management of Remand Homes - For the control and management of every Remand Home established under sub-section (1) of Section 26, a Superintendent and a Committee of Visitors shall be appointed by the State Government and such Superintendent shall be deemed to be the Manager of the Remand Home for the purposes of this Act".
