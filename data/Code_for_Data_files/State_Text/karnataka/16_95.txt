Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1974. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 32 of 1974), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Amendment) Act, 19921 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the Eighth day of October, 1992. First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, on the 4th day of February, 1993, Received the assent of the Governor on the Fourth day of February, 1993
Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 32 of 1974) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in clause (e),- (i) after sub-clause (vi), the following sub-clause shall be inserted, namely - "(vii) a University established under the Karnataka State Universities Act, 1976 (Karntaka Act 28 of 1976) or the University of Agricultural Sciences Act, 1963 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1963)"; (ii) in the Explanation, for items (c), (d) and (e), the following items shall be substituted, namely - "(c) an Improvement Board established under the Karnataka Improvement Boards Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 11 of 1976); (d) a Zilla Parishad or a Mandal Panchayat established under the Karnataka Zilla Parishads. Taluk Panchayat Samithies, Mandal Panchayats and Nyaya Panchayats Act, 1983 (Karnataka Act 20 of 1985); (e) the Bangalore Development Authority constituted under the Bangalore Development Authority Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1976); (f) an Urban Development Authority constituted under the Karnataka Urban Development Authorities Act, 1987 (Karnataka Act 34 of 1987)".
Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In item (ii) of clause (a) of Section 3 of the principal Act, after the words "the local authority" the words "or such officer not below the rank of Group B officer of the State Civil Services", shall be inserted.
Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Amendment) Ordinance, 1992 (Karnataka Ordinance 14 of 1992) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
