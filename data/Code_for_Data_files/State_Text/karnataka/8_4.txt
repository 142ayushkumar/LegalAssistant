Karnataka Health Cess (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Health Cess Act, 1962. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Health Cess Act, 1962 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1962); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Nineteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Health Cess (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Health Cess (Amendment) Act, 1968.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Thirtieth day of October, 1968
Karnataka Health Cess (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Karnataka Health Cess Act, 1962 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1962) (hereinafter referred to as the  principal Act), for the words "naye Paise", the words "Paise", shall be substituted.
Karnataka Health Cess (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 4 of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely:- "4-A. Local authorities entitled to cost of collection of health cess -Where the health cess under this Act is recovered by a local authority, such local authority shall be entitled to deduct ten per cent of the amount recovered as the cost of collection, and the balance shall be paid to the State Government".
Karnataka Health Cess (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the principal Act, for the words "naye Paisa" wherever they occur, the word "Paisa" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Health Cess (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Schedule A to the principal Act - (i) the explanation to Item 1 shall be omitted; (ii) Item 2 shall be omitted; (iii) in Item 3, for the words "under any law for the time being in force", the words and figures "under Section 4 of the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958" shall be and shall always be deemed to have been substituted.
