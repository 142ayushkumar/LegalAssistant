Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) Act, 1974. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act No. 36 of 1974) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1976.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the sixteenth day of December, 1975. Received the assent of the Governor on the Fourth day of March, 1976
Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 13 of the Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act No. 36 of 1974) for the words "one year", the words "two years" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Ordinance, 1975 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 23 of 1975) is hereby repealed.
