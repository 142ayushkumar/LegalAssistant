Karnataka Contract Carriages (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Contract Carriages (Acquisition) Act, 1976. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Contract Carriages (Acquisition) Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 21 of 1976) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-eighth Year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka Contract Carriages (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Contract Carriages (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1997.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the 12th day of December, 1996. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-eighth day of February, 1997
Karnataka Contract Carriages (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 24 of the Karnataka Contract Carriages (Acquisition) Act, 1976, (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act), after clause (iii), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(iv) any public service vehicle including any vehicle covered by permit issued under Section 72 (including reserved stage carriages) or under sub-section (9) of Section 88 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, to which a permit under sub-section (8) of Section 88 of the said Act is issued".
Karnataka Contract Carriages (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Contract Carriages (Acquisition) (Amendment) Ordinance, 1996 (Karnataka Ordinance 5 of 1996) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
