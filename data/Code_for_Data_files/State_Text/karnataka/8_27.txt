Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners Act, 1961. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1961) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirtieth year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1979.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Second day of November, 1979
Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 9 of the Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1961) (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act) - (1) in the heading, for the words "Disability" the words "Removal of members" shall be substituted; (2) at the end of clause (b), the word "or" shall be inserted; (3) after clause (b), the following clauses shall be inserted, namely - "(c) abuses his position as a member so as to render his continuance as a member detrimental to the interest of the Board; or (d) is otherwise unfit to continue as a member". (4) for the words "or the Board, as the case may be, shall declare his office vacant", the following shall be substituted, namely - "may remove such member from office: Provided that no member shall be removed from office on the grounds specified in clause (c) or clause (d) without giving such member an opportunity to submit his explanation".
Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 18 of the principal Act, for the word "fifteen" the word "thirty" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 20 of the principal Act, for the word "fifteen" the word "thirty" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 24 of the principal Act, for the word "two" the word "ten" shall be substituted.
