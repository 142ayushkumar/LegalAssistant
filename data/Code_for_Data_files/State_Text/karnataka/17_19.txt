Karnataka Regularisation of Unauthorised Constructions in Urban Areas (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Regularisation of Unauthorised Constructions in Urban Areas Act, 1991. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Regularisation of Unauthorised Constructions in Urban Areas Act, 1991 (Karnataka Act 29 of 1991) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Regularisation of Unauthorised Constructions in Urban Areas (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Regularisation of Unauthorised Constructions in Urban Areas (Amendment) Act, 19941 . (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Thirteenth day of September, 1994
Karnataka Regularisation of Unauthorised Constructions in Urban Areas (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Karnataka Regularisation of Unauthorised Constructions in Urban Areas Act, 1991 (Karnataka Act 29 of 1991) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for the words "within two years of the commencement of this Act" the words and figures "before the First day of October, 1994" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
Karnataka Regularisation of Unauthorised Constructions in Urban Areas (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2) - (i) after clause (i) the following proviso shall be inserted, namely - "Provided that in the case of Screening Committees in the Districts having Municipal Corporations, Commissioner of the Municipal Corporation shall be the Chairman." (ii) in clause (v), the words "Commissioner, Municipal Corporation or" shall be omitted.
