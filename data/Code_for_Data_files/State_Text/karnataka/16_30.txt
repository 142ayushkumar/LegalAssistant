Karnataka Police (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Police Act, 1963. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Police Act, 1963 (Karnataka Act 4 of 1964) for the purpose hereinafter appearing; Be, it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the forty-ninth year of Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Police (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Police (Amendment) Act, 1998.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the twenty-fifth day of October, 1997. Received the assent of the Governor on the First day of April, 1998 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Police (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 107 of the Karnataka Police Act, 1963 (Karnataka Act 4 of 1964) (hereinafter referred to as "principal Act"), for the words "two hundred rupees", the words "five hundred rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Police (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Police (Amendment) Ordinance, 1997 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 5 of 1997) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
