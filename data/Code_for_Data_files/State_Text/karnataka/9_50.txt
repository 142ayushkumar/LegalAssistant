Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1961_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) Act, 1957 Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twelfth Year of the Republic of India as follows-
Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1961.1 Received the assent of the President on the Thirty-first day of October, 1961
Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act - (1) for clause (a) of sub-section (1), the following clause shall be substituted, namely - "(a) Deputy Commissioner means the Deputy Commissioner of a District or such other officer not below the rank of an Assistant Commissioner as the Government may appoint to discharge the functions of the Deputy Commissioner under this Act"; (2) sub-section (2) shall be omitted.
Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4 of the principal Act - (i) for the word "Collector" wherever it occurs, the words "Deputy Commissioner" shall be substituted; (ii) in the proviso to sub-section (1) for the words "five hundred rupees per acre", the words "the estimated increase in the capital value of the land" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 6 of the principal Act, for the word "Collector", wherever it occurs the words "Deputy Commissioner" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 10 of the principal Act - (i) the proviso shall be omitted; (ii) in clause (c) - (a) for the word "Collector", the words "Deputy Commissioner" shall be substituted; (b) after the words "the authority by which such water rates shall be fixed" the words "and the water rate so fixed may be in lieu of the water rate, if any, payable under any other law in force" shall be added.
Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 10 of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely- "10-A. Application of this Act to the Tungabhadra Project -The provisions of this Act relating to the levy of water rate shall be applicable in respect of any water stored, consumed, distributed or sold by the authority established under Section 66 of the Andhra State Act, 1953 (Central Act 30 of 1953): Provided that no rule or order for the fixation of the water rate and other incidents in respect of such water rate shall be made except with the previous consent of the President of India."
Karnataka Irrigation (Levy of Betterment Contribution and Water Rate) (Amendment) Act, 1961_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 11 of the principal Act, after the words "carry out the purposes of this Act", the words "and such rules may be general for all irrigation works or may be special for one or more irrigation works, as may be specified in such rules" shall be added.
