Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1962) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-ninth year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1998.1 (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint. Received the assent of the Governor on the second day of December, 1998
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 61 of the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1962) (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act) for the words and figures "from the date the Certificate under Section 55 is issued", the words, brackets, figures and letters "from the date of the final order passed by the Tribunal under sub-section (4) or sub-section (5) or sub-section (5-A) of Section 48-A" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 77-A of the Principal Act - (a) in sub-section (1) - (i) for the words "Special Deputy Commissioner", the words "or any other officer authorised by the State Government in this behalf" shall be substituted. (ii) after the words "he may", the words, brackets and figures "within one year from the date of commencement of the Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1997" shall be inserted. (iii) at the end, the following proviso shall be inserted, namely - "Provided that the land so granted together with the land already held by such person shall not exceed 2 hectares of D class of land or its equivalent thereto."
