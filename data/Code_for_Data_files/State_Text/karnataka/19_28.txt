Karnataka Tax on Luxuries (Hotels and Lodging Houses) (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Tax on Luxuries (Hotels and Lodging Houses) Act, 1979. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Tax on Luxuries (Hotels and Lodging Houses) Act, 1979 for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Fortieth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Tax on Luxuries (Hotels and Lodging Houses) (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This act may be called the Karnataka Tax on Luxuries (Hotels and Lodging Houses) (Amendment) Act, 1989.1 2 (2) It shall come into force on the first day of April, 1989. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000. Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixteenth day of April, 1989
Karnataka Tax on Luxuries (Hotels and Lodging Houses) (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Karnataka Tax on Luxuries (Hotels and Lodging Houses) Act, 1979 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1979), for sub-section (1), excluding the provisos, the following shall be substituted, namely- "(1) Subject to the provisions of this Act, there shall be levied and collected a tax on the luxury provided in a hotel in respect of every room (to be known as Luxury Tax) at the following rates, namely - (a) Where the charges for lodging per room per day are not less than thirty five rupees but less than one hundred rupees. Five percent of such charges. (b) Where the charges for lodging per room per day are not less than one hundred rupees but less than three hundred rupees. Ten per cent of such charges. (c) Where the charges for lodging per room per day are not less than three hundred rupees. Fifteen per cent of such charges.
