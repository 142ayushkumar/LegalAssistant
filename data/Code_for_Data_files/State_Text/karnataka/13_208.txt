Mysore Legislature (Prohibition of Simultaneous Membership) Act, 1956_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to provide for the vacation by a person who is chosen a member of both House of the Legislature of the State of Mysore of his seat in one House or the other,  WHEREAS it is necessary to provide, in accordance with Article 190 of the Constitution of India for the vacation by a person who is chosen a member of both HOUSES of the Legislature of the State of Mysore of his seat in one House or the ether: BE it enacted by the Mysore State Legislature in the Seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Mysore Legislature (Prohibition of Simultaneous Membership) Act, 1956_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Mysore Legislature (Prohibition of Simultaneous Membership) Act, 1956.
Mysore Legislature (Prohibition of Simultaneous Membership) Act, 1956_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In this Act unless there is anything repugnant in the subject or context- (1) " Assembly " means the Legislative Assembly of the State of Mysore, (2) " Council " means the Legislative Council of the State of Mysore ; (3) " House" means the Assembly or the Council.
Mysore Legislature (Prohibition of Simultaneous Membership) Act, 1956_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) Any person who is chosen a member of both the Assembly the Council and who has not taken his seat in either House may, by notice in where signed by him and delivered to the Secretary of each of the Houses or to any person authorised by the Governor in this behalf, within ten days from the date of publication in the Mysore Gazette of the declarations that he has been so chosen such publications have been made on different dates, within ten days from the latter of such dates, intimate in which of the Houses he wishes to serve, and thereunder his Seat in the House in which he does not wish to serve shall become vacant. (2) In default of such intimation within the aforesaid period, his seat in the Council shall, at the expiration of that period, become vacant. (3) Any intimation given under sub-section (1) shall be final and irrevocable
Mysore Legislature (Prohibition of Simultaneous Membership) Act, 1956_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka (1) If a person who is already a member of the Assembly and has taken his seat in the Assembly is chosen a member of the Council, his seat in the Assembly shall, on the publication in the Mysore Gazette, of the declaration that be has been so chosen, become vacant. (2) If a person who is already a member of the Council and has taken his seat in the Council is chosen a member of the Assembly, his seat in the Council shall, on the publication in the Mysore Gazette of the declaration that has been so chosen, become vacant.
