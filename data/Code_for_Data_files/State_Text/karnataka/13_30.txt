Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances Act, 1956. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1981.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Eighth day of April, 1981 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) in sub-section (2), for the words, "seven hundred and fifty rupees", the words, "nine hundred and fifty rupees", shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6-C of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), for the words, "seven hundred and fifty rupees", the words, "nine hundred and fifty rupees", shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 9 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), for the words, "Seven hundred and fifty rupees", the words, "nine hundred and fifty rupees", shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12 of the principal Act in clause (b) of sub-section (2), for the words, "one rupee", the words, "one rupee and fifty paise" shall be substituted.
