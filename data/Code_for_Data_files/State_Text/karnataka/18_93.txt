Karnataka Silkworm Seed and Cocoon (Regulation of Production, Supply and Distribution) (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Silkworm Seed and Cocoon (Regulation of Production, Supply and Distribution) Act, 1959. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Silkworm Seed and Cocoon (Regulation of Production, Supply and Distribution) Act, 1959 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1960), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirtieth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Silkworm Seed and Cocoon (Regulation of Production, Supply and Distribution) (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Silkworm Seed and Cocoon (Regulation of Production, Supply and Distribution) (Amendment) Act, 19791 2 . (2) It shall come into force at come. First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, on the Sixth day of November, 1979, Received the assent of the President on the Second day of November, 1979 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Silkworm Seed and Cocoon (Regulation of Production, Supply and Distribution) (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the long title and preamble of the Karnataka Silkworm Seed and Cocoon (Regulation of Production, Supply and Distribution) Act, 1959 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1960) (hereinafter referred to as the " Principal Act"), for the words "and distribution" and "and cocoon" wherever they occur, the words "distribution and sale" and "cocoon and silk yarn" shall respectively be substituted.
Karnataka Silkworm Seed and Cocoon (Regulation of Production, Supply and Distribution) (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 1 of the Principal Act, for the words "and cocoon" and "and distribution", the words "cocoon and silk yarn" and "distribution and sale" shall respectively be substituted.
Karnataka Silkworm Seed and Cocoon (Regulation of Production, Supply and Distribution) (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Principal Act - (1) after clause (c), the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(ca) "Dupion Silk Yarn" means silk yarn reeled out of double cocoons;" (2) for clause (f) and explanation thereto, the following clause shall be substituted, namely - "(f) "Licensed Trader" means a person who is licensed to purchase silk yarn in a silk exchange;" (3) after clause (i), the following clauses shall be inserted, namely - "(i-a) "Reeler" means a person in charge of a reeling establishment and carrying on the business of reeling cocoons; Explanation - In this clause - (a) "Reeling Establishment" means an establishment where silk is reeled from cocoons with the help of any machine or contrivance of any kind worked by power or without power; (b) "Power" means any form of energy which is mechanically transmitted and is not generated by human or animal agency and includes electrical energy; (i-b) "Silk Exchange" means a silk exchange established under Section 10-A for the sale or purchase of silk yarn and includes silk stores;" (4) after clause (k), the following clauses shall be inserted, namely - "(1) "Silk Yarn", means silk reeled out of cocoons and includes twisted silk yarn, dupion silk yarn, spun silk yarn and noil silk yarn; (m) "Spun Silk Yarn" means silk yam spun from pierced or spoilt cocoons, fluff from cocoons, pieces of silk noils or other silk waste; (n) "Twisted Silk Yarn" means two or more silk yarn twisted together and includes warps; and (o) "Twister" means a person in charge of an establishment where twisted silk yarn is produced".
Karnataka Silkworm Seed and Cocoon (Regulation of Production, Supply and Distribution) (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 5 of the Principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely - "5-A. Regulation of possession of silk yarn - No person shall be in possession of silk yarn in excess of the prescribed quantity unless he is - (a) a reeler; (b) a licensed trader; (c) a twister; (d) a weaver; or (e) person authorised in writing by the prescribed officer".
Karnataka Silkworm Seed and Cocoon (Regulation of Production, Supply and Distribution) (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 8 of the Principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely - "8-A. Regulation of sale and purchase of silk yarn, etc - After a silk exchange is established under Section 10-A - (1) (a) no reeler or twister shall, sell or agree to sell silk yarn reeled, or as the case may be, twisted by him; (b) no person other than a licensed trader shall purchase or agree to purchase silk yarn, from a reeler or a twister, except in such silk exchange and except in accordance with such conditions and in such manner as may be prescribed; (2) no person shall, except in such silk exchange use or permit the use or assist, in the use of, any building, room, tent, enclosure, vehicle, vessel or place for the sale of silk yarn by or purchase of silk yarn from, a reeler or a twister or in any manner aid or abet the sale or purchase of silk yarn".
Karnataka Silkworm Seed and Cocoon (Regulation of Production, Supply and Distribution) (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 10 of the Principal Act - (1) in clause (b), for the words "one each of licensed buyers in charge of charaka establishments and filature establishments", the words "three representatives of reelers" shall be substituted; (2) in clause (d), for the words "any licensed buyer", the words "any reeler" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Silkworm Seed and Cocoon (Regulation of Production, Supply and Distribution) (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 10 of the Principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely - "10-A. Establishment of Silk Exchange - (1) The Government may, by notification - (a) specify the place or places at which the silk exchanges shall be located; (b) appoint for each silk exchange, a Silk Market Officer and constitute a committee consisting of four representatives of reelers, two representatives of twisters and two representatives of traders with the Silk Market Officer as Chairman for regulating generally the procedure for the conduct of business in the silk exchange and for the performance of such functions as may be prescribed. (2) All transactions involving sale or purchase of silk yarn in a silk exchange shall be by open auction, the payment of price shall be in cash and the silk yam shall be sold by metric weight".
Karnataka Silkworm Seed and Cocoon (Regulation of Production, Supply and Distribution) (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 11 of the Principal Act, in sub-section (1), in clause (d), for the words "silkworm cocoon" in the three places where they occur, the words "cocoons or silk yarn" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Silkworm Seed and Cocoon (Regulation of Production, Supply and Distribution) (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 10--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12 of the Principal Act - (1) after sub-section (2-A), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(2-B) Any person who contravenes the provisions of Section 8-A, shall on conviction be punished with imprisonment for a term which may extend to three years and with fine which may extend to two thousand rupees: Provided that in the absence of special reasons to the contrary such imprisonment shall not be less than six months and fine shall not be less than one thousand rupees"; (2) in sub-section (4), after the brackets, figure and letter "(2-A)", the brackets, figure and letter "(2- B)" shall be inserted. (3) sub-section (5), shall be omitted.
Karnataka Silkworm Seed and Cocoon (Regulation of Production, Supply and Distribution) (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 11--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12-B of the Principal Act, for the words, brackets, figures and letter "sub-section (2-A)", the words, brackets, figures and letters "sub-sections (2-A) and (2-B)" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Silkworm Seed and Cocoon (Regulation of Production, Supply and Distribution) (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 12--> State(s): Karnataka For Section 13 of the Principal Act, the following section shall be substituted, namely - "13. Suspension or cancellation of licence, forfeiture of property and penalty, etc - (1) The Director of Sericulture in Karnataka may - (a) without prejudice to any punishment under Section 12, after giving the person concerned a reasonable opportunity of being heard, if satisfied that such person has contravened the provisions of Sections 3, 7, 8 or 8-A, suspend or cancel the licence granted to such person; (b) after giving the person concerned a reasonable opportunity of being heard direct that any - (i) silkworm seed, cocoons or silk yarn or any receptacle, apparatus, package or covering containing the same or any other article in respect of which such person has contravened any of the provisions of this Act or rules made thereunder, shall be forfeited to the Government; and (ii) such person shall pay a penalty upto five thousand rupees but not exceeding the value of the property in relation to which the provisions of this Act or rules have been contravened. (2) No person on whom the penalty is imposed under sub-clause (ii) of clause (b) of sub-section (1) shall be liable for prosecution in respect of the same facts for an offence under this Act. (3) Any person aggrieved by the order of suspension or cancellation of license, or forfeiture of property or imposition of penalty under sub-section (1) may appeal to the Sessions Judge of the District within such time, and in such manner as may be prescribed, and the decision of the Sessions Judge on such appeal shall be final".
Karnataka Silkworm Seed and Cocoon (Regulation of Production, Supply and Distribution) (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 13--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 17 of the Principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely - "17-A. Establishment of Development and Price Stabilisation Fund - (1) There shall be constituted in the State of Karnataka a fund called the Karnataka Silkworm, Cocoon and Silk Yarn Development and Price Stabilisation Fund. (2) All moneys received by way of market fees, licence fees, or other fees or charges and all grants or contributions made by the Government shall form part of the Fund established under sub-section (1). (3) The amount at the credit of the said fund shall not be expended except for the purpose of stabilizing the prices of cocoons and silk yarn and for the development of rearing of silkworm seed, reeling and twisting of silk yarn and matters connected therewith and such other matters as may be prescribed".
Karnataka Silkworm Seed and Cocoon (Regulation of Production, Supply and Distribution) (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 14--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 18 of the Principal Act, for clause (h), the following clauses shall be substituted, namely - "(h) the market fee payable by the rearers and the reelers in respect of cocoons sold and purchased in the cocoon market, such fee not exceeding two per cent of the price of the cocoons; (h-a) the market fee payable by the reelers, traders and twisters in respect of silk yarn sold or purchased by them in the silk exchange, such fee not exceeding two per cent of the price of silk yarn; (h-b) amenities and facilities to be provided in the silk exchange including settlement of disputes between the sellers and purchasers of the silk yarn; (h-c) the sitting fee and other allowances payable to the members of the committees constituted under Sections 10 and 10-A; (h-d) matters relating to the Fund constituted under Section 17-A".
