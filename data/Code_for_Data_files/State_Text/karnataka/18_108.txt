Karnataka Societies Registration (Amendment) Act, 1975_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Societies Registration Act, 1960. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Societies Registration Act, 1960 (Karnataka Act 17 of 1960). Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Societies Registration (Amendment) Act, 1975_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Societies Registration (Amendment) Act, 19751 2 . (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint. First published in the Karnataka Gazette, on the Fifteenth day of May, 1975, Received the assent of the Governor on the Third day of May, 1975 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000.
Karnataka Societies Registration (Amendment) Act, 1975_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Societies Registration Act, 1960 (Karnataka Act 17 of 1960) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), after clause (e), the following clause shall be added, namely - "(f) "Year" means the year ending on the 31st day of December or in the case of any society or class of societies the accounts of which are made up to any other date with the previous sanction of the Registrar, the year ending with such date".
Karnataka Societies Registration (Amendment) Act, 1975_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 9 of the principal Act, for the words "ten rupees", the words "twenty rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Societies Registration (Amendment) Act, 1975_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 10 of the principal Act, for the words "two rupees", the words "ten rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Societies Registration (Amendment) Act, 1975_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 11 of the principal Act, the word "calendar" in the two places where it occurs shall be omitted.
Karnataka Societies Registration (Amendment) Act, 1975_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12 of the principal Act  (a) in sub-section (2), the word "calendar" in the two places where it occurs shall be omitted; (b) after sub-section (2), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(3) In the case of a society the accounts of which are made up with the previous sanction of the Registrar to any date other than the 31st day of December, the first balance sheet and the first income and expenditure account of such society after such previous sanction is accorded shall, for the purposes of sub-section (2), be for such period as the Registrar may specify in the order according previous sanction".
Karnataka Societies Registration (Amendment) Act, 1975_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 13 of the principal Act, for the words "one rupee", the words "two rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Societies Registration (Amendment) Act, 1975_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 24 of the principal Act for the words "fifty paise", the words "one rupee" shall be substituted.
