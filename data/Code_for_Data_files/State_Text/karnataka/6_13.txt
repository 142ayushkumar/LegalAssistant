Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Forest Act, 1963. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Forest Act, 1963 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1964); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1978.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Twenty-seventh day of April, 1978. Received the assent of the Governor on the Eighth day of August, 1978
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 28 of the Karnataka Forest Act, 1963 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1964), (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) after the first proviso to sub-section (1), the following further proviso shall be inserted, namely - "Provided further that no such resolution shall be necessary where the proposal relates to regularisation of unauthorised occupation of any reserved forest or portion thereof, if such occupation was prior to the date of commencement of the Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1978."
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Ordinance, 1978 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 3 of 1978) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
