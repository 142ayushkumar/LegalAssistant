Karnataka Stamp (Second Amendment) Act, 1995_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957, (Karnataka Act 34 of 1957) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-sixth year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Karnataka Stamp (Second Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Stamp (Second Amendment) Act, 1995.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the President on sixth day of December, 1996 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Stamp (Second Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 28-A of the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 34 of 1957) after Section 28-A, the following new section shall be inserted, namely:- "28-B. Restriction to registration - Notwithstanding anything contained in any other law for the time being in force, where the transfer of ownership of a motor vehicle is chargeable with duty under sub-clause (a) of clause (3) of Article 20 of the Schedule to this Act, the registering authority under the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 shall not cause the transfer of ownership of such vehicle to be entered in the registration certificate under Section 50 of the Motor Vehicle Act, 1988 unless payment of such duty has been made by the person concerned in respect of transfer of ownership of such vehicle".
