Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1962). Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1977.1 (2) This section and Section 11 shall come into force at once. Section 2 shall be deemed to have come into force on the Thirty-first day of December, 1976 and the remaining sections shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of March, 1974. Received the assent of the Governor on the Second day of May, 1977
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 48-A of the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1962) (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act) for the figures, letters and words "31st day of December, 1976", the figures, letters and words "30th day of June, 1977" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 53 of the Principal Act, for sub-sections (1) and (1-A), the following sub-sections shall be substituted, namely - "(1) The amount of premium in respect of the land of which a tenant or sub-tenant entitled to be registered as occupant under Section 45 shall be payable to the State Government by the tenant or sub-tenant - (i) in a lump sum where the amount payable under Section 51 - (a) does not exceed two thousand rupees; or (b) is payable to a minor, widow, a woman who has never been married or a person who is subject to physical or mental disability; or (c) is payable to an institution referred to in Section 106; (ii) either in a lump sum or in instalments over a period not exceeding twenty years with interest at five and a half per cent per annum as the tenant or the sub-tenant may intimate to the Tahsildar, in other cases: Provided that where payment is in instalments, two thousand rupees shall be paid as the first instalment and the balance in equated annual instalments. (1-A) Every tenant who is registered as an occupant shall be entitled to get assistance from the State Land Development Bank or from a Credit Agency as defined in the Karnataka Agricultural Credit Operations and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1975) for the payment of the premium in accordance with such rules as may be prescribed: Provided that - (a) in cases where the amount is payable in lump sum under Section 51 or in the shape of annuities under Section 106, the State Land Development Bank or the Credit Agency shall disburse the loan in a lump sum; and (b) in other cases, the disbursement of the loan shall be in instalments equal to and contemporaneous with the instalments of the premium."
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 53 of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely - "53-A. Establishment of a separate fund - (1) There shall be constituted for the State of Karnataka an annuity fund. (2) The amount of premia collected from the tenants or sub-tenants of lands belonging to the institutions referred to in Section 106 shall first be credited to the Consolidated Fund of the State and under appropriation duly made by law in this behalf, be entered in and transferred to the Karnataka Annuity Fund and the amount at credit in the said Fund may be invested in such securities or Scheduled Banks as the State Government may direct. The income accruing therefrom shall also form part of the said Fund. (3) The annuities payable to the said institutions shall be paid from out of the said Fund."
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 54 of the Principal Act, in sub-section (1), the portion beginning with the words "and such failure" and ending with the words and figures "under Section 55" shall be omitted.
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka For Section 55 of the Principal Act, the following section shall be substituted, namely - "55. Issue of certificate of registration - On payment of the premium in lump sum or the first instalment thereof, as the case may be, the Tahsildar shall issue a certificate that the tenant has been registered as an occupant. The certificate shall be conclusive evidence of such registration."
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka Section 56 of the Principal Act shall be omitted.
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka Section 59 of the Principal Act shall be omitted.
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 77 of the Principal Act, the word and figures "Section 59" shall be omitted.
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 10--> State(s): Karnataka In clause (A) of Section 112 of the Principal Act, in sub-clause (n), the words "to refund amount and to condone default in payment of premium" shall be omitted.
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 11--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Land Reforms (Fifth Amendment) Ordinance, 1976 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 30 of 1976) and the Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Ordinance, 1977 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 3 of 1977) are hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the Principal Act as amended by the said Ordinances shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the Principal Act as amended by this Act.
