Karnataka Civil Services (Classification and Scale of Pay of Non-Graduate Junior Engineers of the Public Works Department) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Civil services (Classification and Scale of pay of Non-Graduate Junior Engineers of the Public Works Department) Act, 1975. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Civil services (Classification and Scale of pay of Non-Graduate Junior Engineers of the Public Works Department) Act, 1975 (Karnataka Act 9 of 1975); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Civil Services (Classification and Scale of Pay of Non-Graduate Junior Engineers of the Public Works Department) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Civil Services (Classification and Scale of Pay of Non-Graduate Junior Engineers of the Public Works Department) (Amendment) Act, 1976.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the First day of December, 1976
Karnataka Civil Services (Classification and Scale of Pay of Non-Graduate Junior Engineers of the Public Works Department) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In clause (i) of sub-section (1) of Section 2 of the Karnataka Civil Services (Classification and Scale of Pay of Non-Graduate Junior Engineers of the Public Works Department) Act, 1975 (Karnataka Act 9 of 1975), for the figures, letters and word 1st November, 1956", the figures, letters and word "2nd November, 1956", shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
Karnataka Civil Services (Classification and Scale of Pay of Non-Graduate Junior Engineers of the Public Works Department) (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Civil Services (Classification and Scale of Pay of Non-Graduate Junior Engineers of the Public Works Department) (Amendment) Ordinance, 1976 is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
