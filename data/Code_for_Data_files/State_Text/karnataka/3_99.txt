Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 1972_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation Act, 1958. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act No. 16 of 1958). Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 1972_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act 19721 . (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixteenth day of January, 1973 (First published in the Karnataka Gazette on the Twenty-fifth day of January, 1973)
Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 1972_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Schedule II to the Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act No. 16 of 1958), in clause (s) of Article 11, for the words Twenty-five rupees, the words One hundred rupees shall be substituted.
