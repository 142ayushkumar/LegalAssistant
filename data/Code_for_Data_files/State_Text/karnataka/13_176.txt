Mysore Contract (Extension to Agricultural Land) Act, 1954_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act for extending the operation of the Indian Contract Act, 1872, to contracts relating to agricultural land. Whereas, it is expedient to extend the operation of the Indian Contract Act, 1872 (Central Act IX of 1872), to contracts relating to agricultural land in the whole of the State of Mysore except Bellary District. Be it enacted by the Mysore State Legislature in the Fifty Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Mysore Contract (Extension to Agricultural Land) Act, 1954_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Mysore Contract (Extension to Agricultural Land) Act, 1954.1 (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Mysore except Bellary District. (3) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Rajpramukh on the Twentieth day of November, 1954
Mysore Contract (Extension to Agricultural Land) Act, 1954_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka The Indian Contract Act, 1872 (Central Act IX of 1872), shall apply to contracts relating to agricultural land in the same manner and to the same extent as the said Act applies to contracts other than those relating to agricultural land and, be in force in the whole of the State of Mysore except Bellary District.
Mysore Contract (Extension to Agricultural Land) Act, 1954_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka The Contract Act, 1872 (Mysore Act IX of 1872), is hereby repealed.
