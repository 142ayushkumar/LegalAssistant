Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1961. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 9 of 1962). Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1981.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-ninth day of September, 1981
Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 16 of the Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 9 of 1962) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for the words, brackets and figures "within the period of four years from the date on which the Registrar is appointed under sub-section (1) of Section 14", the words, figures and letters "before the expiry of the 31st day of December 1981" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 18 of the principal Act, for the words, brackets and figures, "within a period of four years from the date of appointment of the Registrar under sub-section (1) of Section 14", the words, figures and letters, "before the expiry of the 31st day of December 1981", shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 21 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), for the words, brackets and figures, "four years from the date on which the Registrar is appointed under sub-section (1) of Section 14" the words, figures and letters "the 31st day of December, 1981", shall be substituted.
