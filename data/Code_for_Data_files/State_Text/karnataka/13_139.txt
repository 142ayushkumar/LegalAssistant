Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Municipalities Act, 1964. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Municipalities Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1964), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 1983.1 2 (2) All sections of this Act except clause (b) of Section 5 shall be deemed to have come into force on the twenty fourth day of February, 1983 and clause (b) of Section 5, shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of March, 1983. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty sixth day of April, 1983 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 11 of the Karnataka Municipalities Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1964) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (1) for sub-section (4), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely - "(4) As nearly as may be twenty per cent of the seats in each municipal council shall be reserved for women." (2) Sub-section (6) shall be omitted.
Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 13 of the Principal Act - (1) in sub-section (1) - (a) the words "after previous publication" shall be omitted; (b) in clause (c), for the words "not less than three and not more than five", the words "one or more" shall be substituted; (2) After sub-section (1), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(1-A). No notification under sub-section (1) shall be called in question in any court of law."
Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 14 of the Principal Act - (1) in sub-section (1), after the words "include in a division of a municipality", the words, figures and brackets "along with the additional list of voters prepared under sub-section (1-A) relatable to such division" shall be inserted; (2) after sub-section (1), the following sub-section shall be a inserted, namely - "(1-A). There shall be prepared and revised by the prescribed authority in the prescribed manner and at prescribed intervals an additional list of voters for each municipality. Every person who is ordinarily resident within the municipality and is not less than eighteen years of age on the first day of January of the year in which it is so prepared or revised but excluding a person who was not less than twenty one years of age on the first day of January of the year in which the electoral roll referred to in sub-section (1) was last revised shall be eligible to be included in the additional list of voters so prepared. The provisions of Section 16 of the Representation of the People Act, 1950 (Central Act 43 of 1950), shall, mutatis mutandis, apply for the purpose of this sub-section."
Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 323 of the Principal Act, in sub-section (1) - (a) the words "and after previous publication" shall be omitted; (b) after the words "may by notification" the words "and after previous publication" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Municipalities (Amendment) Ordinance, 1983 (Karnataka Ordinance 1 of 1983), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
