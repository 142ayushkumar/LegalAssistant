Karnataka Municipalities and Other Law (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Municipalities Act, 1964 and the Karnataka Municipal Corporations Act, 1976. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Municipalities Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1964) and the Karnataka Municipal Corporations Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1977) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-ninth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Municipalities and Other Law (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Municipalities and Other Law (Amendment) Act, 1998.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the President on the Ninth day of June, 1998 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Municipalities and Other Law (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka [x x x x x]
Karnataka Municipalities and Other Law (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In the Karnataka Municipal Corporations Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1977), in Section 7, in sub-section (1), in clause (c), the words "and who are registered as electors within the city" shall be omitted.
