Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances Act, 1956. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1957) Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Nineteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1968.1 2 Received the assent of the Governor on the Eleventh day of October, 1968 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the long title and preamble of the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), after the word "Ministers", a comma and the words "Ministers of State and Deputy Ministers" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka For clause (b), of Section 2 of the principal Act, the following clause shall be substituted, namely - "(b) 'Minister', 'Minister of State' and 'Deputy Minister' mean respectively, 'Minister', 'Minister of State' and 'Deputy Minister'; and Minister' includes the Chief Minister".
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 6 of the principal Act, the following sections shall be inserted, namely - "6-A. Salaries of Ministers of State - There shall be paid to each Minister of State a salary of one thousand rupees per mensem. 6-B. Residences of Ministers of State - (1) Each Minister of State shall be entitled, without payment of rent, to the use of a furnished residence in the City of Bangalore throughout his term of office and for a period of fifteen days immediately thereafter, or in lieu of such furnished residence to a house rent allowance at the rate of three hundred and fifty rupees per mensem. The furnishing of a residence provided under this sub-section shall be on such scales as may be prescribed. (2) In a residence used by a Minister of State who is entitled to a house rent allowance in lieu of a furnished residence, three rooms shall be furnished by the State Government on such scales as may be prescribed, to be used for official purposes. 6-C. Conveyance for Ministers of State - (1) The State Government may provide a suitable motor car for the use of each Minister of State. (2) There shall be paid to each Minister of State a conveyance allowance at the rate of three hundred and fifty rupees per mensem. 6-D. Charges payable by the Minister of State and the Government - (1) Each Minister of State shall in respect of the residence and motor car allotted for his use under sub-section (1) of Section 6-B and Section 6-C, be liable to pay the following charges, namely - (a) cost of petrol and oil required for the motor car; (b) when the monthly charges for the consumption of electricity in the residence exceed seventy-five rupees in any month, the charges in excess of such sum. (2) All other charges for the maintenance and upkeep of the residence provided under sub-section (1) of Section 6-B and the motor car provided under Section 6-C, including the cost of repairs thereof, the salaries and allowances of the drivers and cleaners of such motor car, rates and taxes, and all expenditure for the layout and the maintenance of the gardens included in such residence, shall be borne by the State Government".
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 11 of the principal Act - (i) in the heading, for the words "a Minister or a Deputy Minister", the words "a Minister, a Minister of State or a Deputy Minister" shall be substituted; (ii) in the Section, for the words "A Minister or Deputy Minister", the words "A Minister, Minister of State or Deputy Minister" shall be substituted; and for the words "the Minister or the Deputy Minister" in the two places where they occur, the words "the Minister, the Minister of State or the Deputy Minister" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12 of the principal Act - (a) in sub-section (1), for the words "Every Minister and Deputy Minister", the words "Every Minister, Minister of State and Deputy Minister" shall be substituted; (b) in sub-section (2) - (i) for the words "every Minister and Deputy Minister", the words "every Minister, Minister of State and Deputy Minister" shall be substituted; (ii) for the words "a Minister or a Deputy Minister" in the two places where they occur, the words "a Minister, a Minister of State or a Deputy Minister" shall be substituted; (iii) for the words "the Minister or the Deputy Minister", the words "the Minister, the Minister of State or the Deputy Minister" shall be substituted; (iv) in clause (c), after the words "such journeys", the words "and to the prescribed insurance premium for insurance against accidents during such journeys", shall be and shall always be deemed to have been inserted; (c) in sub-section (2-A) - (i) for the words "every Minister and Deputy Minister", the words "every Minister, Minister of State and Deputy Minister" shall be substituted; (ii) in clause (a), after the words "he travels", the words "and in respect of journeys by air, also to the prescribed insurance premium for insurance against accidents during such journeys" shall be and shall always be deemed to have been inserted; (d) after sub-section (2-A), the following sub-sections shall be and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from the first day of January, 1966, namely - "(2-B) When a Minister, Minister of State or Deputy Minister while making a journey by road in a motor car provided by the State Government, has to undertake in the public interest further journey by train or by air, he shall be entitled to the petrol charges incurred by him for the return of the motor car to the city of Bangalore from the place at which he ceases to use the motor car. (2-C) Every Minister, Minister of State and Deputy Minister shall in addition to the allowances payable under sub-section (2), be entitled to use without payment of charges, the motor cars maintained by the State Government at the Karnataka Bhavan in New Delhi". (e) in sub-section (3) - (i) for the words "Ministers and Deputy Ministers", the words "Ministers, Ministers of State and Deputy Ministers" shall be substituted; (ii) for the words "Minister or Deputy Minister", the words "Minister, Minister of State or Deputy Minister" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 13 of the principal Act - (i) for the words "a Minister and a Deputy Minister", the words "a Minister, a Minister of State and a Deputy Minister" shall be substituted; (ii) for the words "the Minister or the Deputy Minister", the words "the Minister, the Minister of State or the Deputy Minister" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 14 of the principal Act - (i) in the heading, for the words "Ministers and Deputy Ministers", the words "Ministers, Ministers of State and Deputy Ministers" shall be substituted; (ii) in the section, for the words "A Minister or a Deputy Minister", the words "A Minister, Minister of State or a Deputy Minister" shall be substituted; and for the words "as Minister or Deputy Minister", the words "as Minister, Minister of State or Deputy Minister, as the case may be" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 15 of the principal Act, after sub-section (1), the following sub-section shall be and shall always be deemed to have been inserted, namely - "(1-A) Any rule under this Act may be made to have effect retrospectively, and if any such rule is made, a statement specifying the reasons for making such a rule shall be laid before both Houses of the State Legislature along with the rule under sub-section (2)".
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1968_Section 10--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Ordinance, 1968 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 1 of 1968) is hereby repealed: Provided that anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act, as if this Act had come into force on the 29th day of May, 1968.
