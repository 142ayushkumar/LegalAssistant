Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Mysore (Religious and Charitable) Inams Abolition Act, 1955, the Karnataka (Sandur Area) Inams Abolition Act, 1976 and the Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition Act, 1977. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Mysore (Religious and Charitable) Inams Abolition Act, 1955 (Mysore Act 18 of 1955), the Karnataka (Sandur Area) Inams Abolition Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 54 of 1976) and the Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition Act, 1977 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1978) for the purpose hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-fourth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Act, 1984.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the twentieth day of December, 1983. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-sixth day of April, 1984
Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Mysore (Religious and Charitable) Inams Abolition Act, 1955 (Mysore Act 18 of 1955), in sub- section (2) of Section 9, in clause (a),- (1) in sub-clause (i), for the words and figures "30th June, 1981" the words and figures "31st March, 1984" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted; (2) in sub-clause (ii), for the words and figures "30th June, 1981" the words and figures "31st March, 1984" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted. x x x x x
Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Inams Abolition Laws (Amendment) Ordinance, 1983 (Karnataka Ordinance 15 of 1983) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the Mysore Act 18 of 1955, Karnataka Act 54 of 1976 and the Karnataka Act 10 of 1978 as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the said Acts as amended by this Act.
