Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1987_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act 30 of 1958) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 19871 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of April, 1987. (First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, on the Thirtieth day of April, 1987)(Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-fourth day of April, 1987)
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4 of the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act 30 of 1958), in sub-section (1) - (i) For the words twenty rupees in the two places where they occur, the words forty rupees shall be substituted; (ii) For the words thirty rupees in the two places where they occur, the words sixty rupees shall be substituted; (iii) For the words fifty rupees in the two places where they occur, the words one hundred rupees shall be substituted.
