Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act, further to amend the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act 30 of 1958) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-first year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1980.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixth day of August, 1980
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act 30 of 1958) (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act) - (1) sub-section (1-A) and the proviso thereto shall be omitted; and (2) in sub-section (2), the words, brackets, figure and letter "and sub-section (1-A)" in the two places where they occur shall be omitted.
Karnataka Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1980_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4-A of the principal Act, sub-section (1-A) and the proviso thereto shall be omitted. Law: Indirect Tax
