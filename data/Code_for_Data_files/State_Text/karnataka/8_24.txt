Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners Act, 1961. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1961); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twentieth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1969.1 Received the assent of the Governor on the Fourteenth day of April, 1969
Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of section 16 of the Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1961) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for the words "Director of Medical Services in Karnataka", the words "Director of Health and Family Planning Services in Karnataka" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Homeopathic Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 37 of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely - "37-A. Provision when the Board is not constituted -Notwithstanding anything contained in this Act, pending the constitution of the Board for the first time under this Act, the powers and functions of the Board shall be exercised and performed by a committee appointed by the State Government consisting of the Director of Health and Family Planning Services in Karnataka as Chairman and four members who shall be practitioners eligible to have their names entered in the register."
