Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1991.1 (2) It shall come into force from such date as may be notified by the State Government. Received the assent of the Governor on the Nineteenth day of April, 1991
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 94-A of the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964), for the words "not exceeding three" the words "not exceeding five" shall be substituted.
