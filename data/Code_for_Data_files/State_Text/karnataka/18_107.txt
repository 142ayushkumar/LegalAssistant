Karnataka Societies Registration (Amendment) Act, 1965_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Societies Registration Act, 1960. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Societies Registration Act, 1960 (Karnataka Act 17 of 1960). Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Sixteenth Year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka Societies Registration (Amendment) Act, 1965_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Karnataka Societies Registration (Amendment) Act, 19651 . First published in the Karnataka Gazette, on the Second day of December, 1965, Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-fourth day of November, 1965
Karnataka Societies Registration (Amendment) Act, 1965_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 27 of the Karnataka Societies Registration Act, 1960 (Karnataka Act 17 of 1960) - (i) for the words "the Registrar may hold an enquiry into the activities of such society, and in respect of every such enquiry the Registrar", the words "the Registrar may hold an enquiry or authorise any officer to hold an enquiry into the activities of such society and in respect of every such enquiry, the Registrar or the Authorised Officer" shall be substituted; and (ii) the following sentence shall be added at the end - "The Authorised Officer shall, on completion of the enquiry, submit a report to the Registrar"
