Karnataka Agricultural Income-tax (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Agricultural Income-tax Act, 1957. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Agricultural Income-tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1957), for the purposes hereinafter appearing: Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-Second year of Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Agricultural Income-tax (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Agricultural Income-tax (Amendment) Act, 1991.1 (2) It shall come into force on the first day of April, 1991. Received the assent of the Governor on the thirtieth day of March, 1991
Karnataka Agricultural Income-tax (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12 of the Karnataka Agricultural Income-tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for clause (c), the following shall be deemed to have been substituted with effect from first April, 1987, namely:- "(c) any sum which he receives as his share out of the agricultural income of a firm or association of persons, if the tax under this Act, has been levied on the agricultural income of such firm or association except where the income of the firm is assessed under Section 19-A or clause (b) of Section 19-B".
Karnataka Agricultural Income-tax (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 32-A of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), for the words "three years" the words "four years" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Agricultural Income-tax (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 61 of the principal Act, in the proviso, for the words "charged by Scheduled Banks for unsecured loans" the words "of twenty four per cent" shall be substituted.
