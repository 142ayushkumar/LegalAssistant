Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances Act, 1956. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1957) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka Legislature in the Forty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1990.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force from the First day of November, 1990. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Second day of February, 1991
Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (a) in sub-section (1), for the words "ten thousand rupees", the words "twenty thousand rupees" shall be substituted. (b) in sub-section (2), for the words "fifteen thousand rupees", the words "thirty thousand rupees" shall be substituted.

Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 16--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 11-A of the principal Act - (i) in sub-section (1) - (a) in clause (a) and (b), for the words "three hundred rupees", the words "One thousand rupees", shall be substituted; (b) for the first and second provisos, of clause (a) the following proviso shall be substituted, namely - "Provided that where any person has served as aforesaid for more than five years there shall be paid to him an additional pension of rupees fifty per mensem for every subsequent completed year but subject to a maximum pension of rupees one thousand five hundred per mensem". (ii) in sub-section (1-A), for the words "seven hundred and fifty rupees" the words "one thousand five hundred rupees", shall be substituted; (iii) after sub-section (4), the following shall be inserted namely - "(5) Every member entitled to pension under clauses (a) and (b) of sub-section (1) - (i) shall be provided with one non transferable pass which shall entitle him at any time to travel by road transport services of the Karnataka State Road Transport Corporation from his ordinary place of residence in Karnataka to Bangalore and back, not more than twelve times in a year, in such class of accommodation therein as may be prescribed; (ii) shall be entitled to free of charge to such accommodation in hospitals and dispensaries maintained by the State Government and to such medical attendance and treatment as may be prescribed".

(b) in clause (b), in item (i), for the words "seventy-five rupees per-diem", the words "one hundred rupees per-diem within the State and two hundred and fifty rupees per-diem outside the State in India", shall be substituted: (c) for clause (ccc) including the proviso, the following shall be substituted, namely - "(ccc) every member including a Minister, a Minister of State, a Deputy Minister, the Chairman, a Speaker, a Deputy Chairman, a Deputy Speaker, a Leader of the Opposition and a Government Chief Whip shall, for every financial year, subject to such rules as may be prescribed, be entitled for reimbursement of air or rail fare paid for journey performed in any part of India, singly or with a companion subject to the maximum limit of rupees ten thousand. A member shall not be entitled to any travelling or daily allowance for such journey". (d) for clause (h), the following shall be substituted namely - "(h) every member shall be paid an allowance of - (i) one thousand five hundred rupees per mensem towards meeting the expenditure incurred by him on telephone calls; (ii) five hundred rupees as consolidated allowances; (iii) five hundred rupees on postal charges; (iv) one thousand rupees as the salary of the personal assistant of the member: in the discharge of his duties as a member".
