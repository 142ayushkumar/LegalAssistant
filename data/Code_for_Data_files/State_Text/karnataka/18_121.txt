Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1958.1 2 Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-ninth Day of March. 1958 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 20 of the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 34 of 1957), (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act), for the figures "1889" the figures "1899" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Sections 11, 32, 34, 39 and 40 of the Principal Act, for the words, "nine naye paise, six naye paise or three naye paise" the words "ten naye paise or five naye paise", shall be substituted.
Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Sections 51 and 52 of the Principal Act, for the words "six naye paise" the words "ten naye paise", shall be substituted.
