Karnataka Land Revenue (Second Amendment) Act, 1991_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-second year of the Republic of India, as follows-
Karnataka Land Revenue (Second Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Revenue (Second Amendment) Act, 1991.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Seventh day of August, 1991. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-ninth day of October, 1991 Karnataka Act No. 28 of 1991 repealed by the Karnataka Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Land Revenue (Second Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka After the proviso to sub-section (1) of Section 163 of the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), the following shall be inserted, namely - "Provided further that notwithstanding anything contained in subsection (1) of Section 87, the Tahsildar shall not declare any such occupancy or alienated holding, to be forfeited to the State Government, where the arrears of land revenue due does not exceed rupees ten thousand."
Karnataka Land Revenue (Second Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka Notwithstanding anything contained in the principal Act, all proceedings initiated for forfeiture of occupancy or alienated holding under Section 163 of the principal Act, in respect of which arrears of land revenue does not exceed rupees ten thousand pending prior to the commencement of the Karnataka Land Revenue (Second Amendment) Act, 1991, shall stand abated.
Karnataka Land Revenue (Second Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Land Revenue (Second Amendment) Ordinance, 1991 (Karnataka Ordinance 8 of 1991) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
