Karnataka Taxation Laws (Second Amendment) Act, 1997_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend certain taxation laws as in force in the State of Karnataka. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend certain taxation laws for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty- eighth year of the Republic of India, as follows:
Karnataka Taxation Laws (Second Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Taxation Laws (Second Amendment) Act, 19971 . (2) It shall come into force at once. (First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, on the Twentieth day of September, 1997)(Received the assent of the Governor on the Twentieth day of September, 1997)
Karnataka Taxation Laws (Second Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958, (Karnataka Act 30 of 1958) - (1) in Section 3, in sub-section (1) - (a) after the words "payment for admission" the words and brackets "(excluding the amount of tax)" shall be inserted; (b) for the words "at the following rates, namely" the words "at seventy per cent of such payment" shall be substituted; (c) the table shall be omitted - (2) Section 3-A shall be omitted; (3) in Section 3-C - (a) in sub-section (1), the word, figure and letter "and 3-A" shall be omitted. (b) in sub-section (2) - (i) the word, figure and letter "and 3-A" shall be omitted; (ii) explanation shall be omitted. (4) in Section 4, in sub-section (1), for the words, figures and letter "tax and the surcharge leviable under Sections 3 and 3-A" in the two places where they occur, the words and figure "tax leviable under Section 3" shall be "substituted. (5) in Section 4-A, in sub-section (1) - (a) the words "and surcharge" and the words, figure and letter "and Section 3-A" shall be omitted; (b) for the table, the following table shall be substituted, namely - Table Sl. No. Local authority population of which Rate of Tax (1) (2) (3) (a) does not exceed twenty five thousand ten per cent of the gross collection capacity (b) exceeds twenty five thousand but does not exceed fifty thousand seventeen and half per cent of the gross collection capacity (c) exceeds fifty thousand but does not exceed seventy five thousand twenty five per cent of the gross collection capacity (6) in Section 4-B, in sub-section (1), the figure and letter "3-A" shall be omitted. (7) in Section 4-C, the figure and letter "3-A" shall be omitted. (8) in Section 6-A, in sub-section (1-A), the word "surcharge" shall be omitted. (9) in Section 7, the explanation shall be omitted. (10) in Section 8-A, the word "surcharge" shall be omitted. (11) in Section 9 - (a) in sub-sections (1), (2) and (3), the words "including surcharge" wherever they occur, shall be omitted; (b) in sub-section (2), in clause (i), the word "surcharge" shall be omitted. (12) in Section 9-C, in the explanation, the word "surcharge" shall be omitted.
