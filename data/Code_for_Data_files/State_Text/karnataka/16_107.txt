Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) Act, 1959, in its application to the State of Karnataka. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) Act, 1959 (Central Act 29 of 1959), in its application to the State of Karnataka. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 19761 . (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Karnataka. (3) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of January, 1976. First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary on the Nineteenth day of June, 1976, Received the assent of the President on the Fourteenth day of June, 1976
Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) Act, 1959 (Central Act 29 of 1959), as amended by the Public Wakfs (Extension of Limitation) Karnataka Amendment Act, 1973 (Karnataka Act 11 of 1973), for the words, figures and letters "the 31st day of December, 1975", the words, figures and letters "the 31st day of December, 1977" shall be substituted.
