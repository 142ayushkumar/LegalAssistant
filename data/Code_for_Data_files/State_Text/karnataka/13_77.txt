Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1957) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the fiftieth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1999.1 2 (2) It shall come into force with effect from the First day of April, 1999. Received the absent of the Governor on the thirty-first day of March, 1999 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10 of the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (1) in the opening paragraph - (a) in item (i), for the words "five percentum", the words "three percentum" shall be substituted; (b) in item (ii), for the words "eight percentum", the words "six percentum" shall be substituted. (2) in clause (b), for the words "five percentum or eight percentum as the case may be", the words "three percentum or six percentum as the case may be" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In the schedule to the principal Act, in Part 'A' - (a) in item 3, for sub-item (1) and the entries relating thereto, the following shall be substituted, namely - "(1) Vehicles in weight laden (b) Exceeding 1000 kgs. but not exceeding 3000 kgs. 600-00 (c) Exceeding 3000 kgs. but not exceeding 5500 kgs. 1,000-00 (d) Exceeding 5500 kgs. but not exceeding 9000 kgs. 1,400-00 (e) Exceeding 9000 kgs. but not exceeding 12000 kgs. 1,800-00 (f) Exceeding 12000 kgs. but not exceeding 15000 kgs. 2,000-00 (g) Exceeding 15000 kgs. 2,000-00 Plus 75-00 for every 250 kgs. or part thereof in excess of 15,000 kgs." (b) in item 4, in sub-item (4), in clauses (a) and (b), in column (3), for the figures "600" and "525" the figures "700" and "625" shall respectively be substituted; (c) in item 7, in clause (b), in column (3), for the figures "500", the figures "1000" shall be substituted.
