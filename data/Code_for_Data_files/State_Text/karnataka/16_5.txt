Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Panchayat Raj Act, 1993. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Panchayat Raj Act, 1993 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1993) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka Legislature in the Fiftieth year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1999.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the Twenty-eighth day of January, 1999. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-eighth day of April, 1999
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4 of the Karnataka Panchayat Raj Act, 1993 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1993) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (1),- (i) for the words "not less than five thousand and not more than seven thousand", the words "not less than ten thousand and not more than sixteen thousand" shall be substituted; (ii) in the first proviso, for the words "not less than two thousand five hundred", the words "not less than six thousand" shall be substituted; (iii) in the second proviso, for the words and brackets "a radius of five kilometres (diameter of ten kilometres)", the words and brackets "a radius of eight kilometres (diameter of sixteen kilometres)" shall be substituted; (iv) in the third proviso, for the words "either less than five thousand or more than seven thousand", the words "either less than ten thousand or more than sixteen thousand" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the princial Act,- (1) in sub-section (1), for the words "every four hundred population", the words "every one thousand population" shall be substituted; (2) in sub-section (4), the first proviso shall be omitted.
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka (1) Notwithstanding anything contained in the principal Act, where a Grama Panchayat (hereinafter referred to as 'new Grama Panchayat') is not constituted before the expiry of the term of office of the members of the Grama Panchayat existing on the date of commencement of the Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1999 (hereinafter referred to as 'old Grama Panchayat') either in respect of the panchayat area existing on the date of such commencement (hereinafter referred to as 'old panchayat area') or a panchayat area declared in accordance with the principal Act as amended by this Act (hereinafter referred to as 'new panchayat area'), the Deputy Commissioner may, by notification appoint either an Administrator or Administrative Committee. (2) The Administrative Committee shall consist of persons who are qualified to be elected as members of Grama Panchayat and the number of such persons shall be equal to the number of members of the old Grama Panchayat: Provided that an Administrative Committee shall as far as possible consist of persons who were the members of the Old Grama Panchayat immediately prior to the completion of term of office of the members of Old Grama Panchayat: Provided further that where the number of members in office at the time of completion of term of office of the members of Old Grama Panchayat was less than the number determined under sub-section (1) of Section 5 of the principal Act, the Deputy Commissioner may appoint additional members to make up the deficiency. (3) Where an Administrative Committee is appointed under sub-section (1), the Deputy Commissioner shall nominate two members of the Administrative Committee respectively as Adhyaksha and Upadhyaksha of the said Committee. (4) An Administrator or, as the case may be, the members of the Administrative Committee including the Adhyaksha and Upadhyaksha shall, subject to the pleasure of the Deputy Commissioner, hold office till the new Grama Panchayat is duly constituted. (5) The Administrator or Administrative Committee appointed under sub-section (1) shall be deemed to be a duly constituted Grama Panchayat for the purpose of the principal Act in respect of the old panchayat area. (6) If on receipt of a report of the Deputy Commissioner or otherwise the Government is satisfied that the Administrative Committee has failed to carry on the functions of the Grama Panchayat as stipulated in the principal Act or for any other valid reason, the Government may by order remove the Administrative Committee, and make such other arrangements as deemed fit for carrying on the functions of the Grama Panchayat. (7) The provisions of the principal Act applicable to the Grama Panchayats in regard to the payment of sitting fee and allowances to members, procedure for conduct of meetings, and constitution of Committees shall mutatis mutandis apply to the Administrative Committee. (8) Any appointment, notification, notice, tax, order, scheme, licence, permission, rule, regulation, bye-law or form made, issued, imposed or granted in respect of the old Grama Panchayat shall continue in force in the old panchayat area until it is superseded or modified by any appointment, notification, notice, tax, order, scheme, licence, permission, rule, regulation, bye-law or form made, issued, imposed or granted under this Act: Provided that if a new Grama Panchayat is constituted for a Panchayat Area which was included in the limits of two or more old Grama Panchayats, the Government may by notification direct that from the dates specified thereunder only such appointment, notification, notice, tax, order, scheme, licence, permission, rule, regulation, bye-law or form made, issued, imposed or granted in respect of such of the old Grama Panchayats shall be applicable to the new Grama Panchayat and only such appointment, notification, notice, tax, order, scheme, licence, permission, rule, regulation, bye-law or form made, issued, imposed or granted shall thereupon continue in force. (9) The un-expended balance of the fund and all other properties vesting in the old Grama Panchayats shall be transferred to such new Grama Panchayat, as the Government may by order direct. (10) The rights and liabilities of the old Grama Panchayat in respect of civil and criminal proceedings, properties, contracts, agreements and other matters or things (including arrears of taxes, fees, cess and rates) arising in, and relating to, any part of the area included in the new panchayat area shall vest in such new Grama Panchayat as the Government may by order direct. (11) Such officers and servants of the old Grama Panchayat may be transferred to such new Grama Panchayats as the Government may by order direct and shall, until other provisions are made in accordance with the principal Act, be entitled to receive the same salary and allowances and be subject to the same conditions of service as they were entitled or subject to, immediately before such transfer. (12) All proceedings including appeals, pending before the old Grama Panchayat or any officer thereof immediately before the constitution of new Grama Panchayat and all prosecutions instituted by or on behalf of the old Grama Panchayat or any officer thereof, pending immediately before such constitution shall be continued by or against and disposed of by them as if the new Grama Panchayat is not constituted. (13) The Government may, by notification, make such provision as appear to it to be necessary or expedient- (i) for making deletions from, additions to, adaptations or modifications in, any rule, bye-law, notification, scheme, permission, licence or form referred to in sub-section (8) in its application to the new Grama Panchayat; (ii) for removing difficulties arising in connection with the working of the old Grama Panchayat or the new Grama Panchayat.
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Ordinance, 1999 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 1 of 1999) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have, been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
