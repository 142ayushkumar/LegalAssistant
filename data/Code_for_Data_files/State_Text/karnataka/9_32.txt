Industrial Disputes (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1963_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, in its application to the State of Karnataka. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 (Central Act 14 of 1947), in its application to the State of Karnataka; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Fourteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows;
Industrial Disputes (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Industrial Disputes (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1963.1 Received the assent of the President on the Twenty-ninth day of November, 1963
Industrial Disputes (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In clause (aa) of sub-section (3) of Section 7-A of the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 (Central Act 14 of 1947), as inserted by the Industrial Disputes (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1962 (Karnataka Act 6 of 1963), after the words "District Judge", the words "for a period of not less than three years", shall be inserted.
