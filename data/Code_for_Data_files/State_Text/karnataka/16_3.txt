Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Panchayat Raj Act, 1993. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Panchayat Raj Act, 1993 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1993), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-eighth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1997.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Repealed by the Karnataka Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the 26th day of February, 1997
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 45 of the Karnataka Panchayat Raj Act 1993 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1993) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (1), after the words "on the expiry of the term of the members of Gramapanchayat", the words "or in the case of casual vacancy in the office of the Adhyaksha or Upadhyaksha" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 141 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2),- (i) in clause (a), after the words "Taluk Panchayat after the first constitution or re-constitution", the words "or the date of subsequent meeting for the purpose of election of Adhyaksha or Upadhyaksha, as the case may be" shall be inserted; (ii) in clause (e),- (a) after the words "or if he is absent", the words "or if the office of Adhyaksha is vacant" shall be inserted; (b) after the words "and if both the Adhyaksha and Upadhyaksha are absent", the words "or if the offices of both Adhyaksha and Upadhyaksha are vacant" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 153 of the principal Act,- (i) in clause (a), after the words "or is incapacitated from functioning", the words "or when the office of Adhyaksha is vacant" shall be inserted; (ii) in clause (b), after the words "in the absence of the Adhyaksha", the words "or when the office of Adhyaksha is vacant" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 180 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2),- (i) in clause (a), after the words "Zilla Panchayat after the first constitution or re-constitution", the words "or the date of subsequent meeting for the purpose of election of Adhyaksha or Upadhyaksha as the case may be", shall be inserted; (ii) in clause (e),- (a) after the words "or if he is absent", the words "or if the office of Adhyaksha is vacant" shall be inserted; (b) after the words "both the Adhyaksha and Upadhyaksha are absent", the words "or if the offices of both Adhyaksha and Upadhyaksha are vacant" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 194 of the principal Act,- (i) in clause (a), after the words "or is incapacitated from functioning", the words "or when the office of Adhyaksha is vacant" shall be inserted; (ii) in clause (b), after the words "in the absence of the Adhyaksha", the words "or when the office of Adhyaksha is vacant" shall be inserted.
