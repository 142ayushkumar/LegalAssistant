Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka State Universities Act, 1976. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka State Universities Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1976), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Fiftieth year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1999.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the 2nd day of August, 1999
Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 21 of the Karnataka State Universities Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1976) (hereinafter referred to as the  principal Act), in sub-section (1) - (1) for clause (4)(a), the following shall be substituted, namely - "(4)(a) The Commissioner of Collegiate Education, Karnataka." (2) in clause (20) - (i) for the word "seven", the word "five" shall be substituted; (ii) sub-clause (b) shall be deemed to have been omitted with effect from the First day of April, 1998; (iii) sub-clause (d) shall be deemed to have been omitted with effect from the First day of June, 1996; (iv) in sub-clause (e), for the word, brackets and letters "(a) to (d)", the word, brackets and letters "(a) and (c)" shall be substituted.
Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 24 of the principal Act - (i) after sub-clause (i), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(i-a) The Commissioner of Collegiate Education, Karnataka". (ii) for clause (xii), the following shall be substituted, namely - "(xii) The Director of Pre-University Education, Karnataka".
Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 26 of the principal Act - (i) for sub-clause (ix-a), the following shall be substituted, namely - "(ix-a) The Director of Pre-University Education, Karnataka". (ii) after sub-clause (ix-a), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(ix-b) The Commissioner of Collegiate Education, Karnataka".
Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 61-B of the principal Act, in sub-section (1) - (i) after clause (c), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(cc) The Commissioner of Collegiate Education, Karnataka". (ii) for clause (g), the following shall be substituted, namely - "(g) The Director of Pre-University Education, Karnataka".
Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka Notwithstanding anything contained in the principal Act, or in any other Act for the time being in force or in the statutes, the ordinances, or the regulations and the rules made under the principal Act, elections held to the various authorities of the University under the principal Act as amended by this Act shall be deemed to be valid and shall be deemed to have been held in accordance with law.
