Karnataka State Legislature Proceedings (Protection of Publication) Act, 1968_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to protect the publication of reports of proceedings of the Karnataka State Legislature. Whereas, it is expedient to protect the publication of reports of proceedings of the Karnataka State Legislature; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Nineteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka State Legislature Proceedings (Protection of Publication) Act, 1968_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka State Legislature Proceedings (Protection of Publication) Act, 1968.1 2 (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Karnataka. Repealed by Act No. 1 of 1976 and shall be deemed to have come into force w.e.f. 9-1-1976. Received the assent of the President on the Third day of December, 1968
Karnataka State Legislature Proceedings (Protection of Publication) Act, 1968_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In this Act, Newspaper" means any printed periodical work containing public news or comments on public news, and includes a news-agency supplying material for publication in a newspaper.
Karnataka State Legislature Proceedings (Protection of Publication) Act, 1968_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) Save as otherwise provided in sub-section (2), no person shall be liable to any proceedings, civil, or criminal, in any Court in respect of the publication in a newspaper of a substantially true report of any proceedings of either House of the Karnataka State Legislature, unless the publication is proved to have been made with malice. (2) Nothing in sub-section (1) shall be construed as protecting the publication of any matter, the publication of which is not for the public good.
Karnataka State Legislature Proceedings (Protection of Publication) Act, 1968_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka This Act shall apply in relation to reports or matters broadcast by means of wireless telegraphy as part of any programme or service provided by means of a broadcasting station situated within the State of Karnataka as it applies in relation to reports or matters published in a newspaper.
