Karnataka Warehouses (Amendment) Act, 1965_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Warehouses Act, 1961. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Warehouses Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 11 of 1962); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Sixteenth year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Karnataka Warehouses (Amendment) Act, 1965_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Karnataka Warehouses (Amendment) Act, 1965.1 2 Received the assent of the Governor on the Ninth day of November, 1965 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000.
Karnataka Warehouses (Amendment) Act, 1965_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 32 of the Karnataka Warehouses Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 11 of 1962), the following section shall be inserted, namely:- "32-A. Warehousemen and depositors to comply with the provisions of Central Act 1 of 1944 - In respect of goods warehoused under this Act to which the provisions of the Central Excise and Salt Act, 1944, are applicable, every warehouseman or depositor shall comply with the provisions of the said Act and the rules and notifications issued thereunder insofar as they are applicable to such goods".
