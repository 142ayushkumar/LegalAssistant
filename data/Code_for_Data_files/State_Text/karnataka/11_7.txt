Karnataka Khadi and Village Industries (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Khadi and Village Industries Act, 1956. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Khadi and Village Industries Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 7 of 1957) and certain other Acts. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Khadi and Village Industries (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Karnataka Khadi and Village Industries (Amendment) Act, 1958.1 Received the assent of the Governor on the Nineteenth day of December, 1958
Karnataka Khadi and Village Industries (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In clause (ii) of Section 14 of the Karnataka Khadi and Village Industries Act, 1956 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for the words "All India Khadi and Village Industries Board" the words "Khadi and Village Industries Commission" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Khadi and Village Industries (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-sections (2) and (3) of Section 21 of the principal Act, for the words "All India Khadi and Village Industries Board", the words "Khadi and Village Industries Commission" shall be substituted.
