Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1976.1 2 (2) It shall come into force on the First day of April, 1976. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixth day of March, 1976
Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 2 of the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (1) for clause (f-2), the following clause shall be substituted, namely - "(f-2) "Business" includes - (i) any trade, commerce or manufacture or any adventure or concern in the nature of trade, commerce or manufacture, whether or not such trade, commerce, manufacture, adventure or concern is carried on with a motive to make gain or profit and whether or not any profit accrues from such trade, commerce, manufacture, adventure or concern; and (ii) any transaction in connection with, or incidental or ancillary to such trade, commerce, manufacture, adventure or concern"; (2) the explanation to clause (k) shall be numbered as "Explanation 1" and after the explanation as so numbered the following explanation shall be inserted, namely - "Explanation 2 - The Central Government or a State Government which whether or not, in the course of business, buy, sell, supply or distribute goods, directly or otherwise, for cash or deferred payment or for commission, remuneration or other valuable consideration shall be deemed to be a dealer for the purposes of this Act"; (3) in clause (o-1) - (a) after the words "or in causing operations in an oil mill", the words "or in a saw mill" shall be inserted; (b) in the explanation - (i) in item (ii), at the end, the word "and" shall be omitted; (ii) item (iii) shall be renumbered as item (iv) and before the item so renumbered the following item shall be inserted, namely - "(iii) "Saw mill" means the plant and machinery with which and the premises including the precincts thereof in which or in any part of which, sawing operation is carried on; and".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka If Section 5 of the principal Act - (1) in sub-section (3-A), for the words "two per cent", the words "three per cent" shall be substituted; (2) after sub-section (3-A), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(3-B) If any person purchasing goods in respect of which he has made a declaration under the proviso to sub-section (3-A) fails without reasonable excuse to make use of the goods for the declared purposes the assessing authority after giving him a reasonable opportunity of being heard may, by order in writing, impose upon him by way of penalty a sum not exceeding one and half times the tax payable on the turnover relating to the sale of such goods at a rate which is equal to the rate prescribed in the Second Schedule: Provided that no prosecution for an offence under clause (h) of sub-section (1) of Section 29 shall be instituted in respect of the same facts in respect of which a penalty has been imposed under the above sub-section".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 16 of the principal Act, for the words "any tax or fee" in the two places where they occur, the words "any tax, penalty or fee" shall be and shall always be deemed to have been substituted.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 29 of the principal Act - (1) in sub-section (1) - (i) after clause (f), the following clauses shall be inserted, namely - "(g) fails to submit a statement as required by sub-section (1) of Section 12-B; (h) after purchasing any goods in respect of which he has made a declaration under the proviso to sub-section (3-A) of Section 5, fails without reasonable excuse to make use of the goods for the declared purposes,"; (2) in sub-section (2), for clause (aaa), the following clause shall be substituted, namely - "(aaa) wilfully submits an untrue statement under sub-section (1) of Section 12-B".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 37 of the principal Act, after sub-section (2), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(3) If the State Government is of the opinion that it is necessary or expedient in the public interest to publish names of any assessees or any other particulars relating to any proceedings under this Act in respect of such assessees, it may cause to be published such names and particulars in such manner as it thinks fit. (4) No publication under this section shall be made relating to any penalty imposed or any conviction for any offence connected with any proceedings under this Act, until the time for presenting an appeal to the Appellate Authority has expired without any appeal having been presented or the appeal, if presented, has been disposed of".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In the Second Schedule to the principal Act - (1) in Serial No. 22, for the entries in Column 2, the following entries shall be substituted, namely - "X-Ray apparatus, films and accessories required for use therewith"; (2) in Serial No. 81-B, after the words "Blasting gunpowder", the words "and other mechanical explosives" shall be inserted; (3) in Serial No. 98, for the entries in Column 2, the following entries shall be substituted, namely - "Tinned, canned and bottled foods and fruits including foods and fruits packed in sealed containers"; (4) in Serial No. 105, for the entries in Column 2, the following entries shall be substituted, namely - "Butter, Ghee and Cheese packed in sealed containers"; (5) in Explanation II, for the words "whisky or rum", the words "whisky, rum or wine" shall be substituted; (6) to the Explanation IV, the following proviso shall be inserted, namely - "Provided that for the period between 1st January, 1959 and 22nd August, 1972, toothbrushes, shaving brushes and combs shall not be deemed to be toilet articles"; (7) after Explanation IV, the following explanation shall be and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from 1st April, 1964, namely - "Explanation IV-A - Toilet soaps in Entry No. 65-A means soaps intended for bathing purposes"; (8) after Explanation VI, the following explanation shall be inserted, namely - "Explanation VII - Where tax has been levied in respect of goods mentioned in Items 98, 99 and 105 such goods taken out from containers and sold loose shall not be liable to tax under this Act, and the burden of proving that tax has been paid on the goods in question shall be on the dealer".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka For the entries in Column 2 of Serial No. 12 of the Third Schedule to the principal Act, the following entries shall be substituted, namely - "Bauxite, Chromite, Iron and Managanese Ores".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka In Entry 29 of Fifth Schedule to the principal Act, after the words "domestic use", the words "except to hotels" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 10--> State(s): Karnataka (1) Notwithstanding anything contained in any judgment, decree or order of any Court, Tribunal or other authority to the contrary, any assessment, reassessment, levy or collection of any tax made or purporting to have been made in respect of toothbrushes, shaving brushes and combs, anything done or any action taken in relation to such assessment, reassessment, levy or collection under the provisions of the principal Act during the period between 1st January, 1959 and 22nd August, 1972, shall be deemed to be as valid and effective as if such assessment, reassessment, levy or collection or action or thing had been made, taken or done under the principal Act, as amended by this Act and accordingly - (a) all acts, proceedings or things done or taken by the Government or by any officer of the Government or by any authority in connection with the assessment, reassessment, levy or collection of such tax shall, for all purposes, be deemed to be and to have always been done or taken in accordance with law; (b) no suit or other proceedings shall be maintained or continued in any Court or before any authority for the refund of any such tax; and (c) no Court shall enforce any decree or order directing the refund of any such tax. (2) For the removal of doubts, it is hereby declared that nothing in sub-section (1) shall be construed as preventing any person - (a) from questioning in accordance with the provisions of the principal Act, as amended by this Act, any assessment, reassessment, levy or collection of tax referred to in sub-section (1); or (b) from claiming refund of any tax paid by him in excess of the amount due from him by way of tax under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
