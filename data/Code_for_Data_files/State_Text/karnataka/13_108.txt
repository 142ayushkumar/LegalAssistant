Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Municipal Corporations Act, 1976. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Municipal Corporations Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1977) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-second year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1981.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Seventh day of August, 1981 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Chapter VII of the Karnataka Municipal Corporations Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1977), (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act) after Section 91, the following section shall be inserted, namely - "91-A. Transfer of employees - (1) Notwithstanding anything contained in this Act, or in any other law, the State Government may transfer any officer or servant of a corporation to a corresponding post in any other corporation or in any local authority constituted or deemed to have been constituted under any law made by the State Legislature or in the Government. (2) The officer or servant transferred under sub-section (1) shall, subject to any rule or other provision made under this Act or under Article 309 of the constitution, be entitled to the same remuneration and be subject to the same terms and conditions of service and to the same rights and privileges as to pension, gratuity and provident fund and such other matters as he would have held under the corporation from which he was so transferred."
Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 99of the principal Act, in sub-section (7), for the words, "from time to time", the words, "from time to time, either prospectively or retrospectively", shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 100 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), for the words "or extend", the words, "or extend, either prospectively or retrospectively", shall be and that be deemed always to have been substituted.
Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 101 of the principal Act - (1) in the proviso, for the words, "may continue", the words, "may continue either prospectively or retrospectively", shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted. (2) in the second proviso, for the words, "shall continue", the words, "may be continued, either prospectively or retrospectively", shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In the principal Act, to sub-section (1) of Section 421, the following proviso shall be and shall be deemed always to have been added, namely - "Provided that no previous publication shall be necessary for any rule made for the first time after the commencement of this Act."
Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 503 of the principal Act, sub-section (3), in clause (cc), for the words, "curtail the said period or extend the same under Section, 101" the words "curtail or extend the said period, either prospectively or retrospectively, under Section 101" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Municipal Corporation Rules, 1977, made in Notification No. HMA 270 MNU 77, dated 19th December, 1977, and published as GSR 390 in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, dated 22nd December, 1977 (hereinafter referred to as the said rules) shall, notwithstanding anything contained in any judgment, decree or order of any court or other authority or in the principal Act, be deemed to be as valid and effective for all purposes as if the said rules had been made under the principal Act as amended by this Act and accordingly - (a) all actions or things taken or done (including appointments and promotions made) under the said rules shall, for all purposes be deemed to be and to have always been taken or done in accordance with law; (b) no; (i) suit or other proceedings shall be maintained or continued in any court or tribunal or before any authority questioning the validity of any action or thing taken or done under the said rules; and (ii) court shall recognise or enforce any decree or order declaring that the said rules or any action or thing taken or done thereunder as invalid, on the ground that the rules were made without giving reasonable opportunity to persons likely to be affected by it to file their objections and suggestions. (2) Notwithstanding anything contained in any judgment, decree or order of any court of other authority or in the Principal Act all appointments of Administrations made or continued before the commencement of this Act shall be deemed to have been validly made for all purposes as if the said appointments had been made under the Principal Act as amended by this Act and accordingly all actions and things taken or done by or under the authority of the Administrators shall be and shall be deemed to have always been validly taken or done and no suit or other proceedings shall lie or be continued in any court of law or any other authority on the ground that at the time when such action or thing was taken or done the appointment or continuance of the Administrator was not authorised by law.
Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Ordinance, 1981 (Karnataka Ordinance 12 of 1981) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
