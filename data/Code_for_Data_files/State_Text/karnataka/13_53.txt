Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1971_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957. In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 3 of the Karnataka State Legislature (Delegation of Powers) Act, 1971 (23 of 1971), the President is pleased to enact as follows:-
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1971_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1971.1 (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may by notification, appoint. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
