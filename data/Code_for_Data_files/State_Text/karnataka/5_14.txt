Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act, further to amend the Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) Act, 1959. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) Act, 1959 (Karnataka Act No. 14 of 1959); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirtieth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) (Amendment) Act, 1979.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Twenty-sixth day of September 1978. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-first day of February, 1979
Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 7 of the Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) Act, 1959 (Karnataka Act No. 14 of 1959) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) for the word "twelve" the word "fifteen" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Electricity (Taxation on Consumption) (Amendment) Ordinance, 1978 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 11 of 1978) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal any action taken or any appointment, notification, order, scheme, rule, form or bye-law made or issued under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been taken, made or issued under the principal Act as amended by this Act and shall continue in force accordingly unless and until superseded by any action taken or any appointment, notification, order, scheme, rule, form or bye-law made or issued under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
