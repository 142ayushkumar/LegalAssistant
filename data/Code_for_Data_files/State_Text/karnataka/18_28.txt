Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957). Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka Legislature in the Fortieth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1989.1 (2) The provisions of this Act other than sub-section (3) of Section 18 shall come into force from the first day of April, 1989 and sub-section (3) of Section 18 shall come into force from such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint. Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixteenth day of April, 1989
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as principal Act), in clause (c) of sub-section (1), after the words "such produce", the words "except coffee" shall be and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from the Eighteenth day of October, 1983.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the principal Act, in the proviso to sub-section (1), the words "sugarcane syrup (processed)" shall be omitted.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5-A of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), after clause (iv), the following shall be inserted namely - "(v) having his manufacturing unit inside the State and having purchased any inputs by furnishing a declaration under first proviso to sub-section (1), uses such inputs contrary to such declaration, the assessing authority, after giving such person a reasonable opportunity of being heard, shall, by order in writing, impose upon him by way of penalty a sum which shall not be less than twice the amount of tax leviable under Section 5 but not exceeding thrice the amount of such tax on the sale of the inputs so purchased".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 8-A of the principal Act - (1) after sub-section (2), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(2-A) The State Government may, by notification, transpose any entry or part thereof from one Schedule to another Schedule and alter the point of levy of sale or purchase, but not so as to enhance the rate of tax in any case: Provided that where the point of levy is altered under this sub-section in respect of any goods and if such goods have been subjected to tax under the unaltered entry, then such goods shall not be subjected to tax under the transferred entry"; (2) in sub-section (3), after the words, brackets and figures "sub-section (1)", the words, brackets, figures and letter "or sub-section (2-A)" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12 of the principal Act, in the first proviso to sub-section (5), after the words and figures "the Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1985", the words "in respect of which a return under sub-section (1) has been submitted, before such commencement" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been inserted.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12-C of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), for the words "for whom the authority prescribed under sub-section (1) of Section 10-A is an Assistant Commercial Tax Officer, is not less than the limits specified under clause (a) of sub-section (5) of Section 5", the words "is not more than five lakh rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 17 of the principal Act - (1) for clause (i) of sub-section (6), the following shall be substituted, namely - "(i) Notwithstanding anything contained in sub-sections (1) to (3), subject to such conditions and in such circumstances as may be prescribed, the assessing authority of the area may, if a dealer liable to tax under Section 5-B so elects, accept in respect of works contracts specified in the Sixth Schedule by way of composition- (a) an amount at the rate of two per cent of his total turnover if such turnover relates to transfer of property in goods (whether as goods or in some other form) involved in the execution of works contract specified in Serial Number 6 of the Sixth Schedule; or (b) an amount at the rate of four per cent of his total turnover, if such turnover relates to transfer of property in goods (whether as goods or in some other form) involved in the execution of works contract other than those specified in Serial Number 6 of Sixth Schedule"; (2) after sub-section (6), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(7) Nothing contained in sub-section (6) shall apply to a dealer registered under the Central Sales Tax Act, 1956 and has furnished a declaration in Form-C under the said Act in respect of goods purchased by him for the purposes of using such goods in the execution of works contract".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 19-A of the principal Act, for sub-section (1), the following shall be substituted, namely - "(1) Notwithstanding anything contained in this Act, the Central Government, or any State Government or an industrial, commercial or trading undertaking of the Central Government or of any State Government shall be entitled, to deduct out of the amounts payable by them to a dealer in respect of works contracts of the nature specified in the Sixth Schedule executed for them an amount equal to the tax payable by such dealer under Section 17, calculated - (a) at the rate of two per cent of the total amount payable to such dealers, if the works contract executed is as specified under Serial Number 6 of the Sixth Schedule; or (b) at the rate of four per cent of the total amount payable to such dealers, if the works contract executed is other than those specified in Serial Number 6 of the Sixth Schedule".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 10--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 20 of the principal Act, in clause (a) of sub-section (5) - (1) in sub-clause (i), the word "enhance" shall be omitted; (2) after sub-clause (i), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(i-a) enhance the assessment including any part thereof whether or not such part is objected to in the appeal".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 11--> State(s): Karnataka In the principal Act, after Section 24, the following shall be inserted, namely - "24-A. Objections to jurisdiction - No objection as to the territorial or pecuniary jurisdiction of any assessing authority shall be entertained or allowed by any Court, Tribunal or Authority in an appeal or revision, unless such objection was taken before the assessing authority at the earliest possible opportunity".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 12--> State(s): Karnataka B-In sub-section (1) of Section 25-B of the principal Act, after sub-clause (i-b) of clause (i), the following shall be and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from the first day of April, 1985 and shall be deemed to have been omitted with effect from the 31st day of March, 1989, namely - "(i-c) rupees eight per tonne when purchased by sugar factories situated in the State of Maharashtra";
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 13--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (7) of Section 28-A of the principal Act, after sub-clause (ii) of clause (a), the following proviso shall be inserted, namely - "Provided that where the person aggrieved is a dealer registered under the Act, such person may appeal to the appropriate Appellate Authority of the area in which he is registered".
