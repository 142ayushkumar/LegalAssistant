Karnataka Rent Control (Retrospective Extension of duration) Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to provide for the extension of the duration of the Karnataka Rent Control Act, 1961. Whereas, it is expedient for the extension of the duration of the Karnataka Rent Control Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1961) with retrospective effect, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be, it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty- third year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Karnataka Rent Control (Retrospective Extension of duration) Act, 1993_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Rent Control (Retrospective Extension of duration) Act, 1993.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Thirty-first day of December, 1992. Received the assent of the Governor on the First day of April, 1993 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Rent Control (Retrospective Extension of duration) Act, 1993_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 1 of the Karnataka Rent Control Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 22of 1961) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (4), for the figures "1992", the figures "1997" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Rent Control (Retrospective Extension of duration) Act, 1993_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka Nothing in this Act shall render any person liable to be convicted of any offence in respect of anything done by him or omitted to be done by him, during the period commencing on the 1st day of January, 1993 and ending on the date of publication of this Act in the Official Gazette, if such act or omission was not an offence but for the retrospective extension of duration of the principal Act by this Act.
Karnataka Rent Control (Retrospective Extension of duration) Act, 1993_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka For the removal of doubt, it is hereby declared that the principal Act, which expired after the 31st December 1992, having been retrospectively extended from that date upto and inclusive of the 31st December, 1997 by this Act, shall be deemed never to have expired at any time and all its provisions as amended and extended by this Act, shall, subject to the provisions of Section 3 of this Act, be deemed to be continuously in force.
