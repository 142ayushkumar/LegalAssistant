Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1974. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 32 of 1974) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Fifty-first year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Amendment) Act, 20001 . (2) It shall come into force at once. First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, on the 27th day of April, 2000, Received the assent of the Governor on the 27th day of April, 2000
Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-clause (ii) of clause (e) of Section 2 of the Karnataka Public Premises (Eviction of Unauthorised Occupants) Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 32 of 1974), the following shall be inserted, at the end namely - "or any company which is a subsidiary (within the meaning of the said Act) of the first mentioned company".
