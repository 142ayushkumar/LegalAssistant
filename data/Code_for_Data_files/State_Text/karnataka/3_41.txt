Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Co-operative Societies Act, 1959. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Co-operative Societies Act, 1959 (Karnataka Act 11 of 1959), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1991.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Seventh day of December, 1991
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka Section 101-C of the Karnataka Co-operative Societies Act, 1959 (Karnataka Act 11 of 1959) shall be omitted.
Karnataka Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka Nothing herein contained shall,- (a) affect the previous operation of the provisions so omitted or anything duly done or suffered thereunder, (b) affect any right, privilege, obligation or liability acquired, accrued or incurred under the provisions so omitted, or (c) affect any pending proceeding in respect of purchase of immovable property in auction by the State Government under the provisions so omitted, and such proceeding shall be continued as if, Section 101-C had not been omitted.
