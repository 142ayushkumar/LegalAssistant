Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1956. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twentieth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1969.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st day of April, 1969. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-eighth day of August, 1969.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Amendment) Act, 1969_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (3) of Section 8 of the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1957) - (1) in clause (a), for the words "to single fare", the words "to one and one fifth times the single fare" shall be substituted; (2) in clause (b), for the words "rupees fifty", the words "rupees one hundred" and for the words "rupees seventy-five", the words "rupees one hundred and fifty" shall respectively be substituted.
