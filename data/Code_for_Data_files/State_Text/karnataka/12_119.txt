Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1956. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1956), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Second Amendment) Act, 1976.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of July, 1974. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Nineteenth day of June, 1976.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries (Second Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka For sub-section (3) of Section 8 of the Karnataka Legislature Salaries Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1957), the following sub-sections shall be substituted, namely - "(3)(a) The Chairman and the Speaker touring outside India on duty shall be entitled to such terms in regard to travelling and other expenses as the Governor may, in each case, by order, determine. (b) Every order issued under clause (a) shall, as soon as may be, after it is issued, be laid before each House of the State Legislature".
