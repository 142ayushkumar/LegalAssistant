Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1958.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Fourteenth day of December, 1957. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-ninth Day of March, 1958 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (1) in the proviso to sub-section (1), for the word "pulses", the words "garlic, gur, onion, potatoes, pulses and their products, sweet potatoes and turmeric" shall be substituted; (2) after sub-section (5), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(5-A) Notwithstanding anything contained in sub-sections (3) and (5), and subject to the provisions of sub-section (1) of Section 8 in respect of - (a) (i) the sale of the goods mentioned in Items 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 27, 28, 29, 30, 31, 32, 34 and 42 of the Second Schedule, and (ii) the purchase of the goods mentioned in Item 3 of the Third Schedule, on which excise duty or additional excise duty levied by the Central Government with effect from the fourteenth day of December, 1957, has not been paid; (b) the sale of goods mentioned in Items 33 and 42 of the Second Schedule held in stock by the dealer on the fourteenth day of December, 1957, on which the said excise duty is not payable, the tax payable under this Act, shall be levied at the rates and at the points specified in the said Second or Third Schedule, as the case may be, on the dealer in such goods whatever his total turnover during the year relating to such goods may be".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6 of the principal Act, clause (i) of sub-section (1) shall be omitted.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka Section 8 of the principal Act shall be renumbered as sub-section (1) of that section, and after the said sub-section (1), the following sub-section shall be added, namely - "(2) Subject to the provisions of sub-section (1) in respect of the sale or purchase of the goods mentioned in Items 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 27, 28, 29, 30, 31, 32, 34 and 42 of the Second Schedule and in Item 3 of the Third Schedule, on which excise duty levied by the Central Government with effect from the fourteenth day of December, 1957 has been paid and of the sale of goods mentioned in Items 33 and 42 of the Second Schedule acquired by a dealer on or after the fourteenth day of December, 1957, on which the said excise duty is not payable shall be exempt from the tax payable under this Act."
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka Section 17 of the principal Act shall be renumbered as sub-section (1) of that section, and (1) in the said sub-section (1) - (i) for clauses (a) and (b), the following words shall be substituted, namely - "from any dealer, if his annual turnover does not exceed twenty thousand rupees"; (ii) in clause (i), the words, brackets and letter "[including a dealer in powerloom cloth specified in clause (a)]" shall be omitted; (2) after sub-section (1), the following sub-section shall be added, namely - "(2) Notwithstanding anything contained in sub-section (1), the Commercial Tax Officer of the area may accept from any dealer liable to payment of tax under sub-section (5-A) of Section 5, in lieu of the amount of tax payable by him under this Act, by way of composition, an amount equal to the tax payable under this Act - (i) on a turnover equal to one-fourth of the turnover of such dealer during the year 1956-57, in the case of a dealer who carried on business in such goods during the whole period of twelve months in that year and was liable to pay sales tax on the basis of such turnover during that year; (ii) on a turnover computed in the manner specified in clause (i) or (ii), as the case may be, of sub-section (1), in the case of any other dealer: Provided that where the amount payable by way of composition under this sub-section - (a) is less than the amount collected by the dealer by way of tax under Section 18 on the sale or purchase of such goods, the whole of the amount collected by way of tax, shall be the amount payable for the purpose of composition; (b) is equal to the amount collected by the dealer by way of tax under Section 18 on the sale or purchase of such goods, the whole of the amount collected by way of tax shall be payable for the purpose of composition; (c) is more than the amount collected by the dealer by way of tax under Section 18 on the sale or purchase of such goods, the whole of the amount collected by way of tax plus the amount necessary to make up the deficiency, shall be payable for the purpose of composition."
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 18 of the principal Act - (1) after the proviso to sub-section (1), the following additional proviso shall be inserted, namely - "Provided further that no dealer liable to payment of tax under sub-section (5-A) of Section 5 shall, after the fourteenth day of December, 1957 collect any amount by way of tax in respect of the sale or purchase of the goods mentioned in the items in the Second and Third Schedules referred to in the said sub-section (5-A)"; (2) to sub-section (2), the following proviso shall be added, namely - "Provided that nothing in this sub-section shall be deemed to apply to a dealer who is permitted to compound under sub-section (2) of Section 17, the tax payable by him in respect of the sale or purchase of any goods, and who has collected any amount by way of tax in respect of the sale or purchase of such goods before the fourteenth day of December, 1957."
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In the Fifth Schedule to the principal Act - (1) after Serial Number 8, with effect from the first day of April, 1958, the following entry shall be inserted, namely - "8-A. All varieties of textiles, namely, cotton, woollen or silken including rayon, art silk or nylon whether manufactured by handloom, powerloom or otherwise but exclusive of pure silk". (2) after Serial Number 32, the following entry shall be added, namely - "33. Electrical energy."
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Ordinance, 1957, is hereby repealed: Provided that anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act as if this Act was in force on the date the Ordinance was promulgated."
