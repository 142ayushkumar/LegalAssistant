Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation Act, 1958. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act 16 of 1958) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 19811 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Seventeenth day of January, 1981. (First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary on the Fourth day of April, 1981)(Received the assent of the Governor on the Fourth day of April, 1981)
Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 44 of the Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation Act, 1958, (Karnataka Act 16 of 1958) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) after the words, brackets and figures (Central Act V of 1908), the words, brackets and figures or under Section 50 of the Bombay Public Trusts Act, 1950 (Bombay Act XXIX of 1950), shall be and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from the 1st day of January, 1976.
Karnataka Court Fees and Suits Valuation (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Schedule II of the principal Act, for Article 18 the following article shall be substituted, namely - 18. Caveat - (1) When filed under the Indian Succession Act - (i) if the value of the property involved does not exceed two thousand rupees. Six rupees and twenty five paise. (ii) if the value of the property involved exceeds two thousand rupees. Twelve rupees and fifty paise. (2) in other cases - (i) if filed in the High Court Ten rupees (ii) if filed in other Courts Five rupees.
