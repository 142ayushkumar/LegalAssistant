Karnataka Urban Water Supply and Drainage Board (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka A bill to amend the Karnataka Urban Water Supply and Drainage Board Act, 1973. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Urban Water Supply and Drainage Board Act, 1973 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1974). Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Urban Water Supply and Drainage Board (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Urban Water Supply and Drainage Board (Amendment) Act, 1976.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Fourth day of March, 1976 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Urban Water Supply and Drainage Board (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Karnataka Urban Water Supply and Drainage Board Act, 1973 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1974) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for the expression "Mysore" wherever it occurs the expression "Karnataka" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Urban Water Supply and Drainage Board (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 9 of the principal Act, for the first proviso to sub-section (1), the following proviso shall be substituted, namely: "Provided that the appointment of the Secretary, Chief Engineer and the Accounts Officer shall be made with the previous approval of the Government."
