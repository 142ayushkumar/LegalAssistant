Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Legal Aid Board Act, 1981. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Legal Aid Board Act, 1981 (Karnataka Act 32 of 1981) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-sixth year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1995.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the 30th day of April, 1995
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka After sub-section (2) of Section 31 of the Karnataka Legal Aid Board Act, 1981 (Karnataka Act 32 of 1981) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(3) Any regulation under this Act may be made, to have effect retrospectively and when any such regulation is made, a statement specifying the reasons for making such a regulation shall be laid before both Houses of the State Legislature along with the regulation".
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 32 of the Principal Act - (i) in the heading for the words "rules and schemes" the words "rules, schemes and regulations" shall be substituted; (ii) for the words and figures "and every scheme framed under Section 16" the words, figures and brackets "every scheme framed under Section 16 and every regulation made under subsection (3) of Section 31" shall be substituted; (iii) for the words "rule or scheme" wherever they occur, the words "rule, scheme or regulation" shall be substituted.
