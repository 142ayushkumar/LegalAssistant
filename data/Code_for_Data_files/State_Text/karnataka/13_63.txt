Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1957), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-fifth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1984.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st day of April, 1984. Received the assent of the Governor on the Thirteenth day of April, 1984 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 3-B of the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1957), (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), the following section shall be inserted, namely:- "3-C. Levy of cess - (1) In addition to the levy of tax under Section 3, surcharge under Section 3-A and additional surcharge under Section 3-B, there shall be levied and paid a cess at the rate of ten per centum of the tax levied under Section 3. (2) The provisions of this Act and the rules made thereunder relating to levy, payment and recovery of tax and the restrictions and penalties for the possession, control or use of the motor vehicle liable to tax under this Act shall mutatis mutandis apply in relation to the cess leviable and payable under sub-section (1). (3) The amount of cess leviable and payable under sub-section (1) shall be rounded off to the nearest multiple of five paise and for this purpose any fraction of a paise shall be ignored and thereafter if such amount contains a part of a rupee consisting of paise then, if such part consisting of paise is not multiple of five, then if the last figure in that part is three, four, eight or nine that part shall be increased to the next higher figure which is a multiple of five and if the last figure is less than three or six or seven that part shall be reduced to the next lower figure which is a multiple of five and the amount so rounded off shall be deemed to be the amount of cess leviable and payable under sub-section (1)."
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka If any difficulty arises in giving effect to the provisions of the principal Act as amended by this Act, the State Government may, by notification, make such provisions as may appear to it to be necessary or expedient for removing the difficulty: Provided that no such order shall be made after the expiry of a period of two years from the date of commencement of this Act.
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In respect of motor vehicles for which the tax is payable under the principal Act for ten years, five years, one year or quarterly as the case may be, tax has already been paid before the commencement of the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1984, then notwithstanding that a taxation card has been granted therefor or endorsement in the taxation card for having collected the tax has been made, the cess payable under Section 2 for the said period shall be paid on or before 15th April, 1985.
