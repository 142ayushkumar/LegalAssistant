Karnataka Money Lenders (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Money Lenders Act, 1961. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Money Lenders Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1962), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-eighth year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka Money Lenders (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Money Lenders (Amendment) Act, 1997.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twentieth day of May, 1998 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Money Lenders (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Money Lenders Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1962) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), after clause (20) including the explanation thereunder, the following shall be inserted, namely - "(21) 'year' means the year commencing on the first day of April".
Karnataka Money Lenders (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6 of the principal Act - (i) in sub-section (1) - (a) the word "annually" shall be omitted; (b) in clause (e), the words and figures "on the 31st day of December", the words and figures "on the 31st day of March" shall be substituted; (ii) in sub-section (4) - (a) in clause (a), for the words "one hundred rupees", the words "five thousand rupees" shall be substituted; (b) in clause (b) - (i) for the words "one hundred rupees for the licence", the words "rupees five thousand" shall be substituted; (ii) for the words "fifty rupees for the licence", the words "rupees two thousand five hundred" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Money Lenders (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 7-A of the Principal Act - (a) for sub-section (1), the following shall be substituted, namely - "(1) Every money lender applying for grant of a licence under Section 7 for the first time or for grant of a fresh licence under that section for the succeeding term, shall at the time of making such application, pay security deposit as provided in sub-section (2)"; (b) in sub-section (2), in the Table for the word "lends" wherever it occurs, the word "invests" shall be substituted; (c) in sub-section (3) - (i) for the words "aggregate amount lent by him during the previous year", the words "the amount invested by him in the business during the previous year" shall be substituted; (ii) the words "and such security deposit shall not carry any interest" shall be deemed to have been inserted at the end with effect from 31st May, 1985; and (iii) in the proviso, for the word "lend", the word "invest" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Money Lenders (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 9 of the principal Act, the following shall be inserted, namely - "9-A. Repayment of Security Deposit - Where a licence has been cancelled by the Registrar, he shall by order in writing refund the security deposit paid but not forfeited under Section 7-B, to the person whose licence has been so cancelled".
Karnataka Money Lenders (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10 of the Principal Act - (i) for the words and figures "from the date on which it is granted to the 31st day of December following", the words "for a term of five years" shall be substituted; (ii) in the proviso, for the word "year", the word "term" shall be substituted; (iii) after the proviso, the following explanation shall be inserted, namely - "Explanation - Where a licence has been granted in the middle of a year, for the purpose of computing the term of licence, the remaining part of the year shall be deemed to be a year".
Karnataka Money Lenders (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 21 of the principal Act, sub-section (4) shall be omitted.
Karnataka Money Lenders (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka Notwithstanding anything contained in any judgment, decree or order of any Court, the provisions of sub-section (3) of Section 7-A of the principal Act as amended by sub-clause (ii) of clause (c) of Section 4 of this Act, shall be deemed always to have been in force and no person who has paid or deemed to have paid the Security Deposit under Section 7-A of the principal Act on or after, 31st May, 1985 and before the commencement of this Act shall be entitled to claim interest on such security deposit and accordingly - (a) no suit or other proceedings shall be entertained or continued in any Court, tribunal or before any authority for the payment of interest on such security deposit; and (b) no Court shall enforce any decree or order directing the payment of interest on such security deposit.
