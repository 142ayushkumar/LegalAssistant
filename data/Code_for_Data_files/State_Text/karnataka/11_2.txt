Kannada Development Authority (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Kannada Development Authority Act, 1994. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Kannada Development Authority Act, 1994 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1994), for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-seventh year of the Republic of India as follows -
Kannada Development Authority (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Kannada Development Authority (Amendment) Act, 1996.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the Assent of the Governor on the Twenty-nineth day of September, 1997
Kannada Development Authority (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (3) of Section 3 of the Kannada Development Authority Act, 1994 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1994) (herein-after referred to as the principal Act). (i) in clause (d), the words "and Parliamentary Affairs" shall be omitted. (ii) after clause (f), the following shall be inserted, namely  "(f-a) Director of Translation ... Member."
Kannada Development Authority (Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 15 of the Principal Act, after clause (c), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(c-a) take action to secure priority for and promotion of Kannada in. the field of education and cultural activities."
