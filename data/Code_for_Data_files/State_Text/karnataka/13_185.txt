Mysore Electrical Industries Limited (Acquisition of Shares) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Mysore Electrical Industries Limited (Acquisition of Shares) Act, 1979. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Mysore Electrical Industries Limited (Acquisition of Shares) Act, 1979 (Karnataka Act 18 of 1979) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Mysore Electrical Industries Limited (Acquisition of Shares) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Mysore Electrical Industries Limited (Acquisition of Shares) (Amendment) Act, 1981.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-third day of April, 1981 Repealed by Karnataka Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Mysore Electrical Industries Limited (Acquisition of Shares) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-clause (vi) of clause (f) of Section 2 of the Mysore Electrical Industries Limited (Acquisition of Shares) Act, 1979 (Karnataka Act 18 of 1979) (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act) the words "Fire and General" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been omitted.
Mysore Electrical Industries Limited (Acquisition of Shares) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 7 of the Principal Act - (i) for the words "within the period of one hundred and twenty days from the specified date" the words and figures "on or before the 30th day of June, 1981" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted; (ii) the proviso shall be omitted.
