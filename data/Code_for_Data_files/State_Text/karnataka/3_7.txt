Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition Act, 1977. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition Act, 1977 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1978) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition (Amendment) Act, 1984.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Thirtieth day of April, 1984
Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition Act, 1977 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1978) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) in sub-section (1), after clause (d), the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(da) "enfranchised inam" means an inam of which there is proof of enfranchisement as required under the Madras Enfranchised Inams Act, 1862 (Madras Act IV of 1862)."
Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 11 of the principal Act, the following proviso shall be inserted, namely - "Provided that where the inam is an enfranchised inam, such application by the inamdar including holder of a minor inam shall be made to the Tahsildar on or before the Thirty-first day of December, 1984. The application shall be decided by the Tahsildar after issuing individual notices to the concerned inamdars and after such verification and enquiry held in such manner as may be prescribed."
Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12 of the principal Act, after sub-section (2), the following proviso shall be inserted, namely - "Provided that such sanction shall not be necessary for an inamdar including the holder of a minor inam to alienate the land of which he is registered as an occupant in an enfranchised inam."
Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 36 of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely - "36-A. Transfer of cases -Any application pending before a Tribunal on the date of the commencement of the Karnataka Certain Inams Abolition (Amendment) Act, 1984, to which the proviso to Section 11 would be applicable if it were filed after such commencement, shall be transferred to the Tahsildar and shall be disposed of by him as if it had been filed before him."
