Karnataka Taxation Laws (Second Amendment) Act, 1996_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend certain taxation laws as in force in the State of Karnataka. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend certain taxation laws for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-seventh year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka Taxation Laws (Second Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Taxation Laws (Second Amendment) Act, 1996.1 2. It shall come into force with effect from the first day of April, 1996. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twentieth day of March, 1996
Karnataka Taxation Laws (Second Amendment) Act, 1996_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Karnataka Entertainments Tax Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act 30 of 1958) - (1) in Section 3-B, in sub-section (5), in clause (a), the words "or reduction in", "or reduced" and the words "or reduction", shall be omitted; (2) in Section 3-C, in sub-section (1), in clause (a), (b) and (c), for the words "one half of the rates specified therein", wherever they occur, the word "nil", shall be substituted; (3) in Section 4-A - (i) in sub-section (1) - (a) in the third proviso, in clauses (i) and (ii), for the words, figure and brackets "one half of the rates specified in the table under sub-section (1)" the word "nil", shall be substituted; (b) in the fourth proviso, in clause (a), for the words "one half of the rates specified therein", the word "nil", shall be substituted; (ii) in sub-section (5), in clause (a), the words "or reduction in", "or reduced" and the words "or reduction", shall be omitted; (4) in Section 7, in sub-section (5), the explanation shall be omitted.
