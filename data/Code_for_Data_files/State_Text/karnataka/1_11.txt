Karnataka Agricultural Credit Operations and Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Agricultural Credit Operations and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1974. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Agricultural Credit Operations and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1975) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Agricultural Credit Operations and Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Agricultural Credit Operations and Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 19841 . (2) It shall come into force at once. (First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, dated on the Twenty-eighth day of April, 1984)(Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-sixth day of April, 1984)
Karnataka Agricultural Credit Operations and Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Karnataka Agricultural Credit Operations and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1975) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) in Section 2 - (1) in clause (a), after the words pisciculture including the words the development of fisheries both inland and marine and, shall be inserted; (2) in clause (e), after item (vii), the following item shall be inserted, namely - (vii-a) the regional rural banks constituted under the Regional Rural Banks Act, 1976 (Central Act 21 of 1976); (3) in clause (f), after the word granted the words whether before or after the commencement of this Act shall be substituted.
Karnataka Agricultural Credit Operations and Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the Principal Act, in sub-section (1), for the words create a mortgage the words, create, by way of hypothecation or otherwise, a mortgage shall be substituted.
Karnataka Agricultural Credit Operations and Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 10 of the principal Act, in Chapter 3, the following section shall be inserted, namely - 10-A. Registration of discharge certificates - Where any document creating a charge or mortgage has been registered in accordance with Section 9 and the details thereof noted in the record of rights under Section 10 and the liability under the financial assistance has been discharged, the credit agency shall issue a certificate to that effect and the provisions of the said sections shall, mutatis-mutandis, apply to the registration of such certificate and to the deletion of the entry made in the record of rights.
Karnataka Agricultural Credit Operations and Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12 of the principal Act, after sub-section (2), the following Explanation and sub-section shall be inserted, namely - Explanation - For the purposes of this section, agriculturist includes his co-obligants and sureties and their legal representatives to the extent of the assets of the deceased in their hands. (3) The actual period during which any proceedings under this Act or rules made thereunder were pending shall be excluded while computing the period of limitation for filing a suit for the recovery of any moneys due in respect of financial assistance under this Act.
Karnataka Agricultural Credit Operations and Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 15 of the principal Act, the following shall be inserted, at the end, namely - or by which it is released from such mortgage or charge.
Karnataka Agricultural Credit Operations and Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 19-A of the principal Act, the following sections shall be inserted, namely - 19-B. Application of Indian Limitation Act, 1963 - The provisions of Sections 4, 5 and 15 of the Indian Limitation Act, 1963 (Central Act 36 of 1963) and Articles 19, 21, 25, 36, 37, 38 and 62 of the Schedule to the said Act, shall apply mutatis mutandis to all the proceedings under this Act and rules. 19-C. Burden of proof - Where validity of a mortgage or charge created under this Act is questioned in a Court of law, notwithstanding anything contained in any law for the time being in force, the burden of proving that it was not created for agricultural or valid purposes shall be on the party alleging it.
