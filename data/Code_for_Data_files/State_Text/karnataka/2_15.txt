Bangalore Development Authority (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Bangalore Development Authority Act, 1976. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Bangalore Development Authority Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1976) for the purpose hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-sixth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Bangalore Development Authority (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Bangalore Development Authority (Amendment) Act, 1995.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Fifth day of October, 1995
Bangalore Development Authority (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Bangalore Development Authority Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1976), in sub-section (3), in clause (c), after the words "Karnataka Engineering Service", the words "or an Officer employed in any undertaking owned or controlled by the State Government", shall be inserted.
