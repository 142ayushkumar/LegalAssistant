Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances Act, 1956. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1957) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-eighth year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1997.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the First day of September, 1997. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twentieth day of September, 1997 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as 'the principal Act') for the words "twenty thousand rupees", the words "fifty thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), for the words "three hundred litres", the words "five hundred litres" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), in clause (a), for the words "three hundred litres", the words "five hundred litres" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6-A of the principal Act, for the words "twelve thousand rupees", the words "thirty thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6-C of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), for the words "three hundred litres", the words "five hundred litres" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 6-D of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), in clause (a), for the words "three hundred litres", the words "five hundred litres" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 7 of the principal Act, for the words "twelve thousand rupees", the words "thirty thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 9 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), for the words "three hundred litres", the words "five hundred litres" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 10--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), in clause (a), for the words "three hundred litres", the words "five hundred litres" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 11--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2) - (i) in clause (b), for the words "six rupees", the words "eight rupees" shall be substituted; (ii) in clause (d), for the words "two hundred and fifty rupees", the words "five hundred rupees" shall be substituted; (iii) in the first proviso to clause (d), for the words "three hundred and fifty rupees", the words "six hundred and fifty rupees" shall be substituted.
