Karnataka Urban Land Tax Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to provide for the levy of tax on urban land in the State of Karnataka. Whereas, it is expedient to provide for the levy by the State Government of tax on urban land in the State of Karnataka; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Urban Land Tax Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Urban Land Tax Act, 1976.1 (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Karnataka. (3) (a) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of April 1976, in the Bangalore Urban agglomeration, the City of Hubli-Dharwar and the City Municipalities of Karnataka, Mangalore, Belgaum, Gulbarga, Bellary, Davangere, Bijapur, Shimoga and Bhadravathi. (b) It shall come into force in such other cities, city municipalities and town municipalities on such date or dates as the State Government may, by notification, specify and different dates may be specified for different cities, city municipalities and town municipalities. Received the assent of the Governor on the Thirteenth day of May, 1976
Karnataka Urban Land Tax Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Karnataka Municipalities Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1964) - (1) in sub-section (2) of Section 94 - (a) clause (a) shall be omitted; (b) in clause (b), for the words "on buildings" the words "on buildings or lands or both" shall be substituted; (2) in Schedule I, in paragraph A - (a) item (i) shall be omitted; (b) in item (ii), the words, figure and brackets "not falling under (i)" shall be omitted.
