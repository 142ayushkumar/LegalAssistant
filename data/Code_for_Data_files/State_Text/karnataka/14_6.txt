Karnataka Nurses, Midwives and Health Visitors (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Nurses, Midwives and Health Visitors Act, 1961. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Nurses, Midwives and Health Visitors Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 4 of 1962) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-second year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Nurses, Midwives and Health Visitors (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Nurses, Midwives and Health Visitors (Amendment) Act, 1981.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Eighth day of April, 1981 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Nurses, Midwives and Health Visitors (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 16 of the Karnataka Nurses, Midwives and Health Visitors Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 4 of 1962) for the words "one rupee", the words "five rupees" shall be substituted.
