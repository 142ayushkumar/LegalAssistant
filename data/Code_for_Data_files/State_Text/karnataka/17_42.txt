Renuka Yellamma Devasthana (Administration) (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Renuka Yellamma Devasthana (Administration) Act, 1974. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Renuka Yellamma Devasthana (Administration) Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 34 of 1974), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature, in the Forty-fourth year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Renuka Yellamma Devasthana (Administration) (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Renuka Yellamma Devasthana (Administration) (Amendment) Act, 1993.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Eighteenth day of May 1993
Renuka Yellamma Devasthana (Administration) (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In clause (14) of Section 3 of the Renuka Yellamma Devasthana (Administration) Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 34 of 1974) for the words "First day of July", the words "First day of April" shall be substituted.
