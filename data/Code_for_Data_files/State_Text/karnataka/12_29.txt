Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act No. 10 of 1962), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-third Year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1992.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twentieth day of April, 1992
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 77 of the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act No. 10 of 1962) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for the word "fifty", the word "seventy-five" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka For Section 109 of the principal Act, the following shall be substituted, namely - "109. Certain lands to be exempt from certain provisions - (1) Subject to the provisions of Karnataka Town and Country Planning Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act No. 11 of 1963) and such conditions and restrictions as may be specified, the State Government, may, by notification, exempt - (i) from the provisions of Section 79-A or 79-B or Section 80, any land in any area to be used for industrial development; or (ii) in the case of educational institution recognised by the State or Central Government, from the provisions of Section 79-A or 79-B or 80, any land in any area to be used for non-agricultural purpose: Provided that the extent of land in respect of which exemption is granted shall, in no case, exceed two units for industrial development and one unit for non-agricultural purpose. (2) Where any condition or restriction specified in the Notification under sub-section (1), has been contravened, the State Government, may, after holding an enquiry as it deems fit, cancel the exemption granted under that sub-section and the land in respect of which such cancellation has been made, shall, as penalty be forfeited to and vest in the State Government free from all encumbrances. No amount is payable therefor."
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1992_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 122-A of the principal Act, for the words, letter and figures "30th day of June, 1984", the words, letters and figures "31st day of December, 1995" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
