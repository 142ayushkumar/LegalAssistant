Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Civil Courts Act, 1964. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Civil Courts Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 21 of 1964) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-fourth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1993.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Twenty-ninth day of April, 1993. Received the assent of the Governor on the Tenth day of February, 1994
Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 22 of the Karnataka Civil Courts Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 21 of 1964) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) for the words "three thousand" and "five hundred" the words, "twenty-five thousand" and "three thousand" shall respectively be substituted.
Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) Notwithstanding anything contained in the principal Act or in any other law or provisions having the force of law, all suits pending on the date of publication of a notification under sub-section (1) of Section 22 of the principal Act which are triable by a Court of Civil Judge or a Munsiffs Court invested with the jurisdiction for trial of suits cognizable by a Court of Small Causes by virtue of such notification, shall stand transferred to and be disposed of by such Court by Civil Judge or Munsiffs Court, as the case may be, as if such suits had been instituted in such Court of Civil Judge or Munsiffs Court. (2) If there be any doubt as to which Court a suit shall stand transferred in accordance with the provisions of sub-section (1), the Court designated by the High Court shall be the Court to which such suit shall be transferred and the decision of the High Court shall be final.
Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment) Ordinance, 1993 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 3 of 1993) and the Karnataka Civil Courts (Amendment No. 2) Ordinance, 1993 (Karnataka Ordinance 9 of 1993) are hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinances, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
