Karnataka Coffee Husk Control Repealing Act, 1959_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to repeal the Coffee Husk Control Acts in force in the State of Karnataka. Whereas, it is expedient to repeal the Coffee Husk Control Acts in force in the State of Karnataka; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Tenth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Coffee Husk Control Repealing Act, 1959_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Karnataka Coffee Husk Control Repealing Act, 1959.1 Received the assent of the President on the Tenth day of January, 1960
Karnataka Coffee Husk Control Repealing Act, 1959_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka Coffee Husk Control Act, 1940 and the Coorg Coffee Husk Control Act, 1940, are hereby repealed.
