Industrial Disputes (Karnataka Amendment and Repealing) Act, 1959_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 and to repeal the Bombay Industrial Relations Act, 1946. Whereas, it is expedient to have a uniform law relating to industrial disputes in the whole of the State of Karnataka and for this purpose to amend the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 (Central Act XIV of 1947), as in force in the Madras Area and the Mysore Area respectively and to repeal the Bombay Industrial Relations Act, 1946 (Bombay Act XI of 1947), as in force in the Bombay Area; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Tenth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Industrial Disputes (Karnataka Amendment and Repealing) Act, 1959_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Industrial Disputes (Karnataka Amendment and Repealing) Act, 1959.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the President on the Tenth day of January, 1960
Industrial Disputes (Karnataka Amendment and Repealing) Act, 1959_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka (1) In Section 10 of the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, as amended by the Industrial Disputes (Madras Amendment) Act, 1949 (Madras Act XII of 1949), sub-section (3-A) shall be omitted. (2) Section 10-A of the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947, as inserted by the Industrial Disputes (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1953 (Karnataka Act 15 of 1953), shall be omitted.
Industrial Disputes (Karnataka Amendment and Repealing) Act, 1959_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) On and from the date of commencement of this Act, the Bombay Industrial Relations Act, 1946 (Bombay Act XI of 1947), as in force in the Bombay Area shall stand repealed and the Industrial Disputes Act, 1947 for the time being in force, shall be applicable to all industries to which the repealed Act was applicable: Provided that any proceeding pending before a Board of Conciliation, a Labour Court, an Industrial Court or a Wage Board constituted under the repealed Act, shall be decided and disposed of under the provisions of the said Act by such Board of Conciliation, Labour Court, Industrial Court or the Wage Board, as the case may be, as if the said Act had not been repealed by this Act; and save as aforesaid, no proceeding shall be entertained by a Board of Conciliation, a Labour Court, an Industrial Court or a Wage Board after the date of coming into force of this Act and every decision or order of any such Board of Conciliation, Labour Court, Industrial Court or Wage Board pronounced or made, before or after the date of coming into force of this Act, shall be enforced in accordance with the provisions of the said repealed Act. (2) The Industrial Disputes (Madras Amendment) Act, 1949 (Madras Act XII of 1949), as in force in the Madras Area and the Industrial Disputes (Karnataka Amendment) Act, 1953 (Karnataka Act 15 of 1953), as in force in the Mysore Area, are hereby repealed.
