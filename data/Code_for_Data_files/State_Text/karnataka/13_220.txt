Mysore Revision of State Laws Act, 1955_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka Whereas, for the purpose of bringing into accord with the Constitution of India and Central Acts extended to the State of Mysore, and for other purposes, it is expedient to repeal certain enactments and to amend certain other enactments. Be it enacted by the Mysore State Legislature in the Sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Mysore Revision of State Laws Act, 1955_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Mysore Revision of State Laws Act, 1955.1 Received the assent of the President on the Thirty-first day of December, 1955
Mysore Revision of State Laws Act, 1955_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka The enactments specified in the First Schedule are hereby repealed.
Mysore Revision of State Laws Act, 1955_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka The enactments specified in the Second Schedule are hereby amended to the extent and in the manner mentioned in the said Schedule. "First Schedule [See Section 2] The Betting Tax Act, 1932 Karnataka Act No. IX of 1932 Throughout the Act, for "Government" substitute "State Government". Section 1 - In sub-section (1), for "the Betting Tax Act, 1932", substitute "The Mysore Betting Tax Act, 1932". Sections 2 and 6 - For "the Mysore Race Courses Licensing Act, 1951", substitute "the Mysore Race Courses Licensing Act, 1952". Omit Sections 12, 13 and 14.
