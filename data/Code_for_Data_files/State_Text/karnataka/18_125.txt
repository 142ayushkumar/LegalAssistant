Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1972_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 34 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1972_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1972.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-third day of December, 1972 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Stamp (Amendment) Act, 1972_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In clause (a) of Section 11 of the Karnataka Stamp Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 34 of 1957), for the words "fifteen naye paise", the words "thirty paise", shall be substituted.
