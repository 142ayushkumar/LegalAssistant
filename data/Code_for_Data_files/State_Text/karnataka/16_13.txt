Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Panchayat Raj Act, 1993. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Panchayat Raj Act, 1993 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1993) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Fifty-first year of the Republic of India as follows:
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Second Amendment) Act, 2000.1 (2) The provisions of sub-clause (ii) of clause (1) of Section 3 shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the Fourth day of August, 1999, provisions of clause (2) of Section 3 shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the Sixteenth day of December, 1999, provisions of Section 2, sub-clause (i) of clause (1) and clause (3) of Section 3 shall be deemed to have been come into force with effect from the Twenty-eighth day of January, 1999, provisions of Sections 10 and 11 shall be deemed to have come into force on the Seventh day of December, 1999, and the remaining provisions shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-fifth day of April, 2000
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4 of the Karnataka Panchayat Raj Act, 1993 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1993) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) in sub-section (1) - (i) for the words "not less than ten thousand and not more than sixteen thousand", the words "not less than five thousand and not more than seven thousand" shall be substituted; (ii) in the first proviso, for the words "not less than six thousand", the words "not less than two thousand five hundred" shall be substituted; (iii) in the second proviso, for the words and brackets "a radius of eight kilometres (diameter of sixteen kilometres)" the words and brackets "a radius of five kilometres (diameter of ten kilometres)" shall be substituted; (iv) in the third proviso, for the words "either less than ten thousand or more than sixteen thousand", the words "either less than five thousand or more than seven thousand" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the principal Act - (1) in sub-section (1) - (i) for the words "every one thousand population", the words "every four hundred population" shall be substituted; (ii) second proviso shall be omitted. (2) after sub-section (2), the following provisos shall be inserted, namely - "Provided that at least one seat each shall be reserved in a Grama Panchayat for the persons belonging to the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes: Provided further that, if no person belonging to the Scheduled Castes is available the seat reserved for that category shall also be filled by the persons belonging to Scheduled Tribes and vice versa". (3) in sub-section (4), before the second proviso, the following proviso shall be inserted, namely - "Provided that the seats reserved under sub-sections (2), (3) and (4) shall be allotted by rotation, to different constituencies in the Panchayat area".
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12 of the principal Act, for the proviso to clause (j), the following shall be substituted, namely - "Provided that nothing in this clause shall apply to a person, if at the time of filing his nomination he gives an undertaking to construct within one year from the date of commencement of his term of office as a member, a sanitary latrine for the use of members of his family and also complies with such undertaking after becoming a member".
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 123 of the principal Act, the following proviso shall be inserted, namely - "Provided that at least one seat each shall be reserved in a Taluk Panchayat for the persons belonging to the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes".
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 128 of the principal Act, for the proviso to clause (j), the following shall be substituted, namely - "Provided that nothing in this clause shall apply to a person, if at the time of filing his nomination he gives an undertaking to construct within one year from the date of commencement of his term of office as a member, a sanitary latrine for the use of the members of his family and also complies with such undertaking after becoming a member".
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 162 of the principal Act, the following proviso shall be inserted, namely - "Provided that at least one seat each shall be reserved in a Zilla Panchayat for the persons belonging to the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes".
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 167 of the principal Act, for the proviso to clause (j), the following shall be substituted, namely - "Provided that nothing in this clause shall apply to a person, if at the time of filing his nomination he gives an undertaking to construct within one year from the date of commencement of his term of office as a member, a sanitary latrine for the use of the members of his family and also complies with such undertaking after becoming a member".
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 206 of the principal Act, for the words "one lakh rupees" the words "two lakh rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 10--> State(s): Karnataka Notwithstanding anything contained in any judgment, decree or order of any Court, Tribunal or other authority - (i) amendment to sub-section (1) of Section 4 and sub-sections (1) and (4) of Section 5 of the principal Act by the Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1999 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1999) and the Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Second Amendment) Act, 1999 (Karnataka Act 21 of 1999) (hereinafter referred to in this section as the said Acts) shall be deemed never to have been made and the provisions of sub-section (1) of Section 4 and sub-sections (1) and (4) of Section 5 of the principal Act as they stood prior to the commencement of the said Acts shall be deemed to have continued to be in force; and (ii) anything done or any action taken or any notification issued under Section 4 or 5 of the principal Act as amended by the said Acts on or after the commencement of the said Acts shall be deemed never to have been done, taken or issued, and accordingly the Panchayat area declared and any action taken or anything done or any notification issued under Section 4 or 5 of the principal Act before such commencement shall be and shall be deemed to be valid and to have continued to be in force.
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 11--> State(s): Karnataka Notwithstanding anything contained in Section 4 of the Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Amendment) Act, 1999 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1999) (hereinafter referred to in this section as the said Act), the Administrator, or as the case may be, members of the Administrative Committee including Adhyaksha and Upadhyaksha appointed by the Deputy Commissioner under the said section in respect of a Grama Panchayat existing on the date of commencement of the said Act shall, subject to the pleasure of the Deputy Commissioner, hold office till a Grama Panchayat is duly constituted under the principal Act as amended by the Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Second Amendment) Act, 2000, and in other respects, the provisions of the said section shall mutatis mutandis apply in respect of the Administrator, or as the case may be, an Administrative Committee so appointed.
Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 12--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Second Amendment) Ordinance, 1999 (Karnataka Ordinance 5 of 1999) and Karnataka Panchayat Raj (Third Amendment) Ordinance, 1999 (Karnataka Ordinance 6 of 1999) are hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinances shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
