Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Sixty-first year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2010. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964), in Section 2, after sub-section (8), the following shall be inserted, namely:- "(8a) "Director of Survey Settlement of Land Records" means and includes the Commissioner of Survey Settlement of Land Records or the Director of Survey Settlement of Land records as the case may be".
