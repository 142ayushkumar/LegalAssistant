Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Legal Aid Board Act, 1981 (Karnataka Act 32 of 1981). Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Legal Aid Board Act, 1981 (Karnataka Act 32 of 1981) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-eighth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1987.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Thirteenth day of May, 1987
Karnataka Legal Aid Board (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the Karnataka Legal Aid Board Act, 1981 (Karnataka Act 32 of 1981), after clause (d) the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(dd) the Commissioner and Secretary to Government, Revenue Department, ex officio".
