Karnataka Lokayukta (Amendment) Act, 1988_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Lokayukta Act, 1984. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Lokayukta Act, 1984 (Karnataka Act 4 of 1985) for the purpose hereinafter appearing. Be, it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the thirty-ninth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Lokayukta (Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Lokayukta (Amendment) Act, 1988.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on Twenty-fourth day of February, 1988 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Lokayukta (Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 8 of the Karnataka Lokayukta Act, 1984 (Karnataka Act 4 of 1985) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (1), in clause (b), for the words "Court or other authority" the words "Court Officer or other authority and has not availed of the same", shall be substituted".
Karnataka Lokayukta (Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12 of the principal Act, for sub-section (6), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely - "(6) The Lokayukta a shall present annually a consolidated report on the performance of his functions and that of the Upa-lokayukta under this Act to the Governor".
Karnataka Lokayukta (Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 17 of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely - "17-A. Power to punish for contempt - The Lokayukta or Upa-lokayukta shall have, and exercise the same jurisdiction powers and authority in respect of contempt of itself as a High Court has and may exercise, and, for this purpose, the provisions of the Contempt of Courts Act, 1971 (Central Act 70 of 1971), shall have effect subject to the modification that the references therein to the High Court shall be construed as including a reference to the Lokayukta or Upa-lokayukta, as the case may be".
Karnataka Lokayukta (Amendment) Act, 1988_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 19 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1) after the words, figures and brackets "clause (12) of Section 2", the words, figures, and brackets other than those falling under clause (ii) and (iv) of sub-section (1) of Section 7 shall be inserted.
