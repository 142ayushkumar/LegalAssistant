Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1957), for the purposes hereinafter appearing: Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-Second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1991. 1 2 (2) It shall come into force with effect from the First day of April, 1991. First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, on the Thirtieth day of March, 1991. Received the assent of the Governor on the Thirtieth day of March, 1991 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, with effect from 29-11-2000
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1957), (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (1),- (1) Clause (a) shall be renumbered as clause (aa), thereof and before the clause (aa) as so renumbered, the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(a) "Classic car" means a motor car manufactured during the period between the year 1940 and 1949 and registered with Karnataka Vintage and Classic car club, in addition to the registration of vehicle under Section 39 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 (Central Act 59 of 1988)"; (2) after clause (h), the following shall be inserted, namely:- "(hh) "Vintage Car" means a motor car manufactured during the year 1939 and earlier and duly registered with Karnataka Vintage and Classic car club in addition to the registration of vehicle under Section 39 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 (Central Act 59 of 1988)."
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Principal Act, in sub-section (1), after the second proviso, the following shall be inserted, namely:- "Provided also that in case of Vintage car and Classic car, the tax shall be levied at the rates specified in Part "AAAA" of the Schedule."
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), after the third proviso and before the explanation, the following shall be inserted, namely:- "Provided further that in case of Vintage car and Classic car specified in third proviso to sub-section (1) of Section 3, in respect of which tax is already paid prior to 1st day of April, 1991, the tax specified in the third proviso to sub-section (1) of Section 3 shall be levied after the expiry of the period for which the tax is paid under sub-section (1) and such tax shall be paid within one month from the date of expiry of the said period."
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 7 of the principal Act, after sub-section (4), the following shall be inserted, namely:- "(5) Notwithstanding anything contained in this Act and the Rules made thereunder, no refund of tax shall be allowed in respect of vehicles classified as Vintage or Classic cars where the tax is paid for the life time of the vehicle as per sub-section (1) of Section 3 of the Act, either on removal of the vehicle to any other State on transfer of ownership or on change of address or on the cancellation of registration."

Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 7--> State(s): Karnataka If any difficulty arises in giving effect to the provisions of the Principal Act as amended by this Act, the State Government, may, by notification in the Official Gazette, make such provisions as necessary or expedient for removing the said difficulty: Provided that no such order shall be made after the expiry of a period of two years from the date of commencement of this Act.
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 8--> State(s): Karnataka (1) Where, in respect of imported cars specified in items 15 and 16 the tax payable under the principal Act for a year, half year or quarter, as the case may be, has been paid before the commencement of the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1991, then notwithstanding that a taxation card has been granted therefor or endorsement in the taxation card for having collected the tax has been made, the excess tax paid if any, under Section 3 for the said year, half year or quarter, as the case may be, shall be refunded or adjusted towards any of the subsequent period in respect of which the tax is due: Provided that in cases where tax is already paid in advance for five years or ten years in one lumpsum at the old rates, the excess tax paid if any, shall be refunded or adjusted towards any of the subsequent period in respect of which the tax is due. (2) Where, in respect of a motor vehicle specified in item 4(1) (d) the tax payable under the principal Act for a year, half year or quarter as the case may be, has been paid before the commencement of the Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1991, then notwithstanding that a taxation card has been granted, therefor or endorsement in the taxation card for having collected tax has been made, the difference of tax payable under Section 3 for the said year, half year or quarter, as the case may be, shall be paid on or before the last date fixed under Section 4 of the principal Act for the next year, half year or quarter as the case may be: Provided that in cases where tax is already paid in advance for five years or ten years in one lumpsum at the old rates, the difference of tax payable shall be paid within a year from the commencement of the Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 1991.
