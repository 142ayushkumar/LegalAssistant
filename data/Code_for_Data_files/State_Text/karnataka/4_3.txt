Karnataka Debt Relief (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Debt Relief Act, 1976. Whereas it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Debt Relief Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1976) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Debt Relief (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Debt Relief (Amendment) Act, 1976.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the twenty-seventh day of October, 1976. Received the assent of the Governor on the twenty-seventh day of November, 1976
Karnataka Debt Relief (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4 of the Karnataka Debt Relief Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1976) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in clauses (e) and (f), after the words "Sub-Divisional Magistrate", the words "or any other Executive Magistrate authorised by the State Government in this behalf", shall be inserted.
Karnataka Debt Relief (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the principal Act,- (i) in sub-section (1), after the words "Sub-Divisional Magistrate", the words and brackets "or any other Executive Magistrate authorised by the State Government in this behalf, (hereinafter called "The Executive Magistrate")" shall be inserted; (ii) in sub-sections (2), (3), (4), (5), (6), (7) and (9), after the words "Sub-Divisional Magistrate", wherever they occur, the words "or the Executive Magistrate, as the case may be", shall be inserted.
Karnataka Debt Relief (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka Where an Executive Magistrate is authorised by the State Government under clauses (e) and (f) of Section 4 or under Section 5, every application made to and every statement filed before the Sub-Divisional Magistrate and proceedings connected therewith pending before him on the date of the commencement of this Act, shall stand transferred to such Executive Magistrate and be disposed of by him as if it had been made or filed before him.
Karnataka Debt Relief (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Debt Relief (Amendment) Ordinance, 1976 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 31 of 1976) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
