Karnataka Cinemas (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Cinemas (Regulation) Act, 1964. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Cinemas (Regulation) Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 23 of 1964) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be, it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-eighth year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka Cinemas (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Cinemas (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1997.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Eighth day of May, 1998
Karnataka Cinemas (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 8 of the Karnataka Cinemas (Regulation) Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 23 of 1964) (hereinafter referred to as the 'principal Act'), after clause (a), the following shall be inserted, namely - (aa) The applicant has paid the entire amount of tax or other dues payable by him under the Karnataka Entertainment Tax Act, 1958 (Karnataka Act 30 of 1958)."
Karnataka Cinemas (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 17 of the principal Act - (1) after sub-section (1), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(1-A) If the licensing authority is satisfied that the holder of a licence has, without reasonable cause, failed to comply with the conditions subject to which, licence has been granted or has contravened any of the provisions of this Act or the rules made thereunder, it may, after giving the holder of the licence an opportunity of showing cause, by an order in writing, revoke the licence or suspend it for such period as it may think fit." (2) In sub-section (2), after the words, figure and brackets "sub-section (1)", the words, figure, letter and brackets 'or sub-section (1-A)" shall be inserted.
