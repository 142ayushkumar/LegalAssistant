Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances Act, 1956. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Seventeenth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1966.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of May, 1964. Received the assent of the Government on the Thirty-first day of August, 1966 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12 of the Karnataka Ministers Salaries and Allowances Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1957), after sub-section (2), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(2-A) While touring outside India, every Minister and Deputy Minister shall be entitled - (a) for journeys by air, train or sea, to one and one-fifth times the single fare of the class by which be travels; (b) to daily allowance at rupees one hundred per day for the days of journey and for the days of halt at any place: Provided that the State Government may, in special circumstances, authorise by order payment of daily allowance at such rate not exceeding rupees one hundred and fifty per day as may be specified in the order."
