Destruction of Records (Mysore Extension and Amendment) Act, 1962_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to extend the Destruction of Records Act, 1917 (Central Act V of 1917) to the whole of the State of Mysore and further to amend it in its application to the State. Whereas it is expedient to extend the Destruction of Records Act, 1917 to the whole of the State of Mysore and further to amend it in its application to the State; Be it enacted by the Mysore State Legislature in the Thirteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Destruction of Records (Mysore Extension and Amendment) Act, 1962_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Destruction of Records (Mysore Extension and Amendment) Act, 1962.1 Received the assent of the Governor on the Seventh day of January, 1963
Destruction of Records (Mysore Extension and Amendment) Act, 1962_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka The Destruction of Records Act, 1917 (Central Act V of 1917) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), as in force in the Bombay Area, Madras Area and Coorg District and as amended by this Act, is hereby extended to, and shall be in force in the whole of the State of Mysore.
Destruction of Records (Mysore Extension and Amendment) Act, 1962_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 1 of the principal Act, after the expression "except the territories which, immediately before the 1st November, 1956 were comprised in Part B States", the expression "other than the territories specified in Clause (a) and Clause (c) of sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the States Reorganisation Act, 1956 (Central Act 37 of 1956)" shall be added.
Destruction of Records (Mysore Extension and Amendment) Act, 1962_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka The Hyderabad Destruction of Useless Records Act, 1305-F. (Hyderabad Area, is hereby repealed: Provided that any reference in any other enactment or in any instrument, to the repealed Act, shall be construed as a reference to the principal Act as extended by this Act: Provided further that any rule made under the repealed enactment shall continue in force and be deemed to have been made or issue under the provisions of the principal Act as extended by this Act unless and until it superseded by any rule made under the principal Act as extended by this Act.
