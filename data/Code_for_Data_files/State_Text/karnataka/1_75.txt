Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1961. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 9 of 1962),- Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Seventeenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1966.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the fifteenth day of March, 1962. Received the assent of the Governor on the Fifteenth day of March, 1966
Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 16 of the Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 9 of 1962), (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act), for the words "within the period of two years from the date on which this Chapter comes into force", the words, figures and brackets "within the period of two years from the date on which the Registrar is appointed under sub-section (1) of Section 14" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 18 of the Principal Act,- (1) in the heading, for the words "on the date of commencement of this Chapter", the words "not qualified for registration under this Act" shall be substituted; (2) for sub-section (1), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely:- "(1) The Registrar shall also prepare in accordance with the provisions of sub-section (2), a list of persons entitled to have their names entered under the said sub-section in a list called a list of persons in practice on the date of commencement of this Chapter." (3) in sub-section (2), for the words "within a period of two years from the date from which this Chapter comes into force", the words, figures and brackets "within a period of two years from the date of appointment of the Registrar under sub-section (1) of Section 14" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 21 of the Principal Act, for the words "two years from the date from which this Chapter comes into force", the words, figures and brackets "two years from the date on which the Registrar is appointed under sub-section (1) of Section 14" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ayurvedic and Unani Practitioners Registration and Medical Practitioners Miscellaneous Provisions (Amendment) Act, 1966_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 30 of the Principal Act, after sub-section (2), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely:- "(2-A) Any rule under this Act may be made to have effect retrospectively and when any such rule is made a statement specifying the reasons for making such a rule shall be laid before both Houses of the State Legislature along with the rule under sub-section (3). All rules made under this Act shall, subject to any modification made under sub-section (3), have effect as if enacted in this Act."
