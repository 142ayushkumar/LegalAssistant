Karnataka Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 1965_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Contingency Fund Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Contingency Fund Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 11 of 1957); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Sixteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 1965_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka This Act may be called the Karnataka Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 1965.1 Received the assent of the Governor on the Fourth day of November, 1965
Karnataka Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 1965_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Contingency Fund Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 11 of 1957) for the words "two crores of rupees", the words "four crores of rupees" shall be substituted.
