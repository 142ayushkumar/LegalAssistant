Karnataka Rent Control (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Rent Control Act, 1961. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Rent Control Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1961); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Rent Control (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Rent Control (Amendment) Act, 1977.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Thirtieth day of December, 1976. Received the assent of the President on the Thirteenth day of April, 1977 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Rent Control (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (4) of Section 1 of the Karnataka Rent Control Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1961), for the words and figures "Thirty-first December, 1976", the words and figures "Thirty-first December, 1977", shall be substituted.
Karnataka Rent Control (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka Rent Control (Fourth Amendment) Ordinance, 1976 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 35 of 1976) is hereby repealed.
