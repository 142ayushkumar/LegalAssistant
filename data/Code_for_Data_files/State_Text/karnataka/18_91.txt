Karnataka Silk Worm Seed, Cocoon and Silk Yarn (Regulation of Production, Supply, Distribution and Sale) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Silkworm Seed, Cocoon and Silk Yarn (Regulation of Production, Supply, Distribution and Sale) Act, 1959. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Silkworm Seed, Cocoon and Silk Yarn (Regulation of Production, Supply, Distribution and Sale) Act, 1959 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1960), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Silk Worm Seed, Cocoon and Silk Yarn (Regulation of Production, Supply, Distribution and Sale) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Silkworm Seed, Cocoon and Silk Yarn (Regulation of Production, Supply, Distribution and Sale) (Amendment) Act, 19811 2 . (2) It shall come into force at once. First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, on the Fourth day of April, 1981, Received the assent of the Governor on the Fourth day of April, 1981 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Silk Worm Seed, Cocoon and Silk Yarn (Regulation of Production, Supply, Distribution and Sale) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10 of the Karnataka Silkworm Seed, Cocoon and Silk Yarn (Regulation of Production Supply, Distribution and Sale) Act, 1959 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1960) (hereinafter referred to as the " Principal Act"), in clause (b) of sub-section (1), after the words "appoint a Market Officer", the words "to levy and collect the market fee payable under this Act in respect of cocoons sold or purchased in the cocoon market", shall be and shall be deemed always to have been inserted.
Karnataka Silk Worm Seed, Cocoon and Silk Yarn (Regulation of Production, Supply, Distribution and Sale) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10-A of the Principal Act, in clause (b) of sub-section (1), after the words "a Silk Market Officer", the words "to levy and collect the market fee payable in respect of silk yarn sold or purchased in the silk exchange" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been inserted.
Karnataka Silk Worm Seed, Cocoon and Silk Yarn (Regulation of Production, Supply, Distribution and Sale) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 18 of the Principal Act, in clause (h-a) of sub-section (2), for the word "traders", the words "licensed traders" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been substituted.
Karnataka Silk Worm Seed, Cocoon and Silk Yarn (Regulation of Production, Supply, Distribution and Sale) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka (1)-Notwithstanding anything contained in any decree, order, or judgment of any court or other authority or the Principal Act, Rule 17-1 of the Karnataka Silkworm Seed, Cocoon and Silk Yarn (Regulation of Production, Supply, Distribution and Sale) Rules, 1960 made in Notification No. CI 26 SLW 80, dated 8th December, 1980 shall be deemed to have been made and to have the force of law from the 20th day of December, 1979. (2) Levy and collection of any market fee made under the Principal Act and the rules made thereunder before the commencement of this Act by the Market Officer of a cocoon market or by the Silk Market Officer of a silk exchange shall, notwithstanding anything contained in any decree, order, or judgment of any Court or other authority be deemed to have been validly made and shall have effect for all purposes as if it had been made under the provisions of the principal Act and the rules made thereunder as amended by this Act and as if the said provisions were in force at all relevant times and accordingly no suit or other legal proceedings shall be entertained or continued in any Court or Tribunal on the ground that such levy and collection was made in pursuance of a provision of the Act or rules which was not in force at the relevant time.
