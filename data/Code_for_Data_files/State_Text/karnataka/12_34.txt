Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1962) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Fifty second year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 2001. 1 (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint. First published in the Karnataka Gazette Extra-ordinary on the 25th day of August, 2001.
Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In section 77A of the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1962) in sub-section (1), the words, brackets and figures "within one year from the date of commencement of the Karnataka Land Reforms (Amendment) Act, 1997" shall be and shall be deemed always to have been omitted.
