Mysore Factories (Control of Dismantling) (Repeal) Act, 1980_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to repeal the Mysore Factories (Control of Dismantling) Act, 1944, (Mysore Act XXIX of 1944). Whereas, it is expedient to repeal the Mysore Factories (Control of Dismantling) Act, 1944 (Mysore Act No. XXIX of 1944). Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-second year of the Republic of India as follows -
Mysore Factories (Control of Dismantling) (Repeal) Act, 1980_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Mysore Factories (Control of Dismantling) (Repeal) Act, 1980.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the President on the Seventeenth day of November, 1981 Repealed by Karnataka Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Mysore Factories (Control of Dismantling) (Repeal) Act, 1980_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka The Mysore Factories (Control of Dismantling) Act, 1944 (Mysore Act XXIX of 1944) is hereby repealed.
