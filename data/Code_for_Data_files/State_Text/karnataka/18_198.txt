Karnataka State Servants (Determination of Age) (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act to amend the Karnataka State Servants (Determination of Age) (Amendment) Act, 1974. Whereas, it is expedient to amend the Karnataka State Servants (Determination of Age) Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act No. 22 of 1974), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka State Servants (Determination of Age) (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka State Servants (Determination of Age) (Amendment) Act, 1977.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-eighth day of July 1977
Karnataka State Servants (Determination of Age) (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the Karnataka State Servants (Determination of Age) Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 22 of 1974) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) in Section 3,- (a) in sub-section (2), after the words "The appointing authority shall" the words "notwithstanding anything contained in any judgment, decree or order of any Court" shall be inserted; (b) in the proviso to sub-section (2), after the words "by the State servant" the words "after he become such servant" shall be inserted.
Karnataka State Servants (Determination of Age) (Amendment) Act, 1977_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the principal Act, in the first proviso to sub-section (1), after the words "by the State Servant", the words "after he became such servant" shall be inserted.
