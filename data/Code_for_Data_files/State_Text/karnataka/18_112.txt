Karnataka Societies Registration (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Societies Registration Act, 1960. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Societies Registration Act, 1960 (Karnataka Act 17 of 1960) for the purposes, hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Societies Registration (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Societies Registration (Amendment) Act, 19901 2 . (2) It shall come into force on the First day of April, 1990. First published in the Karnataka Gazette, Extraordinary, on the First day of April, 1990, Received the assent of the Governor on the First day of April, 1990 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000.
Karnataka Societies Registration (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 8 of the Karnataka Societies Registration Act, 1960 (Karnataka Act 17 of 1960) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (i) in sub-section (1), for the words "fifty rupees", the words "one hundred rupees" shall be substituted; (ii) in sub-section (3) for the words "five rupees", the words "one hundred rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Societies Registration (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 9 of the principal Act - (i) in sub-section (2), for the words "twenty rupees", the words "fifty rupees" shall be substituted; (ii) in sub-section (3), for the words "two rupees", the words "one hundred rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Societies Registration (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 10 of the principal Act - (i) in sub-section (2), for the words "ten rupees", the words "fifty rupees" shall be substituted; (ii) in sub-section (3), for the words "two rupees", the words "one hundred rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Societies Registration (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 13 of the principal Act, for the words "two rupees" the words "one hundred rupees" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Societies Registration (Amendment) Act, 1990_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 24 of the principal Act, for the words "one rupee" occurring for the first time, the words "twenty-five rupees" and for the words "one rupee" occurring for the second time, the words "five rupees" shall respectively be substituted.
