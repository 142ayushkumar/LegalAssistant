Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Forest Act, 1963. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Forest Act, 1963 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1964) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1984.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the thirteenth day of January, 1984. Received the assent of the Governor on the Eleventh day of April, 1984
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 68 of the Karnataka Forest Act, 1963 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1964) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for the words and figures "The Forest Officer who made the seizure under Section 62, may", the words and figures "Subject to such rules as may be prescribed, the Forest Officer who made the seizure under Section 62 or any other Forest Officer, may", shall be substituted.
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 101-A of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted namely - "101-B. Supply of forest produce under agreement by the State Government -(1) No lease, agreement or any other document entered into by the State Government and providing for supply of fire-wood, timber or other forest produce by the State Government shall, at a time, be for a term exceeding five years and the supply under every such lease, agreement or other document shall be subject to the condition of availability of such tree, wood, timber or other forest produce. (2) Any lease or agreement or any other document providing for the supply of tree, wood, timber or other forest produce by the State Government, granted or entered into prior to the commencement of the Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1984 - (a) for a term exceeding five years and in force on the date of such commencement, shall cease to be in force on the expiry of the term specified therein or the period of five years from the date of such commencement, whichever is earlier; (b) shall be and shall be deemed to be subject to the condition of availability of such tree, wood, timber or other forest produce."
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka Section 104-E of the principal Act shall be omitted.
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka The Order No. A6 IND 76/80-81, dated 29th June, 1982 issued by the Chief Conservator of Forests fixing the seigniorage value of trees, timber and other forest produce specified therein shall be deemed to have been validly made under Section 101-A of the Karnataka Forest Act, 1963 and with effect from 23rd February, 1981.
Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Forest (Amendment) Ordinance, 1984 (Karnataka Ordinance 3 of 1984) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
