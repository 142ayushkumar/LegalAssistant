Karnataka Lokayukta (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Lokayukta Act, 1984. It is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Lokayukta Act, 1984 (Karnataka Act 4 of 1985) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be, it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-second year of Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Lokayukta (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Lokayukta (Amendment) Act, 1991.1 2 (2) Section 2 shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the 1st day of January, 1991 and the remaining provisions shall be deemed to have come into force from the 8th day of August, 1991. Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixth day of November, 1991 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Lokayukta (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the Karnataka Lokayukta Act, 1984 (Karnataka Act 4 of 1985) (hereinafter referred to as the "principal Act") in sub-section (3), for the words, "of seven thousand and five hundred rupees and six thousand rupees respectively", the words "equal to that of the Chief Justice of a High Court and that of a Judge of the High Court respectively" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Lokayukta (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 23 of the principal Act, after sub-section (2), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(2-A) Any rule made under this Act may be made with retrospective effect and when such a rule is made the reasons for making the rule shall be specified in a statement laid before both Houses of the State Legislature, subject to any modification made under sub-section (3) every rule made under this Act shall have effect as if enacted in this Act".
Karnataka Lokayukta (Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Lokayukta (Amendment) Ordinance, 1991 (Karnataka Ordinance 9 of 1991) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
