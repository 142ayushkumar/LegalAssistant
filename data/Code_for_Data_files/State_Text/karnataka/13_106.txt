Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Municipal Corporations Act, 1976 Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Municipal Corporations Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 14 of 1977); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirtieth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1979.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Seventeenth day of November, 1978. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-first day of February 1979 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 4 of the Karnataka Municipal Corporations Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act No. 14 of 1977) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (2), in clause (a), for the words "three months" the words "one month" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Municipal Corporations (Amendment) Ordinance 1978 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 12 of 1978) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal any action taken or any appointment, notification, order, scheme, rule form or bye-law made or issued under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been taken, made or issued under the principal Act as amended by this Act and shall continue in force accordingly unless and until superseded by any action taken or any appointment, notification, order, scheme, rule, form or bye-law made or issued under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
