Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-seventh year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1976. 1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the First day of July, 1975. Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixth day of March, 1976 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the proviso to Section 6-B of the Karnataka Sales Tax Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 25 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) for the words "three per cent", the words "four per cent" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka At the end of Section 25-B of the principal Act, the following provisos shall be added, namely - "Provided that in respect of purchase of sugarcane by manufacturers of jaggery using a power crusher, the tax shall, at the option of the assessee, be levied and collected at the following compounded rates, in lieu of tax payable under sub-section (1) - Rs. 200 per annum per motor horse power of power crusher: Provided further that the amount of tax calculated in accordance with the preceding proviso shall be paid in the manner and within such time as may be prescribed".
Karnataka Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka The Karnataka Sales Tax (Fourth Amendment) Ordinance, 1975 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 17 of 1975) is hereby repealed: Provided that anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
