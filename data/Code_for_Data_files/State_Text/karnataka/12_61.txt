Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964) for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1983.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Twenty- eighth day of June, 1983. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twelfth day of September, 1983 Karnataka Act No. 20 of 1983 repealed by the Karnataka Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 95 of the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 12 of 1964) - (1) after sub-section (2), the following proviso shall be inserted, namely: "Provided that in Dakshina Kannada District, subject to any law for the time being in force regarding erection of,buildings or the construction of wells or tanks, an occupant of dry (punja) land who is not - (a) a person registered or liable to be registered as an occupant of such land under Section 48-A of the Karnataka Land Reforms Act, 1961 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1962); or (b) a grantee of such land under Section 77 of the said Act, may, without obtaining the permission required under this sub-section and notwithstanding anything contained therein, divert such land or part thereof to any other purpose after sending a prior notice in that behalf, in the prescribed form to the Tahsildar and paying in the prescribed manner, the fine prescribed under sub-section (7) "and (2) in sub-section (7), after the words, brackets and figure "permitted under sub-section (2)", the words "or is diverted under the proviso to the said sub-section" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Land Revenue (Amendment) Ordinance, 1983 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 7 of 1983) is hereby, repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
