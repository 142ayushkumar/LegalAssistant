Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Second Amendment) Act, 1984_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1957), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-fourth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Second Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Second Amendment) Act, 1984.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Thirtieth day of April, 1984 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Second Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 2 of the Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1957 (Karnataka Act 35 of 1957), (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), after clause (e), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(ee) "Private service vehicle" means an omnibus constructed or adapted to carry more than nine persons (excluding the driver) and used by or on behalf of the owner of such vehicle for the purpose of carrying persons for or in connection with his trade or business or otherwise than for hire or reward;"
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Second Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 7 of the Principal Act, the following explanation shall be inserted at the end, namely:- "Explanation - For the purpose of this section tax includes surcharge and additional surcharge payable under Sections 3-A and 3-B."
Karnataka Motor Vehicles Taxation (Second Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In the Schedule to the principal Act, in Part 'A',- (a) in sub-items (a) and (b) of Item 1, for the words and figures "3 1/2 horse-power" wherever they occur, the figures and letters "300 C.C." shall be substituted; (b) after Item 7, the following shall be inserted, namely:- "7-A.
