Karnataka Legislature Salaries and Pensions (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries and Pensions Act, 1956. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Legislature Salaries and Pensions Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1957) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirtieth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Legislature Salaries and Pensions (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Legislature Salaries and Pensions (Amendment) Act, 1979.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Twenty-seventh day of December, 1978. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Seventeenth day of February, 1979
Karnataka Legislature Salaries and Pensions (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 1 of the Karnataka Legislature Salaries and Pensions Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 2 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) for the words "and Pensions", the words "Pensions and Allowances" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries and Pensions (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the principal Act, for the words "The furnishing of the residence provided under this sub-section shall be on such scales as may be prescribed.", the words "The residence provided under this sub-section shall be furnished on such scales, and the plinth area thereof shall not exceed such limits, as may be prescribed." shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries and Pensions (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 5 of the principal Act, for the words "seven hundred and fifty", the words "three hundred and fifty" shall be and shall be deemed to have been substituted on the 23rd day of March, 1972 and for the words "three hundred and fifty" as so substituted, the words "five hundred" shall be and shall be deemed to have been substituted on the 27th day of May, 1974 and for the words "five hundred" as so substituted, the words "seven hundred and fifty" shall be and shall be deemed to have been substituted on the 26th day of December, 1978.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries and Pensions (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In clause (b) of sub-section (2) of Section 8 of the principal Act, for the words "one rupee", the words "seventy-five paise" shall be and shall be deemed to have been substituted on the 26th day of December, 1978.

Karnataka Legislature Salaries and Pensions (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 12--> State(s): Karnataka For sub-section (1) excluding the Explanation of Section 11-B of the principal Act, the following sub-section shall be and shall be deemed to have been substituted on the 26th day of December, 1978 namely - "(1) Where a member dies before the expiry of his term, there shall be paid to his family a family pension at the rate of two hundred rupees per mensem for the remaining period for which such member would have, but for his death, continued as member".
Karnataka Legislature Salaries and Pensions (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 13--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12 of the principal Act - (1) in clause (ccc), for the words "a Chairman", the words "a Deputy Minister, the Chairman" shall be substituted; (2) in clause (g), for the words "a member shall be entitled", the words "a member including a Minister, a Minister of State, a Deputy Minister, the Chairman, the Speaker, the Deputy Chairman, the Deputy Speaker, a Leader of the Opposition and a Government Chief Whip shall be entitled" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Legislature Salaries and Pensions (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 14--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Legislature Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Second Amendment) Ordinance, 1978 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 16 of 1978) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal any action taken or any appointment, notification, order scheme, rule, form or bye-law made or issued under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been taken, made or issued under the principal Act as amended by this Act and shall continue in force accordingly unless and until superseded by any action taken or any appointment, notification, order, scheme, rule, form or bye-law made or issued under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
