Karnataka State Aid to Industries (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka State Aid to Industries Act, 1959. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka State Aid to Industries Act, 1959 (Karnataka Act 8 of 1960) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Twenty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka State Aid to Industries (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka State Aid to Industries (Amendment) Act, 1978.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Thirteenth day of September, 1978 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka State Aid to Industries (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka After Section 22-A of the Karnataka State Aid to Industries Act, 1959 (Karnataka Act 8 of 1960) (hereinafter referred to as the "Principal Act"), the following section shall be inserted, namely - "22-B. Delegation of powers - The Government may, subject to such conditions as may be prescribed, by notification, delegate all or any of its powers under this Act, other than the power to make rules, to any officer or authority subordinate to it".
Karnataka State Aid to Industries (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 23 of the principal Act - (i) in sub-section (2), in clause (xii), the word "and" at the end shall be omitted, and after clause (xii) as so amended, the following clause shall be inserted, namely - "(xii-a) The conditions subject to which the Government may delegate its power under Section 22-B; and" (ii) sub-section (3) shall be renumbered as sub-section (4) thereof and before sub-section (4) as so renumbered, the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely - "(3) A rule under this Act may be made to have retrospective effect and when such a rule is made the reasons for making the rule shall be specified in a statement which shall be laid before both Houses of the State Legislature. Subject to any modification made under sub-section (4), any rule made under this Act shall have effect as if enacted in this Act."
