Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka State Universities Act, 1976. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka State Universities Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1976), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-ninth year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1998.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Fifteenth day of April, 1999
Karnataka State Universities (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Karnataka State Universities Act, 1976 (Karnataka Act 28 of 1976) - (1) after sub-section (1-B), the following shall be inserted, namely - "(1-C) Notwithstanding anything contained in sub-sections (1), (1-A) and (1-B), after the formation of new districts on or after 1st day of August, 1986, in accordance with Section 4 of the Karnataka Land Revenue Act, 1964 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1964) - (a) the Bangalore University shall have territorial jurisdiction over the area comprising the districts of Bangalore, Bangalore Rural, Kolar and Tumkur; (b) the Gulbarga University shall have territorial jurisdiction over the area comprising the districts of Bellary, Bidar, Gulbarga, Koppal and Raichur; (c) the Karnatak University shall have territorial jurisdiction over the area comprising the districts of Bagalkot, Belgaum, Bijapur, Dharwar, Gadag, Haveri and Uttara Kannada; (d) the Mysore University shall have territorial jurisdiction over the area comprising the districts of Chamarajanagar, Hassan, Mandya and Mysore; (e) the Kuvempu University shall have territorial jurisdiction over the area comprising the districts of Chickmagalur, Chitradurga, Davanagere and Shimoga; (f) the Mangalore University shall have territorial jurisdiction over the area comprising the districts of Dakshina Kannada, Kodagu and Udupi".
