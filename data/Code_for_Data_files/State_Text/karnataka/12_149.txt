Karnataka Lifts (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Lifts Act, 1974. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Lifts Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 24 of 1974) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-fourth year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Lifts (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Lifts (Amendment) Act, 1993.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-fifth day of May, 1993 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Lifts (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Karnataka Lifts Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 24 of 1974) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) - (i) in sub-section (2) for the words "six months", the words "twelve months" shall be substituted; (ii) after sub-section (2), the following proviso shall be inserted, namely - "Provided that on an application being made to the authorised officer and on his recommendation, the Government, if satisfied, may extend the said period by a period not exceeding six months".
Karnataka Lifts (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (4) of Section 4 of the principal Act, for the words "one hundred", the words "two hundred and fifty" shall be substituted.
Karnataka Lifts (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12 of the principal Act, for the word "fifty", the words "one hundred" shall be substituted.
