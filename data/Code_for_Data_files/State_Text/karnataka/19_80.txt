Karnataka Traffic Control (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Traffic Control Act, 1960. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Traffic Control Act, 1960 (Karnataka Act 18 of 1960), for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-seventh year of the Republic of India as follows -
Karnataka Traffic Control (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Traffic Control (Amendment) Act, 1986.1 2 (2) It shall come into force at once. Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000 Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-second day of May, 1986
Karnataka Traffic Control (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka clause (1) of sub-section (2) of Section 14 of the Karnataka Traffic Control Act, 1960 (Karnataka Act 18 of 1960), for the words "one person", the words "two persons" shall be substituted.
