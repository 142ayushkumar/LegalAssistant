Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) Act, 1974. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 36 of 1974); Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1981.1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the Sixth day of April, 1981
Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 5 of the Karnataka Electricity Supply Undertakings (Acquisition) Act, 1974 (Karnataka Act 36 of 1974) after clause (viii) of sub-section (2), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(ix) a sum equal to ten per cent of the aggregate of the amounts specified in clauses (i) to (vi), in consideration of the compulsory nature of acquisition."
