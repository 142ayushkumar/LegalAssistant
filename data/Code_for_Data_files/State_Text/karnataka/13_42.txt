Karnataka Ministers Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Ministers Salaries, Pensions and Allowances Act, 1956. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Ministers Salaries, Pensions and Allowances Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1957) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Thirtieth Year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka Ministers Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Ministers Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1979.1 2 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the Twenty-seventh day of December, 1978. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-fourth day of February, 1979 Repealed by Act No. 22 of 2000, w.e.f. 29-11-2000
Karnataka Ministers Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In the long title, preamble and Section 1 of the Karnataka Ministers Salaries, Pensions and Allowances Act, 1956 (Karnataka Act 5 of 1957) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) for the words "Salaries, Pensions and Allowances", wherever they occur, the words "Salaries and Allowances" shall be and shall be deemed to have been substituted on the 26th day of December, 1978.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 3--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the principal Act, for the words "The furnishing of a residence provided under this sub-section shall be on such scales as may be prescribed" the words, "The residence provided under this sub-section shall be furnished on such scales, and the plinth area thereof shall not exceed such limits, as may be prescribed," shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 4--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 5 of the principal Act, for the words "seven hundred and fifty", the words "three hundred and fifty" shall be and shall be deemed to have been substituted on the 23rd day of March, 1972 and for the words "three hundred and fifty" as so substituted, the words "five hundred" shall be and shall be deemed to have been substituted on the 27th day of May, 1974 and for the words "five hundred" as so substituted, the words "seven hundred and fifty" shall be and shall be deemed to have been substituted on the 26th day of December, 1978.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 5--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (1) of Section 6-B of the principal Act, for the words "The furnishing of a residence provided under this sub-section shall be on such scales as may be prescribed" the words "The residence provided under this sub-section shall be furnished on such scales, and the plinth area thereof shall not exceed such limits, as may be prescribed", shall be substituted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 6--> State(s): Karnataka In sub-section (2) of Section 6-C of the principal Act, for the words "seven hundred and fifty", the words "three hundred and fifty" shall be and shall be deemed to have been substituted on the 23rd day of March, 1972 and for the words "three hundred fifty" as so substituted the words "five hundred" shall be and shall be deemed to have been substituted on the 27th day of May, 1974 and for the words "five hundred" as so substituted, the words "seven hundred and fifty" shall be and shall be deemed to have been substituted on the 26th day of December, 1978.

Karnataka Ministers Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 9--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 12 of the principal Act - (1) in clause (b) of sub-section (2), for the words "one rupee", the words "seventy-five paise" shall be and shall be deemed to have been substituted on the 26th day of December, 1978; (2) in clause (a) of sub-section (2-A), after the words "on duty", the words "or travelling outside India for medical treatment" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 10--> State(s): Karnataka Section 12-A of the principal Act shall be and. shall be deemed to have been omitted on the 26th day of December, 1978.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 11--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 13 of the principal Act, after the words "any other place" the words "whether within or outside India" shall be inserted.
Karnataka Ministers Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 12--> State(s): Karnataka (1) The Karnataka Ministers Salaries, Pensions and Allowances (Amendment) Ordinance, 1978 (Karnataka Ordinance No. 15 of 1978) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, any action taken or any appointment, notification, order, scheme, rule form or bye-law made or issued under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been taken, made or issued under the principal Act as amended by this Act and shall continue in force accordingly unless and until superseded by any action taken or any appointment, notification, order, scheme, rule, form or bye-law made or issued under the principal Act as amended by his Act.
