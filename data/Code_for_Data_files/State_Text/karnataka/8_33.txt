Karnataka Housing Board (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section Preamble--> State(s): Karnataka An Act further to amend the Karnataka Housing Board Act, 1962. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Karnataka Housing Board Act, 1962 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1963), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted by the Karnataka State Legislature in the Forty-ninth year of the Republic of India, as follows -
Karnataka Housing Board (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 1--> State(s): Karnataka (1) This Act may be called the Karnataka Housing Board (Amendment) Act, 1998.1 (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government, may by notification, appoint. Received the assent of the Governor on the Twenty-sixth day of April, 1999
Karnataka Housing Board (Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 2--> State(s): Karnataka In Section 3 of the Karnataka Housing Board Act, 1962 (Karnataka Act 10 of 1963), in sub-section (2), in clause (i), the following shall be inserted, at the end namely - "of which two shall be women".
