Kerala Finance Act, 1996_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala An Act to give effect, to certain financial proposals of the Government of Kerala for the Financial Year 1996-97. Whereas it is expedient to give effect to certain financial proposals of the Government for Financial Year of 1996-97. Be it enacted in the Forty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Kerala Finance Act, 1996_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala (1) This Act may be called the Kerala Finance Act, 19961 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 29th day of July, 1996. Published in Kerala Gazette Extraordinary No. 1344 dated 22-11-1996 Date of Commencement: 29-7-1996.
