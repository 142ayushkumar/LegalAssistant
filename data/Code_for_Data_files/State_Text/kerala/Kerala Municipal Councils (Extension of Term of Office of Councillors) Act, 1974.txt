Kerala Municipal Councils (Extension of Term of Office of Councillors) Act, 1974_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala An Act to provide for the extension of the term of office of the Councillors of municipalities other than the Palai, Punalur, Chalakudy, Tirur and Malappuram Municipalities. Whereas it is necessary to provide for the extension of the term of office of the councillors of all the municipalities other than the Palai, Punalur, Chalakudy, Tirur and Malappuram Municipalities; Be it enacted in the Twenty fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Kerala Municipal Councils (Extension of Term of Office of Councillors) Act, 1974_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala (1) This Act may be called the Kerala Municipal Councils (Extension of Term of Office of Councillors) Act, 19741 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 27th day of May, 1974. Published in Kerala Gazette Ex. No. 774 dated 14-11-1974
Kerala Municipal Councils (Extension of Term of Office of Councillors) Act, 1974_Section 2--> State(s): Kerala Notwithstanding anything contained in the Kerala Municipalities Act, 1960 (14 of 1961), the term of office of the councillors of all the municipalities constituted under the said Act, other than the Palai. Punalur, Chalakudy, Tirur and Malappuram Municipalities, which expires on the first day of June, 1974, shall extend upto noon on the 1 [first day of October 1979.] Substituted by Acts 3/1975, 48/1976, 9/1978 and 7/1979.
Kerala Municipal Councils (Extension of Term of Office of Councillors) Act, 1974_Section 3--> State(s): Kerala (1) The Kerala Municipal Councils (Extension of Term of Office of Councillors) Ordinance, 1974 (4 of 1974), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under this Act.
