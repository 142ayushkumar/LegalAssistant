Evacuee Interest (Separation) Travancore-Cochin Supplementary Act, 1954_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala WHEREAS it is expedient to supplement certain provisions of the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Act, 1951 (Central Act LXIV of 1951) for the purpose hereinafter appearing: It is hereby enacted as follows:-
Evacuee Interest (Separation) Travancore-Cochin Supplementary Act, 1954_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala (1) This Act may be called the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Travancore-Cochin Supplementary Act, 19541 . (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Travancore-Cochin. (3) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 15th day of December, 1952. Published in the Gazette dated 13th April 1954.
Evacuee Interest (Separation) Travancore-Cochin Supplementary Act, 1954_Section 2--> State(s): Kerala The Evacuee Interest (Separation) Act, 1951 (Central Act of LXIV of 1951), shall, so far as it relates to any matter enumerated in List II in the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution of India, stand validated and binding as if it had been passed by the Ligislature of the State of Travancore-Cochin.
Evacuee Interest (Separation) Travancore-Cochin Supplementary Act, 1954_Section 3--> State(s): Kerala The Evacuee Interest (Separation) Travancore-Cochin Supplementary Ordinance, 1953 (Ordinance IV of 1953) is hereby repealed.
