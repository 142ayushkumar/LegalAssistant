Calicut Municipal Corporation (Extension of Time for Reconstitution) Act,1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala An Act to provide for the further extension of the time for the reconstitution of the Corporation of the City of Calicut. WHEREAS it is expedient to provide for the further extension of the time for the reconstitution of the Corporation of the City of Calicut; BE it enacted in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Calicut Municipal Corporation (Extension of Time for Reconstitution) Act,1976_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala (1) This Act may be called the Calicut Municipal Corporation (Extension of Time for Reconstitution) Act, 1976. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 7th day of November, 1976.
Calicut Municipal Corporation (Extension of Time for Reconstitution) Act,1976_Section 2--> State(s): Kerala In this Act, "Corporation" and "Council" means the Corporation of the City of Calicut.
Calicut Municipal Corporation (Extension of Time for Reconstitution) Act,1976_Section 3--> State(s): Kerala (1) Notwithstanding anything contained in the Kerala Municipal Corporations, Act, 1961 (30 of 1961) or in any notification issued thereunder, the time for reconstitution of the council shall stand extended up to noon on the 1 [1st day of October, 1979] (2) The council shall be reconstituted in accordance with the provisions of the said Act on the 2 [1st day of October 1979]. (3) The term of office of the councillors of the reconstituted council shall expire at noon on the 3 [1st day of October,1984]. Calicut Municipal Corporation (Extension of Time for Reconstitution) Act,1976_Section 4--> State(s): Kerala (1) All or any of the functions of the council and of the Mayor, the Deputy Mayor, the standing committees and other committees of the Corporation may, during the interval between the date of commencement of this Act and the date of reconstitution of the council in pursuance of sub-section (2) of section 3, be exercised and performed, as far as may be and to such extent as the Government may determine, by such person or persons as the Government may appoint in that behalf, and any such person may if the Government so direct, receive payment for his services from the Corporation fund. (2) The Government may determine the relationship of the person or persons appointed under sub-section (1), with the Government.
Calicut Municipal Corporation (Extension of Time for Reconstitution) Act,1976_Section 5--> State(s): Kerala the Government until the date of reconstitution of the council in pursuance of sub-section (2) of section 3, and the reconstituted council thereafter, shall be entitled to all the assets and be subject to all the liabilities of the Corporation as on the date of commencement of this Act and on the date of reconstitution respectively.
Calicut Municipal Corporation (Extension of Time for Reconstitution) Act,1976_Section 6--> State(s): Kerala (1) the Calicut Municipal Corporation (Extension of Time for Reconstitution) Ordinance, 1976 (12 of 1976), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under this Act
