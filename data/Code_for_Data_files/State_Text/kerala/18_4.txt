Tirur Municipal Council (Extension of Term of Office of Councillors) Act, 1979_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala An Act to provide for the further extension of the term of office of the councillors of the Tirur Municipality. Whereas it is expedient to provide for the further extension of the term of office of the councillors of the Tirur Municipality; Be it enacted in the Thirtieth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tirur Municipal Council (Extension of Term of Office of Councillors) Act, 1979_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala (1) This Act may be called the Tirur Municipal Council (Extension of Term of Office of Councillors) Act, 19791 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 30th day of November, 1978. Published in Kerala Gazette Ex. No. 233 dated 29-3-1979.
Tirur Municipal Council (Extension of Term of Office of Councillors) Act, 1979_Section 2--> State(s): Kerala Notwithstanding anything contained in the Kerala Municipalities Act, 1960 (14 of 1961), the term of office of the councillors of the Tirur Municipality constituted under the said Act, which expires on the 1st day of December, 1978, shall extend upto the 1st day of October, 1979.
Tirur Municipal Council (Extension of Term of Office of Councillors) Act, 1979_Section 3--> State(s): Kerala (1) The Tirur Municipal Council (Extension of Term of Office of Councillors) Ordinance, 1978 (29 of 1978), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under this Act.
