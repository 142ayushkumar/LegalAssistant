Evacuee Interest (Separation) Kerala Supplementary Act, 1960_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala An Act further to supplement the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Act, 1951. Whereas it is expedient further to supplement certain provisions of the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Act, 1951 (Central Act 64 of 1951), for the purpose hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted in the Eleventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Evacuee Interest (Separation) Kerala Supplementary Act, 1960_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala (1) This Act may be called the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Kerala Supplementary Act, 1960. 1 (2) It shall extend to the whole of the State of Kerala. (3) It shall come into force on such date as the Central Government may appoint under sub-section (2) of section 1 of the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Amendment Act, 1960 (Central Act 27 of 1960). Published in the Gazette Extraordinary dated 30. 09.1960.
Evacuee Interest (Separation) Kerala Supplementary Act, 1960_Section 2--> State(s): Kerala The amendments made to the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Act, 1951 (Central Act 64 of 1951), by the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Amendment Act, 1960 (Central Act 27 of 1960), shall, in so far as they relate to any matter enumerated in List II of the seventh Schedule to the Constitution, be as valid in the state of Kerala as if the provisions contained therein had been enacted by the legislature of the State of Kerala.
