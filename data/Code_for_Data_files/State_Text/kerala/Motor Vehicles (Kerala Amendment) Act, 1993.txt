Motor Vehicles (Kerala Amendment) Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala An Act to amend the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, in its application to the State of Kerala. Whereas it is expedient to amend the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, in its application to the State of Kerala for the purposes hereinafter appearing. Be it enacted in the Forty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Motor Vehicles (Kerala Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala (1) This Act may be called the Motor Vehicles (Kerala Amendment) Act, 19931 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 26th day of February, 1993. Issued under Published in Kerala Gazette Ex. No. 465 dated 22-4-1993.
Motor Vehicles (Kerala Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 2--> State(s): Kerala In Section 71 of the Motor Vehicles Act (Central Act 59 of 1988), (hereafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (4),- (i) for the words "five" and ' ten" the words "ten" and "fifty" shall, respectively, be substituted: (ii) for the words "any company", the words "any co-operative society or to any company" shall be substituted.
Motor Vehicles (Kerala Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 3--> State(s): Kerala (1) The Motor Vehicles (Kerala Amendment) Ordinance, 1993) (3 of 1993), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
