Kerala Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala An Act further to amend the Kerala Advocates' Welfare Fund Act, 1980. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Kerala Advocates' Welfare Fund Act, 1980 for the purposes hereinafter appearing; BE it enacted in the Forty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Kerala Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala (1) This Act may be called the Kerala Advocates' Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1993. 1 (2) It shall come into force at once. Received the assent of the Governor on the 3rd day of March, 1993, and Published in the Kerala Gazette Extraordinary No.233, dtd. the 3rd March 1993.
Kerala Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 2--> State(s): Kerala In section 15 of the Kerala Advocates' Welfare Fund Act, 1980 (21 of 1980) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act),- (i) in sub-section (3), for the words "rupees two hundred", the words "rupees three hundred" shall be substituted; (ii) in sub-section (5), for the words "fifty rupees" and "one hundred rupees", the words "one hundred rupees" and "two hundred rupees" shall, respectively, be substituted.
Kerala Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 3--> State(s): Kerala In section 16 of the principal Act,- (i) in sub-section (4), for the words "every four years of practice at the Bar" the words "every two years of practice at the Bar" shall be substituted; (ii) in sub-section (5), for the words "two thousand rupees per year of practice", the words "three thousand one hundred and twenty-five rupees per year of practice" shall be substituted; (iii) in sub-section (5A), for the words "and thereafter at the rate of two thousand rupees per year of practice", the words and figures "two thousand rupees per year of practice from the 6th day of May, 1989 till the date preceding the date of commencement of the Kerala Advocates' Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1993 and thereafter at the rate of three thousand one hundred and twenty-five rupees per year of practice" shall be substituted
