Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala Whereas it is expedient to consolidate and amend, the law relating to Government Securities issued by Our Government. It is hereby enacted as follows.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala (1) This Act may be called the Travancore Public Debt Act, 11221 . (2) It extends to the whole of Travancore. (3) It shall come into force on such date as Our Government may, by notification in Our Government Gazette, appoint in this behalf. Published in Kerala Gazette No. 51 dated 5-8-1947
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 2--> State(s): Kerala In this Act, unless there is anything repugnant in the subject or context.- (1) Government Security means- (a) a security created and issued whether before or after the commencement of this Act by Our Government for the purpose of raising a public loan, and having one of the following forms namely:- (i) stock transferable by registration in the books of the Public Debt Office; or (ii) a promissory note payable to order; or (iii) a debenture bond payable to order; or (iv) a bearer bond payable to bearer; or (v) a form prescribed in this behalf. (b) any other security created and issued by Our Government in such form and for such of the purposes of this Act as may be prescribed; (2) "Prescribed" means prescribed by rules made under this Act; (3) "Prescribed Officer" means the Financial Secretary or any other Officer appointed by Our Government to be the prescribed officer under this Act; (4) "Rupee" or "Rupees" means "British Rupee" or "British Rupees".
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 3--> State(s): Kerala (1) Subject to the provisions of Section 5, transfer of a Government security shall be made only in the manner prescribed for the making of transfers of securities of the class to which it belongs and no transfer of Government security made after the commencement of this Act shall be valid if- (a) it does not purport to convey the full title to the security, or (b) it is of such a nature as to affect the manner in which the security was expressed by Our Government to be held. (2) Nothing in this Section shall affect any order made by the Prescribed Officer under this Act or any order made by a court upon the Prescribed Officer.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 4--> State(s): Kerala Notwithstanding anything contained in the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1075 (II of 1075) as subsequently amended a person shall not by reason only of his having transferred a Government security, be liable to pay any money due either as principal or as interest thereunder.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 5--> State(s): Kerala (1) In the case of any public office to which Our Government may, by notification in Our Government Gazette, declare this sub-section to apply, a Government security in the form of stock or of a promissory note or of a debenture bond may be held in the name of the office. (2) When a Government security is so held, it shall be deemed to be transferred without any or further endorsement or transfer deed from each holder of the office to the succeeding holder of the office on and from the date on which the latter takes charge of the office. (3) When the holder of the office transfers to a party not being his successor in office a Government security so held, the transfer shall be made by the signature of the holder of the office and the name of the office in the manner and subject to the conditions laid down in Section 3. (4) This Section applies as well to an office of which there are two or more joint holders as to an office of which there is a single holder.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 6--> State(s): Kerala (1) No notice of any trust in respect of any Government security shall be receivable by Our Government nor shall Our Government be bounded by any such notice even though expressly given nor shall Our Government be regarded as a trustee in respect of any Government security. (2) Without prejudice to the provisions of sub-section (1). the Prescribed Officer may as an act of grace and without any liability to the Prescribed Officer to Our Government record in his books such directions by the holder of stock for the payment of interest on or of the maturity value of, or the transfer of, or such other matters relating to the stock as the Prescribed Officer thinks fit.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 7--> State(s): Kerala Subject to the provisions of Section 9 of the executors or administrators of a deceased sole holder of a Government security and the holder of a Succession Certificate issued under the Travancore Succession Certificate Act 1090 (IX of 1090) as subsequently amended or under Part X of the Indian Succession Act, 1925 (XXXIX of 1925) shall be the only persons who may be recognised by the Prescribed Officer as having any title to the Government security: Provided that nothing in this Section shall bar the recognition by the Prescribed Officer of the Manager or the sole surviving male member of a Hindu Undivided family governed by the Mitakshara law as having a title to a Government security when the security appears to the Prescribed Officer to stand in the name of the deceased member of the family and an application is made by such manager or sole surviving member for recognition of his title and is supported by a certificate signed by such authority and after such enquiry as may be prescribed to the effect that the deceased belonged to a Hindu undivided family governed by the Mitakshara law, that the Government security formed part of the joint property of the family and the applicant is the managing or sole surviving male member of the family. Explanation. - The expression "Hindu undivided family governed by the Mitakshara law" shall, for them purposes of this Section, be deemed to include undivided families governed by Malabar law.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 8--> State(s): Kerala Notwithstanding anything contained in the Travancore Contract Act, 1115 (X of 1115):- (a) when a Government security is held by two or more persons jointly and either or any of them dies, the title to the security shall vest in the survivors of those persons, and, (b) when a Government security is payable to two or more persons severally and either or any of them dies, the security shall be payable to the survivor or survivors of those persons or to the representative of the deceased or to any of them: Provided that nothing contained in this Section shall affect any claim which any representative of a deceased person may have against the survivor or survivors under or in respect of any security to which his Section applies. Explanation. - For the purpose of this Section a body incorporated under the Travancore Companies Act, 1114 (IX of 1114) as subsequently amended, or the Travancore Co-operative Societies Act V of 1112, or any other enactment for the time being in force, whether within or without Travancore, relating to the incorporation of association of individuals, shall be deemed to die when it is dissolved.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 9--> State(s): Kerala Notwithstanding anything contained in Section 7, if within six months of the death of a person who was the holder of a Government security or securities the face value of which does not in the aggregate exceed five thousand rupees, probate of his will or letters of administration of his estate or a succession certificate issued under the Travancore Succession Certificate Act, 1090 (IX of 1090) as subsequently amended or under part X of the Indian Succession Act, 1925 (XXXIX of 1925) is not produced to the Prescribed Officer or proof to the satisfaction of the Prescribed Officer that proceedings have been instituted to obtain one of these is not furnished, the Prescribed Officer may determine who is the person entitled to the security or securities or to administer the estate of the deceased and may make an order vesting the security or securities in the person so determined.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 10--> State(s): Kerala When a Government security or securities belong to a minor or a person who is insane and incapable of managing his affairs and the face value of the security or securities does not in the aggregate exceed five thousand rupees, the Prescribed Officer may make such order as he thinks fit for the vesting of such security or securities in such person as he considers represents the minor or insane person.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 11--> State(s): Kerala (1) If the person is entitled to a Government security applies to the Prescribed Officer alleging that the security has been lost, stolen or destroyed or has been defaced or mutilated the Prescribed Officer may, on proof to his satisfaction of the loss, theft, destruction, defacement or mutilation of the security, subject to such conditions and on payment of such fees as may be prescribed, order the issue of a duplicate security payable to the applicant. (2) If the person entitled to a Government security applies to the Prescribed Officer to have security converted into a security of another form or into a security issued in connection with another loan or to have it consolidated with other like securities, or to have it sub-divided, or to have it renewed, the Prescribed Officer may, subject to such conditions and on payment of such fees as may be prescribed, cancel the security and order the issue of a new security or securities. (3) The person to whom a duplicate security or a new security is issued under this Section shall be deemed for the purposes of Section 19 to have been recognised by the Prescribed Officer as the holder of the security; and a duplicate security or new security so issued to any person shall be deemed to constitute a new contract between Our Government and such person and all persons deriving title thereafter through him.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 12--> State(s): Kerala (1) If the Prescribed Officer is of opinion that a doubt exists as to the title to a Government security he may proceed to determine the person who shall for the purposes of the Prescribed Officer be deemed to be the person entitled thereto. (2) The Prescribed Officer shall give notice in writing to each claimant of whom he has knowledge, stating the names of all other claimants and the time when he will determine the person or persons who shall for his purposes be deemed to be entitled to the security. (3) The Prescribed Officer shall give notice in writing to each claimant of the result of the determination so made. (4) On the expiry of six months from the issue of the notices referred to in sub-section (3), the Prescribed Officer may make an order vesting in the person, found by him to be entitled to the security, the security and any unpaid interest thereon.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 13--> State(s): Kerala The rights of all persons in relation to Government Securities shall be determined in connection with all such questions as are dealt with by this Act, by the courts having jurisdiction in the place where interest on the securities is payable whether within or without Travancore.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 14--> State(s): Kerala (1) For the purpose of making any order which he is empowered to make under this Act, the Prescribed Officer may request a District Maginstrate to record or to have recorded the whole or any part of such evidence as any person whose evidence the Prescribed Officer requires, may produce. A District Magistrate so requested may himself record, or may direct any Magistrate of the first class subordinate to him and shall forward a copy thereof to the Prescribed Officer. (2) For the purpose of making a vesting order under this Act the Prescribed Officer may himself record the evidence of any person whose evidence he requires or may receive evidence upon affidavit. (3) A Magistrate or the Prescribed Officer acting in pursuance of this Section may administer an oath to any witness examined by him.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 15--> State(s): Kerala Where the Prescribed Officer contemplates making an order under this Act to vest a Government security in any person the Prescribed Officer may suspend payment of interest on or the maturity value of the security or postpone the making of any order under Section 11 or the registration of any transfer of the security until the vesting order has been made.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 16--> State(s): Kerala (1) Before making any order which he is empowered to make under this Act, the Prescribed Officer may require the person in whose favour the order is to be made to execute a bond with one or more sureties in such form as may be prescribed or to furnish security not exceeding twice the value of the subject matter of the order to be held at the disposal of the Prescribed Officer to pay to the Prescribed Officer or any person to whom the Prescribed Officer may assign the bond or security in furtherance of sub-section (2) the amount thereof. (2) A Court before which a claim in respect of the subject matter of any such order is established may order the bond or security to be assigned to the successful claimant who shall thereupon be entitled to enforce the bond or realise the security to the extent of such claim.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 17--> State(s): Kerala Any notice required to be given by the Prescribed Officer under this Act may be served by Anchal or Post, but every such notice shall also be published by the Prescribed Officer in Our Government Gazette, and on such publication shall be deemed to have been delivered to all persons for whom it is intended.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 18--> State(s): Kerala An order made by the Prescribed Officer under this Act may confer the full title to a Government security or may confer a title only to the accrued and accruing interest on the security pending a further order vesting the full title.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 19--> State(s): Kerala No recognition by the Prescribed Officer of a person as the holder of a Government security, and no order made by the Prescribed Officer, under this Act shall be called in question by any Court so far as such recognition or order affects the relations of Our Government or the Prescribed Officer with the person recognised by the Prescribed Officer as the holder of a Government security or with any person claiming an interest in such security; and any such recognition by the Prescribed Officer of any person or any order by the Prescribed Officer vesting a Government security in any person shall operate to confer on that person a title to the security subject only to a personal liability to the rightful owner of the security for money had and received on his account.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 20--> State(s): Kerala Where the Prescribed Officer contemplates making with reference to any Government security any order which he is empowered to make under this Act, and before the order is made the Prescribed Officer receives from a Court an order to stay the making of such order the Prescribed Officer shall either- (a) hold the security together with any interest unpaid or accruing thereon until the further orders of the Court are received, or, (b) apply to the court to have the security transferred to the Official Trustee in Travancore, pending the disposal of the proceedings before the Court.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 21--> State(s): Kerala Where the Prescribed Officer contemplates making an order under this Act vesting a Government security in any person the Prescribed Officer may, at any time, before the order is made, cancel any proceedings already taken for that purpose and may on such cancellation, proceed anew to the making of such order.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 22--> State(s): Kerala Save as otherwise expressly provided in the terms of a Government security, no person shall be entitled to claim interest on such security in respect of any period which has elapsed after the earliest date on which demand could have been made for the payment of the amount due on such security.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 23--> State(s): Kerala Our Government shall be discharged from all liability on a bearer bond or on any interest coupon of such a bond on payment to the holder of such bond or coupon on presentation on or after the date when it becomes due of the amount expressed therein, unless before such payment an order of a Court has been served on Our Government restraining them from making payment.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 24--> State(s): Kerala Where no shorter period of limitation is fixed by any law for the time being in force, the liability of Our Government in respect of any interest payment due on a Government security shall terminate on the expiry of six years from the date on which the amount due by way of interest became payable.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 25--> State(s): Kerala No person shall be entitled to inspect or to receive information derived from any Government security in the possession or custody of Our Government or from any book, register, or other document kept or maintained by or on behalf of Our Government in relation to Government securities or any Government security, save in such circumstances and manner and subject to such conditions as may be prescribed.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 26--> State(s): Kerala (1) If any person for the purpose of obtaining for himself or for any other person any title to a Government security, makes to any authority under this Act in any application made under this Act or in the course of any inquiry undertaken in pursuance of this Act any statement which is false and which he either knows to be false or does not believe to be true, he shall be punishable with imprisonment for a term which may extend to six months or with fine or with both. (2) No court shall take cognisance of any offence under sub-section (1) except on the complaint of the Prescribed Officer.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 27--> State(s): Kerala The signature of the person authorised to sign Government securities on behalf of Our Government may be printed, engraved or lithographed, or impressed by such other mechanical process as Our Government may direct on the securities. (2) A signature so printed, engraved, lithographed or otherwise impressed shall be as valid as if it had been inscribed in the proper handwriting of the person so authorised.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 28--> State(s): Kerala (1) Our Government may, subject to the condition of previous publication, by notification in Our Government Gazette, make rules to carry out the purposes of this Act. (2) In particular and without prejudice to the generality of the foregoing power such rules may provide for all or any of the following matters, namely:- (a) the forms in which Government securities may be issued; (b) the form of the obligations referred to in clause (v) of sub-clause (a) of clause (1) of Section 2. (c) the manner in which different forms of Government Securities - may be transferred; (d) the holding of Government Securities in the form of stock by the holders of offices other than public offices, and the manner in which and the conditions subject to which Government Securities so held may be transferred; (e) the manner in which payment of interest in respect of Government Securities is to be made and acknowledged; (f) the conditions governing the grant of duplicate, renewed, converted, consolidated and sub-divided Government Securities; (g) the fees to be paid in respect of the issue of duplicate Government Securities and of the renewal, conversion, consolidation and subdivision of Government Securities; (h) the form in which receipt of a Government Security delivered for discharge, renewal, conversion, consolidation or sub-division is to be acknowledged; (i) the manner of attestation of documents relating to Government securities in the form of stock; (j) the manner in which any document relating to a Government Security or any endorsement on a promissory note issued by Our Government may, on the demand of a person who from any cause is unable to write, be executed on his behalf; (k) the form of the bonds referred to the sub-section (1) of Section 16; (l) the circumstance and the manner in which and the conditions subject to which inspection of Government Securities, books, registers and other documents may be allowed or information there from may be given under Section 25; (m) the procedure to be followed in making vesting orders; (n) the authority by whom the certificate referred to in the proviso to Section 7 is to be granted and the manner of making the inquiry therein mentioned. (3) Rules made under this Act shall be published in Our Government Gazette and shall thereupon have effect as if enacted in this Act.
Travancore Public Debt Act, 1947_Section 29--> State(s): Kerala The Travancore Government Securities Act, 1099 (III of 1099), shall cease to apply to Government Securities to which this Act applies and to all matters for which provision is made by this Act.
