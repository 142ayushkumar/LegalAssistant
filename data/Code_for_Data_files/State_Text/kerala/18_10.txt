Travancore-Cochin Electricity Supply Surcharge (Repeal) Act, 1971_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala An Act to repeal Travancore-Cochin Electricity Supply Act, 1955 Whereas it is necessary to repeal the Travancore-Cochin Electricity Supply Surcharge Act, 1955; Be it enacted in the Twenty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Travancore-Cochin Electricity Supply Surcharge (Repeal) Act, 1971_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala (1) This Act may be called the Travancore-Cochin Electricity Supply Surcharge (Repeal) Act, 19711 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st day of April, 1968. Published in Kerala Gazette Ex. No. 301 dated 20-12-1969
Travancore-Cochin Electricity Supply Surcharge (Repeal) Act, 1971_Section 2--> State(s): Kerala The Travancore-Cochin Electricity Supply Surcharge Act, 1955 (14 of 1955), is hereby repealed.
Travancore-Cochin Electricity Supply Surcharge (Repeal) Act, 1971_Section 3--> State(s): Kerala Any amount of surcharge collected by the Kerala State Electricity Board and payable to the Government and any arrears of surcharge recoverable under or by virtue of the provisions of the Act, repealed by Section 2 shall credited, or shall be in the revenue accounts of the said Board as if it were the revenue accruing to that Board, notwithstanding anything contained in the Act so repealed.
