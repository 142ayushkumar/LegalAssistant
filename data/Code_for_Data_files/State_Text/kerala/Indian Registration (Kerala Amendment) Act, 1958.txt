Indian Registration (Kerala Amendment) Act, 1958_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala AN ACT to amend the Indian Registration Act, 1908, in its application to the State of Kerala. WHEREAS it is expedient to amend the Indian Registration Act, 1908, in its application to the State of Kerala; BE it enacted in the Ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Indian Registration (Kerala Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala (1) This Act may be called the Indian Registration (Kerala Amendment) Act, 1958. 1 (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Kerala (3) It shall come into force on such date as the Government may, by notification in the Gazette, appoint. Received the assent of the President on 16-01-1959 and published in the Gazette Extra Ordinary dated 23-01-1959.
Indian Registration (Kerala Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 2--> State(s): Kerala (1) The Indian Registration (Madras Amendment) Act, 1952 (Madras Act XVII of 1952), as in force in the Malabar district referred to in sub-section (2) of section 5 of the State Re-organisation Act, 1956, is hereby repealed and section 23-B inserted by the said Act in the Indian Registration Act, 1908 (Central Act XVI of 1908) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), shall be omitted. (2) The Indian Registration (Travancore-Cochin, Amendment) Act, 1952 (Act XXV of 1952), is hereby repealed and clause (bb) of sub-section (1) of section 69 of the principal Act inserted by the said Act, shall be omitted.
Indian Registration (Kerala Amendment) Act, 1958_Section 3--> State(s): Kerala After clause (b) of sub-section (1) of section 69 of the principal Act, the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(bb) providing for the grant of licences to document writers, the revocation of such licences the terms and conditions subject to which and the authority by whom such licences shall be granted and generally for all purposes connected with the writing of documents to be presented for registration."
