Gandhiji University (Dissolution of the Senate and the Syndicate) Act, 1988_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala An Act to provide for the dissolution of the Senate and the Syndicate of the Gandhiji University WHEREAS it is considered necessary in the interest of the Gandhiji University and in the public interest to dissolve the Senate and the Syndicate of the Gandhiji University and to entrust the powers and functions of the Senate and the Syndicate temporarily to the Vice-Chancellor of the Gandhiji University; BE it enacted in Thirty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Gandhiji University (Dissolution of the Senate and the Syndicate) Act, 1988_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala (1) This Act may be called the Gandhiji University (Dissolution of the Senate and the Syndicate) Act,19881 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 24 th day of December, 1987. Received the assent of the Governor on the 22-04 1988 and published in the Kerala Gazette Extraordinary No. 357, dated the 22-04-1988.
Gandhiji University (Dissolution of the Senate and the Syndicate) Act, 1988_Section 2--> State(s): Kerala In this Act,- (a) "Gandhiji University" means the Gandhiji University constituted under the Gandhiji University Act,1985 (12 of 1985); (b) "Senate" and "Syndicate" means the Senate and the Syndicate of the Gandhiji University; (c) "Vice-Chancellor" means the Vice-Chancellor of the Gandhiji University.
Gandhiji University (Dissolution of the Senate and the Syndicate) Act, 1988_Section 3--> State(s): Kerala Notwithstanding anything contained in the Gandhiji University Act,1985 (12 of 1985) on and from the date of commencement of this Act, the Senate and the Syndicate shall, by virtue of this Act stand dissolved.
Gandhiji University (Dissolution of the Senate and the Syndicate) Act, 1988_Section 4--> State(s): Kerala On the dissolution of the Senate and the Syndicate under section 3, all the members of the Senate and the Syndicate shall be deemed to have vacated their offices as such.
Gandhiji University (Dissolution of the Senate and the Syndicate) Act, 1988_Section 5--> State(s): Kerala All or any of the powers and the functions of the Senate and the Syndicate except the power of the Senate to review the action of the Syndicate as provided in sub-section (1) of section 19 of the Gandhiji University Act, 1985 (12 of 1985), may during the interval between the dissolution of the Senate and the Syndicate under section 3 and the reconstitution of the Senate and the Syndicate in pursuance of section 6, be exercised and performed, as far as may be, and to such extent as the Government may determine, by the Vice Chancellor.
Gandhiji University (Dissolution of the Senate and the Syndicate) Act, 1988_Section 6--> State(s): Kerala The Senate and the Syndicate shall be reconstituted in accordance with the provisions of the Gandhiji University Act,1985 (12 of 1985), within one year from the date of commencement of this Act.
Gandhiji University (Dissolution of the Senate and the Syndicate) Act, 1988_Section 7--> State(s): Kerala (1) If any difficulty arises in giving effect to the provisions of this Act, the Government may, as occasion may require, by order, not inconsistent with the provisions of this Act, remove such difficulty. Every order made under sub-section (1) shall be published in the Gazette and shall, as soon as may be, after it is made, be laid before the State Legislature.
Gandhiji University (Dissolution of the Senate and the Syndicate) Act, 1988_Section 8--> State(s): Kerala (1) The Gandhiji University (Dissolution of the Senate and the Syndicate) Ordinance, 1987 (7 of 1987) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under this Act.
