Kerala Municipal Councils (Extension of Term of Office of Councillors) Act, 1979_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala An Act to provide for the further extension of the term of office of the councillors of municipalities other than the Changanacherry and Tirur Municipalities. Whereas it is expedient to provide for the further extension of the term of office of the councillors of municipalities other than the Changanacherry and Tirur Municipalities; Be it enacted in the Thirtieth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Kerala Municipal Councils (Extension of Term of Office of Councillors) Act, 1979_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala (1) This Act may be called the Kerala Municipal Councils (Extension of Term of Office of Councillors) Act, 19791 . (2) It shall, be deemed to have come into force on the 31st day of October, 1978. Published in Kerala Gazette Ex. No. 228 dated 29-3-1979.
Kerala Municipal Councils (Extension of Term of Office of Councillors) Act, 1979_Section 2--> State(s): Kerala Notwithstanding anything contained in the Kerala Municipalities Act, 1960 (14 of 1961), the term of office of the councillors of all the municipalities constituted under the said Act, other than the Changanacherry and Tirur Municipalities, which expires on the 1st day of November, 1978, shall extend upto noon on the 1st day of October, 1979.
Kerala Municipal Councils (Extension of Term of Office of Councillors) Act, 1979_Section 3--> State(s): Kerala (1) The Kerala Municipal Councils (Extension of Term of Office of Councillors) Ordinance, 1978 (21 of 1978), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under this Act.
