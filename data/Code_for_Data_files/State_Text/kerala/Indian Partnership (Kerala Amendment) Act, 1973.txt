Indian Partnership (Kerala Amendment) Act, 1973_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala An Act to amend the Indian Partnership Act, 1932 , in its application to the State of Kerala. WHEREAS it is expedient to amend the Indian Partnership Act, 1932 , in its application to the State of Kerala; Be it enacted in the Twenty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Indian Partnership (Kerala Amendment) Act, 1973_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala (1) This Act may be called the Indian Partnership (Kerala Amendment) Act, 1973. 1 (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Kerala. (3) It shall come into force at once. Published in the Gazette Extraordinary No.810 dated 24.8.1973.
Indian Partnership (Kerala Amendment) Act, 1973_Section 2--> State(s): Kerala For Schedule I to the Indian Partnership Act, 1932 (Central Act 9 of 1932), the following Schedule shall be substituted, namely:-
