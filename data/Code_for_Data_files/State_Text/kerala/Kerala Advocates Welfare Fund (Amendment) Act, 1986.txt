Kerala Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala An Act to amend the Kerala Advocates' Welfare Fund Act, 1980. WHEREAS it is expedient to amend the Kerala Advocates' Welfare Fund Act, 1980, for the purpose hereinafter appearing; BE it enacted in the Thirty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Kerala Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala (1) This Act may be called the Kerala Advocates' Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act,1986. 1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 5th day of April, 1981. Received the assent of the Governor on the 30th day of November, 1986 and published in the Kerala Gazette Extraordinary No. 1081 dated 30th November, 1986.
Kerala Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 2--> State(s): Kerala To sub-section (1) of section 23 of the Kerala Advocates' Welfare Fund Act,1980 (21of 1980) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), the following proviso shall be added, namely:- "Provided that nothing contained in this sub-section shall apply in respect of any memorandum of appearance filed by an advocate appearing on behalf of the Government."
Kerala Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 3--> State(s): Kerala (1) The Kerala Advocates' Welfare Fund (Amendment) Ordinance, 1986 (61 of 1986), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or deemed to have been done or any action taken or deemed to have been taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
