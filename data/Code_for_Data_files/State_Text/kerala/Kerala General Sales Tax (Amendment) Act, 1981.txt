Kerala General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala An Act further to amend the Kerala General Sales Tax Act, 1963. Preamble. - WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Kerala General Sales Tax Act, 1963, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; BE it enacted in the Thirty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Kerala General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala (1) This Act may be called the Kerala General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1981. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st day of April, 1981.
Kerala General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Kerala In section 5 of the Kerala General Sales Tax Act, 1963 (15 of 1963) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), - (a) in sub-section (1), for the words "thirty-five thousand rupees", the words "fifty thousand rupees" shall be substituted; (b) in sub-section (2), for the words "ten thousand rupees", the words "fifteen thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Kerala General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 3--> State(s): Kerala In section 5A of the principal Act, - (a) in sub-section (2), for the words "thirty-five thousand rupees" and "ten thousand rupees", the words "fifty thousand rupees" and "fifteen thousand rupees" shall respectively be substituted; (b) in sub-section (3), for the words "thirty-five thousand rupees" and "forty thousand rupees", the words "fifty thousand rupees" and "fifty-five thousand rupees" shall respectively be substituted.
Kerala General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 4--> State(s): Kerala In section 7 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), for the words "thirty-five thousand rupees" and "forty thousand rupees", the words "fifty thousand rupees" and "fifty-five thousand rupees" shall respectively be substituted.
Kerala General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 5--> State(s): Kerala In section 13 of the principal Act, - (a) in sub-section (1), for the words "thirty-five thousand rupees", the words "fifty thousand rupees" shall be substituted; (b) in sub-section (2), for the words "ten thousand rupees", the words "fifteen thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Kerala General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 6--> State(s): Kerala In section 17 of the principal Act, in sub-sections (4) and (6), for the words "one lakh rupees", the words "one lakh and fifty thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Kerala General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 7--> State(s): Kerala In section 18 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2A), for the words "one lakh rupees", the words "one lakh and fifty thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Kerala General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 8--> State(s): Kerala In the First Schedule to the principal Act, - (a) in the entry in column (4) against serial number 36, for the figures "40", the figures "50" shall be substituted; (b) in the entry in column (2) against serial number 101, after the word "manufactured", the words "in mill or on handloom" shall be inserted.
Kerala General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1981_Section 9--> State(s): Kerala In the Third Schedule to the principal Act, in the entry against serial number 3, the following shall be inserted at the end, namely :- "except silk fabrics woven on handlooms wholly or partly with mill yarn.".
