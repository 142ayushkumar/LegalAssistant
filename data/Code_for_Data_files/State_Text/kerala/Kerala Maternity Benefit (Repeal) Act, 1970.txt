Kerala Maternity Benefit (Repeal) Act, 1970_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala An Act to repeal the Kerala Maternity Benefit Act, 1957 Whereas it is expedient to repeal the Kerala Maternity Benefit Act, 1957; Be it enacted in the Twenty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Kerala Maternity Benefit (Repeal) Act, 1970_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala (1) This Act may be called the Kerala Maternity Benefit (Repeal) Act, 19701 . (2) It shall come into force on such date as the Government may, by notification in the Gazette, appoint. Published In Kerala Gazette Ex. No. 67, dated 20-02-1970. Received the assent of the Governor on 18th February 1970.
Kerala Maternity Benefit (Repeal) Act, 1970_Section 2--> State(s): Kerala The Kerala Maternity Benefit Act, 1957 (19 of 1957) is hereby repealed.
