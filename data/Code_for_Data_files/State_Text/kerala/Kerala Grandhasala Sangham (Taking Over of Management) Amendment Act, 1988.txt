Kerala Grandhasala Sangham (Taking Over of Management) Amendment Act, 1988_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala An Act further to amend the Kerala Grandhasala Sangham (Taking Over of Management) Act, 1977. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Kerala Grandhasala Sangham (Taking Over of Management) Act, 1977, for the purpose hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted in the Thirty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Kerala Grandhasala Sangham (Taking Over of Management) Amendment Act, 1988_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala (1) This Act may be called the Kerala Grandhasala Sangham (Taking Over of Management) Amendment Act, 19881 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 20th day of May 1988. Published in Kerala Gazette Ex. No. 675 dated 1-8-1988.
Kerala Grandhasala Sangham (Taking Over of Management) Amendment Act, 1988_Section 2--> State(s): Kerala In section 3 of the Kerala Grandhasala Sangham (Taking Over of Management) Act, 1977 (19 of 1977), (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in the proviso to sub-section (6), for the words "eleven years", the words "twelve years" shall be substituted.
Kerala Grandhasala Sangham (Taking Over of Management) Amendment Act, 1988_Section 3--> State(s): Kerala (1) The Kerala Grandhasala Sangham (Taking Over of Management) Amendment Ordinance, 1988 (12 of 1988), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
