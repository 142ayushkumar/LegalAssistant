Epidemic Disease Act, 1897_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala An Act to provide for the better prevention of the spread of Dangerous Epidemic Disease. Whereas it is expedient to provide for the better prevention of the spread of dangerous epidemic disease; it is hereby enacted as follows:-
Epidemic Disease Act, 1897_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala (1) 1 This Act may be called the Epidemic Disease Act, 1 of 1072;2 (2) It extends to the whole of Cochin State. (3) It shall come into force at once. Substituted for Regulation throughout the enactment by Proclamation V of 1112. Passed by the Highness the [Substituted for 'Raja', by Section 2 of Act I of 1109.][Maharaja] of Cochin on the 24th day of Kumbham 1072 corresponding with the 6th day of March 1897.
Epidemic Disease Act, 1897_Section 2--> State(s): Kerala (1) When at any time the Diwan is satisfied that the Cochin State or any part thereof is visited by, or threatened with, an outbreak of any dangerous epidemic disease the Diwan, if he thinks that the ordinary provisions of the law for the time being in force are insufficient for the purpose, may take or require or empower any person to take such measures and, by public notice, prescribe such temporary regulations to be observed by the public or by any person or class of persons as he shall deem necessary to prevent the out-break of such disease or the spread thereof and may determine in what manner and by whom any expenses incurred (including compensation, if any) shall be defrayed. (2) In particular and without prejudice to the generality of the foregoing provisions, the Diwan may take measures and prescribe regulations for- (a) the inspection of any ship or vessel leaving, or arriving at, any port in Cochin and such detention thereof, or of any person intending to sail therein, or arriving thereby, as may be necessary; and (b) the inspection of persons travelling by railway or otherwise and the segregation, in hospital, temporary accommodation or otherwise, of persons suspected by the inspecting Officer of being infected with any such disease.
Epidemic Disease Act, 1897_Section 3--> State(s): Kerala Any person disobeying any Rule or order made under this Act shall be deemed to have committed an offence punishable under section 174 of the Cochin Penal Code (Act I of 1059).
Epidemic Disease Act, 1897_Section 4--> State(s): Kerala No suit or other legal proceeding shall lie against any person for anything done or in good faith intended to be done this Act.
