Kerala Additional Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala An Act to amend the Kerala Additional Sales Tax Act, 1978. WHEREAS it is expedient to amend the Kerala Additional Sales Tax Act, 1978, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; BE it enacted in the Thirtieth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Kerala Additional Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala (1) This Act may be called the Kerala Additional Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1979. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Kerala Additional Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 2--> State(s): Kerala In the long title to the Kerala Additional Sales Tax Act, 1978 (20 of 1978) (See 1978 42 S.T.C. Statutes 87.) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), the words and figures "for the financial year 1978-79" shall be omitted.
Kerala Additional Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 3--> State(s): Kerala In the preamble to the principal Act, the words and figures "for the financial year 1978-79" shall be omitted.
Kerala Additional Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 4--> State(s): Kerala In section 2 of the principal Act, - (a) in the marginal heading, the word and figures "during 1978-79" shall be omitted; (b) in sub-section (1), for the words and figures "for the financial year commencing on the first day of April, 1978", the words and figures "for every financial year commencing with the financial year 1978-79" shall be substituted.
