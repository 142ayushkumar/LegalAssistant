Kerala Grandhasala Sangham (Taking Over of Management) Amendment Act, 1986_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala An Act to amend the Kerala Grandhasala Sangham (Taking Over of Management) Act, 1977. Whereas it is expedient to amend the Kerala Grandhasala Sangham (Taking Over of Management) Act, 1977, for the purpose hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted in the Thirty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Kerala Grandhasala Sangham (Taking Over of Management) Amendment Act, 1986_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala (1) This Act may be called the Kerala Grandhasala Sangham (Taking Over of Management) Amendment Act, 19861 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st day of April. 1982. Published in Kerala Gazette Ex. No. 737 dated 4-8-1986.
Kerala Grandhasala Sangham (Taking Over of Management) Amendment Act, 1986_Section 2--> State(s): Kerala In the Kerala Grandhasala Sangham (Taking Over of Management) Act, 1977 (19 of 1977) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in section 3, in the proviso to sub-section (6), for the words "five years", the words "eleven years" shall be substituted.
Kerala Grandhasala Sangham (Taking Over of Management) Amendment Act, 1986_Section 3--> State(s): Kerala Notwithstanding anything contained in the principal Act, or in any other law for the time being in force, the term of office of the members of the Board of Control appointed under section 3 of the principal Act by Notification No.5895/A3/80/H. Edn. dated the 21st April, 1980, published in the Kerala Gazette Extra-ordinary No. 284 dated the 21st April, 1980, which expired on the 20th day of April, 1982, shall be deemed to have been extended upto and including the date of publication of the Kerala Grandhasala Sangham (Taking Over of Management) Amendment Ordinance, 1984 (16 of 1984), in the Gazette by notification under the proviso to sub-section (6) of the said section as amended by this Act and accordingly anything done or any action taken by the Government or the said Board of Control or any other person or authority in the purported exercise of the powers and functions conferred by or under the principal Act shall not be deemed to be invalid or ever to have been invalid merely on the ground that the term of office of the members of the Board of Control aforesaid had expired on the said date.
Kerala Grandhasala Sangham (Taking Over of Management) Amendment Act, 1986_Section 4--> State(s): Kerala (1) The Kerala Grandhasala Sangham (Taking Over of Management) Amendment Ordinance, 1986 (31 of 1986), is hereby replaced. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or deemed to have been done or any action taken or deemed to have been taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
