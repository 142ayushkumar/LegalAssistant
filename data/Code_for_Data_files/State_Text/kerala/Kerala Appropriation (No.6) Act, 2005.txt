Kerala Appropriation (No.6) Act, 2005_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala An Act to provide for the authorisation of appropriation of moneys out of the Consolidated Fund of the State of Kerala to meet the amounts spent on certain service during the financial year ended on the 31st day of March, 1996 in excess of the amount granted for those services and for that year. WHEREAS, it is necessary to provide for the authorisation of appropriation of moneys out of the Consolidated Fund of the State of Kerala to meet the amounts spent on certain services during the financial year ended on the 31st day of March, 1996 in excess of the amount granted for those services and for that year. BE it enacted in the Fifty sixth year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Kerala Appropriation (No.6) Act, 2005_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala This Act may be called the Kerala Appropriation (No.6) Act, 2005
Kerala Appropriation (No.6) Act, 2005_Section 2--> State(s): Kerala From and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State of Kerala, the sum specified in column (3) of the Schedule amounting to the sum of Rupees twelve lakh ninety-one thousand three hundred and fifty six only shall be deemed to have been authorized to be paid and applied to meet the amounts spent for defraying the charges in respect of the services specified in column (2) of the Schedule during the financial year ended on the 31st day of March, 1996, in excess of the amounts granted for those services and for that year.
Kerala Appropriation (No.6) Act, 2005_Section 3--> State(s): Kerala The sums deemed to have been authorized to be paid and applied from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State of Kerala under this Act shall be deemed to have been appropriated for the services and purposes specified in the Schedule in relation to the financial year ended on the 31st day of March 1996.
