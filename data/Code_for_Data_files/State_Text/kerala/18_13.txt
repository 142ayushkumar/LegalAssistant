Trivandrum Municipal Corporation (Dissolution) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala An Act to provide for the dissolution of the Corporation of the City of Trivandrum. Whereas it is considered necessary in the interest of the municipal government of the City of Trivandrum and in the public interest to dissolve the council and to entrust the powers, duties and functions of the council temporarily to a person appointed by the Government in that behalf; Be it enacted in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Trivandrum Municipal Corporation (Dissolution) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala (1) This Act may be called the Trivandrum Municipal Corporation (Dissolution) Act, 19761 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 8th day of December, 1975. Published in Kerala Gazette Ex. No. 175 dated 22-3-1976.
Trivandrum Municipal Corporation (Dissolution) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Kerala In this Act,- (a) "Corporation " and "Council" mean the Corporation of the City of Trivandrum; (b) "Mayor" and "Deputy Mayor" mean the Mayor and Deputy Mayor of the Corporation.
Trivandrum Municipal Corporation (Dissolution) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Kerala The Councils shall be deemed to have stood dissolved on and from the 8th day of December, 1975.
Trivandrum Municipal Corporation (Dissolution) Act, 1976_Section 4--> State(s): Kerala On the dissolution of the Council under S. 3 all the Councillors of the Corporation including the Mayor and the Deputy Mayor shall be deemed to have vacated their offices.
Trivandrum Municipal Corporation (Dissolution) Act, 1976_Section 5--> State(s): Kerala (1) All or any of the functions of the council and of the Mayor, the Deputy Mayor and of the standing committees and other committees of the Corporation may, during the interval between the dissolution of the council under section 3 and the reconstitution of the council in pursuance of sub-section (1) of S. 6, be exercised and performed, as far as may be and to such extent as the Government may determine, by such person or persons as the Government may appoint in that behalf, and any such person may, if the Government so direct, receive payment for his services from the Corporation fund. (2) The Government may determine the relationship of the person or persons appointed under sub-section (1) with the Government.
Trivandrum Municipal Corporation (Dissolution) Act, 1976_Section 6--> State(s): Kerala (1) The council shall be reconstituted in accordance with the provision of the Kerala Municipal Corporation Act, 1961 (30 of 1961) on the 1 [1st day of October 1979]. (2) The term of office of the Councillors of the reconstituted council shall expire at noon on the 2 [1st day of October 1984]. Substituted by Acts 2/1977. 10/1978, 25/1978. and 8/1979. Substitued by Acts 2/1977. 10/1978, 25/1978. and 8/1979.
Trivandrum Municipal Corporation (Dissolution) Act, 1976_Section 7--> State(s): Kerala The Government, until the date of reconstitution of the council in pursuance of sub-section (1) of S. 6 and the reconstituted council thereafter, shall be entitled to all the assets and be subject to all the liabilities of the council as on the date of dissolution and on the date of reconstitution respectively.
Trivandrum Municipal Corporation (Dissolution) Act, 1976_Section 8--> State(s): Kerala (1) The Trivandrum Municipal Corporation (Dissolution) Ordinance, 1975 (19 of 1975), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under this Act.
