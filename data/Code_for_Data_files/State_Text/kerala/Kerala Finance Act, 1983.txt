Kerala Finance Act, 1983_Section Preamble--> State(s): Kerala An Act to give effect to the financial proposals of the Government of Kerala for the financial year 1983-84. WHEREAS it is expedient to give effect to the financial proposals of the Government of Kerala for the financial year 1983-84; BE it enacted in the Thirty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Kerala Finance Act, 1983_Section 1--> State(s): Kerala (1) This Act may be called the Kerala Finance Act, 1983. (2) Section 2, section 4, clause (1) of section 5 and section 6 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st day of April, 1983, and the remaining provisions of this Act shall be deemed to have come into force on the 13th day of June, 1983. (Sections 2, 3 and 4 of the Finance Act relating to amendments of the Kerala Money Lenders Act, 1958, Kerala Stamp Act, 1959, and the Kerala Land Tax Act, 1961, respectively are not available.)

Kerala Finance Act, 1983_Section 9--> State(s): Kerala (1) The Kerala Finance Ordinance, 1983 (20 of 1983), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under each of the Acts specified in sections 3 to 9 of the said Ordinance, as amended by that Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under that Act as amended by this Act.
