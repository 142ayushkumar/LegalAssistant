Orissa Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> State(s): Orissa An Act further to amend the Orissa Entertainments Tax Act, 1946. BE it enacted by the Legislature c f the State of Orissa in the Forty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Orissa Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 1--> State(s): Orissa This Act may be called the Orissa Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1993.
Orissa Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 2--> State(s): Orissa In section 13 of the Orissa Entertainments Tax Act, 1946;- , (a) in sub-section (1), for the words "for Establishment of low cost Cinema houses" the words "for utilisation, in equal proportion towards establishment of low cost Cinema houses in the State and development of Oriya Film industry" shall be substituted; and (b) in sub-section (2), for the words "for establishment of low cost Cinema houses in the State",, the commas and words "in equal proportion, for establishment of low cost Cinema houses in the State and development of Oriya Film industry" shall be substituted.
