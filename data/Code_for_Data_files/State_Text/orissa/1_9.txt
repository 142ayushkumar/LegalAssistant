Orissa Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> State(s): Orissa An act further to amend the Orissa Advocates Welfare Fund Act, 1987. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Orissa in the Fifty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Orissa Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 1--> State(s): Orissa This Act may be called the Orissa Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 2001.
Orissa Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 2--> State(s): Orissa In the Orissa Advocates Welfare Fund Act, 1987 (hereinafter .referred to as the Principal Act), in section 15 - (i) in sub-section (3), for the words "one hundred", the, words "Two hundred" and for the word "fifty" appearing In the proviso thereto, the words "one hundred" shall be substituted, and (ii) in subjection (5), for the words "Nil", "fifty rupees" and "one hundred rupees" appearing against clause (a), clause (b) and clause(c) thereof, the words "fifty rupees", "one hundred rupees" and 'two hundred rupees" shall, respectively be substituted.
