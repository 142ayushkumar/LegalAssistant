Odisha Muhammadan Marriages and Divorces Registration (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section Preamble--> State(s): Orissa An Act Further to amend the Odisha Muhammadan Marriages and Divorces Registration Act, 1949 BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Odisha in the Sixty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Odisha Muhammadan Marriages and Divorces Registration (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 1--> State(s): Orissa This Act may be called the Odisha Muhammadan Marriages and Divorces Registration (Amendment) Act, 2012.
Odisha Muhammadan Marriages and Divorces Registration (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 2--> State(s): Orissa In the Odisha Muhammadan Marriag es and Divorces Registration Act, 1949, in section 3, for the existing provisos the following proviso shall be substituted, namely:- "Provided that not more than two persons belonging to 'Sunni' sect and not more than two persons belonging to 'Shia' sect may be licensed to exercise the said functions within the same limits.". By Order of the Governor S. K. Mohanty Principal Secretary to Government, I/c.
