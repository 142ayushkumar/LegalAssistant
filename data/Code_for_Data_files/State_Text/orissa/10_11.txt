Orissa Motor Vehicles Taxation (Second Amendment) Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> State(s): Orissa An Act further to amend the orissa motor vbhicles taxation act 1975. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Orissa in the Forty-fourth year of the Republic of India as follow: -
Orissa Motor Vehicles Taxation (Second Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 1--> State(s): Orissa (1) The Act may be called the Orissa Motor Vehicles Taxation (Second Amendment) Act, 1993. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into fores on the 21 st day of September, 1993.
Orissa Motor Vehicles Taxation (Second Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 2--> State(s): Orissa In the Orissa Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1975 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in Section-4, (a) in the first proviso to sub-section (2), for the words two hundred rupees' the words five hundred rupees shall be substituted; and (b) in sub-section (3),- (i) for the words transport vehicle occurring in the opening portion, the word vehicle shall be substituted ; and (ii) the provisos thereto shall be omitted.
Orissa Motor Vehicles Taxation (Second Amendment) Act, 1993_Section 3--> State(s): Orissa (7) The Orissa Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Ordinance, 1993 hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act as amended by this Act.
