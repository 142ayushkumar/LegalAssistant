Orissa State Commission For Backward Classes (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section Preamble--> State(s): Orissa An Act To Amend The Orissa State Commission For Backward Classes Act, 1993. Be enacted by the Legislature. of the State of Orissa in the Forty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Orissa State Commission For Backward Classes (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 1--> State(s): Orissa This Act may be called the Orissa State Commission For Backward Classes (Amendment) Act, 1994.
Orissa State Commission For Backward Classes (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 2--> State(s): Orissa In section 3 of the Orissa State Commission for Backward Classes Act, 1993 f hereinafter referred to as the , principal Act ), in sub-section (2), for clause (d), the following clause shall be substituted, namely;- "(d) a member-Secretary, who is or has been an officer of the State Government not below the rank of a Joint Secretary to the Government of Orissa."
Orissa State Commission For Backward Classes (Amendment) Act, 1994_Section 3--> State(s): Orissa In section 13 of the principal Act,- (i) in sub-section (1) the words and comma "in consultation with the Accountant-General, Orissa" appearing at the end shall be omitted; (ii) for sub-section (2). the following sub-section shall be substituted namely:- "(2) The Accounts of the Commission shall be subject to audits under the Orissa Local Fund Audit Act, 1948" and (iii) sub-section (3) shall be deleted.
