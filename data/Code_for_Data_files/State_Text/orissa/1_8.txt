Orissa Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section Preamble--> State(s): Orissa An Act Further to amend the Orissa Advocates Welfare Fund Act, 1987 Be it enacted by the legislature of the Sate of Orissa in the Forty -eight Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Orissa Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 1--> State(s): Orissa This Act may be called the Orissa Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1997.
Orissa Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 2--> State(s): Orissa In the Orissa Advocates Welfare Fund Act, 1987- (hereinafter referred to as Orissa Act the principal Act) in sub-section (1) of section 22, for the words "two rupees", the words "four rupees" shall be substituted.
Orissa Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 3--> State(s): Orissa In the principal Act, in section 23,- (a) in sub-section (1), after the words "one stamp", the words, brackets and figure or, in case of stamps of the value of two rupees printed prior to the date of commencement of the Orissa Advocates' Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1997 and in stock if any, two stamps" shall be inserted; and (b) in sub-section (5), for the words "unless, the stamps is affixed to it", the words *'unlcss it is stamped as aforesaid" shall be substituted.
