Orissa Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section Preamble--> State(s): Orissa An Act to Amend the Orissa Contingency Fund Act, 1967 Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Orissa in the Thirtieth Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Orissa Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 1--> State(s): Orissa This Act may be called the Orissa Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 19791 . (2) Came into force with effect from the 19th April 1979. For the Bill, See Orissa Gazette Extraordinary dated the 7th March 1979 (No. 289)
Orissa Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 1979_Section 2--> State(s): Orissa In section 2 of the Orissa Contingency Fund Act, 1967 for the words "six cores of rupees", the words "ten crores of rupees" shall be substituted.
