Orissa Universities (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section Preamble--> State(s): Orissa An Act further to amend the Orissa university act, 1989. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Orissa in the Fifty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:
Orissa Universities (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 1--> State(s): Orissa This Act may be called the Orissa Universities ( Amendment) Act, 2002.
Orissa Universities (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 2--> State(s): Orissa In the Orissa Universities Act, 1989 in section 21, for sub-sections (3) and (4), the following sub-sections shall be substituted; namely: (3) The Selection Committee referred to in sub-section (2) shall consist of (i) the Vice-Chancellor as the Chairperson of the Selection Committee; (ii) three outside experts in the concerned subject, to be invited on the basis of the list recommended by the Vice-Chancellor and approved by the Syndicate; (iii) bead of the concerned Faculty: or the Director of Higher Education; (iv) an academician nominated by the Chancellor; (v) head of - the concerned Department and the Director of Higher Education both shall be the members in case of selection to the post of (4) (a) The quorum at a meeting of the Selection Committee shall be four including two outside subject experts. (b) The process of selection for the post of Reader and professor shall be made after inviting the bio-data and the reprints of three major publications and after getting them assessed by the same three experts in the concerned subject who are to be invited to the Selection Committee for selection of the candidates. In case of selection of professor, out of the three major publications submitted by the candidate, one must be a book or research report. The assessment report must be placed before the Selection Committee..
