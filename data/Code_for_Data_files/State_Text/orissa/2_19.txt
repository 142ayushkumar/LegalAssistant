Court-Fees (Orissa Amendment) Act, 1983_Section Preamble--> State(s): Orissa An Act to amend the Court Fees Act, 1870 In the Application to the State of Orissa. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Orissa in the Thirty-fourth year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Court-Fees (Orissa Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 1--> State(s): Orissa (1) The Act may be called the Court-Fees (Orissa Amendment) Act, 19831 . (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, but by notification, appoint in that behalf. For the Bill See Orissa Gazette, extraordinary, dated the 9th March 1983 (No. 282)
