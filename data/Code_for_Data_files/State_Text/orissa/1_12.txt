Agriculturists Loans (Orissa Amendment) Act, 1937_Section Preamble--> State(s): Orissa An Act to amend the Agriculturists' Loans Act, 1884, in its application to the [Substituted vide Adaptation of Laws Order, 1950.][State] of Orissa for a certain purpose Whereas it is expedient to amend the Agriculturists' Loans Act, 1884 (XII of 1884) in its application to the 1[State] of Orissa, for the purpose hereinafter appearing: It is hereby enacted as follows:
Agriculturists Loans (Orissa Amendment) Act, 1937_Section 1--> State(s): Orissa (1) This Act, may be called the Agriculturists' Loans (Orissa. Amendment) Act, 1937. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Agriculturists Loans (Orissa Amendment) Act, 1937_Section 2--> State(s): Orissa In Sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the Agriculturists' Loans Act, 1884 (XII of 1884) after the words "the relief of distress", the words "of indebtedness" shall be inserted.
Agriculturists Loans (Orissa Amendment) Act, 1937_Section 3--> State(s): Orissa The Agriculturists' Loans (Madras Amendment) Act, 1935 (Madras Act, XVI of 1935) in its application to the State of Orissa is hereby repealed.
