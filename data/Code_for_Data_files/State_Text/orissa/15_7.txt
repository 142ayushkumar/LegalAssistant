Shri Jagannath Temple (Amendment) Act, 2008_Section Preamble--> State(s): Orissa An Act further to amend Shri Jagannath Temple Act, 1955. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Orissa in the Fifty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Shri Jagannath Temple (Amendment) Act, 2008_Section 1--> State(s): Orissa (1) This Act may be called Shri Jagannath Temple (Amendment) Act, 2008. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint.
Shri Jagannath Temple (Amendment) Act, 2008_Section 2--> State(s): Orissa In Shri Jagannath Temple Act, 1955, in section 21-B,- (i) in clause (b), for the word 'him', the words "an office holder or employee attached to the Temple" shall be substituted and for the full stop "."appearing at the end, the coma and word "or" shall be substituted; and (ii) after clause (b), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(c) where a Sevak is involved in any criminal offence committed inside the Temple premises or in connection with the affairs of the Temple.". By order of the Governor B. K. Nayak Principal Secretary to Government
