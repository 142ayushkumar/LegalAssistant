Odisha Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section Preamble--> State(s): Orissa An Act Further to amend the Odisha Municipal Corporation Act, 2003 BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Odisha in the Sixty-third Year of Republic of India as follows:-
Odisha Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 1--> State(s): Orissa This Act may be called the Odisha Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2012.
Odisha Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 2--> State(s): Orissa In the Odisha Municipal Corporation Act, 2003 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) for the words "State of Odisha", the words "State of Odisha" shall be substituted.
Odisha Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 3--> State(s): Orissa In section 1 of the principal Act,- (a) in sub-section (1), for the word "Orissa" the word "Odisha" shall be substituted; and (b) in sub-section (2), for the words "State of Orissa" the words "State of Odisha" shall be substituted.
Odisha Municipal Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 4--> State(s): Orissa In the principal Act, in section 70, in sub-section (3), for clause (b), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:- "(b) has been adjudged by a competent court to be of unsound mind or in the opinion of the District Leprosy Officer is suffering from an infectious type of leprosy; or". By Order of the Governor S. K. Mohanty Principal Secretary to Government, I/c.
