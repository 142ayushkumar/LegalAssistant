Orissa Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section Preamble--> State(s): Orissa AN ACT FURTHER TO AMEND THE Orissa Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1975 , BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Orissa in the Fifty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Orissa Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 1--> State(s): Orissa This Act may be called the Orissa Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 2002.
Orissa Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 2--> State(s): Orissa In section 4-A of the Orissa Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1975,- (i) in sub-section (1),- (a) in the opening portion, for the words and numeral "ten times the annual rate of tax in respect thereof as specified in Schedule-L", the words "five per centum of the cost of (he vehicle" shall be substituted, and (b) in the proviso, clause (i) shall be omitted ; (ii) sub-section (5) shall be omitted ; and (iii) for Explanation - I occurring after sub-section (6), the following Explanation shall be substituted, namely :- "Explanation l.-For the purposes of this section, the expression "appointed date" shall mean the date of commencement of the Orissa Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act. 2002.".
