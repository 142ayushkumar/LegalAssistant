Orissa Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section Preamble--> State(s): Orissa An Act Further-To Amend The Orissa Advocate Welfare fund Act, 1987 BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Orissa in the Fifty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Orissa Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 1--> State(s): Orissa (1) This Act may be called the Orissa Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 2002. (2).This section and section 2 shall come into force at once and section 3 shall come into force on such date as the State Government may by notification, appoint.
Orissa Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 2--> State(s): Orissa In Section 22 of the Orissa Advocates Welfare Fund Act, 1987 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) in sub-section (1) for the word "four rupees and its value inscribed thereon' the word "five rupees and for the purpose of utilisation of the stamps printed prior to the commencement of section 3 of the Orissa Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 2002 and in stock if any one rupee, and their Value respectively inscribed thereon" shall be substituted.
Orissa Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 3--> State(s): Orissa In section 23 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), for the words, brackets and figures " one stamp or in case of stamps of the value of two rupees Printed prior to the commencement of the Orissa Advocates Welfare Fund (Amendment) Act, 1997 and in stock if any two stamps the words "stamp or stamps of the value of five rupees" shall be substituted.
