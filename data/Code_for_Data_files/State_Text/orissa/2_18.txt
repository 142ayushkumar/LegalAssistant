Court Fees (Orissa Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 1--> State(s): Orissa (1) This Act may be called the Court Fees (Orissa Amendment) Act, 1997. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint in this behalf.
