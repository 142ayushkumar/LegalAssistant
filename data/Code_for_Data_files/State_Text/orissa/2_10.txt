Code of Criminal Procedure (Orissa Amendment) Act, 1997_Section Preamble--> State(s): Orissa AN ACT to amend The Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 in its application to the State of Orissa, BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Orissa in the Forty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Code of Criminal Procedure (Orissa Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 1--> State(s): Orissa This Act may be called the Code of Criminal Procedure (Orissa Amendment) Act, 1997.
Code of Criminal Procedure (Orissa Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 2--> State(s): Orissa In section 167 of the Code of Criminal Procedure. 1973, in paragraph (a) of the proviso to sub-section (2),- (i) for the words "under this paragraph" the words "under this section" shall be substituted; and (ii) for the words "ninety days' wherever they occur, the words "one hundred and twenty days" shall be substituted.
