Orissa Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 2008_Section Preamble--> State(s): Orissa An Act further to amend the Orissa Contingency Fund Act, 1967 BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Orissa in the Fifty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Orissa Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 2008_Section 1--> State(s): Orissa (1) This Act may be called the Orissa Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 2008. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 4th day of October, 2008.
Orissa Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 2008_Section 2--> State(s): Orissa In sub-section (1) of section 2 of the Orissa Contingency Fund Act, 1967, for the words "one hundred fifty crores of rupees", the words "four hundred crores of rupees" shall be substituted.
Orissa Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 2008_Section 3--> State(s): Orissa The Orissa Contingency Fund (Amendment) Ordinance, 2008 is hereby repealed. By order of the Governor B. K. Nayak Principal Secretary to Government
