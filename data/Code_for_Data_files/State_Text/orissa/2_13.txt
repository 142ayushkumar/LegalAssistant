Orissa Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section Preamble--> State(s): Orissa An Act To Amend The Orissa Contingency Fund Act, 1967 . BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Orissa in the Fiftieth Year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Orissa Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 1--> State(s): Orissa This Act may be called the Orissa Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 1999.
Orissa Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 2--> State(s): Orissa In sub-section (1) of section 2 of the Orissa Contingency Fund Act 1967, for the words "sixty crores of rupees", the words "one hundred forty crores of rupees" shall be substituted.
Orissa Contingency Fund (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 3--> State(s): Orissa The Orissa Contingency Fund (Amendment) Ordinance, 1999 is hereby repealed.
