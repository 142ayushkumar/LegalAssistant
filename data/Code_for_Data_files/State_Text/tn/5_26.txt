Evacuee Interest (Separation) Tamil Nadu Supplementary Act, 1953_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to supplement certain provisions of the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Act, 1951. WHEREAS it is expedient to supplement certain provision of the Evacuee Interest (Separation) Act, 1951 (Central Act 64 of 1951), for the purpose hereinafter appearing; It is hereby enacted as follows:-
Evacuee Interest (Separation) Tamil Nadu Supplementary Act, 1953_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Evacuee Interest (Separation) 1 [Tamil Nadu] Supplementary Act, 19532 . (2) It extends to the whole of the State of 3 [Tamil Nadu] (3) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 15th day of December, 1952. Substituted for Madras by Tamil Nadu A.O.1969. Received the assent of the President on the 30th April, 1953; Published in the Fort St. George Gazette, on the 6th May, 1953. Substituted for Madras by Tamil Nadu A.O.1969.
Evacuee Interest (Separation) Tamil Nadu Supplementary Act, 1953_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu The Evacuee Interest (Separation ) Act, 1951 (Central Act 64 of 1951) so far as it relates to any matter enumerated in List II in the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution, stand validated and binding as if it had been passed by the Legislature of the State of 1 [Tamil Nadu]. Substituted for Madras by Tamil Nadu A.O.1969.
