Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 2013. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In the First Schedule to the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act),- (1) in PART-B, after Serial Number (56) and the entry relating thereto, the following Serial Number and entry shall be inserted, namely:- "(56-A). Gypsum of all forms and descriptions"; (2) in PART-C,- (i) the entries against Serial Number (22) shall be numbered as (i); (ii) after the entries as so numbered, the following entries shall be inserted, namely:- "(ii) Disinfectants and Germicides.".
Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In the Fourth Schedule to the principal Act, in PART-B,- (1) Serial Number 17-A and the entries relating thereto, shall be omitted; (2) after Serial Number 38 and the entries relating thereto, the following Serial Number and entries shall be inserted, namely:- "38-A. Goods which are used for agricultural / horticultural purposes, namely:- (a) Chemical Fertilizers (b) Insecticides, Pesticides, Rodenticides, Weedicides, Fungicides, Herbicides and combinations thereof, Plant-growth promoters, Plant Nutrients, Micro Nutrients and Bio fertilizers". (By order of the Governor) (G. Jayachandran) Secretary to Government, Law Department.
