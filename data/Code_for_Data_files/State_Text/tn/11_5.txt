Tamil Nadu Khadi and Village Industries Board (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Khadi and Village Industries Board Act, 1959 . Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Thirty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Khadi and Village Industries Board (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Khadi and Village Industries Board (Amendment) Act, 1986.
Tamil Nadu Khadi and Village Industries Board (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 25 of the Tamil Nadu Khadi and Village Industries Board Act, 1959 (Tamil Nadu Act 18 of 1959), in sub-section (5), for clause (ii), the following clause shall be substituted, namely :- (ii) all rights, obligations and liabilities (including any liabilities under any contract) legally subsisting and enforceable by or against the Board shall become the rights, obligations and liabilities of the Government.. (By order of the Governor) S. VADIVELU, Commissioner and Secretary to Government, Law Department
