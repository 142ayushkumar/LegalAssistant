Cattle-Trespass (Tamil Nadu Amendment) Act, 1957_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to amend the Cattle-Trespass Act, 1871, in its application to the State of 1 [Tamil Nadu]. Whereas it is expedient to amend the Cattle-Trespass Act, 1871 (Central Act 1 of 1871); in its application to the State of (Tamil Nadu) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; Be it enacted in the Eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:- Substituted for "Madras" by Tamil Nadu A.O., 1969
Cattle-Trespass (Tamil Nadu Amendment) Act, 1957_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu This Act may be called The Cattle-Trespass 1 [Tamil Nadu] (Amendment) Act, 1957.2 Substituted for "Madras" by Tamil Nadu A.O., 1969 Received the assent of the Governor on the 2nd December, 1957. Published in the Fort St. George Gazette, on 11th December, 1957
Cattle-Trespass (Tamil Nadu Amendment) Act, 1957_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In sections 4, 5, 12, 14, 17 and 31 of the Cattle-Trespass Act, 1871 (Central Act 1 of 1871) hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for the words "Magistrate of the District" wherever they occur, the words "District Collector" shall be substituted.
Cattle-Trespass (Tamil Nadu Amendment) Act, 1957_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu After section 22 of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely: "22-A. Appeal against order of compensation - (1) Any person who has been ordered by a Magistrate under section 22 to pay compensation may appeal from the order, as if such person had been convicted on a trial held by such Magistrate. (2) When an order for payment of compensation to a complainant is made in a case which is subject to appeal under sub-section (1), the compensation shall not be paid to him before the period allowed for the presentation of the appeal has elapsed, or, if an appeal is presented, before the appeal has been decided and, where such order is made in a case which is not so subject to appeal, the compensation shall not be paid before the expiration of one month from the date of the order.."
