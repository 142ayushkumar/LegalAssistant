Tamil Nadu Essential Services Maintenance (Repeal) Act, 2006_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to repeal the Tamil Nadu Essential Services Maintenance Act, 2002. BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Fifty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Essential Services Maintenance (Repeal) Act, 2006_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Essential Services Maintenance (Repeal) Act, 2006. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Tamil Nadu Essential Services Maintenance (Repeal) Act, 2006_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu The Tamil Nadu Essential Services Maintenance Act, 2002 is hereby repealed.
Tamil Nadu Essential Services Maintenance (Repeal) Act, 2006_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu All actions taken under the Tamil Nadu Essential Services Maintenance Act, 2002 and all proceedings thereunder pending immediately before the commencement of this Act, before any court or authority shall abate.
