Aadhaar (Enrolment and Update) (Fifth Amendment) Regulations, 2018_Section Preamble--> No. 13012/79/2017/Legal-UIDAI (13) (No. 1 of 2018) - In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (1) and sub-clause (b), (e), (j), (n), (s), (v) and (x) of sub-section (2) of Section 54 of the Aadhaar (Targeted Delivery of Financial and Other Subsidies, Benefits and Services) Act, 2016, the Unique Identification Authority of India hereby makes the following regulations to further amend the Aadhaar (Enrolment and Update) Regulations, 2016 (No. 2 of 2016), as amended from time to time, namely:-
Aadhaar (Enrolment and Update) (Fifth Amendment) Regulations, 2018_Section 1--> (1) These regulations may be called the Aadhaar (Enrolment and Update) (Fifth Amendment) Regulations, 2018 (No. 1 of 2018). (2) These shall come into force from the date of their publication in the Official Gazette.
Aadhaar (Enrolment and Update) (Fifth Amendment) Regulations, 2018_Section 2--> In Schedule II of the Aadhaar (Enrolment and Update) Regulations, 2016, as amended from time to time, S.No. 16 of List B shall be substituted as "Signed Letter having Photo issued by Recognized Educational Institution on letterhead or Photo ID having address issued by Recognized Educational Institution", S.No. 23 of List B shall be substituted as "Certificate of Address having photo issued by MP or MLA or MLC or Gazetted Officer or Tehsildar on letterhead", in List C "A certificate or ID card having photo and Date of Birth (DoB) duly signed and issued by a Government authority" shall be inserted as S.No. 10 and in List C "Photo ID card having Date of Birth, issued by Recognized Educational Institution" shall be inserted as S.No. 11.
Aadhaar (Enrolment and Update) (Fifth Amendment) Regulations, 2018_Section 3--> In Schedule IV of the Aadhaar (Enrolment and Update) Regulations, 2016, as amended from time to time, the form "Aadhaar Data Update/Correction Form" shall be substituted by the form "Aadhaar Address Update/Correction Form".
