Tamil Nadu Appropriation Act, 2017_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to provide for the appropriation of certain further moneys out of the Consolidated Fund of the State for the services and purposes of the financial year which commenced on the 1st day of April 2016. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Appropriation Act, 2017_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Appropriation Act, 2017.
Tamil Nadu Appropriation Act, 2017_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu The State Government may appropriate out of the Consolidated Fund of the State for the services and purposes of the financial year which commenced on the 1st day of April 2016, a further sum not exceeding twenty eight thousand nine hundred and forty two crore sixty nine lakh and forty thousand rupees, being moneys required to meet- (a) the supplementary grants made by the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly for that year, as set forth in column (3) of the Schedule; and (b) the supplementary expenditure charged on the Consolidated Fund of the State for that year, as set forth in column (4) of the Schedule.
Tamil Nadu Appropriation Act, 2017_Section Sch.--> State(s): Tamil Nadu Schedule (By order of the Governor) (S.S. Poovalingam) Secretary to Government-in-charge, Law Department.
