Chennai City Police (Extension to the Cities of Salem, Tiruchirappalli and Tirunelveli) Amendment Act, 2014_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to amend the Chennai City Police (Extension to the Cities of Salem, Tiruchirappalli and Tirunelveli) Act, 1997. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Chennai City Police (Extension to the Cities of Salem, Tiruchirappalli and Tirunelveli) Amendment Act, 2014_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Chennai City Police (Extension to the Cities of Salem, Tiruchirappalli and Tirunelveli) Amendment Act, 2014. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint.
Chennai City Police (Extension to the Cities of Salem, Tiruchirappalli and Tirunelveli) Amendment Act, 2014_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 2 of the Chennai City Police (Extension to the Cities of Salem, Tiruchirappalli and Tirunelveli) Act, 1997, for clause (1), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:- "(1) "City of Salem" means the area declared by the State Government, by notification, to be the City of Salem;". (By order of the Governor) (G. Jayachandran) Secretary to Government, Law Department.
