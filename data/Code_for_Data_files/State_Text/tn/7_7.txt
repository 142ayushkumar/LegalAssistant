Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Thirty-seventh Year of the Republic of India, as follows :-
Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1986. (2)(i) Clauses (a), (b), (d) and (e) of section 2 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st October, 1985. (ii) Clause (c) of section 2 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 3rd March, 1981.
Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In the First Schedule to the Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959 (Tamil Nadu Act 1 of 1959), - (a) after item 10 and the entries relating thereto, the following item and entries shall be inserted, namely :- "10-A Video cassette players and At the point of 15."; recorders, video cassette tapes first sale in the and video computers and parts and State. accessories thereof. (b) for item 88 and the entries relating thereto, the following item and entries shall be substituted, namely :- "88 (a) Cashewnut with shell. At the point of 5 first purchase in the State. (b) Cashewnut kernel At the point of 5."; including processed first sale in the kernel. State. (c) in item 106, in the entries in column (2), for the expression "item 67", the expression "item 159" shall be substituted; (d) in item 117, in the entries in column (2), - (i) in sub-item (ii), for the words "parchment and coated board", the words "and parchment board" shall be substituted; (ii) after the proviso, the following sub-item shall be added, namely :- "(v) Paper and board - laminated, coated or interlined with other materials."; (e) in item 162, in the entries in column (2), the following shall be added at the end, namely :- "and dipping measures, metric pouring measures, conical measures, cylindrical measures, metre-scales, measuring tapes, steel yards and survey chains.". The Statement of Objects and Reasons appended to the Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Amendment) Bill, 1986 (L.A. Bill No. 32 of 1986) (Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Extraordinary No. 208, Part IV, Section 1, dated 23rd April, 1986, page 159.), runs as follows :- "The Government have issued notifications under sub-section (1) of section 59 of the Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959 (Tamil Nadu Act 1 of 1959), amending the First Schedule to the said Act, with effect from 1st October, 1985, so as, - (i) to include a new item 10-A in order to remove the ambiguity in the classification of video cassette players and recorders, video cassette tapes and video computers and parts and accessories thereof and to tax them at 15 per cent; (ii) to recast item 88 in order to levy tax on the first sales of cashewnut kernels including processed kernels, and to continue the levy of purchase tax only on cashewnut with shell, as they were held by courts to be commercially different commodities; (iii) to substitute the figure '67', occurring in item 106, by the figure '159', because fuel gas in item 67 was deleted with effect from 3rd March, 1981, and brought under item 159, grouped with petroleum products; (iv) to recast item 117 in order to levy tax, on processed papers and boards, viz., coated/laminated papers/boards and boards/papers interlined with other materials, under a separate sub-item; (v) to include in item 162, measuring instruments and measures like dipping measures, metric pouring measures, conical measures, cylindrical measures, metre-scales, measuring tapes, steel yards and survey chains, along with weighing instruments and weights, to have uniformity in taxation both on weights and measures. Under sub-section (2) of section 59 of the said Act, a Bill to replace the aforesaid notifications has to be introduced in the present session of the legislature. In the Bill which seeks to replace the notifications, as a consequential measure, the matter pertaining to item (iii) has been given effect to from 3rd March, 1981. The Bill seeks to achieve the above objects."
