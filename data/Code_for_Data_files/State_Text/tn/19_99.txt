Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act, 1971. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning (Amendment) Act, 2017. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 50 of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act, 1971, for the expression "for a period of three years", the expression "for a period of five years" shall be substituted. (By order of the Governor) (S.S. Poovalingam) Secretary to Government-in-charge, Law Department.
