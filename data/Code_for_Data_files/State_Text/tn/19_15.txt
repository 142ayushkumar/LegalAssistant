Tamil Nadu Appropriation (Vote on Account) Act, 2014_Section Preamble--> State(s): 							Tamil Nadu An Act to provide for the withdrawal of certain sums from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State for the services and purposes of a part of the financial year commencing on the 1st day of April 2014. Be in enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Appropriation (Vote on Account) Act, 2014_Section 1--> State(s): 							Tamil Nadu This Act may be called the 								Tamil Nadu Appropriation (Vote on Account) Act, 2014							.
Tamil Nadu Appropriation (Vote on Account) Act, 2014_Section 2--> State(s): 							Tamil Nadu The State Government may withdraw from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State sums not exceeding eighty four thousand five hundred and eighty three crore thirty nine lakh and forty seven thousand rupees, being moneys required to meet- (a) the grants made in advance by the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly for a part of the financial year commencing on the 1st day of April 2014, as set forth in column (3) of the Schedule; and (b) the expenditure charged on the Consolidated Fund of the State for the said part of that year, as set forth in column (4) of the Schedule.
Tamil Nadu Appropriation (Vote on Account) Act, 2014_Section 3--> State(s): 							Tamil Nadu The sums authorised to be withdrawn from and out of the Consolidated Fund under section 2 shall be appropriated for the services and purposes expressed in the Schedule in relation to that year.
Tamil Nadu Appropriation (Vote on Account) Act, 2014_Section Sch.--> State(s): 							Tamil Nadu Schedule (By order of the Governor) (G. Jayachandran) Secretary to Government, Law Department.
