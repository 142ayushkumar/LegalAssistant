Tamil Nadu National Law School (Amendment) Act, 2018_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu National Law School Act, 2012. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu National Law School (Amendment) Act, 2018_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu National Law School (Amendment) Act, 2018. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Tamil Nadu National Law School (Amendment) Act, 2018_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In the long title to the Tamil Nadu National Law School Act, 2012 (herein after referred to as the Principal Act), for the expression "National Law School", the expression "National Law University" shall be substituted.
Tamil Nadu National Law School (Amendment) Act, 2018_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 1 of the Principal Act, for sub-section (1), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely:- "(1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu National Law University Act, 2012.".
Tamil Nadu National Law School (Amendment) Act, 2018_Section 4--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 2 of the Principal Act, (1) clause (m) shall be omitted. (2) after clause (o), the following clause shall be inserted, namely; "(oo) "University" means the Tamil Nadu National Law University;".
Tamil Nadu National Law School (Amendment) Act, 2018_Section 5--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In the principal Act, for the expression "School", wherever it occurs, the expression "University" shall be substituted.
Tamil Nadu National Law School (Amendment) Act, 2018_Section 6--> State(s): Tamil Nadu References to "Tamil Nadu National Law School" and "Tamil Nadu National Law School Act" in any Act or in any rule, notification, proceedings, order, regulation, by-law or other instruments made or continued in force under the Principal Act shall be construed as references to "Tamil Nadu National Law University" and "Tamil Nadu National Law University Act" respectively. (By order of the Governor) (S. S. Poovalingam) Secretary to Government, Law Department.
