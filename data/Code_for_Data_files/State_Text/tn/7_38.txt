Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1998_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959. BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Forty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1998. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st day of April, 1998.
Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 3 of the Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), after sub-section (5), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely :- "(6) Notwithstanding anything contained in sub-section (2) and subject to the provisions of sub-section (1), the tax payable by a dealer in respect of sale of rubber products including tyres, tubes and flaps inside the State, manufactured by him inside the State out of raw rubber purchased inside the State shall be reduced to the extent of an amount equal to the purchase tax paid on raw rubber over and above three per cent : Provided that the dealer purchasing the raw rubber maintains a separate stock account for the raw rubber purchased by him inside the State, showing such particulars as may be prescribed."
Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 12 of the principal Act, - (1) in sub-section (1) - (a) in clause (a), after the proviso, the following proviso shall be inserted, namely :- "Provided further that subject to the provisions of clause (b) and the provisions of sub-section (1-A), a dealer whose total turnover in a year exceeds ten lakhs of rupees but does not exceed twenty lakhs of rupees may exercise his option for a self-assessment for that year on or before the 1st day of May of the succeeding year subject to the condition that he gets his accounts audited and certified by a Chartered Accountant and submits to the assessing authority a copy of the audited and certified statement of accounts and such other statements, as may be prescribed, on or before the 31st day of October of the succeeding year and such return so filed by him shall be accepted without calling for the account.". (b) in clause (b), for item (iii), the following items shall be substituted, namely :- "(iii) if he is doing his business for the first year or last year; or (iv) if the taxable turnover as per return falls short of the taxable turnover as per accounts as audited and certified by a Chartered Accountant by more than one per cent in respect of dealers failing under the second proviso to clause (a)". (2) for sub-section (1-A) the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely :- "(1-A) Notwithstanding anything contained in the provisos to clause (a) of sub-section (1), twenty-five per cent of the total number of such assessments not exceeding ten lakhs of rupees per year and ten per cent of the total number of such assessments exceeding ten lakhs of rupees, but not exceeding twenty lakhs of rupees per year, shall be selected by the Commissioner in such manner as may be prescribed for the purpose of detailed scrutiny regarding the correctness of the return submitted by the dealer in this connection and in such cases, final assessment orders shall be passed in accordance with the provisions of this Act.".
Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Act, 1998_Section 4--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 20 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), in clause (a), for the expression "seventy-five thousand rupees", the expression "one lakh and fifty thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
