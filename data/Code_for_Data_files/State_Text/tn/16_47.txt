Tamil Nadu Prohibition of Forcible Conversion of Religion (Repeal) Act, 2006_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An act to repeal the Tamil Nadu Prohibition of Forcible Conversion of Religion Act, 2002. BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Fifty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Prohibition of Forcible Conversion of Religion (Repeal) Act, 2006_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Prohibition of Forcible Conversion of Religion (Repeal) Act, 2006. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 18th day of May 2004.
Tamil Nadu Prohibition of Forcible Conversion of Religion (Repeal) Act, 2006_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu The Tamil Nadu Prohibition of Forcible Conversion of Religion Act, 2002 is hereby repealed.
