Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1983_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Thirty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1983. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st July, 1983.
Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In the First Schedule to the Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959 (Tamil Nadu Act 1 of 1959), - (a) item 24 and the entries relating thereto shall be omitted; (b) in item 103, for the entries in column (2), the following entries shall be substituted, namely :- "(i) Biscuits; (ii) Toffees; (iii) Chocolates; (iv) Confectionery : (v) Butter; (vi) Ghee; (vii) Cheese; (viii) Milk foods including milk powder; (ix) Condensed milk; and (x) Foods including preparations of vegetables, fruits, milk, cereals, flour, starch, birds, eggs, meat and meat offals, animal blood, fish, crustaceans and molluscs, which are sold under any brand name registered under the Trade and Merchandise Marks Act, 1958 (Central Act 43 of 1958)"; (c) item 143 and the entries relating thereto shall be omitted. The statement of objects and reasons appended to the Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Bill (41 of 1983), (Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Extraordinary No. 375, Part IV, Section 1, dated October 26th, 1983, page 226.) runs as follows :- In the light of the experience gained in the administration of the Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959 (Tamil Nadu Act 1 of 1959), it has been decided to amend the following entries in the First Schedule to the said Act. Accordingly, the Government have issued notifications to take effect from the 1st July, 1983, under sub-section (1) of section 59 of the said Act, so as, - (i) to omit the entry relating to milk foods and to substitute entry 103 relating to biscuits, toffees, etc., and (ii) to omit the entry relating to combs. 2.Under sub-section (2) of section 59 of the said Act, a Bill to replace the aforesaid notifications has to be introduced during the ensuring session of the Legislature. 3.The Bill seeks to replace the notifications issued under sub-section (1) of section 59 of the said Act.
