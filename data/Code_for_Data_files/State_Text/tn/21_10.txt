Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax (Fifth Amendment) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax (Fifth Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax (Fifth Amendment) Act, 2013. (2) (a) Section 2 shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint. (b) Section 3 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st day of April 2013. (c) Section 4 shall come into force at once.
Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax (Fifth Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 19 of the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act),- (1) to sub-section (2), the following proviso shall be added, namely:- "Provided that input tax credit shall be allowed in excess of three per cent of tax for the purpose specified in clause (v)."; (2) in sub-section (4), for the expression "three per cent of tax", the expression "five per cent of tax" shall be substituted.
