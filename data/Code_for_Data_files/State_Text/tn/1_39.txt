Anna University (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Anna University Act, 1978. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Anna University (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Anna University (Amendment) Act, 2013. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Anna University (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 11 of the Anna University Act, 1978, in the second proviso to sub-section (3), for the expression "sixty-five years", the expression "seventy years" shall be substituted. (By order of the Governor) (G. Jayachandran) Secretary to Government, Law Department.
