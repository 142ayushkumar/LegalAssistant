Tamil Nadu Infrastructure Development (Amendment) Act, 2014_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to amend the Tamil Nadu Infrastructure Development Act, 2012. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Infrastructure Development (Amendment) Act, 2014_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Infrastructure Development (Amendment) Act, 2014. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Tamil Nadu Infrastructure Development (Amendment) Act, 2014_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 3 of the Tamil Nadu Infrastructure Development Act, 2012 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (3), after clause (k), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(kk) the Secretary to Government, Agriculture Department, ex-officio;".
Tamil Nadu Infrastructure Development (Amendment) Act, 2014_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 9 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), after clause (j), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(jj) the Secretary to Government, Agriculture Department, ex-officio;". (By order of the Governor) (G. Jayachandran) Secretary to Government, Law Department.
