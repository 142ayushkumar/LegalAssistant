Tamil Nadu Installation of Oil Engines (Temporary Permission) Act, 1954_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to provide for and validate the grant of temporary permissions for the installation of oil engines in areas within the jurisdiction of local authorities in the State of Tamil Nadu. WHEREAS it is expedient to provide for and validate the grant of temporary permission for the installation of oil engines in areas within the jurisdiction of local authorities in the State of Tamil Nadu; BE it enacted in the Fifth Year of Our Republic as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Installation of Oil Engines (Temporary Permission) Act, 1954_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called The Tamil Nadu Installation of Oil Engines (Temporary Permission) Act, 19541 . (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Tamil Nadu. (3) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st May, 1953. Received the assent of the Governor on the 26th August, 1954; Published in the Fort St. George Gazette (Extraordinary), Part IV, Section 2, dated the 1st September, 1954
Tamil Nadu Installation of Oil Engines (Temporary Permission) Act, 1954_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) Where a person who has lawfully installed in any premises, whether in a residential area or not, any machinery or manufacturing plant driven by electric power, applies for permission to install an oil engine to generate power for working such machinery or plant, the Commissioner, Corporation of Madras, the Municipal Council, the District Board or the Panchayat, as the case may be, may grant permission to such person to install such an oil engine. (2) Permission granted under sub-section (1) shall be valid for a period of one year from the date of the grant and, subject to that condition, shall be deemed to be permission granted by the Commissioner, Corporation of Madras, the Municipal Council, the District Board or the Panchayat, as the case may be, under the Madras City Municipal Act, 1919 (Tamil Nadu Act 4 of 1919), the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act 5 of 1920), the Tamil Nadu District Boards Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 1920), or the Tamil Nadu Village Panchayats Act, 1950 (Tamil Nadu Act 10 of 1950).
