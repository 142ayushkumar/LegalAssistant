Arni Jagir Act, 1909_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to include the Ami Jagir in the Schedule to the Tamil Nadu Impartible Estates Act, 1940 WHEREAS it is expedient to declare that the Ami Jagir is impartible and that the jagirdar cannot exercise unrestricted powers of alienation in respect thereof; It is hereby enacted as follows:-
Arni Jagir Act, 1909_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu This Act may be called The Arni Jagir Act, 1909
Arni Jagir Act, 1909_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu The Ami Jagir in the District of North Arcot shall be included in the Schedule to the Tamil Nadu Impartible Estates Act, 1904, as number 11 under the head of North Arcot and be subject to the provisions of the Act.
