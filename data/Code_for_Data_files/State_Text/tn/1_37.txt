Tamil Nadu Animals and Birds Sacrifices Prohibition (Repeal) Act, 2004_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to repeal the Tamil Nadu Animals and Birds Sacrifices Prohibition Act, 1950. Be, it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Fifty-fifth year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Animals and Birds Sacrifices Prohibition (Repeal) Act, 2004_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Animals and Birds Sacrifices Prohibition (Repeal) Act, 2004.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 20th day of February 2004.2 Receive the assent of the Governor on 05th August 2004 Substituted for "Madras" by Tamil Nadu A.O., 1969
Tamil Nadu Animals and Birds Sacrifices Prohibition (Repeal) Act, 2004_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu The Tamil Nadu Animals and Birds Sacrifices Prohibition Act, 1950 (Tamil Nadu Act 32 of 1950 - hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), is hereby repealed.
Tamil Nadu Animals and Birds Sacrifices Prohibition (Repeal) Act, 2004_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu All actions taken under the principal Act and all proceedings there under pending immediately before the 20th day of February 2004, before any Court or authority shall abate.
Tamil Nadu Animals and Birds Sacrifices Prohibition (Repeal) Act, 2004_Section 4--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) The Tamil Nadu Animals and Birds Sacrifices Prohibition (Repeal) Ordinance, 2004 (Tamil Nadu Ordinance 2 of 2004) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under this Act.
