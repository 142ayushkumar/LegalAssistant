City of Madras (Alteration of Name) Act, 1996_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to alter the name of City of Madras. BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Forty Seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
City of Madras (Alteration of Name) Act, 1996_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the City of Madras (Alteration of Name) Act, 1996. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint.
City of Madras (Alteration of Name) Act, 1996_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In this Act, unless the context otherwise requires, law includes any enactment, ordinance, regulation, order, by-law, scheme, notification or other instrument having the force of law in the whole or any part of the State of Tamil Nadu with respect to which the State Legislature has competence to make law.
City of Madras (Alteration of Name) Act, 1996_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu On and from the date appointed under sub-section (2) of section 1, for the word "Madras" occurring in any law in force in this State, the word "Chennai" shall be substituted.
