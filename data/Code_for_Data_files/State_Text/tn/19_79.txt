Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 2013. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 21st day of March 2013.
Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax (Fourth Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In the First Schedule to the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006, in PART-C, the entries against (ii) and (iii) of Serial Number (13-A) shall be omitted. (By order of the Governor) (G. Jayachandran) Secretary to Government, Law Department.
