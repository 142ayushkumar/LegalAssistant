Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Second Amendment Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Act, 1976 . BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil, Nadu in the Forty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Second Amendment Act, 1993_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu This Act may be called the, Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Second Amendment Act, 1993.
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Second Amendment Act, 1993_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Act, 1976, in sub-section (1), for the words " sixteen years and eleven months the words "seventeen years and eleven months " shall be substituted. (By order of the Governor) M. Muniraman, Secretary to Government, Law Department.
