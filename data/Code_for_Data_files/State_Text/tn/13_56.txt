Tamil Nadu Minor Ports Fund Act, 1938_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to provide for the disposal of the balance to the credit of the Minor Ports Fund in the 1 [State of Tamil Nadu]. WHEREAS under sub-section (6) of Section 36 of the Indian Ports Act, 1908, the disposal of the balances from time to time standing to the credit of port fund accounts is subject to the provisions of any local law; AND WHEREAS the 2 [State] Government have directed that all minor ports in the 3 [State of Tamil Nadu] should be regarded as constituting a single port with a common port fund called the Minor Ports Fund: AND WHEREAS subsequently the Tuticorin Port Fund has been constituted as a separate fund under the Tuticorin Port Trust Act, 1924; AND WHEREAS it is expedient to provide by an Act of the 4 [Tamil Nadu Legislature] for the disposal of the balance which is now standing and which may from time to time stand to the credit of the said Minor Ports Fund other than that of Tuticorin; It is hereby enacted as follows:- Substituted for "Province of Madras" by Tamil Nadu A.O. 1970.
