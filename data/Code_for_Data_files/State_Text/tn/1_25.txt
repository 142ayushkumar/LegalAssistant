Agriculturists Loans (Tamil Nadu) Amendment Act, 1935_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to amend the Agriculturists Loans Act, 1884, in its application in the State of Tamil Nadu for a certain purpose. Whereas it is expedient to amend the Agriculturists Loans Act, 1884, in its application to the State of Tamil Nadu for the purpose hereinafter appearing. It is hereby enacted as follows:-
Agriculturists Loans (Tamil Nadu) Amendment Act, 1935_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu This Act may be called The Agriculturists Loans [1 (Tamil Nadu) Amendment] ACT, 1935.2 Substituted by Madras by Tamil Nadu A.O. 1969 Received the assent of the Governor on the 22nd September 1935
Agriculturists Loans (Tamil Nadu) Amendment Act, 1935_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In sub-section (1) of Section 4 of the Agriculturists Loans Act, 1884, after the words the relief of distress the words "or indebtedness", shall be inserted.
