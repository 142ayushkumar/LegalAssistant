Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Amendment Act, 1998_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Act, 1976 . BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Forty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Amendment Act, 1998_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Amendment Act, 1998. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Amendment Act, 1998_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Act, 1976, in sub-section (1), for the words "twenty-one years and eleven months", the words "twenty-two years and five months" shall be substituted. (By order of the Governor) A.K. Rajan, Secretary to Government, Law Department.
