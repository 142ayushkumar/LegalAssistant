The following Act of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly received the assent of the Governor on the 13th June 2011 and is hereby published for general information:- Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Extraordinary, Issue No. 177, Part IV-Section 2, Chennai, Monday, June 13, 2011, Vaikasi 30,Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042. The following Act of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly received the assent of the Governor on the 13th June 2011 and is hereby published for general information:-
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2011. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 25th day of May 2011.
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 89 of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1983 (Tamil Nadu Act 30 of 1983) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in the proviso to sub-section (1), for the expression "ten years", the expression "ten years and six months" shall be substituted.
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu Notwithstanding anything contained in the principal Act, every person exercising the powers and discharging the functions as Special Officer, with effect on and from the 25th day of May 2011-in case of primary co-operative societies and with effect from the 26th day of May 2011 in the case of central and apex co-operative societies, shall be deemed to have been appointed as such Special Officer of the said co-operative societies under sub-section (1) of section 89 of the principal Act, as amended by this Act, and anything done or any action taken by the said Special Officers during the period commencing on the 25th day of May 2011 and ending with the date of publication of this Act in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, shall be deemed to have been validly done or taken under the principal Act, as amended by this Act. (By order of the Governor) R. KATHIRVEL, Secy. to Government-in-charge, Law Department.
