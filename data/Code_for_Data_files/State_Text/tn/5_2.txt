East and West Tanjore Sessions Divisions (Validation) Act, 1931_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to validate the constitution of the Sessions divisions of East and West Tanjore Whereas by the notification of the Local Government in the Law (General) Department, No. 175, dated the 22nd day of June, 1921, the Governor in Council was pleased to direct that, with effect from the last day of September, 1921, the sessions division of Tanjore should be styled as the sessions divisions of West Tanjore consisting of Revenue taluks of Kumbakonam, Papanasam, Tanjore, Mannargudi, Pattukottai and Arantangi of the collectorate or revenue district of Tanjore; And whereas by the notification of the Local Government in the Law (General) Department, No. 177, dated the 22nd day of June, 1921, the Governor in Council was pleased to add to the number of Sessions divisions in the province by establishing, with effect from the 1st day of September, 1921, a new sessions division styled as the sessions divisions of East Tanjore consisting of the Revenue taluks of Shiyali, Mayavaram, Nannilam, Nagapattanam and Tirutturaipundi of the collectorate or revenue district of Tanjore; And whereas doubts have been raised as to the validity of the constitution of the said two Sessions divisions in the revenue district of Tanjore; And whereas it is expedient to remove those doubts and validate the constitution of the said Sessions divisions and the proceedings of the Courts of Session thereof; And whereas the previous sanction of the Governor General has been obtained to the passing of this Act; It is hereby enacted as follows:
East and West Tanjore Sessions Divisions (Validation) Act, 1931_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu This Act may be called the East and West Tanjore Sessions Divisions (Validation) Act, 19311 . Received the assent of the Governor on the 6th March, 1931, and that of the Governor-General on the 21st March, 1931; the assent of the Governor-General was first published in the Fort St. George Gazette of the 14th April, 1931
East and West Tanjore Sessions Divisions (Validation) Act, 1931_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu This Act shall be deemed to have had effect on and from the 1st day of September, 1921.
East and West Tanjore Sessions Divisions (Validation) Act, 1931_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu Notwithstanding anything contained in sub-section (1) of Section 7 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1898, the Sessions division of East Tanjore and the Sessions division of West Tanjore in the revenue district of Tanjore and the Court of Sessions established for each one of the said Sessions divisions shall be deemed to have been and be validly constituted and no proceeding of the Court of Sessions of the said Sessions divisions shall be questioned merely on the ground that the limits of neither of the said Sessions division were or are conterminous with the limits of a district.
