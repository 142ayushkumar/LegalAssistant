Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Seventh Amendment) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959. BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Fifty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Seventh Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Seventh Amendment) Act, 2000. (2)(a) Section 3 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st day of April, 2000. (b) Section 2 shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint.
Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Seventh Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In the First Schedule to the Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), - (1) in Part A, after item 4 and the entries relating thereto, the following item and entries shall be added, namely :- "5. Flour of pulses and grams First sale.". (2) in Part B, - (a) in item 79, after sub-item (ii), the following sub-item shall be inserted, namely :- "(iii) Combined harvester and transplanter."; (b) for item 82-A and the entries relating thereto, the following item and entries, shall be substituted, namely :- "82-A. Waste paper Last purchase.". (3) in Part D, after item 65 and the entries relating thereto, the following item and entries, shall be inserted, namely :- "65-A. Ready-mix concrete. First sale.".
Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Seventh Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In the Third Schedule to the principal Act, in Part-B, in item 81, for the expression "The following, for sale, by any dealer whose total turnover does not exceed Rs. 100 crores in a year", the expression "The following goods, for sale, by any dealer whose turnover in respect of the goods in each sub-item does not exceed rupees three hundred crores in a year" shall be substituted.
