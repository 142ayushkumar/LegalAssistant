Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage (Second Amendment) Act, 2012_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage Act, 1978. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage (Second Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage (Second Amendment) Act, 2012. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint.
Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage (Second Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 37 of the Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage Act, 1978,- (1) in sub-section (7), for the expression "sanction'', the expression "approve" shall be substituted; (2) sub-section (8), shall be omitted; (3) in sub-section (9), for the expression "sanctioned'', the expression "approved" shall be substituted. (By order of the Governor) (G. Jayachandran) Secretary to Government, Law Department.
