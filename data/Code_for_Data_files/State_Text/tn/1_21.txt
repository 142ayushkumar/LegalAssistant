Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Second Amendment Act, 2017_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1987. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Second Amendment Act, 2017_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Second Amendment Act, 2017. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 31st day of May 2017.
Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Second Amendment Act, 2017_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 33 of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1987, in sub-section (1), in the proviso, for the expression "six years", the expression "seven years" shall be substituted.
Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Second Amendment Act, 2017_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) The Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Second Amendment Ordinance, 2017 is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Produce Marketing (Regulation) Act, 1987, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the said Act, as amended by this Act. (By order of the Governor) (S.S. Poovalingam) Secretary to Government-in-charge, Law Department.
