Tamil Nadu Marine Fishing Regulation (Amendment) Amendment Act, 2017_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to amend the Tamil Nadu Marine Fishing Regulation (Amendment) Act, 2016. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Marine Fishing Regulation (Amendment) Amendment Act, 2017_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Marine Fishing Regulation (Amendment) Amendment Act, 2017.
Tamil Nadu Marine Fishing Regulation (Amendment) Amendment Act, 2017_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 1 of the Tamil Nadu Marine Fishing Regulation (Amendment) Act, 2016 (hereinafter referred to as the Amendment Act), for sub-section (2), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely:- "(2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint and different dates may be appointed for different provisions of the Act.".
Tamil Nadu Marine Fishing Regulation (Amendment) Amendment Act, 2017_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 5 of the Amendment Act, for clause (1), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:- "(1) in sub-section (3), for the expression "three nautical miles" in three places where it occurs, the expression "five nautical miles" shall be substituted.". (By Order of the Governor) (S.S. Poovalingam) Secretary to Government-in-charge, Law Department.
