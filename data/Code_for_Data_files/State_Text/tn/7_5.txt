Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959. BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Thirty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1982. (2) (i) Clauses (a) and (d) of section 2 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 8th July, 1981. (ii) Clause (b) of section 2 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st July, 1981. (iii) Clause (c) of section 2 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 15th June, 1981.
Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In the First Schedule to the Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax, 1959 (Tamil Nadu Act 1 of 1959), - (a) in item 3, in the entries in column (2), - (i) after the words, "motor cycles and cycle combinations" and before the words "motor scooters", the following words and brackets shall be inserted, namely :- "cycles (including bicycles, tri-cycles, cycle-rickshaws, tandem cycles, cycle combinations and perambulators) fitted with motor engines."; (ii) the following words and brackets shall be added at the end, namely :- "and motor engines used for being fitted to cycles (including bicycles, tricycles, cycle-rickshaws, tandem cycles, cycle combinations and perambulators)."; (b) items 144, 145 and 147 and the entries relating thereto shall be omitted; (c) in item 150, for the entry in column (2), the following entry shall be substituted, namely :- "Articles of food and drink other than those specified elsewhere in this schedule, sold to customers in hotels classified or approved by the Department of Tourism, Government of India."; (d) after item 160 and the entries relating thereto, the following item and entries shall be added, namely :- "161. Carbon black. At the point of first 8" sale in the State. The Statement of Objects and Reasons appended to the Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax (Third Amendment) Bill, 1981 (L.A. Bill No. 63 of 1981) (Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Extraordinary No. 302, Part IV, Section 1, dated 22nd August, 1981, page 666.), runs as follows :- "The Government issued notifications under sub-section (1) of section 59 of the Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959 (Tamil Nadu Act 1 of 1959), amending the First Schedule to the said Act, so as - (1) to bring 'carbon black', a petrochemical (which was formerly being taxed at different rates resulting in loss of revenue due to Government), under 8 per cent single point tax with effect from 8th July, 1981; (2) to bring motorised cycles and cycle-rickshaws fitted with motor engines and motor engines used for being fitted to cycles, cycle-rickshaws, etc., under 15 per cent single point tax, with effect from 8th July, 1981; (3) to bring sales of articles of food and drink made in hotels classified and approved by the Department of Tourism, Government of India, under the purview of entry 150 of the First Schedule with effect from 15th June, 1981. Earlier sale of articles of food and drink sold in Three Star, Four Star and Five Star Hotels alone were taxed; (4) to delete stationery articles, candles and plastic articles from the First Schedule and to bring them under the multi-point levy of sales tax with effect from 1st July, 1981, in response to the representation from the trade and in order to avoid loss of revenue. 2. Under sub-section (2) of section 59 of the said Act, a Bill to replace the aforesaid notifications has to be introduced in the present session of the Legislature. 3. The Bill seeks to achieve the above object."
