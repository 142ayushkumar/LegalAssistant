Thiruvalluvar University (Amendment) Act, 2006_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Thiruvalluvar University Act, 2002 Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Fifty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Thiruvalluvar University (Amendment) Act, 2006_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu This Act may be called the Thiruvalluvar University (Amendment) Act, 2006.
Thiruvalluvar University (Amendment) Act, 2006_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 12 of the Thiruvalluvar University Act, 2002 (Tamil Nadu Act 32 of 2002), in sub-section (3), for the expression "shall not be eligible for a second term in the same University", the expression "shall be eligible for re-appointment for a further period of three years" shall be substituted. (By Order of the Governor) L. Jayasankaran, Secretary to Government, Law Department.
