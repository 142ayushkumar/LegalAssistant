Tamil Nadu Industrial Establishments (National, Festival and Special Holidays) Amendment Act, 2018_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Industrial Establishments (National, Festival and Special Holidays) Act, 1958. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Industrial Establishments (National, Festival and Special Holidays) Amendment Act, 2018_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Industrial Establishments (National, Festival and Special Holidays) Amendment Act, 2018. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint.
Tamil Nadu Industrial Establishments (National, Festival and Special Holidays) Amendment Act, 2018_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 8 of the Tamil Nadu Industrial Establishments (National, Festival and Special Holidays) Act, 1958 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act),- (1) for the expression "one thousand rupees" at two places where it occurs, the expression "five thousand rupees" shall be substituted; (2) for the expression "two thousand and five hundred rupees", the expression "ten thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Tamil Nadu Industrial Establishments (National, Festival and Special Holidays) Amendment Act, 2018_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 9 of the principal Act, for the expression "five hundred rupees", the expression "one thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Tamil Nadu Industrial Establishments (National, Festival and Special Holidays) Amendment Act, 2018_Section 4--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 12 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), for the expression "five hundred rupees", the expression "five thousand rupees" shall be substituted. (By order of the Governor) (S. S. Poovalingam) Secretary to Government, Law Department.
