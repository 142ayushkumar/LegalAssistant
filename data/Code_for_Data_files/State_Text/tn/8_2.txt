Tamil Nadu Heritage Commission (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to amend the Tamil Nadu Heritage Commission Act, 2012. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Heritage Commission (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Heritage Commission (Amendment) Act, 2017. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint.
Tamil Nadu Heritage Commission (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In the Tamil Nadu Heritage Commission Act, 2012, in section 4, in sub-section (1), clause (n) shall be omitted. (By order of the Governor) (S.S. Poovalingam) Secretary to Government-in-charge, Law Department.
