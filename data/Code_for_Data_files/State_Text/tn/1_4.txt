Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to amend the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Fiftieth Year of the Republic of India as follows: -
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes (Amendment) Act, 1999. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 7 of the Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes Act, 1997 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (11), for the expression "within such time as may be prescribed", the expression "within six months from the date of such reference" shall be substituted.
Tamil Nadu Acquisition of Land for Industrial Purposes (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 12 of the principal Act, for the expression "four per cent", the expression "nine per cent" shall be substituted. (By order of the Governor) K. PARTHASARATHY, Secretary to Government, Law Department.
