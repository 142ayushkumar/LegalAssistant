Indian Stamp Act, 1899 (As applicable in Tamil Nadu)_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to consolidate and amend the law relating to Stamps WHEREAS it is expedient to consolidate and amend the law relating to stamps, it is hereby enacted as follows:-
Indian Stamp Act, 1899 (As applicable in Tamil Nadu)_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Indian Stamp Act, 1899 (As applicable in Tamil Nadu). (2) It extends to the whole of India except the State of Jammu and Kashmir: Provided that it shall not apply to the territories which, immediately before the 1st November 1956, were comprised in Part B States (excluding the State of Jammu and Kashmir) except to the extent to which the provisions of this Act relate to rates of stamp duty in respect of the documents specified in entry 91 of List 1 in the Seventh Schedule to the Constitution. (3) It shall come into force on the first day of July, 1899.
