Courtallam Township (Validation of Taxes and Library Cess) Act, 1960_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to validate the levy, assessments and collection in the Courtallam Township of taxes under the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920, and library cess under the Tamil Nadu Public Libraries Act, 1948. Whereas it is expedient to validate the levy, assessment and collection in the Courtallam Township of taxes under the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act 5 of 1920), and library cess under the Tamil Nadu Public Libraries Act, 1948 (Tamil Nadu Act 24 of 1948) Be it enacted in the Eleventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Courtallam Township (Validation of Taxes and Library Cess) Act, 1960_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu This Act may be called The Courtallam Township (Validation of Taxes and Library Cess) Act, 1960.1 2 Received the assent of the Governor on 11th June, 1960 Published in the Fort St. George Gazette (Extraordinary), Part 4-B dated 15th June, 1960
Courtallam Township (Validation of Taxes and Library Cess) Act, 1960_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In this Act, unless the context otherwise requires- (a) "Courtallam Township" means the Courtallam Township mentioned in sub-section (1) of section 2 of the Courtallam Township Act: (b) " Courtallam Township Act" means the Courtallam Township Act, 1954 (Tamil Nadu Act 26 of 1954) : (c) "Courtallam Township Committee" means the Courtallam Township Committee constituted under sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Courtallam Township Act; (d) " District Municipalities Act" means the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act 5 of 1920) (e) " Public Libraries Act" means the Tamil Nadu Public Libraries Act, 1948 (Tamil Nadu Act 24 of 1948)
Courtallam Township (Validation of Taxes and Library Cess) Act, 1960_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu Notwithstanding anything contained in any judgement, decree or order of any Court, no levy, assessment or collection in the Courtallam Township by the Courtallam Township Committee or by other authority, officer, or person for the period commencing on the 1st October, 1955 and ending with 30th September, 1960, of any tax under the District Municipalities Act or library cess under the Public Libraries Act shall be deemed to be invalid or ever to have been invalid on the ground only that such levy, assessment or collection was not in accordance with the provisions of the District Municipalities Act or the Public Libraries Act or the rules made there under or of any other law for the time being in force in the Courtallam Township and such tax of library cess levied, assessed or collected or purporting to have been levied, assessed, or collected shall for all purposes be deemed to be and to have always been, validly levied, assessed or collected and accordingly- (a) all acts, proceedings or things done or taken by the Courtallam Township Committee or by any other authority, officer or person in connection with the levy, assessment or collection of such tax, or library cess shall for all purposes be deemed to be, and to have always been, done or taken in accordance with law; (b) no suit or other proceeding shall be maintained or continued in any Court against the Courtallam Township Committee or any other authority, officer or person whatsoever for the refund of any tax or library cess so paid; and (c) no Court shall enforce any decree or order directing the refund of any tax or library cess so paid: Provided that no act of omission on the part of any person shall be punishable as an offence which would not have been so punishable if this Act not been passed.
Courtallam Township (Validation of Taxes and Library Cess) Act, 1960_Section 4--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) Without prejudice to the provisions of section 3 and notwithstanding anything contained in the Public Libraries Act, a library cess shall be levied, assessed and collected in the Courtallam Township under and in accordance with the provisions of the Public Libraries Act. (2) For the purpose of sub-section (1), all references in the Public Libraries Act to the District Municipalities Act, the municipal council and the municipalities shall respectively be construed and deemed always to have been construed, as reference to the Courtallam Township Act, the Courtallam Township Committee and the Courtallam Township.
