Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2013. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In the Fourth Schedule to the Tamil Nadu Value Added Tax Act, 2006, in PART-B, for the entry against Serial Number 2, the following entry shall be substituted, namely:- "Aids and implements for differently abled persons as notified by the Government.". (By order of the Governor) (G. Jayachandran) Secretary to Government, Law Department.
