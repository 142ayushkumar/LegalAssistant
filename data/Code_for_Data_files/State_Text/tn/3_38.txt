Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Amendment Act, 1994_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Office) Act, 1976. BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Forty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Amendment Act, 1994_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Amendment Act, 1994.
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Amendment Act, 1994_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Act, 1976, in sub-section (1), for the words "seventeen years and eleven months", the words "eighteen years and eleven months" shall be substituted. (By order of. the Governor) M. Muniraman, Secretary to Government, Law Department.
