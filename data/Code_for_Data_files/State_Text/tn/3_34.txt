Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Amendment Act, 1989_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act farther to amend the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Act, 1976 . BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the Slate of Tamil Nadu in the Fortieth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Amendment Act, 1989_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Amendment Act, 1989. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 7th day of October 1989.
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Amendment Act, 1989_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Act, 1976 (hereinafter referred to the principal Act), in subsection (1), for the words " thirteen years and four months the words " fourteen years and one month " shall be substituted.
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Amendment Act, 1989_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) The Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Amendment Ordinance, 1989, is hereby repeated. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be defined to have been done or taken under the principal Act, as amended by this Act. (By order: of the Governor) P. JEYAS1NGH PETER, Secretary to Government, Law Department.
