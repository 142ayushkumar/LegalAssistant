Tamil Nadu Horse Races (Abolition of Wagering or Betting) Act, 1974_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to provide for the abolition of wagering or betting on horse races in the State of Tamil Nadu BE it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Twenty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Horse Races (Abolition of Wagering or Betting) Act, 1974_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu The Act may be called the Tamil Nadu House Races (Abolition of Wagering or Betting) Act, 19741 . Received the assent of the Governor on the 26th September, 1974; Published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette (Extraordinary) Part 4 Section 2, dated 30th September, 1974
Tamil Nadu Horse Races (Abolition of Wagering or Betting) Act, 1974_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In the Madras City Police and Gaming (Amendment) Act, 1949 (Tamil Nadu Act 7 of 1949), in Section 1,- (1) in sub-section (2) the portion commencing with the expression and Sections 2 and 4 and ending with the expression appoint, shall be omitted; (2) after sub-section (2) the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely; (3) Section 2 and 4 shall come into force on the 31st March, 1975, notwithstanding anything contained in any law for the time being in force or in any notification or order issued by the Government.
Tamil Nadu Horse Races (Abolition of Wagering or Betting) Act, 1974_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu The orders issued by the Government in G.O. Ms. No. 1570, Home Department dated the 7th June, 1973, shall cease to have effect with effect from the date of the publication of this Act in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette.
