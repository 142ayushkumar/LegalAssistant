Tamil Nadu Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Entertainments Tax Act, 1939. BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Forty-eight Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Tamil Nadu Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1997. (2)(a) Section 2 and 3 shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint. (b) Section 4 in so far as it relates to Keeranur Panchayat town shall be deemed to have come into force on the 7th day of August, 1984 and so far as it relates to Karambakudi Panchayat town it shall be deemed to have come into force on the 12th day of December, 1989.
Tamil Nadu Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 4-E of the Tamil Nadu Entertainments Tax Act, 1939 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), for sub-section (1), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely :- "(1) Notwithstanding anything contained in sections 4 and 7, there shall be levied and paid to the State Government a tax (hereinafter referred to as the entertainments tax) calculated at the following rates per month for every connection for television exhibition, namely :- (i) Within the limits of the Municipal Twenty rupees Corporations of Chennai, Madurai, Coimbatore, Tiruchirappalli, Tirunelveli, Salem or in any other Corporation that may be constituted under any law for the time being in force; (ii) Within the limits of Municipalities Fifteen rupees constituted under the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920); (iii) Within the limits of Industrial Townships Ten rupees". and Town Panchayats constituted under the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920) or in the Village Panchayats constituted under the Tamil Nadu Panchayats Act, 1994 (Tamil Nadu Act 21 of 1994).
Tamil Nadu Entertainments Tax (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 13 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), - (i) for the expression "thirty per cent.", the expression "ten per cent.", shall be substituted; (ii) for the expression "seventy per cent." in three places where it occurs, the expression "ninety per cent." shall be substituted.
