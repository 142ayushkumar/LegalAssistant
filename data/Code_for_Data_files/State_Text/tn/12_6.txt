Tamil Nadu Lapsed Acts (Removal of Doubts) Act, 1948_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act for the removal of doubts regarding the operation of certain enactments. Whereas the Acts specified in the Schedule below, Acts made by the Governor of 1 [Tamil Nadu] under a Proclamation issued by him under section 93, of the Government of India Act, will be cease to have effect on the 30th day of April, 1948 on the lapse of two years from the date on which the said Proclamation was revoked; And whereas it is expedient to remove some doubts regarding the operation of those Acts; It is hereby enacted as follows:- Substituted for Madras by Tamil Nadu A.O. 1969.
Tamil Nadu Lapsed Acts (Removal of Doubts) Act, 1948_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Lapsed Acts (Removal of Doubts) Act, 19481 . (2) It shall come into force on the 29th day of April, 1948. Received the assent of the Governor on the 26th April, 1948; first published in the Fort St. George Gazette on the 27th April, 1948
Tamil Nadu Lapsed Acts (Removal of Doubts) Act, 1948_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu Upon the expiry of the Act specified in the Schedule- (a) section 8 of the 1 [Tamil Nadu] General Clauses Act, 1891, shall apply, as if the Acts so specified had then been repealed by a 2 [Tamil Nadu] Act; (b) save as provided in clause (a), the Act, if any, amended by the Act so specified shall have operation as if the latter Acts had never been enacted. Substituted for Madras by Tamil Nadu A.O. 1969. Substituted for Madras by Tamil Nadu A.O. 1969.
