Indian Stamp (Tamil Nadu Third Amendment) Act, 2012_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Indian Stamp Act, 1899 in its application to the State of Tamil Nadu. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Indian Stamp (Tamil Nadu Third Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Indian Stamp (Tamil Nadu Third Amendment) Act, 2012. (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Tamil Nadu. (3) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint.
Indian Stamp (Tamil Nadu Third Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In Schedule I to the Indian Stamp Act, 1899, in Article 58, in clause (a), in the Explanation under sub-clause (i), for the expression "grandchild", the expression "grandchild, brother or sister" shall be substituted. (By order of the Governor) (G. Jayachandran) Secretary to Government, Law Department.
