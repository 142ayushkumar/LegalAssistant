Tamil Nadu Agricultural Service Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Repeal Act, 2018_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to repeal the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Service Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Act, 1986. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Agricultural Service Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Repeal Act, 2018_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Service Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Repeal Act, 2018. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Tamil Nadu Agricultural Service Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Repeal Act, 2018_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu The Tamil Nadu Agricultural Service Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Act, 1986, is hereby repealed. (By order of the Governor) (S. S. Poovalingam) Secretary to Government, Law Department.
