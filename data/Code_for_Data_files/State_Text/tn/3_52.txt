Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Second Amendment Act, 1998_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Act, 1916. Bb it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Forty- ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Second Amendment Act, 1998_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Second Amendment Act, 1998. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 29th day of October 1998.
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Second Amendment Act, 1998_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 4 of the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Act, 1976 (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act), in sub-section (1), for the words "twenty-two years and five months", the words "twenty-two years and eight months" shall be substituted.
Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special Officers) Second Amendment Act, 1998_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) The Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies (Appointment of Special^ Officers) Second Amendment Ordinance, 1998, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act, as amended by this Act. (By Order of the Governor) A.K. Rajan Secretary to Government. Law Department.
