Madras Decentralisation Act, 1914_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to facilitate the administration of certain enactments. WHEREAS it is expedient to facilitate the administration of certain enactments; It is hereby enacted as follows:-
Madras Decentralisation Act, 1914_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu This Act may be called the Madras Decentralisation Act, 1914.
Madras Decentralisation Act, 1914_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu The enactments specified in the third column of the Schedule are hereby amended to the extent and in the manner specified in the fourth column thereof.
Madras Decentralisation Act, 1914_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu Any appointment, notification, order, scheme, rule, form or by-law made or issued by an authority for the making or issuing of which a new authority is substituted by or under this Act shall, unless inconsistent with this Act, be deemed to have been made or issued by such new authority unless and until superseded by an appointment, notification, order, scheme, rule, form or by-law made or issued by such new authority.
