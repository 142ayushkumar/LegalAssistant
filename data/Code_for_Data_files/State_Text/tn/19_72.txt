Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning (Amendment) Act, 2014_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act, 1971. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning (Amendment) Act, 2014_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning (Amendment) Act, 2014. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning (Amendment) Act, 2014_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 9-A of the Tamil Nadu Town and Country Planning Act, 1971, in sub-section (2),- (a) after clause (c), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(cc) the senior most Chief Planner in the Chennai Metropolitan Development Authority;"; (b) after clause (h), the following clause shall be added, namely:- "(i) a Professor in Town Planning in the School of Architecture and Planning, to be nominated by the Anna University.". (By Order of the Governor) (G. Jayachandran) Secretary to Government, Law Department.
