Tamil Nadu Legislature (Prevention of Disqualification) Act, 1967_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to declare that certain offices of profit under the Government shall not disqualify the holders thereof for being chosen as, or for being, members of the State Legislature. Tamil Nadu Legislature (Prevention of Disqualification) Act, 1967_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the 1 Tamil Nadu Legislature (Prevention of Disqualification) Act, 1967. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st day of April, 1964. Substituted by the Tamil Nadu Adaptation of Laws Order, 1969.
Tamil Nadu Legislature (Prevention of Disqualification) Act, 1967_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu It is hereby declared that none of the offices specified in the Schedule shall disqualify, or shall be deemed ever to have disqualified, the holder thereof for being chosen as, or for being, a member of the Legislative Assembly. 1 Consequent on the abolition of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Council by the Tamil Nadu Legislative Council (Abolition) Act, 1986 (Central Act 40 of 1986), the term "or of the Legislative Council" occurred in Section 2 of the Tamil Nadu Legislature (Prevention of Disqualification) Act, 1967, omitted by the Tamil Nadu Adaptation of Laws Order, 1987 (Tamil Nadu Government Gazette No. 654, Part IV, Section 2, dt. 28.10.1987).
