Tamil Nadu National Law School (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to amend the Tamil Nadu National Law School Act, 2012. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu National Law School (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu National Law School (Amendment) Act, 2012. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 31st day of July 2012.
Tamil Nadu National Law School (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 11 of the Tamil Nadu National Law School Act, 2012, in the third proviso to sub-section (3), for the expression "sixty-five years", the expression "seventy years" shall be substituted.
Tamil Nadu National Law School (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu The Tamil Nadu National Law School (Amendment) Ordinance, 2012 is hereby repealed. (By order of the Governor) (G. Jayachandran) Secretary to Government, Law Department.
