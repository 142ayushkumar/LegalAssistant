Tamil Nadu Tax on Luxuries (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Tax on Luxuries Act, 1981. BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Fifty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Tamil Nadu Tax on Luxuries (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Tax on Luxuries (Amendment) Act, 2000. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st day of April, 2000.
