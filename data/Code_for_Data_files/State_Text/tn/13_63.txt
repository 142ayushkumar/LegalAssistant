Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles Taxation Act, 1974. BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Motor Vehicles Taxation (Amendment) Act, 2010. (2) It shall come into force on the 1st day of June 2010.
