Tamil Nadu District Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu 1 An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920. BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:- Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Extraordinary, Issue No. 307, Part IV-Section 2, Chennai, Wednesday, September 7, 2011, Aavani21, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042. The following Act of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly received the assent of the Governor on the 6th September 2011 and is hereby published for general information:- Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, Extraordinary, Issue No. 307, Part IV-Section 2, Chennai, Wednesday, September 7, 2011, Aavani21, Thiruvalluvar Aandu-2042. The following Act of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly received the assent of the Governor on the 6th September 2011 and is hereby published for general information:-
Tamil Nadu District Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 2011. (2) It shall come into force at once.
Tamil Nadu District Municipalities (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 7 of the Tamil Nadu District Municipalities Act, 1920 (Tamil Nadu Act V of 1920), in the first proviso to sub-section (1), for the word "twenty", the word "fifteen" shall be substituted. (By order of the Governor) G. JAYACHANDRAN, Secretary to Government, Law Department.
