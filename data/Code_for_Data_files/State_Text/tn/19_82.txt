Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University (Amendment) Act, 2014_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University Act, 1989. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University (Amendment) Act, 2014_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University (Amendment) Act, 2014. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint.
Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University (Amendment) Act, 2014_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 11 of the Tamil Nadu Veterinary and Animal Sciences University Act, 1989,- (1) to sub-section (1), the following proviso shall be added, namely:- "Provided that if the Chancellor does not approve any of the persons in the panel so recommended by the Committee, he may take steps to constitute another Committee, in accordance with sub-section (2), to give a fresh panel of three different names and shall appoint one of the persons named in the fresh panel as the Vice-Chancellor."; (2) in sub-section (2), for the expression "one shall be nominated by the Board", the expression "one shall be nominated by the Government" shall be substituted. (By order of the Governor) (G. Jayachandran) Secretary to Government, Law Department.
