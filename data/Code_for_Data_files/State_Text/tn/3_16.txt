Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu 1 2 An Act further to amend the Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage Act, 1978. BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:- L. A. Bill No. 4 of 2012, published in T. N. Govt. Gazette Extra., dated 3.02.2012 Issue No.31 pp. 15-16. The following Act of the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly received the assent of the Governor on the 23rd February 2012 and is hereby published for general information:-
Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage (Amendment) Act, 2012. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 25th day of October 2011.
Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu To section 87 of the Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage Act, 1978 (Tamil Nadu Act 28 of 1978), the following proviso shall be added, namely:- "Provided that all taxes, fees and duties, in respect of water supply and sewerage services as levied by the local authority concerned before the date of the such extension, shall be deemed to have been levied by the Board under the provisions of this Act and shall continue to be in force accordingly, until such taxes, fees and duties are revised, cancelled or superseded by anything done or any action taken under this Act ".
Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) The Chennai Metropolitan Water Supply and Sewerage (Amendment) Ordinance, 2012 is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act, as amended by this Act. (By order of the Governor) G. Jayachandran, Secretary to Government, Law Department.
