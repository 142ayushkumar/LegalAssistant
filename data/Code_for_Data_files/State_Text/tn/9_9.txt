Tamil Nadu Indebted Agriculturists (Temporary Relief) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act to provide temporary relief to indebted agriculturists in the State of Tamil Nadu. In exercise of the powers conferred by Section 3 of the Tamil Nadu State Legislature (Delegation of Powers), Act, 1976 (41 of 1976), the President is pleased to enact as follows:
Tamil Nadu Indebted Agriculturists (Temporary Relief) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Indebted Agriculturists (Temporary Relief) Act, 1976. (2) It extends to the whole of the State of Tamil Nadu. (3) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 15th day of January, 1976.
Tamil Nadu Indebted Agriculturists (Temporary Relief) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In this Act, unless the context otherwise required- (a) "agricultural land" means land used for agriculture or horticulture, not being land appurtenant to a residential building; (b) "agriculturist" means a person who owns an interest in agricultural land, and who, by reason of such interest, is in possession of such land or is in receipt of the rents or profits thereof and shall include a lessee; but shall not include: (i) a firm registered under the Indian Partnership Act, 1932 (9 of 1932), or a company as defined in the Companies Act, 1956 (1 of 1956), or a corporation formed in pursuance of an Act of Parliament of the United Kingdom or of any special Indian Law, or (ii) any person was assessed to income-tax under the Income-tax Act, 1961 (43 of 1961), or to agricultural income-tax under the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Income-Tax Act, 1955 (5 of 1955) or to sales - tax under the Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959 (1 of 1959), or under the Central Sales Tax Act, 1959 (74 of 1956), in any of the year 1971-72, 1972-73 and 1974-75. Explanation.- Where a joint Hindu Family or a tarward, tavazhi, Kuttumba or kavaru is an agriculturist, every co parcener or member of the tarward, tavazhi, kutumba or kavaru, as the case may be, shall be deemed to be an agriculturist, provided that he has not been assessed to income-tax or agricultural income-tax or sales-tax in any of the years 1971-72, 1972-73, 1973-74 and 1974-75; (c) "debt" (i) means any sum of money which a person is liable to pay under a contract (express or implied) for, consideration received, and (ii) includes rent in cash or kind which a person is liable to pay or deliver in respect of the lawful use and occupation of agricultural land. Explanation I.- For the purpose of this clause, "rent" in relation to agricultural land, shall mean rent accrued due for the fasli year ending with the 30th day of June, 1974 and for any previous fasli year. Explanation II.- It is immaterial that the sum or produce irrecoverable only by sale of property in enforcement of a mortgage or change or that the contract was entered into by persons predecessor-in-title or by the manager of the joint Hindu family or the karanavan of the tarward or tavazhi or the yajaman of the kutumba or kavaru of which such person was or is a member. Exception I.- "Debt" does not include. (i) rent or compensation for the use and occupation of house property; (ii) rent or compensation for the use and occupation of immovable property (not being house property or agricultural land) accrued due on or after the 16th day of January, 1975; (iii) any liability arising out of a breach of trust; (iv) any liability in respect of maintenance; (v) any sum payable to the State Government or the Central Government or to any local authority whether by way of revenue, tax, cess or loan or otherwise; (vi) any sum payable to any co-operative society, including a land development bank, registered or deemed to be registered under the Tamil Nadu Co-operative Societies Act, 1961 (53 of 1961), provided that the right of the society to recover the sum did not arise by reason of an assignment made subsequent to the 15th day of September, 1974; (vii) wages or remuneration due as salary, or otherwise for service rendered; (viii) any liability in respect of any sum due to; (ix) (As per Act 46 of 1976 item IX was inserted) any liability incurred or arising under any chit, the bye-laws of which have been registered under the Tamil Nadu Chit Funds Act 1961; (x) (As per Act 27 of 1978 item X was inserted) any liability in respect of any sum due to any company which is declared to be Nidhi or Mutual Benefit society under sub-section (1) of Section 620-A of Companies Act; (A) any banking company to which the Banking Regulation Act, 1949 (10 of 1949), applies; (B) the State Bank of India constituted under the State Bank of India Act, 1955 (22 of 1955); (C) any subsidiary bank as defined in clause (k) of Section 2 of the State Bank of India (Subsidiary Banks) Act, 1959 (38 of 1959); (D) any corresponding new bank as defined in clause (d) of Section 2 of the Banking Companies (Acquisition and Transfer of Undertakings) Act, 1970 (5 of 1970); (E) the Agricultural Refinance and Development Corporation, established under the Agricultural Refinance and Development Corporation Act, 1963 (10 of 1963); (F) Any other financial institution notified by the state Government in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette; (d) "pay" with its grammatical variation, includes deliver; (e) "suit" or application" does not include an appeal from a decree or order passed in a suit or application or an application for revision or review.
Tamil Nadu Indebted Agriculturists (Temporary Relief) Act, 1976_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu No suit for the recovery of a debt shall be instituted, no application for the execution of decree for payment of money passed in a suit for the recovery of a debt shall be made, and no suit or application for the eviction of a tenant on the ground of non-payment of a debt shall be instituted or made, against any agriculturist in any civil or revenue court on, and from the date of commencement of this Act and before the expiry of two years (As per Act 1 of 1977 the words "one year and six months" were substituted by "two years".) from the said date. Explanation I: Suit does not include a claim to a set-off made in a suit instituted by an agriculturist. Explanation II: Where a debt is payable by an agriculturist jointly or jointly and severally with a non-agriculturist, no suit or application of the nature mentioned in this Section shall be instituted or made either against the non agriculturist or against the agriculturist before the expiry of the period mentioned in this Section. Explanation III: A suit shall be deemed to be a suit for the recovery of a debt notwithstanding that other reliefs are prayed for in such suit, and a decree shall be deemed to be a decree for payment of money passed in such suit notwithstanding that other reliefs are granted by such decree; Provided that a suit for possession of land shall not be deemed to be a suit for recovery of a debt by reason merely of mesne profits being also prayed for in such suit.
Tamil Nadu Indebted Agriculturists (Temporary Relief) Act, 1976_Section 4--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) All further proceedings in suits and applications of the nature mentioned in Section 3 in which relief is claimed against an agriculturist, not being proceedings for the amendment of pleadings or for the addition, substitution, or the striking off of parties, but otherwise inclusive of proceedings consequent on orders or decrees made in appeals, revision petitions, or applications for review, shall subject to the next succeeding sub-section, stand stayed until the expiry of two years (As per Act 1 of 1875 the words "one year and six months" were substituted by "two years".) from the date of commencement of this Act; Provided that, in regard to property under attachment, the court may pass such orders as it deems necessary for the custody or preservation of the property or for the sale of such property if it is subject to speedy or natural decay, or if in respect of it, expenses of custody or preservation are considered excessive. (2) On application made by the defendant or the respondent or by all the defendants or all the respondents, as the case may be, the stay effected by sub-section (1) in a suit or application shall be dissolved and the suit or application shall be proceeded with from the stage which had been reached when further proceedings in the suit or the application were stayed.
Tamil Nadu Indebted Agriculturists (Temporary Relief) Act, 1976_Section 5--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) In computing the period of limitation or limit of time prescribed for a suit for the recovery of a debt or an application for the execution of a decree passed in such suit, the time during which the institution of, the suit or the making of application was barred by Section 3 or during which the plaintiff or his predecessor-in-title, believing in good faith that Section 3 applied to such suit or such application, refrained from instituting the suit or making the application, shall be excluded. Explanation: "Good Faith" shall have the meaning assigned to it in clause (22) or Section 3 of the General Clauses Act, 1897 (10 of 1897). (2) Where in a suit or an application in which the question of exclusion of time under sub-section (1) arises, the defendant or the respondent, or one of the defendants or respondents, with respect to whom the question is raised, would have been an agriculturist but for the fact that in the year 1971-72, 1972-73, 1973-74 or 1974-75 he had been assessed to income-tax under the Income tax under the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Income-Tax Act, 1955 (5 of 1955), or sales tax under the Tamil Nadu General Sales Tax Act, 1959, (1 of 1959), or under the Central Sales Tax Act, 1956 (74 of 1956), it shall be conclusively presumed that, in refraining from instituting the suit or making the application, the creditor believed in good faith that such defendant or respondent was an agriculturist.
Tamil Nadu Indebted Agriculturists (Temporary Relief) Act, 1976_Section 6--> State(s): Tamil Nadu Every transfer of immovable property by a debtor entitled to the benefit of Section 3 or Section 4, made on or after the date of commencement of this Act and before the expiry of two years (As per Act I of 1975 the words "one year and six months" were substituted by "Two years") from the said date shall, in any suit or other proceedings, with respect lo such transfer, be presumed, until the contrary is proved, to have been made with intent to defeat or delay the creditors of the transferror.
Tamil Nadu Indebted Agriculturists (Temporary Relief) Act, 1976_Section 7--> State(s): Tamil Nadu The provisions of this Act shall have effect notwithstanding anything inconsistent there with contained in the Tamil Nadu Cultivating Tenants Protection Act, 1955 (25 of 1955), the Tamil Nadu Public Trusts (Regulation of Administration of Agricultural Lands) Act, 1961 (57 of 1962), the Malabar Tenancy Act, 1929 (Tamil Nadu Act 14 of 1930), the Tamil Nadu Cultivating Tenants (Special Provisions) Act, 1968 (16 of 1968), the Tamil Nadu Cultivating Tenants Arrears of Rent (Relief) Act, 1972 (21 of 1972), or in the Code of Civil Procedure, 1908 (5 of 1908) or in any other law for the time being in force, or any custom, usage or contract, or decree or order of a court of other authority.
Tamil Nadu Indebted Agriculturists (Temporary Relief) Act, 1976_Section 8--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) The State Government may make rules to carry out the purposes of this Act. (2) All rules made under this Act shall be published in the Tamil Nadu Government Gazette, and unless they are expressed to come into force on a particular day, shall come into force on the day on which they are so published. (3) Every rule made under this Act shall, as soon as possible, after it is made, be placed on the table of both Houses of the Legislature and if, before the expiry of the session in which it is so placed or the next session, both Houses agree in making any modification in any such rule or both Houses agree that the rule should not be made, the rule shall thereafter have effect only in such modified form or be of no effect, as the case may be; so however, that any such modification or annulment shall be without prejudice to, the validity of anything previously done under that rule.
Tamil Nadu Indebted Agriculturists (Temporary Relief) Act, 1976_Section 9--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) The Tamil Nadu Indebted Agriculturists (Temporary Relief) No.2 Ordinance, 1976 (7 of 1976) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the corresponding provision of this Act.
