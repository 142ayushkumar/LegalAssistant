Tamil Nadu Agricultural Labourers-Farmers (Social Security and Welfare) Repeal Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu 1 An Act to repeal the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Labourers-Farmers (Social Security and Welfare) Act, 2006. BE it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:- L. A. Bill No 14 of 2011, published in T. N. Govt. Gazette, Extra., dated 8.2011, Issue No. 268.
Tamil Nadu Agricultural Labourers-Farmers (Social Security and Welfare) Repeal Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Labourers-Farmers (Social Security and Welfare) Repeal Act, 2011. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint.
Tamil Nadu Agricultural Labourers-Farmers (Social Security and Welfare) Repeal Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu The Tamil Nadu Agricultural Labourers-Farmers (Social Security and Welfare) Act, 2006 (Tamil Nadu Act 29 of 2006) is hereby repealed.
Tamil Nadu Agricultural Labourers-Farmers (Social Security and Welfare) Repeal Act, 2011_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu The sum at the credit of the Tamil Nadu Agricultural Labourers- Farmers Social Security and Welfare Fund as on the date of commencement of this Act shall be transferred to such other Fund or account as may be specified by the Government (By order of the Governor) G. JAYACHANDRAN, Secretary to Government, Law Department.
