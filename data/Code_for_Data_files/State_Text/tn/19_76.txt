Tamil Nadu Universities Laws (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section Preamble--> State(s): Tamil Nadu An Act further to amend the Tamil Nadu Universities Laws. Be it enacted by the Legislative Assembly of the State of Tamil Nadu in the Sixty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Tamil Nadu Universities Laws (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 1--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) This Act may be called the Tamil Nadu Universities Laws (Amendment) Act, 2012. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 14th day of July 2012.
Tamil Nadu Universities Laws (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 2--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 12 of the Bharathiar University Act, 1981, in the third proviso to sub-section (3), for the expression "sixty-five years", the expression "seventy years" shall be substituted.
Tamil Nadu Universities Laws (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 3--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 12 of the Bharathidasan University Act, 1981, in the third proviso to sub-section (3), for the expression "sixty-five years", the expression "seventy years" shall be substituted.
Tamil Nadu Universities Laws (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 4--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 12 of the Mother Teresa Women's University Act, 1984, in the third proviso to sub-section (3), for the expression "sixty-five years", the expression "seventy years" shall be substituted.
Tamil Nadu Universities Laws (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 5--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 13 of the Alagappa University Act, 1985, in the third proviso to sub-section (3), for the expression "sixty-five years", the expression "seventy years" shall be substituted.
Tamil Nadu Universities Laws (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 6--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 11 of the Manonmaniam Sundaranar University Act, 1990, in the third proviso to sub-section (3), for the expression "sixty-five years", the expression "seventy years" shall be substituted.
Tamil Nadu Universities Laws (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 7--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 12 of the Periyar University Act, 1997, in the third proviso to sub-section (3), for the expression "sixty-five years", the expression "seventy years" shall be substituted.
Tamil Nadu Universities Laws (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 8--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 10 of the Tamil Nadu Open University Act, 2002, in the second proviso to sub-section (3), for the expression "sixty-five years", the expression "seventy years" shall be substituted.
Tamil Nadu Universities Laws (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 9--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 12 of the Thiruvalluvar University Act, 2002, in the second proviso to sub-section (3), for the expression "sixty-five years", the expression "seventy years" shall be substituted.
Tamil Nadu Universities Laws (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 10--> State(s): Tamil Nadu In section 10 of the Tamil Nadu Teachers Education University Act, 2008, in the second proviso to sub-section (4), for the expression "sixty-five years", the expression "seventy years" shall be substituted.
Tamil Nadu Universities Laws (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 11--> State(s): Tamil Nadu (1) The Tamil Nadu Universities Laws (Amendment) Ordinance, 2012 is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the Bharathiar University Act, 1981 or the Bharathidasan University Act, 1981 or the Mother Teresa Women's University Act, 1984 or the Alagappa University Act, 1985 or the Manonmaniam Sundaranar University Act, 1990 or the Periyar University Act, 1997 or the Tamil Nadu Open University Act, 2002 or the Thiruvalluvar University Act, 2002 or the Tamil Nadu Teachers Education University Act, 2008, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the respective Acts, as amended by this Act. (By order of the Governor) (G. Jayachandran) Secretary to Government, Law Department.
