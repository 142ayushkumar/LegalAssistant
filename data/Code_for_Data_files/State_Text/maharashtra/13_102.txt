Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships Act, 1965. WHEREAS the Governor of Maharashtra had promulgated the Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships (Amendment) Ordinance, 2004, on the 28th January 2004; AND WHEREAS upon the re-assembly of the State Legislature on the 15th March 2004, the Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships (Amendment) Bill, 2004 (L.C. Bill No. I of 2004), for replacing the said Ordinance by an Act of the State Legislature was introduced in the Maharashtra Legislative Council on the 17th March 2004, however, the said Bill could not be passed as the said session of the State Legislature was prorogued on the same day; AND WHEREAS it was considered expedient to continue the operation of the provisions of the said Ordinance and therefore the Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships (Amendment and Continuance) Ordinance, 2004 (Mah. Ord. No. IX of 2004) was promulgated by the Governor of Maharashtra on the 12th May 2004; AND WHEREAS upon the re-assembly of the State Legislature on the 24th May 2004, the said Maharashtra Ordinance No. IX of 2004 was laid before both Houses of the State Legislature and the Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships (Amendment and Continuance) Bill, 2004 (L. C. Bill No. VIII of 2004), for replacing the said Ordinance by an Act of the State Legislature was also introduced in the Maharashtra Legislative Council on the 27th May 2004, and was passed by the Legislative Council on the 9th June 2004. The said Bill, however, could not be passed by the Legislative Assembly and the session of the State Legislature was prorogued on the 11th June 2004; AND WHEREAS it was considered expedient to continue the operation of the provisions of the said Ordinance and therefore, the Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships (Amendment and Continuance) Ordinance, 2004 was promulgated by the Governor of Maharashtra on thel6th July 2004. AND WHEREAS it is expedient to replace the said Ordinance by an Act of the State Legislature; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships (Amendment) Act, 2004. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 28th January 2004.
Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 55 of the Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships Act, 1965 (hereinafter referred to as " the principal Act"), in the proviso to sub-section (1), for the words " two years " the words " three years " shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) The Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships (Amendment and Continuance) Ordinance, 2004 is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
