Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Universities Act. 1994. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Universities Act, 1994, for the purposes hereinafter appearing ; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Act, 2005.
Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In the Schedule to the Maharashtra Universities Act, 1994, in Part I,- (a) in entry 5, in column (2), for the words " The Nagpur University ", the words " The Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University " shall be substituted ; (b) in entry 7, in column (2), for the words " The Amravati University ", the words " The Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University " shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra On the commencement of this Act, any reference to " the Nagpur University " or to " the Amravati University ", as the case may be, in any other enactment, or rules, regulations, bye-laws, notifications or orders issued under any enactment or in any instrument, document or proceedings under its original name shall, unless the context otherwise requires, be deemed and construed to be the reference to " the Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University " or, as the case may be, " the Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University ".
