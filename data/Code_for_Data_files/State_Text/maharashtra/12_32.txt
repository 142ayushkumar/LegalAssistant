Maharashtra Legislature Members (Removal of Disqualifications) (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Legislature Members (Removal of Disqualifications) Act. WHEREAS both Houses of the State Legislature were not in session; AND WHEREAS the Governor of Maharashtra was satisfied that circumstances existed which rendered it necessary for him to take immediate action further to amend the Maharashtra Legislature Members (Removal of Disqualifications) Act, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; and, therefore, promulgated the Maharashtra Legislature Members (Removal of Disqualifications) (Amendment) Ordinance, 2001, on the 10th February, 2001; AND WHEREAS it is expedient to replace the said Ordinance by an Act of the State Legislature; It is hereby enacted in the Fifty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Legislature Members (Removal of Disqualifications) (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Maharashtra Legislature Members (Removal of Disqualifications) (Amendment) Act, 2001. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 15th December, 1999.
Maharashtra Legislature Members (Removal of Disqualifications) (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra A person shall not be disqualified or shall be deemed never to have been disqualified for being chosen as, or for being, a member of the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly or the Maharashtra Legislative Council, merely by the reason of the fact that he holds the office of the Chairman of the Vidarbha Development Board or the Marathwada Development Board or the Development Board for the rest of Maharashtra, established by the Governor of Maharashtra under the Development Boards for Vidarbha, Marathwada and the rest of Maharashtra Order, 1994, issued in pursuance of the State of Maharashtra (Special Responsibility of Governor for Vidarbha, Marathwada and the rest of Maharashtra) Order, 1994 (issued by the President of India in exercise of the powers conferred by clause (2) of article 371 of the Constitution of India); and accordingly in Schedule I to the Maharashtra Legislature Members (Removal of Disqualifications) Act, after entry 21. the following entry shall be added, namely :- "22. The offices of the Chairmen of the Vidarbha Development Board, the Marathwada Development Board and the Development Board for the rest of Maharashtra, established by the Governor of Maharashtra under the Development Boards for Vidarbha, Marathwada and the rest of Maharashtra Order, 1994, issued in pursuance of the State of Maharashtra (Special Responsibility of Governor for Vidarbha, Marathwada and the rest of Maharashtra) Order, 1994."
Maharashtra Legislature Members (Removal of Disqualifications) (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra For the removal of doubt it is hereby declared that the offices of the Chairmen of the Vidarbha Development Board, the Marathwada Development Board and the Development Board for the rest of Maharashtra, insofar as, these are offices of profit under the State Government, shall not disqualify or shall be deemed never to have disqualified the holders thereof for being the members of the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly or the Maharashtra Legislative Council notwithstanding the fact that they have availed themselves of the facilities provided by the State Government, such as honorarium, rent free accommodation, telephones at residence and office, secretarial assistance. Government vehicle and travelling and daily allowances for the journeys undertaken for official purposes or official tours.
Maharashtra Legislature Members (Removal of Disqualifications) (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) The Maharashtra Legislature Members (Removal of Disqualifications) (Amendment) Ordinance, 2001, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the Maharashtra Legislature Members (Removal of Disqualifications) Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken, as the case may be, under the corresponding provisions of the said Act, as amended by this Act.
