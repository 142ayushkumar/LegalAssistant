City of Bombay Municipal Investment Act, 1898_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act to supplement the provisions of the City of Bombay Municipal Act, 1888, with respect to the Investment of Sinking Funds and Surplus Moneys, and to validate certain Debentures. WHEREAS it is expedient to supplement the provisions of the City of Bombay Municipal Act, 1888, with respect to the investment in public securities of sinking funds and surplus moneys of the Municipal Corporation of the City of Bombay; and whereas it is also expedient to remove doubts which have arisen with respect to the validity of certain debentures of the Corporation in which portions of their sinking funds and surplus moneys purport to have been heretofore invested, and to obviate the extinction of such debentures of the Corporation as have been, or may hereafter be, issued in or transferred to the name of the Corporation or to the name of the Municipal Commissioner for the City of Bombay on behalf of the Corporation in respect of any such investments; It is hereby enacted as follows:-
City of Bombay Municipal Investment Act, 1898_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be cited as the City of Bombay Municipal Investment Act, 1898.
City of Bombay Municipal Investment Act, 1898_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra [Repealed by Bom. 13 of 1933, section 42 and Appendix.]
City of Bombay Municipal Investment Act, 1898_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra [Repealed by Bom. 13 of 1933, section 42 and Appendix.]
City of Bombay Municipal Investment Act, 1898_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra All the several debentures of the Corporation heretofore issued, transferred, assigned or endorsed in the name of the Corporation or in the name of the Municipal Commissioner on behalf of the Corporation as specified in Schedule A, and all debentures heretofore issued by way of renewal, consolidation or sub-division or any of the said debentures shall be deemed to be end to have always been valid and negotiable in all respects and in the same manner as if the same had been issued against moneys borrowed form the Secretary of State or any other person.
City of Bombay Municipal Investment Act, 1898_Section 5--> State(s): Maharashtra The signature of the person for the time being holding the office of the Municipal Commissioner for the City of Bombay to a transfer of any debenture standing in the name of the Corporation or of the Municipal Commissioner on behalf of the Corporation shall be valid and sufficient, notwithstanding that such person may not have held the said office at the time when such debenture was issued, transferred, assigned or endorsed to the name of the Corporation or the Municipal Commissioner as aforesaid.
