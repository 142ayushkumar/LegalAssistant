Bombay Shops and Establishments (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Bombay Shops and Establishments Act, 1948. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Bombay Shops and Establishments Act, 1948 (Bom. LXXIX of 1948), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Shops and Establishments (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Bombay Shops and Establishments (Amendment) Act, 2011.1 Received the assent of the President on 11th November, 2013.
Bombay Shops and Establishments (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In Section 7 of the Bombay Shops and Establishments Act, 1948 (Bom. LXXIX of 1948) (hereinafter, referred to as "the principal Act"),- (a) in sub-section (2-A), for the words "upto which it is granted" the words "for a period the day on which it is granted or renewed" the end of the year for of twelve months from shall be substituted ; (b) in sub-section (2-B), for the words "for a period of three years at a time, on payment of the fees for that period, so as to be valid upto the end of the third year from and including the year in which it is granted or renewed, as the case may be", the following shall be substituted, namely "for a period of thirty-six months at a time, on payment of the fees for that period, so as to be valid upto the end of thirty-six months from the date on which it is granted or renewed, as the case may be".
Bombay Shops and Establishments (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 11 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), in clause (a), for the words, figures and letters" closed later than 8.30 p.m." the words, figures and letters "closed later than 10.00 p.m." shall be substituted.
