Maharashtra Project Affected Persons Rehabilitation (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Project Affected Persons Rehabilitaiton Act, 1999. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Project Affected Persons Rehabilitalion Act, 1999 (Mah. XI of 2001), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Sixty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Project Affected Persons Rehabilitation (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Project Affected Persons Rehabilitation (Amendment) Act, 2013.1 Received the assent of the Governor on 6th May, 2013 and was published in the Maharashtra Govt. Gazette, Extra-ordinary, Part VIII, dated 6th May, 2013.
Maharashtra Project Affected Persons Rehabilitation (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 2 of Maharashtra Project Affected Persons Rehabilitation Act, 1999 (Mah. XI of 2001), in clause (2), in sub-clause (a), in the Explanation,- (i) after the words "every brother" the words "or sister" shall be inserted; (ii) for the words "son or sons of any deceased brother all together as one unit" the words "soon or sons or daughter or daughters of each deceased brother or deceased sister as one separate unit of such brother or sister" shall be substituted.
