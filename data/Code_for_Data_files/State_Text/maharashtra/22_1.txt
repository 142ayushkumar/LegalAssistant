Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act to amend the Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation Act, 2000. WHEREAS both Houses of the State Legislature were not in session; AND WHEREAS the Governor of Maharashtra was satisfied that circumstances existed which rendered it necessary for him to take immediate action to amend the Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation Act, 2000, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; and, therefore, promulgated the Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation (Amendment) Ordinance, 2001, on the 12th February, 2001; AND WHEREAS it is expedient to replace the said Ordinance by Act of the State Legislature; It is hereby enacted in the Fifty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2001. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 12th day of February, 2001.
Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 4 of the Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation Act, 2000 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), in clause (o), after the words "five members" the words "including one woman," shall be inserted. 3.Amendment of section 5 of Mah. III of 2001 - In section 5 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), - (a) for the words "consisting of the following members "the words "consisting of the Chairman of the Corporation as the Chairperson, the Vice-Chairman of the Corporation as the Vice-Chairperson and the following members" shall be substituted; (b) in clause (a), in the second column, for the word "Chairman" the words "Member-Secretary" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 18 of the principal Act, in clause (a), after the words "finance the schemes" the words and figures "including the schemes upto 100 hectares of Zilla Parishad;" and for the words "and works of saline and alkaline land" the words, "works of saline and alkaline land and construction of flood protection walls," shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 5--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) The Maharashtra Water Conservation Corporation (Amendment) Ordinance, 2001 is hereby repealed, (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken (including any notification or order issued) under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done, taken or issued, as the case may be, under the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
