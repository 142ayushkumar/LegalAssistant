Maharashtra Tax Laws (Levy and Amendment) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend certain tax laws in operation in the State of Maharashtra. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend certain tax laws in operation in the State of Maharashtra, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; It is hereby enacted in the Fifty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows :
Maharashtra Tax Laws (Levy and Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Maharashtra Tax Laws (Levy and Amendment) Act, 2000. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint and different dates may be appointed for different provisions.
