Maharashtra Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Bombay Sales Tax Act, 1959. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Bombay Sales Tax Act, 1959, for the purposes hereinafter appearing, It is hereby enacted in the Thirty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Maharashtra Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Maharashtra Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1982. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
Maharashtra Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 2 of the Bombay Sales Tax Act, 1959, (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), in clause (26), for the word and figures "and 13,", the figures, word and letter, "13 and 13B" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 13 of the principal Act, for the words, brackets and figures "sub-section (3) of section 7,", the words, brackets and figures "sub-section (2) of section 7," shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1982_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra After section 13A of the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely :- "13B. Purchase tax payable on specified goods- (1) Notwithstanding anything contained in this Act, but subject to the condition of previous publication, the State Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, specify the goods or classes of goods (hereinafter referred to in this section as "the specified goods") in respect of which purchase tax shall be leviable on the turnover of purchase of the specified goods effected by a dealer liable to pay tax under this Act at the rate of purchase tax leviable in respect of the specified goods under Schedule B or Schedule C, as the case may be, unless such purchased goods are resold by such dealer : Provided the, where the specified goods fall in any of the entries in Part II of Schedule C and are purchased by recognised dealer or a commission agent holding a permit and purchasing goods on behalf of a principal who is a recognised dealer and the specified goods so purchased are used, within the State, by such recognised dealer or such principal, in the manufacture of taxable goods for sale and the goods so manufactured are in fact sold, or in the packing of goods so manufactured, the rate of purchase tax shall be four paise in the rupee. (2) Where the specified goods are declared goods, the purchase tax leviable under sub-section (1) shall be subject to the provisions of sub-section (2) of section 7. (3) On issue of the notification under sub-section (1), nothing is section 7, 8, 13 or 13A shall apply to the sale or purchase, as the case may be, of the specified goods.".
