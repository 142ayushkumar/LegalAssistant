Bombay Village Panchayats (Second Amendment) Act, 2002_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Bombay Village Panchayats Act, 1958. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Bombay Village Panchayats Act, 1958 for the purpose hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Village Panchayats (Second Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Bombay Village Panchayats (Second Amendment) Act, 2002.
Bombay Village Panchayats (Second Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 3 of the Bombay Village Panchayats Act, 1958 , in clause (a-2), for the letters, figures and words "less than Rs. 5,000," the words "not exceeding rupees ten thousand includes a Gram Sabha and exceeding rupees ten thousand but less than rupees twenty-five thousand" shall be substituted.
