Bombay Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Bombay Sales Tax Act, 1959. WHEREAS both Houses of the State Legislature were not in session; AND WHEREAS the Governor of Maharashtra was satisfied that circumstances existed which rendered it necessary for him to take immediate action further to amend the Bombay Sales Tax Act, 1959, for the purposes hereinafter appearing ; and, therefore, promulgated the Bombay Sales Tax (Amendment) Ordinance, 2004, on the 29th June 2004 ; AND WHEREAS it is expedient to replace the said Ordinance by an Act of the State Legislature ; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Bombay Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Bombay Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2004. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 29th June 2004.
Bombay Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 40A of the Bombay Sales Tax Act, 1959 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), in sub-section (2), in the Explanation, in clause (b), for the words, brackets and letters "the maximum retail price (M. R. P.) of such tickets sold " the words " the selling price at which the lottery dealer has sold such tickets in the State" shall be substituted.
Bombay Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) The Bombay Sales Tax (Amendment) Ordinance, 2004, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken, as the case may be, under the provisions of the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
