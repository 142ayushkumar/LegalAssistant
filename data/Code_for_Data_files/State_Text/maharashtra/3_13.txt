Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2005.
Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 2 of the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), in clause (10), for the words "an Agriculture and Rural Development Bank" the words " a Co-operative Agriculture and Rural Multipurpose Development Bank " shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 44A of the principal Act, - (a) for the brackets and words " (including a Co-operative Bank but excluding an Agriculture and Rural Development Bank) " the brackets and words " (including a Co-operative Bank and a Co-operative Agriculture and Rural Multipurpose Development Bank)" shall be substituted; (b) the following proviso shall be added, namely:- "Provided that, nothing in this section shall apply to a loan exceeding one lakh rupees given by a Co-operative Agriculture and Rural Multipurpose Development Bank to any member.".
Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 48 of the principal Act,- (a) in clause (d), in the first proviso, for the words " an Agriculture and Rural Development Bank " the words " a Co-operative Agriculture and Rural Multipurpose Development Bank " shall be substituted; (b) in clause (f), for the words " Agriculture and Rural Development Bank " the words " Co-operative Agriculture and Rural Multipurpose Development Bank " shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 5--> State(s): Maharashtra . In section 48A of the principal Act,- (a) in sub-section (1), for the words " an Agriculture and Rural Development Bank " the words " a Co-operative Agriculture and Rural Multipurpose Development Bank " shall be substituted; (b) in sub-section (3), in the proviso, for the words " Agriculture and Rural Development Bank " the words " Co-operative Agriculture and Rural Multipurpose Development Bank " shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 6--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 73C of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), for the words " an Agriculture and Rural Development Bank or an Apex Agriculture and Rural Development Bank " the words " a District Cooperative Agriculture and Rural Multipurpose Development Bank or State Co-operative Agriculture and Rural Multipurpose Development Bank " shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 7--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 73G of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), for clause (iii), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:- " (iii) all District Co-operative Agriculture and Rural Multipurpose Development Banks; " .
Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 8--> State(s): Maharashtra Throughout Chapter XI of the principal Act, including the heading of that Chapter and marginal note to sections thereof, for the words " Agriculture and Rural Development Banks ", " an Agriculture and Rural Development Bank "," State Agriculture and Rural Development Bank "," Primary Agriculture and Rural Development Banks "," District Loan Committee " and " Development Bank " the words " Co-operative Agriculture and Rural Multipurpose Development Banks ", " a Co-operative Agriculture and Rural Multipurpose Development Bank ", " State Co-operative Agriculture and Rural Multipurpose Development Bank ", " District Co-operative Agriculture and Rural Multipurpose Development Bank ", " District Co-operative Agriculture and Rural Multipurpose Development Bank " and " Co-operative Agriculture and Rural Multipurpose Development Bank", respectively, shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 9--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 112 of the principal Act,- (a) in sub-section (2), after the words " Land Development Banks ", the words " or to an Agriculture and Rural Development Bank " shall be inserted; (b) in sub-section (3), after the words " Land Development Banks ", the words " or of the Agriculture and Rural Development Banks " shall be inserted.
Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 10--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 112A of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), in clause (b), for sub-clause (iv), the following sub-clause shall be substituted, namely:- " (iv) the Manager of the District Co-operative Agriculture and Rural Multipurpose Development Bank, ex-officio.".
