Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960. WHEREAS both Houses of the State Legislature were not in session; AND WHEREAS the Governor of Maharashtra was satisfied that circumstances existed which rendered it necessary for him to take immediate action further to amend the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; and, therefore, promulgated the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Ordinance, 2000, on the 14th August, 2000 and the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Ordinance, 2000, on the 13th November, 2000; AND WHEREAS it is expedient to replace the said Ordinances by an act of the State Legislature; It is hereby enacted in the Fifty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Act, 2000. (2) (a) Sections 2 and 4 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 14th August, 2000; and (b) section 3 shall be deemed to have come into force on the 13th November, 2000.
Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 73-ID of the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), in sub-section (1) for the words "simple majority" the words "two-thirds majority" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 73-G of the principal Act, to sub-section (2B), the following proviso shall be added, namely :- "Provided that where the Collector fails to hold election to the committee of any such society, the term of office of the members of the committee of that society shall be deemed to have been extended till the date immediately preceding the date of the first meeting of the newly constituted committee.".
Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 73-H of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), for the words "The committee" the words "Where there is a wilful failure on the part of the committee to hold the election to the committee before the expiration of its term, the committee" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 5--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) The Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Ordinance, 2000 and the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Second Amendment) Ordinance, 2000 are hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken or any order issued under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinances shall be deemed to have been done, taken or issued, as the case may be, under the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
