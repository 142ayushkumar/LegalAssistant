Bombay Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act,2001_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act to amend the Assam Taxation (On Luxuries) Act, 1997. WHEREAS it is expedient to amend the Assam Taxation (On Luxuries) Act, 1997, hereinafter referred to as the principal Act, in the manner hereafter appearing; It is hereby enacted in the Fifty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Bombay Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act,2001_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Bombay Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2000 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 14th November, 2000.

Bombay Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act,2001_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In Schedule 'C' appended to the principal At, in Part II, entry 151A shall be deleted.
Bombay Sales Tax (Second Amendment) Act,2001_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) The Bombay Sales Tax (Amendment) Ordinance, 2000, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding Such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken, as the case may be, under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
