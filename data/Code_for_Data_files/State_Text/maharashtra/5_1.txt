Maharashtra Education and Employment Guarantee (Cess) (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Education and Employment Guarantee (Cess) Act, 1962. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Education and Employment Guarantee (Cess) Act, 1962, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; It is hereby enacted in the Fifty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Education and Employment Guarantee (Cess) (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Education and Employment Guarantee (Cess) (Amendment) Act, 2005.
Maharashtra Education and Employment Guarantee (Cess) (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 7 of the Maharashtra Education and Employment Guarantee (Cess) Act, 1962, after clause (f), the following clause shall be inserted namely :- "(f-a) lands and buildings belonging to a Foreign Consulate in the State, on the principle of reciprocity;"
