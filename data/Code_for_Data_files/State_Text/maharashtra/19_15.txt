Maharashtra Tax Laws (Levy, Amendment and Validation) (Second Amendment and Continuance) Act, 1993_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Tax Laws (Levy, Amendment and Validation) Act, 1992 (See 1992 86 STC Statutes 1.) and to amend the Maharashtra Tax Laws (Levy, Amendment and Validation) (Amendment and Continuance) Act, 1993. (See 1993 90 STC Statutes 89.). WHEREAS both Houses of the State Legislature were not in session; AND WHEREAS the Governor of Maharashtra was satisfied that circumstances existed which rendered it necessary for him to take immediate action further to amend the Maharashtra Tax Laws (Levy, Amendment and Validation) Act, 1992 (See 1992 86 STC Statutes 1.) and the Maharashtra Tax Laws (Levy, Amendment and Validation) (Amendment and Continuance) Act, 1993 (See 1993 90 STC Statutes 89.), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; and, therefore, promulgated the Maharashtra Tax Laws (Levy, Amendment and Validation) (Second Amendment and Continuance) Ordinance, 1993 (See page 1 supra.), on the 30th June 1993. AND WHEREAS it is expedient to replace the said Ordinance by an Act of the State Legislature; It is hereby enacted in the Forty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Maharashtra Tax Laws (Levy, Amendment and Validation) (Second Amendment and Continuance) Act, 1993_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Maharashtra Tax Laws (Levy, Amendment and Validation) (Second Amendment and Continuance) Act, 1993. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 30th June, 1993.
Maharashtra Tax Laws (Levy, Amendment and Validation) (Second Amendment and Continuance) Act, 1993_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 1A of the Maharashtra Tax Laws (Levy, Amendment and Validation) Act, 1992 (See 1992 86 STC Statutes 1.), for the figures, letters and word "30th June 1993" the figures, letters and word "30th September 1993" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Tax Laws (Levy, Amendment and Validation) (Second Amendment and Continuance) Act, 1993_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 3 of the Maharashtra Tax Laws (Levy, Amendment and Validation) (Amendment and Continuance) Act, 1993 (See 1993 90 STC Statutes 89.) hereinafter referred to as "the Tax Laws (Amendment and Continuance) Act", for the figures, letters and word "30th June 1993" the figures, letters and word "30th September 1993" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Tax Laws (Levy, Amendment and Validation) (Second Amendment and Continuance) Act, 1993_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 4 of the Tax Laws (Amendment and Continuance) Act (See 1993 90 STC Statutes 89.), in clause (c), for the figures, letters and words "1st day of July 1993" the figures, letters and words "1st day of October 1993" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Tax Laws (Levy, Amendment and Validation) (Second Amendment and Continuance) Act, 1993_Section 5--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) The Maharashtra Tax Laws (Levy, Amendment and Validation) (Second Amendment and Continuance) Ordinance, 1993 (See page 1 supra.) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the Maharashtra Tax Laws (Levy, Amendment and Validation) Act, 1992 (See 1992 86 STC Statutes 1.) and the Tax Laws (Amendment and Continuance) Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken, as the case may be, under the said Acts as amended by this Act.
