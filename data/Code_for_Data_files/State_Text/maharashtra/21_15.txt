Maharashtra Village Panchayats (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Village Panchayats Act. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Village Panchayats Act, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Sixty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Village Panchayats (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Village Panchayats (Amendment) Act, 2015.
Maharashtra Village Panchayats (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 10-1A of the Maharashtra Village Panchayats Act (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), in the first proviso, for the words, figures and letters "before the 31st December 2013" the words, figures and letters "before the 31st December 2015" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Village Panchayats (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 30-1A of the principal Act, in the first proviso, for the words, figures and letters "before the 31st December 2013" the words, figures and letters "before the 31st December 2015" shall be substituted.
