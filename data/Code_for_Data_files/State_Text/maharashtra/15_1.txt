Maharashtra Official Languages (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Official Languages Act, 1964. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Official Languages Act, 1964, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Sixty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Official Languages (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Official Languages (Amendment) Act, 2015.
Maharashtra Official Languages (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra After section 1 of the Maharashtra Official Languages Act, 1964 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act") the following shall be inserted, namely:- "1A. Marathi to be the official language of State. - The official language of the State of Maharashtra shall be Marathi.".
Maharashtra Official Languages (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 2 of the principal Act, for clause (c), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:- "(c) "Marathi" means the Marathi language in Devnagari script which is adopted in the State under Government Resolutions issued, from time to time.".
