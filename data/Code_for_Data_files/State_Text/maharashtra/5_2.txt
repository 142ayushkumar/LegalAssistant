Maharashtra Education and Employment Guarantee (Cess) (Amendment) Act, 2006_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Education and Employment Guarantee (Cess) Act, 1962. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Education and Employment Guarantee (Cess) Act, 1962, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Education and Employment Guarantee (Cess) (Amendment) Act, 2006_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Maharashtra Education and Employment Guarantee (Cess) (Amendment) Act, 2006. (2) It shall come into force on the 1st August 2007.
Maharashtra Education and Employment Guarantee (Cess) (Amendment) Act, 2006_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In SCHEDULE B appended to the Maharashtra Education and Employment Guarantee (Cess) Act, 1962, entry 15 shall be deleted.
