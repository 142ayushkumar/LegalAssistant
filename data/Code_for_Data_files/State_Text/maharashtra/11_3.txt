Maharashtra Khadi and Village Industries (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Khadi and Village Industries Act. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Khadi and Village Industries Act (XIX of 1960), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Sixty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Khadi and Village Industries (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Khadi and Village Industries (Amendment) Act, 2013.1 Received the assent of the Governor on 21st August, 2013 and was published in the Maharashtra Govt. Gazette, Extra-ordinary, Part VIII, dated 21st August, 2013.
Maharashtra Khadi and Village Industries (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 4 of the Maharashtra Khadi and Village Industries Act (XIX of 1960), to sub-section (1), the following proviso shall be added, namely:- "Provided that, out of the members so appointed at least one member shall be from amongst the members of the Gram Sabha or the Panchayat in the Scheduled Areas referred to in clause (1) of article 244 of the Constitution of India. Explanation- For the purposes of this proviso, the expressions "the Gram Sabha" and "the Panchayat" shall have the same meaning respectively assigned to them in clause (b) and (d) of article 243 of the Constitution of India.".
