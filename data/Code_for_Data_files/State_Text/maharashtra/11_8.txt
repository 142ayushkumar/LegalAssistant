Maharashtra Krishna Valley and Konkan Irrigation Development Corporations (Amendment and Validation) (Continuance) Act, 2004_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation Act, 1996 and the Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation Act, 1997. WHEREAS the Governor of Maharashtra had, on the 5th April, 2004, promulgated the Maharashtra Krishna Valley and Konkan Irrigation Development Corporations (Amendment and Validation) Ordinance, 2004, with retrospective effect, that is with effect from the 27th November, 2003 ; AND WHEREAS upon the re-assembly of the State Legislature on the 24th May 2004, the said Ordinance was laid before both Houses of the State Legislature and the Maharashtra Krishna Valley and Konkan Irrigation Development Corporations (Amendment and Validation) Bill, 2004 (L. A. Bill No. IX of 2004), being a Bill for replacing the said Ordinance by an Act of the State Legislature was also introduced in the Legislative Assembly on the 28th May, 2004. The said Bill, however, could not be passed in the Legislative Assembly and the session of the State Lagislature was prorogued on the 11th June 2004; AND WHEREAS, as provided by Article 213 (2) (a) of the Constitution of India, the said Ordinance would have ceased to operate on the 4th July 2004, being the date on which the period of six weeks from the date of re-assembly of the State Legislature expire was to ; AND WHEREAS it was considered expedient to continue the operation of the provisions of the said Ordinance ; AND WHEREAS both Houses of the State Legislature were not in session; AND WHEREAS the Governor of Maharashtra was satisfied that circumstances existed which rendered it necessary for him to take immediate action to continue the operation of the provisions of the said Ordinance, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; and, therefore, promulgated the Maharashtra Krishna Valley and Konkan Irrigation Developmenmt Corporations (Amendment and Validation) (Continuance) Ordinance, 2004, on the 6th July 2004. AND WHEREAS it is expendient to replace the said Ordinance by an Act of the State Legislature; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Maharashtra Krishna Valley and Konkan Irrigation Development Corporations (Amendment and Validation) (Continuance) Act, 2004_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Maharashtra Krishna Valley and Konkan Irrigation Development Corporations (Amendment and Validation) (Continu ance) Act, 2004. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 27th November, 2003.
Maharashtra Krishna Valley and Konkan Irrigation Development Corporations (Amendment and Validation) (Continuance) Act, 2004_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 5 of the Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation Act, 1996 (hereinafter referred to as " The Krishna Valley Corporation Act"), in sub-section (1), after clause (c), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- " (d) Chief Engineer of the Corporation Member".
Maharashtra Krishna Valley and Konkan Irrigation Development Corporations (Amendment and Validation) (Continuance) Act, 2004_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 12 of the Krishna Valley Corporation Act, in sub-section (1), after the words, figures and brackets" Executive Director as provided in section 4 (1)," the words " Chief Engineer," shall be inserted.
Maharashtra Krishna Valley and Konkan Irrigation Development Corporations (Amendment and Validation) (Continuance) Act, 2004_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 4 of the Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation Act, 1997 (hereinafter referred to as" the Konkan Corporation Act"), in sub-section (1), for clause (m), the following clause shall be substituted, namely :- " (m) One officer not below the rank of Secretary to Government from the cadre of the Engineering Services of the Irrigation Department to be appointed by the State Government as the Member-Secretary of the Corporation, who shall be designated as Executive Director of the Corporation. The Executive Director shall be entitled to take part in all deliberations and proceedings of the meetings of the Corporation but shall have no right to vote.".

Maharashtra Krishna Valley and Konkan Irrigation Development Corporations (Amendment and Validation) (Continuance) Act, 2004_Section 9--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 65 of the Konkan Corporation Act, for the words " Chief Engineer" the words "Executive Director" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Krishna Valley and Konkan Irrigation Development Corporations (Amendment and Validation) (Continuance) Act, 2004_Section 10--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) For the removal of doubt it is hereby declared that, the amend ments to the Krishna Valley Corporation Act or, the Konkan Corporation Act, as the case may be, effected by the Maharashtra Krishna Valley and Konkan Irrigation Development Corporations (Amendment) Ordinance, 2003, shall be deemed never to have been carried out and shall be of no consequence or effect. (2) Notwithstanding anything contained in sub-section (7), any action taken or anything done by the Chief Engineer of the Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation (including any contract or any instrument executed), during the period commencing on the 27th November 2003 and ending on the 5th April 2004, being the date of publication of the Maharashtra Krishna Valley and Konkan Irrigation Development Corporations (Amendment and Validation) Ordinance, 2004, in the Official Gazette, shall be deemed to be valid and effective as if such action had been taken or done or executed, under the provisions of the Konkan Corporation Act, as amended by this Act, in accordance with law ; and no suit, appeal, application or other proceedings shall lie in any court or before any authority for declaring such action, thing or execution as invalid only on the gound that such action taken or thing done (including any contract or any instrument executed), was without authority of law.
Maharashtra Krishna Valley and Konkan Irrigation Development Corporations (Amendment and Validation) (Continuance) Act, 2004_Section 11--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) The Maharashtra Krishna Valley and Konkan Irrigation Development Corporations (Amendment and Validation) (Continuance) Ordinance, 2004 is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken (including any appointment made) under the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done, taken or made under the corresponding provisions of the Krishna Valley Corporation Act or, as the case may be, the Konkan Corporation Act, as amended by this Act.
Maharashtra Krishna Valley and Konkan Irrigation Development Corporations (Amendment and Validation) (Continuance) Act, 2004_Section 12--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) If any difficulty arises in giving effect to the provisions of the Krishna Valley Corporation Act, or, as the case may be, the Konkan Corporation Act, as amended by this Act, the State Government may, as occasion requires, by order published in the Official Gazette, do such act or thing, not in consistent with the objects and purposes of this Act, as may appear to be necessary or expedient for the purpose of removing the difficulty : Provided that, no such order shall be made afterthe expiry of a period of two years from the date of commencement of this Act. (2) Every order made under sub-section (1) shall be laid, as soon as may be, after it is made, before each House of the State Legislature.
