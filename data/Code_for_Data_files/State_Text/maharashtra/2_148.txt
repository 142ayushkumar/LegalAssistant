Bombay Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Bombay Sales Tax Act, 1959. WHEREAS both Houses of the State Legislature were not in session; AND WHEREAS the Governor of Maharashtra was satisfied that circumstances existed which rendered it necessary for him to take immediate action further to amend the Bombay Sales Tax Act, 1959, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; and, therefore, promulgated the Bombay Sales Tax (Amendment) Ordinance, 1999, on the 31st December, 1999 (See 2000 117 STC Statutes 158.); AND WHEREAS it is expedient to replace the said Ordinance by an Act of the State Legislature; It is hereby enacted in the Fifty-first Year or the Republic of India as follows :-
Bombay Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Bombay Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2000. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 1st January, 2000.

Bombay Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) The Bombay Sales Tax (Amendment) Ordinance, 1999, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken, as the case may be, under the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
