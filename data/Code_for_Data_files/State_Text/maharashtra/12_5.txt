Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966. WHEREAS the Governor of Maharashtra had promulgated the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Ordinance, 2004 on the 6th March 2004 ; AND WHEREAS upon the re-assembly of the State Legislature on the 15th March 2004, the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Ordinance, 2004 was laid before both Houses of the State Legislature and a Bill (L. A. Bill No. III of 2004) for converting the said Ordinance into an Act of the State Legislature was also introduced in the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly on the 17th March 2004, however, the said Bill could not be passed as the said Session of the State Legislature was prorogued on the same day ; AND WHEREAS it was considered expedient to continue the operation of the provisions of the said Ordinance and, therefore, the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment and Continuance) Ordinance, 2004 was promulgated by the Governor of Maharashtra on the 18th May 2004; AND WHEREAS upon the re-assembly of the State Legislature on the 24th May 2004, the said Ordinance was laid before both Houses of the State Legislature and the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment and Continuance) Bill, 2004 (L.A.Bill No. VII of 2004), for replacing the said Continuance Ordinance by an Act of the State Legislauture was also introduced in the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly on the 3rd June 2004. The said Bill could not be passed as the Session of the State Legislature was prorogued on the 11th June 2004; AND WHEREAS as provided by Article 2l3(2)(a) of the Constitution of India, the said Continuance Ordinance No. XII of 2004 had ceased to operate at the expiration of six weeks from the date of re-assembly of. the State Legislature, that is, after the 4th July 2004 ; AND WHEREAS it was considered expedient to continue the operation of the provisions of the said Continuance Ordinance ; AND WHEREAS both Houses of the State Legislature were not in Session and the Governor of Maharashtra was satisfied that circumstances existed which rendered it necessary for him to take immediate action to continue the operation of the provisions of the said Continuance Ordinance, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; and, therefore, promulgated the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment and Continuance) Ordinance, 2004. AND WHEREAS it is expedient to replace the said Continuance Ordinance No. XVII of 2004 by an Act of the State Legislature; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2004. (2) Save as otherwise provided in the Act, it shall be deemed to have come into force on the 6th March 2004.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 44A of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966 (hereinafter referred to as " the said Code"),- (a) in sub-section (1),- (i) after clause (ii), for the portion beginning with the words " for a bona-fide industrial use" and ending with the words " conditions, namely:-" the following shall be substituted, namely:- " for a bona-fide industrial use; or (iii) within the area undertaken by a private developer as a special township project, then, no permission for such conversion of use of land shall be required, subject to the following conditions, namely:-" ; (ii) in condition (b), after the words " bona-fide industrial use" the words " or special township project, as the case may be, " shall be inserted; (iii) in condition (d), after the word " industry " the words " or special township project, as the case may be," shall be inserted; (iv) after condition (e), the following proviso shall be added, namely :- " Provided that, the provisions of this sub-section shall not apply to the areas notified as the Eco-sensitive Zone, by the Government of India. "; (b) in sub-section (2), after the words bona-fide industrial use " the words " or special township project, as the case may be," shall be inserted ; (c) in sub-section (5), after the words "bona-fide industrial use" the words " or special township project, as the case may be," shall be inserted ; (d) in the Explanation, after the words " carried on by any person," the words " or the activity of tourism, within the area notified as the tourist place or hill station, by the State Government," shall be inserted and shall be deemed to have been inserted with effect from the 1st July 2000; (e) the existing Explanation shall be renumbered as Explanation I and after the Explanation-I so renumbered, the following Explanation shall be added, namely:- " Explanation-II.-For the purposes of this section, " special township project" means special township project or projects under the Regulations framed for Development of Special Township by the Government, under the provisions of the Maharashtra Regional and Town Planning Act, 1966.".
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 328 of the said Code, in sub-section (2), in clause (xvi-a), after the words " bona-fide industrial use" the words " or special township project" shall be inserted.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) The Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment and Continuance) Ordinance, 2004, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the said Code, as amended by the said Continuance Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the corresponding provisions of the said Code, as amended by this Act.
