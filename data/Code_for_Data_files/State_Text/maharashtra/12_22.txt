Maharashtra Land Revenue Code and the Maharashtra Restoration of Lands to Scheduled Tribes (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra 1 An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966 and the Maharashtra Restoration of Lands to Scheduled Tribes Act, 1974. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966 (Mah. XLI of 1966) and the Maharashtra Restoration of Lands to Scheduled Tribes Act, 1974 (Mah. XIV of 1975), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Sixty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:- The following Act of the Maharashtra Legislature, received the assent of the President on 30th December, 2011 and was published in the Maharashtra Govt. Gazette, Extra-ordinary, Part VIII, dated 30th December, 2011.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code and the Maharashtra Restoration of Lands to Scheduled Tribes (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code and the Maharashtra Restoration of Lands to Scheduled Tribes (Amendment) Act, 2010. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from the 6th July 2004.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code and the Maharashtra Restoration of Lands to Scheduled Tribes (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 36 of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966 (Mah. XLI of 1966) (hereinafter referred to as "the said Code "), in sub-section (3),- (a) for the words "within thirty years of such transfer of possession" the words, figures and letters "within thirty years from the 6th July 2004" shall be substituted; (b) in the first proviso, for the words "within thirty years of such commencement" the words, figures and letters "within thirty years from the 6th July, 2004" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code and the Maharashtra Restoration of Lands to Scheduled Tribes (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 36A of the said Code, in sub-section (4), for the words "within thirty years from the date of the transfer of occupancy" the words, figures and letters" within thirty years from the 6th July, 2004" shall be substituted.

XIV of 1975) (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), in sub-section (!), for the words "within thirty years from the commencement of this Act" the words, figures and letters "within thirty years from the 6th July 2004" shall be substituted.
