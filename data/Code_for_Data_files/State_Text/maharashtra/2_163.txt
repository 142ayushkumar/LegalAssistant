Bombay Shops and Establishments (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Bombay Shops and Establishments Act, 1948. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Bombay Shops and Establishments Act, 1948, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Shops and Establishments (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Bombay Shops and Establishments (Amendment) Act, 2004.
Bombay Shops and Establishments (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 13 of the Bombay Shops and Establishments Act, 1948 (hereinafter referred to as " the principal Act"), in sub-section (1), for the words, figures and letters " later than 8-30 p.m.", the words, figures and letters " later than 9-30 p.m.", shall be substituted.
Bombay Shops and Establishments (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 33 of the principal Act,- (a) sub-section (1) shall be deleted; (b) in sub-section (3), for the word, figures and letters " after 8-30 p.m. ", the words, figures and letters " after 9-30 p.m." shall be substituted.
Bombay Shops and Establishments (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 52 of the principal Act,- (a) for the words " fifty rupees " the words " one thousand rupees " shall be substituted; (b) for the words " five hundred rupees " the words " five thousand rupees " shall be substituted; (c) in the proviso, for the words " twenty rupees " the words " one hundred rupees " shall be substituted.
Bombay Shops and Establishments (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 5--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 53 of the principal Act,- (a) for the words " fifty rupees " the words " one thousand rupees " shall be substituted; (b) for the words "one thousand rupees" the words "five thousand rupees " shall be substituted.
Bombay Shops and Establishments (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 6--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 54 of the principal Act,- (a) for the words " fifty rupees " the words " five hundred rupees " shall be substituted; (b) for the words "one thousand rupees" the words "five thousand rupees " shall be substituted.
Bombay Shops and Establishments (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 7--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 55 of the principal Act,- (a) for the words " one hundred rupees " the words " one thousand rupees " shall be substituted; (b) for the words " five hundred rupees " the words " five thousand rupees" shall be substituted; (c) in the proviso, for the words " five hundred rupees " the words " five thousand rupees " shall be substituted.
Bombay Shops and Establishments (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 8--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 56 of the principal Act,- (a) (i) for the words " one hundred rupees " the words " one thousand rupees " shall be substituted; (ii) for the words "one thousand rupees" the words "five thousand rupees " shall be substituted; (iii) for the words "one hundred and fifty rupees" the words " seven thousand and five hundred rupees " shall be substituted; (iv) for the words " one thousand and five hundred rupees " the words " ten thousand rupees " shall be substituted; (b) in the first proviso,- (i) for the words " one thousand rupees" the words " ten thousand rupees " shall be substituted; (ii) for the words " one thousand and five hundred rupees " the words " fifteen thousand rupees " shall be substituted.
Bombay Shops and Establishments (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 9--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 57 of the principal Act,- (a) for the words " fifty rupees " the words " one thousand rupees " shall be substituted; (b) for the words " five hundred rupees " the words " five thousand rupees " shall be substituted.
Bombay Shops and Establishments (Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 10--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 63 of the principal Act after the Explanation, the following proviso shall be added, namely :- "Provided that, the maximum limit for working overtime shall not exceed three hours and the employer may, for the purpose of the work beyond the said overtime hours, engage additional number of employees.".
