Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax Act, 1958. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax Act, 1958 for the purposes hereinafter appearing;it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax (Amendment) Act, 2002.
Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In the FIRST SCHEDULE to the Bombay Motor Vehicles Tax Act, 1958, in clause (A), in sub-clause (IV), in entry (3),- (a) after sub-entry (b), the following sub-entry shall be inserted, namely :- "(bb) Tourist or ordinary vehicles, permitted to carry more than six passengers, excluding driver, for every passenger carried on each sleeper berth. 15,000"; (b) after sub-entry (c), the following sub-entry shall be inserted, namely :- "(cc) Air conditioned vehicles, permitted to carry more than six passengers, excluding driver, for every passenger carried on each sleeper berth. 20,000";
