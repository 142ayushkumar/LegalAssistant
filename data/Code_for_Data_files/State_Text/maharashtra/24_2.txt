Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961.WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961 for the purposes hereinafter appearing ; It is hereby enacted in the Fifty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2001.
Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 2 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), after clause (16), the following clause shall be inserted, namely :- "(16A) "Irrigation Development Corporation" means the Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation established under the Maharashtra Krishna Valley Development Corporation Act, 1996, or the Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation established under the Vidarbha Irrigation Development Corporation Act, 1997, or the Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation established under the Konkan Irrigation Development Corporation Act, 1997, or the Maharashtra Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation established under the Maharashtra Tapi Irrigation Development Corporation Act, 1997, or the Maharashtra Godawari Marathwada Irrigation Development Corporation established under the Maharashtra Godawari Marathwada Irrigation Corporation Act, 1998;".
Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 143 of the principal Act, - (a) in clause (b), the words, figures and letter "and section 151A" shall be deleted; (b) in clause (c), the words, figures and letter "and section 152A" shall be deleted.
Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra For section 146 of the principal Act, the following section shall be substituted, namely:- "146. Levy of cess on water-rate:-The State Government or the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation from the date of its establishment, may levy a cess not exceeding twenty paise, on every rupee of water-rate leviable under the provisions of the Maharashtra Irrigation Act, 1976 or the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation Act, on the basis of assessment of authorized use of water.".
Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 5--> State(s): Maharashtra For section 148 of the principal Act, the following section shall be substituted, namely:- "148. Manner of levying cess described in section 146:-The cess described in section 146 shall be levied, so far as may be, in the same manner, and under the same provisions of law, as water-rates payable to the State Government or the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation under the Maharashtra Irrigation Act, 1976 or the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation Act.".
Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 6--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 150 of the principal Act,- (a) in sub-section (1), after the words "paid by the State Government" the words "or any Irrigation Development Corporation, from the date of establishment of the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation" shall be inserted; (b) in sub-section (2), after the words "the State Government" the words "or any Irrigation Development Corporation" from the date of establishment of the concerned Irrigation Development Corporation shall be inserted.
Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 7--> State(s): Maharashtra Section 151A of the principle Act shall be deleted.
Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 8--> State(s): Maharashtra Section 152A of the principle Act shall be deleted.
