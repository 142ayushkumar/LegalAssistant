Maharashtra Prevention of Dangerous Activities of Slumlords, Bootleggers, Drug-offenders and Dangerous Persons (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Prevention of Dangerous Activities of Slumlords, Bootleggers, Drug-offenders and Dangerous Persons Act, 1981. WHEREAS, it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Prevention of Dangerous Activities of Slumlords, Bootleggers, Drug-offenders and Dangerous Persons Act, 1981, (Mah. LV of 1981) for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Prevention of Dangerous Activities of Slumlords, Bootleggers, Drug-offenders and Dangerous Persons (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Maharashtra Prevention of Dangerous Activities of Slumlords, Bootleggers, Drug-offenders and Dangerous Persons (Amendment) Act, 2007. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
Maharashtra Prevention of Dangerous Activities of Slumlords, Bootleggers, Drug-offenders and Dangerous Persons (Amendment) Act, 2007_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 3 of the Maharashtra Prevention of Dangerous Activities of Slumlords, Bootleggers, Drug-offenders and Dangerous Persons Act, 1981 (Mah. LV of 1981), in the proviso to subsection (2), for the words "three months", at both the places where they occur, the words "six months' shall be substituted.
