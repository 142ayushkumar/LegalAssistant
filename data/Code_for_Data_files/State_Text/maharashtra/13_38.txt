Maharashtra Medical Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Medical Practitioners Act, 1961. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Medical Practitioners Act, 1961, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Medical Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Medical Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 2005.
Maharashtra Medical Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra Section 37 of the Maharashtra Medical Practitioners Act, 1961 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act") shall be deleted.
Maharashtra Medical Practitioners (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra For removal of doubt it is hereby declared that, section 37 of the principal Act having been deleted, notwithstanding anything contained in any judgment, decree, order or direction of any Court or in a licence or instrument, on or after the date of publication of this Act in the Official Gazette, the permission or liberty granted to any person, who was otherwise not eligible for being registered under the principal Act, to practise in rural areas by virtue of the provisions of the said section 37, shall be deemed to have been cancelled and the privileges conferred on such person shall be deemed to have been withdrawn.
