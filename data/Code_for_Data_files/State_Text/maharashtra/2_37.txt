Bombay Entertainments Duty (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Bombay Entertainments Duty Act, 1923. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Bombay Entertainments Duty Act, 1923, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; It is hereby enacted in the Fifty-first Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Bombay Entertainments Duty (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Bombay Entertainments Duty (Amendment) Act, 2000.
Bombay Entertainments Duty (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 2 of the Bombay Entertainments Duty Act, 1923 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"),- (1) in clause (c), - (a) in entry (vi), the word "or" shall be deleted; (b) in entry (vii), the word "or" shall be added at the end; (c) after entry (vii), the following entry shall be added, namely :- "(viii) responsible for or for the time being in charge of, the management of any dance bar, or responsible for organising performances of western music (classical, light or instrumental) and dances from western countries including folk dances from western countries, or Indian folk dances such as Ras-Garba, Dandiya, Disco-Dandiya or Rain Dandiya or Ras-Garba or Dandiya, by whatever name it is known."; (2) after clause (e), the following clause shall be inserted, namely :- "(e-e) "dance bar" means and includes any bar or permit-room where along with serving liquor, for entertainment, any type of dance is also performed to the tune of any type of music;".
Bombay Entertainments Duty (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 3 of the principal Act, after clause (10), the following of clauses shall be added, namely :- "(11) Notwithstanding anything contained in clause (b) of sub-section (1) or in any other provisions of this Act, there shall be levied and paid in advance, by the tenth day of every calendar month, by the proprietor of every dance bar, the entertainment duty in respect of every dance performance in such dance bar, to the State Government, at the rate specified in the Table below :- Table Serial Number Area Amount of entertainment duty in a dance bar per dance performance, per month (Rupees) (1) (2) (3) 1 Within the limits of Municipal Corporation of Brihan Mumbai. 10,000 2 Outside the limits of Municipal Corporation of Brihan Mumbai. 5,000 (12) Notwithstanding anything contained in clause (b) of sub-section (1) or in any other provisions of this Act, in case of all performances of, - (i) western music (classical, light or instrumental); (ii) western dances including folk dances from western countries; and (iii) Indian folk dances such as Ras-Garba, Dandiya, Disco-Dandiya, or Rain Dandiya or Ras-Garba or Dandiya by whatever name it is known; where the payment for admission to such performance or show exceeds rupees one hundred, there shall be levied and paid, by the proprietor to the State Government, in respect of such performance or show, entertainment duty at the rate specified in clause (b) of sub-section (1).".
Bombay Entertainments Duty (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 6 of the principal Act, - (1) in sub-section (1), for the words "in the case of any area for which a Commissioner of Police has been appointed, the Commissioner of Police or elsewhere, the District Magistrate" the words "the Collector" shall be substituted; (2) in sub-section (2), for the words "the Commissioner of Police or the District Magistrate, as the case may be" the words "the Collector" shall be substituted.
