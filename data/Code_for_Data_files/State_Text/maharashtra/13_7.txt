Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Third Amendment) Act, 2017_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Sixty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows
Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Third Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Third Amendment) Act, 2017.
Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Third Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 152 of the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960,- (a) in sub-section (1), after the figures "78" the figures and letter, "78A" shall be inserted; (b) after sub-section (4), the following sub-section shall be added, namely:- "(5) The State Government may, by order published in the Official Gazette, direct that the powers conferred on it by this section, in circumstances and under such condition if any, as may be specified in the direction, be exercised also by an officer of the rank of Secretary to Government.".
