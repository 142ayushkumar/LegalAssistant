Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Universities Act, 1994. WHEREAS both Houses of the State Legislature were not in session ; AND WHEREAS the Governor of Maharashtra was satisfied that circumstances existed which rendered it necessary for him to take immediate action further to amend the Maharashtra Universities Act, 1994, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; and, therefore, promulgated the Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Ordinance, 2001, on the 21st June 2001 and the Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) (Amendment) Ordinance, 2001, on the 12th July 2001; AND WHEREAS it is expedient to replace the said Ordinances by an Act of the State Legislature ; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Act, 2001. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 15th May 2001.
Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 82 of the Maharashtra Universities Act, 1994, in the second proviso to sub-section (5), for the figures, letters and words " 31st May of the year " the figures, letters and words "15th July of the year" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) The Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Ordinance, 2001 and the Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) (Amendment) Ordinance, 2001, are hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the Maharashtra Universities Act, 1994, as amended by the said Ordinances, shall be deemed to have been done or taken, as the case may be, under the corresponding provisions of the said Act, as amended by this Act.
