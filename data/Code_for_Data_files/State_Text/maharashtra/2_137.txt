Bombay Public Trusts (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Bombay Public Trusts Act, 1950. WHEREAS both Houses of the State Legislature were not in session; AND WHEREAS the Governor of Maharashtra was satisfied that circumstances existed which render it necessary for him to take immediate action further to amend the Bombay Public Trusts Act, 1950, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; and, therefore, promulgated the Bombay Public Trusts (Amendment) Ordinance, 2005, on the 16th September 2005; AND WHEREAS it is expedient to replace the said Ordinance by an Act of the State Legislature; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Public Trusts (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Bombay Public Trusts (Amendment) Act, 2005. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 16th September 2005.
Bombay Public Trusts (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 51 of the Bombay Public Trusts Act, 1950 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"),- (a) in sub-section (2), for the words and figures "Maharashtra Revenue Tribunal constituted under the Bombay Revenue Tribunal Act, 1957" the words "Divisional Commissioner" shall be substituted; (b) in sub-section (4), for the words "Maharashtra Revenue Tribunal" the words "Divisional Commissioner" shall be substituted.
Bombay Public Trusts (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 71 of the principal Act,- (a) for the words "Maharashtra Revenue Tribunal", wherever they occur, the words " Divisional Commissioner" shall be substituted; (b) in the marginal note, for the words "Maharashtra Revenue Tribunal" the words "Divisional Commissioner" shall be substituted.

Bombay Public Trusts (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 5--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) The Bombay Public Trusts (Amendment) Ordinance, 2005, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken (including any notification or order issued), under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done taken or issued, as the case may be, under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
