Maharashtra Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act to amend the Maharashtra Value Added Tax Act, 2002. WHEREAS both Houses of the State Legislature were not in session; AND WHEREAS the Governor of Maharashtra was satisfied that circumstances existed which rendered it necessary for him to take immediate action to amend the Maharashtra Value Added Tax Act, 2002, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; and, therefore, promulgated the Maharashtra Value Added Tax (Amendment) Ordinance, 2005, on the 9th March 2005; AND WHEREAS it is expedient to replace the said Ordinance by an Act of the State Legislature; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Maharashtra Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Maharashtra Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2005. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 9th March 2005.
Maharashtra Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 1 of the Maharashtra Value Added Tax Act, 2002 (hereinafter referred to as " the principal Act"), for sub-section (3), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely:- "(3) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.".
Maharashtra Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 2 of the principal Act, in clause (3), for the figures, letters and words " 1st day of April 2003 " the words, brackets and figures " day appointed by the State Government under sub-section (3) of section 1" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra The Maharashtra Value Added Tax (Amendment) Ordinance, 2005, is hereby repealed.
