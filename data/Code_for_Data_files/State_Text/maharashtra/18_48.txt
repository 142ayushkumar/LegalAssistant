Maharashtra State Commission for Backward Classes (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act to amend the Maharashtra State Commission for Backward Classes Act, 2005. Whereas both Houses of the State Legislature were not in session; And Whereas the Governor of Maharashtra was satisfied that circumstances existed which rendered it necessary for him to take immediate action to amend the Maharashtra State Commission for Backward Classes Act, 2005, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; and, therefore, promulgated the Maharashtra State Commission for Backward Classes (Amendment) Ordinance, 2017 on the 9th January 2017; And Whereas it is expedient to replace the said Ordinance by an Act of the State Legislature; it is hereby enacted in the Sixty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra State Commission for Backward Classes (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Maharashtra State Commission for Backward Classes (Amendment) Act, 2017. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 9th January 2017.
Maharashtra State Commission for Backward Classes (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 3 of the Maharashtra State Commission for Backward Classes Act, 2005 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), in sub-section (2), in clause (c), for the words "six members, one member each" the words "eight members, at least one member each" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra State Commission for Backward Classes (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) The Maharashtra State Commission for Backward Classes (Amendment) Ordinance, 2017, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken (including any notification or order issued) under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done, taken or issued, as the case may be, under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
