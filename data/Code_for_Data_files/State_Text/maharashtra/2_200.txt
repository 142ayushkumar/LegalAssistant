Bombay Village Panchayats and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Bombay Village Panchayats Act, 1958 and the Maharashtra Zilla arishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Bombay Village Panchayats Act, 1958 and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961 for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Bombay Village Panchayats and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Bombay Village Panchayats and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2005.
Bombay Village Panchayats and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 3 of the Bombay Village Panchayats Act, 1958 (hereinafter referred to as " the Village Panchayats Act"), after clause (a-3), the following clause shall be inserted, namely :- " (aa-1) "ballot box" or "ballot paper" includes an electronic voting machine used at an election for giving or recording of votes ;".
Bombay Village Panchayats and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 11 of the Village Panchayats Act, after sub-section (3), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely :- " (3A) The voting at an election shall be by ballot or by electronic voting machine and no votes shall be received by proxy.".
Bombay Village Panchayats and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 2 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961 (hereinafter referred to as the Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act"), after clause (2), the following clause shall be inserted, namely :- " (2A) "ballot box" or "ballot paper" includes an electronic voting machine used at an election for giving or recording of votes ;".
Bombay Village Panchayats and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 5--> State(s): Maharashtra After section 10 of the Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely :- " 10A.Manner of voting.-The voting at an election of the Councillors shall be by ballot or by electronic voting machine and no votes shall be received by proxy.".
Bombay Village Panchayats and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 6--> State(s): Maharashtra After section 58 of the Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely :- " 58A.Manner of voting.-The voting at an election of the members shall be by ballot or by electronic voting machine and no votes shall be received by proxy."
