Maharashtra Forest Development (Tax on sale of forest-produce by Government or Forest Development Corporation) (Continuance) (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Forest Development (Tax on sale of forest-produce by Government or Forest Development Corporation) (Continuance) Act, 1983. WHEREAS both Houses of the State Legislature were not in session; AND WHEREAS the Governor of Maharashtra was satisfied that circumstances existed which rendered it necessary for him to take immediate action further to amend the Maharashtra Forest Development (Tax on sale of forest-produce by Government or Forest Development Corporation) (Continuance) Act, 1983, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; and, therefore, promulgated the Maharashtra Forest Development (Tax on sale of forest-produce by Government or Forest Development Corporation) (Continuance) (Amendment) Ordinance, 2001, on the 22nd June 2001; AND WHEREAS it is expedient to replace the said Ordinance by an Act of the State Legislature; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Maharashtra Forest Development (Tax on sale of forest-produce by Government or Forest Development Corporation) (Continuance) (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Maharashtra Forest Development (Tax on sale of forest-produce by Government or Forest Development Corporation) (Continuance) (Amendment) Act, 2001. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 22nd June 2001.
Maharashtra Forest Development (Tax on sale of forest-produce by Government or Forest Development Corporation) (Continuance) (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 1 of the Maharashtra Forest Development (Tax on sale of forest-produce by Government or Forest Development Corporation) (Continuance) Act, 1983 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), in sub-section (1), the brackets and word "(Continuance)" shall be deleted and shall be deemed always to have been deleted.
Maharashtra Forest Development (Tax on sale of forest-produce by Government or Forest Development Corporation) (Continuance) (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 3 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), for the words "ten per cent." the words "twelve per cent." shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Forest Development (Tax on sale of forest-produce by Government or Forest Development Corporation) (Continuance) (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) The Maharashtra Forest Development (Tax on sale of forest-produce by Government or Forest Development Corporation) (Continuance) (Amendment) Ordinance, 2001, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken, as the case may be, under the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
