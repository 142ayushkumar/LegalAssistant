Maharashtra Legislature Members' Pension (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Legislature Members' Pension Act, 1976. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Legislature Members' Pension Act, 1976 (Mah. I of 1977), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Sixty-fourth Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Maharashtra Legislature Members' Pension (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Legislature Members' Pension (Amendment) Act, 2013.1 Received the assent of the Governor on 21st August, 2013 and was published in the Maharashtra Govt. Gazette, Extra-ordinary, Part VIII, dated 21st August, 2013
Maharashtra Legislature Members' Pension (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 3 of the Maharashtra Legislature Members' Pension Act, 1976 (Mah. I of 1977) (hereinafter referred to as " the principal Act"), in sub-section (1),- (a) for the words "twenty-five thousand rupees" the words "forty thousand rupees" shall be substituted; (b) in the first proviso, for the words "one thousand rupees" the words "two thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Legislature Members' Pension (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 4A of the principal Act, in sub-section (2D), for the words and figures "with effect from the 1st May, 2011, at the rate of rupees twenty-five thousand" the words and figures "with effect from the 1 st August, 2013, at the rate of rupees forty thousand" shall be substituted.
