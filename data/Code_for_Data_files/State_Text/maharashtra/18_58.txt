Maharashtra State Enterprises (Restructuring and Other Special Provisions) (Repeal) Act, 2007_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act to provide for repeal of the Maharashtra State Enterprises (Restructuring and Other Special Provisions) Act, 2000 and for certain matters incidental thereto. WHEREAS it is considered expedient to repeal the Maharashtra State Enterprises (Restructuring and Other Special Provisions) Act, 2000, and to provide for certain matters incidental thereto; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Maharashtra State Enterprises (Restructuring and Other Special Provisions) (Repeal) Act, 2007_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra State Enterprises (Restructuring and Other Special Provisions) (Repeal) Act, 2007.
Maharashtra State Enterprises (Restructuring and Other Special Provisions) (Repeal) Act, 2007_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) The Maharashtra State Enterprises (Restructuring and Other Special Provisions) Act, 2000, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, all proceedings relating to any order made or purported to be made under the Maharashtra State Enterprises (Restructuring and Other Special Provisions) Act, 2000 pending immediately before the commencement of this Act, before any Court, tribunal or other authority shall abate.
