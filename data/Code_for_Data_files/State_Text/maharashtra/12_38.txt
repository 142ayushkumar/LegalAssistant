Maharashtra Legislature Members' Pension (Third Amendment) Act, 2006_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Legislature Members' Pension Act, 1976. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Legislature Members' Pension Act, 1976, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Legislature Members' Pension (Third Amendment) Act, 2006_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Legislature Members' Pension (Third Amendment) Act, 2006.
Maharashtra Legislature Members' Pension (Third Amendment) Act, 2006_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 3 of the Maharashtra Legislature Members' Pension Act, 1976, in sub-section (1), in the second proviso, for the words "disqualified as a Member of the State Legislature" the words "disqualified for being a Member of the State Legislature under clause (1) of article 191 of the Constitution of India" shall be substituted and shall be deemed to have been substituted with effect from the 1st September 2000.
