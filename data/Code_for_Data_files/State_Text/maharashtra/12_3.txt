Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966 . WHEREAS it is observed that over the last few years the number of appeals filed in the State before the Maharashtra Revenue Tribunal under the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966 and other State enactments, is reduced to a considerable extent; AND WHEREAS, having regard to the said fact, the Government of Maharashtra considers it expedient to abolish the Maharashtra Revenue Tribunal and to confer its powers to dispose of the appeals filed before the Tribunal under the Code and various enactments to the concerned Divisional Commissioner; AND WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966 for the purposes aforesaid; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2002. (2) It shall come into force on the 1st May 2002.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 99 of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966 (hereinafter referred to as "the said Code"),- (a) for the words "for reference to the Maharashtra Revenue Tribunal" the words "for reference to the concerned Divisional Commissioner" shall be substituted; (b) for the words "the Revenue Tribunal" the words "the Divisional Commissioner" shall be substituted; (c) for the words "The Revenue Tribunal" the words 'The Divisional Commissioner" shall be substituted ; (d) in the marginal note, for the words "Revenue Tribunal" the words "Divisional Commissioner" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 100 of the said Code, - (a) in sub-section (1), for the words "the Revenue Tribunal" the words "the Divisional Commissioner" shall be substituted; (b) in sub-section (2), for the words "the Revenue Tribunal" the words "the Divisional Commissioner" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra Section 245 of the said Code shall be deleted.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 5--> State(s): Maharashtra Section 246 of the said Code shall be deleted.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 6--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 271 of the said Code, the words "or as the case may be, the Maharashtra Revenue Tribunal" shall be deleted.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 7--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 272 of the said Code, - (a) the words "or as the case may be, the Maharashtra Revenue Tribunal" shall be deleted ; (b) the words "or the Maharashtra Revenue Tribunal" shall be deleted; and (c) the words "or of the Maharashtra Revenue Tribunal" shall be deleted.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 8--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 274 of the said Code including the marginal note, for the words "Maharashtra Revenue Tribunal" the words "concerned Divisional Commissioner" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 9--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 275 of the said Code, for the words" the Maharashtra Revenue Tribunal" the words "the Divisional Commissioner" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 10--> State(s): Maharashtra Chapter XV of the said Code shall be deleted.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 11--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 328 of the said Code, in sub-section (2), in clause (xxxvi), for the words "the Tribunal" the words "the Divisional Commissioner" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 12--> State(s): Maharashtra Schedule 'J' appended to the said Code shall be deleted.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 13--> State(s): Maharashtra On the coming into force of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2002, any proceedings pending before the Maharashtra Revenue Tribunal, on the day immediately preceding the coming into force of the said Amendment Act, shall be transferred to the concerned Divisional Commissioner and shall be disposed of by the concerned Commissioner under the provisions of the said Code or, as the case may be, the relevant Act.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 14--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) Consequent upon the abolition of the Maharashtra Revenue Tribunal, the reference to the Maharashtra Revenue Tribunal in any of the State enactments specified in the Schedule appended to this Act shall be construed as, the reference to the Divisional Commissioner of the concerned Division, appointed under section 6 of the said Code. (2) Unless the context otherwise requires, any reference to the Maharashtra Revenue Tribunal or reference to the Revenue Tribunal or Tribunal, as the case may be, in any other state enactment or any other rules, regulations, bye-laws, notifications, orders issued under any enactment or in any instrument shall, after the commencement of this Act, be construed as a reference to the Divisional Commissioner.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2002_Section 15--> State(s): Maharashtra Nothing contained in section 14 conferring the powers of the Maharashtra Revenue Tribunal to the Divisional Commissioner, shall affect the previous operation of any notification, order, rule or bye-laws issued or made or anything done or suffered under the said Code or under any State enactments as they existed immediately before the date of coming into force of this Act, or any right, privilege, obligation or liability already acquired, accrued or incurred under any such law.
