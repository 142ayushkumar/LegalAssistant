Maharashtra University of Health Sciences (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra University of Health Sciences Act, 1998. WHEREAS all courses in Health Sciences as defined in section 2(17) of the Maharashtra University of Health Sciences Act, 1998, have been affiliated to the Maharashtra University of Health Sciences; AND WHEREAS by enacting the Maharashtra University of Health Sciences (Amendment) Act, 2001 and the Maharashtra University of Health Sciences (Amendment and Continuance) Act, 2002, post graduate degree and diploma courses in Health Sciences were temporarily carved out of the jurisdiction of the Health University and were reaffiliated to the Universities specified in the Schedule to the Maharashtra Universities Act, 1994; AND WHEREAS the Government of Maharashtra considered it expedient to again affiliate the post graduate degree and diploma courses in Health Sciences to the Health University from the academic year 2006-2007; AND WHEREAS both Houses of the State Legislature were not in session; AND WHEREAS the Governor of Maharashtra was satisfied that circumstances existed which rendered it necessary for him to take immediate action further to amend the Maharashtra University of Health Sciences Act, 1998, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; and, therefore, promulgated the Maharashtra University of Health Sciences (Amendment) Ordinance, 2005 on the 31st October 2005; AND WHEREAS it is expedient to replace the said Ordinance by an Act of the State Legislature; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra University of Health Sciences (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Maharashtra University of Health Sciences (Amendment) Act, 2005. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 31st October, 2005.
Maharashtra University of Health Sciences (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 6 of the Maharashtra University of Health Sciences Act, 1998 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), sub-sections (4) and (5) shall be deleted.
Maharashtra University of Health Sciences (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra Notwithstanding the deletion of sub-sections (4) and (5) of section 6 of the principal Act, all colleges and autonomous institutions of Health Sciences admitted to the privileges of, or affiliated to, the Universities specified in the Schedule to the Maharashtra Universities Act, 1994, on or before the date of coming into force of the Maharashtra University of Health Sciences (Amendment) Act, 2005, and all students who, on the said date stood enrolled, by such colleges or institutions for Post Graduate Degree or Diploma in Medical, Dental, Ayurved, Unani, Homoeopathy, Yoga and Naturopathy Sciences, shall, so far as such Post Graduate Degrees or Diplomas are concerned, continue to be affiliated to, or admitted to the privileges of, their respective Universities specified in the Schedule to the Maharashtra Universities Act, 1994, until those students have acquired such Post Graduate Degrees or Diplomas from their respective Universities under the Maharashtra Universities Act, 1994. Explanation:-For the purposes of this section, the expression "Post Graduate Degree or Diploma in Medical Sciences" includes the Post Graduate Degree or Diploma in Occupational Therapy, Physiotherapy, Audiology, Speech Therapy, Prosthetics and Orthotics.
Maharashtra University of Health Sciences (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) The Maharashtra University of Health Sciences (Amendment) Ordinance, 2005, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
