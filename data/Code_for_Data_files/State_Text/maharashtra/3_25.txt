Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Third Amendment) Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960. WHEREAS both Houses of the State Legislature were not in session ; AND WHEREAS the Governor of Maharashtra was satisfied that circumstances existed which rendered it necessary for him to take immediate action further to amend the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; and, therefore, promulgated the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Fourth Amendment) Ordinance, 2001, on the 23rd August, 2001; AND WHEREAS it is expedient to replace the said Ordinance by an Act of the State Legislature; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Third Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Third Amendment) Act, 2001. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 23rd April 2001.
Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Third Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 73-B of the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act (hereinafter referred to as " the principal Act"), - (a) in sub-section (1), - (i) for the word " two" the word " four" shall be substituted ; (ii) in clause (a), the word "and" shall be deleted ; (iii) after sub-clause (a), the following clauses shall be inserted, namely :- "(a-1) one for the members belonging to the Other Backward Classes; (a-2) one for the members belonging to the De-notified Tribes (Vimukta Jatis), Nomadic Tribes or Special Backward Classes ; and"; (b) in sub-section (2), for the word "two" the word "four" shall be substituted; (c) in sub-section (3), after the words "Scheduled Tribes" the words "or Other Backward Classes or De-notified Tribes (Vimukta Jatis) or Nomadic Tribes or Special Backward Classes," shall be inserted ; (d) in sub-section (4) for the word "two" the word "four" shall be substituted; (e) in the Explanation to section 73-B, - (i) in clause (b), the words ", and the expression "Scheduled Tribes" includes "De-notified Tribes and Nomadic Tribes" " shall be deleted; (ii) after clause (b), the following clause shall be inserted, namely :- "(b-1) the expression "Other Backward Classes, De-notified Tribes (Vimukta Jatis) and Nomadic Tribes and Special Backward Classes" means such classes or parts of or groups within such classes as are declared, from time to time, by the State Government to be Other Backward Classes, De-notified Tribes (Vimukta Jatis) and Nomadic Tribes and Special Backward Classes;"; (f) in the marginal note, after the words " Scheduled Tribes" the words ", Other Backward Classes, De-notified Tribes (Vimukta Jatis), Nomadic Tribes Special Backward Classes" shall be inserted.
Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Third Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) The Maharashtra Cooperative Societies (Fourth Amendment) Ordinance, 2001, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken (including any notification or order issued) under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done, taken or issued, as the case may be, under the principal Act, as amended by this Ordinance.
