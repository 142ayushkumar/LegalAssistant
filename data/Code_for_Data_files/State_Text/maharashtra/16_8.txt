Payment of Wages (Maharashtra Amendment) Act, 2004_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Payment of Wages Act, 1936, in its application to the State of Maharashtra. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Payment of Wages Act, 1936, in its application to the State of Maharashtra, for the purposes hereinafter appearing ; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Payment of Wages (Maharashtra Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Payment of Wages (Maharashtra Amendment) Act, 2004.
Payment of Wages (Maharashtra Amendment) Act, 2004_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 1 of the Payment of Wages Act, 1936, in its application to the State of Maharashtra, in sub-section (6), for the words "one thousand six hundred rupees" the words "six thousand five hundred rupees" shall be substituted.
