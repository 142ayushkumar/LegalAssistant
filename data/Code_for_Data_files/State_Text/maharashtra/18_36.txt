Maharashtra Slum Areas (Improvement, Clearance and Redevelopment) (Amendment) Act, 2014_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Slum Areas (Improvement, Clearance and Redevelopment) Act, 1971. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Slum Areas (Improvement, Clearance and Redevelopment) Act, 1971 (Mah. XXVIll of 1971), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Sixty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Maharashtra Slum Areas (Improvement, Clearance and Redevelopment) (Amendment) Act, 2014_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Slum Areas (Improvement, Clearance and Redevelopment) (Amendment) Act, 2014.1 Received the assent of the Governor on 2nd May, 2014.
Maharashtra Slum Areas (Improvement, Clearance and Redevelopment) (Amendment) Act, 2014_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 3Y of the Maharashtra Slum Areas (Improvement, Clearance and Redevelopment) Act, 1971 (Mah. XXVIII of 1971) (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), in sub-section (1), for the figures, letters and word "1st January 1995" the figures, letters and word "1st January 2000" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Slum Areas (Improvement, Clearance and Redevelopment) (Amendment) Act, 2014_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 3Z of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), for the words, brackets and figures "the Maharashtra Slum Areas (Improvement, Clearance and Redevelopment) (Second Amendment) Act, 2001 (Mah. X of 2002)" the words, brackets and figures "the Maharashtra Slum Areas (Improvement, Clearance and Redevelopment) (Amendment) Act, 2014 (Mah. IX of 2014)" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Slum Areas (Improvement, Clearance and Redevelopment) (Amendment) Act, 2014_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra ln section 3Z-1 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), for the figures, letters and word "1st January 1995" the figures, letters and word "1st January 2000" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Slum Areas (Improvement, Clearance and Redevelopment) (Amendment) Act, 2014_Section 5--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 3Z-2 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), for the words, brackets and figures "the Maharashtra Slum Areas (Improvement, Clearance and Redevelopment) (Second Amendment) Act, 2001" the words, brackets and figures "the Maharashtra Slum Areas (Improvement, Clearance and Redevelopment) (Amendment) Act, 2014" shall be substituted.
