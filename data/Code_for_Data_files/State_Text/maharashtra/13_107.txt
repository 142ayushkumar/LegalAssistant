Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships (Second Amendment) Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships Act, 1965. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships Act, 1965, for the purposes hereinafter appearing ; It is hereby enacted in the fifty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships (Second Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships (Second Amendment) Act, 2001.
Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships (Second Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 341F of the Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships Act, 1965 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), after the words and figures "Industrial Development Act, 1961" the words ", City and Industrial Development Corporation of Maharashtra Limited incorporated under the Companies Act, 1956" shall be inserted.
Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships (Second Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 341H of the principal Act, - (a) in clause (a), after the words "Industrial Development Corporation" the words ", City and Industrial Development Corporation of Maharashtra Limited" shall be inserted. (b) in clause (b), after the words "Industrial Development Corporation" the words ", City and Industrial Development Corporation of Maharashtra Limited" shall be inserted.
Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships (Second Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 341J of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), after the words "Industrial Development Corporation" the words ", City and Industrial Development Corporation of Maharashtra Limited" shall be inserted.
