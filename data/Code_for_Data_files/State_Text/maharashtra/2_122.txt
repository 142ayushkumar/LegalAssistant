Bombay Primary Education and the Maharashtra Employees of Private Schools (Conditions of Service) Regulation (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Bombay Primary Education Act, 1947 and the Maharashtra Employees of Private Schools (Conditions of Service) Regulation Act, 1977. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Bombay Primary Education Act, 1947 and the Maharashtra Employees of Private Schools (Conditions of Service) Regulation Act, 1977, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Sixty- second Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Bombay Primary Education and the Maharashtra Employees of Private Schools (Conditions of Service) Regulation (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Bombay Primary Education and the Maharashtra Employees of Private Schools (Conditions of Service) Regulation (Amendment) Act, 2011.
Bombay Primary Education and the Maharashtra Employees of Private Schools (Conditions of Service) Regulation (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 2 of the Bombay Primary Education Act, 1947 (hereinafter, in this Chapter, referred to as "the Primary Education Act"), in clause (20), for the words "shikshan sevak" the words "Assistant Teacher (Probationary)" shall be substituted.
Bombay Primary Education and the Maharashtra Employees of Private Schools (Conditions of Service) Regulation (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 13 of the Primary Education Act, in sub-section (2) in clause (d), for the words "shikshan sevaks" the words "Assistant Teachers (Probationary)" shall be substituted.
Bombay Primary Education and the Maharashtra Employees of Private Schools (Conditions of Service) Regulation (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 16 of the Primary Education Act, in sub-section (2), for the words "shikshan sevaks" the words, "Assistant Teachers (Probationary)" shall be substituted.
Bombay Primary Education and the Maharashtra Employees of Private Schools (Conditions of Service) Regulation (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 5--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 17 of the Primary Education Act, in sub-section (1) in clause (c), for the words "shikshan sevaks," the words "Assistant Teachers (Probationary)," shall be substituted.
Bombay Primary Education and the Maharashtra Employees of Private Schools (Conditions of Service) Regulation (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 6--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 19 of the Primary Education Act,- (a) in sub-section (1), for the words "shikshan sevak" the words "Assistant Teacher (Probationary)" shall be substituted; (b) in sub-section (3), for the words "shikshan sevaks" the words "Assistant Teachers (Probationary)" shall be substituted.
Bombay Primary Education and the Maharashtra Employees of Private Schools (Conditions of Service) Regulation (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 7--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 20 of the Primary Education Act,- (a) in sub-section (1), for the words "Shikshan sevaks" the words "Assistant Teachers (Probationary)" shall be substituted; (b) in sub-section (3), for the words "shikshan sevaks" the words "Assistant Teachers (Probationary)" shall be substituted.
Bombay Primary Education and the Maharashtra Employees of Private Schools (Conditions of Service) Regulation (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 8--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 23 of the Primary Education Act,- (a) in sub-section (6), for the words "shikshan sevaks" the words "Assistant Teachers (Probationary)" shall be substituted; (b) in the marginal note, for the words "shikshan sevaks" the words "Assistant Teachers (Probationary)" shall be substituted.
Bombay Primary Education and the Maharashtra Employees of Private Schools (Conditions of Service) Regulation (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 9--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 27 of the Primary Education Act, in clause (d), for the words "shikshan sevaks" the words "Assistant Teachers (Probationary)" shall be substituted.
Bombay Primary Education and the Maharashtra Employees of Private Schools (Conditions of Service) Regulation (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 10--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 2 of the Maharashtra Employees of Private Schools (Conditions of Service) Regulation Act, 1977 (hereinafter, in this Chapter, referred to as "the Employees of Private Schools (Conditions of Service) Regulation Act"),- (a) in clause (7), for the words "shikshan sevak" the words "Assistant Teacher (Probationary)" shall be substituted; (b) in clause (10), for the words "shikshan sevaks" the words "Assistant Teachers (Probationary)" shall be substituted; (c) in clause (24A), for the words "shikshan sevak" the words "Assistant Teacher (Probationary)" shall be substituted.
Bombay Primary Education and the Maharashtra Employees of Private Schools (Conditions of Service) Regulation (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 11--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 5 of the Employees of Private Schools (Conditions of Service) Regulation Act,- (a) in sub-section (2),- (i) for the words "shikshan sevak" the words "Assistant Teacher (Probationary)" shall be substituted; (ii) in the proviso, for the words "shikshan sevak" the words "Assistant Teacher (Probationary)" shall be substituted; (b) in sub-section (2A), for the words "shikshan seva/(' the words "Assistant Teacher (Probationary)" shall be substituted.
Bombay Primary Education and the Maharashtra Employees of Private Schools (Conditions of Service) Regulation (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 12--> State(s): Maharashtra The terms and conditions prescribed by the Government for the appointment of shikshan sevaks, by issuing Government Resolutions or orders, from time to time, before the date of commencement of the Bombay Primary Education and the Maharashtra Employees of Private Schools (Conditions of Service) Regulation (Amendment) Act, 2011, shall continue to be in force unless they are modified or revoked by the Government.
