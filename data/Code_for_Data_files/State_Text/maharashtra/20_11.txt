Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Universities Act, 1994. Whereas both Houses of the State Legislature were not in session; And Whereas the Governor of Maharashtra was satisfied that circumstances existed which rendered it necessary for him to take immediate action further to amend the Maharashtra Universities Act, 1994, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; and, therefore, promulgated the Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Ordinance, 2016 on the 27th June 2016; And Whereas it is expedient to replace the said Ordinance, by an Act of the State Legislature; it is hereby enacted in the Sixty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Act, 2016. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 27th June 2016.
Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 82 of the Maharashtra Universities Act, 1994 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), after sub-section (5C), following sub-section shall be inserted, namely:- "(5D) Notwithstanding anything contained in sub-section (4) and in the second proviso to sub-section (5), for the academic year 2016-2017, in respect of the recommendations received from the university after the first day of May of the year but before the 27th July 2016 such permission from the State Government shall be communicated to the university on or before the 5th August 2016 and shall be given effect by the university in the same academic year.".
Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) The Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Ordinance, 2016 is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken (including any notification or order issued) under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done, taken or issued, as the case may be, under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
