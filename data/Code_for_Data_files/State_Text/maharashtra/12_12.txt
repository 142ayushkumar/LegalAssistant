Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Fifth Amendment) Act, 2016_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Sixty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Fifth Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Fifth Amendment) Act, 2016.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Fifth Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 35 of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966 (hereinafter referred to as "the said Code"),- (a) in sub-section (2), for the words "at such price not exceeding twenty-four times the assessment thereof" the words "at such price not exceeding twenty-four times the assessment thereof or such amount as may be prescribed, whichever is higher," shall be substituted; (b) in sub-section (4), for the words "equal to three times the assessment" the words "equal to three times the assessment or such amount as may be prescribed, whichever is higher," shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Fifth Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 44 of the said Code, in sub-section (5), for the words "such fine as the Collector may, subject to rules made in this behalf direct not exceeding five hundred rupees" the words "such fine not exceeding five hundred rupees or such amount as may be prescribed, whichever 1s higher, as may be directed by the Collector" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Fifth Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 44A of the said Code,- (a) in sub-section (3), in clause (a), in sub-clause (i), for the words "such penalty not exceeding rupees ten thousand ; as the Collector may subject to the rules, if any, made in this behalf direct" the words "such penalty not exceeding rupees ten thousand or such amount as may be prescribed, whichever is higher, as the Collector may direct" shall be substituted ; (b) in sub-section (4), in clause (a), for the portion beginning with the words" further penalty" and ending with the words" one hundred rupees", the following shall substituted, namely :- "further penalty not exceeding five thousand rupees or such amount as may be prescribed, whichever is higher, for such contravention, and a daily penalty not exceeding one hundred rupees or such amount as may be prescribed, whichever is higher,".
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Fifth Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 5--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 45 of the said Code, in sub-section (2), for the portion beginning with the words "person a penalty" and ending with the words "thirty rupees", the following shall be substituted, namely :- "person a penalty not exceeding three hundred rupees or such amount as may be prescribed, whichever is higher, for such contravention, and a further penalty not exceeding thirty rupees or such amount as may be prescribed, whichever is higher,".
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Fifth Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 6--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 47A of the said Code,- (a) in sub-section (2), for the words "five times" the words "five times or such amount as may be prescribed, whichever is higher, of" shall be substituted ; (b) in sub-section (3), for the words "five times" the words "five times or such amount as may be prescribed, whichever is higher, of" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Fifth Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 7--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 49 of the said Code,- (a) in sub-section (2), in clause (iv), in sub-clause (b), for the word s an d figures "at a rate of 25 paise" the words "at the rate of twenty-five paise or such amount as may be prescribed, whichever is higher," shall be substituted ; (b) in sub-section (10), for the words "not exceeding one hundred rupees" the words "not exceeding one hundred rupees or such amount as may be prescribed, whichever is higher," shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Fifth Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 8--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 50 of the said Code,- (a) in sub-section (2),- (i) for the words "a fine which shall not be less than five rupees but not more than one thousand rupees" the words "a fine which shall be one thousand rupees or such amount as may be prescribed, whichever is higher," shall be substituted; (ii) for the words "not exceeding two thousand rupees" the words "not exceeding two thousand rupees or such amount as may be prescribed, whichever is higher," shall be substituted; (iii) for the words "fifty rupees" the words "fifty rupees or such amount as may be prescribed, whichever is higher," shall be substituted ; (b) in sub-section (4),- (i) for the words "twenty-five rupees" the words "twenty-five rupees or such amount as may be prescribed, whichever is higher" shall be substituted; (ii) for the words "fifty rupees" the words "fifty rupees or as may be prescribed, whichever is higher," shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Fifth Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 9--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 51 of the said Code, for the words "five times", at both the places where they occur, the words "five times or such times as may be prescribed, whichever is higher, of" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Fifth Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 10--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 53 of the said Code, in sub-section (3), for the words "two times the assessment or rent for land" the words "two times the assessment or rent for land or such amount as may be prescribed, whichever is higher," shall be substituted.
