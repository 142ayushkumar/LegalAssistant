Bombay Village Panchayats (Second Amendment) Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Bombay Village Panchayats Act, 1958. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Bombay Village Panchayats Act, 1958, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; It is hereby enacted in the Fifty-second year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Village Panchayats (Second Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Bombay Village Panchayats (Second Amendment) Act, 2001.
Bombay Village Panchayats (Second Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 10 of the Bombay Village Panchayats Act, 1958 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), for subsection (3), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely :- "(3) the names of the members falling under clause (a) of sub-section (1) shall be published by the State Election Commissioner in the prescribed manner.".
Bombay Village Panchayats (Second Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 176 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), in clause (iii), for the words "publication of elected and appointed members of a panchayat" the words, brackets, letter and figure "publication of the names of the Members falling under clause (a) of sub-section (1)" shall be substituted.
