Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961 (Mah. V of 1962), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Sixty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2013.
Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra Section 156 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961 (Mah. V of 1962) shall be deleted.
