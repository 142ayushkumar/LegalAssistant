Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Universities Act, 1994. Whereas both Houses of the State Legislature were not in session; And Whereas the Governor of Maharashtra was satisfied that circumstances existed which render it necessary for him to take immediate action further to amend the Maharashtra Universities Act, 1994 (Mah. XXXV of 1994), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; and, therefore, promulgated the Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Ordinance, 2013 (Mah. Ord. VIII of 2013) on the 30th May, 2013 ; And Whereas it is expedient to replace the said Ordinance, by an Act of the State Legislature; it is hereby enacted in the Sixty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Act, 2013.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 30th May, 2013. Received the assent of the Governor on 7th August, 2013 and was published in the Maharashtra Govt. Gazette, Extra-ordinary, Part VIII, dated 7th August, 2013.
Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 13 of the Maharashtra Universities Act, 1994 (Mah. XXXV of 1994), (hereinafter referred to as " the principal Act"), for sub-section (3), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely:- "(3) The term of the Pro-Vice-Chancellor shall be co-terminus with that of the Vice-Chancellor or till he attains the age of superannuation, whichever is earlier.".
Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) The Maharashtra Universities (Amendment) Ordinance, 2013 (Mah. Ord. VIII of 2013), is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken (including any notification or order issued) under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done, taken or, as the case may be, issued under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
