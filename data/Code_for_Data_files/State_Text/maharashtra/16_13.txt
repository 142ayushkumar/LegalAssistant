Maharashtra Pre-School Centres (Regulation of Admission) (Repeal) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act to repeal the Maharashtra Pre-School Centres (Regulation of Admission) Act, 1996. WHEREAS the Government of Maharashtra considers it expedient to repeal the Maharashtra Pre-School Centres (Regulation of Admission) Act, 1996; It is hereby enacted in the Fifty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Pre-School Centres (Regulation of Admission) (Repeal) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Pre-School Centres (Regulation of Admission) (Repeal) Act, 2000.
Maharashtra Pre-School Centres (Regulation of Admission) (Repeal) Act, 2000_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra The Maharashtra Pre-School Centres (Regulation of Admission) Act, 1996, is hereby repealed.
