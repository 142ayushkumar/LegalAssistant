Maharashtra Stamp (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Stamp Act. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Stamp Act, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Sixty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Stamp (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Stamp (Amendment) Act, 2016.
Maharashtra Stamp (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 70 of the Maharashtra Stamp Act (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), sub-section (2) shall be deleted.
Maharashtra Stamp (Amendment) Act, 2016_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In SCHEDULE I of the principal Act, in Article 1, in clause (1),- (i) in sub-clause (c), in column (1), the word "and" shall be deleted; (ii) for sub-clause (d), the following sub-clauses shall be substituted, namely:- "(d) exceeds rupees 10,000 but is less than rupees 10,00,000; and Fifty rupees. (e) is rupees 10,00,000 and above. One Hundred rupees.".
