Bombay Village Panchayats and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Bombay Village Panchayats Act, 1958 and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961. WHEREAS both Houses of the State Legislature were not in session; AND WHEREAS the Governor of Maharashtra was satisfied that circumstances existed which rendered it necessary for him to take immediate action further to amend the Bombay Village Panchayats Act, 1958 and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; and, therefore, promulgated the Bombay Village Panchayats and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Ordinance, 2001, on the 10th February, 2001; AND WHEREAS it is expedient to replace the said Ordinance by an Act of the State Legislature ; It is hereby enacted in the Fifty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Bombay Village Panchayats and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Bombay Village Panchayats and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2001. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 10th February, 2001.
Bombay Village Panchayats and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 35 of the Bombay Village Panchayats Act, 1958 (hereinafter in this Act referred to as "the Village Panchayats Act"), in sub-section (3A), for the words "six months" the words "one year" shall be substituted.
Bombay Village Panchayats and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 49 of the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961 (hereinafter in this Act referred to as "the Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act"), in sub-section (7), for the words "six months" the words "one year" shall be substituted.
Bombay Village Panchayats and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 72 of the Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, in sub-section (7), for the words " "six months" the words "one year" shall be substituted.
Bombay Village Panchayats and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 5--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 87 of the Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, after the first proviso, the following proviso shall be added, namely :- "Provided further that, if the motion of no-confidence is not moved or, as the case may be, is rejected, no fresh motion of no-confidence shall be brought within a period of one year from the date of the special meeting.".
Bombay Village Panchayats and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 6--> State(s): Maharashtra If any difficulty arises in giving effect to the provisions of the Village Panchayats Act or the Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, as amended by this Act, the State Government may, as occasion arises, by order do anything not inconsistent with the provisions of the Village Panchayats Act or the Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act as amended by this Act, which appears to it to be necessary or expedient for the purpose of removing the difficulty. Provided that, no such order shall be made after the expiry of a period of two years from the date of commencement of this Act.
Bombay Village Panchayats and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2001_Section 7--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) The Bombay Village Panchayats and the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Ordinance, 2001 is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the Village Panchayats Act or the Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken as the case may be, under the Bombay Village Panchayats Act, 1958 or the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961, as amended by this Act.
