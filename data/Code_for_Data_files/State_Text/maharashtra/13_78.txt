Maharashtra Municipal Corporations and Municipal Councils (Amendment) Act, 2006_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Mumbai Municipal Corporation Act, the Bombay Provincial Municipal Corporations Act, 1949, the City of Nagpur Corporation Act, 1948 and the Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships Act, 1965. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Mumbai Municipal Corporation Act, the Bombay Provincial Municipal Corporations Act, 1949, the City of Nagpur Corporation Act, 1948 and the Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships Act, 1965, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-seventh Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Maharashtra Municipal Corporations and Municipal Councils (Amendment) Act, 2006_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra After section 5A of the Bombay Provincial Municipal Corporations Act, 1949 (hereinafter, in this Chapter, referred to as the Provincial Corporations Act), the following section shall be inserted, namely :- "5B. Person contesting election for reserved seat to submit Caste Certificate and Validity Certificate:-Every person desirous of contesting election to a seat reserved for the Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes or, as the case may be, Backward Class of Citizens, shall be required to submit, along with the nomination paper, Caste Certificate issued by the Competent Authority and the Validity Certificate issued by the Scrutiny Committee in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, De-notified Tribes (Vimukta Jatis), Nomadic Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Special Backward Category (Regulation of Issuance and Verification of) Caste Certificate Act, 2000.".
Maharashtra Municipal Corporations and Municipal Councils (Amendment) Act, 2006_Section 5--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 10 of the Provincial Corporations Act, sub-sections (1B) and (1C) shall be deleted.
Maharashtra Municipal Corporations and Municipal Councils (Amendment) Act, 2006_Section 6--> State(s): Maharashtra After section 9A of the City of Nagpur Corporation Act, 1948 (hereinafter, in this Chapter, referred to as "the Nagpur Corporation Act"), the following section shall be inserted, namely :- "9AA. Person contesting election for reserved seat to submit Caste Certificate and Validity Certificate:-Every person desirous of contesting election to a seat reserved for the Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes or, as the case may be, Backward Class of Citizens, shall be required to submit, along with the nomination paper, Caste Certificate issued by the Competent Authority and the Validity Certificate issued by the Scrutiny Committee in accordance with the provisions of the Maharashtra Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, De-notified Tribes (Vimukta Jatis), Nomadic Tribes, Other Backward Classes and Special Backward Category (Regulation of Issuance and Verification of) Caste Certificate Act, 2000.".
Maharashtra Municipal Corporations and Municipal Councils (Amendment) Act, 2006_Section 7--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 15 of the Nagpur Corporation Act, sub-sections (2) and (3) shall be deleted.
Maharashtra Municipal Corporations and Municipal Councils (Amendment) Act, 2006_Section 8--> State(s): Maharashtra After section 9 of the Maharashtra Municipal Councils, Nagar Panchayats and Industrial Townships Act, 1965 (hereinafter, in this Chapter, referred to as "the Municipal Councils Act"), the following section shall be inserted, namely :- "9A.
