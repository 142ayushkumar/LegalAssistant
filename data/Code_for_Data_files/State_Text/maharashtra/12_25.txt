Maharashtra Legislative Council (Chairman and Deputy Chairman) and Maharashtra Legislative Assembly (Speaker and Deputy Speaker) Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Legislative Council (Chairman and Deputy Chairman) and Maharashtra Legislative Assembly (Speaker and Deputy Speaker) Salaries and Allowances Act. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Legislative Council (Chairman and Deputy Chairman) and Maharashtra Legislative Assembly (Speaker and Deputy Speaker) Salaries and Allowances Act, for the purpose hereinafter appearing ; It is hereby enacted in the Fifty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Legislative Council (Chairman and Deputy Chairman) and Maharashtra Legislative Assembly (Speaker and Deputy Speaker) Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Maharashtra Legislative Council (Chairman and Deputy Chairman) and Maharashtra Legislative Assembly (Speaker and Deputy Speaker) Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 2000. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force from the 1st day of August, 2000.
Maharashtra Legislative Council (Chairman and Deputy Chairman) and Maharashtra Legislative Assembly (Speaker and Deputy Speaker) Salaries and Allowances (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 4A of the Maharashtra Legislative Council (Chairman and Deputy Chairman) and Maharashtra Legislative Assembly (Speaker and Deputy Speaker) Salaries and Allowances Act, for the words "one lakh rupees" the words "two lakh rupees" shall be substituted.
