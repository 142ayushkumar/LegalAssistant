Maharashtra Appropriation (Vote on Account) Act, 2017_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act to provide for the withdrawal of certain sums from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State for the services of a part of the Financial Year 2017-2018. Whereas it is expedient to provide for the withdrawal of certain sums from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State for the services of a part of the Financial Year 2017-2018; it is hereby enacted in the Sixty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Appropriation (Vote on Account) Act, 2017_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Appropriation (Vote on Account) Act, 2017.
Maharashtra Appropriation (Vote on Account) Act, 2017_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra From and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State, there may be paid and applied sums not exceeding those specified in column (4) of the Schedule hereto annexed amounting in aggregate to the sum of fifty six thousand five hundred fifty five crores, thirty two lakhs seventy eight thousand rupees towards defraying the several charges which will come in course of payment during the year ending on the thirty-first day of March 2018, in respect of the services and purposes specified in column (2) of the Schedule.
Maharashtra Appropriation (Vote on Account) Act, 2017_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra The sums authorised to be paid and applied from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State by this Act shall be appropriated for the services and purposes expressed in the Schedule in relation to the year ending on the thirty-first day of March 2018.
