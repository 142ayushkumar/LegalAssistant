Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2005.
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 2 of the Maharashtra Land Revenue Code, 1966 (hereinafter referred to as " the said Code "), after clause (33), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(33A) " storage device" means an Electronic Device for retention of data in computer and shall include both hardware and software; ".
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra After section 148 of the said Code, the following section shall be inserted, namely:- "148A.Maintenance of record of rights etc., by using suitable storage device:- The record of rights maintained under section 148 and the land records maintained under the other provisions of this Chapter may also be so maintained by using a suitable storage device.".
Maharashtra Land Revenue Code (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 150 of the said Code, after sub-section (7), the following sub-section shall be added, namely:- "(8) The Commissioner may specify, from time to time, the storage device for preparation, maintenance and updation of all registers and documents to be maintained under section 148A. ".
