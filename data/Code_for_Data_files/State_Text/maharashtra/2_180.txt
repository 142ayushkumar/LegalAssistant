Bombay Steam-Vessels Act, 1864_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra [Survey of Steam-vessels.] Rep. Act VI of 1884.
Bombay Steam-Vessels Act, 1864_Section 15--> State(s): Maharashtra It is hereby declared that sections 292, 293 and 294 of the Merchant Shipping Act (17 & 18 Vict., cap 104)1 , and Schedule S of that Act are and shall be applicable to all steam-vessels proceeding to sea from any port or place in the Presidency of Bombay or its Dependencies, and that, in calculating the penalties mentioned in the said section 293, ten rupees be taken as the equivalent for one pound sterling. See now the Merchant Shipping Act, 1894 (57 & 58 Vict., cap. 60), Collection of Statutes relating to India, Vol. II.
