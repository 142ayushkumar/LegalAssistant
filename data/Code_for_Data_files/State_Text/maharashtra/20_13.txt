Maharashtra Universities (Second Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra 1 An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Universities Act. 1994 Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Universities Act, 1994 (Mah. XXXV of 1994), for the purposes hereinafter appearing: It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:- The following Act of the Maharashtra Legislature, received the assent of the Governor on 12th August, 2011 and was published in the Maharashtra Govt. Gazette, Extra-ordinary, Part VIII, dated 12th August, 2011.
Maharashtra Universities (Second Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Universities (Second Amendment) Act, 2011.
Maharashtra Universities (Second Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 82 of the Maharashtra Universities Act, 1994 (Mah. XXXV of 1994). after sub-section (5), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely:- "(5A) Notwithstanding anything contained in the second proviso to sub-section (5), for the academic year 2011-2012, such permission from the State Government shall be communicated to the university on or before 20th August 2011 and shall be given effect by the university in the same academic year.".
