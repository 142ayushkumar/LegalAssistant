Maharashtra Devdasi System (Abolition) (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act to amend the Maharashtra Devdasi System (Abolition) Act, 2005. Whereas it is expedient to amend the Maharashtra Devdasi System (Abolition) Act, 2005 (Mah. XXXIII of 2006), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Sixty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Devdasi System (Abolition) (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Devdasi System (Abolition) (Amendment) Act, 2013.1 Received the assent of the Governor on 21st August, 2013 and was published in the Maharashtra Govt. Gazette, Extra-ordinary, Part VIII, dated 21st August, 2013.
Maharashtra Devdasi System (Abolition) (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 5 of the Maharashtra Devdasi System (Abolition) Act, 2005 (Mah. XXXIII of 2006) (hereinafter referred to as " the principal Act"), in sub-section (2), for clause (a), the following clause shall be substituted, namely: - "(a) a person, who is or has been a Judge not below the rank of a Selection Grade District Judge, to be appointed by the Government in consultation with the High Court, or a person who is or has been an officer not below the rank of a Secretary to the Government, to be appointed by the Government Chairman;".
Maharashtra Devdasi System (Abolition) (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 8 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), for clause (a), the following clause shall be substituted, namely: - "(a) the Chief Judicial Magistrate or Additional Collector or Chief Executive Officer of the Zilla Parishad or the Superintendent of Police, of the District, or any of the Districts, for which a common District Committee is constituted, to be appointed by the Government Chairman;".
