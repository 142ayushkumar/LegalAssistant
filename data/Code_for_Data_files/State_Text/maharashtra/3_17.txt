Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra 1 An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960. Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960 (Mah. XXIV of 1961), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Sixty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows :- The following Act of the Maharashtra Legislature, received the assent of the Governor on 14th January, 2011 and was published in the Maharashtra Govt. Gazette, Extra-ordinary, dated 14th January, 2011.
Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2010.
Maharashtra Co-operative Societies (Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 73 of the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act, 1960, in sub-section (a-AB), for the portion beginning with the words "Every such member shall execute" and ending with the words "by the State Government by general or special order", the following shall be substituted, namely:- "Every such member,- (i) in case of housing societies, within forty-five days of his assuming the office; and (ii) in case of other societies, within fifteen days of his assuming the office; shall execute a bond to that effect, in the form as specified by the State Government by general or special order.".
