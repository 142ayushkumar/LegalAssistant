Bombay Sales of Motor Spirit Taxation (Amendment and Continuance) Act, 1999_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Bombay Sales of Motor Spirit Taxation Act, 1958. WHEREAS the Governor of Maharashtra promulgated the Bombay Sales of Motor Spirit Taxation (Amendment) Ordinance, 1999, on the 4th October, 1999 (2000 118 STC Statutes 59 (hereinafter referred to as "the said Ordinance"); AND WHEREAS upon the re-assembly of the State Legislature on the 22nd October, 1999, a Bill for converting the said Ordinance into an Act of the State Legislature could not be introduced in the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly for want of time as the session of the State Legislature prorogued on the 23rd October, 1999; AND WHEREAS as provided by article 213(2)(a) of the Constitution of India, the said Ordinance would have ceased to operate after the 3rd December, 1999, the date on which the period of six weeks from the date of re-assembly of the State Legislature was to expire; AND WHEREAS it was considered expedient to continue the operation of the provisions of the said Ordinance by law; AND WHEREAS both Houses of the State Legislature were not in session and the Governor of Maharashtra was satisfied that circumstances existed which rendered it necessary for him to take immediate action to continue the operation of the provisions of the said Ordinance, for the purposes hereinafter appearing and therefore, the said Ordinance was repealed by withdrawal and the Bombay Sales of Motor Spirit Taxation (Amendment and Continuance) Ordinance, 1999 was promulgated on the 2nd December, 1999, with retrospective effect, that is from the 4th October, 1999. AND WHEREAS it is expedient to replace the Bombay Sales of Motor Spirit Taxation (Amendment and Continuance) Ordinance, 1999, by an Act of the State Legislature; It is hereby enacted in the Fiftieth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Bombay Sales of Motor Spirit Taxation (Amendment and Continuance) Act, 1999_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Bombay Sales of Motor Spirit Taxation (Amendment and Continuance) Act, 1999. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 4th October, 1999.
Bombay Sales of Motor Spirit Taxation (Amendment and Continuance) Act, 1999_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 5 of the Bombay Sales of Motor Spirit Taxation Act, 1958 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), in sub-section (2), - (a) for the words and figures "not exceeding 30 per cent.", the words and figures "not exceeding 40 per cent." shall be substituted; (b) after the words "different kinds of motor spirit" the words "and for different areas" shall be added at the end.
Bombay Sales of Motor Spirit Taxation (Amendment and Continuance) Act, 1999_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) The Bombay Sales of Motor Spirit Taxation (Amendment and Continuance) Ordinance, 1999, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken, as the case may be, under the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
