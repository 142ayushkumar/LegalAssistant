Electricity (Supply) (Maharashtra Amendment) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Electricity (Supply) Act, 1948, in its application to the State of Maharashtra. WHEREAS both Houses of the State Legislature were not in session; AND WHEREAS the Governor of Maharashtra was satisfied that circumstances existed which rendered it necessary for him to take immediate action further to amend the Electricity (Supply) Act, 1948, in its application to State of Maharashtra, for the purposes hereinafter appearing, and, therefore, promulgated the Electricity (Supply) (Maharashtra Amendment) Ordinance, 2000, on the 13th July, 2000; AND WHEREAS it is expedient to replace the said Ordinance by an Act of the State Legislature ; It is hereby enacted in the Fifty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Electricity (Supply) (Maharashtra Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Electricity (Supply) (Maharashtra Amendment) Act, 2000. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 13th July, 2000.
Electricity (Supply) (Maharashtra Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 8 of the Electricity (Supply) Act, 1948, in its application to the State of Maharashtra (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), after the words "The Chairman and other members of the Board shall", the following shall be inserted, namely :- "subject to the pleasure of the State Government.".
Electricity (Supply) (Maharashtra Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) The Electricity (Supply) (Maharashtra Amendment) Ordinance, 2000, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the principal Act, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken, as the case may be, under the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
