Maharashtra Live-stock Improvement (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Live-stock Improvement Act. Whereas, it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Live-stock Improvement Act (XXII of 1933), for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Sixty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Live-stock Improvement (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Live-stock Improvement (Amendment) Act, 2013.1 Received the assent of the Governor on 13th August, 2013 and was published in the Maharashtra Govt. Gazette, Extra-ordinary, Part VIII, dated 13th August, 2013.
Maharashtra Live-stock Improvement (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 6 of the Maharashtra Live-stock Improvement Act (XXII of 1933), after the first proviso, the following proviso shall be added, namely:- "Provided further that, every licence for the keeping of a bull in the Scheduled Areas referred to in clause (1) of article 244 of the Constitution of India shall be granted by such live-stock officer, after consultation with the concerned Village Panchayat.".
