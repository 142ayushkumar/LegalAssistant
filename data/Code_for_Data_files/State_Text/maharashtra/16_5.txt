Maharashtra Paramedical Council (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act to amend the Maharashtra Paramedical Council Act, 2011. Whereas it is expedient to amend the Maharashtra Paramedical Council Act, 2011, for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Sixty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Paramedical Council (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Maharashtra Paramedical Council (Amendment) Act, 2017. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification in the Official Gazette, appoint.
Maharashtra Paramedical Council (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In the long title of the Maharashtra Paramedical Council Act, 2011 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), for the words "paramedical practitioners" the words "paramedical practicing personnel" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Paramedical Council (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In the preamble to the principal Act, for the words "paramedical practitioners" the words "paramedical practicing personnel" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Paramedical Council (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 2 of the principal Act,- (a) after clause (c), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(c-a) "paramedical practicing personnel" means the person engaged in the provision of services ancillary to or required as assistance in the teaching or practice or both of the modern scientific medicine or Ayurvedic system or Unani system or Homeopathic system of medicine, as their principal occupation; "; (b) for clause (i), the following clause shall be substituted, namely:- "(i) "registered paramedical practicing personnel" means the paramedical practicing personnel registered under section 26; ".
Maharashtra Paramedical Council (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 5--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 4 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1),- (a) after clause (v), the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(v-a) The President of the Maharashtra Council of Indian Medicine, ex-officio member;"; (b) in clause (vii), for the words "paramedical practitioners" the words "paramedical practicing personnel" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Paramedical Council (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 6--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 19 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2),- (a) in clause (d), for the words "paramedical practitioners" the words "paramedical practicing personnel" shall be substituted; (b) in clause (e), for the words "paramedical practitioner" the words "paramedical practicing personnel" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Paramedical Council (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 7--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 22 of the principal Act,- (a) in sub-section (3), for the words "paramedical practitioners" the words "paramedical practicing personnel" shall be substituted; (b) in sub-section (4), for the words "paramedical practitioners", at both the places where they occur, the words "paramedical practicing personnel" shall be substituted; (c) in sub-section (5), for the words "paramedical practitioner" the words "paramedical practicing personnel" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Paramedical Council (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 8--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 26 of the principal Act,- (a) in sub-section (1), for the words "paramedical practitioners" the words "paramedical practicing personnel" shall be substituted; (b) in sub-section (2), for the words "paramedical practititoner" the words "paramedical practicing personnel" shall be substituted; (c) in sub-section (3), for the words "paramedical practitioner" the words "paramedical practicing personnel" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Paramedical Council (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 9--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 28 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), for the words "paramedical practitioner" the words "paramedical practicing personnel" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Paramedical Council (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 10--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 31 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), for the words "paramedical practitioner", at both the places where they occur, the words "paramedical practicing personnel" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Paramedical Council (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 11--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 32 of the principal Act, for the words "paramedical practitioner", at both the places where they occur, the words "paramedical practicing personnel" shall be substituted.
