Shree Siddhi Vinayak Ganpati Temple Trust (Prabhadevi) (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Shree Siddhi Vinayak Ganpati Temple Trust (Prabhadevi) Act, 1980. WHEREAS, both Houses of the State Legislature were not in session; AND WHEREAS, the Governor of Maharashtra was satisfied that circumstances existed which rendered it necessary for him to take immediate action further to amend the Shree Siddhi Vinayak Ganpati Temple Trust (Prabhadevi) Act, 1980; and, therefore, promulgated, the Shree Siddhi Vinayak Ganpati Temple Trust (Prabhadevi) (Amendment) Ordinance, 2003, on the 3rd November 2003; AND WHEREAS, it is expedient to replace the said Ordinance by an Act of the State Legislature ; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Shree Siddhi Vinayak Ganpati Temple Trust (Prabhadevi) (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) This Act may be called the Shree Siddhi Vinayak Ganpati Temple Trust (Prabhadevi) (Amendment) Act, 2003. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on the 3rd November 2003.
Shree Siddhi Vinayak Ganpati Temple Trust (Prabhadevi) (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 18 of the Shree Siddhi Vinayak Ganpati Temple Trust (Prabhadevi) Act, 1980 in sub-section (2), after the existing proviso, the following proviso shall be added, namely :- "Provided further that, if the Committee is satisfied that the patient requires urgent financial aid to meet the medical expenses of emergent nature, it may sanction financial aid of an amount upto rupees twenty five thousand without previous sanction of the State Government; and such amount shall be paid directly, by cheque, in the name of the concerned hospital. The Executive Officer shall, within three days from such payment, prepare a detailed report and forward the same along with a copy of the necessary medical certificate and a copy of the resolution of the Committee to the State Government for information.".
Shree Siddhi Vinayak Ganpati Temple Trust (Prabhadevi) (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra (1) The Shree Siddhi Vinayak Ganpati Temple Trust (Prabhadevi) (Amendment) Ordinance, 2003, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the Shree Siddhi Vinayak Ganpati Temple Trust (Prabhadevi) Act, 1980, as amended by the said Ordinance, shall be deemed to have been done or taken, as the case may be, under the Shree Siddhi Vinayak Ganpati Temple Trust (Prabhadevi) Act, 1980, as amended by this Act.
