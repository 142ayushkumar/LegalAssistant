Maharashtra Slum Areas (Improvement, Clearance and Redevelopment) (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Slum Areas (Improvement, Clearance and Redevelopment) Act, 1971. WHEREAS it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Slum Areas (Improvement, Clearance and Redevelopment) Act, 1971, for the purpose hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra Slum Areas (Improvement, Clearance and Redevelopment) (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Slum Areas (Improvement, Clearance and Redevelopment) (Amendment) Act, 2005.
Maharashtra Slum Areas (Improvement, Clearance and Redevelopment) (Amendment) Act, 2005_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 2 of the Maharashtra Slum Areas (Improvement, Clearance and Redevelopment) Act, 1971, in clause (i), in sub-clause (a), in para (ii), for the words "ten years" the words "eight years" shall be substituted.
