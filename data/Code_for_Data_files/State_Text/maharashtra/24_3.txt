Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act further to amend the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961. WHEREAS, it is expedient further to amend the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961 for the purposes hereinafter appearing; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-fourth Year of the Republic of India, as follows:-
Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2003.
Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 144 of the Maharashtra Zilia Parishads and Panchayat Samitis Act, 1961 (hereinafter referred to as "the principal Act"), for the words "determined by the State Government" the words "determined by the concerned Divisional Commissioner" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 151 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), for the words "by the State Government" the words "by the concerned Divisional Commissioner" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 4--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 152 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), for the words "by the State Government" the words "by the concerned Divisional Commissioner" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 5--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 153 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), for the words "paid by the State Government" the words "paid by the Collector" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 6--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 155 of the principal Act,- (a) In sub-section (1), for the words "State Government" at both the places where they occur, the words "concerned Divisional Commissioner" shall be substituted; (b) in sub-section (2), for the words "State Government" the words "concerned Divisional Commissioner" shall be substituted; (c) after sub-section (3), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely :- "(3A) The approval to utilise the cess collected, on works and development schemes in respect of the subjects of activities enumerated in the First and Second Schedules shall be given by the concerned Divisional Commissioner and the approval to utilise the cess collected, on works and development schemes other than those enumerated in the First and Second Schedules, shall be given by the State Government."; (d) in sub-section (4), for the words "State Government may, notwithstanding anything contained in the relevant Code, by notification in the Official Gazette" the words "concerned Divisional Commissioner may, notwithstanding anything contained in the relevant Code, by district local publicity media" shall be substituted; (e) in sub-section (5),- (i) for the words "determined by the State Government" the words "determined by the concerned Divisional Commissioner" shall be substituted; (ii) for the words "paid by the State Government" the words "paid by the Collector to the Zilla Parishad" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 7--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 156 of the principal Act, in sub-section (4), for the words "paid by the State Government to the Zilla Parishad concerned" the words "paid by the Collector to the Zilla Parishad" shall be substituted.
Maharashtra Zilla Parishads and Panchayat Samitis (Amendment) Act, 2003_Section 8--> State(s): Maharashtra In section 158 of the principal Act,- (a) for sub-section (3), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely :- "(3) The Joint District Registrar and the Collector of Stamps of a District shall, pay to the Zilla Parishad an amount, equal to the extra duty realised under sub-section (1) in respect of the properties situated within the jurisdiction of the Zilla Parishad, after deducting such proportion thereof as cost of collection, as the State Government may specify in the order made in this behalf."; (b) in sub-section (4), the words "of grant-in-aid" shall be deleted; (c) sub-section (5) shall be deleted.
