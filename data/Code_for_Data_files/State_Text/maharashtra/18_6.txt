Maharashtra (Supplementary) Appropriation Act, 2006_Section Preamble--> State(s): Maharashtra An Act to authorise payment and appropriation of certain further sums from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State for the services of the year ending on the Thirty-first day of March 2006. Whereas by virtue of article 204 of the Constitution of India, read with article 205 thereof, it is necessary to provide for the passing of an Appropriation Act for the appropriation of further sums from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State for the services of the year ending on the thirty-first day of March 2006; and for the purpose of authorising payment of the said sums; it is hereby enacted in the Fifty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Maharashtra (Supplementary) Appropriation Act, 2006_Section 1--> State(s): Maharashtra This Act may be called the Maharashtra (Supplementary) Appropriation Act, 2006.
Maharashtra (Supplementary) Appropriation Act, 2006_Section 2--> State(s): Maharashtra From and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State, there may be paid and applied sums not exceeding those specified in column (4) of the Schedule hereto annexed amounting in aggregate to the sum of seven thousand three hundred ninety-three crores, fifteen lakhs and thirty-five thousand rupees only towards defraying the State for the several charges which will come in course of payment during the year ending on the thirty-first day of March 2006, in respect of the services and purposes specified in column (2) of the Schedule.
Maharashtra (Supplementary) Appropriation Act, 2006_Section 3--> State(s): Maharashtra The sums authorised to be paid and applied from and out of the Consolidated Fund of the State by this Act shall be appropriated for the services and purposes expressed in the Schedule in relation to the year ending on the thirty-first day of March 2006.
