Jharkhand Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section Preamble--> State(s): Jharkhand An Act to amend Jharkhand Value Added Tax Act, 2005 (Jharkhand Act 05, 2006). Be it enacted by Legislature of Jharkhand in the Sixty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Jharkhand Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 1--> State(s): Jharkhand (i) This Act shall be called the Jharkhand Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2017. (ii) It shall extend to the whole of the State of Jharkhand. (iii) It shall be deemed to have come into force w.e.f. 23 September, 2015.
Jharkhand Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 2--> State(s): Jharkhand The amendments, as made in Jharkhand Value Added Tax Act (Jharkhand Act 05, 2006) and as amended in sub-section (3) and sub-section (4) of section 18, by Jharkhand Act 10, 2015 dated 23 September, 2015, and Jharkhand Act 05, 2016 dated 9 February, 2016, shall be deemed to effective from 23 September, 2015.
Jharkhand Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 3--> State(s): Jharkhand The amendments as made in Jharkhand Value Added Tax Act (Jharkhand Act 05, 2006) and as amended in clause (ix) of sub-section (8) of section 18, and in sub-section (10) of section 18, and insertion of clause (xviii) in sub-section (8) of section 18, by Jharkhand Act 10, 2015 dated 23 September, 2015, shall deemed to be effective from 23 September, 2015.
Jharkhand Value Added Tax (Amendment) Act, 2017_Section 4--> State(s): Jharkhand The amendments as made in Jharkhand Value Added Tax Act (Jharkhand Act 05, 2006) and a proviso as inserted in clause (iii) of sub-section (4) of section 18, by Jharkhand Act 05, 2016 dated 9 February, 2016, shall be deemed to effective from 23 September, 2015.
