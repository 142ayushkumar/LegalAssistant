Administrators-General (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 2010_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh 1 An Act further to amend the Administrators-General Act, 1963 in its application to Uttar Pradesh It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows:- Received the assent of the President on August 25, 2011 and published in the U.P. Gazette, Extra., Part 1. Section (Ka), dated 7th September, 2011, pp. 2-3.
Administrators-General (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Administrators-General (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 2010.
Administrators-General (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 2010_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In Section 29 of the Administrators-General Act, 1963, in sub-section (1) for the words "two lakhs rupees" wherever occurring the words "ten lakh* rupees" shall be substituted.
