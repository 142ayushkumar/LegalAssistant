Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax (Amendment) Act, 1965_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT further to amend the U. P. Entertainments and Betting Tax Act, 1937 for the purpose hereinafter appearing. IT IS HEREBY enacted in the Sixteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax (Amendment) Act, 1965_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax (Amendment) Act, 1965.
Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax (Amendment) Act, 1965_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh For sub-section (1) of section 3 of the U. P. Entertainments and Betting Tax Act, 1937, the following shall be substituted, namely:- "(1) There shall be levied and paid on all payments for admission to any entertainment a tax (hereinafter referred to as entertainment tax) at a rate not exceeding seventy-five per cent of the payment for admission as the Government may from time to time specify by notification in this behalf, and the tax shall be collected by the proprietor and be paid to the Government in the manner prescribed."
