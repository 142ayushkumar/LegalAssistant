Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (As passed by the Uttar Pradesh Legislature) An Act further to amend the United Provinces Excise Act, 1910. IT is Hereby enacted in the Fifty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 2000. (2) It shall come into force on April 1, 2000.
Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 28 of the United Provinces Excise Act, 1910, hereinafter referred to as the principal Act, in sub-section (3), in each of the clauses (a), (c), (d) and (e) for the figure and words "Rs.15 per litre" occuring against item 2 (a) in the fable, the figure and words "Rs. 30 per litre" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) The uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Ordinance, 2000 is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the provisions of the principal Act as amended by the Ordinance referred to in subjection (1), shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act as amended by this Act as if this Act were in force at all material tines. By order, Y. R. TRIPATHI, Pramukh Sachiv.
