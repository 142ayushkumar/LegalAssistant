Gaon Panchayats and Kshettra Samitis (Extension of Term) Act, 1971_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh An Act further to amend the U.P.Panchayat Raj Act, 1947 and the Uttar Pradesh Kshettra Samitis and Zila Parishads Adhiniyam, 1961 It is hereby enacted in the Twenty-Second Year of the Republic of India as follows :
Gaon Panchayats and Kshettra Samitis (Extension of Term) Act, 1971_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Gaon Panchayats and Kshettra Samitis (Extension of Term) Act, 1971.1 Published in U.P. Gazette, dated 22nd August, 1971, at 2.
Gaon Panchayats and Kshettra Samitis (Extension of Term) Act, 1971_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh Amendment incorporated in the relevant Act.
Gaon Panchayats and Kshettra Samitis (Extension of Term) Act, 1971_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh Amendment incorporated in the relevant Act.
Gaon Panchayats and Kshettra Samitis (Extension of Term) Act, 1971_Section 4--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh The Uttar Pradesh Gaon Panchayats and Kshettra Samitis (Extension of Term) Ordinance, 1971 is hereby repealed.
