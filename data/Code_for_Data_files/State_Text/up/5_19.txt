Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT further to amend the Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax Act, 1979. IT IS HEREBY enacted in the Forty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax (Amendment) Act, 1995. (2) Section 2 and section 3 shall be deemed to have come into force on October 10, 1994, section 4 shall be deemed to have come into force on January 3, 1995, and the remaining provisions shall come into force at once.
Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 8 of the Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax Act, 1979, hereinafter referred to as the principal Act, for sub-section (3), the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely:-"(3) Where the payment for admission to an entertainment, together with the tax, charges for maintenance of cinema premises and extra charge for air cooling or air conditioning facility, is not a multiple of fifty paise, then notwithstanding anything contained in sub-section (1) or sub-section (2) or any notification issued thereunder, the tax shall be increased to such extent and be so computed that the aggregate of such payment for admission to entertainment, the tax, charge for maintenance of cinema premises and extra charge for air cooling or air conditioning facility Is rounded off to the next higher multiple of fifty paise and such Increased tax shall also be collected by the proprietor and paid to the Government in the manner prescribed."
Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 3-A of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), in clause (a), for the words "twenty-five paise" the words "One rupee" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 4--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 12 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1) for the words "two thousand rupees" the words "ten thousand rupees" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 5--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) The Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax (Amendment) Ordinance, 1994 and the Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax (Amendment) Ordinance, 1995 are hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the provisions of the principal Act, as amended by the Ordinances referred to in sub-section (1), shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act as amended by this Act, as if the provisions of this Act were in force at all material times.
