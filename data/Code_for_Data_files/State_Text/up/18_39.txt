Societies Registration (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh An Act further to amend the Societies Registration Act, 1860 in its application to Uttar Pradesh It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows-
Societies Registration (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Societies Registration (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 2011.1 Received the assent of the President on September 28, 2013 and published in the U.P. Gazette, Extra., Part 1, Section (Ka), dated 9th October, 2013
Societies Registration (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh After Section 4-A of the Societies Registration Act, 1860 as amended in its application to Uttar Pradesh the following section shall be inserted, namely- "4B(1) At the time of registration/renewal of a society, list of members of General Body of that society shall be filed with the Registrar mentioning the name, father's name, address and occupation of the members. The Registrar shall examine the correctness of the list of members of the General Body of such society on the basis of the register of members of the General Body and minutes book thereof, cash book, receipt book of membership fee and bank pass book of the society. (2) If there is any change in the list of members of the General Body of the society referred to in sub-section (1), on account of induction, removal, registration or death of any member, a modified list of members of General Body, shall be filed with the Registrar, within one month from the date of change. (3) The list of members of the General Body to be filed with the Registrar under this section shall be signed by two office bearers and two executive members of the society."
