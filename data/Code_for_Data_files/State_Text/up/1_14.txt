Uttar Pradesh Antanim Zila Parishad (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1963_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT to amend the Uttar Pradesh Antarim Zila Parishad Act, 1958. WHEREAS the Uttar Pradesh Antarim Zila Parishad (Sanshodhan) Adhyadesh, 1962, was promulgated by the Governor on December 31, 1962, to amend the Uttar Pradesh Antanim Zila Parishad Act, 1958 ; AND WHEREAS it is expedient and necessary that the said Ordinance be replaced by an Act of the Legislature ; IT is hereby enacted in the Fourteenth year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Antanim Zila Parishad (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1963_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Antanim Zila Parishad (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1963.
Uttar Pradesh Antanim Zila Parishad (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1963_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In sub-section (3) of section 1 of the Uttar Pradesh Antarim Zila Parishad Act, 1958, for the words and figures "31st day of December, 1962", the words and figures "30th day of June, 1963, except in the case of Uttarakhand Division where it shall expire on the 31st day of December, 1967" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Antanim Zila Parishad (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1963_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh The Uttar Pradesh Antarim Zila Parishad (Sanshodhan) Adhyadesh, 1962, is hereby repealed and the provisions of sections 6 and 24 of the U. P. General Clauses Act, 1904, shall apply as if it were an enactment repealed and re-enacted by an Uttar Pradesh Act.
