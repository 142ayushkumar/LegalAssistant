Uttar Pradesh State Universities (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh An Act further to amend the Uttar Pradesh State Universities Act, 1973 It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:- Statement of Object With a view to contributions made by Manayavar Shri Kanshi Ram Ji for the upliftment of the country and the society, it has been decided to rename the Uttar Pradesh Urdu Arabi Pharsi University, Lucknow as the Manayavar Shri Kanshi Ram Ji Urdu Arabi Pharsi University, Lucknow to commemorate Manayavar Shri Kanshi Ram Ji. The Uttar Pradesh State University (Amendment) Bill, 2011 is introduced accordingly.
Uttar Pradesh State Universities (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh State Universities (Amendment) Act, 2011.
Uttar Pradesh State Universities (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In Section 4 of the Uttar Pradesh State Universities Act, 1973 herein referred to as the principal Act, in sub-section (1-A), in clause (e) for the words "Uttar Pradesh" the words "Manayavar Shri Kanshi Ram Ji" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh State Universities (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In Section 5 of the principal Act, in sub-section (7) for the words "Uttar Pradesh" the words "Manayavar Shri Kanshi Ram Ji" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh State Universities (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 4--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In Section 7-B of the principal Act for the words "Uttar Pradesh" the words "Manayavar Shri Kanshi Ram Ji" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh State Universities (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 5--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In the schedule to the principal Act, in the entry appearing at Serial No. 11, in Column 2 for the words "Uttar Pradesh" the words "Manayavar Shri Kanshi Ram Ji" shall be substituted.
