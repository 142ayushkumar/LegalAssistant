Uttar Pradesh State Commission for Women (Repeal) Act, 2004_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT to repeal the Uttar Pradesh State Commission for Women Act, 2001. It is hereby enacted in the Fifty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows:- Statement of Object The Uttar Pradesh State Commission for Women Act, 2001 (U.P. Act no. 34 of 2001) was been enacted to establish a State Commission for Women. The said Commission was not serving the purpose properly due to which it had last its utility. It was, therefore decided to repeal the said Act to abolish the existing State Commission for Women. Since the State Legislature was not in session and immediate legislative action was necessary to implement the aforesaid decision, the Uttar Pradesh State Commission for Women (Repeal) Ordinance, 2004 (U. P. Ordinance no. 2 of 2004) was promulgated by the Governor on January 20, 2004. This Bill is introduced to replace the aforesaid Ordinance.
Uttar Pradesh State Commission for Women (Repeal) Act, 2004_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh State Commission for Women (Repeal) Act, 2004. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on January 20, 2004.
Uttar Pradesh State Commission for Women (Repeal) Act, 2004_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh The Uttar Pradesh State Commission for Women Act, 2001 is hereby repealed.
Uttar Pradesh State Commission for Women (Repeal) Act, 2004_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh The Uttar Pradesh State Commission for Women (Repeal) Ordinance, 2004 is hereby repealed. By order, R. B. RAO
