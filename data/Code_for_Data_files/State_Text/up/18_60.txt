Uttar Pradesh State Law Commission (Repeal) Act, 2012_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh An Act to repeal the Uttar Pradesh State Law Commission Act, 2010. It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows-
Uttar Pradesh State Law Commission (Repeal) Act, 2012_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh State Law Commission (Repeal) Act, 20121 . 2. It shall be deemed to have come into force on September 11, 2012. Received the assent of the Governor on March 26, 2013 and published in the U.P. Gazette, Extra., Part 1, Section (Ka), dated 28th March, 2013
Uttar Pradesh State Law Commission (Repeal) Act, 2012_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh The Uttar Pradesh State Law Commission Act, 2010 is hereby repealed.
Uttar Pradesh State Law Commission (Repeal) Act, 2012_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh The Uttar Pradesh State Law Commission (Repeal) Ordinance, 2012 is hereby repealed.
