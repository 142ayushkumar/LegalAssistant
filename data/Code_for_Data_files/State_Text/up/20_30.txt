United Provinces Expiring Laws Continuance Act, 1948_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh 1 An Act to continue certain expiring laws promulgated by His Excellency the Governor of the United Provinces by virtue of Section 93 of the Government of India Act, 1935 Whereas the Acts mentioned in the schedule (hereinafter called "Principal Act") were promulgated by his Excellency the Governor of the United Provinces by virtue of the Proclamation under Section 93 of the Government of India Act, 1935, dated November 3, 1939, as amended from time to time ; And whereas the principal Acts are, in so far as they are in force and are temporary in their duration, limited to expire on March 31, 1948 ; And whereas it is expedient to provide for the continuance, as in this Act mentioned as the principal Act and of the enactments amending and affecting the same : It is hereby enacted as follows ;- Received the assent of the Governor-General on March 30, 1948, under Section 76 of the Government of India Act, 1935, as adapted by the India (Provisional Constitution) Order, 1947, and was published in the United Provinces Gazette U.P., Extraordinary, dated April 1, 1948.
United Provinces Expiring Laws Continuance Act, 1948_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the United Provinces Expiring Laws Continuance Act, 1948.
United Provinces Expiring Laws Continuance Act, 1948_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) The principal Acts mentioned in column 1 of the schedule shall, subject to the modification and to the extent specified in column 2, be permanent Acts. (2) Any unrepeated enactments which are temporary in their duration shall, in so far as they amend or affect the principal Act continued by the foregoing provision of this Act, be continued in like manner whether they are mentioned in the schedule or not.
