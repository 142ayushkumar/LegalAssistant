Uttar Pradesh Antarim Zila Parishad (Dwiteeya Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1963_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT to amend the Uttar Pradesh Antarim Zila Parishad Act, 1958 It is hereby enacted in the Fourteenth year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Antarim Zila Parishad (Dwiteeya Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1963_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Antarim Zila Parishad (Dwiteeya Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1963.
Uttar Pradesh Antarim Zila Parishad (Dwiteeya Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1963_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In sub-section (3) of section 1 of the Uttar Pradesh Antarim Zila Parishad Act, 1958, the fullstop at the end shall be deleted and thereafter the following shall be added :- "and Unnao District where it shall expire on the 31st day of December, 1963."
Uttar Pradesh Antarim Zila Parishad (Dwiteeya Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1963_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) The Uttar Pradesh Antarim Zila Parishad (Sanshodhan) Adhyadesh, 1963 is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the said Adhyadesh shall be deemed to have been done or taken under this Act, as if this Act had commenced on the 29th day of June, 1963.
