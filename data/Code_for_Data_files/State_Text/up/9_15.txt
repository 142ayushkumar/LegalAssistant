Indian Partnership (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh An Act further to amend the Indian Partnership Act, 1932 in its application to Uttar Pradesh It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows-
Indian Partnership (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Indian Partnership (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 20111 . (2) It extends to the whole of the Uttar Pradesh. Received the assent of the President on January 11, 2013 and published in the U.P. Gazette, Extra., Part 1, Section (Ka), dated 28th January, 2013
