United Provinces Board of Revenue Act, 1922_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh An Act to transfer to the Local Government or to some other authority certain non-judicial powers now exercisable by the board of Revenue under certain enactments. Whereas it is expedient to transfer to the local Government or to one other authority certain non-judicial powers now excersiable by the Board of Revenue under certain enactment and whereas the Governor-General has given his previous sanction to the passing of this Act, as required by sub-section (3) of Sec 80-A of the Government of India Act:
United Provinces Board of Revenue Act, 1922_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the United Provinces Board of Revenue Act, 1922. (2) It extends to the territories 1 (xxx) administered by the 2 [State Government] of 3 [Uttar Pradesh]. Note.-This Act has been excluded to Rampur by Sec. 8 and Sch. of the Rampur (Application of Laws) Act XII of 1960 from December 30, 1949, and to Banaras and Tehri-Garhwal by Banaras and Tehri-Garhwal (Application of Laws), Order 1949 , from November 30, 1949. (3) It shall come into force on such day as the 4 [State Government] may, by notification in the 5 [Official Gazette] appointed in this behalf. Note.- The Act came into force on April 1923, vide Notification No. 913/1-418, dated March 29, 1923, vide Gazette 1923, part. 1, p. 355. The words "for the time being" omitted by A.O- 1937.
