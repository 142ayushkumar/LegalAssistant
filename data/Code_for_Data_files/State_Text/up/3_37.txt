Code of Criminal Procedure (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1978_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh 1 An Act further to amend the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 in its application to Uttar Pradesh Received the assent of the President on October 5, 1978, published in U. P. Gazette, Extra., dated 9th October, 1978, p. 2.
Code of Criminal Procedure (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Code of Criminal Procedure (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1978. (2) It extends to the whole of Uttar Pradesh.
Code of Criminal Procedure (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In Section 24 of the Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973- (a) in sub-section (1), after the words ' Public Prosecutor" the words "and one or more Additional Public Prosecutors" shall be inserted and be deemed always to have been inserted; (b) after sub-section (6), the following sub-section shall be inserted and be deemed always to have been inserted, namely : "(7) For the purposes of sub-sections (5) and (6), the period during which a person has been in practice as a pleader, or has rendered service as a Public Prosecutor, Additional Public Prosecutor or Assistant Public Prosecutor shall be deemed to be the period during which such person has been in practice as an advocate."
