Uttar Pradesh Secondary Education Services Selection Board (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT further to amend the Uttar Pradesh Secondary Education Services Selection Board Act, 1982. It is hereby enacted in the Fiftieth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Secondary Education Services Selection Board (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Secondary Education Services Selection Board (Amendment) Act, 1999. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on January 25,1999.
Uttar Pradesh Secondary Education Services Selection Board (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh After section 33-D of the Uttar Pradesh Secondary Education Services Selection Board Act, 1982, hereinafter referred to as the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely:- "33-E.Reseission of orders:-The Uttar Pradesh Secondary Education Services Commisiion (Removal of Difficulties) Order, 1981 the Uttar Pradesh Secondary Education Services Commission (Removal of Difficulties) (Second) Order, 1981, the Uttar Pradesh Secondary Education Services Commission (Removal of Difficulties (Third) Order, 1982 and the Uttar Pradesh Secondary Education Services Commission (Removal of Difficulties) (Fourth) Order,1982 are hereby rescinded."
Uttar Pradesh Secondary Education Services Selection Board (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) The Uttar Pradesh Secondary Education Services Selection Board (Amendment) Ordinance, 1999 is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the provisions of the principal Act as amended by the Ordinance referred to in sub-section (1) shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act as amended by this Act as if the provisions of this Act were in force at all material times.
