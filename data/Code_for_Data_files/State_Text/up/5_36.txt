Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (As passed by the Uttar Pradesh legislature) AN ACT further to amend the United Provinces Excise Act, 1910 It is Hereby enacted in the Thirty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1984. ; (2) It shall he deemed to have come into force on April 1, 1983.
Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 77 of the United Provinces Excise Act, 1910, hereinafter referred to as the principal Act, after the proviso thereto, the following proviso shall be added, namely.: "Provided further that notwithstanding anything to the contrary contained in this section, or in any contract, judgement, decree or order, the notification no. 3842-E/XIII-512-83, dated May 25, 1983, made by the State Government in exercise of the powers under section 30 shall have effect and be- deemed always to have effect on and from April 1, 1983."
Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) The Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Ordinance, 1984, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything don ; or any action taken under the principal Act as amended by the Ordinance referred to in sub-section (1), shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act as amended by this Act, as if the provisions of this Act were in force at all material times. ' By order, B. L. LOOMBA, Sachiv.
