Uttar Pradesh Homoeopathic Medicine (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT further to amend the Uttar Pradesh Homoeopathic Medicine Act, 1951 IT IS HEREBY enacted in the Thirty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Homoeopathic Medicine (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Homoeopathic Medicine (Amendment) Act, 1984. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on August 7, 1984.
Uttar Pradesh Homoeopathic Medicine (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 53 of the Uttar Pradesh Homoeopathic Medicine Act, 1951, hereinafter referred to as the principal Act, in sub-section (1) for the words "six years" the words "seven years" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Homoeopathic Medicine (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) The Uttar Pradesh Homoeopathic Medicine (Amendment) Ordinance, 1984, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the provisions of the principal Act as amended by the Ordinance referred to in sub-section (1), shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act as amended by this Act as if the provisions of this Act were in force at all material times.
