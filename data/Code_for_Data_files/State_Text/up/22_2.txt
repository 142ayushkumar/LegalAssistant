Uttar Pradesh Water Management and Regulatory Commission (Repeal) Act, 2012_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh An Act to repeal the Uttar Pradesh Water Management and Regulatory Commission Act, 2008 It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows-
Uttar Pradesh Water Management and Regulatory Commission (Repeal) Act, 2012_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Water Management and Regulatory Commission (Repeal) Act, 20121 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on October 31, 2012. Received the assent of the Governor on March 26, 2013 and published in the Uttar Pradesh Gazette, Extraordinary, Part 1, Section (Ka), dated 28th March, 2013
Uttar Pradesh Water Management and Regulatory Commission (Repeal) Act, 2012_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh The Uttar Pradesh Water Management and Regulatory Commission Act, 2008 is hereby repealed.
Uttar Pradesh Water Management and Regulatory Commission (Repeal) Act, 2012_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh The Uttar Pradesh Water Management and Regulatory Commission (Repeal) Ordinance, 2012 is hereby repealed.
