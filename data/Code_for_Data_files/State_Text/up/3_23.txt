Uttar Pradesh Civil Laws (Reforms and Amendment) Act, 1963_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh An Act to amend the Uttar Pradesh Civil Laws (Reforms and Amendment) Act, 1954. If is hereby enacted in the Fourteenth year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Civil Laws (Reforms and Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Civil Laws (Reforms and Amendment) Act, 1963. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on November 30, 1954.
Uttar Pradesh Civil Laws (Reforms and Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh [Section of this Amending Act has been incorporated the proper place.]
Uttar Pradesh Civil Laws (Reforms and Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh Any act or thing done before the date of publication of this Act in the Gazette, which would have been validly and legally done if the amendment made in the principal Act by this Act had been in force on all material dates, shall be deemed to have been validly and legally done.
