Uttar Pradesh Hindu Public Religious Institutions (Prevention of Dissipation of Properties) (Temporary Powers) (Continuance) Act, 1975_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT further to extend the life of the Uttar Pradesh Hindu Public Religious Institutions (Prevention of Dissipation of Properties) (Temporary Powers) Act, 1962. IT IS HEREBY enacted in the Twenty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Hindu Public Religious Institutions (Prevention of Dissipation of Properties) (Temporary Powers) (Continuance) Act, 1975_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Hindu Public Religious Institutions (Prevention of Dissipation of Properties) (Temporary Powers) (Continuance) Act, 1975.
Uttar Pradesh Hindu Public Religious Institutions (Prevention of Dissipation of Properties) (Temporary Powers) (Continuance) Act, 1975_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In sub-section (3) of section 1 of the Uttar Pradesh Hindu Public Religious Institutions (Prevention of Dissipation of Properties) (Temporary Powers) Act, 1962, for the words "Twelve years", the words "fourteen years" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Hindu Public Religious Institutions (Prevention of Dissipation of Properties) (Temporary Powers) (Continuance) Act, 1975_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh The Uttar Pradesh Hindu Religious Institutions (Prevention of Dissipation of Properties) (Temporary Powers) (Continuance) Ordinance, 1974 is hereby repealed.
