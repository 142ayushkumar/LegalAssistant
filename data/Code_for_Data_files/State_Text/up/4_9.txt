Uttar Pradesh Doctor Bhimrao Ambedkar University (Repeal) Act, 1994_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh An Act to repeal the Uttar Pradesh Doctor Bhimrao Ambedkar University Act, 1989. IT IS HEREBY enacted in the Forty-fifth Year of the Republic of INDIA as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Doctor Bhimrao Ambedkar University (Repeal) Act, 1994_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Doctor Bhimrao Ambedkar University (Repeal) Act, 1994. (2) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint in this behalf.
Uttar Pradesh Doctor Bhimrao Ambedkar University (Repeal) Act, 1994_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh The Uttar Pradesh Doctor Bhimrao Ambedkar University 1989 is hereby repealed.
