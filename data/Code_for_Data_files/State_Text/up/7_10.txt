Uttar Pradesh Go Seva Ayog (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh An Act further to amend the Uttar Pradesh Go Seva Ayog Adhiniyam, 1999 It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows-
Uttar Pradesh Go Seva Ayog (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Go Seva Ayog (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2013.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on June 6, 2013. Received the assent of the Governor on September 24, 2013 and published in the Uttar Pradesh Gazette, Extra., Part 1, Section (Ka), dated 26th September, 2013
Uttar Pradesh Go Seva Ayog (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In Section 4 of the Uttar Pradesh Go Seva Ayog Adhiniyam, 1999 hereinafter referred to as the principal Act,- (a) In sub-section (1) for clause (a) the following clause shall be substituted, namely- "(a) Principal Secretary/Secretary to the Government in the Animal Husbandry Department Ex-officio Member" (b) In sub-section (2) for the word "Vice-Chairperson" the words "Chairperson and the Vice-Chairperson" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Go Seva Ayog (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 2013_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) The Uttar Pradesh Go Seva Ayog (Sanshodhan) Adhyadesh, 2013 (U.P. Ordinance No. 7 of 2013) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the provisions of the principal Act as amended by the Ordinance referred to in sub-section (1) shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act as amended by this Act as if the provisions of this Act were in force at all material times.
