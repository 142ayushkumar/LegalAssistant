Uttar Pradesh Sugarcane Cess (Validation) Repeal Act, 2001_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh An Act to repeal the U.P. Sugarcane Cess (Validation) Act, 1961 . BE it enacted by Parliament in the Fifty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Uttar Pradesh Sugarcane Cess (Validation) Repeal Act, 2001_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the U.P. Sugarcane Cess (Validation) Repeal Act, 2001.
Uttar Pradesh Sugarcane Cess (Validation) Repeal Act, 2001_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh The U.P. Sugarcane Cess (Validation) Act, 1961 is hereby repealed. SUBHASH C. JAIN, Secy. to the Govt. of India.
