Uttar Pradesh Public Services (Tribunal) (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh An Act further to amend the Uttar Pradesh Public Services (Tribunal) Act, 1976 It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows-
Uttar Pradesh Public Services (Tribunal) (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Public Services (Tribunal) (Amendment) Act, 2013.1 (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on July 19, 2013. Received the assent of the Governor on September 24, 2013 and published in the U.P. Gazette, Extra., Part 1, Section (Ka), dated 26th September, 2013
Uttar Pradesh Public Services (Tribunal) (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In Section 3 of the Uttar Pradesh Public Services (Tribunal) Act, 1976 hereinafter referred to as the principal Act,- (a) in sub-section (8), in the proviso for clause (a) the following clause shall be substituted, namely- "(a) in the case of Chairman, the age of seventy years; and" (b) after sub-section (8-a) the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely- "(8-b) The provisions of sub-section (8) as amended by the Uttar Pradesh Public Services (Tribunal) (Amendment) Act, 2013 shall apply also to the Chairman holding office on the commencement of the said Act.
Uttar Pradesh Public Services (Tribunal) (Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) The Uttar Pradesh Public Services (Tribunal) (Amendment) Ordinance, 2013 (U.P. Ordinance No. 9 of 2013) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the provisions of the principal Act as amended by the Ordinance referred to in sub-section (1) shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act as amended by this Act as if the provisions of this Act were in force at all material times.
