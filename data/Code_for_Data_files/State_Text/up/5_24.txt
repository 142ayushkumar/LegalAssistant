Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT further to amend the Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax Act, 1979. IT IS HEARE BY enacted in the Fifty-first Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2000. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on January 25, 2000.
Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 3-A of the Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax Act, 1979, in sub-section (1), in clause (a) for the words "One rupee" the words "One rupee and fifty paise" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax (Second Amendment) Act, 2000_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) The Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax (Amendment) Ordinance, 2000 is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the provisions of the principal Act as amended by the Ordinance referred to in sub-section (1) shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act as amended by this Act, as if the provisions of this Act were in force at ail material times.
