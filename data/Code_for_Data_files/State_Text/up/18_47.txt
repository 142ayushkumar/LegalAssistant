Uttar Pradesh Sri Kashi Vishwanath Temple (Second Amendment) Act, 2013_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh An Act further to amend the Uttar Pradesh Sri Kashi Vishwanath Temple Act, 1983 It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows-
Uttar Pradesh Sri Kashi Vishwanath Temple (Second Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Sri Kashi Vishwanath Temple (Amendment) Act, 20131 . Received the assent of the Governor on March 26, 2013 and published in the U.P. Gazette, Extra, Part 1
Uttar Pradesh Sri Kashi Vishwanath Temple (Second Amendment) Act, 2013_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In Section 14 of the Uttar Pradesh Sri Kashi Vishwanath Temple Act, 1983 for clause (j) the following clauses shall be substituted, namely- (j) to distribute free of cost Bhasm made out of Belpatra etc. received in temples' offering among devotees; (k) to do research related to Vedic Culture, to provide opportunities related to research, to set up cost free research centres, to organize free of cost education classes, to establish cost free technical education for uniting Vedic Culture with technical education and to provide opportunities for employment, to introduce welfare schemes for the society with the help of organizations having similar objectives and to open schools for Vedic Culture's education; (l) to open and organize free of cost medical centers for poor, weak and helpless devotees; (m) to provide free of cost facilities of Annakshetrum for devotees; (n) to open and provide free of cost lodging place for devotees, arrange free of cost stay and also to set up research centres relating to Vedic Culture and technical education; (o) to arrange and distribute free of cost prasadams distribution amongst devotees, to distribute clothes etc. and other ingredients received in offerings among poor and helpless people; (p) to publish and distribute free of cost magazines for awareness to fulfill the object of the trust and time organize seminars and meetings; (q) to do all such things as may be incidental and conducive to the efficient management of the affairs of Temple and its endowments and the convenience of the pilgrims and worshippers.
