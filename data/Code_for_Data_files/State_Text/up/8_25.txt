Uttar Pradesh Homoeopathic Medicine (Second Amendment) Act, 1991_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT further to amend the Uttar Pradesh Homeopathic Medicine Act, 1951. IT IS HEREBY enacted in the Forty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows : -
Uttar Pradesh Homoeopathic Medicine (Second Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Homoeopathic Medicine (Second Amendment) Act, 1991.
Uttar Pradesh Homoeopathic Medicine (Second Amendment) Act, 1991_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 53 of the Uttar Pradesh Homoeopathic Medicine Act, 1951, in sub-section (1) for the words "Fourteen years" the words "Fifteen years" shall be substituted.
