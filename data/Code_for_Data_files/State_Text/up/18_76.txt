Uttar Pradesh State Universities (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh An Act further to amend the Uttar Pradesh State Universities Act, 1973 It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-third Year of the Republic of India as follows-
Uttar Pradesh State Universities (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh State Universities (Amendment) Act, 20121 . (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on August 16, 2012. Received the assent of the Governor on March 26, 2013 and published in the U.P. Gazette, Extra., Part 1, Section (Ka), dated 28th March, 2013
Uttar Pradesh State Universities (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In Section 4 of the Uttar Pradesh State Universities Act, 1973 hereinafter referred to as the principal Act, in sub-section (1-A), in clause (e) for the words "Manyavar Shri Kanshi Ram Ji", the words "Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti", shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh State Universities (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In Section 5 of the principal Act, in sub-section (7) for the words "Manyavar Shri Kanshi Ram Ji" the words "Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh State Universities (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 4--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In Section 7-B of the principal Act for the words "Manyavar Shri Kanshi Ram Ji" the words "Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh State Universities (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 5--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In the Schedule to the principal Act, in the entry appearing at Serial No. 11, in Column No. 2 for the words "Manyavar Shri Kanshi Ram Ji" the words "Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh State Universities (Amendment) Act, 2012_Section 6--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) The Uttar Pradesh State Universities (Amendment) Ordinance, 2012 (U.P. Ordinance No. 6 of 2012) is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the provisions of the principal Act as amended by the Ordinance referred to in sub-section (1) shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act as amended by this Act as if the provisions of this Act were in force at all material times.
