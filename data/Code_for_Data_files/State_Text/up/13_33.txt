Uttar Pradesh Muslim Waqfs (Repeal) Act, 2005_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT to repeal the Uttar Pradesh Muslim Waqfs Act, I960. IT IS HEREBY enacted in the Fifty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows :- Statement of Object The Uttar Pradesh Muslim Waqfs Act, 1960 (U.P. Act. no. XVI of 1960) has been enacted to provide for better governance, administration and supervision of certain classes of waqfs in Uttar Pradesh. Since the said Act has ceased to operate in the light of the provisions of sub-section (3) of section 112 of the Waqf Act, 1995 (Act no. 43 of 1996) and has become redundant with effect from January 01, 1996 (the date of commencement of the said central Act of 1996), it has been decided to repeal the said Act of 1960. The Uttar Pradesh Muslim Waqfs (Repeal) Bill, 2005 is introduced accordingly.
Uttar Pradesh Muslim Waqfs (Repeal) Act, 2005_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Muslim Waqfs (Repeal) Act, 2005. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on January 1, 1996.
Uttar Pradesh Muslim Waqfs (Repeal) Act, 2005_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh The Uttar Pradesh Muslim Waqfs Act, 1960 is hereby repealed.
