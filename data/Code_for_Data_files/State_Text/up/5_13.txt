Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT further to amend the United Provinces Entertainments and Betting Tax Act, 1937. It is hereby enacted in the Twenty-fifth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax (Amendment) Act, 1974.
Uttar Pradesh Entertainments and Betting Tax (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 3 of the United Provinces Entertainments and Betting Tax Act, 1937, in sub-section (1), for the words "seventy-five per cent" and "cent per cent", the words "eighty per cent" and "one hundred and ten per cent" shall respectively be substituted.
