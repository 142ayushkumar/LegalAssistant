Uttar Pradesh Commission for Minorities (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In pursance of the provisions of clause (3) of Article 348 of the Constitution of India, the Governor is pleased order me publication of the following English translation of the Uttar Pradesh Alpasankhyak Ayog (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1999 (Uttar Pradesh Adhiniyam Sankhya 23 of 1999) as passed by the Uttar Pradesh Legislature and assented Co by the Governor on May 8, 1999. An Act further to amend the Uttar Pradesh Commission for Minorities Act, 1994 It is hereby enacted in the Fiftieth Year of the Republc of India as follows:-
Uttar Pradesh Commission for Minorities (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Commission for Minorities (Amendment) Act, 1999.
Uttar Pradesh Commission for Minorities (Amendment) Act, 1999_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 4 of the Uttar Pradesh Commission for Minorities Act, )994 for sub-section (1) the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely:- "(1) (a) The Chairman and every Member shall hold office for a term of one year from the date he assumes office. (b) The provisions of clause (a) shall apply also to the Chairman and every member who assumed his office before the commencement of the Uttar Pradesh Commission for Minorities (Amendment) Ad, 1999. (c) The term of the Chairman and every Member, who has completed on or before the commencement of the Act referred to in clause (b), the period of one year from the date he assumed his office shall expire on such commencement."
