Uttar Pradesh Imposition of Ceiling on Land Holdings (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT further to amend the Uttar Pradesh Imposition of Ceiling on Land Holdings Act, 1960 IT IS HEREBY enacted in the Thirty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Imposition of Ceiling on Land Holdings (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Imposition of Ceiling on Land Holdings (Amendment) Act, 1986. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on January 13, 1986.
Uttar Pradesh Imposition of Ceiling on Land Holdings (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In sections 13, 20 and 21 of the Uttar Pradesh Imposition of Ceiling on Land Holdings Act, 1960, hereinafter referred to as the principal Act, for the word 'District Judge', wherever occurring, the word 'Commissioner' shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Imposition of Ceiling on Land Holdings (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 38 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2),- (a) for the word 'District Judge' the word 'Commissioner' shall be substituted ; (b) for the words 'Additional District Judge, Civil Judge or Additional Civil Judge', the word 'Additional Commissioner' shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Imposition of Ceiling on Land Holdings (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 4--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh All proceedings and appeals under sections 13, 20 and 21 of the principal Act, pending immediately before the commencement of this Act before any District Judge, Additional District Judge, Civil Judge or Additional Civil Judge shall stand transferred to the Commissioner and shall be disposed of by the Commissioner in accordance with the provisions of the principal Act, as amended by this Act.
Uttar Pradesh Imposition of Ceiling on Land Holdings (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 5--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) The Uttar Pradesh Imposition of Ceiling on Land Holdings (Amendment) Ordinance, 1986, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the provisions of the principal Act, as amended by the Ordinance referred to in sub-section (1), shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act, as amended by this Act, as if this Act were in force at all material times.
