Uttar Pradesh Civil Laws (Reforms and Amendment) Amendment Act, 1963_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT to amend the Uttar Pradesh Civil Laws (Reforms Amendment) Act, 1954. IT is hereby enacted in the Fourteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Uttar Pradesh Civil Laws (Reforms and Amendment) Amendment Act, 1963_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Civil Laws (Reforms and Amendment) Amendment Act, 1963. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on November 30, 1954.
Uttar Pradesh Civil Laws (Reforms and Amendment) Amendment Act, 1963_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh Between sub-section (1) and sub-section (2) of section 3 of the Uttar Pradesh Civil Laws (Reforms and Amendment) Act, 1954 (hereinafter called the Principal Act), the following new sub-section (1-A) shall be inserted :- "(1-A) Notwithstanding anything contained in sub-section (1), the High Court may transfer to any District Judge or Additional District Judge subordinate to it, any appeal from an original decree or order in case the valuation whereof does not exceed ten thousand rupees."
Uttar Pradesh Civil Laws (Reforms and Amendment) Amendment Act, 1963_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh Any act or thing done before the date of publication of this Act in the Gazette, which would have been validly and legally done if the amendment made in the principal Act by this Act had been in force on all material dates, shall be deemed to have been validly and legally done.
