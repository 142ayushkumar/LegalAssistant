Uttar Pradesh Muslim Waqfs (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT to amend the Uttar Pradesh Muslim Waqfs Act, 1960 IT IS HEREBY enacted in the Fourteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Muslim Waqfs (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Muslim Waqfs (Amendment) Act, 1963. (2) Sections 1, 7 and 8 of this Act shall come into force at- once and sections 2 to 6 shall be deemed to have come into force on September 3, 1960.
Uttar Pradesh Muslim Waqfs (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh For sub-section (2) of section 2 of the Uttar Pradesh Muslim Waqfs Act, 1960 (hereinafter referred to as the Principal Act) the following shall be substituted : "(2) This Act shall also apply to the endowments hitherto governed by the Husainabad Endowment Act, 1878, the trusts known as King's side and the Queen's side of Shahnajaf Trust, the Waqfs known as Waqf Abbu Saheb, Rauza Kazmani Waqf and Waqf Wazir Begum, Lucknow and any other endowment, trust or waqf hitherto administered by the Trustees appointed under the said Husainabad Endowment Act; and all these endowments, trusts and waqfs shall be deemed to be waqfs for the purpose of this Act : Provided that the State Government shall have the power to make, by notification in the official Gazette, such adaptations, whether by way of modification, addition or omission, not affecting the substance of the provisions of this Act, as it may deem fit, in its application to the aforesaid endowments, trusts and waqfs."
Uttar Pradesh Muslim Waqfs (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In clause (5) of section 3 of the Principal Act- (i) the words "or endowment" shall be deleted ; and (ii) for the words "any endowment as such" the word "waqfs" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Muslim Waqfs (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 4--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In the proviso to sub-section (2) of section 10 of the Principal Act, for the words "Properties which were hitherto governed by the Husainabad Endowment Act, 1878, or which were administered by the Trustees appointed thereunder" the words and figures "waqfs, endowments and trusts referred to in sub-section (2) of section 2" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Muslim Waqfs (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 5--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 19 of the Principal Act- (i) In sub-section (1), the words "or endowments" shall be deleted ; and (ii) in clause (f) of sub-section (2), for the words "waqfs hitherto governed by the Husainabad Endowment Act, 1878 and the properties administered by the Trustees appointed thereunder", the words and figures "waqfs, endowments and trusts referred to in sub-section (2) of section 2" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Muslim Waqfs (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 6--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 47 of the Principal Act- (i) In sub-section (1), for the words "properties which were hitherto governed by the Husainabad Endowment Act, 1878, or which were administered by the Trustees appointed thereunder" the words and figures "waqfs, endowments and trusts referred to in sub-section (2) of section 2" shall be, substituted ; and (ii) in sub-section (2), for the word "properties" the words "waqfs, endowments and trusts" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Muslim Waqfs (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 7--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh Any adaptations of the provisions of the Principal Act made under the proviso to sub-section (2) of section 2 or any scheme of administration settled under section 47 of the said Act or any action taken thereunder shall be deemed to have been so made, settled or taken under the said Act as amended by this Act, any judgment, order or decree of any court notwithstanding.
Uttar Pradesh Muslim Waqfs (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 8--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) The Uttar Pradesh Muslim Waqfs (Amendment) Adhyadesh, 1963, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal anything done or any action taken under the said Adhyadesh shall be deemed to have been done or taken under this Act, as if this act had commenced on August 14, 1963.
