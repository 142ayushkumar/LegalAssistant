Uttar Pradesh Electricity (Temporary Powers of Control) (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT to amend the U. P. Electricity (Temporary Powers of Control) Act, 1947, for certain purposes WHEREAS it is expedient to amend the U. P. Electricity (Temporary Powers of Control) Act, 1947, for certain purposes ; IT IS HEREBY enacted in the Fourteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Electricity (Temporary Powers of Control) (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Electricity (Temporary Powers of Control) (Amendment) Act, 1963. (2) It extends to the whole of Uttar Pradesh.
Uttar Pradesh Electricity (Temporary Powers of Control) (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In sub-section (4) of section 1 of the U. P. Electricity (Temporary Powers of Control) Act, 1947, for the figure "1963", the figure "1968" shall be substituted.
