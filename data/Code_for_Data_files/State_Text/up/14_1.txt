Uttar Pradesh Nagar Mahapalika (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1963_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT to amend the Uttar Pradesh Nagar Mahapalika Adhiniyam, 1959 for the purpose hereinafter appearing. IT IS HEREBY enacted in the fourteenth year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Nagar Mahapalika (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1963_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Nagar Mahapalika (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1963.
Uttar Pradesh Nagar Mahapalika (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1963_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In sub-section (1) of section 580 of the Uttar Pradesh Nagar Mahapalika Adhiniyam, 1959, for the words "four years" the words "five years" shall be substituted.
