Uttar Pradesh Regulation of Money- Lending (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT to amend the Uttar Pradesh Regulation of Money-Lending Act, 1976 It is hereby enacted in the Twenty-ninth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Regulation of Money- Lending (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Regulation of Money- Lending (Amendment) Act, 1978.
Uttar Pradesh Regulation of Money- Lending (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 2 of the Uttar Pradesh Regulation of Money-Lending Act, 1976, hereinafter referred to as the principal Act, in sub-section (1), for clause (a), the following clause shall be substituted and be deemed always to have been substituted, namely :- "(a) any loan or advance by or any deposit with any bank or a cooperative society."
Uttar Pradesh Regulation of Money- Lending (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 7 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1)- (a) after the words, "Any person carrying on the business of Money- Lending on the date of commencement of this Act wishing to carry on such business after such commencement", the words 'or any person Wishing to commence such business after the said date" shall be inserted and be deemed always to have been inserted ; (b) in the proviso, for the words "from the date of such commencement", the words and figures "from the date of commencement of the Uttar Pradesh Regulation of Money-Lending (Amendment) Act, 1978" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Regulation of Money- Lending (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 4--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 14 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), the following proviso shall be inserted and be deemed always to have been inserted, namely : "Provided that nothing in this sub-section shall apply to a transaction where a fresh bond or promissory note is executed in lieu of a bond or promissory note which remains wholly or partly unpaid."
Uttar Pradesh Regulation of Money- Lending (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 5--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh For section 18 of the principal Act, the following section shall be substituted and be deemed always to have been substituted, namely:- "18. Bar on certain suits by money-lender:- (1) No suit on the basis of any loan, agreement or security referred to in sub-section (1) of section 15 shall be instituted - by a money, unless at the time of advancing such lenders. loan or making such agreement or taking such security- (a) such money-lender held a valid certificate of registration ; or (b) such money-lender had applied for such certificate and the same had not been refused ; or (c) the period specified in the proviso to sub-section (1) of section 7 had not expired.
Uttar Pradesh Regulation of Money- Lending (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 6--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 26 of the principal Act, in sub-section (1), the following proviso shall be inserted, namely : "Provided that the Registrar may on an application by such moneylender, for sufficient cause condone the delay and accept the statement submitted within three months from the date of the commencement of the Uttar Pradesh Regulation of Money-Lending (Amendment) Act, 1978."
Uttar Pradesh Regulation of Money- Lending (Amendment) Act, 1978_Section 7--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh For section 29 of the principal Act, the following section shall be substituted and be deemed always to have been substituted, namely:- "29. Repeal:- The Usurious Loans Act, 1918, as amended in its application to Uttar Pradesh, is hereby repealed with effect from August 10, 1976 in relation to loans and advances to which the provisions of this Act apply."
