Uttar Pradesh Civil Judges (Designation) Act, 1936_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh 1 An Act to effect a change in the designation of the office hitherto known as that of Subordinate Judge. Whereas it has been resolved that the office hitherto known as that of "Subordinate Judge" shall in future be know as that of "Civil Judge" ; And whereas it is expedient to recognise and give effect to the change so made in the designation of the said office ; It is hereby enacted as follows :- INTRODUCTION The Act was passed to effect a change in the designation of the office then known as that of Subordinate Judge. The designation of the office of Subordinate Judge was changed as that of Civil Judge. The Act received the assent of the Governor of Uttar Pradesh on July 11, 1936, and of the Governor-General on August 7, 1936, and was published on August 15, 1936. Received the assent of the Governor of [Uttar Pradesh] on the 11th July 1936, and of the Governor General on the 7th August, 1936, and was published under Section 81 the Government of India Act on the 15th August, 1936.
Uttar Pradesh Civil Judges (Designation) Act, 1936_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the (Uttar Pradesh) Civil Judges (Designation) Act, 1936. (2) It extends to Uttar Pradesh. (3) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may by notification in the Gazette appoint in this behalf.
Uttar Pradesh Civil Judges (Designation) Act, 1936_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In Sections 3, 4, 6, 10, 11, 13, 18, 21, 22, 23, 24, 25, 26, 27, 31 and 36 of the Bengal, Agra and Assam Civil Courts Act, 1887, in its application to Uttar Pradesh, for the word "subordinate" the word "Civil" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Civil Judges (Designation) Act, 1936_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 21, 22, 25, 26, 29, 30, 31, 32, 35, 39, 40 and 44 of the Oudh Courts Act, 1925, for the word "subordinate" the word "civil" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Civil Judges (Designation) Act, 1936_Section 4--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh The Acts specified in the schedule are hereby amended to the extent and in the manner mentioned in the second column thereof.
Uttar Pradesh Civil Judges (Designation) Act, 1936_Section 5--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh The office of a Civil Judge shall for all purposes be deemed to be no inferior to that of a subordinate Judge.
