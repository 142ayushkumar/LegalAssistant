Uttar Pradesh Development (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT to amend the Uttar Pradesh Area Development Act, 1976 . IT IS HEREBY enacted in the Thirty-eighth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Uttar Pradesh Development (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Development (Amendment) Act, 1987. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on December 19, 1986.
Uttar Pradesh Development (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 4 of the Area Development Act, 1976, hereinafter referred to as the principal Act, in sub-section (1), after clause (b) the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- (bb) the Collectors of the Districts falling in the Area.
Uttar Pradesh Development (Amendment) Act, 1987_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) The Uttar Pradesh Area Development (Amendment) Ordinance 1986, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the provisions of the principal Act, as amended by the Ordinance referred to in sub-section (1), shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act, as amended by this Act as if the provisions of this Act were in force at all material times.
