Uttar Pradesh Sugarcane (Purchase Tax) (Amendment) Act, 1965_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT further to amend the Uttar Pradesh Sugarcane (Purchase Tax) Act, 1961. IT IS hereby enacted in the Sixteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Sugarcane (Purchase Tax) (Amendment) Act, 1965_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Sugarcane (Purchase Tax) (Amendment) Act, 1965.
Uttar Pradesh Sugarcane (Purchase Tax) (Amendment) Act, 1965_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In sub-section (1) of section 3 of the Uttar Pradesh Sugarcane (Purchase Tax) Act, 1961- (i) in clause (a) for the words "naye Paise" the word "Paise" shall be substituted ; and (ii) in clause (b) for the words "twenty-five Naye Paise per quintal", the words "fifty Paise per quintal" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Sugarcane (Purchase Tax) (Amendment) Act, 1965_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) The Uttar Pradesh Sugarcane (Purchase Tax) (Amendment) Ordinance, 1965 is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under this Act as if this Act had commenced on the seventh day of January, 1965.
