Uttar Pradesh Hindu Religious Institution (Prevention of Dissipation of Properties) (Repeal) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT to repeal the Uttar Pradesh Hindu Public Religious Institutions (Prevention, of Dissipation of Properties) Act, 1962 IT IS HEREBY enacted in the Fifty-first year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Uttar Pradesh Hindu Religious Institution (Prevention of Dissipation of Properties) (Repeal) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Hindu Religious Institution (Prevention of Dissipation of Properties) (Repeal) Act, 2000. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on August 26, 2000.
Uttar Pradesh Hindu Religious Institution (Prevention of Dissipation of Properties) (Repeal) Act, 2000_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh The Uttar Pradesh Hindu Public Religious Institutions (Prevention of Dissipation of Properties) Act, 1962 is hereby repealed.
Uttar Pradesh Hindu Religious Institution (Prevention of Dissipation of Properties) (Repeal) Act, 2000_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh The Uttar Pradesh Hindu Public Religious Institutions (Prevention of Dissipation of Properties) (Repeal) Ordinance, 2000 is hereby repealed.
