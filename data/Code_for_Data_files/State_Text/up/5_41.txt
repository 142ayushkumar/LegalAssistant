Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (As passed by the Uttar Pradesh Legislature) AN further to amend the United Provinces Excise Act, 1910. It Is Hereby enacted in the Forty-eighth Yearthe Republic of India as follows:-
Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1997.
Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 3 of the United Provinces. Excise Act, 1910, hereinafter referred to as the principal Act, after clause (3-a) the following clause shall be inserted, namely:- "(4) 'manufactory' means a unit other than a distillery, where Indian made foreign liquor is manufactured and bottled."
Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 17 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2), for the words "or brewery" the words "brewery or manufactory" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 4--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 18 of the principal Act, in clause (c) for the words "or brewery" the words "brewery or manufactory" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 5--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 19 of the principal Act, for the word "brewery" the words "brewery, manufactory" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 6--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 29 of the principal Act, in clause (e) for the words "or brewery" wherever occuring the words "brewery or manufactory" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 7--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 41 of the principal Act, in clause (b) for the words "or brewery" the words "brewery or manufactory" shall he substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1997_Section 8--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 60 of the Principal Act, in sub-section (1) in clauses (d) and (f), for the word "brewery" wherever occuring the words "brewery, manufactory" shall be substituted. By order, R. D. MATHUR, Pramukh Sachiv.
