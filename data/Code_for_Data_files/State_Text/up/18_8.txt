Sanjay Gandhi Post Graduate Institute of Medical Sciences (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh 1 An Act further to amend the Sanjay Gandhi Post Graduate Institute of Medical Sciences Act, 1983 It is hereby enacted in the Sixty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows:- Received the assent of the Governor on September 29, 2011 and published in the U.P. Gazette, Extra., Part 1, Section (Ka), dated 30th September, 2011, pp. 3-4.
Sanjay Gandhi Post Graduate Institute of Medical Sciences (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Sanjay Gandhi Post Graduate Institute of Medical Sciences (Amendment) Act, 2011.
Sanjay Gandhi Post Graduate Institute of Medical Sciences (Amendment) Act, 2011_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In Section 4 of the Sanjay Gandhi Post Graduate Institute of Medical Sciences Act, 1983 for sub-section (2) the following sub-section shall be substituted, namely:- "(2) Out of seven persons to be nominated under clause (f) of sub-section (1) five persons including four medical experts shall be nominated by the State Government out of a panel to be prepared by a committee consisting of the following persons- (i) the Director, Central Drugs Research Institute, Lucknow; (ii) the Vice-Chancellor, Chhatrapati Sahuji Maharaj Medical University, Lucknow; (iii) the Vice-Chancellor, Lucknow University; (iv) the Director, Institute of Medical Sciences, Banaras Hindu University, Varanasi; (v) the Director, Post Graduate Institute of Medical Education and Research, Chandigarh; (vi) the Director, All India Institute of Medical Sciences, New Delhi; (vii) two eminent medical experts to be nominated by the President of the Institute; (viii) the Secretary/Principal Secretary, Medical Education Department, Government of Uttar Pradesh; (ix) the Director, Sanjay Gandhi Post Graduate Institute of Medical Sciences, Lucknow (Convenor).".
