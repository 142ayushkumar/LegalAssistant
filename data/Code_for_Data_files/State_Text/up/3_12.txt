Uttar Pradesh Cinemas (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT further to amend the Uttar Pradesh Cinemas (Regulation) Act, 1955. IT IS HEREBY enacted in the Forty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Cinemas (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Cinemas (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1995. (2) It shall come into force on such date, as the State Government may, by notification appoint in this behalf.
Uttar Pradesh Cinemas (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 2 of the Uttar Pradesh Cinemas (Regulation) Act, 1955, hereinafter referred to as the principal Act, clauses (a) and (aa) shall be re-numbered as clauses (aa) and (ma) thereof respectively and before clause (aa) as so re-numbered the following clause shall be inserted, namely :- "(a) 'appellate authority' means the State Government when the appeal is preferred against an order of the Entertainment Tax Commissioner. Uttar Pradesh. and the Divisional Commissioner when the appeal is preferred against an order of the District Magistrate ; "
Uttar Pradesh Cinemas (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 5 of the principal Act, in sub-section (3) for the words "the State Government and the State Government" the words "the appellate authority and the appellate authority" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Cinemas (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 4--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 6-A of the principal Act,- (a) in sub-section (1), after the words "rules made thereunder" the words "and may seize such films and video cassettes as are found being exhibited or kept in contravention thereof" shall be inserted : (b) after sub-section (4), the following sub-section shall be inserted, namely :- "(5) Every film or video cassette seized under sub-section (1) shall, as soon as may be, be produced before the court having jurisdiction which may make such order as it thinks fit for its proper custody. (6) Films and video cassettes seized under sub-section (1) shall be liable to confiscation by an order of the court having jurisdiction : Provided that no order of confiscation shall be made until the expiration of one month from the date of seizure and without hearing any person who may claim any right thereto and considering the evidence, if any, which he produces in respect, of his claim."
Uttar Pradesh Cinemas (Regulation) (Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 5--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 7 of the principal Act- (a) in sub-section (1-A), in clause (b) for the words "State Government" the words "appellate authority" shall be substituted ; (b) in sub-section (4) for the words "State Government" the words "appellate authority" shall be substituted ; (c) in sub-section (5); for the words "State Government" the words "appellate authority" shall be substituted.
