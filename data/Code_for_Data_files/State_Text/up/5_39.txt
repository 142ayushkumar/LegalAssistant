Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (As passed by the U. P. Legislature) AN Act further to amend the United Provinces Excise Act, 1910. It is Hereby enacted in the Fortieth Year of the Republic of India as follows:
Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1989. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on October 27, 1988.
Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 28 of the United Provinces Excise Act, 1910, hereinafter referred to as the principal Act, in sub-section (3),- (i) in clause (a), for the figure and words "4 per litre", occurring against item 2 (a) in the table, the figure and words "5 per litre" shall be substituted; (ii) in each of the clauses (c) , (d) and (e), for the figure and words "2 per litre" occurring against item 2 (a) in the table, the figure and words "5 per litre" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) The Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Ordinance, 1988, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the pro visions of the principal Act, as amended by the Ordinance referred to in sub-section (1), shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act, as amended by this Act, as if the provisions of this Act were in force at all material times. By order, NARAYAN DAS, Sachiv.
