Court Fees (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1989_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh An Act further to amend the Court Fees Act, 1870, in its application to Uttar Pradesh. It is hereby enacted in the Fortieth Year of the Republic of India as follows :
Court Fees (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Court Fees (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1989. (2) It extends to the whole of Uttar Pradesh. (3) It shall come into force on such date2 as the State Government may, by notification, appoint in this behalf.
Court Fees (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1989_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In Schedule II to the Court Fees Act, 1870, as amended in its application to Uttar Pradesh, in Article 1 (Application or petition), in clause (f),- (a) in sub-clause (i), for the words "Five rupees" the words "One hundred rupees" shall be substituted; and (b) in sub-clause (ii), for the words "Ten rupees" the words "Two hundred rupees" shall be substituted.
