Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (As passed by the Uttar Pradesh Legislature) AN ACT further to amend the United Provinces Excise Act, 1910. It is hereby enacted in the Thirty-fourth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1983. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on May 9, 1983.
Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 28 of the U. P. Excise Act, 1910, hereinafter referred to as the principal Act, in sub-section (3), in clause (b), for the figures and words "2 per litre of alcohol" occurring against items 1 and 2 in the table, the figure and words "5 per litre of alcohol" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Act, 1983_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) The Uttar Pradesh Excise (Amendment) Ordinance, 1983, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the provisions of the principal Act, as amended by the Ordinance referred to in sub-section (1), shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act as amended by this Act, as if the provisions of this Act were in force at all material times. By order, G. B. SINGH, Sachiv.
