Uttar Pradesh Electricity (Temporary Powers of Control) (Continuance) Act, 1970_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT to provide for the continuance, for a limited period, of the U. P. Electricity (Temporary Powers of Control) Act, 1947. IT IS HEREBY enacted in the Twenty-first Year of Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Electricity (Temporary Powers of Control) (Continuance) Act, 1970_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Electricity (Temporary Powers of Control) (Continuance) Act, 1970.
Uttar Pradesh Electricity (Temporary Powers of Control) (Continuance) Act, 1970_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In sub-section (4) of section 1 of the U. P. Electricity (Temporary Powers of Control) Act, 1947, for the figures "1968", the figures "1973" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Electricity (Temporary Powers of Control) (Continuance) Act, 1970_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh The Uttar Pradesh Electricity (Temporary Powers of Control) (Continuance) Act, 1968, is hereby repealed.
