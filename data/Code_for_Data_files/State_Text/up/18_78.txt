Uttar Pradesh Sthaniya Nikaya (Alpakalik Vyavastha) (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1965_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT further to amend the Uttar Pradesh Sthaniya Nikaya (Alpakalik Vyavastha) Adhiniyam, 1963. IT is hereby enacted in the Sixteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Sthaniya Nikaya (Alpakalik Vyavastha) (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1965_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Sthaniya Nikaya (Alpakalik Vyavastha) (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1965.
Uttar Pradesh Sthaniya Nikaya (Alpakalik Vyavastha) (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1965_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh The fullstop occurring at the end of section 3 of the Uttar Pradesh Sthaniya Nikaya (Alpakalik Vyavastha) Adhiniyam, 1963 shall be substituted by a colon and thereafter the following proviso shall be added, namely :- "Provided that if for some reason beyond the control of the State Government, it is not possible to hold or complete the general election to a local body or to notify its re- constitution before 31st day of December, 1964, the State Government may by notification in the official Gazette direct that in relation to such local body this section shall have effect as if for the words and figures 'thirty-first day of December, 1964' the words and figures 'thirteeth day of June, 1965' were substituted."
Uttar Pradesh Sthaniya Nikaya (Alpakalik Vyavastha) (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1965_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) The Uttar Pradesh Sthaniya Nikaya (Alpakalik Vyavastha) (Dwitiya Sanshodhan) Adhyadesh, 1964 is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the said Ordinance shall be deemed to have been done or taken under this Act as if this Act had commenced on the 29th day of December, 1964.
