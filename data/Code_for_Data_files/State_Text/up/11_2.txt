Uttar Pradesh Khadi and Village Industries Board (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT further to amend the Uttar Pradesh Khadi and Village Industries Board Act, 1960. IT is hereby enacted in the Twenty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Uttar Pradesh Khadi and Village Industries Board (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Khadi and Village Industries Board (Amendment) Act, 1976.
Uttar Pradesh Khadi and Village Industries Board (Amendment) Act, 1976_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 32 of the Uttar Pradesh Khadi and Village Industries Board Act, 1960, in sub-section (2), in clause (d), the words "to the extent of the fund and properties of the Board vested in it" shall be omitted.
