Uttar Pradesh State Commission for Women (Repeal) Act, 2000_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT to repeal the Uttar Pradesh State Commission for Women Act, 1999. It is Hereby enacted in the Fiftieth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh State Commission for Women (Repeal) Act, 2000_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh State Commission for Women (Repeal) Act, 2000. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on December 9, 1999.
Uttar Pradesh State Commission for Women (Repeal) Act, 2000_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh The Uttar Pradesh State Commission for Women Act, 1999 is hereby repealed.
Uttar Pradesh State Commission for Women (Repeal) Act, 2000_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh The Uttar Pradesh State Commission for women (Repeal) Ordinance, 1999 is hereby repealed. By order, Y. R. TRTPATHI
