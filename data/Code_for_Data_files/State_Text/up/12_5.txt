Uttar Pradesh Laws (Replacement of References to Old Coinage by New Decimal Coinage) Act, 1963_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT to replace references to old coinage in certain U. P. Laws, by new decimal coinage IT IS HEREBY enacted in the Fourteenth year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Laws (Replacement of References to Old Coinage by New Decimal Coinage) Act, 1963_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Laws (Replacement of References to Old Coinage by New Decimal Coinage) Act, 1963.
Uttar Pradesh Laws (Replacement of References to Old Coinage by New Decimal Coinage) Act, 1963_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh The enactments specified in the fourth column of the Schedule to this Act are hereby amended to the extent and in the manner mentioned in the fifth column thereof.
