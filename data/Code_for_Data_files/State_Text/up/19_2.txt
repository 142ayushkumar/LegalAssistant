Uttar Pradesh (Temporary) Accommodation Requisition (Continuance) Act, 1969_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT to provide for the continuance of the U. P. (Temporary) Accommodation Requisition Act, 1947, for a further period of three years. IT IS HEREBY enacted in the Twentieth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh (Temporary) Accommodation Requisition (Continuance) Act, 1969_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh (Temporary) Accommodation Requisition (Continuance) Act, 1969.
Uttar Pradesh (Temporary) Accommodation Requisition (Continuance) Act, 1969_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In sub-section (5) of section 1 of the U. P. (Temporary) Accommodation Requisition Act, 1947, for the figures "1969" the figures "1972" shall be substituted.
