Uttar Pradesh Sri Kashi Vishwanath Temple (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT to amend the Uttar Pradesh Sri Kashi Vishwanath Temple Act, 1983. It is hereby enacted in the Thirty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Sri Kashi Vishwanath Temple (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Sri Kashi Vishwanath Temple (Amendment) Act, 1986.
Uttar Pradesh Sri Kashi Vishwanath Temple (Amendment) Act, 1986_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 6 of the Uttar Pradesh Sri Kashi Vishwanath Temple Act, 1983, in sub-section (2), for clause (e), the following clause shall be substituted, namely :- "(e) Secretary, and in his absence, Special Secretary if any, to the Government of Uttar Pradesh in Dharmarth Karya Vibhag--ex-officio;".
