Uttar Pradesh Higher Education Grants Commission (Repeal) Act, 1986_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh An ACT to repeal The Uttar Pradesh Higher Education Grants Commission Act, 1974 It is hereby enacted in the Thirty-seventh Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Higher Education Grants Commission (Repeal) Act, 1986_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Higher Education Grants Commission (Repeal) Act, 1986.
Uttar Pradesh Higher Education Grants Commission (Repeal) Act, 1986_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh The Uttar Pradesh Higher Education Grants Commission Act, 1974 is hereby repealed.
