Uttar Pradesh (Temporary) Accommodation Requisition (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT to amend the United Provinces (Temporary) Accommodation Requisition Act, 1947 for certain purposes IT IS HEREBY enacted in the fourteenth year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh (Temporary) Accommodation Requisition (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh (Temporary) Accommodation Requisition (Amendment) Act, 1963.
Uttar Pradesh (Temporary) Accommodation Requisition (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In sub-section (5) of section 1 of the United Provinces (Temporary) Accommodation Requisition Act, 1947 (hereinafter called the principal Act) for the figure "1963" the figure "1969" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh (Temporary) Accommodation Requisition (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 2 of the principal Act- (i) for clause (b) the following shall be substituted :- "(b) Court means the Court of Munsif, or where there is no Munsif, the court of Civil Judge, in whose territorial jurisdiction the accommodation lies" ; and (ii) after clause (b) the following new clause shall be inserted :- "(bb) District Judge means the District Judge in whose territorial jurisdiction the accommodation lies."
Uttar Pradesh (Temporary) Accommodation Requisition (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 4--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh For section 7 of the Principal Act the following shall be substituted :- "7. Appeals against orders of court:- (1) An appeal from a decision of the Court under this Act shall lie to the District Judge within thirty days. (2) The provisions of sections 4, 5 and 12 of the Indian Limitation Act, 1908 shall be applicable to appeals under this Act."
Uttar Pradesh (Temporary) Accommodation Requisition (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 5--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 8 of the principal Act- (i) in sub-section (1) in the first sentence between the words "was requisitioned" and the full-stop occurring at the end, the words "and deliver possession thereof to him" shall be inserted ; (ii) after sub-section (1) the following new sub-section shall be added :- "(1-A) For purposes of delivery of possession of the accommodation under sub-section (1) the District Magistrate shall in the manner prescribed by rules evict or cause to be evicted all persons who might be in occupation of the accommodation at the time of its release from requisition, and in doing so he may use such force as may be necessary." ; and (iii) after sub-section (4) the following shall be added as a new sub-section :- "(5) Nothing in this section shall prevent the District Magistrate from releasing from requisition only a part of the accommodation : Provided that where the District Magistrate releases from requisition only a part of the accommodation with the concurrence of the owner, the release shall be on such terms as to compensation or otherwise as may be agreed upon between him and the owner : Provided further that where the release from requisition of only a part of the accommodation is without the concurrence of the owner or where no agreement as specified in the first proviso as to the terms of the release is reached, such reduction, if any, shall be made in the compensation payable to the owner as may be determined by the Court on a reference made to it by the District Magistrate, and the provisions of sub-section (2) of section 6 shall mutatis mutandis apply to such reference."
Uttar Pradesh (Temporary) Accommodation Requisition (Amendment) Act, 1963_Section 6--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh The existing section 10 of the principal Act shall be renumbered as sub-section (1) thereof and thereafter the following shall be added as a new sub-section :- "(2) The owner shall not ordinarily be required to execute repairs at a cost exceeding one months letting value of the accommodation in a year. If in the opinion of the District Magistrate the accommodation needs any special repairs and the aggregate cost of all repairs is in any year likely to exceed one months letting value of the accommodation and the owner does not agree to carry out such repairs, the District Magistrate may refer the matter to the Court which shall make such order and on such terms, including those relating to payment of compensation, as it may consider necessary."
