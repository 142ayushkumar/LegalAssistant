Indian Tolls (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1995_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT further to amend the Indian Tolls Act, 1851 in Us application to Uttar Pradesh IT IS HEREBY enacted in the Forty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows : -
Indian Tolls (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Indian Tolls (Uttar Pradesh Amendment Act, 1995. (2) It extends to the whole of Uttar Pradesh. (3) It shall be deemed to have come into force on January 10, 1995
Indian Tolls (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh After section 1-A of the Indian Tolls Act, 1851, as amended in its application to Uttar Pradesh, hereinafter referred to as the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted namely:- "1-B. Definition:- In this Act- "Authorised person" [means any person authorised under section 2-B".
Indian Tolls (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh in section 2 of the principal Act, for the words "made or repaired" the words "made, maintained or repaired" shall be substituted.
Indian Tolls (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 4--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 2-A of the principal Act- (a) in the marginal hedging, for the words "the Corporation" the words "an Authorised person' shall be substituted, - (b) for the words "which has been or shall hereafter be made, maintained or repaired at the expense of the .Uttar Pradesh State Bridge Corporation Limited, a Government Company registered under the Companies 1956 (hereinafter referred to as the 'Corporation)", the words "which has been or shall hereafter be made, maintained or repaired, at the expense of an Authorised Person" shall be substituted, : (c) for the words "made or repaired" the words "made, maintained or repaired" shall be substituted.
Indian Tolls (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 5--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh For section 2-B of the principal Act, the following section shall fee substituted, namely ;- "2-B. Power of an authorised person to manage and collect tolls etc.:- Without prejudice to the generality of the provisions of sections 2 and 2-A, and subject to any rules made under this Act, or to any general or special orders of the State Government, and in accordance with any agreement that may be made in this behalf, the State Government may authorise any; person to manage and collect all tolls levied upon any road or bridge which has been or shall here after be made, maintained or repaired at the expense of such person, or the maintenance and repairs whereof has beep or shall be transferred to such person by the State Government."
Indian Tolls (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 6--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 2-C of the principal Act- (a) in the marginal heading for the words "the Corporation" the words, "the Authorised Person" shall be substituted; (b) in sub-section (1), for the words "the State Government or the Corporation" wherever they occur, the words "the State Government or the Authorised Person" shall be substituted; (c) in sub-section (2), for the words "and in the case of the Corporation, the Managing Director thereof" the words "and in case of an Authorised Person, if he is an individual, the Authorised Person himself, any in any other case, the person incharge of the affairs thereof (by whatever name called)" shall be substituted.
Indian Tolls (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 7--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 2-D of the principal Act, for the words "the Corporation" the words "the Authorised Person" shall be substituted.
Indian Tolls (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 8--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh After section 2-Fof the principal Act, the following section shall be inserted, namely,- "2-G. Bar to manage or collect tolls by local authority:- Notwithstanding anything contained in any law for the time being in force, no local authority shall, with effect from the date of commencement of the Indian Tolls (Uttar Pradesh" Amendment) Act, 1995 manage or collect tolls in respect of which the State Government or an Authorised person may manage or collect the tolls under this Act."
Indian Tolls (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 9--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 8 of the principal Act, the following proviso shall be inserted namely :- "Provided that the tolls collected by an Authorised person shall not be deemed- public revenue."
Indian Tolls (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Act, 1995_Section 10--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) The Indian Tolls (Uttar Pradesh Amendment) Ordinance, 1995 is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the provisions of the principal Act, as amended by the Ordinance referred to in sub-section (1), shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act, as amended by this Act, as if the provisions of this Act were in force at all material times.
