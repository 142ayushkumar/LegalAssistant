Uttar Pradesh Motor Gadi (Yatri-Kar) (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT further to amend the Uttar Pradesh Motor Gadi (Yatri-Kar) Adhiniyam, 1962 IT IS HEREBY enacted in the Thirty-second Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Uttar Pradesh Motor Gadi (Yatri-Kar) (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Motor Gadi (Yatri-Kar) (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1981. (2) It shall be deemed to have come into force on April 1, 1981.
Uttar Pradesh Motor Gadi (Yatri-Kar) (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1981_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 3 of the Uttar Pradesh Motor Gadi (Yatri-Kar) Adhiniyam, 1962, hereinafter referred to as the principal Act, in sub-section (3-A), for the words "insurance of passengers", the words "providing relief to passengers in case of accidents" shall be substituted.
Uttar Pradesh Motor Gadi (Yatri-Kar) (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1981_Section 3--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In section 30 of the principal Act, in sub-section (2) , after clause (a) the following clause shall be inserted, namely :- "(aa) prescribing the principles for determination of the amount of relief referred to in sub-section (3-A) of section 3 and the manner in which it shall be paid"
Uttar Pradesh Motor Gadi (Yatri-Kar) (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1981_Section 4--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) The Uttar Pradesh Motor Gadi (Yatri-Kar) (Amendment) Ordinance, 1981, is hereby repealed. (2) Notwithstanding such repeal, anything done or any action taken under the provisions of the principal Act as amended by the Ordinance referred to in sub-section (1), shall be deemed to have been done or taken under the corresponding provisions of the principal Act as amended by this Act, as if the provisions of this Act were in force at all material times.
