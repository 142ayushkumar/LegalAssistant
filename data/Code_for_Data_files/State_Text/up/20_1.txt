U. P. (Temporary) Control of Rent and Eviction (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1963_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh AN ACT to amend the U. P. (Temporary) Control of Rent and Eviction Act, 1947 for the purpose of extending its life. IT IS HEREBY enacted in the Fourteenth Year of the Republic of India as follows :-
U. P. (Temporary) Control of Rent and Eviction (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1963_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh This Act may be called the U. P. (Temporary) Control of Rent and Eviction (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1963.
U. P. (Temporary) Control of Rent and Eviction (Sanshodhan) Adhiniyam, 1963_Section 2--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh In sub-section (4) of section 1 of the U. P. (Temporary) Control of Rent and Eviction Act, 1947, for the figure "1963" the figure "1969" shall be substituted.
