Uttar Pradesh Imposition of Ceiling on Land Holdings Act, 1960_Section Preamble--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh An Act to provide for the imposition of ceiling on land holdings in Uttar Pradesh and certain other matters connected therewith Whereas it is necessary in the interests of the community to ensure increased agricultural production and to provide land for landless agricultural labourers and for other public purposes as best to sub serve the common goods; And, whereas a more equitable distribution of land is essential; And, therefore, it is expedient to provide for the imposition of ceiling on land holdings in Uttar Pradesh for the aforementioned purposes; It is hereby enacted in the Eleventh Year of the Republic of India as follows :
Uttar Pradesh Imposition of Ceiling on Land Holdings Act, 1960_Section 1--> State(s): Uttar Pradesh (1) This Act may be called the Uttar Pradesh Imposition of Ceiling on Land Holdings Act, 19601 . (2) It extends to the whole of Uttar Pradesh. 2 [(3) It shall come into force at once except in the areas mentioned in Section 2, where it shall come into force from such date and in such manner as may be notified under that section, and different dates may be appointed for different areas. Received the assent of the President of India on 24th December, 1960 and published in the U.P. Gazette (Extra.), dated 3rd January, 1961. In its application to transferred territories from Bihar, Sub-section (3) shall stand omitted, vide U.P. Act No. 52 of 1976 (w.e.f. 15-10-1976).
