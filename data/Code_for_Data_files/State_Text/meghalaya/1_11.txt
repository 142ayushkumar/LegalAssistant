Meghalaya Amusements and Betting Tax (Amendments) Act, 1981_Section Preamble--> State(s): Meghalaya An Act further to amend the Meghalaya Amusements and Betting Tax Act (Assam Act VI of 1939), as adopted by Meghalaya (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act) Be it enacted by the Legislature of Meghalaya in the Thirty-second year if the Republic of India as follows:
Meghalaya Amusements and Betting Tax (Amendments) Act, 1981_Section 1--> State(s): Meghalaya (1) This Act may be called the Meghalaya Amusements and Betting Tax (Amendments) Act, 1981.1 (2) It shall have the like extent as the principal Act. (3) It shall come into force on such date as the State Government may, by notification, appoint. Received the assent of the Governor on the 10th April, 1981
