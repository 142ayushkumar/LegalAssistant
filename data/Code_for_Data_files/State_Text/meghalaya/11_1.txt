Meghalaya Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section Preamble--> State(s): Meghalaya An Act further to amend the Meghalaya Sales Tax Act (Assam Act XVII of 1947 as adopted and modified by Meghalaya) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act). BE it enacted by the Legislature of Meghalaya in the thirty-fourth year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Meghalaya Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 1--> State(s): Meghalaya (1) This Act may be called the Meghalaya Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1983. (2) It shall have the like extent as the principal Act. (3) It shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 19th day of September, 1983.
Meghalaya Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 2--> State(s): Meghalaya In Schedule to the principal Act, item serial No. 4 shall be deleted.
Meghalaya Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 3--> State(s): Meghalaya In Schedule III to the principal Act :- (i) the words "but excluding wheat and wheat products" occurring under column 2 in item 1 shall be deleted; and (ii) the following shall be inserted as item 12, namely :- "Serial No. Description 12 Flour, including atta, maida, suji and bran."
Meghalaya Sales Tax (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 4--> State(s): Meghalaya The Meghalaya Sales Tax (Amendment) Ordinance, 1983 (See (1983) 54 STC Statutes 94.), is hereby repealed.
