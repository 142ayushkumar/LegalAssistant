Meghalaya Electricity Duty (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section Preamble--> State(s): Meghalaya An Act further to amend the Meghalaya Electricity Duty Act (Assam Act 29 of 1964 as adapted by Meghalaya) hereinafter referred to as the principal Act Be it enacted by the Legislature of Meghalaya in the twenty-fifth year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Meghalaya Electricity Duty (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 1--> State(s): Meghalaya (1) This Act may be called the Meghalaya Electricity Duty (Amendment) Act, 1974. (2) It shall have the like extent as the principal Act. (3) It shall come into force at once.
Meghalaya Electricity Duty (Amendment) Act, 1974_Section 2--> State(s): Meghalaya In sub- section (1) of Section 3 of the principal Act, for the words, "two naye paise" the words, "three naye paise" shall be substituted.
