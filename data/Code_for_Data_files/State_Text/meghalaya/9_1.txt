Prevention of Disqualification (Members of the Legislative Assembly of Meghalaya) (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section Preamble--> State(s): Meghalaya An Act further to amend the Prevention of Disqualification (Members of the Legislative Assembly of Meghalaya) Act, 1972. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Meghalaya in the Sixty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Prevention of Disqualification (Members of the Legislative Assembly of Meghalaya) (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 1--> State(s): Meghalaya (1) This Act may be called the Prevention of Disqualification (Members of the Legislative Assembly of Meghalaya) (Amendment) Act, 2015. (2) It shall come into force on and from 1st October, 2015.
Prevention of Disqualification (Members of the Legislative Assembly of Meghalaya) (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 2--> State(s): Meghalaya In the Schedule to the Prevention of Disqualification (Members of the Legislative Assembly of Meghalaya) Act, 1972, entry No. 9 shall be omitted.
Prevention of Disqualification (Members of the Legislative Assembly of Meghalaya) (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 3--> State(s): Meghalaya The Prevention of Disqualification (Members of the Legislative Assembly of Meghalaya) Act, 1970 (Act I of 1970) is repealed and consequent upon such repeal the provision of Section 22 of the Meghalaya Interpretation and General Clauses Act, 1972 shall apply. (L. M. Sangma) Special Secretary to the Government of Meghalaya, Law Department.
