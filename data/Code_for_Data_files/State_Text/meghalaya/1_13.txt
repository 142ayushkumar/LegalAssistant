Meghalaya Appropriation (No. 1) Act, 2015_Section Preamble--> State(s): Meghalaya An Act to authorise payment and appropriation of certain further sums from and out of the consolidated Fund of Meghalaya for the services of Financial year 2014-2015 Be it enacted by the Legislative of the State of Meghalaya in the Sixty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Meghalaya Appropriation (No. 1) Act, 2015_Section 1--> State(s): Meghalaya (1) This Act may be called the Meghalaya Appropriation (No. I) Act, 2015.
Meghalaya Appropriation (No. 1) Act, 2015_Section 2--> State(s): Meghalaya From and out of the Consolidated Fund of Meghalaya there may be paid and applied sums not exceeding those specified in Column (3) of the Schedule amounting in the aggregate to the sums of Rs.417,68,18,668 (Rupees four hundred seventeen crores, sixty eight lakhs, eighteen thousand, six hundred and sixty eight) only towards defraying the several charges which will come in course of payment during the financial year ending on the thirty first day of March 2015 in respect of the services specified in Column (2) of the Schedule.
Meghalaya Appropriation (No. 1) Act, 2015_Section 3--> State(s): Meghalaya The sums authorised to be withdrawn from and out of the Consolidated Fund of Meghalaya by this Act, shall be appropriated for the services and purposes expressed in the Schedule in relation to the financial year 2014-2015.
