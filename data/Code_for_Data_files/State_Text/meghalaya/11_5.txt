Meghalaya Special Courts (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section Preamble--> State(s): Meghalaya An Act to amend the Meghalaya Special Courts Act, 2014 (Act No. 7 of 2014) hereinafter referred to as the principal Act. Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Meghalaya in the Sixty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows:-
Meghalaya Special Courts (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 1--> State(s): Meghalaya (1) This Act may be called the Meghalaya Special Courts (Amendment) Act, 2015. (2) It shall come into force with immediate effect.
Meghalaya Special Courts (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 2--> State(s): Meghalaya The existing preamble in the principal Act shall be omitted and the following new preamble shall be substituted, namely,- "to provide for establishment of Special Courts for expeditious trial of any offence against the State or any individual and for matters connected therewith or incidental thereto."
Meghalaya Special Courts (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 3--> State(s): Meghalaya (1) The existing sub-section (f) of section 2 shall be omitted and a new sub-section (f) shall be substituted, as follows,- "(f) "offence" means violation of law or an act of omission or commission made punishable under any law for the time being inforce;" (2) In sub-section (h) of section 2 after the words and figure "section 3" the words "of this Act" shall be inserted. (3) After sub-section (h) of section 2, a new sub-section (i) shall be inserted as follows,- "(i) "Public Prosecutor" means any person appointed under section 24 of the Code, and includes a Special Public Prosecutor".
Meghalaya Special Courts (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 4--> State(s): Meghalaya In sub-section (1) of section 5 the following amendment shall be made, namely,- (1) In between the words "If" and "the" appearing in first line the words "in the opinion of" shall be inserted; (2) The words "is of the opinion that" appearing in the first line between the words "Government" and "there" shall be omitted; (3) In between the words "person" and "that" appearing in the third line the words "or persons, individually or collectively, shall be inserted; and (4) In between the words "by" and "Special" appearing in third line the word "a" shall be inserted.
Meghalaya Special Courts (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 5--> State(s): Meghalaya In section 7 the following amendment shall be made, namely,- (1) In between the words "person" and "alleged" appearing in first line the words "or persons, individually or collectively, charged with" shall be inserted; (2) The words "alleged to have committed" appearing in first and second line shall be omitted; and (3) In between the words "principal" and "conspirator" appearing in third line the word "accused" shall be inserted.
Meghalaya Special Courts (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 6--> State(s): Meghalaya In sub-section (1) and sub-section(2) of section 8 the following amendment, shall be made, namely,- (1) In sub-section (1) in between the words "of" and "cases" appearing in second line the word "such" shall be inserted; (2) In sub-section (1) the words "before a Magistrate" appearing after the word "cases" shall be omitted; (3) In sub-section (2) the words "persons conducting a" appearing in fourth line shall be omitted; (4) In sub-section (2) in between the words "be" and "deemed" appearing in fifth line the words "conducted by" shall be inserted; (5) In sub-section (2) the words "deemed to be" appearing in fifth line shall be omitted; and (6) In sub-section (2) after the word "Prosecutor" appearing in last line the words "as defined in this Act" shall be inserted.
Meghalaya Special Courts (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 7--> State(s): Meghalaya In section 13, between the words "sixty" and "to" appearing in last line the words "days which may be extended" shall be inserted.
Meghalaya Special Courts (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 8--> State(s): Meghalaya In section 16, between the words "anything" and "in good faith" appearing in second line, the word "done" shall be inserted. L. M. Sangma, Special Secretary to the Government of Meghalaya, Law Department.
