Legislative Assembly of Meghalaya (Members' Pension) (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section Preamble--> State(s): Meghalaya An Act further to amend the Legislative Assembly of Meghalaya (Members' Pension) Act, 1977. Be it enacted by the Legislative of the State of Meghalaya in the Sixty-sixth Year of the Republic of India as follows,-
Legislative Assembly of Meghalaya (Members' Pension) (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 1--> State(s): Meghalaya (1) This Act may be called the Legislative Assembly of Meghalaya (Members' Pension) (Amendment) Act, 2015. (2) It shall come into force at on and from the 1st October, 2015.
Legislative Assembly of Meghalaya (Members' Pension) (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 2--> State(s): Meghalaya In Section 3 (1) of the Legislative Assembly of Meghalaya (Members' Pension) Act, 1977, hereinafter to as principal Act, as amended for the words "ten thousand", the words "seventeen thousand" shall be substituted.
Legislative Assembly of Meghalaya (Members' Pension) (Amendment) Act, 2015_Section 3--> State(s): Meghalaya After Section 4A of the principal Act, the following explanation shall be inserted, namely,- "Explanation - 'Services' means services under the Government as Government servant but does not include service as elected representative". L. M. Sangma, Special Secretary to the Government of Meghalaya, Law Department.
