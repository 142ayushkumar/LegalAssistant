Meghalaya Purchase Tax (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section Preamble--> State(s): Meghalaya An Act further to amend the Meghalaya Purchase Tax Act (Assam Act XIX of 1967 as adopted and modified by Meghalaya) (hereinafter referred to as the principal Act). BE it enacted by the Legislature of Meghalaya in the thirty-fourth year of the Republic of India as follows :-
Meghalaya Purchase Tax (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 1--> State(s): Meghalaya (1) This Act may be called the Meghalaya Purchase Tax (Amendment) Act, 1983. (2) It shall have the like extent as the principal Act. (3) It shall be deemed to have come into force on and from the 27th day of August, 1983.
Meghalaya Purchase Tax (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 2--> State(s): Meghalaya In the schedule to the principal Act, for the existing item 5, the following shall be substituted, namely :- "Serial Name of taxable goods Rate of tax No. 5. Timber (converted and unconverted) Twenty-five paise excluding firewood. per rupee value at which the taxable goods are purchased."
Meghalaya Purchase Tax (Amendment) Act, 1984_Section 3--> State(s): Meghalaya The Meghalaya Purchase Tax (Amendment) Ordinance, 1983 (See (1983) 54 STC Statutes 76.), is hereby repealed.
